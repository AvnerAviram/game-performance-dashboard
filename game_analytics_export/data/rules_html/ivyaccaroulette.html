    Acca Roulette

    Objective:

    The objective of the game isto accurately guess the outcome of the roulette spin (whether the right number or the right group) based on the questions answered and the player will win if the outcome of the roulette wheel matches with the answered question.

    The Game:

    Acca Roulette is played using a wheel with 37 numbered slots (1-36, plus a 0), the player must predict all the 6 questions in the BET SLIP and click on ‘PLACE BET & SPIN’ after choosing the stake amount to be wagered. The ball is spun around the wheel and will come to rest in one of the numbered slots, indicating the winning number/ group.

    How To play

    • The game is a prediction-based game where player must choose either YES or NO to the questions displayed in the BET SLIP to start the game.
    • The player will be asked 6 questions, the player must answer YES or NO to each question.
    • The associated odds are added to the “BET SLIP”.  This is repeated until all the questions in the BET SLIP are answered.
    • After answering all the questions, player need to select the bet amount to be wagered. Once player has finished placing bets, Click on PLACE BET & SPIN button to start the game.
    • When the ball comes to rest, the winning number is highlighted on the wheel.
    • Game will automatically continue if the previous spin is win, if not new game will be initiated.
    • PLACE BET button will be enabled and available after choosing YES or NO options from 2nd question.
    • Win amount for the spin depends on the option player selected for that question and will be shown to the player on game screen after Win icon.
    • Player has an option to place bet from second question by skipping to answer for further questions.
    • First Question YES/NO odds are multiplied with the further selected question odds for calculating the payouts. For example, if player selects all YES for the questions displayed, then the Total will be multiplication of all YES odds for the questions.
    • Player has an option to ‘Cashout/Half Cashout’ the winnings only after third spin win. This “Cashout/Half Cashout” options are available for 4th and 5th spins as well. The Cashout amount will be 90% of the win amount*stake selected by the player. Half Cashout is half of the Cashout amount will be awarded to the player. Half Cashout amounts will be added to the player balance once all the spins for the questions are completed.
    • Player can click on ‘Spin’ button to continue with further questions without Cashing out the winnings.
    • All pays are rounded towards most accurate accumulated return to the player.
    • All rounding in this roulette uses the "Half Round Up" method. This means cents go up if the value has decimal point greater than or equal to 5 and stay with same value if the decimal point is less than 5.

      Round off examples

      • 1.7650 becomes 1.77
      • 1.7760 becomes 1.78
      • 1.7640 becomes 1.76
    • All payouts (Cashout, Half Cashout, total win amounts) will be rounded according to this 'Half Round Up' method.
    • STAKE: The total bet amount is shown in the STAKE meter at the bottom of BET SLIP.
    • TOTAL: Total amount is the multiplication of win amounts for all 6 questions based on player choice.
    • Maximum win from this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency.
    • Minimum and maximum bet for a bet slip will be displayed on the game UI after answering Question 2. Any bets below or above the minimum or maximum bets will be rejected.
    • Maximum bet is set to player based on Maximum win which is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency.
    • POTENTIAL RETURNS: Potential Returns in the game are calculated as selected STAKE * TOTAL. Differences in potential return values will be corrected after placing a bet.
    • Game balance is displayed at the bottom of the game screen.
    • Tap on the Help button from the game menu on the top right of the game screen to view the game rules.
    • To switch on or off sound in the game, tap on Sound button that is located at the right top corner of the screen in the game menu. Player can disable sounds option in the settings panel as well.
    • Turbo-mode: Wheel spin animation will speed up on selecting turbo mode in the settings panel.
    • Tele Type: Keeping this check box checked will stop the tele type animation coming for the questions. Uncheck this box to continue tele type animation in the game.

    LUCKY DIP: Player can click this button option which selects the lowest odds between YES/NO for all the further questions. Lucky dip will randomly pick the questions from the set. Lucky Dip option will disappear when player choose YES/NO for 5th question.

    Mobile Options:

    • Tap the Hamburger Menu Icon to view options such as the Help, settings and Deposit in mobile.
    • Tap the Home option to navigate back to lobby screen.
    • Tap the Help button to view the game rules.
    • Tap the Sound icon to stop the game music and vice versa.
    • Tap the ‘$’ option to navigate to deposit screen.
    • Tap anywhere on the screen to come out from the options.

    Browser Button Panel Commands:

    • Click on Game Rules to view the detailed rules of the game as web page.
    • Click on Game Logs to view the logs of the games you’ve played.
    • Click on Responsible Gaming to open Responsible gaming page.
    • Click on Real/Play to switch to Real or Play mode game.
    • Click on Deposit button to deposit money into your account.

    Theoretical Return to Player:

    The theoretical return to player of this game ranges from 88.07% - 90.98% depending on player strategy.

    The theoretical return to player for Cashout ranges from 80.66% - 85.81% depending on player strategy

    The theoretical return to player for Half Cashout ranges from 79.96% - 83.85% depending on player strategy.

    Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

    Game History:

    Detailed gameplay history can be accessed by logging into your account on the desktop website and choosing menu “Game Logs”.

    Adding Funds to your Account:

    To add funds to your account, click on Deposit in My Account then select the desired deposit option. The time taken to transfer funds and the fees charged will vary depending on which deposit option you choose. Funds can only be added at the beginning of a new game.

    What do I do if I reach my betting limits?

    To add funds to your account, click on the “DEPOSIT” button in the browser common panel, then select the desired deposit option.

    I could not complete the game. What should I do?

    If your game is disconnected during game play, new game will be restored not the current game when you connect back again, and you can check the current game details in game logs.

    If you are still having problems, please contact our 24/7 Customer Care team.
