    Bison Fury

    Game Objective:

    Bison Fury is a five reel, "All Ways Slot” game with 1024 possible ways to win in the main game as well as free spins. Symbols pay from left to right in consecutive combinations (Ways), including reel 1. The Scattered Sunset symbols do not pay. When three or more Scattered Sunset symbols appear anywhere in view, Free Spins feature is triggered. The free spins feature can be re-triggered within free spins. The Eagle Coin wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4 and substitutes for all other symbols, except for Scattered Sunset symbols.

    How to play:

    • Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet is set. You can change the bet value by clicking the + or - buttons either side of the BET meter.
    • The bet is 1 time of the bet value and is shown near BET in the lower left of the screen.
    • MAX BET: Click on ‘MAX BET’ to select the maximum possible bet. If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled.
    • Paytable: The paytable can be viewed by clicking on  the ‘i’ button located in a panel above the bet panel. The prizes displayed in the paytable are per unit bet and are shown as multipliers. You can go back to the game by clicking on the back on the paytable screen.
    • Spin: The SPIN button in the lower right corner starts the game.
    • Volume: The volume icon will play the game music. Click on the Volume icon to mute the volume.
    • CREDITS: Your remaining game balance after you have placed the desired bet is shown in ‘BALANCE’ meter i.e. the current game balance minus the total bet placed.
    • 'AUTOSPIN': Select the AUTOSPIN for hands-free play. The available options are:
    • Number of spins: The game will spin for the number of rotations selected from the slider menu. You can choose a maximum of 50 spins and play until your balance is more than the total bet placed.
    • Spin till Win equals or exceeds: The game will automatically spin until the win amount is equals or exceeds the amount entered.
    • Spin till feature trigger: The game will automatically spin until the next feature is triggered.
    • The auto spin game is terminated whenever The Big One Jackpot is triggered between auto spins.
    • To make use of the Auto spin feature, click on the 'AUTOSPIN' button in the bottom of the game screen. When the Auto spin window opens, check the boxes next to the option(s) you'd like to play with. Select from the drop-down menu where applicable. Click on 'START' to activate the selected options and start the spins. Similarly, click on Stop Auto spin button to stop the AUTO SPIN feature and return to the game screen.
    • When playing with multiple options and conditions selected, the condition that is satisfied first will take precedence over the others, and the remaining selected conditions will be disabled. You can then choose to either select the same set of options again or opt for new option(s) and proceed with the game.
    • 'AUTO SPIN' option will be disabled in the demo-money mode.
    • Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. ­To change your bet, increase or decrease the Bet shown on the game screen.
    • Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen.

    Game Settings:

    • To make use of Game Settings, click on the ‘settings button’ at the bottom of the game screen in between Paytable and Sound icons.
    • Volume: Players can set the volume of the sound effects and ambience by sliding using the volume button.
    • Sound effects: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game win animation sounds.
    • Ambience sound: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game background sound.
    • Quick spin: By checking this option, game play is faster than the regular spin.
    • Space bar to spin: By checking this option, the space bar can be used to trigger a spin. This option stays disabled if the intro video is not available for a game.

    • If two of the same symbols appear on reel 1 and 2, followed by three of that symbol appears on reel 3 in different rows, the way win does not pay for 5 of that symbol combination together. Instead, it will pay for 3 separate way wins using those 3 of the same symbols appearing on reel 3.
      e.g. If symbol nine lands on reel 1 and 2, and 3 x nine symbols land on reel 3, there will be three different way wins for the symbol nine. Each way would be a win with three consecutive occurrences of the symbol nine.
      The reel positions for the symbol nine way wins will be as below:
      0, 1, 2
      0, 1, 7

    • Way wins are multiplied by total bet amount. The Scattered Sunset symbols do not have any payout. There are 1024 possible ways to win in the main game as well as free spins.

    Game Rules:

    • Bison Fury is a five reel, "All Ways Slot” game with 1024 possible ways. 
    • Total Bet is 40 times the selected Bet Value.
    • All payouts are made according to paytable.
    • BET amount can be changed by using the ‘-‘and ‘+’ buttons.
    • SPIN begins the game with currently selected BET.
    • Way wins are multiplied by the amount total bet.
    • Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid.
    • All Possible Wins are added to balance.
    • All symbols pay left to right, starting from the left-most reel except for scatter symbol which pays anywhere in reel.
    • Eagle Coin is a Wild symbol which substitutes for all symbols except for Scattered sunset symbol.
    • Wild (Eagle Coin) and Scatter (Sunset) symbols do not have payout.
    • Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol appears only on reels 2,3 and 4 in both base game and Free Spins and doesn’t appear on 1 and 5 reels.
    • The Free Spins feature will be triggered when 3 or more Scattered Sunset symbols appear anywhere on the reel view.
    • Scattered Sunset symbol appear during Free Spins will trigger same number of free spins awarded before.
    • Malfunction voids all pays and plays..
    • The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 USD or its equivalent amount in your currency during the main game or bonus game. Further Free Spins or wins will be forfeited if the highest win is reached during the Free Spins.
    • Gameplay history can be accessed by logging into player account on the desktop website and choosing menu “History” inside any game.

    Free spins feature:

    • The Free Spins feature will be triggered when 3 or more Scattered Sunset symbols appear anywhere on the reel view.
    • Players will be awarded 8 Free Spins for 3 scatters, 20 Free Spins for 4 scatters and 50 Free Spins for 5 scatters.
    • During the Free Spins feature Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol acts as a sticky wild.      
    • All way wins during Free Spins are multiplied by triggering bet of free spins.
    • The Feature re-trigger during the Free Spins will add Free Spins based on the number of scatter (Sunset) symbols appeared on reels i.e. 3 Scatters will re-trigger 8, 4 scatters will re-trigger 20 and 5 scatters will re-trigger 50 free spins.

     

    Sticky Wild Rules: 

    • During the Free Spins feature, if the Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol appears in view awards re-spins, a random number between 1-5 is displayed on the Wild(Eagle Coin) symbol and the Wild(Eagle Coin) sticks to the position until counter on respective Wild(Eagle coin) reaches to 0.
    • The Wild (Eagle Coin) will stick in place as the re-spins are counted down to 0.
    • The number shown on any Eagle Coin(s) counts down for each re-spin.
    • During re-spins further wild (Eagle Coins) in view will add more sticky wilds (Eagle Coins) and re-spins. The more awarded re-spins are added to the re-spin counter displayed at the bottom of the game.
    • The sticky Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol disappears on completion of re-spins and the player will be continued with the remaining Free Spins.

    • If you get disconnected in the middle of the base game, the software will automatically complete play for you. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the 'History' button once you have logged back in.
    • If you are disconnected in the middle of the feature round, you will be prompted to complete it after logging back in. After completing the gamble feature, you can continue game play, or you can select another game of your choice.

    2.What should I do if the balance is low to place bet?

    • Click on ‘Deposit’ button at bottom of the game screen and deposit.
