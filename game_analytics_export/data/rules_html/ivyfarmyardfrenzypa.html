    Farmyard Frenzy

    Game Objective:

    • Farmyard Frenzy is a 5x3 reels slot game with 10 fixed paylines.
    • Free spins feature is triggered when 3 or more scatter symbols land in view..
    • The “Golden Egg” feature is activated when Golden Egg appears anywhere in the reel view during the feature along with the wild (Girl) symbol ,Will symbol will collect the golden egg and moves to golden egg feature.
    • The ‘PAYTABLE’ below shows winnings for $1.00 bet

    Game Information:

    • Game is played with 10 fixed number of lines.
    • Game currently starting with minimum bet.
    • The TOTAL BET is Number of fixed lines multiplied by the bet per line.
    • Bet per Line can be changed by using the   and  buttons.
    • SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the current selected BET.
    • Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE.
    • The paytable will always show the respective symbol payout amounts for the currently selected bet on 10 fixed paylines.
    • Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline.
    • Wins on different bet lines are all added to your total.
    • Coinciding wins on different paylines are added to total.
    • All symbols pay from left to right.
    • The Ticker messages will appear on the bottom of game screen.
    • Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will update, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at the bottom of the game.
    • The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and demo-money modes, except that play-money games do not contribute to the jackpot amount and auto spin option will not be available in demo-money mode.

    Game Rules:

    1. Farmyard Frenzy is a 5-reel slot game with 10 Fixed pay lines.
    2. All payouts are made according to the paytable.
    3. Pay table will show the win amount for the currently selected bet on 10 fixed pay lines.
    4. SPIN begins the game with the currently selected BET and fixed LINES.
    5. MAX BET selects 10 lines and maximum bet/line value.
    6. Only the highest win on each pay line is paid.
    7. Coinciding wins on different pay lines are added to your total balance.
    8. Egg symbols contain bonus numbers for the Free Spins round.
    9. 3 or more Scatter (Rooster) symbols anywhere in view triggers the Free spins feature round.
    10. All symbols pay from left to right, starting from leftmost, except the scatter (Rooster) symbol.
    11. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
    12. The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 USD during main game or Free spins. Further Free spins or line wins will be forfeited if the highest win is reached during the free spins.
    13. Further Free spins or line wins or egg bonus collection will be forfeited if the highest win is reached during the Free spins.
    14. Theoretical return to player is 96.42%.
    15. Scatter (Rooster) symbol do not have any payout during base game and also Golden Egg will appear on reel 3 only during base game and feature game.
    16. If 3 or more egg symbols appear on a payline, then payout will be paid according to the paytable in base game and feature rounds.
    17. The min bet is $0.20 and the max bet is $50.00.

    Free spins feature:

    1. The Free Spins feature is triggered when 3 or more Scatter (Rooster) symbols appear anywhere on the reels during the main game.
    2. 3 Scatter (Rooster) symbols will provide 10, 4 Scatter (Rooster) symbols will provide 15 and 5 Scatter (Rooster) symbols will provide 20 Free spins respectively.
    3. During Freespins, Scatter (Rooster) symbol does not appear.
    4. Wild (Girl) symbol substitutes for all symbols. Wild symbol appears only in the Free spins and Golden Egg feature.
    5. Free spins are played with the same bet of the triggering spin.
    6. Egg symbols appearing during the Free spins contain bonus values.
    7. Any egg in view is collected by the Girl at the bonus value shown and added to total win.
    8. Different Egg symbols can appear during Free spins.
    9. Egg symbols with 2 eggs have 1 and 2 as multiplier; Egg symbols with 3 eggs will have 5, 10 and 15 as multipliers; Egg symbols with 4 eggs will have 20, 25 and 50 as multipliers, to the total bet.
    10. Scatter (Rooster) wins will be added to line wins.
    11. Payout for the Eggs will be repeated for each wild symbol in view if there are more than one wild symbol in reel view.

    Golden Egg Feature:

    1. When the Golden Egg symbol appears anywhere in view along with the Girl symbol the Golden Egg Feature will be activated.
    2. Two extra free spins will be awarded for each Girl symbol and a Golden Egg symbol in view.
    3. All other symbols are removed during the Golden Egg feature except for Eggs, Girl and blanks (Farmyardfenzy). This paying reel set will continue for rest of the Free spins.
    4. The feature can re-trigger with further Free spins.

    FAQ’s:

    1. I could not complete the game. What should I do?

    • If you get disconnected in the middle of the base game, the software will automatically complete play for you. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the ‘History’ button once you have logged back in.
    • If you are disconnected in the middle of the feature round, you will be prompted to complete it after logging back in. After completing the feature round, you can continue game play, or you can select another game of your choice.

    2.What should I do if the balance is low to place bet?

    • Click on ‘Deposit’  button at bottom of the game screen and deposit.

    3. If you are still having problems, please contact our 24/7 Customer Care team.
