    Slingo Fire and Ice

    INTRODUCTION

    SLINGO FIRE AND ICE IS A FAST PACED GAME WITH A DOUBLE SLINGO GRID.

    MOVE UP THE FIRE PAY LADDER TO INCREASE THE CASH VALUES ON THE ICE LADDER.

    MOVE UP THE ICE PAY LADDER TO WIN CASH.

    SELECT YOUR STAKE AND PRESS START GAME TO BEGIN.

    YOU HAVE 10 SPINS TO MATCH NUMBERS ON THE REEL WITH THE GRID.

    COMPLETE SLINGOS TO MOVE UP THE PAYLADDERS.

    FIRE SYMBOLS MARK THE FIRE GRID.

    ICE SYMBOLS MARK THE ICE GRID.

    MIXED SYMBOLS MARK BOTH GRIDS.

    WILDS

    FIRE WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK THE FIRE GRID.

    WILD ALLOWS YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER ADJACENT TO WHERE THEY LAND.

    ICE WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK THE ICE GRID.

    MIXED WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK BOTH GRIDS.

    FREE SPINS AND BLOCKERS

    FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ADD AN ADDITIONAL SPIN.

    BLOCKER SYMBOLS DO NOTHING.

    JACKPOTS

    COMPLETE A DOUBLE SLINGO TO ACTIVATE THE JACKPOT REEL.

    LAND A FIRE GEM TO WIN THE FIRE JACKPOT.

    LAND AN ICE GEM TO WIN THE ICE JACKPOT.

    JACKPOT REEL IS ACTIVE FOR ALL REMAINING SPINS ONCE THE DOUBLE SLINGO IS COMPLETE.

    JACKPOT REEL CAN CONTAIN FIRE GEMS, ICE GEMS OR LOSE SYMBOLS.

    EXTRA SPINS

    EXTRA SPINS ARE AVAILABLE AFTER EACH GAME.

    PRICES MAY BE IN EXCESS OF YOUR BASE STAKE.

    CHOOSING SPIN FIRE WILL APPLY MATCHES ONLY TO THE FIRE GRID.

    CHOOSING SPIN ICE WILL APPLY MATCHES ONLY TO THE ICE GRID.

    YOU CAN CONTROL EXTRA SPIN LIMITS IN PLAY CONTROLS.

    SLINGO WINS

    THERE ARE 12 WIN LINES AND 11 AWARDS ON THE PAYTABLE BECAUSE THE LAST NUMBER ON THE GRID WILL ALWAYS AWARD AT LEAST 2 WINNING LINES.

    BEST STRATEGY

    THE BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF WILDS AND SUPER WILDS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAY CLOSER TO A SLINGO.

    WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA ARE AVAILABLE THE PREFERENCE IS GIVEN TO THE POSITIONS THAT ARE INCLUDED IN THE MOST SLINGOS (WIN LINES).

    FOR EXAMPLE THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN THE HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES.

    WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA EXIST AGAIN A RANDOM CHOICE IS MADE.

    PLAY CONTROLS

    PLAY CONTROLS CAN BE SELECTED FROM THE MENU.

    SET THE MAXIMUM PRICE TO BE OFFERED AS AN EXTRA SPIN.

    SET THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF EXTRA SPINS TO BE OFFERED.

    SET THE MAXIMUM STAKE FOR ANY SINGLE GAME.

    SET THE MAXIMUM LOSS FOR ANY SINGLE GAME.

    THE GAME WILL AUTOMATICALLY END IF ANY OF THE SET LIMITS ARE REACHED.

    GENERAL

    BASED ON BEST STRATEGY THIS GAME HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 96.04%

    BASED ON BEST STRATEGY EACH EXTRA SPIN HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 95.05%

    FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS IN EXTRA SPINS.

    IN EXTRA SPINS SOME POTENTIAL PRIZES MAY REQUIRE SUPER WILDS.

    EXTRA SPINS ARE ONLY OFFERED IF A WIN SPIN IS ACHIEVABLE ON THE NEXT SPIN.

    EXTRA SPIN PRICES ARE ROUNDED TO THE NEAREST WHOLE VALUE WHICH MAY AFFECT THE RTP.

    MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS.

    FREE GAMES

    IF A PLAYER IS AWARDED A FREE GAME THEY WILL BE ABLE TO PLAY THE BASE GAME FOR FREE AT A PREDEFINED STAKING VALUE.

    WHEN THE BASE GAME IS COMPLETED, THE WINNINGS ACCRUED BY COMPLETING HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL OR DIAGONAL SLINGOS OR OTHER SPECIAL SYMBOL BONUSES WILL BE DISPLAYED TO THE PLAYER AND COLLECTED.

    FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, THE FREE GAME IS CONSIDERED AS A BONUS CASH STAKE.

    PENDING GAMES

    INCOMPLETE GAMES WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY COMPLETED 3 HOURS AFTER LAUNCH.

    ANY REMAINING SPINS WILL BE PLAYED OUT WITH A RANDOM NUMBER GENERATOR USED TO MAKE ANY PLAYER DECISIONS.

    ANY WINNINGS WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY CREDITED.
