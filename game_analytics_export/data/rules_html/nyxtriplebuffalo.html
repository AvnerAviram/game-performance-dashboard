    Triple Buffalo

    RTP: 95.0%

    Volatility: 10.4

    Game Rules

    Malfunction voids all pays and play

    This game is played at 720 ways and all 5 reels are always in play.

    Select Bet

    Click the Spin button to spin the reels

    Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits state

    Highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination.

    Double symbols substitute as 2-of-a-kind symbols.

    Triple symbols substitute as 3-of-a-kind symbols.

    Wild symbols substitute for any symbol except for the Free Games Bonus and Pick Bonus symbols

    Your winnings are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier

    Free Games bonus

    3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2,3, and 4 awards the Free Games Bonus

    Awards 6 free games.

    Free Games are awarded on a separate screen with different reels

    Free games are played at the same bet multiplier as the triggering game.3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards 3 additional free games.

    PICK BONUS

    Three Pick Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Pick Bonus.

    Select one to set the available awards and volatility for the Pick Bonus

    The Bonus features 5 tiers.

    On each tier, select a stone face to reveal a credit award or Bonus Complete symbol and advance to the next tier.

    The Pick Bonus ends when a Bonus Complete symbol is selected, or when 5 credit awards have been selected.

    JACKPOT

    Three or more Jackpot symbols appearing anywhere awards a Jackpot credit award
