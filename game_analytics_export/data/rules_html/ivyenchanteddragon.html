    ENCHANTED DRAGON

    GAME OBJECTIVES:

    • ENCHANTED DRAGON is a 6-reel slot game with 4096 WAYS.
    • Symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT, starting from left most reelin consecutive combinations (WAYS) starting from Reel 1 Except for DRAGON BONUS, RED ENVOLEPE, CASH, and JACKPOT symbols.
    • 3 or more DRAGON BONUS symbols in view activate the BONUS FEATURE CHOICE, which allows the player to choose one of four options with different volatility.
    • Land the RED ENVELOPE symbol in the base game and the FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS to collect all the CASH and JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) awards in view.

    HOW TO PLAY:

    • BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘+’ or ‘-‘buttons on either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. Clicking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET.
    • REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game.
    • PLAY: The PLAY button in the center right position starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the center bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode.
    • PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the HAMBURGER icon and selecting the PAYTABLE option. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game.
    • VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF.
    • CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Players current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen.
    • AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play.
      • To configure AUTOPLAY: Click the AUTOPLAY button and then select the required number of plays. AUTOPLAY begins automatically.
      • The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time.
    • In play-money mode, the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled.

    MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP):

    • Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information.
    • Click the ‘i’ icon to view game information and rules.
    • Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards.
    • Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game play.

    GAME RULES:

    • ENCHANTED DRAGON is a 6-reel slot game with 4096 WAYS. 
    • The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons.
    • Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the current selected or default BET amount.
    • All payouts are made according to the paytable. The paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET.
    • All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive reels, starting from the left most reel, except for the DRAGON BONUS, RED ENVELOPE with CASH or JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbols which pay for landing anywhere in view.
    • DRAGON BONUS symbol acts as a scatter symbol.
    • The DRAGON BONUS symbol and RED ENVELOPE symbol wins are added to way wins.
    • Only the highest win in each possible combination will be paid.
    • All possible wins are added to the player BALANCE.
    • Each CASH symbol that lands in view will have a random Multiplier applied to the current selected BET amount from the range: 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x.
    • In the base game and FORTUNE SPINS feature, the DRAGON BONUS symbol payout is awarded when 3 or more DRAGON BONUS symbols appear anywhere in view. 
    • Landing the RED ENVELOPE symbol with CASH/JACKPOT symbols anywhere in view will collect and award all CASH and JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) awards in view.
    • Each RED ENVELOPE symbol collects all CASH and JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbols. (e.g., If RED ENVELOPE symbols land on reels 2 and 3 then both RED ENVELOPE symbols will collect all CASH and JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) in view). 
    • 3 or more DRAGON BONUS symbols landing anywhere in view will activate the BONUS FEATURE CHOICE.
    • The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except for DRAGON BONUS, CASH, JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, and MEGA) and RED ENVELOPE symbols. 
    • The RED ENVELOPE symbol will not appear in the FORTUNE SPINS feature.
    • The WILD symbol will not appear on reel 1 in the base game and will not appear on reels 1, 5 and 6 during the FORTUNE SPINS feature.
    • MIN BET is #{uiConfig.minBet} and MAX BET is #{uiConfig.maxBet}.
    • The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency during base game or FORTUNE SPINS or FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS features. Further FORTUNE SPINS, FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS wins and way wins will be forfeited if the highest win is capped.
    • The maximum win in this game is capped to 140,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency during base game or FORTUNE SPINS or FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS features. Further FORTUNE SPINS, FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS wins and way wins will be forfeited if the highest win is capped.
    • The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 94.34%.
    • The theoretical return to players over 1,000,000,000 games is (RTP) is 94.34%..
    • Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “Game logs” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history.
    • Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
    • Win animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (desktop).

    BONUS FEATURE CHOICE:

    • 3 or more DRAGON BONUS symbols in view during the base game awards the associated payout and the BONUS CHOICE selection of FORTUNE SPINS WITH 5X Multiplier or FORTUNE SPINS WITH 3X Multiplier or FORTUNE SPINS WITH 2X Multiplier or the FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature.
    • The DRAGON BONUS symbol pays,
      • 1x BET for 3 DRAGON BONUS symbols.
      • 2x BET for 4 DRAGON BONUS symbols.
      • 3x BET for 5 DRAGON BONUS symbols.
      • 5x BET for 6 DRAGON BONUS symbols. 
    • FORTUNE SPINS and the FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS features are played at the selected BET of the triggering game.

    • During FORTUNE SPINS feature the WILD symbol transforms into a WILD Multiplier and Multiplies the qualifying wins by the Multiplier shown on the WILD symbol. 
    • Wins in FORTUNE SPINS with 5x Multiplier are multiplied by 5 for all wild substituted wins. Similarly, the wins for FORTUNE SPINS with 3x Multiplier and FORTUNE SPINS with 2x Multiplier are multiplied by 3 and 2 respectively.
    • If two or more WILD Multipliers appear in the same spin, the corresponding Multiplier values are multiplied, and way wins are given.
    • During FORTUNE SPINS feature landing 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 DRAGON BONUS symbols anywhere in view will award 2, 5, 8, 10, or 15 EXTRA FORTUNE SPINS respectively.

    FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS:

    • 3, 4, 5 or 6 DRAGON BONUS symbols will award 4, 6, 8 or 10 spins in the FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature plus the associated DRAGON BONUS symbol payout. 
    • Only RED ENVELOPE, CASH, JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) and BLANK symbols are in play during the FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature. 
    • RED ENVELOPE symbol will appear on all reels during FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature.
    • Each RED ENVELOPE symbol collects and awards all CASH VALUE and JACKPOT symbols if appear anywhere in view (e.g., If the RED ENVELOPE symbol lands on both reels 2 and 3 along with CASH and JACKPOT symbols, the CASH and JACKOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbol awards will be paid twice).
    • The FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature will end, when there are no more FORTUNE DRAGON SPINS remaining.

    JACKPOT:

    • The JACKPOT feature can be triggered during the base game and FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature.
    • A JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbol(s) payout can be won if the RED ENVELOPE symbol lands in view alongside the JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbols.
    • All the JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) symbols that are landed and in view along with RED ENVELOPE symbol, will award respective JACKPOT (MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA) payout to the player (Example: If 2 JACKPOTS MINOR and MAJOR symbols land in the view along with RED ENVELOPE symbol, then both the JACKPOTS payout are awarded).
    • JACKPOTS are named as MINOR, MAJOR and MEGA. Each JACKPOT will display the corresponding values:
    • MINOR = 25x BET. 
    • MAJOR = 250x BET. 
    • MEGA = 2500x BET.

    FAQ:

    I could not complete the game. What should I do?

    If you are disconnected in the middle of the game, the software platform will automatically complete the current game cycle. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the GAME LOGS button once you have logged back into the website.

    If you are disconnected after triggering or in the middle of the FORTUNE SPINS or FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature rounds on reconnection, player will be able to start the disconnected FORTUNE SPINS or FORTUNE DRAGON BONUS feature rounds, if not already started, or resume from where it was disconnected.

    What should I do if my BALANCE is too low to place a BET?

    Click the DEPOSIT button at bottom of the game screen and deposit funds into your account.

    If you are still having problems, please contact our 24/7 Customer Service Team.
