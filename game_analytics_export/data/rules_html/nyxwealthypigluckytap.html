    User Interface

    ‘+’ / ‘-’ buttons increase / decrease the bet value — the bet value is the total bet per game round.

    Starts a game round at the current bet value.

    Displays the autoplay options.

    Stops the autoplay session.

    The numerical value displayed within the button shows the number of autoplay game rounds remaining.

    Bet Deﬁnitions

    Current Bet: The current bet is the value that appears above  .

    Minimum Bet: The minimum bet is the lowest possible bet value, which is selected by pressing   until the bet value no longer decreases.

    Bet Multiplier: The bet multiplier is defined as the current bet divided by the minimum bet.

    Autoplay Options

    Autoplay will allow game rounds to be played automatically.

    Click on the Autoplay button and select the number of Autoplay game rounds.

    Additional Information

    The overall return to player is 96.67%.

    The game outcomes are determined by a random number generator that has been certified by an approved test house.

    Misuse and malfunction voids all pays and plays.

    The above 2 points may be subject to further terms, conditions and processes of the gaming site operator.

    Wins

    A winning play occurs when the piggy bank breaks completely during the game round.

    3 to 16 prizes can be awarded on a winning play, plus a chance to trigger the Free Games feature, if all 3   are attained at the same time.

    Multiplier Feature

    If 10 or fewer prizes are awarded on any winning play, the Multiplier Feature may randomly be activated.

    If the Multiplier Feature is activated, a Random Multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, or x7 will appear above the broken piggy bank. All Currency Wins will be multiplied by the Random Multiplier.

    Free Games

    10 Free Games are awarded if the 3  are awarded on a winning play.

    10 additional Free Games can be retriggered if the 3  are awarded on a winning play during Free games.

    The Multiplier Feature is always active for all wins in Free Games.
