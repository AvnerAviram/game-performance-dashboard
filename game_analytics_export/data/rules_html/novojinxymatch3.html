    Jinxy Match 3

    Game Theme and Graphics

    Jinxy is an unique combination of a casual match3 game play with a chance to win after each move. The game
    features Special Symbols, created by the player.

    Main Game

    Try your chance in this hot 7-rows, 8-column match game. Create a match of 3 or more symbols of the same
    color by switching two non-diagonal neighboring symbols. Symbols in match explode and new symbols fall from
    the top into the game board. There are win values from 0,5 to 100 times bet.
    Win Symbols: After every move, there is a chance for a win symbol to drop onto the board. You win as soon you successfully remove one of those symbols from the board. Collect the win value of a win symbol by moving
    it to the bottom of the board or by clearing it via a special symbol. Every win symbol gathered by you has its
    value added to your Credits.

    Matching Symbols: Symbols fall onto the board from above. If 3 identical symbols form a vertical or horizontal
    match, they are cleared from the board. If 4 or 5 symbols can be aligned in one of the formations, then they cleared from the board and depending on the formation - additional special symbols are created.

    Special Symbols

    Cross Special Symbols: resemble the basic symbols in colour and shape, but are marked with special arrows.
    If they are combined with 2 or more symbols of the same colour, they cause an explosion across the entire
    vertical column and horizontal line.
    This is how they are created:
    If you can clear a match of 4 identical symbols, vertically or horizontally, then a cross special symbols is created.


    Combine Cross Special symbols by switching it with other Special Symbols for an additional effect.


    Bomb Special Symbols: can only be moved by one position in any direction. It doesn't matter if the target
    location is occupied by a basic, win or special symbol. The detonation of a bomb special symbol is caused
    simply by moving it - it clears up to 12 surrounding symbols.
    This is how they are created:
    If a match of 5 identical symbols in a L - or T - form is cleared from the board then a bomb special symbol is
    created in its wake.


    Combine Bomb Special symbols by switching it with other Special Symbols for an additional effect.


    Bell Special Symbols: can only be moved by one position in any direction. The swap can be with an adjacent
    basic or special symbol. When a bell special symbol is swapped with another basic symbol, all symbols of the
    same colour turn into bell special symbols and immediately explode.
    This is how they are created:
    If a horizontal or vertical match of 5 identical symbols is cleared from the board, then a bell special symbol is
    created in its wake.


    Combine Bell Special symbols by switching it with other Special Symbols for an additional effect.

    Tutorial

    • The tutorial shows how to play the game.
    • The tutorial will explain the features of this game.
    • After the tutorial, playing the free game is possible or a click on "START TO PLAY FOR MONEY" will skip
      the free game and go straight to the real money game.

    Rules

    • Switch two neighboring symbols.
    • Match 3 or more of the same symbols to explode them.
    • Every move costs a bet.
    • After every move Win Symbols might be added to the board.
    • If 4 symbols of the same colour are matched (in line horizontally or vertically), a Cross Special Symbol is
      created. The Cross Special Symbol needs to match with 2 or more symbols of the same colour in order be cleared from the board.
    • If 5 symbols of the same colour are matched in a L or T shape, a Bomb Special Symbol is created. Bomb
      Special Symbols can be swapped in any direction, with any symbol, special symbol or win symbol.
    • If 5 symbols of the same colours in a row or column are matched, a Bell Special symbol is created. Bell
      Special Symbols can be swapped in any direction, with any symbol or special symbol.
    • Combine Special symbols by switching them with each other for an additional effect.
    • After every move, there is a chance for win symbols to drop onto the board. You gather win symbols by
      moving them to the bottom of the board or by hitting them with the explosion of a special symbol. Every symbol gathered by you has its value added to your Credits.
    • The player can change the bet before every move (except tutorial).
    • The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
