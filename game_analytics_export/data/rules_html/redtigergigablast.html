Game Features

Star Wilds

Star Wilds substitute for all paying symbols and count as the symbol which forms the longest winning combination on a single win line.

Wild Blast

Blast indicators appear above the reels and on the row sides. When a Star Wild lands within a row or reel with a Wild Blast symbol, it triggers an explosion, converting adjacent symbols into more Star Wilds. When both horizontal and vertical explosions are active, they create a double explosion, and if a Star Wild is already present, its multiplier increases by 1. Star Wilds can be boosted with x2, x3, or x5 multipliers. When both a multiplier and explosion are simultaneously active, the multiplier takes precedence. Explosions occur 1 by 1, starting with the Star Wilds on the reels and then moving to the newly created ones, with no impact on the Free Spins symbols.

Free Spins

Launching Free Spins is as simple as landing 3 or more Free Spins Scatters for a star-studded voyage. You'll earn 10, 15, 20, or 25 Free Spins depending on whether you land 3, 4, 5, or 6 Scatters. While you're already on your Free Spins adventure, finding 3 more Scatters adds an extra 5 spins—a celestial bonus boost! To top it off, the Wild Blast feature is more likely to activate during Free Spins.

Feature Buy

1. Wild Blast: For 3x the stake, trigger a Wild Blast with any modifier during the spin. 2. Free Spins: For 75x stake, land 3 or more Free Spins to access to the bonus round.
