    North Pole Power Tap

    1. Overview

    North Pole Power Tap is a game where the aim is to test your strength and reach the top of the meter to win. Each successful hit will award cash prizes and can also trigger the Bonus.

    RTP - 95%

    3. Special Symbols and Features

    3.1. Winning hit

    A winning hit will award between 3 & 10 Cash Prizes and light some of the B-O-N-U-S letters.

    Possible Cash Prizes (x BET): 0.2x, 0.4x, 0.6x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.2x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 6x, 8x, 12x, 16x, 24x, 40x, 80x.

    On any winning hit a random multiplier of between x2 and x10 can also boost the cash values.

    Lighting all 5 B-O-N-U-S letters will trigger the Bonus.

    Winning hit animations will play randomly.

    3.2. Losing hit

    A losing hit will not award any winnings.

    Losing hit animations will play randomly.

    3.3. Bonus

    The Bonus is triggered by lighting all 5 B-O-N-U-S letters on a winning hit.

    15 Taps are awarded.

    Any successful Tap will highlight one cash prize or boost the win multiplier.

    The win multiplier starts at x1 and increases by x1 every time a multiplier is awarded.

    When the Bonus ends, all highlighted prizes will be collected and the final multiplier applied.

    Minimum number of Cash Prizes = 5.

    Maximum number of Cash Prizes = 10.

    Maximum multiplier = x10

    5. Auto spin feature

    The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0.

    5.1. Desktop version

    Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following:

    • Number of auto spins: click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play.
    • Loss limit: click + or - to select the value of the loss limit;
    • Stop if a win reaches: click + or - to select the value of the maximum win;
    • Stop if JACKPOT is won (only for games with Progressive Jackpots): to enable this setting, tick the checkbox; to disable it, remove the tick.

    Click the ACTIVATE  button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE  button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature.

    Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time.

    5.2. Mobile version

    Tap the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following:
