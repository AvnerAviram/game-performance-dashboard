Get ready to deck the halls and roll the reels with Santa's Winter Wilds™, a magical 6×4 slot game with 25 win-lines, that will transport players to a land of winter wonder.  

This holiday season, Inspired is taking players on a fortune-filled festive adventure, with a game that's descended from Inspired's hit slot, Call of the Wild™. Santa's Winter Wilds is set to make Christmas merrier and wins even grander! 

Packed with base game wins and fun surprises, the game's mystery symbol reel modifier, represented as a gift, can appear, and deliver players with random symbols or a chance to enter the Bonus.  

Landing six or more Wild symbols sets off the Wild Win & Spin Bonus where players will receive three spins to collect as many Wilds as they can before the thrilling Wild Win Spin takes over. Landing more Wilds resets their spins to three, offering endless chances to stack up those wins. But beware, if no new Wild symbols land, spins decrease by one. When a player is down to their last spin, the vibrant reels spin once more, awarding them with a final win before the Bonus ends.  

If the reels are filled with Wild symbols, a whopping 25x six-of-a-kind Wild symbol award is granted, for truly enormous rewards! 

With its vibrant festive reels, Santa's Winter Wilds™ is set to take players on a wild ride of epic winter wins! As a medium volatility slot, it has a return to player (RTP) rate of 94.53%, aimed at the casual online casino player.
