FEATURES

WILD SANTA SYMBOL

Wild Santa symbols expand downward, becoming part of winning combinations. Expanded symbols count as wild on every covered position. Non-expanding Wild Santa symbols are single wilds. Each reel can have only one Wild Santa symbol.

WILD GIFT SYMBOL

When a Wild Santa symbol expands through a Wild Gift symbol, it transforms into a multiplier. Multiplier values range from 2x to 200x. If multiple expanded Wild Santas with multipliers form a winning combination, their values are added together before being applied to the win.

NIGHT BEFORE CHRISTMAS BONUS

Land 3 FS scatter symbols simultaneously in the base game to trigger a bonus with 10 free spins! In the Night Before Xmas feature, your chances of landing Wild Santa and Wild Gift symbols increase compared to the base game. Achieving 3 FS symbols during the feature grants 4 extra free spins. If 4 FS symbols land at once in the Night Before Xmas feature, the Santa Claus Is Coming To Town feature activates after the current spin. If the remaining free spins are below 10, they are set to 10; otherwise, the current number remains unchanged.

SANTA CLAUS IS COMING TO TOWN BONUS

Land 4 FS scatter symbols at the same time in the base game to trigger this bonus feature with 10 free spins!
In the Santa Claus Is Coming To Town feature each reel can be activated by landing a Wild Santa symbol on that reel! Once a reel is activated, a Wild Santa symbol is guaranteed to land on that reel on all remaining free
spins.
Landing 3 FS symbols at the same time during the feature awards 4 additional free spins.

SPECIAL SYMBOLS

The Wild Gift symbol substitutes for all other symbols in the pay table.
The Wild Santa symbol substitutes for all other symbols in the pay table.
