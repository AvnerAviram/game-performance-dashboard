    Foxin' Wins Again Reveal

    Foxin’ Wins Again Reveal is a match and win game. During play, the goal is to match 3 or 4 symbols to win a prize. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 85.01%.

    Game Rules

    • Match 3 or 4 symbols to win a prize
    • Match 3 Free Game Symbols to win Free Game
    • During Free Game collect BELL to go up the Prize Table
    • Prize Awarded depends on number of BELLS collected
    • More than one prize can be won per game
    • Payouts are made according to the Paytable
    • Wins are multiplied by the amount staked per game
    • Malfunction voids all pays and plays

    How to Play

    1: Choose your BET

    2: Select PLAY

    3: Swipe the panels to reveal a hidden symbol

    4: Select REVEAL ALL to reveal all hidden symbols.  

    MAIN GAME SCREEN BUTTONS:


    BET:

    Adjusts the BET. Use the slider to adjust the BET up or down.


    PLAY:

    		 To purchase reveal card and continue


    REVEAL ALL:

    Automatically reveal all the symbols


    AUTOPLAY:

    When more than zero AUTOPLAYs is selected the SPIN button will display the number of AUTOPLAYs that will be played


    STOP AUTOPLAY:

    Once auto play begins the SPIN button turns into a STOP AUTOPLAY button and the number of AUTOPLAYs remaining will be displayed in the STOP button.


    HOME:

    		 Accesses casino menu


    SETTINGS:

    		 Accesses the SETTING page for SOUND, INFO and AUTOPLAY options

    SETTINGS PAGE BUTTONS:

    SHORTCUT MENU: Touch anywhere on the screen for 3 seconds for the shortcut menu to appear. You can access the AUTOPLAY, INFO options.


    AUTOPLAY:

    Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays. The slider increases or decreases the number of autoplays. The available autoplays ranges from 0 to 100.


    INFO:

    Accesses rules for the game.


    SOUND:

    Switches sound on/off.

    Keyboard Shortcuts:

    Space bar: Pressing the Space bar will initiate Spin

    The game displays all your details in the following display fields:

    Balance : Displays your total balance

    Bet : The total wager staked.

    Win : Displays the amount won in the play

    Malfunction voids all pays and plays
