    SLINGO STARBURST

    HOW TO PLAY

    SLINGO STARBURST IS THE NEW WAY TO PLAY YOUR FAVORITE SLOT GAME.

    WIN INCREASINGLY VALUABLE WIN SPINS ON STARBURST FOR COMPLETING SLINGOS.

    SELECT YOUR STAKE AND PRESS START GAME TO BEGIN

    YOU HAVE 10 SPINS TO MATCH NUMBERS IN THE REEL WITH THE GRID

    COMPLETE SLINGOS TO WIN SPINS ON STARBURST

    STARBURST WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBERS IN THE COLUMN ABOVE.

    THEY THEN HOLD IN PLACE FOR A RESPIN ON THE OTHER REELS

    WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE COLUMN ABOVE

    FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ADD AN ADDITIONAL SPIN

    3 OR MORE PURPLE GEMS IN VIEW AWARD AN INSTANT CASH PRIZE

    5 X PURPLE GEM = 2.5X

    4 X PURPLE GEM = 1X

    3 X PURPLE GEM = 0.5X

    SUPER WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE GRID

    BLOCKER SYMBOLS DO NOTHING

    WIN SPINS

    STARBURST BONUS AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH A GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE STARBURST SYMBOL

    RED BAR SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN ENHANCED WIN UTILISING THE BAR SYMBOL

    RED 7 SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN ENHANCED WIN UTILISING THE 7 SYMBOL

    BAR SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE BAR SYMBOL

    7 SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE 7 SYMBOL

    YELLOW GEM SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE YELLOW GEM SYMBOL

    GREEN GEM SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE GREEN GEM SYMBOL

    ORANGE GEM SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE ORANGE GEM SYMBOL

    BLUE GEM SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN SPIN ON STARBURST WITH AN GUARANTEED WIN UTILISING THE BLUE GEM SYMBOL

    EXTRA SPINS

    EXTRA SPINS ARE AVAILABLE AFTER EACH GAME

    PRICES MAY BE IN EXCESS OF YOUR BASE STAKE

    YOU CAN CONTROL EXTRA SPIN LIMITS IN PLAY CONTROLS

    COLLECT, OR BUY MORE SPINS

    SLINGO WIN LINES

    THERE ARE 12 WIN LINES AND 11 AWARDS ON THE PAYTABLE BECAUSE THE LAST NUMBER ON THE GRID WILL ALWAYS AWARD AT LEAST 2 WINNING LINES.

    PLAY CONTROLS

    PLAY CONTROLS CAN BE SELECTED FROM THE MENU

    SET THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF EXTRA SPINS TO BE OFFERED

    SET THE MAXIMUM PRICE TO BE OFFERED AS AN EXTRA SPIN

    SET THE MAXIMUM STAKE FOR ANY SINGLE GAME

    SET THE MAXIMUM LOSS FOR ANY SINGLE GAME

    THE GAME WILL AUTOMATICALLY END IF ANY OF THE SET LIMITS ARE REACHED

    BEST STRATEGY

    BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF WILDS AND SUPER WILDS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAYER CLOSER TO A SLINGO.

    WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA ARE AVAILABLE THE PREFERENCE IS GIVEN TO THE POSITIONS THAT ARE INCLUDED IN THE MOST SLINGOS (WIN LINES).

    FOR EXAMPLE, THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN A HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES.

    WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA EXISTS AGAIN A RANDOM CHOICE IS MADE.

    GENERAL

    BASED ON BEST STRATEGY THIS GAME HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 96.66%

    BASED ON BEST STRATEGY EACH EXTRA SPIN HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 95.47%

    FREE SPINS AND RE-SPIN WILD SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS IN EXTRA SPINS

    IN EXTRA SPINS SOM POTENTIAL PRIZES MAY REQUIRE SUPER WILDS

    EXTRA SPINS ARE ONLY OFFERED IF A WIN SPIN IS ACHIEVABLE ON THE NEXT SPIN

    EXTRA SPIN PRICES ARE ROUNDED TO THE NEAREST WHOLE VALUE WHICH MAY AFFECT THE RTP

    MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS

    FREE GAMES

    IF A PLAYER IS AWARDED A FREE GAME OF SLINGO STARBURST THEY WILL BE ABLE TO PLAY THE BASE GAME FOR FREE AT A PREDEFINED STAKING VALUE.

    WHEN THE BASE GAME IS COMPLETED, THE WINNINGS ACCRUED BY COMPLETING HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL OR DIAGONAL SLINGOS OR OTHER SPECIAL SYMBOLS BONUSES WILL BE DISPLAYED TO THE PLAYER AND COLLECTED

    FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, THE FREE GAME IS CONSIDERED AS A BONUS CASH STAKE

    PENDING GAMES

    INCOMPLETE GAMES WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY COMPLETED 24 HOURS AFTER LAUNCH

    ANY REMAINING SPINS WILL BE PLAYED OUT WITH A RANDOM NUMBER GENERATOR USED TO MAKE ANY PLAYER DECISIONS

    ANY WINNINGS WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY CREDITED
