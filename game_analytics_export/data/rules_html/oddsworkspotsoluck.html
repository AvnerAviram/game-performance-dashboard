    Pots O' Luck

    POTS 'O LUCK™

    GAME RULES

    Reveal 3 matching Symbols to win a guaranteed prize and trigger the game’s First Bonus Level.

    Reveal 4 matching Symbols on the First Bonus Level’s expanded 4x4 card to upgrade your existing prize and trigger the game’s Final Bonus Level.

    Reveal 5 matching Symbols on the Final Bonus Level’s expanded 5x5 card to upgrade your existing prize one more time.

    Prize payouts are made according to the Paytable at the bottom of the screen.

    Wins are multiplied by the amount staked per scratch card.

    BETTING CONTROLS

    Make your bet selection by pressing the + and – buttons either side of the display on the main screen.

    Press the PLAY button when you are happy with your selection to start the game.

    AUTOPLAY (Not applicable in UK jurisdiction)

    Press the AUTOPLAY button to automatically play through the fixed number of games indicated on the button at the bet level currently selected.

    The option to change the number of games initially shown on the AUTOPLAY button can be found in the settings section of the HELP menu (‘?” button).

    After pressing the AUTOPLAY button it will change to become a STOP button. Clicking this allows you to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time before the fixed number of games has expired.

    DISCONNECTION AND MALFUNCTION

    Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
