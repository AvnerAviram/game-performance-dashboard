    Football Frenzy

    The aim of the game is to try and get a football into one of cups at the bottom of the screen.

    Getting Started
    Once the game has loaded, you're shown a tutorial on how to play. You can control the tutorial with the left and right arrow buttons.

    Hit "Start" to move on, to setting your stake.

    Setting Your Stake
    You can set your stake with the left and right arrow buttons. The higher the stake.

    Hit "Play" once you're ready to play.

    Playing the Game
    You'll now see the bagatelle style play area presented as a football pitch.

    At the top of the screen you'll see a football rotating in a semi circle through 3 segments for direction.

    Once you're happy with the direction, follow the on-screen instructions and click the screen for kick off.

    This fires the ball onto the pitch area in the general direction chosen.

    Free Kick
    Landing in one of the "Free Kick" holes on the pitch, gives you another ball at the top of the screen, which you can user for another kick off. The initial ball remains locked in the hole.

    Once the new ball has landed at the bottom, the locked ball returns to the top also, hence free kick. 3 of these are allowed in one credit.

    Penalty Bonus
    If the football falls into the "Penalty" hole, then you'll get to a different screen featuring a goalkeeper, for a "Penalty Bonus".

    Different areas of the net award you with different values. You're prompted to select an area to shoot. When the ball automatically fires and a goal is scored, you're then awarded the value according to the area of the net you selected. You don't win if the keeper saves the ball.

    Game Recovery
    If a real play game is interrupted (i.e. started but not completed due to connectivity issues or accidentally closing the browser etc), any winnings will be correctly credited to your account and will be exact according to the result, prior to the game being interrupted.

    If the game is started but "Kick Off" has been played, upon resumption you will be presented with the chosen stake in its default in play state.

    If you already released the ball, upon resumption you will be presented with the final outcome of the instant win with result displayed.

    The sequence and values of events leading to the display of the total award, may vary from the animation prior to game recovery. This will not affect the result of the game after the game has recovered.
