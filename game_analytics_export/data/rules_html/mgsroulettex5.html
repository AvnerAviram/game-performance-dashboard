    Roulette x5

     

     

    About Roulette x5

    Roulette x5 is an adaptation of European Roulette, with the same rules and payouts, but with the addition of a dice roll bonus that can multiply your winnings up to x5.

     

    • General
    • Game Payouts
    • How to play
    • Game controls
    • Game Features
    • Game statistics
    • Game settings
    • Return to Player(RTP)

     

    General

    • Roulette x5 plays like a classic European Roulette, but after every winning spin there is a twist - a chance to multiply your winnings with the dice bonus!
    • The total bet is shown at all times and this is what will be debited from your balance for each spin. This game does not use Coins.
    • Win amount is based on the total bet amount and type of bet placed.
    • Total bet is the sum of all bets placed for the spin.
    • Bets can be placed on the classic Roulette table as well as on the Racetrack which allows to place alternative bets.
    • Different types of bets can be combined and different value chips can be used in one spin.
    • After a winning spin the player is presented with the dice roll bonus, which can multiply the total winnings.
    • The payouts for all market types are fixed and the payout amount is based on the total bet.
    • On desktop, the payouts are displayed by hovering over the market type.
    • On mobile, the payouts are displayed in the game menu.
    • The dice roll bonus consists of six dice being rolled. If at least four dice present the same number, the total win for the corresponding spin is multiplied.
    • If the outcome of the spin does not match your prediction and the spin is not winning, the game is over and the player can start a new round.
    • Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

     

    Game Payouts

     

    Player can choose what bet types and amounts to be placed for each spin.

     

                    

    Each bet type has different payout and bet limits, as per below:

     

    1. Straight Up

    A bet on a single number (from 0 to 36).

    Pays: 35/1

     

    2. Split

    A bet on two adjacent numbers.

    Pays: 17/1

     

    3. Street

    A bet on three consecutive numbers located on the same line (row bet; e.g., 1, 2, 3). Pays: 11/1

     

    4. Trio

    A bet on three numbers including zero (0, 1, 2 or 0, 2, 3).

    Pays: 11/1

     

    5. Basket

    A bet on four numbers including zero (0, 1, 2, 3).

    Pays: 8/1

     

    6. Corner

    A bet on four numbers next to each other, the chip is placed in the middle of the numbers (square bet).

    Pays: 8/1

     

    7. Six Line

    A bet on two adjacent lines (street bets) (the 6 different numbers in 2 rows of 3 numbers; e.g., 1, 2, 3 + 4, 5, 6).

    Pays: 5/1

     

    8. Dozen

    A bet on a group of 12 numbers (1st 12 are 1-12, 2nd 12 are 13-24, 3rd 12 are 2536).

    Pays: 2/1

     

    9. Column

    A bet on 12 numbers that are in any given column (bets are located on the right side of the table).

    Pays: 2/1

     

    10. Low or high

    A bet on whether the winning number will be low (1-18) or high (19-36). Pays: 1/1

     

    11. Red or black

    A bet on whether the winning number will be red or black.

    Pays: 1/1

               

    12. Odd or even

    A bet on whether the winning number will be odd or even.

    Pays: 1/1

     

    13. Tiers

    A bet of 6 chips on 12 numbers from Tiers section. One chip on: 5/8, 10/11, 13/16, 23/24, 27/30 and 33/36.

    Pays: The maximum payout is 17/1.

     

    14. Orphelins

    A bet of 5 chips on 8 numbers from Orphelins section. One chip on: 1, 6/9, 14/17, 17/20 and 31/34.

    Pays: The maximum payout is 35/1.

     

    15. Voisins du Zero

    A bet of 9 chips on 17 numbers from Voisins du Zero section. Two chips on: 0/2/3 and 25/26/28/29, and one chip on 4/7, 12/15, 18/21, 19/22, and 32/35. Pays: The maximum payout is 17/1.

     

    16. Neighbours

    A bet of 5 chips on 5 numbers - the selected number and 2 neighbour numbers on each side. (5 straight bets).

    Pays: The maximum payout is 35/1.

     

    How to play

    • The wheel has 37 bays, numbered 0 to 36. The payouts for all the classic bets match classic Roulette odds.
    • The goal is to predict on which number the ball will land. If the outcome matches your prediction, player wins.
    • Select the chips to use for placing bets. Various chips and bet types can be combined for each spin.
    • Bets can be placed on the classic Roulette table as well as on the Racetrack which allows to place alternative bets.
    • On desktop, click and drag, to place multiple chips in one go using our Quick Chips function.
    • On mobile, slide your finger across the chosen numbers to place multiple chips in one go using our Quick Chips function.
    • Multiple chips can be placed on one number, the colours will change to the highest possible colour of the chip denomination.
    • After bet placement Undo, Clear and Spin buttons become active. Confirm the bet by clicking Spin button, or amend the bet.
    • After the Spin button is clicked, the wheel spins and the ball drops into a numbered bay.
    • The result is shown and is entered in the winning numbers box and also highlighted on the table.

                      

    • If the spin is winning, six dice are presented over the wheel display and are rolled. If at least 4 dice match, winnings are multiplied.
    • After a winning spin the player is presented with the dice roll bonus, which can multiply the total winnings.
    • Match any 4 dice to activate 10% bonus.
    • Match any 5 dice to activate 50% bonus.
    • Match any 6 dice to activate x5 bonus.
    • If the spin is not winning, the game is over and the player can start a new round.
    • After the spin has ended the Rebet button becomes active.
    • Previously played game rounds can be seen in Bet History.

     

    Game controls

     

    • Balance box: Displays your Casino balance.

       

    • Total bet: Displays the total amount of chips currently placed on the table.

       

    • Win box: Displays your total win after a winning spin.

       

    • Spin button: Confirms the bet and starts the game.

       

    • Settings button: Allows to adjust in-game settings (audio volume, sound effects, music, turbo mode, big wheel).

       

    • Menu button: Opens paytable, bet history and game rules.

       

    • Undo button: Removes the last chip placed on the table.

       

    • Clear button: Clears all chips placed on the table.

       

    • Rebet button: Repeats the bet placed for the previous spin.

       

    • x2 button: Doubles the bet after using rebet button.

       

    • Chip rack: Allows to pick the chips to be used to place the bet.

       

    • Winning numbers box: Displays last 22 winning numbers on desktop and mobile landscape view. 5 numbers are displayed on mobile portrait view.

       

    • Game statistics: Displays winning numbers statistics for last 22 rounds. Game statistics can be shown or hidden during the gameplay.

       

    • Bet history: Shows the Last 100 results.

       

    • View toggle button: Change the view between Table and Racetrack on mobile.

       

    • Deposit: Opens banking page for a quick deposit.

    Game settings

     

    • Game settings: You can change your game play and sound settings.
    • Desktop:
    • Click Settings.
    • Choose the required settings.
    • Close the window to return to game.

                 

    • Mobile:
    • Tap menu.
    • Choose Settings tab.
    • Choose the required settings.
    • Exit the menu to return to game.

       

    • Available settings:
    • Audio volume: Adjust the volume of in-game audio or turn it off completely.
    • Sound effects: Adjust the volume of in-game sound effects or turn them off completely.
    • Music: Adjust the volume of in-game music or turn it off completely.
    • Turbo: Adjust the speed. This does not change the result of the spins.
    • Big Wheel: Switch to bigger view of the wheel during the spin. This does not affect the gameplay and results.

       

    • Some settings (Turbo) may not be available based on casino and/or country.
