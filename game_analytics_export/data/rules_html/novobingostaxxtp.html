    GAME DESIGN

    Main Game

    Your aim in Thunder Power is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 3125 winning paths running from left to right. During base game spins when Coin symbols land on the reels they are placed in the respective position on the BINGO STAXX board. Coin symbols landing on respective Diamond, Crown or Star positions are paid immediately to the player.

    BINGO STAXX Feature

    5 Coins in a vertical, horizontal, or diagonal line award the BINGO STAXX Feature. Coin prize values accumulate until a BINGO STAXX line is formed. A completed BINGO STAXX line on the Bingo board will award the player the prizes on that line and the board will reset.

    Completing a BINGO STAXX line with the Diamond symbol included awards a wheel spin. Possible prizes include Grand Jackpot, Maxi Jackpot or Mini Jackpot or up to 35x bet.

    Completing a BINGO STAXX line with the Crown symbol included awards a wheel spin. Possible prizes include Maxi Jackpot, Mini Jackpot or up to 40x bet.

    Completing a BINGO STAXX line with the Star symbol included awards a wheel spin. Possible prizes include Mini Jackpot or up to 20x bet.

    Possible Coin prizes on the reels are up to 16x total bet.

    Rules

    All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right.

    Wild substitutes for all symbols except Coins, Temple and Arrow. Scatter wins and allpay wins are added.

    Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
