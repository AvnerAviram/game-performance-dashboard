Bars and Bells

Game Rules

  • Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play
  • Pays left to right on consecutive reels
  • The Won field displays winnings in credits.
  • All 20 lines always played
  • In the flash version space bar can be used to spin
  • Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet
  • Select BET of 20, 40, 60, 80, or 100 Credits per line
  • In the mobile version you set the Bet and Credits Per Line from the game settings menu by pushing the game settings button in the bottom right corner.
  • Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current Credits Per Line selected
  • Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits Per Line staked
  • Only the highest win on a pay line pays
  • Wins on different paylines are added together.
  • Wild BELL substitutes for any symbol except BONUS
  • Higher credit denominations:
  • 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000

Streak Breaker FEATURE

  • Flash only
    • Select "ON" top right corner to activate Streak Breaker feature.
  • Mobile only
    • Select the game settings button in the bottom right corner and tap the feature on button to activate Streak Breaker feature.
  • Cost is extra 5 credits per spin to play Streak Breaker feature
  • Feature pays random amount for 5 consecutive zero-win spins in a row.
  • Streak Breaker is not active during FEATURE

Shake 'n' Pays FEATURE

  • The Shake 'n' Pays bonus is not based on any combination of reel icons, but instead is awarded at random. The range of possible awards is between 20 and 25000 credits.
  • There are 4 levels of payouts. Each level has a range of payouts associated with it.
  • Level 1: 20 - 25 credits x number of credits bet per line.
  • Level 2: 50 - 100 credits x number of credits bet per line.
  • Level 3: 200 - 500 credits x number of credits bet per line.
  • Level 4: 750 - 5000 credits x number of credits bet per line.
  • For example, if you bet 5 credits per line and won a Level 4 Shake 'n' Pays bonus, you'd win between 3750 and 25 000 credits (i.e., between 750 * 5 and 5000 * 5). It is possible to win a Shake 'n' Pays bonus for more than one level at the same time, so you may see a bonus win of more than the maximum shown here.
  • Shake 'n' Pays bonuses are not awarded during free spins.

BONUS

  • Bars & Bells symbols located and appearing anywhere on 2nd, 3rd, and 4th reels triggers BONUS
  • Bonus anywhere on reels 2, 3, 4 triggers bonus feature
  • BONUS award is free spins
  • To determine number of free spins and win multiplier, pick icons in BONUS game until you win 3 same
  • 20, 15, 10, 7, 5 Free Spins can be won
  • If 20 Free Spins are won, all wins are multiplied by 3
  • If 15, 10, 7, 5 Free Spins are won, all wins are multiplied by 2
  • Bonus can be re-triggered multiple times
  • Bonus re-triggers automatically win the same spins and multiplier as the initial Bonus and are immediately added to the Free Spin tally on the info bar.
  • Free spin win amounts are based on the same Credits Per Line bet as the triggering spin amount
  • Feature can be awarded during Bonus

Autospin (Flash only)

  • Click the AUTO button and select the number from the pop-up menu, then click START
  • Click on STOP to cancel Autospin.
  • Autospin is deactivated after a bonus or feature is triggered, it must be reactivated.
