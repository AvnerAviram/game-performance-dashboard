    Triple 0 Roulette

    Game Objective :

    • The objective of the game is to accurately guess the outcome of the roulette spin (whether the right number or the right group).
    • Triple Zero Roulette is played with a wheel containing 39 numbered slots (1 to 36, plus a 0, 00 and a 000), a ball and a layout on which the player may place a bet (or bets) covering either a single number or groups of numbers.
    • After the player places the desired bets, the ball is spun around the wheel and will come to rest in one of the numbered slots, indicating the winning number/ group.

    How to Play :

    1. Select the amount you want to wager by tapping on the chip of that denomination. The selected chip will be raised.
    2. All possible bet types are defined in Paytable section.
    3. You may place as many bets as you like, depending on the type of bets or the maximum table limit.
    4. To remove a chip from the table, tap on UNDO or CLEAR buttons.
    5. You can get information about your available gaming balance, total stake, and total win at the bottom of the game screen.
    6. Once you have finished placing your bets, spin the wheel by tapping on the ‘SPIN’ button.
    7. When the ball comes to rest, the winning number is highlighted on the wheel.
    8. When you hit the SPIN or REBET/SPIN, the ball will spin on the wheel. The win amount is calculated as per the Paytable given, and your winnings are shown in a Win meter which comes up when there is a win. This win amount is added to the game balance.
    9. After the ball stops, the number will be displayed over the spinning wheel.
    10. The last 12 winning numbers are shown in the top part of the screen, with the most recent outcomes to the left.
    11. After the game finishes, the default chip for the new game remains the same as your previous bet. If you want to place a new bet, tap on the desired betting spots and place the bet.
    12. The game can be played without bets placed on the table.
    13. To repeat the same bet, tap the REBET button.
    14. To repeat the same bet and spin the wheel, tap the REBET/SPIN button.
    15. Tap on the Paytable button from the game menu on the bottom left corner of the game screen to view the game rules.
    16. There is the ability to enable/disable ‘drag to bet’ feature in the game through Settings button from the game menu.
    17. Turbo-mode: There is the ability to turn off/on animation in the game through Settings button from the game menu.

    Neighbors Table :

    • The Neighbors Table view can be accessed by clicking NEIGHBORS button shown on the right side of the game view.
    • There are 5 numbered NEIGHBORS buttons that can be selected and then click on a number on the table to bet on that number and the numbers Neighboring it to the left and the right.

    Examples :

    1. Clicking the NEIGHBORS button labelled 1 and then clicking on the number 2 on the table will place bets on number 2 and the numbers to the immediate left and right of 2 (21 on the left and 25 on the right).
    2. Clicking the NEIGHBORS button labelled 4 and then clicking on the number 2 on the table will place bets on the number 2 and 4 numbers that are to the immediate left and 4 numbers that are to the immediate right of 2 (15, 9, 4, 21 on the left and 25, 17, 34, 6 on the right).
    3. Once you have placed your bet, click SPIN to start the game.

    The Roulette Wheel :

    • Clicking SPIN after making a bet will spin the ball in the Roulette wheel.
    • The wheel is spun in one direction and a ball is spun on the wheel in the opposite direction and then the ball will land in one of the slots.
    • If the ball lands in a slot you bet on, you will win a payout according to your bet. This win amount is added to the game balance.
    • The slot the ball landed in will be shown in the top-left.

    Browser Panel Commands :

    1. Click on RULES to view the detailed rules of the game as a web page.
    2. Click on HISTORY to view the players game history.
    3. The HISTORY functionality is applicable only for the real-money game play.
    4. Click on REAL MONEY/ DEMO PLAY to switch between REAL-MONEY or DEMO-MONEY mode.
    5. Click on the DEPOSIT button to deposit money into the player account.

     Statistics Window :

    • Game Statistics enable you to view your previous results.
    • The statistics window displays the last 12 results.
    • The statistics window indicates red results with numbers in red square, black results with numbers in black square and result of 0,00 and 000 in green square.
    • Game Statistics are device specific.

    Return to the player:

    • For the bet types 'Straight', 'Split', 'Street', 'Corner bet or Square bet or Quarter bet', 'Line', 'Dozen', 'Column’ RTP is 92.30%.
    • For the bet types 'Top Line bet' RTP is 89.74%.
    • For the bet types 'Red', 'Black', 'Even', 'Odd', '1 to 18', '19 to 36', RTP is 92.30%.
    • MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS.

    Game History :

    Detailed gameplay history can be accessed by logging into your account on the desktop website and choosing menu 'History'.

    Adding Funds to your Account :

    To add funds to your account, click on Deposit in My Account then select the desired deposit option. The time taken to transfer funds and the fees charged will vary depending on which deposit option you choose. Funds can only be added at the beginning of a new game.

    What do I do if I reach my betting limits?

    To add funds to your account, click on the “DEPOSIT” button in the browser common panel, then select the desired deposit option.

    I could not complete the game. What should I do?

    If your game is disconnected during game play, the new game will be restored not the current game when you connect back again, and you can check the current game details in “History”.

    If you are still having problems, please contact our 24/7 Customer Care team.
