    BetMGM Lucky Bonus Draw Keno

    BETMGM LUCKY BONUS DRAW KENO

    Introduction

    • BETMGM LUCKY BONUS DRAW KENO is a Keno game with 80 numbers to choose from and 20 numbers randomly drawn at every round. You may select between 2 to 10 numbers. Of the 20 numbers drawn, the last number drawn is the number representing the Golden Coin that triggers the bonus draw with a X5 multiplier.

    How to Bet

    • Choose between 2 to 10 numbers in the 80 numbers grid. Press on a desired number on the grid to select it. The color of the number on the grid will change to a different color to show that the number has been selected. To unselect a number, press on a selected number. You may also press and drag to select multiple numbers.
    • You may use the stake selection menu to change your stake.
    • Once 2 or more numbers have been selected, press on the Play button to start the game. The game will randomly draw 20 numbers out of the 80 numbers.
    • When the drawn number lands on a number on the grid that has not been selected, the number on the grid will change to a distinct color. If the drawn number lands on a selected number, the number will change to a different color than previously.

    Lucky Bonus Draw Number

    • At the end of the regular 20 numbers draw, if your selected numbers match sufficient numbers, it will register as a win and you will receive a payment according to the paytable.
    • The last drawn number (the 20th ball) is always the Golden Coin number. If the Golden Coin number lands on one of your selected numbers, it will automatically trigger a bonus event which will see the game draw an additional 5 random numbers giving you a chance to match additional numbers from your number pick selection. All winnings from the bonus draw are paid X5.

    How to Win

    • Winnings are paid according to the paytable and depends on how many numbers you have selected and how many numbers matched. Winnings payout is indicated in the paytable and will adjust accordingly based on the numbers selection and the stake amount.

    Terms and Return to Player

    • In the event of a game in real play being interrupted, (i.e. started but not completed due to connectivity issues or accidentally closing the browser, etc.), depending on the previous state of the game, it will either resume from the point of the last draw or display the outcome of the game already in play. Any winnings will be correctly credited to the player account and will be exact according to the result prior to the game being interrupted.
    • In the event of a non-responsive game or problem during a bonus round, the game will repeat the incomplete game and display the result to the player when the game is next launched.
    • In the event of equipment failure or planned/unplanned terminations, progressive jackpot parameters, (including jackpot values and incremental percentages), are fully restored with identical parameters, after restart.
    • The odds of getting a particular outcome are always the same at the start of every game. The odds do not vary based on prior outcomes, bet size, balance size, numbers picked, time of day, day of the week, etc. For example, if the top award is won on a given play of the game, the chances of winning the top award on the next play are exactly the same. Likewise, prior losses do not change future odds.
    • Malfunction Voids all pays and plays
