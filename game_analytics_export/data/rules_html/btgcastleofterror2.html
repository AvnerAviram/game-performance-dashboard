Details

Descend once more into darkness with Castle of Terror 2, the ultimate horror slot that delivers electrifying thrills at every spin! This bone-chilling adventure is packed with monstrous features as Bonus Respins awaken the reels! Chaos erupts when you land 2 Scatters for 10 Free Spins with 2 bonus effects or amplify the terror with 3 bonus effects when you land 3 Scatters! Every spin cranks up the tension as multipliers grow and features collide in a symphony of suspense. Feeling fearless? Bonus Buy lets you seize control, enter the lab and unleash the horrors within! Castle of Terror 2 is waiting… if you dare!

BONUS RESPIN

One Scatter sparks a Bonus Respin, sending a surge of monstrous energy through the reels! The nature of the experiment depends on where the Scatter appears:

Brains - Extra Brains added to all reels.

Alive - A random Win Multiplier is applied to all wins.

Shock - Wilds will reveal random multipliers.

Monster - A random symbol becomes Wild on reels 2 to 5.

Horror - A random reel will be filled with Wilds.

If one or more Scatters appear during the Bonus Respin, the lab erupts in chaos, triggering 10 Free Spins for a spooktacular time!

FREE SPINS

One Scatter surges with unholy energy, triggering a Bonus Respin. But when two or three Scatters strike, the experiment escalates unleashing 10 Free Spins! The Win Multiplier begins at 1x but mutates after every winning spin, amplifying your rewards with each electrifying jolt! Your Free Spins will be infused with two or three Bonus effects, depending on which reels the triggering Scatters landed on. If 2 or more Scatters appear during Free Spins, the machine surges again - granting 4 extra Free Spins plus 2 more for each additional Scatter beyond the second!

BONUS BUY

Dare to play Doctor and take fate into your own hands! Tap the Bonus Buy icon to ignite the laboratory and awaken monstrous wins instantly! For 100x your stake, spark 10 Free Spins infused with 2 volatile Bonuses! For 300x your stake, unleash 10 Enhanced Free Spins, electrified with 3 powerful Bonuses! Step into the lab, pull the lever, and let the madness begin…
