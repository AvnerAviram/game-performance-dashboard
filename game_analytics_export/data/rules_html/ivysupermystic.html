    Super Mystic

    Game Objective:

    Super Mystic is a five-reel, multi-payline 'Gold Jackpot' slot game. You win based on the combination of the various symbols displayed on the selected winning payline(s). The 'paytable' in game shows how much you will win for each combination. Free spin feature with 10 free spins will be triggered when at least 3 sandstorm symbols appear on the screen. Free Spins can re-trigger within free spins. For each bet placed, the house adds a percentage of the bet amount to all the four running Gold Jackpot levels.

    Game Information:

    • Game is played with 9 variable lines, which are displayed on either sides of the game screen.
    • LINES can be changed by using the  and buttons.
    • BET amount can be changed by using the  and  buttons.
    • SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the current selected BET and LINES.
    • Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE.
    • Pay line wins are multiplied by the amount: ‘bet per line’ and gets added to other payline wins.
    • Highest win amount is paid on each selected pay line.
    • Coinciding wins on different bet lines are all added to your total.
    • Scatter wins (represented by Sandstorm symbol) are multiplied by total bet and added to pay line wins.
    • All symbols pay from left to right, except the scatter symbol (represented by Sandstorm) which pays anywhere in view.
    • On the winning line the winning symbols will slightly animate with bounce in animation and the win line will be colored and remaining will be grayed out.
    • The Ticker messages will appear on top of Game screen in Pink color.
    • Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will increase, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN under the bottom of the game.

    Gold Jackpot:

    The Gold Jackpot consists of all four jackpots at different levels. The levels start with Gold Blitz Jackpot and move up through Gold Quick Jackpot and Gold Super Jackpot to the highest, Gold Mega Jackpot. All four jackpots grow progressively as bets are made. For each spin on any of the Gold Jackpot games, the house adds a small credit to the four running jackpots. Each Gold Jackpot level can be won while betting any amount, irrespective of hitting any paying or non-paying combination.

    How to play:

    • Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet per payline is set for all 9 lines on the slot machine. You can increase the bet per line by clicking the  button to the right of 'BET', or you can decrease the bet by clicking the  to the left. Bet and lines will not get adjusted to the next possible bet, if your balance drops below total bet placed, then click on  button to deposit.
    • Setting 'LINES': Similarly, nine lines are selected by default. You can decrease the number of lines by clicking the  button to the left of 'LINES', or select more lines by clicking the  button to the right. When the number of lines is increased, all the lines number and the line path will be colorized to represent the selected paylines. These lines and their path are decolorized as the number of paylines is decreased.
    • MAX BET: To play with MAX BET, click on . If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled.
    • The number of selected lines are multiplied by the 'bet per line'. The total bet amount is displayed beside the 'BET' label, which can be found at the bottom of the game screen.
    • Paytable: The paytable can be viewed either by clicking the  button located in bottom of game screen beside ‘BET’ label or by clicking  button located at the bottom of the screen beside the Line meter. The prizes displayed in the paytable are per unit bet, and are shown as multipliers. To get the effective payout per line, multiply the respective prize with the bet per line. You can go back to the game by clicking on the button on the paytable screen. Clicking on the  button will open the rules screen along with Paytable and allow you to read the game rules.
    • Spin: Click on SPIN  icon to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations on the selected lines and any scatter wins will be paid according to the paytable.
    • Result: Wins are indicated by colorized paylines animated symbols, and in case of a scatter win, by animated symbols, with the total win amount being shown at the bottom of the screen beside 'WIN'.
    • Volume:  icon will play the game music. Click on  icon will mute the volume .
    • CREDITS: Your remaining game balance, after you have placed the desired bet, is shown in ‘BALANCE’ meter i.e. the current game balance minus the total bet placed.
    • 'AUTOSPIN': You can use this feature to choose all or any of the various options for 'hands free' game play. The available options under this feature are:
      • Number of spins: The game will spin for the number of rotations selected from the drop-down menu. You can choose any number of games and play until your balance is more than the total bet placed.
      • Spin till Win equals or exceeds: The game will spin until the win amount is equal to or exceeds the amount selected from the drop down menu.
        • Spin till Loss not exceeds: The game will automatically spin until the loss limit is reached. Loss will be calculated with (Loss = Total bets - Total wins)
          • Total bets=accumulated total bets
          • Total wins=accumulated total wins
          • (((Total bets- Total wins) + current game bet) <= Loss limit target) then next auto spin will be allowed.
      • Spin till feature trigger: The game will spin until the next feature is triggered.
      • Autospin game is terminated whenever a Gold Jackpot is triggered in-between the Autospin.

    • To make use of the autospin feature, click on the 'AUTOSPIN' button in the bottom right of the game screen. When the autospin window opens, check the boxes next to the option(s) you'd like to play with. Select from the drop-down menu where applicable and click ‘Ok’ button. Click on 'START' to activate the selected options and start the spins. Similarly, click on 'CANCEL' to skip the 'AUTOSPIN' feature and return to the game screen.
    • When playing with multiple options and conditions selected, the condition that is satisfied first will take precedence over the others, and the remaining selected conditions will be disabled. You can then choose to either select the same set of options again or opt for new option(s) and proceed with the game.
    • 'AUTOSPIN' option is not available in the play-money mode.
    • Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. To change your bet, increase the coin value.­
    • Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen.

    Game Rules:

    • Play 1 to 9 lines.
    • All symbols pay left to right, except scattered Sandstorm symbol, which pay in either direction.
    • Only the highest win on each selected payline is paid.
    • Coinciding wins on different lit lines are added together.
    • Line wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line.
    • Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet.
    • Scatter wins are added to payline wins.
    • All wins are paid on selected paylines only, except scatter symbols, which pay anywhere.
    • The City symbol substitutes for all other symbols except the scattered Sandstorm.
    • All jackpot wins are credited to your account balance immediately.
    • Malfunctions Voids all Pays and Plays.
    • The game and game rules are identical both in real-money and play-money modes except that play-money mode does not contribute to the jackpot amount.

    Feature game play:

    • The bonus feature is triggered when three or more Sandstorm symbols appear anywhere on reels.
    • You are then awarded 10 free games, during which all prizes are tripled.
    • The feature can be re-triggered.
    • Free games are played using the number of line and the bet amount you selected in the trigger game.
    • Free game wins are added to payline and scatter wins.
    • Wins are transferred to the credit meter at the end of the base game and at the end of each stage in the feature.

    • Bonus round features and free game features cannot win the jackpot amount.

    FAQ’s:

    1. I could not complete the game. What should I do?
      • If you get disconnected in the middle of the base game, the software will automatically complete the game for you. You will be able to find out the result by tapping on the 'Game Logs' button once you have logged back in.
      • If you are disconnected after triggering free spin or in the middle of free spin, on reconnection, the software will automatically start the free spin round, if not already started, or resume the game where it was disconnected. After completing the feature, you can continue game play, or you can select another game of your choice.
    2. What should I do if the balance is low to place bet?
      • Click on ‘Deposit’  button at bottom of the game screen and deposit.
