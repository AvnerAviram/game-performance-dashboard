    Final Score World Cup

    Final Score World Cup is a fast-paced football results based game. Players should select two teams and can bet on a variety of outcomes concerning how many goals are scored between the two teams selected by the player or what the result will be. Players can also bet on special events in a match which are penalty missed, penalty scored, injury time goal and red card. The game also has a bonus feature, which is a penalty, and is available whenever a player has a winning bet resulting in winnings that are not over half of the max win amount

    The game is designed in such a way that different teams have different skills and this will affect the odds and results for a game between two teams. The new odds for bets are highlighted when the player changes one of the teams so that the player can see the new odds.

    top

    Information

    • The betting limits are shown on the game screen title bar.
    • The total bet placed in a game should be less than the max bet and the individual bets are restricted so that the winnings are less than maximum payout.

    top

    How to play

    • Selecting teams: players will be able to select the two teams from drop down lists available towards the top left of the game window. Clicking on the option 'Random Fixture' will automatically select two teams. The country that is selected on the left side denotes the Home Team and the country on the right side denotes Away Team.
    • Setting 'BETS': select a coin value and then click on the bet you would like to place. The stake on that bet is then increased by the selected coin amount. There are different coin values available. If your game balance is less than your bet, you need to buy-in amount to continue game play. The amount bet with each chip is the coin value in the currency the player has selected.
    • The player can bet on a variety of outcomes concerning how many goals are scored in a game of football or what the result will be.
    • The list of available bets are as follows:
      Match Betting: Any Home Win, Any Draw Win, Any Away Win
      Correct Score Betting:
         Home Team to win, 1-0, 2-0, 3-0, 4-0, 2-1, 3-1, 3-2, other home win.
         Draw: 0-0, 1-1, 2-2, 3-3, other draw

    I could not complete the game. What should I do?

    If you get disconnected during the main game there will be an incomplete game on reconnection, if there is a win (that is not over half of the maximum winnings limit) Bonus feature will be available for selection.

    If you get disconnected when the main game is completed and there is no win, the game is over. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the 'Game Logs' button once you have logged back in

    If you are disconnected after Bonus is made available or in the middle of the Bonus feature, on reconnection the software will automatically take you to the Bonus round. You can complete the bonus round.

    During AutoDraw if you get disconnected and there is win (that is not over half of the maximum winnings limit) in the current Auto Draw game on reconnection, Bonus feature will be available for selection and the Auto Draw setting will be reset.

    During AutoDraw if you get disconnected and there is no win in the current Auto Draw game on reconnection, the game is over and the Auto Draw setting will be reset. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the 'Game Logs' button once you have logged back in.
