    Getting Started:

    Bonus Roulette is a roulette game with 37 potential outcomes for each spin of the wheel. Several different bet types are available.

    (Back to top)

    How to play:

    The Total stake is displayed at all times.

    Set your bet

    • Place chips on the table by selecting the position on the table that represents the bet you wish to place.
    • Bets can be placed on numerous positions on the table. For example, to place a split bet, select the position in-between two numbers on the table.
    • Pressing Repeat Bet will repeat the chip placement from the previous game.
    • Pressing Clear All Bets or Clear Last Bet will remove any bets placed in error.

    Start the game

    • Select 'BET' to start your game.
    • The SPACE BAR key on the keyboard can often be used as an alternative for pressing the primary on screen button.
    (Back to top)

    How do you win?

    • A win is awarded if the ball lands on a number on the wheel which corresponds to any bets placed on the table.
    • Once the ball has landed, any winnings are paid and the chips are cleared.
    • The previous results of the roulette wheel spin are displayed above the betting table.
    • Awards are detailed in the pay table.

    Bets and Payouts

    • Your winnings will be credited to your account immediately.
    • You will lose your bet amount if you lose the game.

    Gamble Phase

    If a win is awarded on the roulette spin, then the game will transition to the gamble phase.

    In the Gamble phase, there is a wheel with the following slices populating the wheel:

    • Lose - All winnings are lost.
    • Half - All winnings are halved.
    • 1x - All winnings are unchanged.
    • 2x - All winnings are doubled.
    • 3x - All winnings are tripled.

    Pressing Bet will spin the wheel.

    Pressing Collect will collect the current winnings and return to the base game.

    The gamble phase ends when winnings reach 0 or collect is pressed.
