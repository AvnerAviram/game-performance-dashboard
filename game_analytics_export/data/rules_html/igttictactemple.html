    Tic Tac Temple

    The Temple Gods are feeling generous in Tic-Tac Temple! Win a prize when you line up three matching symbols in any row, column or diagonal. Watch for the special Sun symbol: when it appears it not only substitutes for any symbol, increasing the chances of even more wins, it also activates three Bonus turns!

    How to Bet

    Ticket Cost

    Displays the current ticket cost.

    Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

    Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

    Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

    How to Play

    Press the PLAY button to start the game. Reveal 3 matching symbols in any row, column or diagonal to win.

    The Sun symbol substitutes for any symbol and awards 3 bonus turns for more chances to win!

    The Sun symbol can appear on any grid position on the first turn only.

    Bonus turns cannot be re-triggered.

    Rules

    Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

    Wins display as currency.

    Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

    The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
