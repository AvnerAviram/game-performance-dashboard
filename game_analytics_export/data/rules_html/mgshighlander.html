    Highlander

    Highlander™ is a five reel, 243 ways and 600 coin slot machine, including: 

    • Regular symbols
    • Wild symbol
    • Scatter symbol
    • 2 Scatter feature
    • Free Spins bonus game
    • Paytable Achievements feature 

    Game Rules

    General

     

    • All 243 pay ways are permanently enabled. 
    • Your bet is dependent on the number of coins and coin size played.
    • A bet multiplier of 40 applies per spin. For example, 5 coins bet per spin x 40 bet multiplier = 200 total coins bet.
    • To convert your bet amount in coins to credits, multiply the total number of coins bet by the coin size bet. For example, 200 total coins bet x 0.01 coin size = 2 total credits bet.
    • All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination.
    • All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins.
    • Multiple regular winning combinations are paid per pay way. 
    • If there is more than one regular winning combination for different symbols on a pay way, you are paid out for each combination.
    • If there is more than one regular winning combination for the same symbol on a pay way, you are paid out the value of the highest win.
    • Only one scatter winning combination is paid out per spin and you are paid out the value of the highest win.
    • Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins.
    • If you have a winning combination, the win is highlighted on the reels. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again.

     

    Regular symbols

     

    • ach other on an enabled pay way.
    • Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, starting from the first slot reel.
    • Regular payouts depend on the number of coins you bet per spin.  

    Wild symbol

     

    • This symbol does not substitute for the scatter symbol.
    • This symbol also creates its own combination.

     

     

    Scatter symbol

     

    • This symbol does not need to appear on an enabled pay way to win.
    • This symbol cannot be substituted by the wild symbol.
    • This symbol does not appear during the free spins.
    • Scatter payouts depend on your total bet.

     

    Scatter feature

     

    • This feature activates when only two scatter symbols appear on the reels during the regular game.
    • The two scatter symbols are transformed into extra wild symbols.
    • This symbol substitutes for the wild symbol. 
    • This symbol does not substitute for the scatter symbol.
    • This symbol does not create its own win.
    • This feature is not available during the free spins.

                 

    Free Spins bonus game

     

    • The Quickening Wilds™ feature is randomly activated on any spin.
    • Lightning strikes to transform a random symbol into a wild symbol. 
    • On the first lightning strike one symbol transforms. 
    • On each subsequent lightning strike the number of symbols transformed increases by 1.
    • The transformed symbols are only held for one spin. 
    • The last bet you placed in the game is used as the free spins bet amount.
    • Free spins cannot be re-activated.
    • Winnings are automatically collected during the bonus game. 

     

    Paytable Achievements feature

     

    • Tracks your win status by highlighting symbol combinations you have won.
    • When you win all combinations for the same symbol it is highlighted.
    • Achievements are permanently highlighted on the payout table, even after you exit the game.
    • Regular game wins are logged as achievements.
    • Wild symbol substitute wins are not logged as achievements.
    • Free spins wins are not logged as achievements.
