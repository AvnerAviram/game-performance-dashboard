    25P ROULETTE

    Game Objective:

    The objective of the game is to accurately guess the outcome of the Roulette spin (whether the right number or the right group). Roulette is played using a wheel with 37 numbered slots (1-36, plus a 0), a ball and a layout upon which the player may place bets covering a single number or groups of numbers. After the player places the desired bets, the ball is spun around the wheel and will come to rest in one of the numbered slots, indicating the winning number/ group.

    How to play:

    • Place chips on the table by selecting the position on the table that represent the bet you wish to place.
    • Chips are worth 0.25. Based on selecting the same bet over and over again, chips increments are as follows: 0.25 chips are placed until bet value reaches 1. The Chip value will increase by 1 until the bet value reaches 15, at which point the chip value will increase in increments of 5 until the bet value reaches 50. The chip value will increase by 10 until the bet value reaches 200, at which point the chip value will be worth 50 until the bet value reaches 1,000. The chip value will then then increase in increments of 100 for any more bets.
    • All possible bet types are defined in paytable section.
    • You may place as many bets as you like, depending on the type of bets or the maximum table limit.
    • To remove a chip from the table, click on the CLEAR ALL BETS or CLEAR LAST BET buttons.
    • Information on your available game balance and total amount bet is shown.
    • Once you have finished placing your bets, spin the wheel by clicking on the ‘BET’ button.
    • When the ball comes to rest, the winning number is highlighted on the wheel.
    • When you hit BET, the ball will spin on the wheel. The win amount is calculated as per the paytable given and your winnings are shown in a win metre which comes up when there is a win. This win amount is added to the game balance.
    • After the ball stops, the number will be displayed over the spinning wheel.
    • The last 10 winning numbers are shown in the top part of the screen in desktop and mobile landscape and last 5 winning numbers in left part of the screen in mobile portrait, with the most recent outcomes to the right in desktop and mobile landscape and bottom in mobile portrait.
    • To repeat the same bet, click on the REPEAT BET button.
    • To repeat the same bet and spin the wheel, click on the REPEAT BET & click on BET button.
    • Neighbours: There is the possibility to place continuous bets by clicking on ‘NEIGHBORS table', which is available at the bottom of the screen.
    • Click on the HELP button below the game screen to view the game rules.
    • To switch on or off sound in the game, click on the Sound button that is located at the right top corner of the screen in the game menu.
    • There is the ability to enable/disable ‘drag to bet’ feature in the game through MENU button below the game screen.
    • Turbo-mode: There is the ability to turn off/on animation in the game through MENU button below the game screen and for UK players this option will be in disable mode.

    Bet Limits:

    There is a maximum bet limit for each type of bet, as well as an overall table maximum limit.

    Table minimum limit - the total amounts of bets placed before each spin must be more than or equal to 0.25Pence/0.25Cents. 

    Table maximum limit - the total amount of bets placed before each spin must be less than or equal to the maximum bet requirements posted on the table.

    Neighbours Table:

    • The neighbours table view can be accessed by clicking on the NEIGHBOURS table which is shown in left corner below the main game table.
    • Neighbour bet places the bet on 2 numbers either side of a  selected number. The numbers on the neighbours table are laid out in the same order as on Roulette wheel.

    Example:  If a bet is placed on 0, individual bets worth 0.25 will be placed on 0, 3, 15, 26 and 32.

    The Roulette Wheel:

    • Clicking on SPIN after making a bet will spin the ball in the Roulette wheel.
    • The wheel is spun in one direction i.e. clockwise and a ball is spun on the wheel in the opposite direction. The ball will land in one of the slots.
    • If the ball lands in a slot you bet on, you will win a payout according to your bet. This win amount is added to the game balance.

    Browser Button Panel Commands:

    • Click on Game Logs to view the logs of the games you've played.
    • Click on Responsible Gaming to open Responsible Gaming page.
    • Click on Real/Play to switch to real- or play-money mode.
    • Click on Deposit button to deposit money into your account.

    Statistics window:

    • Game statistics enable you to view your previous results.
    • The statistics window displays the last 10 results in desktop and landscape modes and last 5 results in portrait mode.
    • The statistics window indicates red results with numbers in red, black results with numbers in black and result of 0 in green. 
    • The last winning number will be shown in black box with an arrow shown in “Last Number”.
