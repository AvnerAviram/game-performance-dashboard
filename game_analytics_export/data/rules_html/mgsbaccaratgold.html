    Baccarat Gold

    About Baccarat Gold

    Baccarat Gold is played with eight standard decks of 52 playing cards that are shuffled when a portion of the deck is dealt.

    This game:

    • Has the option to place up to nine Banker bets or Player bets per hand.
    • Pays winning Banker bets less a 5% commission on the win amount.
    • Has high odds.
    • Has seven types of bets:
      1. Banker bet
      2. Player bet
      3. Tie bet
      4. Banker and Tie bet
      5. Player and Tie bet
      6. Banker Pair bet
      7. Player Pair bet 
    • Has optional side bets that make you eligible for bonus payouts.  
    • Enables you to squeeze and rotate cards.

    Game Rules

    Click the links for more information about the game rules.

    General Rules

    1. You can place up to nine Banker bets or Player bets per hand. Each bet is resolved independently, against the same hand.
    2. The player's first card is always dealt before the banker's first card.
    3. King's, Queen's, Jack's and Ten's have a zero point count. 
    4. If both the Banker’s hand and your hand have the same value, the hands tie.
    5. The dealer burns up to three cards after each shuffle and cut.
    6. Malfunctions void all plays and pays 

    Betting and Payout Rules

    1. You can place a dual bet on a Tie bet and a Banker bet or a Player bet. Each bet is resolved independently.
    2. If you placed a Tie bet and win, you are paid out at odds of 8:1 on your regular bet amount.
    3. If the hands tie and you did not place a Tie bet, your bet is returned to you.
    4. Banker bets are paid less a 5% commission.
    5. The 5% commission is only charged on winning bets.
    6. Regular wins are added to bonus wins.

    Third Card Rules 

    1. If the player stands and the banker's hand has a value of 5 or less, the banker receives a third card.
    2. If the banker or player's hand has a value of 8 or 9, both hands automatically stand. This rule overrides all others.
    3. If the player's hand has a value of 5 or less, the player receives a third card.
    4. If the player receives a third card, use the chart below to determine if the banker receives a third card:

    • Squeeze and Rotate Rules

    1. When playing a face-down game, you can peek at particular cards. You can also rotate cards to change their orientation on the table. 

    • You squeeze a card by placing your mouse pointer over the edge of the card and dragging the card back.
    • You rotate a card by placing your mouse pointer near the edge of the card and clicking the highlighted arrows. 
    1. If the card is dragged more than half way back, it automatically flips the card over.
    2. The hand you bet on determines the cards you can peek at: 
    • If you place a Banker bet, you can peek at the Banker's hand.
    • If you place a Player bet, you can peek at the player's hand.
    • If you place a Tie bet, Banker Pair bet or a Player Pair bet, peeking is not available.
    • If you place a dual bet on a Banker bet and Tie bet, you can peek at the banker's hand.
    • If you place a dual bet on a Player bet and Tie bet, you can peek at the player's hand.
    • If you place a dual bet, you can peek at the banker or player's hand.
    • If a third card is dealt to the hand you bet on, you can peek at it. 

    Bonus Rules

    • This game has two side bets that qualify you for bonus payouts:
      • Banker Pair bet
      • Player Pair bet 
    • You can place a dual bet on a Banker Pair bet and a Player Pair bet. Each bet is resolved independently.
    • You must place a side bet to qualify for bonus payouts.
    • The side bets are not compulsory.
    • The side bet is placed before any hands are dealt.

    Playing the game

    The objective of the game is to predict if the banker or player's hand will be closer to the value of 9, or if the hands will tie.

    In this game, you can place up to nine Banker bets or Player bets per hand.

    To play:

    1. Click a chip in the Chip Selection area to select the chip size you want to bet. You can view more chip sizes by clicking the arrows.
    2. Place your bet. How 
    • Click the Banker betting area to place a bet on the Banker's hand winning the game.
    • Click the Player betting area to place a bet on the player's hand winning the game.
    • Click the Tie betting area to place a bet on the banker and player's hands having the same value.
    • Click the Banker Pair betting area to place a side bet on the banker’s first two cards being a pair.
    • Click the Player Pair betting area to place a side bet on the player’s first two cards being a pair.
    • Click a combination of betting areas to place a dual bet.  
    • To increase your bet, click the bet you want to increase. The chip pile increases by the chip size currently selected.
    • To reduce or remove a bet, right-click the bet you want to reduce or remove. The chip pile decreases by the chip size currently selected.
    • To reduce or remove a bet, hold down the Shift key on your keyboard and click the bet you want to reduce or remove. The chip pile decreases by the chip size currently selected.

    Game Options

    You can change your game play and sound settings. The options are:

    Game Play Settings

    • Displays the game play options:

    Auto Rebet

    • This option automatically places the same number of chips bet in your previous game, on the table, when a new game begins.
    Quick Deal
    • This option increases the deal speed.

    Remove Losing Hands

    • This option keeps your losing hands on the table
    Roadmap Background Colour
    • Enables you to change the background colour of the Roadmap. 
    Sound Settings
    • Displays the sound options.

    To change the game options:

    1. Click Options.
    2. Choose the required settings.
    3. Click OK.

    Highest Wins
