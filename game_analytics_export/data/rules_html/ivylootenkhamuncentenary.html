    LOOTENKHAMUN CENTENARY EDITION

    ­­­LOOTENKHAMUN - CENTENARY EDITION

     

    GAME OBJECTIVES:

    • LOOTENKHAMUN CENTENARY EDITION is a 5-reel slot game with 25 fixed paylines.
    • Winning lines pay from LEFT to RIGHT only.
    • 3 CALENDAR symbols appear anywhere on reels 2,3 and 4 in the BASE GAME will trigger the FREE SPINS feature.
    • MEGA SPINS feature can be awarded when the CALENDAR counter reaches the year 2022 in the BASE GAME. 
    • The WILD symbol substitutes for all other symbols, except the CALENDAR and PYRAMID symbols.
    • 1 of the 4 PHARAOH’S POTS can be won in the PHARAOH’S POTS feature.
    • The PHARAOH’S POTS feature is triggered randomly when one or more PYRAMID symbols appear on any of the 5 reels. Segments are filled by landing the PYRAMID symbol anywhere on the corresponding reel.
    • Collecting 4 segments of a PYRAMID above the associated reel also triggers the WILD TABLETS feature and activates a sticky expanding WILD symbol for the next 4 games on that corresponding reel.

    HOW TO PLAY:

    • BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘+’ or ‘-‘ buttons either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. Clicking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET.
    • REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game.
    • PLAY: The PLAY button in the centre right position starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the centre bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode.
    • PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the HAMBURGER icon and selecting the PAYTABLE option. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game.
    • VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF.
    • In play-money mode, the AUTO PLAY option will be disabled.
    • CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Players current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen.
    • AUTO PLAY: Select AUTO PLAY for hands-free play. For UK/Ontario users the AUTO PLAY option will be disabled.
      • To configure AUTO PLAY: Click the AUTO PLAY button then select the required number of plays. AUTO PLAY begins automatically.
      • The remaining AUTO PLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTO PLAY at any time.

    MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP):

    • Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information.
    • Click the ‘i’ icon to view game information and rules.
    • Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards.
    • Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game play.

    GAME RULES:

    • LOOTENKHAMUN – CENTENARY EDITION is a 5x4 reel slot game with 25 fixed paylines.
    • The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons.  
    • Click on the SPIN button to begin the game with the current selected or default BET amount. 
    • All payouts are made according to the paytable. The paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET. 
    • All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT, starting from the left most reel in consecutive order.
    • PYRAMID and CALENDAR symbols do not have any payout.  
    • All possible wins are added to the players BALANCE. 
    • Only the highest win amount is paid on each payline. In case they are equal, the WILD symbol wins are awarded (e.g., WILD, WILD, WILD, WILD, ANUBIS is counted as a WILD symbol win rather than the ANUBIS symbol).  
    • The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the CALENDAR and PYRAMID symbols.  
    • The WILD symbol payout will take precedence over any other symbol payout if the WILD symbol payout is higher. 
    • In the BASE GAME, CALENDAR symbols only appear anywhere on reels 2,3,4 and do not appear on reels 1 and 5.
    • In the FREE SPINS and MEGA SPINS feature there are no CALENDAR or PYRAMID symbols. 
    • When PHARAOH’S POTS and WILD TABLETS features are triggered within a spin, then the PHARAOH’S POTS feature will be triggered first followed by the WILD TABLETS feature. 
    • The CALENDAR year counter in the BASE GAME is persistent to the BET placed and is platform dependent.
    • The PYRAMID segments that are collected above the reel in the BASE GAME are persistent to the BET placed and are platform dependent.
    • The CALENDAR symbols are active during WILD TABLETS feature in the BASE GAME and will trigger FREE SPINS feature if 3 CALENDAR symbols land in reels 2,3 and 4.
    • The CALENDAR symbols are active during WILD TABLETS feature in the BASE GAME and will trigger MEGA SPINS if CALENDAR counter reaches the year 2022. 
    • When one or two CALENDAR symbols land anywhere in reels 2,3,4, the CALENDAR year counter will be updated accordingly. If 3 CALENDAR symbols land then the CALENDAR year counter will not be updated.
    • Upon initialization for each stake, the CALENDAR year counter is initialized with a random value between 1946 and 1950.
    • The CALENDAR year counter resets to the year 1922 after the completion of the MEGA SPINS feature.
    • When two CALENDAR symbols land in further spins while the CALENDAR year counter is 2021, the CALENDAR year counter is updated to 2022, the MEGA SPINS feature is activated, and the CALENDAR year counter resets to 1923 after MEGA SPINS are finished.
    • If WILD TABLETS feature and FREE SPINS/MEGA SPINS feature trigger at a time then the FREE SPINS/MEGA SPINS feature triggers first and is followed by WILD TABLETS feature.
    • MEGA SPINS or FREE SPINS cannot be activated simultaneously with PHARAOH'S POTS feature.
    • In the BASE GAME, normal WILD symbol that occur anywhere else in the view will not expand down by one position.
    • The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency. Further wins or features will be forfeited if the maximum win amount is reached during a game.
    • MIN BET is #{uiConfig.minBet} and MAX BET is #{uiConfig.maxBet}.
    • The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 96.00%. 
    • Win animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (desktop).
    • Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “GAME LOGS” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. 
    • Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

    WILD TABLETS: 

    • During the BASE GAME, each PYRAMID symbol which lands in view will fill 1 segment of the PYRAMID positioned above the corresponding reel. 
    • Each PYRAMID located above the associated reel is initially filled with 2, 1, 2, 1, 2 segments for each of the 5 reels and for each stake.
    • Once all 4 segments are filled and the PYRAMID has been filled, the WILD TABLETS feature will be triggered on the associated reel during the subsequent spin.  
    • The WILD TABLET symbol will persist and expand by one position downwards over the subsequent four games in a particular reel. For each spin, the bet is deducted from the balance, with a maximum of 4 WILD TABLETS. 
    • Up to 5 WILD TABLETS reels can be active at the same time in the BASE GAME, FREE SPINS and MEGA SPINS features. 
    • A WILD TABLETS reel ends after the 4th game and the WILD TABLETS symbols will disappear at the start of the next game, resetting the PYRAMID segments of that associated reel to zero.

    FREE SPINS:  

    • The FREE SPINS feature is awarded when 3 CALENDAR symbols appear anywhere on reels 2,3 and 4 in the BASE GAME.
    • FREE SPINS are played automatically. 
    • The FREE SPINS feature starts with WILD TABLETS, which is initially active on 1st position of 3rd reel only.
    • After each FREE SPIN, the WILD TABLET symbols remain and expand by 1 position downwards. 
    • FREE SPINS are played with a 2x BET MULTIPLIER.  
    • Any further WILD symbols landing during FREE SPINS act as WILD TABLET which remain and expand by 1 position downwards. 
    • The FREE SPINS feature will end when there are no WILD TABLET symbols in view. 
    • Regular WILD symbols will not appear behind WILD TABLET symbols. 
    • FREE SPINS feature is played at the selected BET of the triggering game. 

    MEGA SPINS: 

    • MEGA SPINS feature can be awarded when the CALENDAR counter reaches the year 2022 in the BASE GAME. 
    • The MEGA SPINS feature starts with WILD TABLETS, which is initially active on 1st position of reels 2 and 4.
    • After each MEGA SPIN, the WILD TABLET symbols remain and expand down by 1 position. 
    • MEGA SPINS are played with 4x BET MULTIPLIER.
    • Any further WILD symbols landing during MEGA SPINS act as WILD TABLET which remain and expand by 1 position downwards. 
    • The MEGA SPINS feature will end when there are no WILD TABLETS symbols in view. 
    • The CALENDAR year counter resets to the year 1922 after the completion of the MEGA SPINS feature.
    • Regular WILD symbols will not appear behind WILD TABLET symbols. 
    • MEGA SPINS are played at the selected BET of the triggering game. 

    PHARAOH’S POTS: 

    • The PHARAOH’S POTS feature is awarded randomly when one or more PYRAMID symbols appear on any of the 5 reels in BASE GAME.
    • The PHARAOH’S POTS feature consists of a 4 level PYRAMID. The player starts on the LEVEL 1 (the lowest level) and can progress up the levels collecting cash.
    • LEVEL 1 has 7 selection segments. LEVEL 2 has 5 selection segments. LEVEL 3 has 3 selection segments. LEVEL 4 has 1 selection segment. 
    • Each segment can reveal either a BET MULTIPER, PHARAOH’S POTS icon, BLANK or an UP ARROW. 
    • If the player reveals a, 
      • PHARAOH’S POTS icon: The player is awarded the corresponding PHARAOH’S POT amount. PHARAOH’S POTS feature ends. 
      • BLANK: The player is awarded another segment selection on the current active LEVEL. 
      • BET MULTIPLIER:  A BET MULTIPLIER of 1x,2x,3x,4x or 5x is awarded. BET MULTIPLIER multiplies the current BET amount which is added to the PYRAMID POT display. At the end of this feature total value displayed in the PYRAMID POT is added to the player balance.
      • ARROW: The player progresses up to the next LEVEL of the PYRAMID and activates the next PHAROAH’S POT prize. 
    • If the player reaches LEVEL 4 (the top level) of the PYRAMID, the segment will be auto revealed to award top PHARAOH’S POT of 1000x.
    • The PHARAOH'S POTS feature has a reaction timer – timed to automatically reveal segments if not chosen in time.
    • The PHARAOH’S POTS prizes are,
      • LEVEL 1: 15x BET amount.
      • LEVEL 2: 40x BET amount.
      • LEVEL 3: 200x BET amount.
      • LEVEL 4: 1000x BET amount.

    FAQ:

    I could not complete the game. What should I do?  

    If you are disconnected in the middle of the game, the software platform will automatically complete the current game cycle. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the GAME LOGS button once you have logged back into the website.  

     

    What should I do if my BALANCE is too low to place a BET?  

    Click the DEPOSIT button at bottom of the game screen and deposit funds into your account.
