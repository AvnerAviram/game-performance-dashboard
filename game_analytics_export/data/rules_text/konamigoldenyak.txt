Golden Yak RULES Play Bet 40 for 1024 ways. Free Spin wins are added to Bonus symbol win. Bonus wins are added to ways win. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reels spins. Base game and free spins have different reel set. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except BONUS, LEVEL UP and BLUE TEMPLE which pays any. All wins shown at 1 credit bet. All wins multiplied by bet multipliers. Payments are made according to the pay table. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. 1024 WAYS For all symbols except BONUS, LEVEL UP and BLUE TEMPLE, winning combinations pay through any position on reels 1, 2, 3, 4 & 5 respectively. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Free Spins 3 or 4 BONUS triggers 7 Free Spins at Level 1. 2, 3 or 4 LEVEL UP activates 7 Free Spins in the next level. LEVEL UP is substituted by BLUE TEMPLE when next level is already activated, and more Free Spins and can appear only on reels 2 and 4. Multipliers vary between 2x and 10x, depending on level being played. Multipliers combine up to 100x! Free Spins are played at the same bet multiplier as the triggering game.
