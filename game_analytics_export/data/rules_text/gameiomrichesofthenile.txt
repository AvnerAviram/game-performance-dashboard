Riches of the Nile Riches of the Nile is a skin of Red Silk under Ultimate Choice Jackpots family, featuring a Jackpot Pick Bonus game. The game features short term persisting WILD REEL in the base and free games where entire reel becomes WILD for a limited period. Jackpot pick bonus is the true pick bonus where player can win either credit prize or one of jackpots. Malfunction voids all pays and play Base game During Base game, collecting 2 ORBS on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel. WILD REEL turns all symbols in WILD for next 2 spins (Bonus symbol turns into Bonus wild). 7 Free Spins are awarded by 3 or more Bonus symbols and/or Bonus Wild. During base game, any ORB on the screen may trigger JACKPOT PICK BONUS randomly. Bonus Game(s) Riches of the Nile has 2 Bonus games; the Free Spins Bonus and Jackpot Pick Bonus Free Spins Bonus 7 Free Spins are awarded by 3 or more Bonus and/or Bonus Wild symbols. During Free Spins 2, 3, 4 or 5 Bonus and/or Bonus Wild symbols awards extra 2, 3, 5 or 10 free spins respectively. During Free Spins, collecting 1 ORB on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel and WILD REEL will stay rest of the free spin. Jackpot Pick Bonus During base game, any ORB on the screen may trigger JACKPOT PICK BONUS randomly. During Jackpot Bonus, player may select 1 of 10 objects. Objects may reveal and award credit prizes or JACKPOT which reveal and awards one of jackpot prizes. Jackpot Pick bonus is not available during free spins bonus.
