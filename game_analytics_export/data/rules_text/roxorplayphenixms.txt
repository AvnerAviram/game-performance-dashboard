Secrets of the Phoenix Megaways Secrets of the Phoenix Megaways is a 6-reel slot that features an extra horizontal reel to help form winning combinations. Each reel, including the extra horizontal reel, can have up to 7 symbols. Each spin can have up to 117,649 ways to win! Wins are awarded on left to right adjacent symbols. Cascading Reels A Cascading Feature where you can win multiple times on every wager. Winning symbols are removed and replaced with new symbols; leading to exciting chain reactions and more potential wins! Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 95.39% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $95.39 of wins. Rules Total bet is 20 x coin size. All wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. The maximum win from a single cascade is 200,000 x coin size. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 7/16/2024
