Free Spin Bonus Free Spin Bonus is triggered by 3 or more Free Spin symbols landing scattered on the reels. 3 Free Spin Symbols landing scattered awards 8 free spins and 60 credits multiplied by bet multiplier. 4 Free Spin Symbols landing scattered awards 12 free spins and 90 credits multiplied by bet multiplier. 5 Free Spin Symbols landing scattered awards 25 free spins and 150 credits multiplied by bet multiplier. 6 Free Spin Symbols landing scattered awards 50 free spins and 300 credits multiplied by bet multiplier. During the Free Spin, an extra wild is added to the top of reel 1 and another on top of reel 6, so both reels 1 and 6 have 5 positions. Free Spins can retrigger, awarding as outlined above. Maximum number of Free Spins is 99. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spin Bonus.
