Cherry8 GAME INFORMATION Cherry8 is an 8-line slot game. All wins except scatter wins are paid from left-to-right starting with the leftmost reel, and top-to-bottom starting with the top row. Certain symbols can pay as scatter wins in addition to line wins. Refer to the extra prizes section for further information. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. CHERRY BONUS Every time a 2x CHERRY prize is awarded, the CHERRY BONUS counter will decrement, and when this reaches zero, the CHERRY BONUS game is triggered. You can also trigger the CHERRY BONUS from a 3 of-a-kind CHERRY symbol payline win. One Free Spin is awarded, and only 1-BAR, 2-BAR and 3-BAR, CHERRY and RED 8 symbols are visible. If a triggering spin results in more 2x CHERRY prizes than is necessary to decrement the counter to zero, and/or more than one 3x CHERRY prize is awarded, you are awarded one extra Free Spin for each of the additional triggers. For example, if the counter is on 2, and three 2x CHERRY prizes are visible together with two 3x CHERRY prizes, you are awarded four (one for the initial trigger, three for the additional triggers) Free Spins in the CHERRY BONUS. Free Spins cannot retrigger. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. It is not possible to trigger other bonus games when this bonus is active. {cherrySymbol} BONUS SYMBOL 1-BAR BONUS Winning a 3x 1-BAR prize triggers the 1-BAR BONUS. Eight Free Spins are awarded, and only SUPER BAR, 1-BAR, 2-BAR and 3-BAR symbols are visible. There is a SUPER BAR symbol which substitutes for any BAR symbol. A prize of 5x line bet is also awarded for each SUPER BAR symbol in view. If a spin results in multiple 3x 1-BAR prizes, you are awarded one extra Free Spin for each additional 1-BAR prize. For example, four 3x 1-BAR prizes awards eleven (8 + 1 + 1 + 1) Free Spins in the 1-BAR BONUS. Free Spins cannot retrigger. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. {1barSymbol} BONUS SYMBOL BELL BONUS Every time a 3x BELL prize is awarded, the BELL BONUS counter will decrement, and when this reaches zero, the BELL BONUS game is triggered. Six Free Spins are awarded. The reels change to have only 1-BAR, 2-BAR, 3-BAR, BELL and RED 8 symbols visible. If a triggering spin results in more 3x BELL prizes than is necessary to decrement the counter to zero, you are awarded one extra Free Spin for each of the additional triggers. For example, if the counter is on 2 and the game spins in four 3x BELL prizes, you are awarded eight (6 + 1 + 1) Free Spins. Free Spins cannot retrigger. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. {bellSymbol} BONUS SYMBOL GAMBLE GAME The GAMBLE button has 3 states, OFF/ON/5x. Press the button to toggle between them. • OFF - No wins are gambled. • ON - All reel wins that do not trigger FREE SPINS award the GAMBLE option. • 5x - Reel wins greater than or equal to 5x the stake that do not trigger FREE SPINS offer the GAMBLE option. Wins from the FREE SPINS bonuses cannot be gambled. Choose a Red or Black card to start the GAMBLE, or select COLLECT to collect your winnings and end the GAMBLE. If the card shown matches the colour you picked, you win and your prize is doubled. Losing the gamble will end the game with no win. After 5 successful gambles the win is collected automatically. The gamble game may not be available in your jurisdiction. GAME RULES Line wins are awarded for between 1 and 3 of-a-kind symbols from left-to-right and top-to-bottom. When between 1 and 9 of certain symbols are visible, a scatter prize is paid in addition to the line win. Your balance, total bet and winnings are displayed on the screen at all times. Payouts are made according to the Paytable. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. The SUPER BAR substitutes for 1, 2 and 3-BAR symbols ONLY. The SUPER BAR is only visible in the 1-BAR BONUS game and appears only on reels 2 and 3. Last modification date: 12/10/2024
