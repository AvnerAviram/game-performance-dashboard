Gold Stacks 88 Lunar Festival GAME RULES Minimum bet 88 coins. 5 Gold Stacks are played. Choose your Bet. All reels are played for All Pays cost of 88 coins. Bet is the All Pays cost multiplied by Coin Size. The SPIN button will immediately commence play. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, including scatters. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Highest win paid per winning combination. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Scatter wins are added to EXTRA WAYSTM wins. EXTRA WAYSTM wins are the winning combinations that can occur anywhere in all positions on all reels. All wins are multiplied by the active Coin Size. Game RTP: 94.78%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible to confirm coins registered before game start. Last modification date: 4/16/2024
