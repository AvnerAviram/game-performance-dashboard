Monkey in the Bank Monkey In The Bank GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Plays. Beginning with the left-most reel, pays left to right on consecutive reels. The Won field displays winnings in credits. All games are played with 20 paylines. Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet. In the flash version space bar can be used to spin. Select Multiplier Bet Level (1x to 10x). Win up to two ways on every payline: Highest WILD-only line win paid in addition to highest WILD-substituting line win. Only highest scatter win is paid. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. Scatter wins (if available) are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter. Piggy and Barrel Wild substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER and each other. Five natural Piggy Wild symbols on a payline at maximum bet level award the Jackpot, however Jackpot cannot be won on a free spin. Higher credit denominations:1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. Mobile only Tapping SPIN repeats last bet Free Spins Feature 2 or more consecutive SCATTER symbols starting from the leftmost reel will start the FREE SPINS FEATURE . 3 or more consecutive SCATTER symbols starting from the leftmost reel also award a SCATTER pay. All pays double for free spins. Jackpot cannot be won on a free spin. Free spins are played at the same Multiplier Bet Level as the triggering spin. Additional free spins can be won during the Free Spins Feature. Auto Spin: In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. In settings, choose Auto Spin "ON" and select the desired number of autospins. Select the STOP button to cancel Autospin. Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus.
