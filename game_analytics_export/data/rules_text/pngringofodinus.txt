Ring of Odin Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 15 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. Game Rules Ring of Odin is a 5x3-reel video slot game played with 10 fixed paylines. Land SCATTERS on reels 1, 3 and 5 to trigger 4 FREE SPINS. Land a single SCATTER in ODIN'S RING to trigger 4 MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS. During FREE SPINS, each SCATTER that lands will award +1 FREE SPIN. During MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS, each SCATTER that lands will award +1 MULTIPLIER FREE SPIN. ODIN'S RING: This feature can activate on ANY spin. The symbol that lands in ODIN'S RING will be the chosen symbol. ODIN'S RING will multiply into 9 Rings, covering the middle three reels. During a single re-spin, if the chosen symbol lands in 1 or more ODIN'S RINGS then DRAUPNIR RE-SPIN(S) feature will be triggered. The amount of chosen symbols landing in ODIN'S RINGS will determine the number of DRAUPNIR RE-SPINS. DRAUPNIR RE-SPINS: If triggered, the chosen symbol will be duplicated into all 9 ODIN'S RINGS for the duration of the DRAUPNIR RE-SPIN(S). The chosen symbol except SCATTER becomes STACKED symbols and stick on the middle three reels for the duration of all re-spins. MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS: Scatter in ODIN'S RING gives the chance to trigger MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS. SCATTER can appear on reels 2, 3, or 4 during the single re-spin. Landing SCATTER symbols in ODIN’S RING during the single re-spin adds +1 to the multiplier, up to x9. If FREE SPINS and ODIN'S RING are triggered during the same game round, FREE SPINS will begin once all re-spins including DRAUPNIR RE-SPINS have concluded. If FREE SPINS and MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS are awarded in a single game round, only MULTIPLIER FREE SPINS are awarded. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. The theoretical return to player of this game is 96.20%. Actions Paytable- Toggles the display of the paytable. Auto Play- Click the AUTO PLAY button to enable/disable the Auto Play feature. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Spin- Starts the round by spinning the reels. Stop- Stops the reels more quickly. Fast Play- Toggle on for a significantly faster gameplay. Last modification date: 1/4/2023
