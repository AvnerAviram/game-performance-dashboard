Wild Wild Emerald GAME RULES All Pays Reel Power cost is 50 coins. Bet is 50 coins x Coin Size. Game starts with 3 symbol positions in height on all 5 reels with 243 ways. Reels 3, 4 and 5 may randomly grow to a height of 4 symbol positions with 576 ways or 5 symbol positions with 1125 ways. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. REEL POWER Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. Scatter wins and WILD BONUS prizes are added to REEL POWER wins. RETURN TO PLAYER (RTP) RTP: 95.05% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 4/10/2024
