Gold of Fortune God Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules GOLD OF FORTUNE GOD is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot game with 243 ways to win! The game features 4 BONUS prizes: The values are: GRAND: 5000X the total bet, MAJOR: 500X the total bet, MINOR: 50X the total bet, and MINI: 20X the total bet. By default, these prizes are disabled and can only be activated during the DRAGON’S FORTUNE feature. The WILD (DRAGON) symbol substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER (Gate of Kingdom). Landing a DRAGON symbol in the SILVER RING during base game triggers DRAGON’S FORTUNE feature. In the base game, trigger the DRAGON’S FORTUNE feature for 3 initial re-spins. A GOLDEN RING appears and is always available throughout the DRAGON’S FORTUNE feature. Only cash prize symbols land on the reels, revealing 2X, 3X, and 5X total bet prizes. Once a cash prize symbol lands in the GOLDEN RING, it reveals a BONUS Prize (MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI). Each cash prize symbol landing resets the RE-SPINS to 3. Fill the reels with prize symbols to get the GRAND prize! 3 or more SCATTER (Gate of Kingdom) symbols trigger the KINGDOM’S TREASURE feature. In KINGDOM'S TREASURE, two features will be played sequentially: the DRAGON’S WHEEL and the DRAGON’S FORTUNE. In DRAGON’S WHEEL, the number of wheel spins is determined by the number of SCATTERS (Gate of Kingdom) landed. 3 SCATTERS = 1 Spin 4 SCATTERS = 2 Spins 5 SCATTERS = 3 Spins The Multiplier values in the DRAGON’S WHEEL are X2(3), X3(2), X5(1), X7(1), and X10(1). The selected MULTIPLIERS will be added together and will be carried over to the DRAGON’S FORTUNE feature. The wheel presentation does not represent real probabilities. After DRAGON’S WHEEL, DRAGON’S FORTUNE will play. DRAGON’S FORTUNE awards 3 initial RE-SPINS. Two GOLDEN RINGS appear and are always available throughout the feature. Only cash prize symbols land on the reels, revealing 2X, 3X, 5X, 8X and 10X total bet prizes. Once a cash prize symbol lands in the GOLDEN RING, it reveals a BONUS Prize (MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI). Each cash prize symbol landing resets the RE-SPINS to 3. Fill the reels with prize symbols to get the GRAND prize! The MULTIPLIER will apply when the RE-SPIN is concluded. It will only apply to the cash prizes. The average RTP is 96.18%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. There are 243 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable.
