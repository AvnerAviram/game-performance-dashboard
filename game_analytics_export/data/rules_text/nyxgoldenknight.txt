Golden Knight RTP: 95.0% VOLATILITY: 8.8 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play. Select Bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol and Wild Scatter symbol. Wild Scatter symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. Each Wild Scatter symbol on the reels appears with a credit value or as a Wild symbol. Wild Scatter symbol can appear with the following credit values: Reel 1 – 25 to 100 credits Reel 2 – 25 to 100 credits Reel 3 – 25 to 2000 credits Reel 4 – 25 to 1000 credits Reel 5 – 25 to 10,000 credits Credit values have been pre-multiplied by the bet multiplier. For each game, all credit values on a reel are the same. At the end of each game, win the sum of the displayed credit values. Wild Scatter symbol only appears on all reels. Wild symbol does not contribute to Scatter Bucks pays. Wild symbol appears naturally on reels 2, 4, and 5. Wild symbol appears on all reels via Wild Scatter symbol substitution. FREE GAMES BONUS 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 7 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Different reels are used during the Free Games Bonus. Free Games Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol and Free Games Wild Scatter symbol. Free Games Wild Scatter symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. Each Free Games Wild Scatter symbol on the reels appears with a credit value or as a Free Games Wild symbol. Free Games Wild Scatter symbol can appear with the following credit values: Reel 1 – 25 to 100 credits Reel 2 – 25 to 100 credits Reel 3 – 25 to 2000 credits Reel 4 – 25 to 1000 credits Reel 5 – 25 to 10,000 credits Credit values have been pre-multiplied by the bet multiplier. For each game, all credit values on a reel are the same. At the end of each game, win the sum of the displayed credit values. Free Games Wild Scatter symbol with a credit value contributes to Scatter Bucks pays. Free Games Wild Scatter symbol appears on all reels. Free Games Wild Scatter symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Wild symbol does not contribute to Scatter Bucks pays. Free Games Wild symbol appears naturally on reels 1, 2, 4, and 5.
