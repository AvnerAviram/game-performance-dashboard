Pirate Plunder Pirate Plunder™ Brand extension of Rakin' Bacon. Second game in the Rakin' Bacon Deluxe family. Game features Pirate theme with free spins and pick bonus. Free spins offer up to 7 high reels with collection mechanic and Mini-retrigger. Match 3 pick with the ability to boost Jackpot values during the pick. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. GAME FEATURES Free Spins Bonus 3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols awards 8,10, or 12 Free Spins, respectively. Player begins at 3 high reel set. Land special symbols to increase reel height independently per reel. During the Free Spins Bonus 2,3,4 or 5 bonus symbols award additional 3,8,10 or 12 Free Spins, respectively. Additional FS awarded once the 12 special symbols and when 16 special symbols are collected all wins multiplied by 2 for the remaining free spins. Jackpot Pick Bonus This Feature is randomly triggered any time a wild symbol lands on the reels during the base game. The pick bonus allows you to pick until you get three matching Jackpot symbols, The bonus Jackpot you win is based on the three of the same jackpot symbols that you choose. These correspond to the Grand, Major, Minor or Mini Jackpot. Each Jackpot symbol has the possibility of being a "BOOST" symbol. "BOOST" symbols increase the respective Jackpot by its reset amount. Power XStream Evaluation This Feature maximizes the number of ways to win by evaluating awards based on the number of symbols in any position.
