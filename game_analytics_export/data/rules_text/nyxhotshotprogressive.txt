Hot Shot Progressive GAME RULES Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RTP is 96.5%. GAME IN GAME BONUS Any 3 or more of BLAZING 7S, DIAMOND LINE, DOUBLE JACKPOT, TRIPLE JACKPOT, or SEVEN TIMES PAY initiates the Game-in-Game scatter bonus. The Game-in-Game scatter bonus initiates 1 spin from each 3 reel game displayed. If the player does not win on any of the Game-in-Game plays, all of the initiating Game-in-Games will respin. This will repeat until at least one winning Game-in-Game is achieved. Scatter pays are added to payline wins. Alternative reels are used during Game-in-Game Bonus. Game-in-Game plays only utilize the CENTER PAYLINE unless otherwise noted. DIAMOND LINE 4 of a kind wins only occur on DIAMOND PLAYLINE.
