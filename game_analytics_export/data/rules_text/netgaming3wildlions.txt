Game Rules: The game is played with 5 fixed paylines and each game costs a minimum of 10 fixed coins. Wins are paid according to the Paytable. All symbols pay from leftmost reel to right in consecutive order. Only the highest win is paid per selected line. Lion symbols substitute for all symbols. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For this game, the maximum long term expected payback is 94.43%. Lion Features: Mystery Lion: When a Blue Lion symbol lands on reel 1, it awards a mystery reward. Expanding Lion: When a Green Lion symbol lands on reel 2, it expands to cover the entire reel and then the win is awarded. Multiplier Lion: When a Red Lion symbol lands on reel 3, a multiplier 3x is applied to all the wins. If 2 or more Lions land simultaneously, their features are combined. Super Spins: 3 Super Spins can be awarded when there is at least 1 Lion visible on the reels. 1 Lion gives less chance to award Super Spins. 2 Lions gives higher chance to award Super Spins. 3 Lions guarantee Super Spins. During each Super Spin at least 1 Lion is guaranteed to appear on any 1 of the reels, additional Lions can land naturally. If 2 Blue Lions appear on reel 1, their mystery wins are summed up If 2 Red Lions on reel 3, their multipliers are multiplied together and then applied to all the wins. Super Spins cannot be retriggered. Buy Bonus: Buy Bonus feature is available only in the base game. Buy Bonus feature is played at 50xTotal Cash Bet. Buy Bonus feature guarantees entry into the Super Spins feature. The long-term maximum expected payback is 94.69% with the Buy Bonus feature Auto Spin Game Rules: Auto Spin is similar to a regular spin, you can choose Auto Spin for a selected number of spins. Auto Spin automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. As soon as Auto Spin begins, the Spin button is replaced with the Stop button with the counter displaying the number of Auto Spins. Auto Spin can be stopped at any time by tapping the Stop button. Auto Spin will stop when any feature rounds are triggered. Auto Spin will stop if you are disconnected while playing.
