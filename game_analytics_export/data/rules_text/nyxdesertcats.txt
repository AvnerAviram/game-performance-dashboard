Desert Cats Game Introduction Desert Cats™ is a 7x4 slot game which features a Free Spins Bonus and WILD reels on every spin! WILD is wild for all symbols except for scatter symbols. LION is a scatter symbol and is present on all reels. During the base game, 2 adjacent reels will be replaced by reels consisting of WILD symbols. WILD symbols may contain red gems in the base game. Land 6 or more red gems to be awarded Jackpots. Jackpots are shown above the reels and are only available in the base game. Bets Selecting your bet: To adjust the BET, use the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons to increase or decrease the Total Bet. Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Free Spins Bonus Land 3, 4, or 5 LION symbols to be awarded 5 free spins, 15 free spins and 10x total bet, or 50 free spins and 15x total bet respectively. 2 sets of 2 adjacent reels will always be replaced by WILD reels during the Free Spins Bonus. Paylines RTP is 95.8% There are a total of 50 paylines. All win amounts and configurations are shown in the paytable. Jackpots The jackpots available in this game are MINOR, MEDIUM, MAJOR, SUPER, and MEGA. These correspond to 6-10 red gems that may appear during the base game. The value of each jackpot is dependent on the initial bet. Disclaimer Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Only active paylines can register wins. Winning combinations may contain only one 1x1 symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel.
