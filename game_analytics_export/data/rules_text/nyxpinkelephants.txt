Pink Elephants Note: The Pink Elephants video slot is referred to as Pink Elephants, The Game or Game. Pink Elephants is a video slot with 6 reels and 4096 pay ways. The game features Mystery scatter and a bonus game with free spins, symbol transformation and retriggers. The theoretical return to player is 96.1%. A game round is started with the action button and plays the game with the selected bet level. No bets can be altered during a game round. Auto play plays the game automatically for the number of game rounds selected in the auto play page. All game payout and win combinations are paid out according to the pay table. Only the highest win per pay way is paid out, if in sequence from leftmost to right and in combinations according to the pay table. All pay way wins during a game round are added and presented at the end of the game round. All wins are presented and paid out in the selected currency. The wild symbol substitutes for any other symbol except the scatter symbol. 3 or more scatter symbols trigger the Bonus game consisting of free spins. In the Main game, 1-5 Mystery scatter symbols may be triggered in any spin, increasing the chance to win the Bonus game. In the Bonus game, Elephant Orbs are collected in order to fill the symbol meters. When a meter is filled, the symbol transforms into an Elephant symbol for the remainder of the Bonus game and the player is awarded with 1 extra Free Spin. In the Bonus game, 2 or more scatters trigger respin. The amount of free spins awarded depends on the number of scatters. The Bonus game is played with the same bet as the spin that triggered the bonus game. In the event of game malfunction all affected bets and pays are rendered void.
