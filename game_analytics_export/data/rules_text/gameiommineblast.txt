Mine Blast Mine Blast is the second math model in the Ultimate Choice Jackpots game family, featuring 3-level Jackpots. The game features a short term persistent WILD REEL in the base game where the entire reel becomes WILD for a limited period. Jackpot Pick Bonus is a true pick bonus where the player can win either a credit prize or one of the Jackpots. GAME FEATURES Wild Reel Feature During Base game, collecting 3 DETONATORS on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel. DETONATORS may have multipliers of 2x or 3x. DETONATORS appearing on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. WILD REEL turns all symbols into WILD for next 3 spins. (BONUS turns into BONUS WILD) Multiplier DETONATOR activates MULTIPLIER WILD REEL for that reel. MULTIPLIER WILD REEL turns all symbols into WILD MULTIPLIER for next 3 spins. Free Spins Bonus 5 Free Spins are awarded by 3 BONUS or BONUS WILD symbols At the start of free games, 1x, 2x, 3x and/or 5x DETONATORS are placed on top of Reels 2, 3 and 4. Each free games, DETONATORS on Reels 2, 3 and/or 4 may activate WILD REEL or MULTIPLIER WILD REEL for that reel. Jackpot Pick Bonus During base game, any DETONATOR on the screen may trigger Jackpot Pick Bonus randomly. During Jackpot Bonus, player may select 1 of 10 objects. Objects may reveal and award credit prizes or JACKPOT which reveal and awards one of jackpot prizes. Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
