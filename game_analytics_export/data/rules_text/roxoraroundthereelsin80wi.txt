Around The Reels In 80 Wilds Around the Reels in 80 Wilds Bonus 3 or more BONUS symbols, landing on any reels, trigger the Around the Reels in 80 Wilds Bonus . 7 free spins are awarded initially. Start in London and collect WILDs to progress to the next destination. Upon reaching each destination, 5 extra free spins are awarded, and certain symbols are replaced by additional WILDs on the reels too. Collect a total of 80 WILDs to go around the world and return to London, to earn an additional prize of 2500 (x Coin Size) for completing the journey, awarded at the end of the bonus. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 95.77% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Rules All pays shown with a wager of 1 coin. All wins multiplied by coin size. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Highest win paid on any lit payline. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel.
