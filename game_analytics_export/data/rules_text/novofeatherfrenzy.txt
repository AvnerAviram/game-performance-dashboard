Feather Frenzy Rules Wild substitutes for all symbols, except scattered 'Golden Egg' and 'Keys' All pays are for wins of left to right on selected lines. All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Highest win only paid per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Main Game Your aim in Feather Frenzy is to land 5 matching symbols on any line to receive the greatest rewards. All winning patterns start onthe first reel on the left and run along the win lines to the right. The Diamond is wild and replaces all symbols except the Golden Egg Game Theme and Graphics Escape to the tropics with some feathered friends in Feather Frenzy. In this 5 reel 20 line slot join our colorful gang of birds in a search for the golden egg. Land 3 or more of these and you will enter the free games where our charming feathered friends may be locked in cages with each cage having a bonus prize attached up to 100x bet. During the Free Games watch out for the golden Key, land this to unlock all bird cages on screen and collect the bonus prizes. Free Games Feature 3 or more Golden Egg symbols trigger up to 25 Free Games. Caged birds pay up to 100x bet when a Key symbol appears on screen. Feature trigger: 3x, 4x or 5x Golden Egg symbols Feature hit rate: 140-170 games. RTP The Return to player for this game is 95.14%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
