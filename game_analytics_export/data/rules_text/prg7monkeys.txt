7 Monkeys Characteristics and features All winning combinations pay from left to right only All wins are multiplied by bet per line Bets are made in coins per line To control bets, user is able to change the value of a coin in real currency and also increase the number of coins bet per line Balance, bets, wins are all displayed in both coins and real currency at all times There are 7 symbols in the game. All symbols except for Scatter are stacked 5 symbols – 5 stylized monkey portraits 1 Wild symbol – a baboon portrait, substitutes for all symbols except for scatter 1 Scatter symbol – represented by a banana until won, when won there`s a small monkey behind the banana. Triggers the free spins round Free spins feature Three or more scatter symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Free spins feature Depending on the number of scatter symbols hit, the user is a awarded a different number of free spins with a different multiplier, as follows: 3 Scatters - 50 free spins, 1x multiplier 4 Scatters – 100 free spins, 2x multiplier 5 Scatters – 150 free spins, 3x multiplier Paytable Artwork English
