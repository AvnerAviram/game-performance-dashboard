Scarab Kingdom About this Game Welcome to Scarab Kingdom. This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. This game has 30 lines. It features Pharaoh's Free Spins with progressive multiplier, Cleo’s Bonus, Wild Scrolls and Scarab Scatters. General Win on a line pays if in succession from the leftmost to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols and Scarab Scatters symbols. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Wild Scrolls can trigger in the base game and in Pharaoh's Free Spins. It is not possible to trigger Cleo’s Bonus within Pharaoh's Free Spins. Scarab Scatter Collection In the base game, a Bonus Bar is located above the reel set. Each reel has a Scarab Holder located above the reel. When a Scarab Scatter lands, it is added to the Scarab Holder and to the Bonus Bar. 3 Scarab Scatters in a Scarab Holder trigger the Wild Scrolls feature on that reel. Scarab Scatters do not appear on the reels on which Wild Scrolls is active. Collecting 100 Scarab Scatters triggers the Cleo’s Bonus feature. Scarab Holders, Wild Scrolls, Bonus Bar, and remaining reels persist for each bet level. Scarab Scatter symbols appear in base game only. Wild Scrolls The Wild Scrolls feature turns all positions on a reel into Wild symbols. On every spin, Wild symbols on a Wild Scroll shift down one position on the reels. Free Spins symbols are visible through the Wild Scrolls. Free Spins can be won when Wild Scrolls is active. Cleo's Bonus In Cleo’s Bonus, there are 4 types of bonus coins. Each type has 3 coins. l In total, there are 12 bonus coins. Each coin type corresponds to one cash prize: X15 bet, X40 bet, X200 bet, and X5000 bet. Revealing 3 of the same coin type awards the corresponding cash prize according to the PAYTABLE. When the feature ends, the Bonus Bar count is reduced by 100. Pharaoh's Free Spins Free Spins symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game. 3 Free Spins symbols awards an indefinite amount of Free Spins. Free Spins begins with a multiplier of X1. l After every spin, the multiplier increases by 1. Every individual win in Free Spins is multiplied by the current multiplier. At the beginning of Free Spins, Wild Scrolls is automatically active on reel 3. Landing a regular Wild on any reel activates Wild Scrolls. Regular Wilds do not appear on the same reel where Wild Scrolls is active. Wild Scrolls activate on the same spin it was triggered. The feature ends when Wild Scrolls are no longer present on the visible reel area. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount.
