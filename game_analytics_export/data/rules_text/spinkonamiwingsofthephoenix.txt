Wings of the Phoenix BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. PHOENIX substitutes for WOMAN, CRYSTALS, VIAL, FAN, STAFF, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: WOMAN, CRYSTALS, VIAL, FAN, STAFF, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. MYSTERY FEATURE The Mystery Feature is randomly triggered during or after the reel spin. When triggered, all A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 are randomly transformed into one of the following symbols: PHOENIX, WOMAN, CRYSTALS, VIAL, FAN or STAFF. All A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 are transformed into the same symbol. Wins are paid after transformation. Different reels are used during the feature. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 GEM trigger 8, 15 or 30 free games respectively. The Mystery Feature can frequently occur during the free games. Additional free games can be won during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
