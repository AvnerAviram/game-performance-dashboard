Colossal Diamonds Colossal Diamonds is a classic slot game with a single payline that showcases a blend of classic symbols like bars and sevens, along with innovative wild multipliers. The excitement is heightened as players can achieve considerable payouts with the combination of these symbols, especially when the multiplier wilds land on the third reel. GAME FEATURES Wild Multiplier Feature Wild multipliers ([10x], [5x], [4x], [3x], [2x]) exclusively appear on the third reel. These wilds can substitute for all symbols, except when that specific symbol is the sole paying symbol on the payline. When combined with the "Diamond" symbol, these multipliers can grant colossal wins, with potential payouts ranging from 80x Bet (for Diamond, Diamond, Wildx2) up to an astonishing 400x Bet (for Diamond, Diamond, Wildx10). Classic Payouts Get three of the same Diamond, Cherry, 7, or Bar symbols in the line for a payout. Mixed 7s and mixed bars also grant payouts. Payouts are also offered for 1 or 2 Diamond or Cherry symbols. Lastly, a single wild on reel 3 will result in a pay.
