The Control Panel can be opened by clicking the Ellipsis (…) located above and left of the Win meter, and is closed by clicking anywhere on the Game Screen. Clicking the PLAY button or pressing the SPACE bar will also close the Control Panel and spin the reels. PLACING BETS TOTAL BET is Game Cost x COIN Bet. TOTAL BET can be changed by clicking the '-' and '+' buttons next to COIN Bet. AUTOPLAY Mode AUTO button is located on the Control Panel. Opening the Control Panel will pause game play and allow access to AUTO button. Clicking the AUTO button toggles between Auto ON and Auto OFF. When Auto ON, auto play will continue until player turns Auto OFF. State of AUTO button will pause when Feature is triggered, and reinstated at end of feature. Clicking the AUTO button toggles between Auto ON and Auto OFF. When Auto ON, game will play for 99 spins or until player turns Auto OFF. --> TURBO Mode TURBO button is located on the Control Panel. Clicking the TURBO button toggles between Turbo ON and Turbo OFF. When Turbo Mode is on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster. PLAY To start the game, click the PLAY button or press the SPACE bar. Hold the PLAY button or SPACE bar for continuous play. Release the PLAY button or SPACE bar to end continuous play session.
