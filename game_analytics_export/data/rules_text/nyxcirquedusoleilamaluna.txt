Cirque Du Soleil Amaluna OTHER SYMBOLS WILD substitutes for all symbols appearing in the base game, except for scatter symbols. SCATTER and BONUS are scatter symbols. WILD and SCATTER appear on all base game reels. BONUS appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. JACKPOT FEATURE Land 3 or more SCATTER symbols anywhere on the reels to win jackpots. Jackpots can only be awarded from the base game. MYSTERY WILD FEATURE Any base game spin that does not trigger the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE may award the MYSTERY WILD FEATURE. If the MYSTERY WILD FEATURE is triggered, 4 to 12 positions on the reels are randomly covered with MYSTERY WILD symbols during the spin. MYSTERY WILD substitutes for all symbols except for scatter symbols. The MYSTERY WILD FEATURE is only available during the base game. BONUS WHEEL FEATURE Land 3 scattered BONUS symbols to trigger the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE. If only 2 scattered BONUS symbols land on the reels, an extra BONUS symbol may be added to the reels after the spin ends. If an extra BONUS symbol is added, the symbol that is covered by the BONUS symbol will appear as a watermark and pays as usual. One spin of the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE is awarded. Prizes on the wheel include: MOON GODDESS FREE GAMES METEOR WILDS FREE GAMES PICK BONUS FEATURE RESPIN AND WIN FEATURE CASH AWARDS The BONUS WHEEL FEATURE ends when a feature or a cash award is won. The BONUS WHEEL FEATURE is only available during the base game. CASH WHEEL FEATURE Land on the cash award segment of the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE to trigger the CASH WHEEL FEATURE. The cash award segment is denoted by a $ symbol. One spin of the CASH WHEEL FEATURE is awarded when this feature is triggered. The CASH WHEEL FEATURE ends when cash awards are won. Possible cash awards are 5x to 60x total bet. The awards on the wheel are displayed as cash values. These values are already multiplied by the total bet. METEOR WILDS FREE GAMES Land on the METEOR WILDS wheel segment during the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE to trigger METEOR WILDS FREE GAMES. Five free games and 1x total bet are awarded. METEOR symbols substitute for all symbols during the feature, and pays the same as WILD. METEOR symbols fall onto the reel set at the start of each spin, and will remain on the reels for the duration of the spin. Between 4 and 12 METEOR symbols are guaranteed to land each spin. Free games are played with the same winning combinations as the base game. Free games use bonus reels that differ from the base game reels. BONUS and SCATTER symbols are not available in this feature. Additional free games cannot be awarded during this feature. The feature will end when there are no free games remaining. MOON GODDESS FREE GAMES Land on the MOON GODDESS wheel segment during the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE to trigger MOON GODDESS FREE GAMES. To begin, 7 free games, 1x total bet, and a line multiplier of 1x are awarded. Land MOON symbols to win additional prizes. MOON substitutes for all symbols in this feature. MOON is only available in this feature and appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Each MOON symbol awards one of the following: Additional free games: 1, 2 or 3. Increased line multiplier: +1, +2 or +3 is added to the existing multiplier Cash award from 5x to 62.5x total bet The line multiplier may not exceed x10. If this value is reached, MOON symbols will not award multiplier increases, but award an alternate prize. Cash prizes shown on screen are already multiplied by the total bet. The feature ends when no free games are remaining. BONUS and SCATTER symbols are not available in this feature. Free games are played with the same winning combinations as the base game. Free games use bonus reels that differ from the base game reels. PICK BONUS FEATURE Land on PICK BONUS during the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE to trigger the PICK BONUS FEATURE. Pick from 12 feathers to reveal a cash award. All cash awards that are picked are added to the total win. With each pick, the player will receive either a "LEVEL UP" or "COLLECT" message. A "LEVEL UP" message will advance the player to the next level, where another pick is awarded. Larger cash awards can be picked at higher levels. A "COLLECT" message will end the feature. Cash awards include : Level 1: up to 8x total bet Level 2: up to 15x total bet Level 3: up to 30x total bet Level 4: up to 50x total bet Cash awards displayed on screen have already been multiplied by the total bet. The first pick is guaranteed to reveal a "LEVEL UP" message. RESPIN AND WIN FEATURE Land on the RESPIN AND WIN wheel segment during the BONUS WHEEL FEATURE to trigger the RESPIN AND WIN FEATURE. Before the feature begins, a cash prize of 5x to 125x total bet is awarded. This amount is already multiplied by the total bet. The RESPIN AND WIN feature consists of a feature-only bonus wheel. One spin of the wheel is awarded. Possible awards include : MOON GODDESS FREE GAMES METEOR WILDS FREE GAMES PICK BONUS FEATURE The feature ends when a prize from the wheel is awarded.
