Raiden Basic Game Rules GAME IS ALWAYS PLAYED AT 20 LINES WITH A MINIMUM BET OF 20 CREDITS LINE WINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY THE BET PER LINE WINS ON ALL LINES ARE ADDED ONLY HIGHEST TRIGGER WIN PAYS SCATTER WINS ARE MULTIPLIES BY THE LINES PLAYED TIMES THE BET PER LINE SCATTER WINS ARE ADDED TO THE LINE WINS MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS ONLY HIGHEST WINNER PAID PER LINE PAYOFFS ON REGISTERED CREDITS ONLY ALL WINS ARE SHOWN IN CREDITS LINE WINS MUST OCCUR ON ADJACENT REELS, BEGINNING WITH THE LEFTMOST REEL Cascade Feature Rules Scatters do not have a frame color If there is a line win and at least one symbol frame color matches the weapon color then the cascade feature begins Once the cascade feature is triggered all symbols with the matching frame color will be destroyed and the next symbols in the reel strip will take their place As the next symbols fall into place a new weapon color will be determined Wins will be calculated on the new matrix and the cascade feature will continue until there are no wins and no matching colors If a weapon spinner lands as a BOMB and there is a line win then all symbols besides scatters are removed Scatter pays along with feature triggers are only evaluated after the cascade feature finishes The multiplier bar can fill during the cascade feature. The multiplier value will increase by one for every four symbols removed from the previous matrix The multiplier is re-evaluated each time symbols are destroyed and does not accumulate throughout the entire cascade feature The multiplier value will be applied to line wins only, it will not be applied to scatter wins The multiplier is reset to one each time the reels spin The cascade feature is not active during free games Free Game Feature Any 3 or more Raiden V symbols trigger 15 free games Free games can be retriggered Different reels are used during the free games Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the bonus feature RTP: The theoritical win is 94.5%.
