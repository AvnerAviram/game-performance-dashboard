Howling Treasures Game Features Wild Symbol 5 WILD symbols on a single payline will award the highest payout in the game – a gorgeous 2,000 x coins! WILD substituting in a winning combination DOUBLES the prize. Bonus Wheel Feature 3 or more BONUS symbols landing on the base game reels will award the Bonus Wheel Feature. Each prize on the Bonus Wheel awards a Free Spins series. Bonus Wheel prizes: 5 Free Spins with Multiplier x2 10 Free Spins with Multiplier x3 15 Free Spins with Multiplier x3 20 Free Spins with Multiplier x3 20 Free Spins with Multiplier x5 Land 3 BONUS symbols during the Free Spin Feature to earn a retrigger where the number of spins you initially won will be added to your current total. Return To Player (RTP) The RTP for this game is 94.95% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $94.95 of wins. Game Rules Total bet is 25 lines x coin size. 25 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 7/2/2024
