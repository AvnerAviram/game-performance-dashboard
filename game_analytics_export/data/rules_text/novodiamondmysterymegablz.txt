Diamond Mystery - Mega Blaze GENERAL INFORMATION Game Type 5-line, 3 reels video slot with Jackpot Diamonds Feature Theme Fruits GAME FEATURES Double or nothing gamble Diamond Mystery™ Jackpot Feature PAYOUT Default maximum Win 750 times bet per line Hit Frequency (%) Approx. 8% Jackpot Four-level Diamond Mystery™ Jackpot Feature. Volatility Variations Variations (%) 95 Return (%) 95.48 RTP = 95.48% GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Mega Blaze is a 3x3, 5 lines slot game with glossy fruits. Main Game Wins pay from left to right on win lines and a Jackpot Feature that can trigger on any non-winning spin. The same fruit symbol on all 9 positions of the reels doubles the total win. Jackpot Diamonds Feature The Jackpot Diamonds Feature uses the four-level Diamond Mystery ™ Jackpot Feature . Each mystery level has specific gems. The base win of each jackpot depends on your stake. The base win is 5.000 times your stake for the Diamond Jackpot, 1.250 times your stake for the Red Gem Jackpot, 200 times your stake for the Blue Gem Jackpot, and 50 times your stake for the Green Gem Jackpot. Green Gem - 1st level (lowest jackpot value) Blue Gem - 2nd level Red Gem - 3rd level Diamond - 4th and highest level Once triggered, the player is taken to the Jackpot Cards game where he/she can pick one of 12 face-down cards. The player picks cards until 3 matching gem symbols have been revealed. The Jackpot Diamonds Mystery level corresponding to the revealed gem will be awarded. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Symbols pay from left to right and must be consecutive. All wins pay only on lines in play. Highest win only paid per win line. Line wins are added.
