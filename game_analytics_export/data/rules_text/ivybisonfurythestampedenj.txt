Bison Fury The Stampede Game Objective: Bison Fury the stampede is a five reel, "All Ways Slot” game with 1024 possible ways to win in the main game as well as free spins. Symbols pay from left to right in consecutive combinations (Ways). Free spins are triggered with three or more Scattered Sunset symbols appearing anywhere in view. The free spins feature can be re-triggered within free spins. The Eagle Coin wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4 and substitutes for all other symbols, except for Scattered Sunset symbols. During any spin stampeding Bisons can extend the reels up to a maximum of 8 symbols, giving 32768 ways to win. How to play: Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet is set. Game Rules: Bison Fury the Stampede is a five reel, All Ways Slot game with 1024 possible ways. Stampeding Bisons can extend the reels up to 8 symbols increasing the win possibilities to 32768 ways. All payouts are made according to paytable. Total Bet is 40 times the selected Bet value. BET amount can be changed by using the ‘-‘and ‘+’ buttons. SPIN begins the game with currently selected BET. Way wins are multiplied by the amount total bet. Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid. All Possible Wins are added to balance. All symbols pays from left to right in consecutive reels, starting with the left most reel except for the scatter sunset symbol. Eagle Coin is a Wild symbol which substitutes for all symbols except for Scattered sunset symbol. Wild (Eagle Coin) and Scatter (Sunset) symbols do not have payout. Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol appears only on reels 2,3 and 4 in both base game and Free Spins and doesn’t appear on 1 and 5 reels. The Free Spins feature will be triggered when 3 or more Scattered Sunset symbols appear anywhere on the reel view. If free spins, rising bison & top reel bison triggered at a time, then top reel bison will take precedence followed by rising bison feature. Free Spins will continue after completing these 2 features. If we get both Rising Bison & Top reel bison feature at a time, then we can get maximum of 8x5 reel set. All pays are shown in USD currency. Gameplay history can be accessed by logging into player account on the desktop website and choosing menu History inside any game. If top reel bison and rising bison features are triggered at the same time, then top reel bison will add bison symbols first then rising bison will stampede and will add extra symbols to the reel set. However top reel bison symbols remain at the top of the reel. The min bet is $0.80 and the max bet is $30.00 . The theoretical return to player of this game is 93.11 %. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free spins feature: The Free Spins feature will be triggered when 3 or more Scattered Sunset symbols appear anywhere on the reel view. Players will be awarded 8 Free Spins for 3 scatters, 16 Free Spins for 4 scatters and 40 Free Spins for 5 scatters. During the Free Spins feature Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol acts as a sticky wild. All way wins during Free Spins are multiplied by triggering bet of free spins. The Feature re-trigger during the Free Spins will add Free Spins based on the number of scatter (Sunset) symbols appeared on reels i.e. 3 Scatters will re-trigger 8, 4 scatters will re-trigger 16 and 5 scatters will re-trigger 40 free spins. Scatter symbols do not have any direct payout. Sticky Wild Rules: During the Free Spins feature, if the Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol appears in view awards re-spins, a random number between 1-5 is displayed on the Wild(Eagle Coin) symbol and the Wild(Eagle Coin) sticks to the position until counter on respective Wild(Eagle coin) reaches to 0. The Wild (Eagle Coin) will stick in place as the re-spins are counted down to 0. The number shown on any Eagle Coin(s) counts down for each re-spin. During re-spins further wild (Eagle Coins) in view will add more sticky wilds (Eagle Coins) and re-spins. The more awarded re-spins are added to the re-spin counter displayed at the bottom of the game. The sticky Wild (Eagle Coin) symbol disappears on completion of re-spins and the player will be continued with the remaining Free Spins. If we have both Sticky Wild appears along with Rising Bison Feature & Top Reel Bison, then the re-spins awarded for the sticky wild feature will be continued with the extended reel set. E.g., If free spin 1 gets a rising bison on reels 2 and 3 and gets a wild. The extra height of reels 2 and 3 will stay until the re-spin feature ends. Once re-spins feature activates, Rising Bison & Top Reel Bison Feature’s will not be activated. Rising Bison Feature: The Rising Bison Feature can be triggered in any game. Bison’s will stampede vertically from bottom to top and the height of the reels will expand, up to as many as all five reels. The extended reel contains all symbols. The Height of the reel is random. If player gets more than one stampeding bison then the height of the newly added reel will be same as first reel. Winnings will be paid according to the paytable. Top Reel Bison Feature : The Top Reel Bison Feature Can be triggered in any game. Bison’s will stampede horizontally from right to left, across the top of the reels adding Bison symbol. Up to 5 Bison symbols, 1 per reel will be added randomly. Jackpot Rules: The Big One jackpot can be triggered anytime, either during normal spin, auto spin or after the Free spin feature game. By clicking on the SPIN button, the wheel rotates and stops on any one jackpot name and the respective jackpot amount is awarded.
