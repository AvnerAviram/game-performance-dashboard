Quick Hit Platinum Triple Blazing 7s Quick Hit Platinum Triple Blazing 7s The objective of the game is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. HOW TO PLAY SELECT YOUR STAKE - If you wish to adjust this, click the STAKE/LINE '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. LINES This meter displays the number of lines being played. All lines are always in play. STAKE This meter displays the total amount wagered per spin. START THE GAME Click the SPIN button to start your game. The game contains an AUTOPLAY function that allows the player to select a quantity of games for continual play. Select your desired number of spins by clicking on the numbered buttons that are revealed when you press AUTOPLAY. Click 'Stop' to cancel Autoplay. A bonus event will stop the Autoplay. TOT A L WIN This meter displays the total amount won for any single spin. HELP All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiples of the stake per line. These can be viewed by pressing the HELP button and are displayed as cash values in the game which change as the stake per line is adjusted. SPECIAL PAYS All pays except scatter pays are adjacent on an active payline. All pays except scatter pays are left to right on an active payline starting with the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid per line played. Scatter pays are paid for symbols appearing in any position on the reels. Only highest scatter win of each symbol paid per play. Only one scatter win of each symbol paid per play. Scatter pays are multiplied by the stake wagered. Paytable is dynamic and reflects actual payouts based on current wager. Wins on different lines are added together and scatter wins (if available) are added to pay line wins. All pays in currency. All active paylines are fixed. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. WILD symbol on an active payline is wild when matched with other symbols to make a winning combination and substitutes for all symbols except FREE GAMES BONUS symbol, QUICK HIT symbol and QUICK HIT PLATINUM symbol. FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE Any 3 FREE GAMES BONUS symbols appearing on or within one position of the first payline, on reels 2, 3 and 4 at the same time awards the FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE in addition to the normal scatter pay. To begin the FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE, select a PICK tile from the grid of 20 tiles. Continue until 3 like free games options, as listed below have been revealed: 20 FREE GAMES AT 3X NORMAL PAY 15 FREE GAMES AT 2X NORMAL PAY 11 FREE GAMES AT 2X NORMAL PAY 10 FREE GAMES AT 2X NORMAL PAY 7 FREE GAMES AT 2X NORMAL PAY 5 FREE GAMES AT 2X NORMAL PAY If the WILD BONUS tile is revealed, it will count as 1 match toward the 3 needed for all 6 free games options, as well as award 5 additional free games. If more than 1 free game option occurs simultaneously, only the highest free games option will be awarded. The free games reels differ from the base game reels. All free games are played with the stake that were in play when the bonus feature was triggered. Additional free games may be accrued during the FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE. Additional free games are added to the remaining free games at the same rate and odds as the initiating round of free games. The bonus feature ends when no free games remain, or when 50 free games have been played. FREE GAME BONUS appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4. QUICK HIT PLATINUM symbol awards are not available during free games. QUICK HIT symbol scatter wins are available during the FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE.
