Druidess Gold GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. All credits staked are multiplied by the credit value. All wins pay left to right on consecutive reels starting with the first reel excluding scatters which pay any. Select credits per spin (50, 100, 150, 200 or 250). Only the highest combination is paid per way. Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current credits selected. The Won field displays winnings in Credits. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. 1296 Ways to Win. Clicking SPIN or space bar repeats the last bet. Scatter wins (if available) are added to reel wins. Only highest scatter win is paid. WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. REELFECTA REEL Reel 3 is the [REELFECTA REEL] and is comprised of 16 individual positions. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. RING OF PROTECTION BONUS Bonus symbol appears on the outer positions of Reel 3 [REELFECTA REEL]. 5 or more Bonus symbols trigger the Ring of Protection Bonus. Win up to 12 free spins with locked wilds. Lock in 12 Wilds to trigger a mega spin. Free Spins During each free spin, starting at the top left corner and moving clockwise on the outer [REELFECTA REEL], a specific reel will display either a [wild] or [poison] symbol. Poison will not appear during the first 3 spins. If a Free Spin Wild appears, the Free Spin Wild will be locked in and award an extra free spin, up to a maximum 12 Free Spins. If all 12 positions in outer reel have wild, 1 Mega Spin is rewarded. If a poison appears free spins end and poison pays 2x total amount bet. Free Spin Wild substitutes for all other symbols except poison. Mega Spin Expands to cover all 16 positions on Reel 3 [REELFECTA REEL] making all 16 positions wild. Free Spins ends upon the conclusion of Mega Spin. Different bonus reels are used during each Free Spin and Mega Spin. Free Spins and Mega Spin are played at the triggering bet. AUTO SPIN In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. Click the AUTO SPIN button and select the number from the pop-up menu.
