WAGERING OPTIONS Players can tailor their experience with three wager options that determine how many reels are active. A 1-credit wager activates only the first reel, while 3 credits activate the first two reels, and a 10-credit wager brings all three reels into play. Bigger bets unlock bigger potential, with the maximum credit award climbing from 10 credits at the lowest wager to an impressive 21,040 credits at max bet. JACKPOT FEATURE Jackpot prizes take the action to another level and can only be won while playing at max bet. Each jackpot tier is tied to specific symbol and multiplier combinations, ensuring unique paths to big wins. Lining up the powerful 10, 1 with a 3x Spin Symbol, and 20 combination awards the game’s top-tier jackpot. Other prize combinations unlock the second, third, or fourth tier jackpots, each one delivering escalating excitement and payout potential. RESPIN FEATURE The Respin Feature is where the action really comes alive. On each reel, certain numbers are paired with Spin, 2x Spin, or 3x Spin Symbols. When one of these symbols lands fully visible on an active reel, it can trigger a respin of any blank reels, extending gameplay for up to two respins in a single round. At higher wagers, even more numbers are held in place, creating greater opportunities for winning combinations. Once the feature ends, all winning numbers combine for a final award, with multipliers stacking to amplify wins!
