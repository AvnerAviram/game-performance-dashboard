Kingdom Below Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Symbol wins pay for any winning payline combination without any gaps. Only the highest win is paid per active win line. Game Rules Kingdom Below is a 5-Reel Video Slot with 12-fixed paylines. A floating beast sits above the reels, when he flares up one of three actions can occur: Grab Feature: Collects all prizes on the reels beneath the beast; Slide n Grab: The beast starts on the left most reel and slides along the top of the reels collecting all prizes on all of the reels. A Multiplier that is equal to the number of reels containing grab prizes is then applied to the total win of the Slide n Grab feature; Under Reel collection: A mystery grab prize will be pulled from beneath the reels from the lava floor, these prizes can be either a grab prize, a dud symbol that pays nothing or in Free Spins only, a special Scatter symbol that allows the player to progress on the Free Spins trail. The Grab Feature and Under Reel features can trigger simultaneously resulting in any prizes on the reels and any Under Reel prizes being awarded together. The Slide n Grab cannot trigger the same round as the Grab Feature and or Under Reel collection features. The possible grab prizes are: 1x, 2x, 5x, 10x, 30x and 50x the current bet size and are displayed as cash values on the symbols. 3+ Scatters trigger Free Spins and awards 10 Initial Free Spins, Scatter symbols can land on any reels. During Free Spins, a 12 position trail is in play; Collecting Scatters allows progression along the trail. At every 2 steps along the trail you will be awarded either an extra pick or additional Free Spins. Positions 2, 6 and 10 offer extra picks and positions 4, 8 and 12 retrigger Free Spins, with an additional 10 Free Spins awarded at those steps. Win Multipliers for each retrigger stage are set to x2, x3 and X10. Scatters may only be collected if they are on the same reel as the beast as part of the Slide n Grab feature or as part of the Under Reel collection. Upon reaching the final 12th position of the trail, no additional Free Spins will be awarded and the maximum number of Free Spins achievable (including any additional Free Spins awarded through pick games) is 50 Free Spins. Multipliers in Free Spins apply to grab wins only! Line wins in Free Spins are not Multiplied. At the start of Free Spins, you will be presented with a pick bonus that will award one of eight possible Modifiers to the Free Spins game. And you will have the opportunity to win more Modifiers through trail progression. Each of the 8 Modifiers offer two variations: regular Modifier and upgraded Modifier. Free Spins Modifiers are: Regular Modifiers: 5 additional Free Spins awarded, More Grab triggers, More Slide n Grab triggers, More Grab Symbols, The Lowest Paying Symbol is removed, At least 1 Grab Symbol on every Free Spin (Win Not Guaranteed), Grab Multiplier increased to x2 (applied to Grab wins only), 1 random Grab Symbol will be sticky for 1 spin. Upgraded Modifiers: 10 additional Free Spins awarded, More Grab triggers at x2 Multiplier (applied to Grab wins only), More Slide n Grab triggers at x2 Multiplier (applied to Grab wins only), More Grab Symbols and lowest paying Grab Symbols removed, 3 Lowest Paying Symbols removed, At least 3 Grab Symbols on every Free Spin (Win Not Guaranteed), Grab Multiplier increased to x5 (applied to Grab wins only), 2 random Grab Symbols will be Sticky for 1 spin. Only 4 Modifiers can be active throughout Free Spins. Only one type of each specific Modifier can be active through pick bonus rounds and so it is not possible for both a regular and upgraded versions of Modifiers to be active at the same time. Where more than one Multiplier value is in effect, the total Multiplier will be the sum total of all active or applicable Multipliers; So a Free Spins Multiplier of X2 and a Grab Feature Multiplier of X5 will form a total Multiplier of x7 for a Grab Feature. Active Multipliers do not apply to all wins and will be feature specific, for example Slide n Grab Multipliers only apply to grab wins when the Slide n Grab Feature is triggered. Modifier Multipliers are automatically applied to grab prizes on the reels. Meaning that during a grab feature in Free Spins, a grab prize will display it’s value already increased. Example: normal grab prize of 1 shows (when grab feature is triggered) as a prize value of 2. The average RTP is 96.25%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Auto Play: You can enable/disable the Auto Play feature in the menu. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Note: if Auto Play mode is not disabled and a feature that requires interaction is triggered, a pick is made at random. This does not affect the theoretical published RTP.
