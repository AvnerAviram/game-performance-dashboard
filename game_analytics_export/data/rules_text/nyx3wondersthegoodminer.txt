GEMS Each GEM that appears may trigger the corresponding feature from top of the reel. TNT Each TNT that appears explodes all touching GEMS during the super spin feature. BIRD Each BIRD that appears will reveal one of the unlocked feature prizes. EXTRA SPINS! : Awards one extra spin. EXTRA PRIZE : Increments gem prizes by up to 4x total bet. BONUS When it appears, The gold blast feature may be triggered. 3 WONDERS SUPER SPINS FEATURE One or more GEM types may trigger the Super Spin feature. 5 Super Spins are awarded. Each GEM symbol can trigger one individual feature type. There are 3 types of Super Spins: EXTRA SPIN, EXTRA MULT and EXTRA PRIZE. Super Spins can be one Super Spin type, or a combination of up to 3 Super Spin types. Any BIRD that appears will award one of the active feature prizes. During the SUPER SPINS feature, each TNT which appears explodes all GEM symbols which are touching it. All exploded GEM prizes will be won immediately. Each GEM can be exploded by one TNT only. GEM prizes are awarded after all BIRD events occur. GEM prizes can be (multiple of total bet) : 0.4x, 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x. Any exploded GEM of inactive feature(s) during the Super Spin may unlock the feature and award 2 extra spins. “Touches” means in any position 1 space to the left, right, above, below, and diagonal to the TNT symbol (up to 8 positions total). Super Spins cannot be retriggered. Super Spins are played at the same bet as the triggering spin. Extra Spin Each Extra Spin that appears awards one extra Super Spin. Extra Prize Each Extra Prize that appears, increments GEM prizes with a random prize. Random prizes can be (multiple of total bet) : 0.4x, 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x. Extra Mult Total win multiplier start at 1. Each Extra Mult that appears increments total win multiplier by 1. All wins during the Super Spin feature will be multiplied by total win multiplier. GOLD BLAST FEATURE When the BONUS symbol appears, it may award the GOLD BLAST feature. If GOLD BLAST is triggered, 3 TNT and 3 switches are presented. GOLD BLAST feature has 3 rounds. Player may select one switch per round. Each switch explodes the TNT above it, revealing a prize. Possible prizes are (multiple of total bet) : 1.2x, 1.6x, 2x, 2.4x, 2.8x, 3.2x, 3.6x, 4x, 5.2x, 8x, 20x, 200x, 2000x. After TNT has exploded, 3 TNT and 3 switches are shown again, and the player may select another switch. GOLD BLAST feature concludes when no rounds remain. GOLD BLAST feature cannot be retriggered. GAME RULES Player selects a Total Bet to play 1024 ways. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels. Each symbol in the win can only be used once per winning combination. WILD substitutes for all symbols except GEMS, TNT, BIRD and BONUS. WILD symbols appear on reel 2, 3 and 4 during base game only. All wins with 1 WILD substituting are 2x. All wins with 2 WILDS substituting are 4x. All wins with 3 WILDS substituting are 8x. During base game, BONUS symbol appears on reel 1 only.
