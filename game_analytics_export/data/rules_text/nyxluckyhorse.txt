Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols. Wild Symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. Free Games Rules During the base game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels starts the Free Games Feature Awards between 5 and 7 free games when playing more than 5 lines. Awards between 2 and 7 free games when playing 5 lines. Free Games Bonus symbol only appears on reels 3, 4, and 5. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols. Free Games Wild Symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. Free Games Expanding Wild is wild and substitutes for all symbols in the same position on each reel from the initial Free Games Expanding Wild 1 symbol, up to and including reel 2. Wins are evaluated after Free Games Expanding Wild 1 symbol expands. Free Games Expanding Wild 1 symbol appears only on reels 3, 4, and 5. The Free Games Feature uses the same reels. During the Free Games Feature, all Free Games Bonus symbols are replaced with Free Games Expanding Wild 1 symbols. Free games are automatically played on the same lines at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Feature.
