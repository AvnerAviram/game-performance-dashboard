Detroit Lions Deluxe GAME RULES SYMBOL TRIGGERED PROGRESSIVES Progressives are available at max bet only. Progressive triggering symbols must land on active paylines to award progressives. At max bet, when three Detroit Lions Wild symbols land on payline 9, the top progressive is awarded. When three Detroit Lions Wild symbols land on paylines 1 to 8, the bottom progressive is awarded. Specific Detroit Lions Wild symbol combinations on active paylines can result in winning multiple progressives on a single play. The top and bottom progressive awards can only trigger once on a single play, subsequent jackpots at max bet along paylines 1-8 award a flat pay credit amount of 1,000 credits. At less than max bet, flat prizes are awarded. Prizes are multiplied by bet per line. Three Detroit Lions Wild symbols on payline 9 awards 4,000 credits and three Detroit Lions Wild symbols on payline 1 to 8 awards 300 credits. Specific Detroit Lions Wild symbol combinations on active paylines can result in winning multiple flat prizes on a single play. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers. DETROIT LIONS WILD SYMBOL The Detroit Lions Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for any symbol except for multiplier symbols to make a winning combination. FIXED MULTIPLIER SYMBOLS Multipliers multiply winning combinations except progressives. Two or more multipliers on a payline multiply together and multiply the winning combination. The maximum total multiplier for a winning combination is 75X. 2X multipliers are available on 777, 7, and Double Bar symbols. 3X multipliers are available on 77, 7 and Single Bar symbols. 5X multipliers are available on 777, Double Bar and Single Bar symbols. DOUBLE NUDGE TM When one or more Detroit Lions Wild symbols appear during a reel stop, wins in the initial reel stop are paid first then each Detroit Lions Wild symbol can nudge up or down one position to create a new reel stop combination to be evaluated. The nudge direction is dictated by the arrow on the symbol. All Detroit Lions Wild symbols nudge at the same time, no matter the direction. If applicable, wins for the initial nudge combination are paid, then each Detroit Lions Wild symbol can nudge a second time up or down one position to create a new reel stop combination to be evaluated. The Detroit Lions Wild symbol will not nudge above line 2 or below line 3. Detroit Lions Wild symbols can nudge one or two positions depending on its initial landing location.
