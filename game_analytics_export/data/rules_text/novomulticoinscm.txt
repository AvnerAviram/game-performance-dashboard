Multi Coins Main Game The main game is played on 3 reels. The 5 win lines offer you 5 chances to win. If this is not enough, the Wild symbol will help to increase your chances and will substitute for almost any symbol to complete winning combinations! Game Features Cash Collect Feature When each reel contain at least 1 Yellow Coin symbol, you will receive all values, and/or bonuses on the Yellow Coin symbols. Hold & Spin Feature When each reel contains at least 1 Coin symbol, and at least 1 of the Coin symbols is a Red Coin symbol, the Hold & Spin Feature will be triggered. 3 Free Spins will be awarded, and any Red Coin symbols will be held. The values, or bonuses, on the Yellow Coin symbols will be added to the value of every Red Coin symbol. For every Red Coin symbol landing the “Mini”, “Minor” and “Major” will increase! The more Red Coins you collect the faster your winnings will increase. At the end of the feature you will receive all collected prizes and bonuses. Pile of Coin Feature Each coin landing on the reels can trigger the Pile of Coins Feature. The pile of coins shown on top of the reels will collapse and the Hold & Spin Feature will start.
