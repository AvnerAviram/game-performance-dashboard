25 Card Cash Introduction Flip the 25 face down cards. Find 3, 4 or 5 like symbols in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line to win a prize! Find a Gold Card to unlock the GOLD CARD BONUS or find 3 wheel symbols anywhere to unlock the BONUS GAME. Start the game Select the stake amount, then to start the game press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game, any winnings are displayed. To play again press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. How to play At the start of the game there are 25 face down cards. Select each card to turn the card to reveal a symbol. Or press the REVEAL ALL button to flip all cards. Find a Gold Card to unlock the GOLD CARD BONUS or find 3 wheel symbols anywhere to unlock the BONUS GAME. GOLD CARD BONUS GAME Pick cards. Every card adds to your prize. Keep going until you find the COLLECT card. At this point all prizes revealed during the game are won. BONUS GAME Press SPIN to start the wheel. Win the prize the wheel stops on. Winning Wins are achieved by: Finding 3 or more like symbols in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line. Win the corresponding prize on the prize table. GOLD CARD BONUS GAME - win all prizes revealed during the game. BONUS GAME - win the prize the wheel stops on at the end of the game. General information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by player do not affect the outcome of the game. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 90.96% Last modification date: 12/6/2017
