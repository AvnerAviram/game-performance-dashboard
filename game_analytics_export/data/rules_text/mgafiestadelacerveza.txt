Fiesta de la Cerveza PRIZE PLAN PRIZE LINES MAIN GAME 20 prize lines. Lines wins are paid from left to right. Getting 3 Free Spins symbols on a prize line awards 10 Free Spins for each winningline. The Wild symbol subtitutes all symbols except the Bonus symbols. Maximum prize is 2373 * bet. MULTIPLIERS Each winning drop increases the multiplier, up yo a maximum of ‘x5’ in the main game and ‘x15’ in Free Spins, and a new drop is awarded. When no prize is won, the counter is reset. FREESPINS Getting 3 Free Spins symbols in a prize line awards 10 Free Spins. 10 Free Spins are givenfor each winning line. These spins do not consume balance and all prizes obtaiined accumulate. More Free Spins can be obtained within Free Spins. BEERS MINI-GAME Three ‘Bonus’ symbols give Access to this mini-game. In this mini-gam, 5 jars Will appear. The player must select jars until all have been selected or the EXIT sign appears. The final prize is the sum of the accumulated prizes. The prize maximum is 260 * bet.
