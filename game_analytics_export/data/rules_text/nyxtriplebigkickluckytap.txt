Wins A winning bet occurs when at least one football ( or ) is filled and displays a multiplier. A goal is selected and possible prizes are removed until a final prize is displayed. The multiplier on the football multiplies that selected prize and awards the total pay. If more than one football is filled on a wager displaying a multiplier, each football will go to a separate goal with a uniquely selected prize. Multiplier Feature The following multipliers can be awarded: : 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 10x, 15x, 20x : 10x, 20x, 30x, 40x, 50x, 75x, 100x The selected prize shown inside a goal will be multiplied by the football's multiplier.
