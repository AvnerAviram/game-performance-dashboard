Goddess of the Nile GAME RULES STACKS Wild symbols may appear on any of the 5 reels in up to 4-stacks and are wild for all symbols except Bonus symbol. FREE SPIN BONUS Bonus symbol is a scattered symbol. Three or more scattered Bonus symbols award a credit prize and trigger the Free Spin Bonus. During the Free Spin Bonus, an alternate set of reels is used. The credit prize is 2x the total bet for 3 Bonus symbols, 10x the total bet for 4 Bonus symbols and 25x the total bet for 5 Bonus symbols. During the free spins, a bonus multiplier reel with 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x and 10x multipliers spins next to the reels. All line awards are multiplied by the multiplier adjacent to the 5th symbol on the payline. During the free spins, 3 or more Bonus symbols award an additional 7 spins and the credit prize. The maximum number of available free spins is 35. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION MENU Press to open and close game settings and view game information.
