Fu Dao Le The objective of the game is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. HOW TO PLAY SELECT YOUR BET Press the TOTAL BET button to choose your bet. STAKE This meter displays the total amount wagered per spin. START THE GAME Click the SPIN (the button showing an arrow) button to start your game. If the game contains an AUTOPLAY function that allows the player to select a quantity of games for continual play, select your desired number of spins by clicking on the numbered buttons that are revealed when you press AUTOPLAY. Click the same button to cancel Autoplay. A bonus event will stop the Autoplay. TOTAL WIN This meter displays the total amount won for any single spin. PAYS There are 243 ways to win. All pays except scatter pays are paid when like symbols appear on adjacent reels from left to right starting with the leftmost reel. Scatter pays are paid for symbols appearing in any position on the reels. Only highest like symbol win paid per winning combination. Scatter pays are paid in addition to way pays. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. One or more WONUS in a winning combination is wild and substitutes for all available symbols except ENVELOPE 1, ENVELOPE 2 and CLUMPED WILD. During the base game, WONUS appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. EXPANDING WILDS During the base game, a partial stack of CLUMPED WILD on reel 3 will nudge up or down to create a full stack of CLUMPED WILD on reel 3. One or more CLUMPED WILD in a winning combination is wild and substitutes for all available symbols except WONUS, ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2. During the base game, CLUMPED WILD appears on reel 3 only. MYSTERY STACKED REELS Each reel contains stacked mystery symbol positions that are replaced with one random symbol after each reel spin is complete. All mystery symbol positions are replaced with the same symbol per spin. Mystery symbol positions may be replaced with any symbol except CLUMPED WILD, WONUS, ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2. RED ENVELOPE JACKPOT Before each base game spin, ENVELOPE 1 may be added to reel 1 and ENVELOPE 2 may be added to reel 5. ENVELOPE 1 appearing on reel 1 and ENVELOPE 2 appearing on reel 5 awards the Red Envelope Jackpot. The Red Envelope Jackpot is available at any bet level. The watermarked symbol appearing on ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2 substitutes for the indicated symbol and pays as described in the pay table. The Red Envelope Jackpot is available during the base game only. ENVELOPE 1 appears on reel 1 only. ENVELOPE 2 appears on reel 5 only. JACKPOT BONUS FEATURE A full stack of CLUMPED WILD on reel 3 may randomly start the Jackpot Bonus Feature. If the Jackpot Bonus Feature is started, select a COIN from the 15 available coins. Continue selecting until 3 like Jackpot options have been revealed: 3 MAXI awards the Maxi Jackpot and the Jackpot Bonus Feature ends. 3 MAJOR awards the Major Jackpot and the Jackpot Bonus Feature ends. 3 MINOR awards the Minor Jackpot and the Jackpot Bonus Feature ends. 3 MINI awards the Mini Jackpot and the Jackpot Bonus Feature ends. The Jackpot Bonus Feature is available at any bet level. The chance to win the Jackpot Bonus Feature increases with total bet. The Jackpot Bonus Feature is available during the base game only. FREE GAMES BONUS FEATURE 3 scattered WONUS starts the Free Games Bonus Feature. 8 free games in addition to 2x the total bet is awarded. All free games are played with the bet level that was in play when the Free Games Bonus Feature was started. Additional free games may be awarded during the Free Games Bonus Feature and are added to the remaining free games. For each scattered WONUS appearing on reel 5, one additional free game is awarded. The Free Games Bonus Feature ends when no free games remain. During the free games, WONUS in a winning combination is wild and substitutes for all available symbols except WILD, 2x WILD, and 3x WILD. During the free games, WILD, 2x WILD, or 3x WILD in a winning combination are wild and substitute for all available symbols except WONUS, 2x WILD, and 3x WILD. One or more 2x WILD in a winning combination doubles the pay for that winning combination. One or more 3x WILD in a winning combination triples the pay for that winning combination. The free games use bonus reels that differ from the base game reels. ENVELOPE 1, ENVELOPE 2, the Red Envelope Jackpot, the Expanding Wilds, and the Jackpot Bonus Feature are not available during the Free Games Bonus Feature. The Mystery Stacked Reels are available during the Free Games Bonus Feature. WILD, 2x WILD, and 3x WILD are available during the Free Games Bonus Feature only. During the free games, WILD is available in reels 2, 3, and 4 only. During the free games, 2x WILD, and 3x WILD is available on reel 3 only. During the free games, WONUS is available on reel 5 only.
