Wild Storm Legionnaire FREE SPINS FEATURE 8 Free Spins are awarded when 3 or more SCATTER symbols appear. Free Spins feature is played at the same bet as triggering bet. During the Free Spins a COLLECT symbol locks in the middle position of reel 3. During the Free Spins a wild reel may appear to cover entire reel 2 and/or reel 4. Free Spins feature can not be retriggered. HOLD & RESPIN FEATURE 1 or more COLLECT along with 2 or more COIN symbols trigger the HOLD & RESPIN feature. 3 respins are awarded. During the Hold & Respins feature, only COIN and COLLECT symbols are available. All COIN prizes are collected by each COLLECT symbol during the HOLD & RESPIN feature. All COIN symbols are held, and other positions spun. If another COIN or COLLECT appears respins reset to 3. COIN prizes on screen are paid only when a COLLECT symbol appears. COIN prizes can be $0.40, $0.60, $0.80, $1.00, $2.00, $3.00, $4.00, $5.00, $10.00, $20.00 or $25.00. If all positions are filled with COIN symbols, then $35.52 is awarded and the Hold & Respin feature ends. If multiple features are triggered simultaneously, they will be played in the following order: 1- Hold & Respin 2- Free Spins GAME RULES Play 432 ways for $1.00. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. SCATTER wins are added to way wins. SCATTER symbols only appear during base game. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. Each symbol in the win can only be used once per winning combination. WILD appears on reels 2 and 4 only. G Each WILD in a win combination doubles the prize. 1 WILD in a winning combination multiplies the win by 2. 2 WILDS in a winning combination multiplies the win by 4.
