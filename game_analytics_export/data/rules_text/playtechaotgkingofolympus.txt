Age of the Gods King of Olympus 5-Reel 25-Line Slot The objective of Age of the Gods™ King of Olympus is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. TO PLAY THE GAME This game is played with a fixed number of 25 active paylines. Your total bet per game round is your line bet × 25. Total bet per game round = line bet × active paylines. The line bet can be selected on the BET OPTIONS panel. Click '−' or '+' under LINE BET to choose the line bet. Click the TURBO MODE button to switch Turbo Mode on or off. When turned on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster. Clicking the SPIN button spins the reels with the currently selected total bet. During the reel spin the SPIN button changes into STOP . Clicking STOP ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin result. Mouse over the AUTO PLAY button and click on an option to start the Auto play function. During the Auto play mode the AUTO PLAY button changes into STOP . Click this STOP button to stop the Auto Play session. Select the number of spins to be played automatically or choose UNTIL FEATURE to spin until the Free Games feature is triggered. The number of auto spins left is displayed above the smaller STOP button, except when the UNTIL FEATURE option is selected. To enter the triggered feature, click the CLICK TO START button. After the end of the feature, click CONTINUE on the winnings summary screen to return to the main game or resume the Auto play mode (if there are any auto spins left to play). The paytable can be accessed via the INFO page. MAXIMUM WIN LIMIT The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information, see the Terms and Conditions section. ABOUT PAYOUTS Wins are calculated according to the Paytable which can be accessed via the Menu. Line win = line bet × corresponding multiplier according to the paytable. SCATTER win = total bet × corresponding multiplier according to the paytable. If more than 1 active payline has a winning combination, the winnings are added up. If 2 winning combinations occur on the same line, you will be paid the value of the higher winning combination. Winning combinations must start from the leftmost reel, and the symbols have to be consecutive along a payline. The SCATTER symbol is an exception to these rules. More information about the SCATTER symbol can be found below. The accumulating winnings and all payline wins are shown for each winning spin. In the case of large wins, a Win popup opens displaying the total spin revenue. WILD SYMBOL The Zeus symbol is the WILD symbol in the game. The WILD symbol can stand for any symbol, except for the SCATTER symbols to make the best possible winning combination. There is also a separate payout for 2 or more WILD symbols on an active payline. This is paid out instead of the regular symbol win if the win amount from WILD symbols is larger than the win from the regular symbols (by WILD standing in). SCATTER SYMBOL The Temple is the SCATTER symbol in the game. The SCATTER symbols do not have to occur on any particular payline. If there are 2 or more SCATTER symbols in the spin results, the payout earned is multiplied by the total bet and added to payline winnings. If 3 or more SCATTER symbols appear simultaneously anywhere on the reels during the main game, the Free Games feature is triggered. FREE GAMES 3 or more SCATTER symbols in any reel position during the main game trigger 10 Free Games with an increasing win multiplier and a frozen EXTRA WILD symbol. Free Games begin with a win multiplier of X2. This multiplier value increases by 1 after every second spin during Free Games: Free Games Spins Win Multiplier 1st and 2nd X2 3rd and 4th X3 5th and 6th X4 7th and 8th X5 9th and 10th X6 All wins received from a Free Games spin will be multiplied by the multiplier for that spin. This means that any winnings from the first and second Free Games spins are multiplied by X2, while any winnings from the third and the fourth spins are multiplied by X3, and so on. The EXTRA WILD symbol is frozen in the middle position of reel 3 during all Free Games and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER . During the Free Games, the reels are spun automatically using the same bet per line as on the spin that won the Free Games. If 3 or more SCATTER symbols appear in any position on the reels during Free Games, a payout is won but more Free Games are not triggered. At the end of the feature a win summary appears. The Game Win is the amount of money won from the spin that triggered the feature. The Feature Win is the amount of money won from the feature. The Total Win is the sum of the Game Win and the Feature Win. Click CONTINUE on the win summary to return to the main game. The Free Games win multiplier starts at X2 on the first spin every time the feature is triggered.
