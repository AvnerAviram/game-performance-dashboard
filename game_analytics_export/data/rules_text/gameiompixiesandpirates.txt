Pixies Vs Pirates 1.2 Game Rules Pixies vs Pirates is a video slot with three features; xNudge Wilds, xWays Mystery Chest and Battle Rounds Free Spins. A 6-reel video slot with 15 symbols. Up to 11 664 win ways (see pay table for more info). A Wild symbol substitutes for any symbol, except Scatter and xWays Mystery Chest. A Wild symbol substitutes for the highest possible winning combination according to the pay table. A winning combination pays from left to right. Coinciding wins on multiple combinations are paid out. Winning combinations and pays are made according to the pay table. Different reel configurations are used, depending on the game mode (main game or any of the bonus modes). xNudge Wilds: Will always nudge to fully visible. xNudge Wilds: For every step the xNudge Wild nudges, the Wild multiplier increases by 1. xNudge Wilds: May only land on reel 2 to 5. xWays Mystery Chest: Reveals up to 4 of the same symbols. xWays Mystery Chest: May expand its reel up to 6 symbols high and potentially give 11 664 win ways. xWays Mystery Chest: May only land on reel 2 to 5. 3 scatters awards 6 Battle Rounds Free Spins. 4 scatters awards 8 Battle Rounds Free Spins. Scatters may only land on reel 2 to 5. Battle Rounds: Auto selects Pixie Queen or Pirate Captain randomly after 20 seconds. The theoretical return to the player for this game is 95.91%. A malfunction voids all winnings. Malfunction in gaming hardware/software; all affected bets are refunded. All game states will be saved for 6 months. Autoplay automatically plays the game for selected number of rounds or when any of the advanced autoplay setting criteria’s are fulfilled. When changing autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect upon completion of the game round or feature. Some autoplay features may be mandatory for some jurisdictions. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/8/2020
