LootEnKhamun Centenary Edition ­­­LOOTENKHAMUN - CENTENARY EDITION GAME OBJECTIVES: LOOTENKHAMUN CENTENARY EDITION is a 4x5 reel slot game with 25 fixed paylines. Winning lines pay from LEFT to RIGHT only. 3 CALENDAR symbols appear anywhere on reels 2,3 and 4 in the BASE GAME will trigger the FREE SPINS feature. MEGA SPINS feature can be awarded when the CALENDAR counter reaches the year 2022 in the BASE GAME and next time if 3 Calendar symbols land in view, then MEGA SPINS feature is activated . The WILD symbol substitutes for all other symbols, except the CALENDAR and PYRAMID symbols. The PHARAOH’S POTS feature is triggered randomly when one or more PYRAMID symbols appear on any of the 5 reels. Segments are filled by landing the PYRAMID symbol anywhere on the corresponding reel. Collecting 4 segments of a PYRAMID above the associated reel triggers the RESPINS feature and activates a sticky expanding WILD symbol for the next 4 RESPINS on that corresponding reel. GAME RULES: LOOTENKHAMUN CENTENARY EDITION is a 4x5 reel slot game with 25 fixed paylines. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the SPIN button to begin the game with the current selected or default BET amount. All payouts are made according to the paytable. Paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET. All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT, starting from the left most reel on adjacent reels. PYRAMID and CALENDAR Symbols do not have any payout. All possible wins are added to the players BALANCE. Only the highest win amount is paid on each payline. In case they are equal, the WILD symbol wins are awarded (e.g., WILD, WILD, WILD, WILD, ANUBIS is counted as a WILD symbol win rather than the ANUBIS symbol). The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the CALENDAR and PYRAMID symbols. The WILD symbol payout will take precedence over any other symbol payout if the WILD symbol payout is higher. In the BASE GAME, CALENDAR symbols only appear anywhere on reels 2, 3, 4 and do not appear on reels 1 and 5. In the FREE SPINS and MEGA SPINS feature there are no CALENDAR or PYRAMID symbols. When PHARAOH’S POTS and RESPINS features are triggered within a spin, then the PHARAOH’S POTS feature will be triggered first followed by the RESPINS feature. The CALENDAR year counter in the BASE GAME is persistent to the BET placed and is platform dependent. The PYRAMID segments that are collected above the reel in the BASE GAME do not persist from game to game. When one or two CALENDAR symbols land anywhere in reels 2, 3, 4 , the CALENDAR year counter will be updated accordingly ( E.g : 1 CALENDAR symbol only adds 1 year on the CALENDAR on the game screen). If 3 CALENDAR symbols land then the CALENDAR year counter will not be updated. Upon initialization for each stake, the CALENDAR year counter is initialized with value 1970. When one or two CALENDAR symbols land in further spins of base game, while the CALENDAR year counter is 2021. the CALENDAR year counter is updated to 2022 even if 2 Calendar symbols appears and next time if 3 Free Spins symbols land in view, then MEGA SPINS feature is activated. The CALENDAR year counter resets to the year 1922 after the completion of the MEGA SPINS feature. If RESPINS feature and FREE SPINS/MEGA SPINS feature trigger at a time then the FREE SPINS/MEGA SPINS feature triggers first and is followed by RESPINS feature. MEGA SPINS or FREE SPINS cannot be activated simultaneously with PHARAOH'S POTS feature. In the BASE GAME, normal WILD symbol that occur anywhere else in the view will not expand down by one position. Up to 5 WILD TABLETS reels can be active at the same time in the RESPINS, FREE SPINS and MEGA SPINS features. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 96.29%. The win display count-up animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing SPACEBAR (desktop). MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. All pays are in USD. RESPINS: Pyramid symbols only appear in the base game. During the BASE GAME, each PYRAMID symbol that lands in view, will have a chance to light between 1-4 segments of the PYRAMID positioned above the corresponding reel. If one or more reels have all 4 segments filled in PYRAMID above, the RESPINS feature will be triggered. Initially 4 RESPINS will be awarded and RESPINS are played automatically. During RESPINS , WILD TABLETS should activate at the top position of each triggering reel. WILD TABLET symbols will stick and expand by one position downwards over the next four RESPIN games over the triggering reel. A WILD TABLETS reel ends after the 4 th RESPIN and the WILD TABLETS symbols will disappear at the start of the next game, resetting the PYRAMID segments of that associated reel to zero. RESPINS will re-trigger, if any of the pyramid landed in view during RESPINS filled with 4 segments above the reel. All winnings during RESPINS will be accumulated in TOTAL WIN and collected at the end of the game to BALANCE. WILD TABLETS: WILD TABLETS will be activated in RESPINS, FREE SPINS, and MEGA SPINS. WILD TABLETS add expanding sticky Wilds to the reel while active. WILD TABLET Symbol expands by one position downwards over the subsequent RESPIN for a triggering reel. Once the WILD TABLET Symbol has expanded to the bottom position, the feature ends and the WILD TABLET is removed on the following spin. FREE SPINS: FREE SPINS feature will be awarded when 3 CALENDAR symbols appear anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4 in the BASE GAME. Initially 5 FREE SPINS will be awarded and FREE SPINS feature will end when there are no WILD TABLET symbols in view. FREE SPINS are played automatically. FREE SPINS are played with a 2x BET MULTIPLIER. FREE SPINS feature starts with WILD TABLETS, which is initially active on the 3rd reel only. After each FREE SPIN, the WILD TABLET symbols decrease by 1 position downwards. Any further WILD symbols landing during FREE SPINS act as WILD TABLET which stick and expand downwards and resetting the FREE SPINS counter to the correct level. Regular WILD symbols will not appear behind WILD TABLET symbols. FREE SPINS feature is played at the selected BET of the triggering game. MEGA SPINS: MEGA SPINS feature can be awarded when the CALENDAR counter reaches the year 2022 in the BASE GAME and next time if 3 CALENDAR Symbols land in view, then MEGA SPINS feature is activated . Initially 5 MEGA SPINS will be awarded and MEGA SPINS feature will end when there are no WILD TABLET symbols in view. next time if 3 calendar symbols land in view, then mega spins feature is activated . MEGA SPINS feature starts with WILD TABLETS, on reels 2 and 4. After each MEGA SPIN, the WILD TABLET Symbols stick and expand downwards by 1 position. MEGA SPINS are played with 4x BET MULTIPLIER. Any further WILD symbols landing during MEGA SPINS act as WILD TABLET which stick and expand downwards and resetting the MEGA SPINS counter to the correct level. The CALENDAR year counter resets to the year 1922 after the completion of the MEGA SPINS feature. Regular WILD symbols will not appear behind WILD TABLET symbols. MEGA SPINS are played at the selected BET of the triggering game . PHARAOH’S POTS: The PHARAOH’S POTS feature is awarded randomly when one or more PYRAMID symbols appear on any of the 5 reels in BASE GAME. The PHARAOH’S POTS feature doesn't trigger during RESPINS. The PHARAOH’S POTS feature consists of a 4 level PYRAMID. The player starts on the LEVEL 1 (the lowest level) and can progress up the levels collecting cash. LEVEL 1 has 7 selection segments. LEVEL 2 has 5 selection segments. LEVEL 3 has 3 selection segments. LEVEL 4 has 1 selection segment. The player selects a segment at the current level. Each segment can reveal either a BET MULTIPER, PHARAOH’S POTS icon, BLANK or an UP ARROW. If the player reveals a, PHARAOH’S POTS icon: The player is awarded the corresponding PHARAOH’S POT amount. PHARAOH’S POTS feature ends. BLANK: The player is awarded another segment selection on the current active LEVEL. BET MULTIPLIER: A BET MULTIPLIER of 1x,2x,3x,4x or 5x is awarded. BET MULTIPLIER multiplies the current BET amount which is added to the PYRAMID POT display. At the end of this feature total value displayed in the PYRAMID POT is added to the player balance. ARROW: The player progresses up to the next LEVEL of the PYRAMID. If the player reaches LEVEL 4 (the top level) of the PYRAMID, it will award PHARAOH’S POT of 1000x. The PHARAOH’S POTS prizes are, LEVEL 1: 15x BET amount. LEVEL 2: 40x BET amount. LEVEL 3: 200x BET amount. LEVEL 4: 1000x BET amount.
