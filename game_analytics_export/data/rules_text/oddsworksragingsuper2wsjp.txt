Raging Super two Ways Smash Pots Overview Raging Super2Ways Smash Pots™ is a 5-reel, 5-row, cascading videoslot, with the following features SuperSymbols™ SuperStretch™ Smash Pots™ Raging Elephants Buffalo Stampede Wild Hippos Free spins with multiplying symbols Bonus Raid™ Symbols: 2 High-paying symbols: Lion, Cheetah 3 Medium symbols: Hyena, Springbok, Meerkat 3 Low symbols: Ace, King, Queen 3 feature symbols: Hippo, Buffalo, Elephant Wild: Serengeti scene SCATTER Multiplier symbol (free spins only) S, M, A, S & H jackpot symbols SuperSymbols™ Only SuperSymbols pay. To form a SuperSymbol a cluster of at least 2x2 of the same symbol type must land adjacent to each other. These will then combine into a single SuperSymbol. The symbol cannot be attached diagonally If one or more additional symbols of the same type is immediately adjacent to this SuperSymbol, they cause the SuperSymbol to further expand along that entire side. Any symbols that then become newly adjacent to the expanded SuperSymbol remain as individual symbols. After the panel evaluation all non-paying symbols are removed. Any SuperSymbols then fall to the lowest possible point. A cascade happens and the panel is again evaluated for new or expanding SuperSymbols.SuperSymbols of the same type that land on top of each other with the same width or adjacent to each other with the same height, will combine into a single SuperSymbol. When no new SuperSymbols are created or expanded, the round ends and wins are paid based on the final panel. SuperStretch™ If two or more expanded SuperSymbols would occupy the same reel positions, the highest-priority (most valuable) symbol shifts upward to create space and give it the best chance to be collected. Priority is: Bonus Symbols first, then SuperSymbols in paytable order from highest-paying to lowest-paying. New rows can be created when the top of the panel is pushed higher by a symbol expansion/movement upwards. The maximum number of paying rows is 15. Any shapes that reach that level are limited in size. The panel can extend past 15 rows to display final SuperSymbols, but the SuperSymbols cannot grow any further. Raging Elephants Triggered when between 1 and 4 Raging Elephant symbols land on an initial paid or free spin. Up to 4 elephant symbols can land. An elephant rages across the bottom of the panel, randomly from either left to right or right to left. This results in the panel on the impacted side stretching by 1 reel. Each instance of an elephant symbol expands the panel by an additional reel. Post charge, the elephant symbol becomes a wild symbol. The feature can extend the leftmost and rightmost reels up to 2 extra reels each. Buffalo Stampede Triggered when a Cape Buffalo symbol lands on an initial paid or free spin. Buffalos stampede across the bottom of the panel. Between 1 to 10 symbols crack and fall away one by one. The symbols above drop down. One or more new symbols are added at top of the column to replace those that were removed. SuperSymbols can be created and expanded as normal. The feature always results in a guaranteed win. Only one Cape Buffalo symbol can land per panel. Wild Hippos Triggered when a Hippo symbol lands on an initial paid or free spin. The Hippo symbol cannot land on the bottom row. The Hippo opens his mouth and chomping down while moving downwards to randomly converts all the symbols below it into a column of wild symbols. The original Hippo symbol then turns into a wild symbol, and then the normal symbol expansion/pay evaluation/cascade sequence takes place. Only a single Hippo symbol can land per panel. WILDS Substitute for any potentially winning symbol except Scatter and feature symbols, and Multiplier symbols (in free spin) and serves to create or expand SuperSymbols. Each wild can affect more than one SuperSymbol. Raging Free Spins Free spins are triggered when a cluster of at least 2x2 Bonus symbols is formed. The number of free spins awarded is equivalent to 2 X the number of Bonus symbols in the cluster. New Multiplier symbols can land with a 2x, 3x or 5x value. If a Multiplier symbol lands adjacent to a SuperSymbol, the SuperSymbol will absorb the multiplier value and the multiplier symbol will then be shown in a deactivated state. If a SuperSymbol is affected by more than 1 multiplier, then the result is the product of the multiplier values (e.g. a 2X and a 3X multiplier acting upon a SuperSymbol will multiply any wins by 6x). A SuperSymbol's multiplier cannot exceed x100 If a single multiplier lands adjacent to more than one SuperSymbol then each SuperSymbol is multiplied by the value of that multiplier. The multiplier must be adjacent to an initial symbol. The Multiplier symbol is removed from the panel with each cascade re-spin. If no new symbols land to cause a new or enlarged SuperSymbol, but a new Multiplier lands adjacent to one or more SuperSymbols then a new cascade will result. If two or more SuperSymbols of the same type combine, and one or more of them has a multiplier value, then: For when only one shape holds a multiplier: the newly combined SuperSymbol retains that same multiplier value For when more than one of the shapes hold a multiplier: the value of the multiplier of the newly combined SuperSymbol is equal to the sum of the multipliers of the SuperSymbols that joined (e.g. A SuperSymbol with a X2 multiplier combines with a SuperSymbol with a X3 multiplier to make a new SuperSymbol with a X5 multiplier) The Raging Elephant, Buffalo and Hippo features can be triggered during Free Spins. Free Spins can re-trigger - The number of extra free spins awarded is equivalent to 1 X the number of Bonus symbols in the cluster. Progressive Jackpot Land all five S-M-A-S-H jackpot symbols in reels 1 to 5 to trigger one of the progressive jackpots at its current value. The 3 progressive jackpots available in this game can be won by any player on a paid spin and are randomly triggered. Each pot starts at a fixed amount, namely the seed. The seed values are $5,000.00 - $5,000.00, $150.00 - $150.00, $50.00 - $50.00, for the Gold, Silver and Bronze pots respectively. Each bet placed in this game and any participating game that is linked to this jackpot contributes proportionally to the prize pots. The odds of winning the jackpot are proportional to the total bet amount; the bigger the bet, the better the chances to win. Overall game RTP is announced in the game help and the percentage of RTP related to the jackpot is noted separately. Any game interruption will not affect the jackpot contribution or awarding process. The player will be notified in case of winning the jackpot or in case of another player winning. The colour of the 'H' symbol in the 5th reel can be Bronze, Silver or Gold and determines which jackpot has been awarded. Simultaneous jackpot wins cannot occur. Should two players appear to win the jackpot at the same time the first player to trigger the jackpot will win the whole jackpot amount, the second player would win the reserve pot plus any further contributions made. The rules for Jackpot discontinuation and Jackpot fund allocation are defined by each Casino Operator at their own discretion.
