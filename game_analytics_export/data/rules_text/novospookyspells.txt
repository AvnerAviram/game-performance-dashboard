This 5 reel slot with 243 different ways to win, boasts three exciting Features. Firstly, a 4 level jackpot with a hold and spin feature. Collect Bats during the jackpot feature spins to increase your credits and a chance to collect Jackpot bonuses. If the screen is filled with Bats the Grand Jackpot is awarded. Secondly, in the thrilling Stacked Wild feature, get a full stack of wild Cauldrons on screen and the "Bonus Prize" selection is triggered. Thirdly, a Free Games feature with a chance to re-trigger! Main Game With Free Games, Wild Stacks and the Spooky Spells Jackpot feature game. Feature Free games Feature The scatter Spellbook symbol reveals the path to the Free Games round. Land three scatter Spellbook symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 and the Free Games Feature is triggered with 10 Free Games with ALL WINS x2. Free Games can be re-triggered. Stacked Wild with Bonus Prizes Land 3 wild Cauldron symbols stacked in full view on reels 2 or 4 to trigger the Bonus Prize feature. A Wild Stack on reel 2 or reel 4 will display 3 Cauldrons with one Bonus Prize selection. Wild Stacks on both reels 2 and 4 will reveal 6 Cauldrons with two Bonus Prize selections. The Bonus Prizes can be credit prizes of up to 10x total bet, a Jackpot award or 10 Free Games. Lucky Spin Jackpots Feature Look out for the lightning, when it appears on the screen get ready for frightfully epic wins. 6 or more Bat symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Spooky Spells Jackpots Feature. The triggering Bat symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Bat lands on the reels the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. All prizes on Bats are paid at the conclusion of the bonus spins, or if the screen is filled with Bats. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on the screen are filled with Bats, Bat prizes can award, Major, Minor or Mini Jackpots or up to 15x total bet. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Spell Book and Bats Scatter wins and allpay wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
