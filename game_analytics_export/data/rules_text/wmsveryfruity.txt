Very Fruity How to Play Game Introduction Very Fruity is a classical fruit game with a twist that is designed to deliver players lots of action and frequent rewards in a fresh style of game play. The reel has 20 win lines 5 Sun Scatter Symbols landing on any combination of reels trigger the Sun Bonus along with 5 Free Games. Malfunction voids all pays and play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Base Game Fixed 20 Lines play All wins left to right only except Sun Bonus Feature and Big Cherry Bonus. All wins on active lines only except Sun Bonus Feature and Big Cherry Bonus. Sun Bonus Feature wins and Big Cherry Bonus are added to line wins. Only highest winner paid on each payline. Wins on different lines are added together. All wins except Sun Bonus and Bonus prizes multiplied by bet per line. Sun Bonus 5 Sun Scatter Symbols on any combination of reels trigger the Sun Bonus along with 5 Free Games. The sun symbols each have a winning amount that is awarded immediately. The Sun Bonus is the total value of all Sun values combined. Free Games 5 or more scattered SUN award 5 free games. Each big cherry scatter that appears awards a value equal to the Sun Bonus that triggered that set of free games. Total bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the free games. Pay Lines The game contains 20 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. The expected return for this game is 95.51%.
