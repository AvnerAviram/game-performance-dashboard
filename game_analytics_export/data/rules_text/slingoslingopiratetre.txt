DURING EACH ROUND, ONE OF THREE TYPES OF SHIPS WILL APPEAR AND SAIL BY MOVING 1 SQUARE AT A TIME. THE PIRATE CAPTAIN WILL ORDER THE CANNONS ON THE HORIZONTAL AND VERTICAL REELS TO FIRE. EACH SHIP HIT WILL AWARD A MULTIPLIER PRIZE AND THE SHIP WILL BECOME DAMAGED. WHEN A SHIP IS HIT ENOUGH TIMES IT WILL SINK AND AWARD THE PLAYER A BONUS. THE FEATURE ENDS WHEN THE PLAYER RUNS OUT OF SPINS OR ALL SHIPS HAVE BEEN SUNK. SCATTER BONUS WHEEL THE BONUS WHEEL IS TRIGGERED BY 3 OR MORE SCATTER ICONS. THERE ARE 3 LEVELS OF BONUS WHEELS, GOLD, RUBY AND DIAMOND. THE GOLD WHEEL CAN TRIGGER THE RUBY WHEEL, AND THE RUBY WHEEL CAN TRIGGER THE DIAMOND WHEEL. THE MINIMUM WIN ON THE BONUS WHEEL IS X2 THE BASE STAKE. THE MAXIMUM WIN IS X100.
