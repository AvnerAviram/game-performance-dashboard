Lost City of Incas Game Rules General GAME HAS FIXED 25 LINES. ONLY HIGHEST WIN PAID PER SELECTED LINE. ALL WINS PAY LEFT TO RIGHT EXCEPT SCATTER WHICH PAYS ANY. ALL WINS MULTIPLIED BY BET PER LINE EXCEPT SCATTER. SCATTER WINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY TOTAL BET. SCATTER WINS ARE ADDED TO PAYLINE WINS. THE THEORETICAL PERCENTAGE RETURN TO PLAYER IS 96.497%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. Scatter Rules SCATTER WINS MULTIPLIED BY TOTAL BET STAKED SCATTER PAYS ANY SCATTER WINS ADDED TO PAYLINE WINS MYSTERY FEATURE: EACH REEL CNTAINS A STACK OF MYSTERY POSITIONS WHICH ARE REPLACED BY A GAME SYMBOL EXCEPT WILD AND SCATTER DURING EACH REEL SPINS. RANDOMLY THE MYSTERY STACKS CAN TURN INTO GOLD AND ALL SYMBOLS REVEALED BY MYSTERY WILL PAY 2X. IF A SYMBOL TURNS GOLDEN THEN ALL OCCURENCES OF THAT SYMBOL WILL TURN GOLDEN AND WILL PAY 2X. DURING FREE SPINS, ALL MYSTERY STACK ARE GOLD AND PAY 2X. TEMPLE OF THE SUN BONUS: SYMBOLS SCATTERED ANYWHERE ON THE REELS TRIGGER THE TEMPLE OF THE SUN BONUS ROUND. PLAYER IS AWARDED THE NUMBER OF PICKS EQUAL TO THE TRIGGERING SYMBOLS AND IS SHOWN A TOTAL OF 12 HIDDEN OPTIONS TO PICK FROM. EACH PICK REVEALS CERTAIN NUMBER OF FREE SPINS. PLAYER IS AWARDED THE SUM OF ALL REVEALED FREE SPINS AND CAN AWARD UP TO 19 FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS WIN UP TO 40,000 COINS FREE SPINS ARE PLAYED WITH DIFFERENT REEL STRIPS. DURING THE FREE SPINS, ALL SYMBOLS REVEALED BY MYSTERY STACKS ARE GOLD. A, K, Q AND JSYMBOLS WILL NOT APPEAR DURING FREE SPINS. DURING FREE SPINS IF 5, 4, 3 SCATTER SYMBOLS SCATTERED ANYWHERE ON THE REELS THEN PLAYER IS OFFERED A PICK FROM 3 HIDDEN OPTIONS WHICH CAN REVEAL 6, 8 OR 10 ADDITIONAL FREE SPINS. ALL BETS AND LINES PLAYED ARE THE SAME AS THOSE THAT STARTED THE TEMPLE OF THE SUN BONUS. FREE SPINS WINS ARE ADDED TO PAYLINE WINS. ACHIEVEMENT FEATURE AN ARTIFACT IS DISPLAYED NEXT TO THE REELS DURING THE BASE GAME. ANYTIME A NON-ROYAL SYMBOL(I.E. EMPEROR, WOMEN, CHEST, MAP, LLAMA, FLOWER) TURNS INTO GOLDEN, THE GAME AWARDS A PIECE OF THE ARTIFACT. ONCE AN ARTIFACT IS COMPLETED, IT IS STORED ON THE MAP DEPENDING UPON THE LEVEL, AND A NEW ARTIFACT APPEARS IN ITS PLACE. NEXT TO THE REELS DURING BASE GAME. THERE ARE A TOTAL OF 5 INCREASING LEVELS THAT CONSIST OF A SET NUMBER OF ARTIFACTS, NEXT LEVEL IS ACHIEVED WHEN ALL THE ARTIFACTS OF THAT LEVEL ARE REVEALED. AFTER COMPLETING 5 LEVELS, THE PLAYER GOES ON A NEVER ENDING QUEST. AFTER COLLECTING REQUIRED NUMBER OF ARTIFACTS, PICK ONE OF THE ARTIFACTS TO WIN A MULTIPLIER AWARD RANGING FROM 1X TO 20X WHICH GETS MULTIPLIED BY THE AVERAGE BET OF ALL SPINS SINCE THE LAST PICKING AWARD. ACHIEVEMENT AWARDS ARE EARNED ONLY IN THE BASE GAME AND ARE NOT AVAILABLE IN FREE SPINS. Level 1 : Each artifact requires 7 pieces to complete and the room contains 6 artifacts.
