2x3x Rush 2X 3X Rush 2X 3X Rush is a vibrant 3x4 game with 5 lines that are always active. This game combines classic slot symbols, a unique reelplay area, and the exciting potential of multipliers. Free Games are triggered by 3 or more Bonus symbols anywhere on the grid. The Free Games themselves are quite explosive as only 2 Bonus symbols are needing to re-trigger, often resulting in very high numbers of Free Games. GAME FEATURES Multiplier Feature • Wild 2X symbols appear on reels 1 and 4 • Wild 3X symbols appear on reels 2 and 3 • A win with 1 2X symbol doubles the payout; a win with 2 2X symbols quadruples it. • A win with 1 3X symbol triples the payout; a win with 2 3X symbols multiplies it by 9. • Combining 1 2X and 1 3X in a win multiplies the payout by 6. • Combining 2 2X and 1 3X in a win multiplies the payout by 12. • Combining 1 2X and 2 3X in a win multiplies the payout by 18. • Landing 2 2X and 2 3X on a payline awards the top jackpot. • A payline with 1 2X on reel 1 and 2 3X on reels 2 and 3 awards the second highest jackpot. Free Games • 4 Bonus symbols scattered across the reels award 20 Free Games. • 3 Bonus symbols scattered across the reels award 10 Free Games. • During Free Games, 4 Bonus symbols retrigger 20 additional Free Games. • During Free Games, 3 Bonus symbols retrigger 10 additional Free Games. • 2 Bonus symbols during Free Games provide an extra 5 Free Games. Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
