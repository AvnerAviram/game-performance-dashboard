The Book Main Game Your aim is to land at least 3 identical symbols on adjacent reels starting from left to right. Clumped Bonus symbols trigger a clumped x3 Free Spins Bonus Feature and the Magician's Book can award Wild symbols randomly on any spin of the reels. Clumped x3 Free Spins Bonus Feature When Clumped x3 Free Spins Bonus Feature symbols land on reels 2, 3 and 4 you win Free Spins. Three to nine Free Spin symbols on the reels award between 5 and up to as many as 135 Free Spins. It is possible to win more Free Spins during a Free Spins Bonus round. 3x3x3 Free Spin symbols = 135 Free Spins 3x3x2 Free Spin symbols = 90 Free Spins 3x2x2 Free Spin symbols = 60 Free Spins 3x3x1 Free Spin symbols = 45 Free Spins 2x2x2 Free Spin symbols = 40 Free Spins 3x2x1 Free Spin symbols = 30 Free Spins 2x2x1 Free Spin symbols = 20 Free Spins 3x1x1 Free Spin symbols = 15 Free Spins 2x1x1 Free Spin symbols = 10 Free Spins 1x1x1 Free Spin symbols = 5 Free Spins Free Spins are played on reels in a different setting, which the Magician's Book will reveal. If the book reveals a Mouse symbol, the Free Spins will be played in the kitchen of a French bistro, the Lion symbol represents the African savannah and the Dragon symbol represents the medieval castle setting. The symbol that the book reveals (Mouse, Lion or Dragon) will become a Wild symbol during that round of the Clumped x3 Free Spins Feature only. Magical Wild Book Feature An Altering Wild symbol may be awarded on any spin of the reels in this game. On any spin, there is a chance that one of the regular card symbols (10/J/Q/K/A) may be randomly assigned as a Wild symbol for that spin. When this feature is active, the pages of the Wild Magic Book will turn and reveal a symbol (10/J/Q/K/A) that will act as a Wild symbol for that spin. Every symbol on the reels that matches the designated Wild symbol becomes a Wild symbol on the active reels for that spin only. The Wild symbols can substitute for any regular symbol to complete winning combinations. Rules Only the highest winning combination per active win line pays. Symbols must be on adjacent reels, on win lines, starting on the most left reel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
