Cake Off Free Spins Feature During Free Spins play the Cake Off Vote. 5 in view awards 20 Free Spins. 4 in view awards 15 Free Spins. 3 in view awards 10 Free Spins. Before Free Spins commence, the player chooses one of the 5 cakes. The player makes their selection, then presses start to begin the Free Spins. After every Free Spin, each cake in view is multiplied by the current stake (€2 in High Roller games) and added to the corresponding cake's bonus pot. At the end of Free Spins, the cake with the highest bonus pot is declared the winner. It is possible to have multiple winners. The winning cake's bonus pots are added to the total of any win line wins that have been accumulated and the final amount awarded to the player. If any of the winning cakes match the chosen cake, the bonus pot for that cake is doubled and awarded to the player. If 1 or 2 scatter symbols land in view they add to the 'Stick or Swap' trail. When the trail is full 'Stick or Swap' is awarded. 'Stick or Swap' gives the player the opportunity to either 'Stick' with the existing selected cake or 'Swap' to another cake. When the 'Stick or Swap' bonus is completed, the trail is cleared and can be filled again during any remaining Free Spins. It is possible to complete the trail on the last Free Spin. If 3 or more scatters land in view on any single Free Spin the stated number of Free Spins are added to any remaining Free Spins. Free Spins reels only contain cake symbols and scatter symbols. Each Free Spins reel contains an equal number of cake symbols. If 'Stick or Swap' is achieved on the last Free Spin and the player's currently selected cake is not the winning cake then the player's selection is automatically swapped to a winning cake. Autoplay Select your number of spins and a loss limit to enable the start autoplay button. You can select the generated loss limit or enter a custom value. Autoplay will stop if the number of spins is completed or the loss limit is reached. Your loss limit is reached when your net losses are equal to the value selected or a further spin would increase net losses higher than set loss limit. Additionally you may enter a win limit that stops autoplay when a single game win is equal to or exceeds the value.
