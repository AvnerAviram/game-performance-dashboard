Ultra Hot Deluxe Ultra Hot™ deluxe is the epitome of a classic slot! With its seven colourful fruit symbols, it screams the flair of a typical "Fruit Machine" or "Fruity", as they are affectionately known. Three reels spin in your chances of making huge winnings! Main Game The aim of Ultra Hot™ deluxe is to get the same symbol showing on all 3 reels. In the regular game, winning combinations run left to right across the paylines. To land a win, a complete winning combination must occupy a payline, without any other symbols coming between the winning symbols. Same fruit symbols on all positions double the scheduled prize Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Symbols pay from left to right and must be consecutive. All wins pay only on lines in play. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
