Timeless Rose RTP: 92.0 - 97.0% VOLATILITY: 16.5 – 16.9 MEDIUM GAME RULES During the base game Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose are wild and substitute for all non-wild symbols except Bonus Symbols. Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose appear only on reels 2, 3, and 4. During the base game Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, or Red Rose appearing in any position on the reels awards a multiplier value. The multiplier value awarded depends on the Spin-crease feature. If more than one Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, or Red Rose appears, the sum of the multiplier values is awarded. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the game level increases. Initially, Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose award 2x, 2x, 2x, and 3x multiplier values, respectively. When certain game level thresholds are reached, the multiplier values awarded by Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose increase. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 3x after reaching game level 25. The multiplier value awarded by Pink Rose increases to 3x after reaching game level 50. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 4x after reaching game level 100. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 4x after reaching game level 250. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 5x after reaching game level 500. The multiplier value awarded by Purple Rose increases to 3x after reaching game level 1000. The multiplier value awarded by Pink Rose increases to 4x after reaching game level 2500. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 5x after reaching game level 5000. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 6x after reaching game level 10000. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as Spin-crease points are accumulated. Each bet placed awards 20 Spin-crease points times the bet multiplier. The total amount of Spin-crease points accumulated is counted across all sessions played on the current user account. Initially, Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose award 2x, 2x, 2x, and 3x multiplier values, respectively. The Spin-crease meter fills as Spin-crease points are accumulated. When the Spin-crease meter fills completely, the multiplier values awarded by Purple Rose, Pink Rose, Yellow Rose, and Red Rose increase. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 3x after 2500 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Pink Rose increases to 3x after 10000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 4x after 35000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 4x after 130000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 5x after 500000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Purple Rose increases to 3x after 1850000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Pink Rose increases to 4x after 7000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Yellow Rose increases to 5x after 25000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. The multiplier value awarded by Red Rose increases to 6x after 100000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. After 100000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated the meter will remain visible and display 100% complete. The rate of the Spin-crease progress is automatically adjusted based on the exchange rate of the currency in play. This game is played at 243 ways. All 5 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Values displayed in the [Game Rules/Help Screens] have not been pre-multiplied, except where stated. Wins subject to verification. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 Bonus Symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Bonus Select. Awards 1 pick. Bonus Symbols is a scatter symbol. Bonus Symbols appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Free Games Bonus is triggered during the Bonus Select. The Bonus Select features 3 options. If option 1 was chosen Golden Roses appears with an 8x, 10x, 12x, or 15x multiplier value. If option 2 was chosen Golden Roses appears with a 6x, 7x, 8x, or 10x multiplier value. If option 3 was chosen Golden Roses appears with a 4x, 5x, 6x, or 7x multiplier value. The Bonus Select ends when 0 picks remain. The Free Games Bonus begins immediately afterwards. Golden Roses is wild and substitutes for all non-wild symbols. Golden Roses appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. During the Free Games Bonus, Golden Roses appearing in any position on the reels appears with and awards one of four multiplier values. The multiplier values that Golden Roses can appear with depend on which option was selected during the Bonus Select. If option 1 was chosen Golden Roses appears with an 8x, 10x, 12x, or 15x multiplier value. If option 2 was chosen Golden Roses appears with a 6x, 7x, 8x, or 10x multiplier value. If option 3 was chosen Golden Roses appears with a 4x, 5x, 6x, or 7x multiplier value. If more than one Golden Roses appears, the sum of the multiplier values is awarded. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same ways at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus.
