Rickety Cricket Game Rules Play 1 to 25 lines LINES can be changed by using the up and down arrows BET amount can be changed by using the up and down arrows SPIN begins the game with the current BET and LINES BET MAX begins the game with the maximum LINES and current BET Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE Payline wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins Feature wins are added to scatter and payline wins Win combinations pay from left to right Flower appears on All Reels and substitutes for all symbols except Bonus and Scatter Bonus symbol appears on Reels 2,3 and 4 only. 2x bet scatter pay for 3 Bonus Symbols in the base game only Bonus Round Feature The Free Spin Bonus feature is awarded when 3 Bonus symbols appear in any position on reels 2,3,4 Player is awarded 8 free games with a random game multiplier Five game multipliers are possible: x2,x3,x4,x5,x8 Feature can be retriggered, and an additional 8 games will be awarded up to a maximum of 98 games SCATTER Feature The Rickety Cricket Scatter Feature functions very differently from traditional scatter pays The feature is initiated by the Scatter Trigger appearing on reel 1 Bug Zapper symbol The Scatter Trigger appears on reel 1 only Once the Scatter Trigger appears in any position on reel one, reels 2,3,4,5 are analysed for TOTAL QUANTITY of Queen Bee, Fire Fly, and Mosquito That total number is then compared to the scatter feature paytable to pay the corresponding award Paytable
