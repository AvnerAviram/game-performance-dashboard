Just Jewels Deluxe Rules All pays are for adjacent symbols of a kind on selected lines except 'Euro', which pays any. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Main Game Ten paylines across five reels await you. The win pattern may start on any reel and follow the paylines from left to right and vice versa. The 'Euro' symbol is the Scatter symbol and it wins in any position. Game Theme and Graphics If you desire diamonds and crave stacks of cash, then Just Jewels™ deluxe is the game for you. Spin the reels and find the winning combinations.
