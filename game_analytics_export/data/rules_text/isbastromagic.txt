Astro Magic How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different selected lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each selected line is paid. Winnings from Scatter and bonus rounds are added to the total win. Flash version Note : On the info bar located under the main game buttons, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. To play, follow these steps: Use the coin value select button to choose a coin value. The coin value select button is located in the bottom-left side of the main game screen and shows the currently selected coin value. Use the LINES button to choose the number of active lines. Note : The current bet amount, in coins, is displayed in the BET box. Click SPIN to place the bet and spin the reels once. You can click the BET MAX button to place the maximum bet (all lines active) for the currently selected coin value and spin the reels once. The total amount won, in coins, is shown in the WIN box. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the paytable for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN), and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. Special Symbols and Features Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except Scatter and any bonus symbols. Scatter Scatters pay anywhere on the reels and award: 2 Scatter symbols, 2 x total bet 3 Scatter symbols, 5 x total bet 4 Scatter symbols, 25 x total bet 5 Scatter symbols, 200 x total bet Note : The Scatter winnings shown in the paytable are in coins and already multiplied by the total bet. Instant bonus 3, 4 or 5 Instant bonus symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Instant bonus round. During this type of bonus round, click or tap each Instant bonus symbol in order to win a random prize. Each Instant bonus symbol can award: a random number of free spins, between 1 and 10 a random multiplier of the total bet, between 5 x total bet and 50 x total bet Note : During free spins, you cannot win additional free spins. Galaxy bonus 3, 4 or 5 Galaxy bonus symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Galaxy bonus round. During this type of bonus round, you can win a random number of coins, between 15 and 550 x total bet. Autospin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the autospin feature automatically when the selected limit plus your cumulative winnings minus your cumulative bets is less than 0. Note : The autoplay feature does not automatically start any bonus rounds – player input is required. Flash version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window where you can set up the autospin feature. On the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window, you can set up the following: Loss limit - enter a number for the loss limit value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the loss limit value Stop if WIN reaches - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side and then enter a number for the WIN value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the WIN value; click the button on its left side again to disable it Stop if JACKPOT is won - (only for games with Progressive Jackpots or with a specific Jackpot value displayed) - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side; click the button on its left side again to disable it Number of AUTO SPINS to play - use the left and the right arrow buttons to select the number of autospins Click START to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window and start the autospin feature with the currently selected bet and number of autospins or click CANCEL to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window without starting the autospin feature. Click the STOP xx/yy button to stop the autospin feature at any time; xx represents the remaining number of autospins and yy represents the number of autospins to play that you selected on the AUTO SPINS SETTINGS window.
