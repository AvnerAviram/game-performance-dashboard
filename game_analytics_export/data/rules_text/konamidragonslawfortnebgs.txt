Dragon's Law Fortune Bags BASIC GAME RULES MINIMUM BET IS 88 CREDITS. ALL WINS ARE SHOWN IN CURRENCY EXCEPT CREDIT PRIZES SHOWN. LINE WINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY CREDITS WAGERED PER LINE.THE GRAND OR MAJOR MAY BE WON RANDOMLY ON ANY BOUGHT GAME. THE CHANCE OF WINNING GRAND AND MAJOR WILL CHANGE WITH THE CREDITS BET. THE VALUES FOR GRAND AND MAJOR SCALE WITH THE DENOMINATION. THE VALUES FOR MINOR BONUS AND MINI BONUS WILL SCALE WITH THE DENOMINATION AND CREDITS BET. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. A PLAYER CANNOT INFLUENCE THE RESULT OF A GAME BY STOPPING THE REEL SPINS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING CORRECT CREDITS ARE REGISTERED BEFORE PLAY. THE GAME DISPLAY DOES NOT INDICATE HOW CLOSE YOU WERE TO WINNING, AND CANNOT NECESSARILY BE USED TO DETERMINE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING OR LOSING IF YOU CONTINUE TO PLAY. FEATURE GAME RULES (DRAGON'S LAW FEATURE) RANDOMLY, EXTRA [WILD] SYMBOLS APPEAR ON THE REELS DURING OR AFTER THE REEL SPIN. IF EXTRA [WILD] STOPS IN THE SAME POSITION AS [RED TRIGGER], [BLUE TRIGGER], [GOLD TRIGGER] OR [PURPLE TRIGGER], THEY WILL CHANGE TO [RED WILD&TRIGGER], [BLUE WILD&TRIGGER], [GOLD WILD&TRIGGER] OR [PURPLE WILD&TRIGGER] RESPECTIVELY. [RED WILD&TRIGGER] SUBSTITUTES FOR [WILD] AND [RED TRIGGER]. [BLUE WILD&TRIGGER] SUBSTITUTES FOR [WILD] AND [BLUE TRIGGER]. [GOLD WILD&TRIGGER] SUBSTITUTES FOR [WILD] AND [GOLD TRIGGER]. [PURPLE WILD&TRIGGER] SUBSTITUTES FOR [WILD] AND [PURPLE TRIGGER]. FREE GAME FEATURES WHENEVER 1 OR 2 [RED TRIGGER] APPEAR THERE IS A CHANCE THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE MAY BE TRIGGERED. WHENEVER 1 OR 2 [BLUE TRIGGER] APPEAR THERE IS A CHANCE THE DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE MAY BE TRIGGERED. WHENEVER 1 OR 2 [GOLD TRIGGER] APPEAR THERE IS A CHANCE THE JACKPOT FEATURE MAY BE TRIGGERED. 3 OR MORE [RED TRIGGER], [BLUE TRIGGER] OR [GOLD TRIGGER] TRIGGERS THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE, DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE OR JACKPOT FEATURE RESPECTIVELY. IF ONLY THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE IS TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF ONLY THE DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE IS TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF ONLY THE JACKPOT FEATURE IS TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE JACKPOT FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF ONLY THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE AND DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE ARE TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE DOUBLE ACTION DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF ONLY THE DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE AND JACKPOT FEATURE ARE TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE DOUBLE ACTION JACKPOT FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF ONLY THE DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE AND JACKPOT FEATURE ARE TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE DOUBLE GAMES JACKPOT FEATURE IS AWARDED. IF DOUBLE ACTION FEATURE, DOUBLE GAMES FEATURE AND JACKPOT FEATURE ARE TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIMARY GAME, THE TRIPLE FEATURE IS AWARDED. ALL [RED TRIGGER], [BLUE TRIGGER] AND [GOLD TRIGGER] ARE REPLACED WITH [PURPLE TRIGGER] DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURES. IF THE DRAGON'S LAW FEATURE IS TRIGGERED DURING THE FEATURE, EXTRA [WILD] SYMBOL WILL APPEAR ON THE REELS DURING AND/OR AFTER THE REEL SPIN. CREDITS BET PER LINE AND LINES PLAYED ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FEATURE. LETTER PRIZES AND BONUS CREDIT PRIZE ARE EQUIVALENT TO [WILD] AND [PURPLE WILD&TRIGGER]. CREDITS BET PER LINE AND LINES PLAYED ON ALL SET ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FEATURE. FEATURE GAME RULES (JACKPOT FEATURE) 10 FREE GAMES AWARDED. 3, 4 OR 5 [PURPLE] ADD 8, 12 OR 20 FREE GAMES RESPECTIVELY. EVERY [WILD] THAT APPEARS DURING THE FEATURE WILL AWARD A LETTER OR BONUS CREDIT PRIZE AFTER THE REEL SPIN. POSSIBLE LETTER PRIZES FOR THE GRAND JACKPOT ARE G, R, A , N AND D. POSSIBLE LETTER PRIZES FOR THE MAJOR JACKPOT ARE M, A, J, O AND R. POSSIBLE LETTER PRIZES FOR THE MINOR JACKPOT ARE M, I, N, O, AND R. POSSIBLE LETTER PRIZES FOR THE MINI JACKPOT ARE M, I, N AND I. POSSIBLE BONUS CREDIT PRIZES ARE 88 MULTIPLIED BY THE BET PER LINE. IF ALL LETTER PRIZES FOR A JACKPOT OR BONUS ARE COLLECTED, THE CORRESPONDING JACKPOT OR BONUS IS AWARDED AND COLLECTED LETTER PRIZES FOR THAT JACKPOT OR BONUS ARE RESET. PROGRESSIVE METER AMOUNT IS RESET EACH TIME IT HAS BEEN PAID, BEFORE ANY OTHER PROGRESSIVES ARE PAID. DIFFERENT REELS ARE USED DURING THE FEATURE. CREDITS BET PER LINE AND LINES PLAYED ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FEATURE.
