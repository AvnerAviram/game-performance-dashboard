Silver Luxe Coin Run Main Game The main game is played on 1, 2 or 3 reels and 1 win line. All values on the Coin-symbols which land on the win line, will add up and the result will be multiplied by any eventual multiplier. When a spin only results in 1 or 2 multipliers, the multipliers will be held and a free re-spin will be offered for an extra chance for a win. Try to combine bonuses and multipliers to give your winnings a boost! Whilst playing the main game, the progressive Free Games Meter, positioned on the big coin, will increase once in a while. The Free Games Feature will start randomly but must start when the meter hits 15. Free Games Feature During the Free Games Feature you really start to win big. The games are not only for free but all wins pay scattered as well! Silver Lux Linked Progressive Jackpot At the top of the game two progressive jackpots are presented, a Major Jackpot and a Grand Jackpot. Both jackpots are linked to the jackpots of other Silver Lux games. A Coin-symbol with a "grand" label and a Coinsymbol with a "major" label are present on the 3rd reel only. When one of these symbols land on the win line the respective jackpot is yours! Rules All wins pay at any position on the win line. All wins are presented in credits, except the Grand and Major Jackpot which are presented in the actual currency. Scatter wins and line wins are added. More than 1 Multiplier symbol may be included in a winning combination, when this happens, each multiplier will multiply the winnings. The multipliers don't multiply the Grand Jackpot or Major Jackpot. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 7/26/2024
