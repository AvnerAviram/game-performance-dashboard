Deck the halls About Deck the Halls Deck the Halls is a five reel, thirty payline, and three hundred coin slot machine, with: A wild, multiplier symbol. A scatter symbol. A Free Spins bonus game. [top] Game Payouts Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. · Regular Payouts All payout tables display the number of coins that are won for each combination. The total number of coins paid out depends on the number of coins you play per payline. Regular wins are calculated by taking the number of coins won, multiplied by the number of coins played per payline. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size you select. For a combination of regular symbols to produce a winning regular combination, the following criteria must be met: · The symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. · Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Scatter Symbol Payouts Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on an enabled payline to win. For a combination of scatter symbols to produce a winning scatter combination, the following criteria must be met: Completes a winning scatter combination when two or more appear on the reels. Cannot be substituted by the wild, multiplier symbol. Free Spins Bonus Game Payouts You are awarded ten free spins. All wins are doubled during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. [top] Wild, Multiplier Symbol The Deck the Halls symbol is a wild, multiplier symbol. This symbol: Doubles the payout of any combination it completes as a wild symbol. Creates its own winning combination when multiple symbols appear in a line on an enabled payline. These payouts are not multiplied. Does not substitute for the scatter symbol. During the Free Spins bonus game, this symbol creates a wild symbol stack. Scatter Symbol The Bells symbol is a scatter symbol. This symbol: Completes a winning scatter combination when two or more appear on the reels. Activates the Free Spins bonus game when three or more appear on the five reels. Cannot be substituted by the wild, multiplier symbol. Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the scatter symbol combination payout by the total number of regular coins bet. Free Spins Bonus Game Three or more Bells symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. In this game: You are awarded ten free spins. All wins are doubled during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. So, if three or more Bells symbols are scattered on the reels during the free spins, you are awarded additional free spins. The extra free spins are added to the remaining number of free spins. The Deck the Halls symbol: Awards a 4x multiplier value on the payout of any combination it completes. Creates a wild symbol stack. The Santa symbol creates a symbol stack. To play the bonus game: 1. The reels automatically start spinning the free spins. 2. The free spins are complete. Your total bonus win amount is counted in the bonus Win box. 3. You are automatically returned to the regular slot game. Playing the game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on the payline. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. To play: 1. Click Lines to select the number of paylines you want to play. 2. Place a bet. How? Click Increase (+) or Decrease (-) to select the coin size you want to bet. To increase the number of coins you want to bet, click Coins . To bet the maximum number of coins permitted, click Bet Max . The number of coins bet increases to the maximum and the game automatically starts playing. 3. Click Spin . The reels start spinning. If you have a winning combination lined up on the payline, the win is highlighted on the reels. You are paid out the win amount as displayed in the Win box. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again.
