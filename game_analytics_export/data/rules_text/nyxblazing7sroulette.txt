Blazing 7s Roulette OVERVIEW At the start of each game, a random number is drawn from 0 to 36, including 00. The draw result will be shown where the ball comes to rest on the wheel. If a successful selection is made, the winning bet will be multiplied by the win odds and awarded. The side bet is activated if the ball lands on 7, 17, or 27. HOW TO BET Select chip values from the chip selector and place chips on the table by selecting positions on the table. Alternatively, the neighbours bet view may be used. HOW TO PLAY To begin the game, press the 'SPIN' button. Select the 'NEIGHBOURS' button to enter the neighbours bet view. Select the 'REBET' button to repeat the previous bet. Select the 'CLEAR' button to remove all current chips from the table. Select the 'DOUBLE' button to double the bet currently on the table. Select the 'UNDO' button to undo the last bet action on the table. NEIGHBOURS Select the amount of adjacent numbers by pressing the '+' and '-' buttons. A chip placed on the neighbours view will automatically add chips on each side equal to the number in the centre. SIDE BET The side bet is activated if the ball lands on 7, 17, or 27. Three additional random numbers will be drawn from 0 to 36, including 00. The draw results will be shown where the three balls come to rest on the wheel. The Player is then paid out on the result according to the below paytable.
