Sharky's Choices - Win Ways Game Theme and Graphics Delve deep into the colorful world of Sharky with up to 117649 ways to win. Fish for Fixed Wilds or Random Wilds or let yourself be surprised by the Mystery Choice Feature to get huge amounts of winnings. Main Game Sharky's Choices is an all pay game (no win lines) played on 6 reels with 2-7 symbols on each reel. There is no fixed symbol size and they can change on each spin. With 3-6 scattered Bonus symbols in any position on the screen you will trigger the Feature selection in which you are able to choose between the Fixed Wilds Feature, Mystery Choice Feature and Random Wilds Feature. Feature Fixed Wilds Feature 3/4/5/6 Scatter trigger 7/12/15/20 Free Games respectively. Appearing wilds stay on its reel until the end of the feature. Wilds appear on reels 2-5 only. Wild Multipliers can be x1, x2 or x3. Up to 7 Wild symbols may appear randomly on reels 2-5, whereby 2 Wilds are guaranteed. 1/2/3/4/5/6 Scatter/Bonus symbols trigger 1/2/3/5/7/10 additional Free Games respectively. Mystery Choice Feature The player has the option to choose the Mystery Choice Feature. If he or she chooses this option, one of the two Wild Features is chosen randomly and the number of awarded Free Games is also chosen randomly. 3 Bonus symbols can randomly award 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 12, 15, 17 or 20 Free Games. 4 Bonus symbols can randomly award 10, 11, 12, 13, 15, 17, 20, 22 or 25 Free Games. 5 Bonus symbols can randomly award 13, 14, 15, 16, 18, 20, 22, 25 or 30 Free Games. 6 Bonus symbols can randomly award 18, 19, 20, 21, 23, 25, 27, 30 or 35 Free Games. 1/2/3/4/5/6 Scatter/Bonus symbols trigger 1/2/3/5/7/10 additional Free Games respectively. Random Wilds Feature 3/4/5/6 Scatter/Bonus symbols trigger 15/18/25/30 Free Games respectively. 0-6 Wilds on random positions on reels 2-5 in each spin. Wild multipliers are x2 or x3. 1/2/3/4/5/6 Scatter/Bonus symbols trigger 1/2/3/5/7/10 additional Free Games respectively Rules Win Ways (no win lines) 6 reels No fixed symbol sizes.
