Pinata Bucks Game Title Pinata Bucks is a Bet 10, 1296 ways 5 reel slot game (with Reelfecta), with a Pinata Lock Spins Bonus. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1,2 and 3. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.74% with any bet. Game Rules PINATA LOCK SPINS BONUS Player is awarded 5 Pinata Lock Spins. Pinata Lock Spins are played using an alternate 4x4 reel grid. Any {BLUE PINATA} that lands will lock in for the duration of the Bonus. {BLUE PINATA} only appears in Pinata Lock Spins. Whenever locked {BLUE PINATA} form a square they will combine into a larger locked {BLUE PINATA}. After 5 spins each {BLUE PINATA} will be smashed to award prizes. 1x1, 2x2, 3x3 and 4x4 {BLUE PINATA} award prizes shown below. ADDITIONAL RULES 10 Coins plays 1296 ways. All wins shown at selected bet. Sun is WILD and substitutes for all symbols, except SCATTER. Sun appears on reel 3 and during base games only. 6 or more SCATTERS trigger Pinata Lock SpinsBonus. For all symbols, winning combinations pay through any position reel 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 respectively. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier.
