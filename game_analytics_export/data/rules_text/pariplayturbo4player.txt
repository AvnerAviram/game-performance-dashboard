Turbo4Player Game Rules Turbo4Player kicks classic slots up a notch with up to 108 win lines of progressive jackpot action. Win lines crisscross the reels and the quick turnover jackpot guarantees minimum and maximum payouts! Choose from four different game modes, PLUS a superbet mode. Jokers award mystery win prizes in all of the game modes, with turbocharged mystery win prizes in superbet mode! Try your chances at a BIG WIN or a MEGA WIN in this hot new 27 win lines per game (up to 108 win lines total), 3 to 6 reel, 3 row video slot game. The colorful reels are filled with retro fruit symbols, bells, bars, sevens, stars, and jokers. Choose from five different game play modes that each have a different number of reels, win lines, and bet options. The five (5) different game mode options are: GAME 1: 3 Reels / 27 Win Lines / 1 Coin Minimum Bet GAME 2 (1+2): 4 Reels / 54 Win Lines / 2 Coin Minimum Bet GAME 3 (1+2+3): 5 Reels / 81 Win Lines / 3 Coin Minimum Bet GAME 4 (1+2+3+4): 6 Reels / 108 Win Lines / 4 Coin Minimum Bet GAME 5 (1+2+3+4+SUPERBET): 6 Reels / 108 Win Lines + SUPERBET FEATURE / 8 Coin Minimum Bet Game features include JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS that award MYSTERY WINS in all of the different game modes. In SUPERBET mode, JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS award higher value MYSTERY WIN prizes and the prize values increase based upon how many JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS are visible on the reels. All bets feed a PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT that features guaranteed minimum and maximum payouts. The odds of winning the PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT increase with higher stakes. Click/Tap the SPIN or AUTO PLAY BUTTONS to start playing. Activate MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. You can also use these buttons to choose to play GAME 1, GAME 2, GAME 3, GAME 4, or GAME 5 (SUPERBET). The theoretical minimum payback percentage for this game is 95.00 % GAME 1, 95.00 % GAME 2, 95.00 % GAME 3, 95.00 % GAME 4, and 95.00 % GAME 5 (SUPERBET). Disclaimer: Only the highest winning combination per active win line pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. Features Mystery Win Symbols JOKER SYMBOLS are MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS in every game mode, including SUPERBET mode. In games 1 – 4, three (3) JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS on adjacent reels awards a MYSTERY WIN prize. In SUPERBET mode, two (2) to six (6) JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS scattered anywhere on the reels awards a MYSTERY WIN prize. In SUPERBET mode, the values of the MYSTERY WIN prizes are higher than in games 1 – 4, and the prize values increase as the number of triggering JOKER MYSTERY WIN SYMBOLS increase. Mystery Jackpot – PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT Every time any player plays the game, 0.5% of every bet is added to the MYSTERY JACKPOT (PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT). The minimum guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout is 500 EUR. There is a guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout when it hits the maximum jackpot value of 5,000 EUR.
