Philly Slots GAME RULES Double Jackpot Wild, Teal Jackpot Wild, and Black Jackpot Wild symbols are wild and substitute for seven and bar symbols that also land on the payline. One Double Jackpot Wild symbol in a winning sevens or bars combination pays 2x prize value. Two Double Jackpot Wild symbols in a winning sevens or bars combination pays 4x prize value. Only highest winner paid per line. DOUBLE JACKPOT PROGRESSIVE When 3 Wild symbols land on an active payline, the associated Progressive tier is awarded. See Progressive paytable for prize values. Top Progressive is available only at max lines bet. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers.
