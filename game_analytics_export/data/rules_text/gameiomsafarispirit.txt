Safari Spirit How to play The ‘TOTAL BET’ is the ‘BET’ multiplied by the number of ‘LINES’. GAME HISTORY: This can be viewed by clicking on the ‘clock’ button while in real play mode. RESULT: Any wins are indicated by the highlighted lines are symbols animating wherever applicable. Wins are show in the message box. The game rules are identical in both real play and demo play modes. The player is responsible for checking the correct bet has been registered before commencing play. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules ‘Elephant’ substitutes for all symbols except scatters. ‘Landscape’ is a scatter. ‘Elephant’ appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only. All wins begin with left most reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Wins on different lines are added. The game has return to player of 94.32%. Free Games Feature 20, 12 or 8 free games are triggered by 5, 4 or 3 scattered ‘Landscape’ respectively. During the Free Games Feature, any 3 or more scattered ‘Landscape’ will trigger an additional 5 free games. During the Free Games Feature, Paw wins are multiplied by 2. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/8/2020
