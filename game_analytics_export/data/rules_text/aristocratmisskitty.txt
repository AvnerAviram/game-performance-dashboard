Miss Kitty Autoplay AUTO PLAY AUTOMATICALLY INITIATES A SPECIFIED NUMBER OF BOUGHT GAMES AT THE CURRENT TOTAL BET. CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET AND PRESS AUTOPLAY PRESS EXIT TO RETURN TO GAME WITHOUT INITIATING AUTO PLAY SPINS. SELECT A NUMBER OF AUTO PLAY SPINS. PRESS START TO IMMEDIATELY COMMENCE PLAY. THE SELECTED NUMBER OF GAMES WILL BE PLAYED WHILE THERE IS SUFFICIENT BALANCE ON THE CREDIT METER OR UNTIL FREE GAMES ARE TRIGGERED. DURING AUTO PLAY, PRESS stop TO CANCEL REMAINING AUTO PLAY SPINS. STICKY WILD FREE GAMES FEATURE WHEN 3 SCATTERED APPEAR 10 FREE GAMES ARE WON. PRESS THE PLAY BUTTON TO START THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. THE FREE GAMES WILL START AUTOMATICALLY AFTER 2 MINUTES. APPEARING DURING THE FREE GAMES STAYS IN THE WINDOW AT THAT POSITION FOR ANY Wild THE REMAINDER OF THE FREE GAMES AND SUBSTITUTES FOR ALL SYMBOLS EXCEPT SCATTERS. AS INDICATED BY ALTERNATING DISPLAY OF THESE SYMBOLS. FREE GAMES FEATURE CAN BE TRIGGERED AGAIN DURING THE FREE GAMES FEATURE ONCE ONLY, WITH 5 FREE GAMES AWARDED. TOTAL BET IS THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. Press ANY button other than BACK and NEXT to return to game. GAME RULES ALL WINS SHOWN IN CREDITS. 50 LINES ARE PLAYED FOR LINE COST OF 25 CREDITS. 1 CREDIT BUYS 2 LINES! MINIMUM BET 25 CREDITS. CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET. TOTAL BET IS THE LINE COST MULTIPLIED BY THE BET MULTIPLIER. SELECT +,- or MAX BET TO CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET. THE PLAY BUTTON WILL IMMEDIATELY COMMENCE PLAY. WHILE THE REELS ARE SPINNING YOU MAY PRESS THE PLAY BUTTON TO STOP THEM. TOUCH CREDIT, TOTAL BET OR WIN METER TO SHOW IN CURRENCY OR CREDITS. ALL WINS ON PLAYED LINES ONLY EXCEPT SCATTERS. WINS ON DIFFERENT LINES ARE ADDED. HIGHEST WIN ONLY ON EACH LINE. SCATTER WINS ARE ADDED TO LINE WINS. LINE WINS MULTIPLIED BY BET MULTIPLIER. SCATTER WINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY THE TOTAL CREDITS BET.. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. Last modification date: 5/23/2023
