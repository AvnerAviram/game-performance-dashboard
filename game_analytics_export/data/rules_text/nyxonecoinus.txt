One Coin The game is played on one reel. All Bonus symbols pay only when drawn in the central position of the reel, being completely visible. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-5x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols award corresponding Jackpots. Mystery Cash symbol awards prizes ranging from 1x-100x player's bet. Drawing the Hold the Jackpot™ symbol in the central position of the reel activates the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. The maximum win amount is 500x bet. SPECIAL FEATURES HOLD THE JACKPOT™ BONUS GAME The game is played on one reel. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each win resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. All prizes are collected on the Total Win counter. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or the GRAND Jakpot symbol is drawn in the central position of the reel. Mystery Multiplier™ symbol randomly multiplies the current Total Win prize by 2-10x. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of prizes collected on the Total Win counter, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. GRAND Jackpot symbol awards the GRAND Jackpot of 500x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and prizes collected on the Total Win counter are not added to it. CHANCE LEVEL™ The game is played with the base bet set by default. However, the Chance Level™ feature can be enabled during the base game and you can select one of 3 levels: Chance Level™ x2 – the base bet is multiplied by 2, giving you at least a 2x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x5 - the base bet is multiplied by 5, giving you at least a 6x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x10 - the base bet is multiplied by 10, giving you at least a 13x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. The Chance Level™ feature does not affect the paytable, thus symbol values remain the same as for the base bet in both the base game and the Bonus Game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game average return to player: 96.17% Player Return Percentage (% RTP – Percentage Return to Player) is the expected percentage of bets that a particular Game will return to the Player in the long run. It is equal to the total amount of contributions to the total returns. GAMBLE FEATURE The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. The Gamble feature can be activated only with the current win value during the ongoing game session. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. Each draw is independent of the previous one and has the same probability of winning. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the current win counter and returns to the main game. The Gamble feature can be played for a total of 7 rounds. The number of rounds in the Gamble feature depends on the max win available in the game. The win value from doubling up in the next round cannot be higher than: USD 50,000 Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
