Viper City Heist Viper City Heist Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Symbol wins pay for any winning payline combination without any gaps. Only the highest win is paid per active win line. Game Rules Viper City Heist is a game with 15 individual reels, formatted into 5 columns and 3 rows, with 20 fixed paylines. This game has special symbols: "WILD (Boss)," "SCATTER (VAULT)," and "BONUS prize symbols". The WILD (Boss) substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER and BONUS prize symbols, helping to create winning combinations. The SCATTER (VAULT) triggers the FREE SPINS feature, called VAULT RAID, when enough SCATTER symbols appear on the reels. BONUS prize symbols can trigger QUICK HIT or HEIST RE-SPINS. There are 2 types of BONUS prize symbols in this game: the BRIEFCASE and the MONEY BAG. The BRIEFCASE awards special prizes: GRAND (1000X your total bet), MAJOR (100X your total bet), and MINOR (20X your total bet). The MONEY BAG awards prizes of 0.5X, 1X, 2X, 3X, 4X, 5X, 6X, 7X, 8X, 9X, 10X, 11X, 12X, 13X, 14X, or 15X your total bet. BONUS prize symbols can land in both the Base Game and VAULT RAID. The QUICK HIT feature may trigger on a non-winning spin during the Base Game or VAULT RAID if there are 3 or less BONUS prize symbols on the reels. 1, 2, or 3 BONUS prize symbols present on the reels can be awarded. The HEIST RE-SPINS feature triggers when 4 or more BONUS prize symbols land on the reels during the Base Game or VAULT RAID. It starts with 3 RE-SPINS, which decrease by 1 after each spin unless 1 or more BONUS prize symbol (BRIEFCASE or MONEY BAG) land, resetting the RE-SPIN counter to 3. BONUS prize symbols that trigger HEIST RE-SPINS, as well as any that appear afterward, will stick on the reels for the entire session. During HEIST RE-SPINS, only BONUS prize symbols appear on the reels. The feature ends when the RE-SPINS counter reaches 0 without new BONUS prize symbols, or when all reel positions are filled with BONUS prize symbols. All BONUS prize symbols are awarded at the end of the feature. The VAULT symbol is a SCATTER. Landing 2 VAULTS on the middle row of columns 2 and 4 triggers the VAULT RAID feature. Before the VAULT RAID begins, the VAULT WHEEL is presented. The VAULT WHEEL spins to determine the initial number of FREE SPINS (6, 8, 10, 12, 14, or 20) and initial MULTIPLIER (X2, X3, X4, or X5). During the VAULT RAID, the QUICK HIT and HEIST RE-SPINS feature can be triggered. The MULTIPLIER is applied to all wins, including BONUS prize symbols, if QUICK HIT or HEIST RE-SPINS are triggered during VAULT RAID. VAULT RAID can be re-triggered by landing 2 VAULTS on the middle row of columns 2 and 4, awarding 2 additional FREE SPINS and increasing the MULTIPLIER by 1 for the remaining spins. If the MULTIPLIER reaches 10, subsequent re-triggers award 4 additional FREE SPINS. The maximum MULTIPLIER that can be reached is 10, and FREE SPINS is capped at 150. Any VAULT symbols landing after reaching the maximum FREE SPINS will not award additional spins. It is possible to trigger both the HEIST RE-SPINS and VAULT RAID on the same spin. If this scenario occurs, then the VAULT RAID will commence after the HEIST RE-SPINS conclude. The average RTP is 94.43%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Minimum Bet: 0.10 Maximum Bet: 100.00 Actions Paytable - Toggles the display of the paytable. Auto Play - Click the AUTO PLAY button to enable/disable the Auto Play feature. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Hyper Spin - Pressing and holding down the SPACE button activates Hyper Spin mode after 1.5 seconds. The spins speed up immensely but slow down if a feature is activated. Spin / Start / Spacebar - Starts the game round with the currently selected bet. Press Spin to begin. Stop - Stops the reels more quickly. Fast Play - Toggle on for a significantly faster gameplay. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated.
