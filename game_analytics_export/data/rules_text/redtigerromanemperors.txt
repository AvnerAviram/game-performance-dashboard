Each symbol advances the power bar above its corresponding reel: Pompey the Great on reel 2, Julius Caesar on reel 3, and Marcus Licinius Crassus on reel 4. The Gold SPQR symbol, which can only land on reel 3, is an enhanced version that fills all three power bars at once. Only one Gold SPQR can appear per spin, and it never lands with a Silver SPQR. Roman Emperors Pompey the Great: Triggers a respin where all low-paying symbols are removed. Respins continue until a win occurs. Julius Caesar: Triggers a respin with 3–6 random Wilds. If no win results, the Wilds are removed, and a new respin begins with a fresh set of Wilds. This repeats until a win lands. Marcus Licinius Crassus: Triggers a respin with a win multiplier of x3, x5, x10, or x20, shown above the reels. Respins continue until a win is formed. When multiple features activate together, they occur in the following order: Pompey clears low-paying symbols, Caesar adds Wilds, and Crassus applies the multiplier.
