Scratch Merlins Millions Merlins Millions SCRATCH Card is a match and win game. During play, the goal is to match 3 symbols to win a prize. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.171%. Game Rules Press "PLAY" to begin game Press to select amount staked per scratch card Press "Reveal All" or scratch to reveal hidden symbols Match 3 symbols to win a prize More than one prize can be won per scratch card Payouts are made according to the Paytable Wins are multiplied by the amount staked per scratch card How to Play 1: Choose your BET 2: Select PLAY 3: Swipe the Scratchable panels to reveal a hidden symbol 4: Select REVEAL ALL to reveal all hidden symbols. MAIN GAME SCREEN BUTTONS: BET: Adjusts the BET. The UP/DOWN buttons adjust the BET up or down. PLAY: To purchase scratch card and continue REVEAL ALL: Automatically reveal all the symbols AUTOPLAY: When more than zero AUTOPLAYs is selected the PLAY button will display the number of AUTOPLAYs that will be played. STOP AUTOPLAY: Once auto play begins the PLAY button turns into a STOP button and the number of AUTOPLAYs remaining will be displayed in the STOP button. HOME: Accesses casino menu SETTINGS: Accesses the SETTING page for info and AUTOPLAY options SETTINGS PAGE BUTTONS: AUTOPLAY: Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays. The UP/DOWN buttons increase or decrease the number of autoplays. The available autoplays are 5, 10, 15, 20, 25. INFO: Accesses rules for the game. The game displays all your details in the following display fields: Balance: Displays your total balance Bet: The total wager staked. Win: Displays the amount won in the play Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 10/22/2018
