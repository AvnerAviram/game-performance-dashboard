Mo Mummy Mighty Pyramid Game Rules 20 lines are played for a line cost of 50 Coins. Bet is 50 Coins x Coin Size. All wins on played lines only, except Scatters and Cash Collect feature prizes. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only on each line. Highest win only for scatters. Line wins are multiplied by the Coin Size. Scatter wins, CASH COLLECT FEATURE prizes and Jackpots are added to line wins.RTP: 95.06%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game.
