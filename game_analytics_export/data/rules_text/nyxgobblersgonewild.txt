Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Any combination of 3 or more Wild Symbol 1, Wild Symbol 2, or Wild Symbol 3 counts as a win. Wild Symbol 1, Wild Symbol 2, or Wild Symbol 3 in any position on reel 5 initiates the Activated Wilds Feature. Wild Symbol 1 transforms all High Pay 3 symbols appearing on the reels to Wild Symbol 1 for the current game. Wild Symbol 2 transforms all High Pay 4 symbols appearing on the reels to Wild Symbol 2 for the current game. Wild Symbol 3 transforms all High Pay 5 symbols appearing on the reels to Wild Symbol 3 for the current game. Wins are evaluated after all transformations are complete. Free Games Rules. During the base game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 stars the Free Games Bonus. Awards 7 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Wild 1, Free Games Wild 2, and Free Games Wild 3 are wild and substitute for all symbols. Any combination of 3 or more Free Games Wild 1 symbol, Free Games Wild 2 symbol, or Free Games Wild 3 symbol count as a win. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games.
