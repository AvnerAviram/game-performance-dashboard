Triple Treasures PRIMARY GAME PLAY: Player selects lines and bet per line. Max bet set game at maximum lines and maximum bet per line. Spin begins the game with selected set bet. Award amounts are shown in paytable per credit bet per line. Only highest win amount awarded on each selected payline. Win combinations are justified left-to-right on consecutive reels. WILD substitutes all symbols and is found on reels 1, 3 and 5 only. Each WILD substitution multiplies the pay by 3, for a maximum of a 27x multiplier. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
