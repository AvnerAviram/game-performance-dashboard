Ghosts N Gold Game Rules 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications: Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 - for first reel on the left, 6 - for the rest of the reels Number of ways 3888 Number of coins 30 Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes (Treasure Chest) Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes (Bonus symbol) Bonusround No Free spins Yes Instant win No RTP 95.95% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note: In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: 1.Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value and the number of coins to bet per line. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. 2.Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Mobile version To play, follow these steps: 1.Tap to open the settings screen. 2.On the settings screen, tap then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value and the number of coins to bet per line. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. 3.Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features 3.1. Wild The Wild symbol (Treasure Chest) replaces any other symbols except the Bonus symbol. 3.2. Free Spins Three or four Bonus symbols anywhere on the reels trigger a number of Free Spins, according to the paytable. The Bonus symbol can only land on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5, one per reel. During Free Spins, the amount bet remains the same as the bet on the spin that has triggered the Free Spins and cannot be changed. Note: Additional Free Spins cannot be triggered during current Free Spins. 3.3. Wild Ghost Spins During normal spins and Free Spins, Wild Ghost Spins can be randomly triggered and the Wild Ghost will swoop over the reels and drop between 3 and 8 Wild symbols in random positions on the reels. Wild Ghost Spins are more frequent during Free Spins. 4.
