Max Megaways 3 Max Megaways III Help Introducing the game Max Megaways III is a 6-reel Megaways™ game with up to 117,649 ways to win, featuring a Megadozer™ that delivers bonuses. How to play Select your stake by clicking the left and right arrow buttons on the stake. The stake value is displayed in the stake display. All payouts are multiplied by the stake of each individual spin. Auto play To set up Auto play press the Auto button. This will display Auto play panel which the player can use to modify the number of spins, set a loss limit or set a win limit. When a loss limit has been set the player can select Start to commence auto play. When Auto play is on, the Play button will change to a Stop button. The remaining Auto plays will be displayed in the Stop button. The player can cancel Auto play by clicking the Stop button. Auto play will stop automatically if any of the limits set is reached or anything occurs that requires player intervention. Auto play may not be available in your jurisdiction. Start the spin Press the Play button to start the spin at the stake displayed. The reels will spin and then come to stop, displaying the result. Every base game spin begins with a Win Multiplier of 1. Bonus Buy Press the Bonus Buy button to access the Bonus Buy menu. You may buy 10 Free Spins for 100 times the Free Spins Stake, or 15 Free Spins for 200 times the Free Spins Stake. The Free Spins Stake can be changed by pressing the plus and minus buttons on the Free Spins Stake display. The Free Spins Stake will apply to each Free Spin. Press the price to buy the bonus for that price. The price will be deducted from your balance. Bonus Buy may be limited or unavailable in your jurisdiction. Symbol Wins A win is matching consecutive symbols, regardless of height, from left to right starting from the leftmost reel. Highest win paid per winning combination. All winning combinations are added together. See PAYS for further information. Reactions All winning symbols, except Scatters, are part of a reaction and are replaced by symbols coming from above. Wild Wild substitutes for all symbols except Scatters. Wild occurs on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only. Megadozer™ There is a Coin Dozer above the reels which may deliver one or more Bonus Coins during any spin, including reactions. All coins in the base game are Silver or Gold. If a Gold Coin falls over the edge, all reels increase to display seven symbols. Every Silver Coin that falls over the edge reveals one of these bonuses: • A Win Multiplier for the current spin increments by one. All wins for the remainder of the spin are multiplied by the Win Multiplier. • Ace, King, Queen, Jack, Ten or Nine is revealed, and all instances of that symbol are replaced by a higher paying symbol. • A random symbol is added to the reel which counts for multiple instances as shown. • Each symbol in the reel is replaced by a Wild. Scatter There are 2 Scatters: the regular Gold Scatter and the Diamond Megascatter. 2 Scatters in any position award 10 Free Spins. If one of the triggering Scatters is a Megascatter, 15 Free Spins are awarded instead. 2 additional Free Spins are awarded for each Scatter beyond the second. Gold Scatter occurs on reels 1 and 6 only. Diamond Megascatter occurs on reel 6 only. Diamond Megascatter does not occur in the Free Spins. Free Spins Bonuses Choose 2 doors to reveal your Free Spins Bonuses. During the Free Spins, the revealed bonuses are delivered when Bonus Coins fall over the edge. The available Free Spins Bonuses are: • Increase Multiplier: The Win Multiplier increments by 1. • Extra Spins: Every third 'Extra Spins' coin awards 2 extra Free Spins. • Increase Ways: The reel increases to display 7 symbols. If the reel is already displaying 7 symbols, the bonus has no effect. • Bonus Wild: A Wild is added to the reel and may display a multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, or x10. Wins with a multiplier are multiplied accordingly. • Multiplier Symbol: A random symbol is added to the reel which counts for multiple instances as shown. If the reel is already displaying 7 symbols, the bonus has no effect. Free Spins Free Spins are played with an unlimited Win Multiplier that starts at 1 and is increased by 1 after each reaction. 2 or more Scatters in any position at the end of a Free Spin award an additional 2 Free Spins, plus 2 for each Scatter beyond the second. The stake that was used to trigger the Free Spins will be used for the Free Spins duration. At the end of the Free Spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited into the player's account and regular play then resumes. Return to Player (RTP) The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by dividing the total payouts by the total bets from a simulation of numerous game rounds. Values reflect the maximum expected RTP and do not change. The RTP is defined at different stages or modes of the game.
