BEST STRATEGY THE BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF JOKERS AND SUPER JOKERS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAYER CLOSER TO COMPLETING A SLINGO. FOR EXAMPLE THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN A HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA EXIST AGAIN A RANDOM CHOICE IS MADE.
