Main Game All wins start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations on adjacent reels. All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Sync 'N' Spin ™ Following a winning spin, symbols involved will respin. Any new wins are paid and the process repeats until no new wins are in view. Free Spins Fully lit multiplier icons are awarded by achieving two wins with matching symbols of a kind. Any initial completed multiplier icon awards 10 Free Spins. Each additional completed multiplier icon awards a further 5 Free Spins. The highest number below any fully lit multiplier icon determines the starting position on the ladder. All Free Spins wins are multiplied by the value reached on the ladder. A Wild symbol in view during Free Spins moves you up the ladder by one. During Free Spins, completed multiplier icons award a further 5 Free Spins. Rules All prizes start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations of a kind, consecutive from left to right on selected lines, except scatters. Free Spins can be won again during Free Spins. Free Spins are played at trigger bet. Wild symbol may only appear on all reels 2, 3, 4or 5. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 10/28/2025
