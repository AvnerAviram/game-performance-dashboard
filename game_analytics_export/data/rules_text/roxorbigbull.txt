Big Bull GAME RULES Total bet is 10 x Coin Size. 10 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Base Game Modifiers Add Cash Prize Randomly, on any given spin with one or more Collector Symbol on the reels, there's a chance for Cash Prize symbols to appear. Modifier Add Cash Prize only activates during the Base Game. Modifier Add Cash Prize contributes towards the Persistent Progression count. Random Collection Randomly, on any given spin with Cash Prize symbols on the reels, there's a chance they will be individually paid without the need of the Collector symbol to be on the reels. Modifier Random Collection only activates during the Base Game. Modifier Random Collection does not contribute towards the Persistent Progression count. Persistent Progression During the Base Game, if any WILD/COLLECTOR symbol collects any CASH PRIZE symbols, one token is awarded to the player for that spin. Collect all tokens needed to progress to the next level. Each level brings a new Modifier into the Free Spins Bonus only.
