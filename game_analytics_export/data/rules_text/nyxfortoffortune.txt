Fort of Fortune RTP: 92.0% - 97.0% VOLATILITY: 16.4 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 4 King, Queen, or Knight symbols. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 1 A, K, Q, J, or 5 symbol. Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the game level increases. Double Knight appears only at or above game level 25. Double Queen appears only at or above game level 50. Double King appears only at or above game level 75. Triple Knight appears only at or above game level 100. Triple Queen appears only at or above game level 150. Triple King appears only at or above game level 250. Quadruple Knight appears only at or above game level 500. Quadruple Queen appears only at or above game level 750. Quadruple King appears only at or above game level 1000. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the total amount bet increases. The total amount bet on this game is counted across all sessions played on the current user account. As the total amount bet increases, the following symbols will be unlocked and appear on the reels in order: Double Knight – Bet 3000 credits to unlock, Double Queen – Bet an additional 7000 credits to unlock, Double King – Bet an additional 20000 credits to unlock, Triple Knight – Bet an additional 70000 credits to unlock, Triple Queen – Bet an additional 200000 credits to unlock, Triple King – Bet an additional 700000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Knight – Bet an additional 2000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Queen – Bet an additional 7000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple King – Bet an additional 40000000 credits to unlock. When the final symbol is unlocked, the meter will remain visible and display 100% complete. FREE GAMES BONUS During the Base Game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any positions on reels 1, 2, and 3 starts the Free Games Bonus. 11 or 12 free games are awarded. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Bonus symbols appears only on reels 1, 2, and 3. Free Games Wild symbol is wild substitutes for all symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 4 Free Games King, Free Games Queen, or Free Games Knight symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Free Games A, Free Games K, Free Games Q, Free Games J, or Free Games 5 symbol. Free Games Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Free Games Bonus symbol uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus symbol. The Free Games Bonus symbol does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus symbol ends when 0 free games remain. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels.
