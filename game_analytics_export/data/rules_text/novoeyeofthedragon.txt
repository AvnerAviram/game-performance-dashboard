Eye of the Dragon Game Theme and Graphics Leave your everyday life behind and enter a land of magic and mysticism – play Eye of the Dragon! In the company of a warrior pair your aim is to track down the dragon covered in green scales and look deep into its eyes: If you can hold his gaze you are granted access to the Free Games with a special expanding symbol Main Game The aim with Eye of the Dragon is to line up five identical symbols on up to 10 win lines that are running from left to right. The symbol of the Red-Haired Warrior pays the highest winnings. The Dragon's Eye is the Scatter symbol. Moreover, it acts as Wild symbol which can substitute for other symbols and increase your winning chances. Free Games with Special Expanding Symbol 3 or more Scatters on the screen trigger 10 Free Games with Special Expanding Symbol. One Special Expanding Symbol is randomly selected at the start of the Free Games. During Free Games the Special Symbol expands to cover all 3 positions on the reel and pays in any position on lines played. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. All line combinations start at the leftmost reel. All prizes are on selected lines, except Scatters. All line combinations are on adjacent reels. Scatter symbols pay at any position on screen. Highest win only paid per selected line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. When a credit is less than the minimum bet, the residual credit will be used to start the wheel, which will grant a game at the minimum bet if the arrow stops in the green sector. Free Games can be won again during the Free Games. Free Games are played at trigger bet and lines. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
