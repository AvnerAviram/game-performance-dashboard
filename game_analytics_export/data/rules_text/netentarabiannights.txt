Arabian Nights Game Theme and Graphics The game panel at the bottom of the game displays the available amount in the player account, as well as the amount bet and won. Buttons for the game settings menu, audio, game rules and auto play, are located to the left on the game panel. The display above the game panel shows the current bet level (number of coins per bet line), the current number of bet lines, available cash in coins and the current coin value. Main Game Arabian Nights is a 5-reel, 10-line, video slot featuring wild and scatter symbols, and free bonus spins. Wild Substitution The Arabic man is a wild symbol that can substitute for any other symbol to complete a winning bet line, except for scatters and Jackpot wins. Wins are doubled on bet lines with wild symbols. Scatters and Free Spins The oil lamp is a scatter symbol. Wins with scatter symbols pay the multiplier (displayed in the paytable) multiplied by the total bet (for example, 5 scatter symbols pay 500 multiplied the total bet). 3 to 5 scatters award 15 free spins. During bonus spins, all wins are tripled, except for Jackpot wins. Jackpot The Jackpot can only be won if playing for MAX BET and the reels come to a stop with 5 Arabic man symbols displayed on any bet line. Note : The Jackpot is a progressive jackpot, where every time the game is played by any player a portion of the bet is added to the Jackpot.
