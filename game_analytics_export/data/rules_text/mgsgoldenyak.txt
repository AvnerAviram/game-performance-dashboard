Golden Yak Golden Yak is a 4x5 reel, 1024 ways game. Return to Player (RTP) General Bonus symbol Level Up symbol Blue Temple symbol Wild symbol Free Spin Bonus Autoplay Return to Player (RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time. The RTP is fixed and does not change. This game has an RTP of 95.52%. General All 1024 payways are permanently enabled. Minimum bet per spin is at 40 Coins. If you have a winning combination on the payway, the win is highlighted on the reels. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Highest win paid per winning combination. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except BONUS, LEVEL UP and BLUE TEMPLE which pays any. Regular wins in paytable are displayed in coins. All wins are multiplied by Bet multiplier, calculated as follows: Given that your Total Bet is 0.40 Credits per spin, Bet Multiplier = Total bet / 40 Coins * 100 = 0.40 Credits / 40 * 100 = 1 Your total payout amount in Coins is calculated as follows: Total Payout Amount = Bet multiplier * Values displayed on the paytable Example: Given that your Total bet is 0.40 Credits and 5 of a kind Kings line up, Total Payout Amount is calculated as follows: Bet multiplier = Total bet / 40 Coins * 100 = 0.40 Credits / 40 * 100 = 0.01 Credits * 100 = 1 Total Payout Amount (In Coins) = 1 * 125 = 125 Total Payout Amount (In Credits) = Total Payout Amount (In Coins) / 100 = 125 / 100 = 1.25 Credits The game ends when there is no winning combination after which you can play again.
