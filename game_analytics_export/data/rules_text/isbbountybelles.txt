Bounty Belles 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning way spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 - during normal spins 3-5 - during Wild Bounty Mode Number of ways 243-1125 Number of coins you can bet 20 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round No Free s pins Yes Instant win Yes RTP 95.99% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins - player input is required. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Bonus Symbols, to form winning combinations. Note: Wild symbols only appear on reels 1 and 2. 3.2. Bonus Symbol Landing 3 or more Bonus symbols will trigger the Free Spins . 3.3. Wild Bounty Mode Wild symbols only appear on reels 1 and 2. Landing Wild symbols on reels 1 and 2 will start the Wild Bounty Mode. Reels 3, 4 and 5 can expand by 1 or 2 rows higher during the Wild Bounty Mode. Landing any Bounty symbols on reel 3, 4 or 5 while in Wild Bounty Mode will award the bounty amounts attached to them. During Wild Bounty Mode , any Bounty Symbols which land in view on consecutive reels, starting with reel 3, will award the amounts attached to them. Bounties are only paid on consecutive reels and must start on reel 3. Note: Bounties are only paid by landing them on consecutive reels. Note: The values displayed on the Bounty symbols are shown in the selected currency/coin. Possible values are: Reel 3 Bounties : 2x , 3x , 4x Bet Reel 4 Bounties : 5x, 6x, 8x Bet Reel 5 Bounties : 30x, 50x, 100x Bet Note: Wild Bounty Mode can be triggered on any spin. 3.4. Most Wanted On reel 5 only Most Wanted Bounty prizes exist. Minor, Major and Grand, for each one of these the Bounties amount is awarded. 3.5. Free Spins Landing 3 or more Bonus symbols will trigger Free Spins . During Free Spins Wild Bounty Mode can be triggered on any spin. Note: Free Spins can be re-triggered once. x3 Bonus - 10 Free Spins . x4 Bonus - 12 Free Spins . x5 Bonus - 15 Free Spins. 3.6.Deadman's Draw Randomly during Free Spins any win can be multiplied from a draw of cards. Multiplier cards will be dealt, then shot and removed, and the last card that will be left will award the multiplier shown on the card to the current win. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play. Click the ACTIVATE button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature. Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time. 5.2.
