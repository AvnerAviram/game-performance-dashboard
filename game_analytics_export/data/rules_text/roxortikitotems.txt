Tiki Totems Game Features Stacked Wild During a single spin, if a win causes a whole vertical reel of symbols to rain down, this reel will be replaced with a stacked wild. Stacked wilds give you multiple chances of winning as the whole reel is classed as one large wild symbol. Stacked wilds only last for a single raining reel before they are replaced with new symbols falling onto the reels. Raining Reels A cascading feature where you can win multiple times on every wager. Winning symbols are removed and replaced with new symbols; leading to exciting chain reactions and more potential wins! Free Spin Bonus 4 or more consecutive Raining Reel wins will trigger the Free Spin bonus. 7, 10, 15, 20 or 25 free spins are awarded for 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8+ consecutive wins respectively. Additional free spins are awarded for 3 or more consecutive Raining Reel wins in the bonus, as displayed on the bonus meter. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 94.97% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $94.97 of wins. Rules Total bet is 25 x coin size. 25 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/29/2024
