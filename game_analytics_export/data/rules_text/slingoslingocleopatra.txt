Slingo Cleopatra SLINGO BASICS MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER BY COMPLETING SLINGOS. SELECT YOUR STAKE AND PRESS SPIN TO BEGIN. SLINGO BASICS YOU HAVE 10 SPINS TO MATCH NUMBERS ON THE REEL WITH NUMBERS ON THE GRID. COMPLETE SLINGOS TO MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER. SYMBOLS WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE COLUMN ABOVE. FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ADD AN EXTRA SPIN SYMBOLS SUPER WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE GRID. SYMBOLS SCATTER SYMBOLS ADD FEATURE FREE SPINS UP TO A MAXIMUM OF 180. PAYLADDER COMPLETE SLINGOS TO MOVE UP THE PAYLADDER AND ACCUMULATE BONUS FREE SPINS. STEP 3 AND ABOVE ADDS MORE BONUS FREE SPINS. THE NUMBER OF FEATURE FREE SPINS ACCUMULATE AS YOU CLIMB THE LADDER. FEATURE FREE SPINS PLAY YOUR ACCUMULATED FREE SPINS IN THE IGT CLEOPATRA SLOT GAME. CLEOPATRA FEATURE FREE SPINS: MINIMUM WIN:X2 MAXIMUM WIN:X10.000 FEATURE FREE SPINS PRESS SPIN TO START PLAYING. EXTRA SPINS EXTRA SPINS ARE AVAILABLE AFTER EACH GAME. THE PRICE OF EACH SPIN IS DEPENDANT ON THE GRID POSITION AND POTENTIAL PRIZE. PRICES MAY BE IN EXCESS OF YOUR BASE STAKE. YOU CAN CONTROL EXTRA SPIN LIMITS IN PLAY CONTROLS. THE PRICE OF THE NEXT SPIN IS SHOWN ON THE SPIN BUTTON. COLLECT OR BUY MORE SPINS. PRICES ARE ROUNDED TO THE NEAREST WHOLE VALUE WHICH MAY ALTER THE RTP. WIN LINES THERE ARE 12 WIN LINES AND 11 AWARDS BECAUSE THE LAST NUMBER ON THE GRID WILL ALWAYS AWARD AT LEAST 2 WINNING LINES. BEST STRATEGY THE BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF WILDS AND SUPER WILDS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAYER CLOSER TO A SLINGO. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA ARE AVAILABLE THE PREFERENCE IS GIVEN TO THE POSITIONS THAT ARE INCLUDED IN THE MOST SLINGOS (WIN LINES). FOR EXAMPLE, THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN A HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES. PLAY CONTROLS PLAY CONTROLS CAN BE SELECTED FROM THE MENU. SET THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF EXTRA SPINS TO BE OFFERED. SET THE MAXIMUM PRIZE TO BE OFFERED AS AN EXTRA SPIN. SET THE MAXIMUM STAKE FOR ANY SINGLE GAME. SET THE MAXIMUM LOSS FOR ANY SINGLE GAME. THE GAME WILL AUTOMATICALLY END IF ANY OF THE SET LIMITS ARE REACHED. GENERAL BASED ON THE BEST STRATEGY THIS GAME HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 94.01%. BASED ON THE BEST STRATEGY EACH EXTRA SPIN HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 94.00%. FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS IN THE EXTRA SPINS.
