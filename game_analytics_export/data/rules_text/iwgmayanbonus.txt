Mayan Bonus Introduction Destroy the masks to reveal a prize. Match 3 like amounts to win Start of Game To start the game, press the ‘Play’ button located below the bet amount. Once the ‘Play’ button has been selected the stake is deducted from the account balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game the outcome is displayed on a message panel. You can play the game again at the same stake amount and same numbers as the previous game by pressing the 'Play' button located on the message panel. Once the 'Play' button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance. Pressing the 'Set Bet' button will cause the 'Set Stake' interface to appear where you can alter the stake. How to Play Destroy the masks by clicking or tapping on them. Each mask will reveal a prize amount underneath. Reveal 3 matching amounts to win. Discovering a Golden Ball fills one of the 9 Bonus Game slots. Collect all 9 Golden Balls to unlock the Bonus Game for a guaranteed prize! Any Golden Balls collected remain between games. Winning Ways to win Match three identical prize amounts and win the corresponding prize amount. Collect all 9 Golden Balls to unlock the Bonus Game for a guaranteed prize! General Information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by the player do not affect the outcome of the game. The theoretical average return to player(RTP) is 89.86 % Last modification date: 6/9/2017
