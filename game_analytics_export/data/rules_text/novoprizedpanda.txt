Prized Panda Main Game Prized Panda is played on 5 reels, each 3 rows in height, with 30 fixed winlines. Winning combinations run from the left to right across active win lines, except scatter symbols. The attraction of this game are The Pay Day prizes. When 3 or more Pay Day symbols appear scattered on the reels, the corresponding Pay Day prize is awarded. The Panda symbol is the WILD symbol and substitutes for all symbols, except Pay Day and Red Envelopes symbols. 3 Red Envelopes scattered on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the Free Spins. Trigger Feature 3 Red Envelopes scattered on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the feature - which rewards 10, 15 or 20 Free Spins radomly Free Spins The free spins bonus in Prized Panda slots can be triggered if you collect three Red Envelope symbols anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4. Trigger the bonus and you will receive 10, 15 or 20 free spins randomly to play, plus a 3x multiplier, meaning every win you land during the bonus will be tripled in value. Retrigger the bonus feature for an additional 10, 15 or 20 free spins by collecting another three Red Envelope symbols. Rules All line wins are for combinations of kind. All line wins are for combinations left to right except scatters. All line wins start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations on adjecent reels. All wins are on selected lines except scatters. Highest win only paid per active line. Scatters pay in any position. The Panda substitutes for all symbols except Red Envelopes and Pay Day. Line wins are multiplied by bet per line. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Gamble Feature Maximum gamble amount is 500x the total bet. For higher wins or during autoplay, gambling is not possible.
