The Enforcer Game rules 'Cattle Brand' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Cattle Brand' appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. 'Sheriff' is a scatter. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 93.84%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games Feature 8 free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered ' Sheriff '. During the Free Games Feature, wherever ' Cattle Brand ' appears it is held in position until the completion of the Free Games Feature. During the Free Games Feature, ' Sheriff ' appears on reels 1 and 5 only and substitutes for all symbols. During the Free Games Feature, any ' Sheriff ' appearing on reel 1 will randomly reveal a multiplier of x1, x2 or x3 as displayed. During the Free Games Feature, any ' Sheriff ' appearing on reel 5 will randomly reveal a multiplier of x1, x2 or x3 as displayed. When only one ' Sheriff ' symbol with multiplier x1, x2 or x3 substitutes in a winning combination, the prize for that winning combination is multiplied by x1, x2 or x3 respectively. When two ' Sheriff ' symbols with multiplier x1, x2 or x3 substitute in a winning combination, the prize for that winning combination is multiplied by the product of both Sheriff Multiplier values. During the Free Games Feature, the Feature Paytable is applied. During the Free Games Feature, scattered ' Sheriff ' awards no prize. During the Free Games Feature, 3 additional free games are triggered by any 2 or more scattered ' Sheriff '. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
