Gems Up BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 60 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters and credit prizes. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in currency except for credit prizes. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. The GEMS JACKPOT and the FINE GEMS JACKPOT amounts shown have been multiplied by the bet level and the denomination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. WILD substitutes for DIAMOND, RUBY, PINK RHOMBUS, PINK TEARDROP, PURPLE TEARDROP, A, K, Q and J. SIGN appears on reels 2, 3 & 4 only. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. PRIZE FEVER FEATURE Randomly before a reel spin, a group of symbols may turn into credit prizes. If 4 reels are completely filled with credit prizes of the same color, the GEMS jackpot is awarded. If 5 reels are completely filled with credit prizes of the same color, the FINE GEMS jackpot is awarded. Credit prizes shown have been multiplied by the bet level. Credit prize wins are multiplied by the denomination. FREE GAME FEATURE ANY 3 SIGN award 5 free games. All A, K, Q and J are upgraded to 60, 30, 15 and 15 respectively. 2 SIGN award 2 additional free games, and each SIGN becomes [+1 SIGN]. All credit prizes are awarded at the end of each spin. 3 SIGN award 5 additional free games, and RUBY, PINK RHOMBUS, PINK TEARDROP and PURPLE TEARDROP are upgraded to 300, 240, 180 and 120 respectively for all remaining free games. SIGN, WILD and DIAMOND are replaced with 2000, 1000 and 500 respectively for all remaining free games. Additional free games cannot be won during the remaining free games. Credit prizes shown are at 1 bet level. Credits bet and lines played remain the same as the game that triggered the feature. Different reels are used during the free games.
