Megaburst Repeater Main Game Screen Game Objective: Megaburst Repeater is a 5-reel, 3-row slot game featuring stacked wilds with re-spins. Wins pay both sides except for 5 of a kind (With or without wild substitution), which is paid only once and a repeat feature can be activated at any time. Once activated it can be pressed after any win to repeat the last winning sequence. Paytable Paylines Game Information: Game is played with 10 fixed number of lines. When you enter the game a default total bet is set. The total BET is Number of Fixed lines multiplied by the selected BET/LINE. BET/LINE can be changed by using the and buttons. SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the Spin button with the current selected BET. Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE. Coinciding wins on different lines are all added to your total. All winning combinations on a bet line from either leftmost to right and/or rightmost to left are paid. Line Pays are from both left to right and right to left except for 5 of a kind (With or without wild substitution), which is paid only once. The Ticker messages will appear on the bottom of game screen. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will update, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at the bottom of the game. The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and play-money modes, except that Auto spin option will not be available in play-money mode. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Volume: icon will play the game music. Click on icon will mute the volume . AUTO SPIN: You can use this feature to choose all or any of the various options for 'hands free' game play. The available options under this feature are: Number of spins: The game will spin for the number of rotations selected from the slider menu. You can choose a maximum of 1000 spins and play until your balance is less than the total bet placed. For UKGC player the maximum number of auto spins available will be 100. Spin till Win equals or exceeds: The game will spin until the win amount is equal to or exceeds the amount entered in the numeric text box. Spin till Loss not exceeds: The game will automatically spin until the loss limit is reached. Loss will be calculated with (Loss = Total bets - Total wins) Total bets=accumulated total bets Total wins=accumulated total wins (((Total bets- Total wins) + current game bet) <= Loss limit target) then next auto spin will be allowed. Spacebar to spin: By checking this option, Spacebar key can be used to trigger a spin. Game Rules: Megaburst Repeater is a 5-reel slot game with 10 Fixed lines. The spin button plays the game with current bet value. Max bet selects 10 fixed lines and maximum bet value. Payline wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE. Electric Wild symbol substitute for all symbols. All winning combinations on a bet line from either leftmost to right and/or rightmost to left are paid. Line Pays are from both left to right and right to left except for 5 of a kind (With or without wild substitution), which is paid only once. Coinciding wins on different lines are added to total. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Re-spin Feature: Electric Wild is a wild symbol which substitutes for all symbols The Electric Wild symbol can only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4. One or more Electric Wilds appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, or 4 activates the Electric Wild feature. During the Electric Wild feature, the Wilds expand to cover the entire reel and remain in place while the other reels re-spin. If a new Electric Wild appears during a re-spin, it expands and holds in place together with any previously expanded Wilds for another re-spin. The Electric Wild feature ends when no new Electric Wilds appear during a re-spin. For each Wild one re-spin is awarded. The maximum number of re-spins allowed is 3. Repeat Feature: The Repeat button can be activated at any time. Once activated it can be pressed after a win to repeat the last winning sequence. Player will be able to use repeat functionality only once per spin to repeat last winning sequence if repeat button is activated. Current Bet amount will be deducted from player balance if player tries to use the Repeat feature. Player cannot use repeat feature, if player does not have win in current spin. The player can use it any time after any win or save it for bigger wins. Up to 3 repeats can be held “stacked” from game to game! The best strategy is to repeat winning sequences greater than or equal to 10x stake. If 2 repeats are stacked, the best strategy is to repeat winning sequences greater than or equal 6x stake. If 3 repeats are stacked, the best strategy is to repeat winning sequences greater than 4x stake. This feature persists from game to game, by stake and is platform dependent. The repeat-feature will automatically collect if 3 features are stacked and the value exceeds best strategy IE 4X stake, after alerting the player via pop up box. The player can over-ride best strategy by selecting NO and saving “stacked” repeats for bigger wins. Browser Panel Commands: Click on Responsible Gaming to navigate to the Responsible gaming page.
