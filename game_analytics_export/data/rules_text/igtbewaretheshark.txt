Beware the Shark 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 (4 in Dangerous Waters Free Spins) Number of lines 20 (30 in Dangerous Waters Free Spins) Number of coins you can bet Bet: 20 Side Bet: 30 Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes Scatter symbols Yes (Bonus) Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP Normal Game: 94.03% Side bet- Shark bet: 94.04% Bonus buy - Free Spins: 94.37% Bonus buy- Dangerous Waters Free Spins: 94.37% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Wild Wild can substitute for all symbols except Bonus . 3 or more Wilds on a spin trigger Roaming Wild Modifier in the base game. Stacked Wild - only available on colliding wilds in Dangerous Waters Free Spins . 3.2. Modifiers On any spin a modifier can be triggered: Roaming Wilds - Landing 3 or more Wilds will activate the Roaming Wilds feature. This will respin the reels and shuffle the Wilds - if another Wild lands in view, this will activate another respin after any wins are paid. Lose to Win - Awards either a winning spin or a bonus trigger from a losing outcome. Respin - Nets lock winning symbols in place and the reels respin to try to improve any wins. The process continues until no further winning symbols land, at which point all wins are paid. 3.3. Free Spins Get 3 Bonus symbols in reels 2, 3 and 4 to trigger the bonus feature. Pick your Bonus : Roaming Wild Free Spins 10 Free Spins where any wild remains a persistent roaming wild. Any collisions during the Free Spins will increase the win multiplier. Wild symbols which land will shuffle to a new location each spin. Roaming Wild Free Spins is played on 3x5 reels over 20 winlines. No retriggers are available. Dangerous Waters Free Spins 7 Free Spins where any wild remains a persistent roaming Wild . Any collisions during the Free Spins will increase the win multiplier and increase the Wild into a super stacked symbol for that spin. Wild symbols which land will shuffle to a new location each spin. Dangerous Waters Free Spins is played on 4x5 reels over 30 winlines. No retriggers are available. 3.4. Bonus Buy Bonus Buy costs 100x total bet and is guaranteed to trigger the Free Spins Bonus . The Shark Bet is not active during a Bonus Buy . 3.5. Shark Bet Turn on the Shark Bet for more chance to trigger the Bonus. Shark Bet will add 50% to the cost of each spin. Paytable values will remain the same as the base bet. 4. Other game buttons 4.1. AUTO SPIN FEATURE The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins: click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play. Loss limit: click + or - to select the value of the loss limit; Stop if a win reaches: click + or - to select the value of the maximum win; Stop if JACKPOT is won (only for games with Progressive Jackpots): to enable this setting, tick the checkbox; to disable it, remove the tick. Click the ACTIVATE button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature. Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time. 5.2.
