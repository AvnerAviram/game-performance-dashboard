Bison Fury Megaways GAME OBJECTIVES: BISON FURY MEGAWAYS TM is a six reel, ‘MEGAWAYS TM ' Jackpot slot’ game with up to 200704 possible ways to win. There is a horizontal reel positioned above reels 2,3,4 & 5. Symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive combinations (WAYS). The SUNSET symbol acts as a scatter. When 4 or more SUNSET symbols land anywhere in view the FREE SPINS feature is triggered. 8, 12 or 16 FREE SPINS are awarded depending on the number of triggering SUNSET symbols 4, 5 or 6+. FREE SPINS can be re-triggered during the FREE SPINS. GOLDEN COIN symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except the SUNSET symbol. HOW TO PLAY: BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘+’ or ‘-‘ buttons either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. Clicking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET. REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game. PLAY: The PLAY button in the centre right position starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the centre bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode. PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the HAMBURGER icon and selecting the PAYTABLE option. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game. VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF. CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Players current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen. AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play. For UK/Ontario users the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. To configure AUTOPLAY: Click the AUTOPLAY button then select the required number of plays. AUTOPLAY begins automatically. The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time. In play-money mode, the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP): Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information. Click the ‘i’ icon to view game information and rules. Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the gameplay. BISON FURY MEGAWAYS TM is a six reel, ‘ALL WAYS SLOT’ game with 200704 possible ways to win. Each reel will contain symbols from 2 to 7 randomly. The game has a horizontal Top reel above the main reels which contains 4 further symbols. These symbols in the Top reel are included in winning combinations above the corresponding vertical reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. The Top reel also cascades, but from right to left as opposed to vertically. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the current selected or default BET amount. All payouts are made according to the paytable. The paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET. All symbols pay from left to right in consecutive reels, starting with the left most reel except the Scattered SUNSET symbol. Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid. All possible wins are added to the players BALANCE. GOLDEN COIN and SUNSET symbols do not have a direct payout. GOLDEN COIN symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except the SUNSET symbol. GOLDEN COIN symbol appears on all reels except reel 6 in base game and reels 1 & 6 in FREE SPINS. The SUNSET symbol acts as a scatter. When 4, 5 or 6+ SUNSET symbols land anywhere in view, 8, 12 or 16 FREE SPINS are awarded. FREE SPINS will be initiated only after the current spin cascades are completed. Win count-up animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (desktop). Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. All payouts are made in USD. The theoretical return to player of this game excluding jackpot is 92.14%. The theoretical return to player of this game including jackpot is 95.14%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. CASCADING WINS: All symbol wins produce cascades and will be destroyed and will be replaced by new cascading symbols. The Top reel symbols will be replaced from the right. The Main reel symbols will be replaced from above. Cascading feature will stop automatically if there are no new winning combinations to form a win. All wins accumulated during these cascading wins are credited to player after the cascade feature has been completed. The cascading wins will stop automatically when the number of cascades reaches 9 though there is a win in the 9th cascade in base game, FREE SPINS and RESPINS. FREE SPINS: When 4, 5 or 6+ SUNSET symbols land anywhere in view 8, 12 or 16 FREE SPINS are awarded. During FREE SPINS the GOLDEN COIN symbol becomes a STICKY WILD. On landing any GOLDEN COIN symbol will give between 1 to 6 RESPINS randomly. RESPINS count down from the number allocated and do not count as a FREE SPIN. The number of active RESPINS count is shown on the GOLDEN COIN symbol. RESPINS and FREE SPINS retrigger will be initiated only after the current FREE SPIN cascades are completed. The reel size of the current spin will be retained throughout the RESPINS. The remaining FREE SPINS are carried on, once all the RESPINS are completed. Each RESPIN coin will have a limit of 9 cascades within each RESPIN round. Further GOLDEN COIN symbols landed during RESPINS will trigger more RESPINS with a further 1-6 RESPINS being awarded for each new GOLDEN COIN symbol landed. During FREE SPINS, if multiple GOLDEN COINS appear on the reel view, the GOLDEN COIN with the highest count will be the RESPINS count and then the RESPINS round will continue. During the RESPINS when a new GOLDEN COIN symbol lands on the reels with a count less than the existing GOLDEN COIN count, then the RESPINS count does not change. However, if the new GOLDEN COIN count is greater than the existing GOLDEN COIN count, then the difference between the highest existing GOLDEN COIN count and the new highest GOLDEN COIN count is added to the RESPINS meter. For every respin completed, the GOLDEN COIN count is decreased by 1. When the GOLDEN COIN count reaches to null, then the GOLDEN COIN symbol will disappear. Each FREE SPIN will also have a limit of 9 cascades which are counted separately from the RESPINS. During FREE SPINS 4, 5 or 6+ SUNSET symbols landed awards 4, 6 or 8 extra FREE SPINS respectively. During RESPINS, if a GOLDEN COIN or SUNSET symbol lands behind the STICKY WILD will not be taken into consideration for feature trigger. When a GOLDEN COIN is part of the symbol wins, it will explode in base game but not in FREE SPINS and RESPINS. The FREE SPINS round is complete when all the RESPINS and FREE SPINS have been completed. FREE SPINS and RESPINS are played at the same BET as the triggering FREE SPINS game.
