Club 2000 Deluxe GAME RULES Club 2000 Deluxe is a quintessential classic TWIN slot game that features TWO side-by-side games, with double reels that are packed with cool retro symbols and special features. Try your chances at a BIG WIN or a MEGA WIN in this hot new 1 win line, 3 reel, 3 row (per game) twin video slot game. The colorful reels are filled with retro fruit machine symbols, scatter symbols, and special mystery win symbols. Play the left set of reels, the right set of reels, or both sets of reels at the same time, complete with synchronized spinning action. Click the SPIN or AUTO PLAY BUTTONS to start playing. Click MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. You can also use these buttons to choose to play GAME 1, GAME 2 or GAME 1 + 2. MYSTERY CLUB SYMBOL Two (2) or three (3) MYSTERY CLUB symbols scattered anywhere on adjacent reels awards a mystery prize. A winning combination must start with a MYSTERY CLUB symbol on the first reel. Winning combinations with two (2) or three (3) symbols pay from the left to the right, starting on the first reel. SCATTER SYMBOLS When 3 SCATTER symbols land anywhere on reels 1, 2 and 3 on a single spin (scattered), a prize will be awarded. Click/Tap the SPIN or AUTOPLAY buttons to start playing. Activate MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. The theoretical minimum payback percentage (RTP) is 95.00% Disclaimer: If an interrupted game is not finished within 90 days, your game will be terminated. If your game gets terminated you will lose every active bet. Only the highest winning combination pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. MAIN SETTINGS INTRO SCREEN The default setting for the INTRO SCREEN is ON. When set to OFF, the INTRO is not visible. INCLUDE JACKPOT (IF APPLICABLE): Stop AUTO PLAY in case of a jackpot win (when applicable). Note: AUTO PLAY options can be different for some jurisdictions.
