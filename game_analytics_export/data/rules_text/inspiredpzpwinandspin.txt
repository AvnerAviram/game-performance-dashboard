Prize Piggy Win And Spin WIN LINES All games are played with 25 win lines. Wins can occur across any or all of the win lines. Only the highest award is paid per winning combination. All wins pay left to right across adjacent reels, except Bonus, starting from the leftmost reel. Wins on different played lines are added. WILD SYMBOL Substitutes for all symbols except Bonus Scatter. APPEARS ON REELS 2,3,4 AND 5 IN THE STANDARD SPINS BASE GAME ONLY WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS FEATURE IN THE BASE GAME AND WIN & SPIN BONUS, APPEARING ON ALL REELS. THERE ARE 2 TYPES OF WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS, PINK PIG CASH SYMBOLS AND GOLDEN PIG JACKPOT SYMBOLS PINK PIG WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS DISPLAY A CASH VALUE THE POSSIBLE VALUES FOR THE CASH WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS ARE1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x AND 9xTHE ACTIVE BET. (NOTE – TURNING FORTUNE BET ON DOES NOT INCREASE THE VALUES OF THE PIGS) GOLDEN PIG WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS DISPLAY EITHER MINI, MINOR OR MAJOR JACKPOTS THE MINI JACKPOT AWARD IS 10x BASE BET THE MINOR JACKPOT AWARD IS 25x BASE BET THE MAJOR JACKPOT AWARD IS 100x BASE BET LANDING 6 OR MORE OF ANY TYPE OF WIN & SPIN SYMBOL TRIGGERS THE WIN & SPIN BONUS. THE WIN & SPIN VALUES ARE ONLY AWARDED IN THE WIN & SPIN BONUS OR FROM THE FLYING PIG FEATURE IN FORTUNE SPINS. GOLDEN PIG COIN TRIGGER EVERY GOLDEN PIG WIN & SPIN SYMBOL WHICH LANDS ON THE REELS DURING THE BASE GAME WILL ALSO ADD A COIN TO THE COIN PILE DISPLAYED ABOVE THE REELS. EACH TIME THE COIN PILE IS ADDED TO THERE IS A CHANCE OF IT INCREASING IN SIZE, UP TO A MAXIMUM OF 5 LEVELS. THE SIZE OF THE COIN PILE DOES NOT INCREASE THE CHANCE OF TRIGGERING THE WIN & SPIN BONUS. FOLLOWING ADDING ANY COINS FROM THE REELS THERE IS A CHANCE THE COIN PILE MAY TUMBLE AND FALL OVER THE REELS TRIGGERING THE WIN & SPIN BONUS. THERE IS A HIGHER CHANCE OF TRIGGERING THE BONUS IF MORE THAN ONE COIN IS ADDED IN THE SAME GAME. IF THE NUMBER OF WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS ON THE REELS IS LESS THAN 6, EXTRA WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS WILL BE ADDED BEFORE THE BONUS BEGINS. THERE IS AN INCREASED CHANCE OF LANDING GOLDEN PIG SYMBOLS DURING THE BONUS IF TRIGGERED IN THIS WAY. WIN & SPIN BONUS THE WIN & SPIN BONUS IS AWARDED WHEN LANDING 6 OR MORE WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS IN ANY POSITION. IT CAN ALSO BE TRIGGERED WHEN ADDING TO THE COIN PILE FROM ANY GOLDEN PIG WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS LANDING ON THE REELS IN THE BASE GAME. AT THE START OF THE BONUS ANY WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS IN VIEW ARE LOCKED IN PLACE. INITIALLY 3 SPINS ARE AWARDED, DURING A SPIN ANY WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS LANDING IN EMPTY POSITIONS ARE LOCKED IN PLACE AND THE SPINS ARE RESET BACK TO 3. THE BONUS CONTINUES UNTIL THE PLAYER HAS NO SPINS REMAINING OR ALL 15 POSITIONS ARE FILLED WITH A WIN & SPIN SYMBOL. AT THE END OF THE BONUS ALL WIN & SPIN VALUES IN VIEW ARE AWARDED. IF ALL 15 POSITIONS HAVE BEEN FILLED, THE GRAND JACKPOT PRIZE IS AWARDED IN ADDITION TO THE ACCUMULATED WIN. THE GRAND JACKPOT AWARD IS 1000x BASE BET. WIN & SPIN BONUS DURING THE BONUS IF NO WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS HAVE BEEN ADDED ON THE FINAL SPIN THERE IS A CHANCE A FLYING PIG MAY APPEAR. IF THE FLYING PIG IS TRIGGERED, MINI WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS APPEAR IN THE EMPTY POSITIONS ON THE REELS AND ONE OR MORE WILL BE ACTIVATED BY THE FLYING PIG, TURNING ANY HIT INTO A WIN & SPIN SYMBOL OF THE SAME TYPE. AT THE END OF THE FLYING PIG SEQUENCE ANY REMAINING MINI WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS DISAPPEAR AND IF ALL 15 POSITIONS HAVE NOT BEEN FILLED THE SPINS ARE SET BACK TO 3. Fortune Spins AS AN ALTERNATIVE TO SPINNING THE NORMAL REELS, A DIFFERENT BASE GAME CAN BE CHOSEN. THE FORTUNE SPINS GAME CAN BE SELECTED VIA THE FORTUNE SPINS BUTTON. DURING FORTUNE SPINS ALL SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS OTHER THAN THE WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS. EXTRA WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS ARE ALSO ADDED TO THE REELS AND THERE IS A HIGHER CHANCE OF TRIGGERING THE BONUS. AT THE END OF ANY SPIN A FLYING PIG MAY APPEAR AND AWARD THE PIG CASH VALUES. FORTUNE SPINS CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY BET. Fortune Bet FORTUNE BET CAN BE ENABLED FOR BOTH STANDARD AND FORTUNE SPINS. FORTUNE BET ALLOWS THE PLAYER TO SELECT A NEW BET MULTIPLIER AT 1.5X THE CURRENT BET. WITH FORTUNE BET ENABLED MORE GOLDEN PIG WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE REELS, INCREASING THE CHANCE OF TRIGGERING THE BONUS VIA THE COIN PILE. AWARD VALUES FOR WIN LINES AND WIN & SPIN SYMBOLS ARE NOT INCREASED BY THE INCREASED BET MULTIPLIER. Gamble The gamble can be configured to be on, on if the win is greater than or equal to 5x bet, or off. If the gamble is on, any winnings awarded during the game will offer the gamble feature. If the gamble is on and if the win is greater than or equal to 5x bet, any winnings greater than or equal to 5x bet awarded during the game will offer the gamble feature. The gamble threshold does not increase with Fortune Bet enabled. The gamble entry trigger is based on the base bet before any bet multipliers are applied. When the gamble is entered, two wheels are presented. The left wheel offers the chance to increase the winnings by one third, double the winnings, or triple the winnings. The right gamble option offers the chance to gamble for the Win and Spin bonus, Super Win and Spin bonus, or Mega Win and Spin bonus. The win value can be adjusted by pressing the '-' and '+' arrows, this in turn will adjust the winning and losing segments of the wheel. Press either wheel during the feature to gamble your winnings and spin the pointer. If the pointer stops on the green segment, the cash value or Win and Spin bonus are awarded. If the pointer stops on the red segment, the player loses their winnings and exits to the base game. When the potential winning area of the gamble wheel is at its minimum size the chance of success could be significantly less than the visual representation shown. Winnings can be gambled up to a maximum win amount of x2500 base bet or $250,000.00, whichever is the lower value. Once the maximum win amount is reached, winnings are automatically collected and the gamble will be exited. The maximum that can be achieved via the bonus gamble is the Mega Win and Spin bonus. Once this is reached the bonus wheel is disabled while the player is still able to gamble on the cash wheel. Collecting the Win and Spin bonus outcome starts the bonus with 6 symbols landed. Super Win and Spin bonus guarantees 1 or more of these will be GOLDEN PIG symbols. Mega Win and Spin bonus guarantees all symbols which land at the start and during the bonus will be GOLDEN PIG symbols. Press the 'COLLECT' button during the feature to collect your winnings. The expected payback for the gamble game is 100% SPIN CHANCE Spin Chance is triggered automatically if the credit is reduced below the current price of play, yet is still greater than zero. Spin Chance offers the player an opportunity to wager their remaining credit in an attempt at winning an additional base game spin at the current bet. To play Spin Chance, select the 'PLAY' button to begin moving the pointer across the meter. Alternatively, select the 'NO THANKS' button to exit Spin Chance with the remaining credit. If the pointer stops on the green segment, a spin at the current bet is awarded and plays automatically. If the pointer stops on the red segment, the player loses any remaining credit and exits to the base game. The red and green segments are proportional to the amount of credit in relation to the current bet. The chance of winning a spin is determined by this ratio. Bonus Buy Bonus Buy allows the player to purchase direct entry to the bonus feature. Purchasing the feature immediately starts a game that guarantees at least 6 WIN & SPIN symbols to land in view. Bonus Buy is purchased for 60x the current bet. Feature awards are determined by the current bet.
