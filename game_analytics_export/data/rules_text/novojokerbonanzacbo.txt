Joker's Bonanza Game Theme and Graphics Experience the excitement of Joker’s Bonanza! Land 3 or more diamonds scatters to climb the cash ladder. Check out Free Games featuring the diamond respin feature that increases the win potential! Main Game All wins start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations of a kind, consecutive from left to right on selected lines, except scatters. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Every new game, the cash values on the cash ladder flip to reveal a new set of cash values and bomb positions. Cash Ladder 3 or more diamond symbols that land in view increment the cash ladder. Landing on a bomb position on the ladder blows up the position, cascading values above down. The player is awarded the final cash value that lands into the highlighted area, Free Games 3 Bonus scatter symbols triggers x10 Free Games. Bonus scatters may only appear on reels 2, 3 or 4. Any diamond(s) that land during Free Games triggers the diamond respin feature. Only additional diamonds may land during a respin. Free Games can be retriggered during Free Games. Rules All prizes start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations of a kind, consecutive from left to right on selected lines, except scatters. Free Games can be won again during Free Games. Free Games are played at trigger bet. Diamond scatter symbols may appear on all reels. Bonus scatter symbol may only appear on all reels 2, 3, or 4. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
