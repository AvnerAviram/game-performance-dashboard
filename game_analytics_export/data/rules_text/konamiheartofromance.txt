Heart of Romance BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. Two symbols appear in each frame on reel 3. For each lit line passing through reel 3, the symbols in each frame are evaluated separately when part of a winning combination. HEART appears on reel 3 only and substitutes for WOMAN, MASK, JEWELS, GLOVES, GLASSES, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLS 1st, 2nd, 4th and 5th reels contain a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: WOMAN, MASK, JEWELS, GLOVES, GLASSES, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3 or more GEM trigger the feature. Select one of the following free game features. 20 free games: All HEART change to HEART2X, HEART3X or HEART5X when part of a winning combination. 10 free games: All HEART change to HEART5X, HEART8X or HEART10X when part of a winning combination. Mystery choice: Mystery number of free games with a mystery set of multipliers. 5 free games: All HEART change to HEART10X, HEART15X or HEART30X when part of a winning combination. 3 free games: HEART change to HEART15X, HEART30X or HEART40X when part of a winning combination. HEART2X, HEART3X, HEART5X, HEART8X, HEART10X, HEART15X, HEART30X and HEART40X substitute for WOMAN, MASK, JEWELS, GLOVES, GLASSES, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If either HEART2X, HEART3X, HEART5X, HEART8X, HEART10X, HEART15X, HEART30X or HEART40X substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X, 5X, 8X, 10X, 15X, 30X or 40X respectively. Different reels are used during the free games. Any 3 or more GEM re-trigger the selected free game feature during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 7/12/2022
