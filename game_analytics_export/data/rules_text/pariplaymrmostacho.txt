Mr Mostacho Mr Mostacho is a 5x3 video slot with 25 paylines featuring wilds with up to 500x multipliers, and free spins. Wins are awarded for 3, 4, and 5 of a kind symbols from left to right. The return to player (RTP) is 96.00%. This is a high volatility game. Mr Mostacho 'WILD' Symbol Wilds replace all symbols except the scatter symbol. Wilds can award multipliers of up to 500 times the bet. Scatter symbol If 3 or more scatters land, the free spins round will be triggered. Free Spins When 3 or more scatters land on the reels, the free spins round will be triggered. 3 scatters will award 10 free spins, 4 scatters will award 20 free spins, and 5 scatters will award 30 free spins. GAME RULES Payline wins pay if in succession from the left most reel to the right most reel. Only the highest win per payline is paid. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the Paytable. Max bet plays the game at the highest bet level. A payline win is equal to the values shown on the Paytable multiplied by the bet per line. Simultaneous wins on different paylines are added. Balance displays money available to wager. Malfunction voids all play. AUTOPLAY SETTINGS (IF AVAILABLE) NUMBER OF SPINS: Select the number of spins to auto-play. ON ANY WIN : Turn on this option to stop autoplay on any win.
