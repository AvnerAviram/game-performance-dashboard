Emperor's Gold Game Help Game Introduction Emperor's Gold is a 5x3 high slot featuring Expanding Dragon Wilds and Free Spins. 3 or more Scatter Symbols awards Free Spins where the Picture Symbols can upgrade for bigger wins. Dragon Symbol The Dragon symbol expands vertically and substitutes for all symbols except scatter symbols at low stakes. The Dragon symbol expands vertically and substitutes for all symbols including scatter symbols at higher stakes. Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. 3 or more Scatter symbols activate Free Spins. Scatters and Dragon Symbols can add extra Free Spins. Big Bet A Big Bet game initially consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. Additional Big Bet Spins can be won from Bonus Trails. In Big Bet spins, the Dragon Symbol expands and then persists until Free Spins is triggered or the end of the game. A Super Bonus Trail may be available that awards extra Big Bet Spins. A Mega Bonus Trail may be available that awards extra Big Bet Spins or Free Spins. How to Play Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. All Big Bet line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as cash values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 98%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/5/2020
