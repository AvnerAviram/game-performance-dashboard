Key Bet Roulette INTRODUCTION TO KEYBET ROULETTE WHEN THE SPIN BUTTON IS PRESSED A RANDOM NUMBER BETWEEN 0 AND 37 (37 BEING KEY BET) IS DRAWN. THE RESULT OF THIS DRAW WILL THEN BE SHOWN AS WHERE THE BALL COMES TO REST. THE ADDITIONAL KEY BET BETTING OPTION IS A SIDE BET THAT CAN BE PLACED ALONGSIDE THE STANDARD ROULETTE TABLE BETS. THIS 'KEY BET' POSITION IS SEPARATE FROM THE MAIN TABLE, SO SPLIT BETS ARE NOT POSSIBLE ON THE NEW KEY BET POSITION. RTP IS 95.34%. ADJUST YOUR UNIT STAKE FOR EACH BET BY TOUCHING THE CHIP AGAIN TO INCREASE YOUR STAKE. BET ON WHEEL SELECT WHERE YOU WANT TO PLACE YOUR CHIPS BY TOUCHING THE ON-SCREEN WHEEL. NEIGHBOURS BETS ANY BET PLACED WILL AUTOMATICALLY PLACE THE SAME BET ON ADJACENT NUMBERS. THE NUMBER OF ADJACENT BETS IS SELECTABLE WITHIN THE CENTRE OF THE TRACK. KEY BET THE KEY BET IS A SIDE BET THAT CAN BE PLAYED ALONGSIDE THE STANDARD ROULETTE BETS. THIS KEY BET POSITION IS SEPARATE FROM THE MAIN TABLE, SO SPLIT BETS ARE NOT POSSIBLE ON THE KEY BET POSITION. PLACE A BET ON THE KEY BET AND THE POT VALUE WILL BE DISPLAYED. THIS CAN BE UP TO A MAXIMUM OF 100X THE KEY BET STAKE. THE KEY BET DISC SPINS EVERY GAME AND HIGHLIGHTS THE AWARD OF A MULTIPLIER OR POT POSITION FOR A WINNING KEY BET WAGER. THE ROULETTE WHEEL WILL THEN SPIN. IF THE BALL LANDS IN THE KEY BET POSITION AND THE PLAYER HAS WAGERED ON THIS POSITION, THEY WILL WIN THE HIGHLIGHTED AWARD. BUTTONS NEIGHBOUR BETS - ENTERS THE NEIGHBOURS BETTING SCREEN. BET ON WHEEL - ENTERS THE WHEEL BETTING SCREEN. HELP AND ODDS - ACCESSES THE GAME HELP AND GAME ODDS. CLEAR LAST CHIP - REMOVES THE MOST RECENT CHIP ADDED. CLEAR ALL CHIPS - TO REMOVE ALL CHIPS FROM THE TABLE. DOUBLE BET - DOUBLES ANY BET PLACED ON THE TABLE. REPEAT LAST BET - REPEATS THE PREVIOUS BET. STAKE - CHANGES THE CHIP VALUE BETWEEN LOW, MEDIUM AND HIGH. Last modification date: 1/29/2019
