Egyptian Riches Base Game WILD is wild for all symbols except FEATURE. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Feature Description 3 or more scattered FEATURE trigger the Egyptian Riches Feature. Scatter pay monetary awards have already been multiplied by the total line bet. Scatter pays are added to payline wins. The player selects 1 of 5 SCARABS for a monetary award. Once the award is given to the player, the player makes selections from a grid of TREASURES. Each selected TREASURE may reveal a monetary award, PICK ANOTHER or COLLECT. Every time the player selects a TREASURE that reveals a monetary award, the award amount of all selected SCARABS are added to the TREASURE's award amount and the combined total is awarded. If the player reveals a "pick another" then the player selects another SCARAB for an award and returns to selecting TREASURES from the grid. The player continues picking TREASURES until a COLLECT is revealed. SCARAB awards : 1x - 7x total line bet. TREASURES monetary awards : 1x - 6x total line bet Once an award has been found, that particular prize is removed from the pool of available awards. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 4/4/2017
