Midnight in Tokyo The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and at least 243 ways twin. All wins are paid for combinations of 3, 4 or 5 identical symbols on any positions on subsequent reels, starting from the first reel. Only the highest win per way is paid. The game features twWild symbols which split adjoining symbols int2 or 3, increasing the number of ways twin. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except for Moon and Bonus symbols. SPECIAL FEATURES Bonus Jackpot Game A draw of at least 3 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Bonus Jackpot Game. The bonus round is played on a 5x3 grid with 15 separate reels, where Bonus and Moon symbols can be drawn. Bonus symbols that activated the round and all Bonus and Moon symbols drawn during the bonus stick tthe reels. Bonus and Moon symbols may be drawn on any reel. 3 re-spins are granted at the beginning of the bonus round. Every time a Bonus or Moon symbol is drawn, the number of re-spins resets t3. The bonus ends when all re-spins are finished or all reels are filled with symbols. At the end of the round, the prize is paid for the sum of Moon symbol prizes and the number of collected Bonus symbols. Depending on the number of collected Bonus symbols, the following prizes are available: a prize of x5 the stake for 3 Bonus symbols; a prize of x10 the stake for 4 Bonus symbols; a prize of x150 the stake for 5 Bonus symbols (Major Jackpot); a prize of x1000 the stake for 6 Bonus symbols (Grand Jackpot). The bonus is played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated it. SPECIAL SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOL This Wild splits vertically adjoining symbols. If Wild is located on the top or bottom row, it split the adjoining symbol int3. WILD SYMBOL This Wild splits horizontally adjoining symbols. If Wild is located on the outermost reel, it splits the adjoining symbol int3. MOON SYMBOL The Moon symbol represents prizes ranging from x1-x10, x15 or x20 the stake. These prizes are paid only in the Bonus Jackpot Game. BONUS SYMBOL A draw of 3 Bonus symbols triggers the Bonus Jackpot Game. During the base game, this symbol can be drawn only on reels 1, 3 and 5.
