Fluffy Favourites Fairground After changing the history of soft slots forever with Fluffy Favourites, and then cementing the characters into the hearts of the playing public with Fluffy Too we are delighted that the third instalment in the Fluffy Family has arrived. Fluffy Favourites Fairground is a wonderfully rendered game, offering the chance to win up to 25 free spins and an amazing “Hook a Fluffy” feature game offering players the chance to win up to 100x their bet. This is a timeless 5 reel, 25 line slot game that shows why Fluffy is everyone’s Favourite! How to Play Welcome to the Fairground where your Fluffy Friends can’t wait to see you and take you on an exciting ride. Choose your initial wager and select how many paylines you want to start with (up to 25 available). If you wish you can select the Autoplay feature, which allows you to play up to 100 games in a row. Amongst the colourful, cute and beautifully rendered symbols we have the Big Top Scatter, and keep an eye out for the Pink Elephant which is a second scatter as well as being the game’s Wild Substitute. The Pink Elephant can replace all but the Big Top to help you complete a winning combination. And to make this Pink Elephant even more magical, when it appears in a payline you’ll DOUBLE your prize! Amazing characters, exciting features and a successful formula, we know that this Fairground is going to be the place to play! Bonus Feature Hook-A-Fluffy This special game will be triggered when you spin 3 or more Big Tops anywhere on the reels. You’ll be granted one pick per Big Top, each one awarding you up to 100x your total wager. Good luck! FREE Games When you spin 3 Pink Elephant Scatters, you’ll be rewarded with 15 FREE Games, when you spin 4, you’ll get 20, and if 5 appear, you’ll get 25 FREE Games! Line wins are TRIPLED during this bonus feature, and in addition, it can be reactivated up to 15 times. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
