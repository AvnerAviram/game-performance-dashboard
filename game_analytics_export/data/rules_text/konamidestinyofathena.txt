Destiny of Athena BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels. Highest win only on each lit line. Wins on different lit lines are added. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. WILD substitutes for ATHENA, SWORD, SHIELD, HORN, ARMBAND, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. Appears on reels 2, 3, 4, & 5 only. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Theoretical return to the player is 95.92%. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLS Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: ATHENA, SWORD, SHIELD, HORN, ARMBAND, A, K, Q, J, 10 or 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. SELECTION FEATURE GAME RULES 3 OWL trigger a selection feature. During the selection feature, 3 OWL appear and contain either ALL WINS X20, X5, X3 OR X2 - 8 FREE GAMES or ALL WINS X100, X25, X15 OR X10 - 8 SUPER FREE GAMES. Select OWL to reveal either ALL WINS X20, X5, X3 OR X2 - 8 FREE GAMES or ALL WINS X100, X25, X15 OR X10 - 8 SUPER FREE GAMES. If ALL WINS X20, X5, X3 OR X2 - 8 FREE GAMES is revealed, then the Free Game Feature is awarded. If ALL WINS X100, X25, X15 OR X10 - 8 SUPER FREE GAMES is revealed, then the Super Free Game Feature is awarded. If ALL WINS X100, X25, X15 OR X10 - 8 SUPER FREE GAMES is revealed and there are additional features remaining, an additional 8 super free games will be awarded for each additional feature remaining. FREE GAME FEATURE If ALL WINS X20, X5, X3 OR X2 - 8 FREE GAMES is revealed during the Selection Feature, the player is awarded 8 free games. Any free game with a win, will have all wins on that game multiplied by a randomly selected multiplier from X20, X5, X3 or X2. 3 OWL appearing during a single spin accumulate during the feature and will take the player back to the Selection Feature at the end of the Free Game Feature until all additional features remaining are played or the Super Free Game Feature is awarded. The Super Free Game Feature cannot be won during the free game feature. Credits bet remain the same as the game that triggered the feature. SUPER FREE GAME FEATURE If ALL WINS X100, X25, X15 OR X10 - 8 SUPER FREE GAMES is revealed during the selection feature, the player is awarded 8 super free games. Any super free game with a win, will have all wins on that game multiplied by a randomly selected multiplier from X100, X25, X15 or X10. 3 OWL award 8 additional super free games. The Selection Feature and Free Game Feature cannot be won during the Super Free Game Feature. Credits bet remain the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 7/5/2022
