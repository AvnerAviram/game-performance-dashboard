Lady of Avalon How to Play Game Introduction This is a 10 win line game with a Free Spins Bonus that features a randomly selected expanding symbol. When the selected special symbol lands on the reels as part of a winning combination, all other special symbols will expand to fill the reels. RTP is 96% Lady of Avalon Symbol 3, 4 or 5 Lady of Avalon symbols in view awards 10 Free Spins. The Lady of Avalon symbol is both a wild symbol and a line win symbol during the base game. The Lady of Avalon symbol substitutes for all symbols except the special symbol selected during Free Spins. Free Spins Bonus 3, 4 or 5 Lady of Avalon symbols in view at the end of a spin will award 10 Free Spins. The Free Spins Bonus contains a randomly selected expanding special symbols. "If 3, 4 or 5 Lady of Avalon symbols are in view during Free Spins, an additional 10 Free Spins will be awarded. " Free Spins use the same award card as the base game spin that triggered the feature. Special Symbol The special symbol is selected from those above at the start of the Free Spins Bonus. If special symbol land on a reels and create a win, all other instances of this symbol will expand to fill the reel. When special symbols expand, win lines are evaluated on the expanded reels, ignoring any unexpanded reels. Special symbols pay from left to right using all 10 win lines and use the same awards as the base game. Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button.
