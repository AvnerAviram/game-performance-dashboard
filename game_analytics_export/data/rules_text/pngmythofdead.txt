Myth of Dead Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Symbol wins pay for any winning payline combination without any gaps. Only the highest win is paid per active win line. Game Rules MYTH OF DEAD is a 5-reel, 3-row game with 10 paylines. 3 or more SCATTER symbols on any reel trigger FREE SPINS mode. 3 X SCATTER awards 10 FREE SPINS. 4 X SCATTER awards 15 FREE SPINS. 5 X SCATTER awards 20 FREE SPINS. SCATTER symbols can’t land during FREE SPINS. INSTANT PRIZES are attached to God Symbols and their values are random at every spin. INSTANT PRIZES can be collected during WILD RE-SPIN and FREE SPINS. The possible prize values are 1x, 2x, 5x total bet during the WILD RE-SPIN. During FREE SPINS the possible prize values are 1x, 2x, 5x, 10x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 250x total bet. Landed WILD symbols collect INSTANT PRIZE values during the WILD RE-SPIN and FREE SPINS. WILD RE-SPIN can trigger randomly during the base game. WILDS substitute for all other symbols, except SCATTERS. At the beginning of FREE SPINS, one of four FREE SPIN BOOSTERS is randomly selected. Pyramid Power BOOSTER increases the frequency of landing God Symbols. The Scarab Scramble BOOSTER increases the frequency of landing WILD symbols. The Ankh Ascend BOOSTER increases the multiplier for the INSTANT PRIZE on the God Symbols by x1. The Khopesh BOOSTER awards 3 extra FREE SPINS. Landed WILD symbols are added to the CORONATION PATH panel during FREE SPINS. Each 4th WILD symbol landing during the FREE SPINS feature awards 5 extra FREE SPINS and increases the multiplier for the INSTANT PRIZES on the God Symbols by x1. CORONATION PATH has a total of 12 reachable coronations. The INSTANT PRIZE multiplier will be x2 by reaching the 4th FINAL CORONATION, x3 by reaching the 8th CORONATION PATH, and x4 by reaching the 12th CORONATION PATH. In the case of starting the FREE SPINS with the Ankh Ascend BOOSTER, the INSTANT PRIZE multiplier will be x2 by default, x3 by reaching the 4th CORONATION PATH, x4 by reaching the 8th CORONATION PATH, and x5 by reaching the 12th CORONATION PATH. The average RTP is 94.20%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 3000 times total bet. Last modification date: 12/4/2024
