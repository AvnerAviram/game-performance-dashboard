Roma Bwin UPPER GAME IN ORDER TO PLAY THIS GAME IT WILL BE REQUIRED TO HAVE ENOUGH BONUS AND CREDITS. WHENEVER A PRIZE IS OBTAINED, UNLESS IT IS A BLUE 7 PRIZE, THE PLAYER WILL BE OFFERED TO EITHER RISK A PORTION OF THE PRIZE OR TO COLLECT IT, UNTIL THERE ARE NO MORE PORTIONS LEFT. CHOOSING TO RISK A PORTION MAY ALLOW THE PLAYER TO EITHER OBTAIN THE DOUBLE OF THAT PORTION OR THE EQUIVALENT VALUE OF THAT PORTION IN BONUS. MAIN GAME IN THIS GAME, EACH PRIZE COULD EITHER BE RISKED, BY PRESSING THE PLAY BUTTON, OR TO BE COLLECTED, BY PRESSING THE COLLECT BUTTON. IN CASE THE PRIZE IS RISKED, IT WILL EITHER BE GIVEN THE AMOUNT OF A ONE LEVEL HIGHER PRIZE OR THE EQUIVALENT AMOUNT OF BONUS. ONCE THE LINE OF THE THREE BELLS IS REACHED, THE GAME BECOMES AN UP DOWN, WHERE THE POSSIBLE PRIZES GO UP OR DOWN IN THE PRIZE PLAN. NUDGES THE ADVANCE FUNCTION ALLOWS THE PLAYER TO MOVE REELS FORWARD ONE PLACE SEEKING A WINNING COMBINATION. THE NUMBER OF ADVANCES CAN BE BETWEEN 1 AND 4. AUTO WHEN NUDGES ARE ACTIVATED, IT ALLOWS YOU TO ROTATE THE REELS AUTOMATICALLY, ALWAYS RESULTING IN THE BEST OPTION FOR THE PLAYER. IF THE BUTTON IS PERMANENTLY LIT, THIS MEANS THAT THE AUTO OPTION IS ACTIVATED. OTHERWISE, IF THE BUTTON IS FLASHING, THIS INDICATES THAT THE AUTO OPTION IS NOT ACTIVATED. HOLDS ALLOWS UP TO TWO REELS TO BE HELD UP DURING A GAME TO INCREASE THE PROBABILITY OF GETTING PRIZE. AUTO PLAY ACTIVATE THE AUTOMATIC GAME MODE. THE GAMES WILL BE LAUNCHED AUTOMATICALLY IF THE PLAY BUTTON IS KEPT PRESSED AUTO RISK ACTIVATE THE AUTOMATIC GAME MODE. THE GAMES WILL BE LAUNCHED AUTOMATICALLY IF THE PLAY BUTTON IS KEPT PRESSED. THE MAXIMUM PRIZE THAT CAN BE OBTAINED IN A MINIGAME IS THE MAXIMUM PRIZE OF THE BET IN WHICH THE GAME HAS BEEN MADE. MINI-GAMES ARE ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES. MINIGAME COLISEUM 3 COLISEUMS IN THE TOP GAME GIVE ACCESS TO THE COLISEUM GAME, FOR THE DRAW FOR THE BEST PRIZE. MINIGAME ROULETTE 3 SHIELDS IN THE TOP GAME GIVE ACCESS TO THE ROULETTE GAME, PRESS PLAY TO SPIN THE ROULETTE WHEEL.
