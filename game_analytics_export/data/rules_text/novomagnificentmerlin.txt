Magnificent Merlin GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Magnificent Merlin™ is a 5 reel slot with 576 different ways to win and a jam-packed bonus feature. Main Game With Wild symbols and a Bonus Spins Feature game, this slot is sure to keep you entertained. Your aim in Magnificent Merlin™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 576 winning paths running from left to right. Bonus Spins Feature Look out for Coin symbols, when 5 or more Coin symbols appear anywhere on screen they trigger the Bonus Spins Feature. The Coin symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Coin lands on the reels, the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. All prizes on Coin symbols are paid at the conclusion of the bonus spins, or if the screen is completely filled with Coins. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on the screen are opened and filled with Coins. Coin symbol prizes can award, Major, Minor or Mini Jackpots or up to 40 x total bet. Getting a +1 Row Coin will add another row to the feature. Landing a Multiplier Coin will increase the multiplier on the left hand side of the reels, when the feature ends all Coin Prizes will be multiplied by this multiplier (except for the Grand Jackpot and the Major Jackpot). Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Coin, Multiplier Coin or Extra Row Coin symbols. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 9/5/2024
