Diamond Cash: Almighty Wolf Game Theme and Graphics Get ready to travel north for grand rewards in Almighty Wolf™. This 5 reel 25 line slot, is host to some exciting Features. Firstly, a Diamond Re-Spin Feature. Secondly, a Collect Feature combining all diamonds on screen into one big prize. Last but not least the Diamond Cash™ Jackpots Feature. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across the win lines in the base game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on a win line without any other symbols between them. The 'Wild' symbol substitutes all other symbols except Diamond and Collect Moon symbols and can therefore complete a winning combination. Collect Feature In the Base Game, the Moon acts as a Collect symbol. When less than 6 Diamond symbols appear on screen including the Collect Moon symbol, all prizes on Diamond symbols will be immediately paid at the end of the current round. The Collect Moon symbols appear only on reel 5. When 6 or more Diamond symbols appear including the Collect Moon symbol, the Collect Moon will become the value of the combined triggering Diamond prizes. Diamond Cash™ Jackpot Feature 6 or more Diamond symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Diamond Cash™ Jackpot Feature. The triggering Diamond symbols are held and 3 Bonus Spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Diamond lands on the reels, the Bonus Spins counter is reset to 3. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on the screen are filled with Diamonds. Diamond prizes can award Major, Minor and Mini Jackpots or up to 100x total bet. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Collect Moon and Diamond. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 3/13/2024
