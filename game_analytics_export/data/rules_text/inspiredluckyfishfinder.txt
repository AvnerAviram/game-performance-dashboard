Lucky Fish Finder Introduction Lucky Fish Finder is played on a 5x5 grid, where a player selects bubbles to reveal prizes. How to Play Select the number of boots on the grid by pressing the arrows next to the image of a boot. Select the 'CHANGE STAKE' button to increase or decrease the size of the bet. Game Rules Click on a bubble to reveal either a fish or a boot. Each fish you reveal increases your prize. After every successful pick, you can choose another bubble if you still have picks remaining, or you can cash out. If you reveal a boot, you will lose all the money you have accumulated and the game ends. You can choose to have 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10 or 15 boots on the grid. Choosing more boots means bigger prizes. Your winnings will be credited to your account immediately. You will lose your bet amount if you lose the game. General Information Game Recovery In the event of an unexpected interruption/termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. Payback Information The game complies with all relevant regulations and technical standards of the Gibraltar Gambling Commission, the Malta Gambling Commission and the Great Britain Gambling Commission. Your odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. This reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays, over an extended period of time. Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed £250,000.00. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every stake configuration. This game has been independently fairness tested. The expected payback for the game is between 93.60% and 96.96%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/23/2024
