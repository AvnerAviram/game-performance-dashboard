Ghostly Towers Main Game Ghostly Towers™ has a 5x3 reel window and 20 winlines. The game is orientated in Portrait mode for both Desktop and Mobile. There are 3 modifiers that can be triggered in the base game, and also during the Free Spins bonus. Wickedly Weird Wilds Modifier During a reel spin, Pierre may appear and add up to 15 Wild symbols to the reels. Mental Multiplier Mania Modifier During a reel spin, Pierre may appear and may add a multiplier of up to x20. Spooky Symbol Slither Modifier After a reel spin, Pierre may appear and swipe away one or more reel symbols. They are replaced by new matching symbols and any resulting wins are paid. Free Spins Bonus 3x Free Spins symbols awards 5 free spins and 6 random gems. 4x Free Spins symbols awards 5 free spins and 8 random gems. 5x Free Spins symbols awards 5 free spins and 10 random gems. Collect five matching gems to award Wickedly Weird Wilds, Mental Multiplier Mania or Spooky Symbol Slither bonuses. The free spins counter will lock while bonuses occur and gems count down. Rules All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. All wins pay only on lines in play. Scatter symbols in any position triggers free spins. Multiple bonuses can be won in one game. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are added. The paytable always shows the prizes for the currently selected bet and number of lines. Free spins are played at trigger bet and lines. Wild symbol may appear on all reels. All game outcomes are independent and randomly determined. The actual RTP is calculated as Total Win / Total Bet over a large number of games played and reflects the prizes awarded by a game as a percentage of all bets made. The expected RTP can vary widely in either direction for a small number of games due to statistical variance.
