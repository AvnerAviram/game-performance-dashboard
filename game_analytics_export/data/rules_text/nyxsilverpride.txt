Silver Pride Silver Pride is a Bet 40, 1024 ways 4x5 Reel slot game with a Free Game bonus. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.265% with any bet. The Win field displays winnings in credits. Spacebar can be used to spin. Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Free Game wins are added to wins displayed in the WIN meter. WILD WILD TREE is WILD and substitutes for all except DIAMOND. Wild Tree appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. SCATTER WIN 8, 15 OR 20 FREE GAMES with 3, 4, or 5 DIAMONDS respectively. All wins shown at 1 credit bet. All wins multiplied by the bet multiplier. GAME RULES Play bet 40 for 1024 ways. All wins shown at 1 credit bet. All wins multiplied by the bet multiplier. Win 8, 15 or 20 Free Games with a 3, 4 or 5 DIAMOND symbol win respectively. During Free Games each WILD TREE that appears on reels 2,3 or 4 will MULTIPLY any prize it is substituting in by either x2 or x3 up to total maximum of x27. 5 Additional Free Games are awarded if Any 2 scattered DIAMOND Symbols occur during any Free Game. Free Games can be retriggered. Free Games are played at the triggering bet. Different reel strips are used in Free Games. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except scatter which pays any. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For all symbols except scatters, winning combinations pay through any position on reels 1,2,3,4 & 5 respectively. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. How to Play 1: Choose your BET 2: SPIN MAIN GAME SCREEN BUTTONS: BET: The Bet that you want to play can be adjusted through the (UP) and (DOWN) buttons. The Max Bet button (coin icon) will automatically select the maximum bet. SPIN: Spins the reels. AUTOPLAY: Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays for a certain amount of spins. Options for spins range from 0 spins to 100 spins. STOP: Stop allow players to stop the reel spin immediately to see final results. STOP AUTOPLAY: Once auto play begins the SPIN button turns into a STOP AUTOPLAY button and the number of AUTOPLAYs remaining will be displayed in the STOP button. CONTINUE: During free games, this button when appears allow players to skip to the next free game. HOME: Accesses casino menu. SETTINGS: Accesses the SETTING page for BET and AUTOPLAY options.
