SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES TOTEM CRYSTAL Activates the MYSTIC TOTEM. May land in normal game and in Free Spins. MYSTIC TOTEM When activated, the Mystic Totem beams energy into random locked or unlocked tiles. Any locked tiles hit by the energy beam will uncover WILDS, while existing unlocked tiles will convert to identical symbols or additional Totem Crystals. The feature is repeated for each Totem Crystal which lands on the reels. LOCKED ROW The slot has one Locked Row in the normal game and an additional two when FREE SPINS are triggered. Tiles in Locked Rows can only be revealed by the MYSTIC TOTEM. All revealed tiles from a Locked Row will relock after the end of the spin, or at the end of a FREE SPINS round. The maximum possible number of Locked Rows is four in BONUS FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS SYMBOL Land three Free Spins symbols to unlock 10 FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS FREE SPINS awards two extra Locked Rows. If energy from the MYSTIC TOTEM selects a tile from one of the Locked Rows, it will be unlocked as a WILD for the initial spin. In subsequent Free Spins it will remain unlocked but will behave just like a regular tile on the slot, producing a random result on each spin but offering more potential ways to win. BONUS FREE SPINS Land three FREE SPINS scatter symbols during FREE SPINS to unlock ten more bonus rounds and one extra Locked Row. Extra FREE SPINS may be awarded multiple times, but the maximum number of Locked Rows on the slot is always four. WILD SYMBOL Substitutes for all paying symbols. Only occurs when a symbol on one of the Locked Rows is revealed for the first time. The prize value from one payline is equal to the total stake divided by the number of paylines, multiplied by the symbol multiplier advertised in the paytable. Where multiple wins occur on multiple win lines, the total win values are summed. If more than one win occurs on the same payline, the largest win is paid. Example: Total stake is 2. Total paylines are 20. Symbol multiplier for length of 5 is 300. The win for 1 payline with this symbol and length would be calculated as such: 2 / 20 * 300 = 30. The prize value from one way or cluster in a Ways or Winning Clusters game is equal to the stake multiplied by the symbol multiplier advertised in the paytable. Where multiple wins occur on multiple ways or clusters, the total win values are summed. Example: Total stake = 2. Symbol win multiplier = 2. Win = 2x2 = 4.
