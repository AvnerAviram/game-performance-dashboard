Bison Keno GAME OBJECTIVE: The BISON KENO game is an 80-spot KENO game with 20 number draws. The FREE DRAWS feature is triggered when 3 GOLD COINS are hit. BONUS NUMBERS can be triggered randomly after every game. HOW TO PLAY: Pick your Numbers: Choose between 3 and 10 numbers, the numbers chosen are highlighted. BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘+’ or ‘- ‘buttons either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. C licking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET. PLAY: The PLAY button starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the centre bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode. QUICK PICK: Selects the chosen number of picks randomly with each press. CLEAR: Will clear all the current selected numbers by player. SPEED: The speed can be adjusted with 3 settings. PAYTABLE: View is displayed on game screen based on 3 to 10 numbers selected by player. VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF. CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Players current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen. AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play. For UK/Ontario users the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. To configure AUTOPLAY: Click the AUTOPLAY button then select the required number of plays. AUTOPLAY begins automatically. The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time. In play-money mode, the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP): Tap the Hamburger Menu Icon to view Game Rules . The game rules are displayed under the ‘i’ icon. Tap the close icon to go back to the game play . GAME RULES: BISON KENO is a standard 80 spot KENO game with a 20 number draw. BET values can be changed by clicking on ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Pick between 3 to 10 numbers out of 80 numbers by clicking the number on the game screen. The numbers picked will be highlighted. The first 3 numbers picked will be GOLD COINS irrespective of the numbers picked. The GOLD COIN numbers can be altered by deselecting them and selecting a different number. Clicking on PLAY begins the game with the currently selected BET. 20 numbers out of 80 will be randomly drawn and highlighted on the screen. Bison can stampede after any game and award 3, 4, 5, 8, 10 or 12 BONUS NUMBERS in addition to the 20 numbers already drawn. If the drawn numbers match with player-picked numbers, then these numbers will be highlighted and a win will be awarded based on the KENO PAYTABLE. The PLAY button appears only when 3 GOLD COINS are picked. The KENO PAYTABLE is determined by how many numbers are selected by the player and appears only when a minimum of 3 numbers are picked. Payout will not be awarded without choosing 3 numbers or payout will not be available for the matched picks in the corresponding PAYTABLE. Payout information is updated dynamically on the in game PAYTABLE prior to submitting bets. Only highest win will be paid to the player. “MARKED” displays the total number of numbers picked by the player. “HIT” displays the total number of drawn numbers matching with picked numbers. “QUICK PICK” selects the chosen number of picks randomly with each press. “CLEAR” will clear all the current selected numbers. “SPEED” is used to adjust the speed of the client picks with 3 different speed options available. All pays are shown in USD currency. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 94.11%. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “History” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “Game logs” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. The MIN BET is #{uiConfig.minBet}, and the MAX BET is #{uiConfig.maxBet}. FREE DRAWS: Hitting 3 GOLD COINS will award 2, 3, 4, or 5 FREE DRAWS. FREE DRAWS are played at the BET of the triggering draw. The GOLD COIN positions will be retained throughout the FREE DRAWS round. The initial selection of the player picks remains constant throughout the FREE DRAWS. In FREE DRAWS, the number of picks remains the same as the number of picks at which the FREE DRAWS are triggered. The 3 GOLD COINS in the game show the number of FREE DRAWS remaining and the counter decreases by 1 for every FREE DRAW. A random multiplier can be awarded after any FREE DRAW among ‘ 2x, 3x, or 5x’ which multiplies the current win. For every GOLD COIN hit during FREE DRAWS an additional FREE DRAW is awarded to the player. FREE DRAWS can retrigger any number of times during this feature. Stampeding bison run across the screen and awards 5, 8, 10, or 12 BONUS NUMBERS after any FREE DRAW randomly. BONUS NUMBERS: BONUS NUMBERS can be awarded after both main and FREE DRAW games. Stampeding bison run across the screen and award 3, 4, 5, 8, 10, or 12 BONUS NUMBERS after any draw in main game and 5, 8, 10, or 12 BONUS NUMBERS after any FREE DRAW randomly. Wins are evaluated once BONUS NUMBERS are drawn. Only the highest win will be paid to the player after drawing the extra numbers. If the GOLD COINS are drawn in the BONUS NUMBERS , FREE DRAWS are retriggered. Browser Button Panel Commands: Click on RULES to view the detailed rules of the game as a web page. Click on HISTORY to view the players game history. The HISTORY functionality is applicable only for the real-money game play. Click on REAL MONEY/ DEMO PLAY to switch between REAL-MONEY or Demo-MONEY mode. Click on the DEPOSIT button to deposit money into the player account. RETURN TO THE PLAYER: The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 94.11%. FAQ’s: I could not complete the game. What should I do? If you are disconnected in the middle of the game, the software platform will automatically complete the current game cycle. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the “History” button once you have logged back into the website. If you are disconnected after triggering or in the middle of the FREE DRAWS feature, the player will be able to start the disconnected FREE DRAWS, if not already started, or resume from where it was disconnected. What should I do if my BALANCE is too low to place a BET? Click the DEPOSIT button at bottom of the game screen and deposit funds into your account.
