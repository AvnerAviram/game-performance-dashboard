Spirit of the Lamp Game Features Raining Reels A cascading feature where you can win multiple times on every wager. Winnings symbols are removed and replaced with new symbols; leading to exciting chain reactions and more potential wins! Walking Wild Any wild not involved in a cascade will trigger the Walking Wild feature where it will walk to one position to the left. Free Spins Bonus 4 or more Cascading Reel wins will trigger the Free Spins bonus. 7, 10, 15, 20, 25 free spins are awarded for 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8+ consecutive wins. Additional free spins are awarded for 3 or more consecutive Cascading Reel wins in the bonus, as displayed on the bonus meter. Spreading Wild At the start of each free spin, a magic zone will release from the magic lamp. A different magic zone usually covers part of the reels during each free spin. If a Genie WILD lands into the magic zone, or a Walking Genie WILD walks into the magic zone, all the symbols under the magic zone will transform into WILDs. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 96.19% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $96.19 Rules Total bet is 25 lines x coin size. 25 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/23/2024
