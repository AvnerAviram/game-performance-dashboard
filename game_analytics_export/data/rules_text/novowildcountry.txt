Wild Country GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Venture into the untamed American wilderness in Wild Country™ In this "ALLPAYS" 5 reel slot your aim is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 1024 winning paths running from left to right. In this frontier land the rugged mountain peak is the BONUS symbol. Land 3 or more on the reels and be rewarded with up to 20 Free Games. The jeweled paw is the WILD symbol and substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS (mountain). During the Free Games feature the Wild (paw) has the ability to multiply any game win up to x27. Main Game The Grizzly Bear is stacked and the highest paying symbol, Grizzly bears in any win combination to unleash screen shaking wins. Eagle, Cougar, Deer and Salmon fill the game screen. The jeweled paw is the WILD symbol and substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS (mountain). Free Games Feature 3, 4 or 5 Bonus (mountain) awards 8, 15 or 20 Free Games respectively. Free Games can be re-trriggered and in addition to this, get 2 scattered Bonus (mountain) for 5 extra free games. During the free games the Wild symbol (paw) will multiply any wins by x2 or x3, for a maximum possible multiplier of x27. Rules Wild substitutes for all symbols, except scattered 'Mountain'. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on 1024 winways, excluding Scatters. Highest win only paid per winway. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RTP The theoretical return to player for this game is 95.16% • The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
