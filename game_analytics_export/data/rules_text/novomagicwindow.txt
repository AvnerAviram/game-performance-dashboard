Magic Window Game Theme and Graphics Peep through the Magic Window and discover the world behind. Spin the reels to open three or more windows and watch out for symbols getting golden. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across active win lines in the normal game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on an active win line without any other symbols coming between them. The "Magic Window" symbol substitutes all other symbols and can therefore complete a winning combination. And what's more: land 3 or more "Magic Window" symbols anywhere on the reels to trigger 10 free spins. Feature Trigger: 3 or more SCATTER trigger 10 free games and open to randomly reveal a first SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOL Description: After a retrigger, an additional SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOL is randomly chosen from the remaining set of symbols (except scatter). Up to 9 SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOLS are possible. Each SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOL expands to cover all three positions on the respective reels and pays on any position. Free games can be won again during the feature. Feature top prize: 5000 * credits bet per line Feature hit rate: Approx. 170-210 games Rules SCATTER substitutes for all symbols except scattered SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOLS. All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins.
