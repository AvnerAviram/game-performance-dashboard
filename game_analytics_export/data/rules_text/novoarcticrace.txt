Arctic Race Brace yourself as the race is on for the grand prize in Arctic Race™. Mush, mush, mush these dogs are in a rush, your teams aim is to land 5 identical winning symbols form left to right on one of the 243 winning paths. Main Game In this alpine location you will find our intrepid racer ready to go, he is the most rewarding symbol, get 5 left to right and he will return you the greatest reward. Your team of dogs are the Wild symbol and will add to any combination on screen. An alpine vista, sled and campfire make up the remaining game symbols. Feature Collect 3 golden trophies on reel 1, 3 and 5 to be eligible to compete in the Arctic Race. Strap in for the TROPHY RESPIN FEATURE where you will collect trophies on 15 individual reels. Here you will hold and spin the reels in the hunt for more free spins and cash prizes for every trophy collected. You get 3 initial spins, every time you collect an additional trophy your spin counter is reset to 3. When there are no spins remaining its time to collect your prize ! During the sprint to the finish line you have the chance to win up to x100 for any trophy collected on screen. Rules Wild substitutes for all symbols, except scattered 'Trophy'. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. Highest win only paid per winway. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to player (RTP) is 95.17%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/16/2020
