Great Fortune Game Theme and Graphics Great Fortunes can be found in this "ALL PAYS", 5x4 video slot, with 1024 ways to win in each game. The 'Emperor', along with his 'Dragon', 'Phoenix', 'Koi' and 'Frog', will bring you great luck and all the wins, while the 'Yin Yang' symbols can multiply wins by up to x27 during the Free Games Feature. Main Game The 'Emperor' symbol is stacked on all reels, and awards the highest wins. The 'Yin Yang' symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except the 'Gold Coin'. The 'Gold Coin' symbol appears on all reels, and leads the way to the fortunes of the Free Games Feature Feature 3, 4 or 5 'Gold Coin' symbols appearing anywhere on the reels award 8, 15 or 20 Free Games, respectively. Free Games can be re-triggered. In addition, 2 'Gold Coin' symbols will award an extra 5 Free Games. During Free Games, each Wild 'Yin Yang' symbol in a winning combination will multiply the win by x2 or x3. A maximum multiplier of x27 can be achieved. Rules Wild 'Yin Yang' substitues for all symbols, except scattered 'Gold Coin'. All pays are for wins of left to right, except scatter 'Gold Coin', which pay any. All prizes are on 1024 win ways, except scatter 'Gold Coin'. Highest win only paid per win way. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
