Triple Topaz Game Rules The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 94.9%. Malfunction voids all pays and play This game is played at 720 ways and all 5 reels are always in play. Select Bet Multiplier Click the Spin button to spin the reels Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits stated Highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Double Topaz symbols substitute as 2-of-a-kind Topaz symbols. Triple Topaz symbols substitute as 3-of-a-kind Topaz symbols. Wild symbols substitute for any symbol except for the Free Games Bonus and Find Your Fortune Bonus symbols Your winnings are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier Free Games bonus 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2,3, and 4 awards the Free Games Bonus Awards 6 free games. Free Games are awarded on a separate screen with different reels Free games are played at the same bet multiplier as the triggering game. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards 3 additional free games. FIND YOUR FORTUNE BONUS Three Find Your Fortune Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Find Your Fortune Bonus . Select a green, yellow, or red gem to set the available awards and difficulty for the Find Your Fortune Bonus The Find Your Bonus features 5 tiers. On each tier, select a blue gem to reveal a credit award or Bonus Complete symbol and advance to the next tier. The Find Your Fortune Bonus ends when a Bonus Complete symbol is selected, or when 5 credit awards have been selected. Last modification date: 1/9/2019
