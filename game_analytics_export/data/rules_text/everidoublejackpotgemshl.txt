Double Jackpot Gems High Limit GAME RULES SPECIAL SYMBOLS Diamond, Ruby and Amethyst symbols are wild and substitute for Seven and Bar symbols that also land on the payline. One Diamond symbol in a winning Sevens or Bars combinations pays 2x prize value. Two Diamond symbols in a winning Sevens or Bars combination pays 4x prize value. Only highest winner paid per line. DOUBLE JACKPOT PROGRESSIVE When 3 Wild Gems land on an active payline, the associated Progressive tier is awarded. See Progressive paytable for prize values. Top Progressive is available only at max lines bet. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers.
