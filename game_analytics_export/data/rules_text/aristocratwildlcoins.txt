FREE GAMES FEATURE A win of 5, 4 or 3 scattered vase awards 20, 10 or 7 free games respectively. If Vase substitutes in a win the total number of free games triggered is multiplied by 2, 3 or 5. Extra Bonus Wilds Feature can be triggered during the Free Games Feature. Free Games Feature can be triggered again during the Free Games Feature. TOTAL BET is the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Press the PLAY button or touch the FREE GAMES WON! banner to start the Free Games Feature. The free games will start automatically after 2 minutes. GAME RULES All wins shown in credits. Choose your TOTAL BET. 30 lines are played for line cost of 60 credits. Minimum bet 60 credits. TOTAL BET is the line cost multiplied by the bet multiplier. Select +,- or MAX BET to choose your TOTAL BET. The PLAY button will immediately commence play. While the reels are spinning you may press the PLAY button to stop them except during Extra Bonus Wilds Feature. Touch CREDIT, TOTAL BET or WIN meter to show in currency or credits. All wins on played lines only except scatters. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier and the number of lines played. Game RTP: 94.54%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS.
