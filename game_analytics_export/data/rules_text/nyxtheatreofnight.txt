Theatre of Night Game Rules Play 10 to 25 lines Payouts are made according to the Paytable All wins are multiplied by the bet. Highest win only on each selected payline for either direction. Wins on different paylines are added. All wins occur on selected lines. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels and begin with rightmost reel and pay right to left. WILD substitutes for all other symbols. WILD appears only on reels 2,3 and 4 during base game. WILD can appear on all reels during FIREFLY RE-SPINS only. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. FIREFLY RE-SPINS FEATURE 6 Firefly Re-spins are awarded whenever WILD appears on 2,3 and 4 reels. Reel 3 becomes a STACKED WILD during the FireFly Re-spins Selected symbols will become Wilds during the Re-spins Firefly respins feature cannot be re-triggered Firefly Re-spins are played at the lines and bet of the trigger game. How to Play The Lines and Bet that you want to play can be adjusted through the (-) and (+) buttons alongside the respective options. The Max button for Lines or Bet will automatically select the maximum for the respective field. Note: You can also select the amount of lines you wish to play by clicking on the line tag number on the side of the reels. Spin: Spins the reels. Autoplay: Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays for a certain amount of spins. Options for spins range from 5 spins to 50 spins. Info: Access pay awards for winning symbol combinations. Also contains the rules for the game. The game displays all your details in the following display fields: Balance: Displays your total balance Bet: Displays the staked amount per line to be played. Lines: Displays the amount of line(s) to be played. Win: Displays the amount won in the spin Total Bet: The total wager staked. It consists of the Bet multiplied by the amount of lines being played.
