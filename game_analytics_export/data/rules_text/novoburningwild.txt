Burning Wild Main Game Burning Wild™ is played on 5 reels, each 3 rows high, with 5 lines always in play. All winning combinations run from left to right on win lines, except Scatter symbols. Gold Scatter symbols appear on all reels. Blue Scatter symbols may only appear on reels 1, 3 and 5. The Wild Firebird appears on reels 2, 3 and 4. If there is a chance for a win, the Wild Firebird expands over all positions on the reel. Gamble Feature It is possible to gamble any win up to 5 times with a maximum stake of 500x total bet. For higher wins or during autoplay, gambling is not possible. Rules All pays are for combinations left to right except scatters. Scatters pay for combinations of any. All wins on selected lines except scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Line prizes are multiplied by bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. RTP: 95.18 The maximum win from a single game is 196.800 Last modification date: 11/10/2020
