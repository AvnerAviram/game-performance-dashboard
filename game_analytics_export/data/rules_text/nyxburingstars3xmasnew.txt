Burning Stars 3 Xmas The game has 9 independent reels on a 3x3 grid. Wins are paid for at least 4 identical symbols on any of the reels. When two symbol combinations of the same value are drawn, the win will be paid only for the combination with the symbol that has the higher position on the paytable. Only the highest win is paid. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbol. SPECIAL FEATURES Hold the Jackpot Bonus A draw of at least 3 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Bonus symbols that activated the Hold the Jackpot Bonus and those drawn during the bonus stay on the reels. 3 respins are granted at the beginning of the bonus round. Every time a Bonus symbol is drawn, it resets the number of Respins to 3. The bonus ends when all respins are finished or the all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. When Hold the Jackpot Bonus ends, the prize is paid for the number of collected Bonus symbols, according to the paytable. Hold the Jackpot Bonus is played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated the bonus. Gamble Feature The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the current win counter and returns to the main game. The gamble feature has seven rounds. Low volatility: The payouts you receive in this mode are more frequent but in most cases smaller. This level is perfect for players who prefer shorter game sessions. High volatility: In this mode you can hit BIG WINs but they are not as frequent as winning in Low mode. High is an excellent mode for players who are not afraid of taking a risk to win big.
