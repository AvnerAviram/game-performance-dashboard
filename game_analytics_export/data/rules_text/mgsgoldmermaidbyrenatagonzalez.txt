Gold Mermaid by Renata Gonzalez Untitled Document PRIZE PLAN MAIN GAME 10 award lines. Line wins are paid from left to right. FREE SPINS Obtaining a 3 Scatters figure will award you with 10 free spins. These spins do not consume credits and accumulate all the prizes obtained. You can get more free spins within Free Spins. EXPANDING SYMBOL A special EXPANDING SYMBOL is randomly chosen to help you win more on free spins. Watch it expand to cover the reel when more prizes are in store! Symbols can appear anywhere along active line to win! BONUS GAME MINIGAME SELECTION Three 'Bonus' figures, or more, give access to the mini-game selection screen. Several entries to the mini-game selection screen will unlock, one after the other, accesses to bonus games. BUBBLES BONUS GAME If the first mini-game is selected the mini-game bubbles will be accessed. Is a draw among 5 prizes. The player must stop the draw at the time he wants. The final prize will be the corresponding prize that is illuminated. ROULETTE BONUS GAME If the second mini-game is selected the mini-game roulette will be accessed. The goal of the game is to spin a roulette with 8 prizes. The final winning is the prize marked on the roulette when the roulette stops. PEARL BONUS GAME If you select the third mini-game you will access the mini-game pearls. The game consists of a ring of 3 quantities that will be hidden. The player chooses a possibility by getting the prize shown. PRIZE LINES RTP is 96.01% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000
