Breaking Bad Collect Em and Link 5-reel 30-line slot The objective of Breaking Bad™: Collect Em & Link™ is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. To play the game: Press ► on the entry screen to enter the main game. You can also press DON'T SHOW NEXT TIME to skip the entry screen the next time you play. To open bet settings, press the BET button. Select your total bet in the drop-down menu and press Confirm. Press TURBO to activate Turbo Mode, making the reels spin faster. Pressing the TURBO button again ends the Turbo Mode session. Press the SPIN button to spin the reels with the current bet. In the case of a winning spin, the WIN field displays the accumulating winnings. On a given payline, only the highest winning combination pays while simultaneous winnings on different paylines are accumulated. Wins are calculated according to the paytable. Your win will equal your line bet multiplied by the corresponding value according to the paytable. Paytable: To open the paytable, press INFO. To navigate the paytable, scroll up or down, or swipe the screen. To close the reference pages and return to the game, press the back arrow button. Paylines: This game is played with a fixed number of 30 active paylines. The total bet per game round is the line bet × 30. Paylines are represented by lines that appear over the reels as illustrated in the paytable. Winning combinations must start from the leftmost reel, and the same symbol has to be on consecutive reels and appear on the same payline. There is a difference between the line bet and the total bet. The line bet shows how much is being bet on a single payline. The total bet shows how much is being bet in total on a single spin. Payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet. Wild Symbol The symbol with Heisenberg and the word 'WILD' on it is the WILD symbol in the game. The WILD symbol can substitute for all symbols except Collect'Em™ or any kind of Coin symbol. Collect'Em™ Symbol The symbol with words 'COLLECT'EM' on it is the Collect'Em™ symbol in the game. The Collect'Em™ symbol can land on reels 1 and/or 5 only during the main game, and on any reel during the Free Games feature. During the main game, landing a Collect'Em™ symbol on reels 1 and/or 5 will collect all coin symbols - Prize Coin, Cash Coin and Free Games Coin - which landed on the reels on that spin. During the main game, when a Collect'Em™ symbol lands on reels 1 and/or 5 and a Link Coin or a Mega Link Coin symbols land anywhere on the reels, the Heisenberg Collect & Link feature is triggered. Coin Symbols: There are 5 Coin symbols in the game: The symbol with the cash value on it is the Cash Coin symbol. For example, a 3.00 symbol. The symbol with the +(number) on it is the Free Games Coin symbol. For example, a +3 symbol. The symbol with the diamond on it is the Prize Coin symbol. The symbol with the word 'Link' on it is the Link Coin symbol. The symbol with the words 'Mega Link' on it is the Mega Link Coin symbol. Collect'Em™ Feature The Collect'Em™ Feature is triggered by simultaneously landing a Collect'Em™ symbol on reels 1 and/or 5 during the main game, or on reels 1-5 during the Free Games, and a coin symbol anywhere on the reels on the same spin. Each instance of a coin symbol awards you the cash prize or the number of free games accordingly. Note: Cash Coin symbols contain values of x0.5, x1, x1.5, x2, x3, x5, x10, x15 multiplied by the total bet. Note: Free Games Coin symbols contain values of +3, +4, +5, +7, +10. When initiating a new spin, all the coin symbols will reset. Heisenberg Collect & Link Feature The Heisenberg Collect & Link feature is triggered by simultaneously landing a Collect'Em™ symbol on reels 1 and/or 5 during the main game, or on reels 1-5 during the Free Games, and the Link Coin or the Mega Link Coin symbol anywhere on the reels simultaneously. Press ► to start the feature. Once the feature starts, the Link Coin becomes a Cash Coin symbol with a random value of 2x, 3x or 5x the total bet, the Mega Link Coin turns into a Cash Coin symbol with 1 of the following values: 5x, 10x or 15x the total bet. Note: when the feature is triggered by the Mega Link Coin symbol, purple Cash Coin symbols can appear on the reels with 1 of the following values: x25, x40, and x100. Note: All Coin and Collect'Em™ symbols lock in position for the entirety of the feature. The player is awarded 3 respins. During the feature, if a Coin symbol lands on the reels, the counter resets to 3. Note: Multiple Coin symbols can appear on the reels simultaneously. The feature ends when the number of respins reaches 0 or the reels are filled with symbols. All prizes in view are collected once the feature ends. Press ► on the winning summary screen to return to the main game. Walter's Stash Grand Prize During the Heisenberg Collect & Link feature, if the reels are filled with Coin symbols, the Walter's Stash Grand Prize is awarded. Note: Walter's Stash Grand Prize is an additional prize to all collected Coin symbols. Breaking Bad Free Games During the main game, when a Collect'Em™ symbol lands on reel 5, and 1 or more Free Games Coin symbols land anywhere on the reels, the Breaking Bad Free Games feature is triggered. Each Free Games Coin symbol that lands awards the number of free games shown on it. If 2 or more Free Games Coin symbols land on the reels, the total number of free games won is equal to the sum of all numbers on each Free Games Coins symbol. Press ► to start the Free Games feature. During free games, the Collect'Em™ symbol can appear on all reels and locks on the reels for 3 spins, whenever it lands. Note: Each Collect'Em™ symbol has its own counter of spins on the top-right corner of the symbol. During free games, each Collect'Em™ symbol collects all Cash Coin, Prize Coin and/or Free Games Coin symbols that land, awarding the respective prize once for each Collect'Em™ symbol on the reels. If the Heisenberg Collect & Link feature is triggered during Free Games, when Free Games resume, all Collect'Em™ symbols are transferred to the Free Games grid. Note: The counter on the Collect'Em™ symbols remains frozen during the Heisenberg Collect & Link feature and resumes once the Free Games continue. Free Games can be retriggered by simultaneously landing a Collect'Em™ and a Free Games Coin symbol anywhere on the reels. Press ► on the winning summary screen to return to the main game. Magnets, B**ch! The Magnets, B**ch! feature can only trigger on reel 1 or 5 during the main game. If you miss the Collect'Em™ symbol, there is a chance a magnet will appear; the reels will shake and the Collect'Em™ symbol may be pulled back onto reel 1 or 5. Collect'Em™ Prizes Simultaneously landing a Collect'Em™ symbol on reel 1 and/or 5 during the main game, or on any reel during Free Games, and a Prize Coin symbol anywhere on the reels trigger a Collect'Em™ Prize. There are 4 types of prizes that can be won: GRAND, MAJOR, MINOR and MINI. Each prize is a fixed amount based on the bet value. The awarded prize is selected randomly. Each prize can be won multiple times if more than 1 Collect'Em™ is locked on the reels during Free Games. Multiple Prize Coin symbols can land on the same spin. Return to Player: The average percentage return to player (RTP) is 93.56%.
