Joker 10000 Deluxe Joker 10000 Deluxe is a 3-reel slot game with a supermeter game. The game has 1 to 5 pay lines. Wins are awarded for 3 of a kind symbols from left-to-right, and 2x or 3x joker wins when playing the supermeter game. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is between 92% and 97%. To win you must match 3 symbols on an active payline. Wins in the reel game can be gambled red or black, to double your prize. Alternatively press SPIN to transfer your winning to the supermeter where the 10000 prize can be won. If winnings in the base game are 5001 coins or more, this win cannot be gambled or played in Supermeter game. Each game costs 2x the selected coin value, multiplied by the number of selected pay lines. GAME RULES Payouts are made according to the Paytable All wins multiplied by the coin value Highest win paid per winning combination Coinciding wins are added All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol 3 of a kind wins are paid once only Malfunction voids all pays and plays Supermeter Game When a win is obtained in the reel game (and when playing 5 lines only) you can transfer it to the supermeter by pressing the SPIN button. In the supermeter game, the bet is fixed at 20 coins. Joker symbols are active and any 2 symbols awards a mystery prize of between 20 and 10000. Any 3 Joker symbols in views pays three mystery prizes, and 3 Joker symbols on a payline will award the 10000 prize. Supermeter wins cannot be gambled via the red or black game. You can choose to collect your winnings at any time by pressing the COLLECT button. The win will be transferred to your balance, and the supermeter game is ended. Supermeter Gamble Pressing the “SUPER GAMBLE” button when in the Supermeter Game will start the Supermeter Gamble. You can select your gamble odds by pressing the '+' and '-' buttons. Pressing the "0 - 10000" button selects the odds for the 10000x prize. To gamble, press the "GAMBLE" button. If you do not wish to play the Supermeter Gamble, and return to the supermeter game, press the "EXIT" button. The Supermeter Gamble runs at 100% RTP. Winnings are automatically collected if the value of the Supermeter is 10000 or greater.
