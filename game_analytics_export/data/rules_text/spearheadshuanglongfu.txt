Shuang Long Fu Shuang Long Fu is a 5 reel by 3 row, 243 ‘ways pays’ videoslot featuring Exotic Wilds frequently occurring and rewarding different multipliers each spin as well as a chance at the prizepot bonus and the fortune collector. There are three different Exotic Wilds in this game, [Green Dragon], [Red Dragon], and [Double Dragon]. At the end of each spin, every [Red Dragon] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel and have a random multiplier assigned from 3x to 7x. At the end of each spin, every [Green Dragon] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel and have a random multiplier assigned from 2x to 5x. At the end of each spin, any reel that contains both a [Green Dragon] and [Red Dragon] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel as a [Double Dragon] and have a random multiplier assigned from 5x to 50x. If exotic wilds land on multiple reels during the same spin, wins that include multiple Exotic Wilds take the sum of all the Exotic Multipliers included in the spin. [Green Dragon] Substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Red Dragon], and [Double Dragon]. [Red Dragon] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Green Dragon], and [Double Dragon]. [Double Dragon] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Red Dragon], and [Green Dragon]. [Green Dragon], [Red Dragon], and [Double Dragon] only count as 1 way per reel. Free Spins and Choose your Volatility Options Landing 3 or more [Scatter] during the base game triggers the free spins Choose 1 of 3 Free Spin options for Medium to Extreme Volatility Multiplier Boost, Extra Exotic, Extreme Exotic The Multiplier Boost option awards 12 Initial Free Spins with Medium Volatility 3 [Scatter] Award an additional 12 Free Spins. Each [Special symbol] awards an extra free spin and increases the multiplier range of all Exotic Wild types from 1 to 3. An increase range is done by adding the random amount from 1 to 3 to all the multipliers. For example, if the multiplier range of the Exotic Wilds is increased by 3, then the range of [Green Dragon] increases from 2x to 5x to a new range of 5x to 8x, the range of [Red Dragon] increases from 3x to 7x to 6x to 10x, and the range of [Double Dragon] wilds are increases from a range of 5x to 50x to 8x to 53x. The increased range of the Exotic Wilds apply to all wilds for all remaining free spins, including the spin that the [Special Symbol] has landed. As more [Special Symbol] land during free spins the Exotic Wild multipliers will continue to increase further. The increased multiplier from Special Symbols is capped at 20x. The Extra Exotic option awards 10 Initial Free Spins with High Volatility 3 [Scatter] Award an additional 10 Free Spins. Collect [Red Tokens] to convert more symbols to [Red Dragon] wilds Collecting 3 [Red Tokens] will convert all [Hong Bao Envelope] Symbols into [Red Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Red Tokens] will convert all [Orange] Symbols into [Red Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Red Tokens] will convert all [Dumpling] Symbols into [Red Dragon] Collect [Green Tokens] to convert more symbols to [Green Dragon] wilds Collecting 3 [Green Tokens] will convert all [Feng Shui Cat] Symbols into [Green Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Green Tokens] will convert all [Feng Shui Frog] Symbols into [Green Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Green Tokens] will convert all [Feng Shui Fish] Symbols into [Green Dragon] The Extreme Exotic option awards 8 Initial Free Spins with Extreme Volatility 3 [Scatter] award an additional 8 Free Spins. [Green Dragon] during the Extreme Exotic free spins option always pay from 10x to 50x. [Red Dragon] during the Extreme Exotic free spins option always pay from 10x to 100x. [Double Dragon] during the Extreme Exotic free spins option always pay from 10x to 500x. Furthermore, landing multiple exotic wilds on the same spin have compounded win potential as the total win multiplier is calculated by taking the product of all multipliers included in the win together rather than the sum. Scatter Coin Prizes Scatter Coins show prize amounts visible when landing on the reels. In addition to the multiplier ranges on the multiplier wheel, some multipliers on the wheel have a coin background. If a multiplier wheel lands on a multiplier with a coin background, the player will be awarded the sum of all scatter coin prize amounts on the reels, including any prizepots. If more than one exotic wild lands, it is possible to win the sum of all scatter coin prize amounts on the reels more than once as each multiplier that lands with a coin background awards the sum of all scatter coin prizes on the reels. Scatter Coin Prizes can be won in the base game, the multiplier boost free spins and the extreme Exoctic free spins only.
