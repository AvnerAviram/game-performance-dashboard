Slingo Elf Blitz SLINGO ELF BLITZ IS A MULTI-PICK ELF RACING THEMED SLINGO GAME.SELECT AN ELF AND REACH A MULTIPLIER ON THE TRACK FOR A CASH PRIZE. SELECT YOUR STAKE AND PRESS START GAME TO BEGIN. YOU HAVE 10 SPINS TO MATCH NUMBERS ON THE REEL WITH THE GRID. EACH SYMBOL ON THE REEL IS RED, BLUE, GREEN OR BLANK. A MATCH MOVES THE CORRESPONDING ELF ALONG THE TRACK. WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE COLUMN ABOVE. FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ADD AN ADDITIONAL SPIN. WILDS CAN BE RED, BLUE, GREEN OR BLANK AND MOVE THE CORRESPONDING ELF ALONG THE TRACK WHEN MAKING A MATCH. SUPER WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE GRID. SUPER WILDS CAN BE RED, BLUE, GREEN OR BLANK AND MOVE THE CORRESPONDING ELF ALONG THE TRACK WHEN MAKING A MATCH. SELECT AN ELF FOR EACH GAME. CROSSING THE FINISH LINE WITH YOUR ELF AWARDS A JACKPOT. IF YOUR ELF REACHES A MULTIPLIER (DISPLAYED ON THE TRACK) THEN YOU ARE AWARDED A CASH PRIZE. MULTIPLIER X INITIAL STAKE. ONLY THE HIGHEST VALUE REACHED ON THE TRACK IS AWARDED AT THE END OF THE GAME. EXTRA SPINS EXTRA SPINS ARE AVAILABLE AFTER EACH GAME. THE PRICE OF EACH SPIN IS DEPENDENT ON THE GRID POSITION AND POTENTIAL PRIZES PRICES MAY BE IN EXCESS OF YOUR BASE STAKE. COLLECT OR BUY MORE SPINS. THE PRICE FOR THE NEXT SPIN IS SHOWN ON THE SPIN BUTTON. SLINGO WIN LINES THERE ARE 12 WIN LINES AND 11 AWARDS ON THE PAYTABLE BECAUSE THE LAST NUMBER ON THE GRID WILL ALWAYS AWARD AT LEAST 2 WINNING LINES. BEST STRATEGY THE BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF WILDS AND SUPER WILDS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAYER CLOSER TO A SLINGO. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA ARE AVAILABLE THE PREFERENCE IS GIVEN TO THE POSITIONS THAT ARE INCLUDED IN THE MOST SLINGOS (WIN LINES). FOR EXAMPLE THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN THE HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA EXIST AGAIN A RANDOM CHOICE IS MADE. GENERAL BASED ON BEST STRATEGY THIS GAME HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF: RED TRAIL 96.29%, BLUE TRAIL 96.35%, GREEN TRAIL 96.29% BASED ON BEST STRATEGY EACH EXTRA SPIN HAS A MINIMUM RTP OF: RED TRAIL 95.20%, BLUE TRAIL 95.20%, GREEN TRAIL 95.20% IN EXTRA SPINS SOME POTENTIAL PRIZES MAY REQUIRE SUPER WILDS. EXTRA SPINS ARE ONLY OFFERED IF AN INCREASED PRIZE IS ACHIEVABLE ON THE NEXT SPIN. EXTRA SPIN PRICES ARE ROUNDED TO THE NEAREST WHOLE VALUE WHICH MAY AFFECT THE RTP. FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS IN EXTRA SPINS. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. FREE GAMES IF A PLAYER IS AWARDED A FREE GAME OF SLINGO ELF BLITZ THEY WILL BE ABLE TO PLAY THE BASE GAME FOR FREE AT A PREDEFINED STAKING VALUE. WHEN THE BASE GAME IS COMPLETED, THE WINNINGS ACCRUED BY COMPLETING HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL OR DIAGONAL SLINGOS OR OTHER SPECIAL SYMBOL BONUSES WILL BE DISPLAYED TO THE PLAYER AND COLLECTED. FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, THE FREE GAME IS CONSIDERED AS A BONUS CASH STAKE. PENDING GAMES AN INCOMPLETE GAME WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY COMPLETED 24 HOURS AFTER LAUNCH. ANY REMAINING SPINS WILL BE PLAYED OUT WITH A RANDOM NUMBER GENERATOR USED TO MAKE ANY PLAYER DECISIONS. ANY WINNINGS WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY CREDITED. Last modification date: 12/7/2023
