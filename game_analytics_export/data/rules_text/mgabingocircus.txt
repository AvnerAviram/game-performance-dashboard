Circus STANDARD GAMES RULES Bingo-based draw in which 30 balls are drawn from a drum containing 60 balls the player has up to four cards with 15 numbers on each one. If a win figure is matched on a card, the prize associated will be awarded from the paytable. EXTRA BALL RULES Sometimes the player is offered the option of buying extra balls at additional cost. PRIZE TABLE When bonus labeled figure in prize plan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS GAME 1 The objective is to scratch cells. The final prize will be the sum of the prizes unhidden. BONUS GAME 2 A clown walks through a path with forks. Every time the clown stops at a fork you can choose the path. The prize is the one hidden below the reached destination. When bonus labeled figure in prize plan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS GAME 3 The objective is to choose one of the buckets. The prize will be the one hidden beneath it. BONUS GAME 4 The objective of the game is to select balls on screen. The final prize is the sum of the prizes uncovered. RTP:90.1 The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 or 10,000xBet - whichever is reached first (depending on currency.
