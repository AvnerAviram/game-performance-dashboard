Dancing Drums Link Fortune PAYTABLE All wins on multiple ways are added together. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. The Base Game REEL WAYS™ contains up to 243 ways to win. All REEL WAYS™ and Scatter pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Scatter pays are in addition to REEL WAYS™ pays. WILD WILD is WILD for all symbols except REDCOIN, BLUECOIN, and WHITECOIN. WILD appears on reels 2, 3, and 4. POTS During the Base Game, if 1 or more REDCOIN appear on the screen, then the REDPOT may close. During the Base Game, if 1 or more BLUECOIN appear on the screen, then the BLUEPOT may close. During the Base Game, if 1 or more WHITECOIN appear on the screen, then the WHITEPOT may close. For each WILD that appears during the Base Game, any combination of REDPOT, BLUEPOT, WHITEPOT may close. On any given spin, it is possible that more than one pot will close, and in any combination. The size of REDPOT, BLUEPOT, and WHITEPOT over time has no effect on the outcome. JACKPOT FREE SPINS BONUS If Free Spins are triggered by the REDPOT closing, the player is awarded Jackpot Free Spins Bonus. During the Jackpot Free Spins Bonus, GRAND FUBABY, MAJOR FUBABY, MINOR FUBABY, and MINI FUBABY overlay symbols are added to the reel set, and reveal a symbol below the overlay symbol when added to the jackpot meter. Jackpot symbols are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. Each meter has 3 spaces. If all spaces are filled for the meter, then the symbols are cleared, and the corresponding jackpot is awarded. An alternate set of reels is used. At the beginning of the Free Spins, the player is presented with 5 options, please see the table on Free Spins Bonus page for details. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to Base Game except REDCOIN, BLUECOIN, and WHITECOIN do not appear. MULTIPLIER WILD FREE SPINS BONUS If Free Spins are triggered by the WHITEPOT closing, the player is awarded Multiplier Wild Free Spins Bonus. During the Multiplier Wild Free Spins Bonus, MULTIPLIER WILD may land with a x2 or x3 multiplier. All winning combinations involving MULTIPLIER WILD are multiplied by the multiplier displayed on MULTIPLIER WILD. MULTIPLIER WILD appears only during Multiplier Wild Free Spins Bonus. MULTIPLIER WILD appears on reels 2, 3, and 4. An alternate set of reels is used. At the beginning of the Free Spins, the player is presented with 5 options, please see the table on Free Spins Bonus page for details. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to Base Game except WILD, REDCOIN, BLUECOIN, and WHITECOIN do not appear. REEL WAYS™ Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. GAME RULES Only the highest win paid per winning combination. The chances of getting particular outcome are always the same at the start of every game. In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. LEGAL NOTICES A stable Internet connection is required.
