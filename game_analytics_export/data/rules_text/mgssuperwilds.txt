Superwilds Game Rules General All wins pay Left to Right on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. All wins are paid on lines played. Highest win paid on any played line. All wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Wins on different lines are added together for total game win. Minimum wager is 0.01. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.912%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PLAYS AND PAYS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE TO CONFIRM WAGER. CHOOSE LINES PLAYED, THEN BET PER LINE TO ACTIVATE GAME. SUBSTITUTION PAYS (WILD SYMBOL RULES FEATURE) MULTIPLIER MAN (the Tripler), MULTIPLIER MAN (the Doubler), EXPANDO-MAN (the Expanding), THE SPIN TWINS (Extra Free Spins) and WILD substitute for all symbols in the base game. Wilds substitute for all symbols. When one or more Wilds substitute in a win, highest win is paid. PAYTABLE ALL PAYS SHOWN WITH A WAGER OF 0.01. FEATURE SUPERWILD FREE SPINS 3 Wilds in any position trigger Superwild Free Spins. Initially 10 free spins are awarded, plus the feature will be enhanced by the type of Superwild that triggered the feature. There are 4 types of Superwilds: MULTIPLIER MAN (the Tripler) - All wins x3 in the Free Spins. MULTIPLIER MAN (the Doubler) - All wins x2 in the Free Spins. EXPANDO-MAN (the Expanding) - In Free Spins, Wild will expand to all positions on the reel. THE SPIN TWINS (Extra Free Spins) - 5 extra Free Spins are awarded. The Wilds triggering the feature can be combined. If more than 1 multiplier is awarded, the multipliers are both applied (for example, if 2 MULTIPLIER MAN (the Triplers) trigger the feature, all wins will be multiplied by 9). Feature cannot be re-triggered. Total Wager is the same as the game that triggered the Superwild Free Spins feature. Last modification date: 1/31/2017
