Minting It Hold and Win 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 ( +4 on Money Reels) Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet Bet: 20 Side Bet: 30 Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes (Coin Symbols, Minting It, Super Minting It Symbol) Bonus round Yes Free s pins No Instant win No The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right, beginning with the far-left reel. All line win symbols must appear on consecutive reels. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. On the info bar, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). Collect & Money Reels Minting it! symbol appears only in base game and can land with up to 3 other coins. If it lands with other coins, it will collect the corresponding values on the Money reels . Money reels will present new values and prizes on each spin. 3.2. Jackpot Symbols Mini Jackpot (10x) can appear on bronze row only. Minor Jackpot (20x) can appear on silver row only. Major Jackpot (50x) can appear on gold row only. Grand Jackpot (100x) can appear on platinum row only. 3.3. Hold & Win Minting Respins Any 5 or more coins in view will trigger Hold & Win Minting Respins , awarding 3 respins. The Money Reels are locked from the triggering spin and will not change during Hold & Win Minting Respins . Only Bronze coin, Silver coin, Gold coin, Platinum coin and Super Minting it! symbols are in play. Values from the Money Reels are won by landing coins or a Super Minting it! symbol. Each coin that lands will award the prize from the corresponding row on the Money Reels . Each Super Minting it! symbol, when pressed, will reveal 1 of 3 posible coins ( Platinum , Gold , Silver ). It will then collect all values on the money reel up to and including the coin revealed. Money reels values in total bet: Bronze : 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x- Mini : 10x Silver : 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x- Minor : 20x Gold : 15x, 20x, 30x, 40x- Major : 50x Platinum : 40x, 50x, 60x, 80x- Grand : 100x 3.4. Bonus Buy Purchase Hold & Win Minting Respins for 75 x total bet. 3.5. Mint Bet Mint Bet costs 50% extra and increases the chances of triggering features! 4. Other game buttons 4.1. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play.
