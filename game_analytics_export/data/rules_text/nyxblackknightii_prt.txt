Black Knight II Base Game Wild FEATURE WILD is wild for all symbols including FEATURE, and may appear only on reels 2,3, and 4. WILD will only count as 1 FEATURE per reel no matter how many spaces on a reel it covers. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. All line pays are already multiplied by bet per line. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Feature Description 3 or more consecutive FEATURE beginning with the leftmost reel trigger the Free Spin Feature, and award 7 Free Spins. All scatter pay awards are already multiplied by the total bet. Click the "CLICK TO START" button to start the free spins. WILD is wild for all symbols including FEATURE, and may appear only on reels 2,3, and 4. Any WILD appearing on reels 2,3 or 4 will nudge to fill the entire reel, and all pays will be evaluated after WILD nudges. WILD will then lock in place for the remaining free spins. During free spins, an alternate set of reels is used. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. FEATURE does not appear during Free Spins. During free spins, the bet multiplier and active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Scatter pays are added to the payline wins. The Feature cannot be retriggered. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
