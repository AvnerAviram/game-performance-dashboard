HOT4SHOT – GAME FEATURES WILD SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOLS substitute for all symbols, except the BONUS symbol. SUPERBET In SUPERBET mode, all six (6) reels are active. WILD STAR SYMBOLS are WILD SYMBOLS, like they are in the other game modes, and WILD STAR SYMBOLS also act as a MULTIPLIER when they are part of a winning combination. If there is one (1) WILD STAR SYMBOL in a winning combination, a x2 MULTIPLIER will be applied to the winning combination. If there are two (2) WILD STAR SYMBOLS in a winning combination, a x5 MULTIPLIER will be applied to the winning combination. HOT SHOT FEATURE Three (3) or more BONUS SYMBOLS (HOT SYMBOLS) scattered anywhere on adjacent reels awards the HOT SHOT FEATURE. The HOT SHOT FEATURE is played on a different game screen, where the reels are transformed into a shooting gallery. Symbols are lined up like targets on three different rows that are activated row by row. Lights will pulsate across the symbols/targets on row one (1) until you click the HOT SHOT button. The last symbol that lit up before you clicked the button will be the target that you hit, which is the winning symbol for that row. You will then shoot at the target symbols on row two (2), and then row (3) as they light up. The values from the symbols you shoot/win on each of the three different rows will be added together and added to your balance before you return to the regular reels to continue playing.
