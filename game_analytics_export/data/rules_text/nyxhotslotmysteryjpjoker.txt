Hot Slot: Mystery Jackpot Joker The game has 3 reels, 3 rows and 5 fixed bet lines. Wins are awarded for drawing 3 symbols of a kind on an active bet line, from leftmost to right on consecutive reels. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and Special symbols. Drawing 3 Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels pays the win. The maximum win amount is 1000x bet. SPECIAL FEATURES MYSTERY JOKER JACKPOT GAME Drawing 9 Mystery Jackpot Joker symbols activates the Mystery Joker Jackpot Game. MINOR, MAJOR, JOKER, or GRAND Jackpot is randomly drawn, awarding prizes ranging from 15x-100x, 100x-300x, 300x-600x, or 1000x player’s bet respectively.
