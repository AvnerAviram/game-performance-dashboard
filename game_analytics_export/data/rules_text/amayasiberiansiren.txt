Siberian Siren Siberian Siren GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. Beginning with the left-most reel, pays left to right on consecutive reels. The Won field displays winnings in credits. All games are played with 40 paylines. Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet. In the flash version space bar can be used to spin. Select Multiplier Bet Level (1x to 5x). Only the highest winning combination per payline pays. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. The WHITE TIGER symbol is WILD and substitutes for ALL symbols except the GOLDEN TIGER symbol. The WHITE TIGER symbol appears on all reels except reel 1. One natural SIREN symbol and four WHITE TIGER symbols on payline 1 at maximum bet level award the Jackpot in the base game only. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. Mobile only Tapping SPIN repeats last bet Free Spins Bonus 3 or more FABERGE EGG symbols on a played line, on consecutive reels, starting with the leftmost reel, award FREE SPINS. Free Spin Tally Free spins may be triggered more than once per spin, in which case the awarded free spins are totalled together. The number of free spins awarded per winning combination are as follows: 5 FABERGE EGG symbols awards 3 free spins. 4 FABERGE EGG symbols awards 2 free spins. 3 FABERGE EGG symbols awards 1 free spin. Jackpot cannot be won on a free spin. Free spins are played at the same Multiplier Bet Level as the triggering spin. Additional free spins can be won during the Free Spins Feature. Different Reels are used during Free Spin Games. Wild Retrigger Feature The GOLDEN TIGER symbol is a SCATTER symbol. The GOLDEN TIGER symbol only appears in the Free Spins Bonus and only on reels 2, 3 and 4. Each scattered GOLDEN TIGER symbol awards an additional free spin. The GOLDEN TIGER symbol is WILD and substitutes for ALL symbols. Auto Spin: In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for a selectable number of spins. In settings, choose Auto Spin "ON" and select the desired number of autospins. Select the STOP button to cancel Autospin. Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus. Autospin is deactivated after a bonus or feature. PAYS
