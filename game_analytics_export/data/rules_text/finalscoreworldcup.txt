Final Score World Cup Final Score World Cup is a fast-paced football results based game. Players should select two teams and can bet on a variety of outcomes concerning how many goals are scored between the two teams selected by the player or what the result will be. Players can also bet on special events in a match which are penalty missed, penalty scored, injury time goal and red card. The game also has a bonus feature, which is a penalty, and is available whenever a player has a winning bet resulting in winnings that are not over half of the max win amount The game is designed in such a way that different teams have different skills and this will affect the odds and results for a game between two teams. The new odds for bets are highlighted when the player changes one of the teams so that the player can see the new odds. top Information The betting limits are shown on the game screen title bar. The total bet placed in a game should be less than the max bet and the individual bets are restricted so that the winnings are less than maximum payout. top How to play Selecting teams: players will be able to select the two teams from drop down lists available towards the top left of the game window. Clicking on the option 'Random Fixture' will automatically select two teams. The country that is selected on the left side denotes the Home Team and the country on the right side denotes Away Team. Setting 'BETS': select a coin value and then click on the bet you would like to place. The stake on that bet is then increased by the selected coin amount. There are different coin values available. If your game balance is less than your bet, you need to buy-in amount to continue game play. The amount bet with each chip is the coin value in the currency the player has selected. The player can bet on a variety of outcomes concerning how many goals are scored in a game of football or what the result will be. Draw: 0-0, 1-1, 2-2, 3-3, other draw Away Team to win: 0-1, 0-2, 0-3, 0-4, 1-2, 1-3, 2-3, other away win. Total Goals: Under 2, Exactly 2, Over 2 Specials: Penalty Missed, Penalty Scored, Injury Time Goal, Red Card. Max Bet: The sum of all individual bets should not be more than $1000 USD Max Winnings: The maximum amount that a player can win in a game is $150,000 USD. The individual bets are restricted so that the winnings are less than maximum payout. 'View Decimal Odds': The ''View Decimal Odds'' option is available towards the right of the game window. Click on the option will display the decimal odds for the available bets. To calculate the winnings for a bet multiply the bet amount by the corresponding decimal odds 'View Fractional Odds': The ''View Fractional Odds'' option is available towards the right of the game window. Click on the option will display the Fractional odds for the available bets. To calculate the winnings for a bet multiply the bet amount by the corresponding fractional odds and add bet amount to the result. They are two windows displayed in the middle of the game screen. The top window shows various animations along with the goal results. The second window shows the events occurring in the match. PLAY: Click on 'Bet Now' button available towards the right bottom of the game window to get the main game started. This involves an entire game of football being played with key moments being displayed. The key moments will include goals, penalties, red cards, half time and full time. The game will begin with the refs whistle and each goal is also met with an audible cheer, as is half-time. The final whistle signifies the end of the game and there will then be an audible cheer/sigh to accompany the winning or losing bets. After the final whistle the player will be shown the bets they won on (if any) and how much they won on each bet Total win amount will also be shown. If there is a winning the player will have an option to collect their winnings or go to the Bonus round. Bonus will only be available if the winnings are not over half of the max win amount. Full time Option: During game play when clicked on the option 'Full time >>' the game will directly display the end result. Repeat Bet: Clicking on Repeat bet option, the player will then have the option to make his previous bet as same bets for the next game. TURBO BET: If Turbo mode is enabled then the game will go straight to showing the final results, rather than showing the match details as it plays out as normal. Clicking on the option TURBO BET will enable the TURBO mode and change the option to TURBO BET ON. Collect: Clicking on 'Collect' button will add the winnings to the game balance and end the game. Bonus: The game has a bonus feature, which is a penalty and is available whenever a player has a winning in main game (as long as they have not won over half of the maximum winnings limit). When player has a win in the main game Bonus & Collect options will be available. Clicking on 'Bonus' option will take the player to the Bonus feature. In Bonus feature player bets on whether a penalty will be scored or not. There are 50% chances of scoring penalty. They then win double their winnings if they picked correctly or lose all their winnings if not. If player had picked correctly then they can collect or make a choice of score/miss and bet again in the same way for another penalty, this would continue until the player decided to collect or they lost. (Player has to collect if the wins go over half of the max winnings) (WINNINGS: If there is a win from the main game the WINNINGS meter displays the total wins from the main game. During the Bonus feature the WINNINGS meter is updated after every round. Repeat Bet/Change Bet: If you have already bet, the settings selected will be automatically carried over to the subsequent play. Simply click on 'REPEAT BET' to play with the same bet again. To change your bet, click on the bets you wish to place. To remove the bet placed either click on clear last bet or clear all bets options or you can click on Shift+ Left Mouse click simultaneously.. CLEAR LAST BET: Clicking on clear last bet option will clear the last bet placed in the particular game. CLEAR ALL BETS: Clicking on clear all bets will clear all the bets placed in the particular game. RESULTS HISTORY: Results history is located at the top right of the game window. Results History displays the results for the previous games played by the player. Click on 'More' option below Results History to expand the Results History screen. BALANCE: Your remaining game balance, after you have placed the desired bet, is shown under 'BALANCE'. TOTAL STAKE: The sum of all the bets placed in the game is reflected in the 'TOTAL STAKE' meter available at the bottom of the game window. 'AUTODRAW': You can make use of this feature for 'hands free' game play. Auto Draw plays can be set from 5 games to a maximum of 20 games. The number of games selected to be played with Auto Draw will all be played with the same selections and bets placed. After selecting the desired teams and placing the bets, choose the Auto Draw Option from 5 to 20 Draws. Clicking on Bet Now will activate the AutoDraw . Stop Auto Draw: During AutoDraw, once the game is completed you can choose the 'Stop Auto Draw' ' button to deactivate the AUTODRAW options. During AutoDraw the Bonus is available only for the final game of the selected auto draw game numbers if there is a win (that is not over half of the maximum winnings limit) and auto draw is not stopped. If player stops the Auto Draw the game is final for the Auto Draw but Bonus is not available. Click on 'GAME LOGS' to view the logs of the games you've played. In Game Logs Home Team denotes the country that is selected on the left side in the game and Away Team denotes the country on the right side. Mute/Sound: The option to turn of the sounds is available at the top middle of the game window. When the button is read as 'Mute' sounds will be audible. When the button is read as 'Sound' sounds will be off in the game window. By clicking on the mute/sounds buttons it can be switched accordingly. The box to the left of the match timer shows whether any of the special events have occurred, which include four symbols (X, Tick, Plus and Rectangle symbol) 'X' Denotes Penalty Missed. 'Tick' Denotes the Penalty Scored '+' denotes the Injury Time Goal 'Rectangle' denotes the Red Card If any of the above special events occur the corresponding symbol in the box turns red. Click on 'Game Rules' to view the game rules of the game. Click on 'REBUY' to buy more funds into the game from your account. Click on 'EXIT GAME' to leave the game and return to the lobby. Quick Deposit: Quick Deposit option is available at the right of the footer in the game window. On a successfully deposit when a player clicks on Quick Deposit in the game window it gives an option for the player to make easy and instant deposit. If you get disconnected during the main game there will be an incomplete game on reconnection, if there is a win (that is not over half of the maximum winnings limit) Bonus feature will be available for selection. If you get disconnected when the main game is completed and there is no win, the game is over. You will be able to find out the result by clicking on the 'Game Logs' button once you have logged back in If you are disconnected after Bonus is made available or in the middle of the Bonus feature, on reconnection the software will automatically take you to the Bonus round. You can complete the bonus round. During AutoDraw if you get disconnected and there is win (that is not over half of the maximum winnings limit) in the current Auto Draw game on reconnection, Bonus feature will be available for selection and the Auto Draw setting will be reset. During AutoDraw if you get disconnected and there is no win in the current Auto Draw game on reconnection, the game is over and the Auto Draw setting will be reset.
