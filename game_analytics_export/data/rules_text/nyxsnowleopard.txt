Snow Leopard Game Introduction Snow Leopard is a 6 reel game with 40 win lines available. One reel will always be Wild. 3 or more Scatter Symbols award Free Spins. Free Spins 3 or more Scatter Symbols award up to 50 Free Spins in the standard game. The Wild Reel on entry will remain Wild for the entire Free Spins feature. Additional Wild reels can roll in. Free Spins contain Scatter Symbols, allowing extra Free Spins to be won. Additional information can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. All play is over TWO sets of Reels, doubling the number of lines to 80. A Free Spins Trail collects Scatter Symbols from both reels, increasing the chance of Free Spins. The Wild Reels may have multipliers applied that increase the size of line wins. Additional information can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. How to Play Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 40 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as cash values. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 97.75%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 Last modification date: 6/4/2020
