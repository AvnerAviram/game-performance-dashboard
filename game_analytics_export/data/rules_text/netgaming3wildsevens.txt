3 Wild Sevens How to Play Slots: Choose the amount you would like to bet with. Control the bet amount by tapping the bet icon. Winnings are automatically added to the account balance. Total Win is displayed under the WIN section of the screen after each win. Game Rules: The game is played with 5 fixed paylines and each game costs a minimum of 10 fixed coins. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Prizes are multiplied by the coin value. All symbols pay from left to right in consecutive order. Only the highest win is paid per selected line. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For this game, the long term expected payback is 94.43%. Wild Features: Mystery Wild: When a Wild symbol lands on reel 1, it awards a Mystery reward. Expanding Wild: When a Wild symbol lands on reel 2, it expands to cover the entire reel and then the win is awarded. Multiplier Wild: When a Wild symbol lands on reel 3, a multiplier 3x is applied to all the wins. If 2 or more Wilds land simultaneously, their features are combined. Super Spins: 3 Super Spins can be awarded when there is at least 1 Wild visible on the reels. - 1 Wild gives less chance to award Super Spins. - 2 Wilds gives higher chance to award Super Spins. - 3 Wilds guarantee Super Spins. During each Super Spin at least 1 Wild is guaranteed to appear on any 1 of the reels, additional Wilds can land naturally. If 2 Mystery Wilds appear on reel 1, their mystery wins are summed up. If 2 Multiplier Wilds appear on reel 3, their multipliers are multiplied together and then applied to all the wins. Super Spins cannot be retriggered.
