Rambo GAME RULES Rambo is a highly animated twenty (20) win line, five (5) reel, three (3) row video slot game. Game features include WILD SYMBOLS and EXPLOSIVE WILD SYMBOLS while three (3) or more SCATTER SYMBOLS award FREE SPINS with MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS. Click/Tap the SPIN or AUTOPLAY buttons to start playing. Activate MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. The theoretical minimum payback percentage (RTP) is 94.47 % Disclaimer: If an interrupted game is not finished within 90 days, your game will be terminated. If your game gets terminated you will lose every active bet. Only the highest winning combination pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. SCATTER SYMBOLS 3 or more SCATTER SYMBOLS in view award FREE SPINS. SCATTER SYMBOLS award 10 FREE SPINS SCATTER SYMBOLS award 20 FREE SPINS SCATTER SYMBOLS award 50 FREE SPINS During the feature, 3 or more SCATTER SYMBOLS in view award EXTRA FREE SPINS. SCATTER SYMBOLS award 10 EXTRA FREE SPINS SCATTER SYMBOLS award 20 EXTRA FREE SPINS SCATTER SYMBOLS award 50 EXTRA FREE SPINS WILD SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOLS substitute for all symbols, except SCATTER SYMBOLS. MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS substitute for all symbols, except SCATTER SYMBOLS. MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS are only active in the FREE SPINS FEATURE on second (2), third (3) and fourth (4) reels. When a winning combination is made with MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS, the resulting win will be multiplied by two (2), three (3) or four (4). Combinations of MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS are added together. EXPLOSIVE WILD SYMBOLS EXPLOSIVE WILD SYMBOLS are only active on second (2), third (3) and fourth (4) reels. When EXPLOSIVE WILD SYMBOL lands on the reel, it explodes to make the reel entirely covered with WILD SYMBOLS in the Base game or MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS in the FREE SPINS FEATURE, and awards three (3) free RE-SPINS. After every RE-SPIN, the stacked WILD SYMBOLS automatically move one (1) position downwards until they leave the reel. MAIN MENU GAME SETTINGS INTRO SCREEN The default setting for the INTRO SCREEN is ON. When set to OFF, the INTRO is not visible. ANIMATIONS The default setting for ANIMATIONS is ON. When set to OFF, special animations are not visible. Win animations and symbol animations are always visible. SPACEBAR SPIN The default setting for SPACEBAR SPIN is ON. When set to OFF, it is not possible to spin the reels using the SPACEBAR key. GAME SOUNDS The default setting for GAME SOUNDS is ON. When set to OFF, only the SOUND EFFECTS are disabled. BACKGROUND SOUNDS The default setting for BACKGROUND SOUNDS (if available) is ON. When set to OFF, only the BACKGROUND MUSIC is disabled. Click anywhere on the screen to start spinning. SPIN SETTINGS AUTO PLAY TURBO SPIN Click/tap the TURBO SPIN button to activate TURBO SPIN. The default setting for TURBO SPIN is OFF. When set to ON, the reels spin faster than normal spins. TURBO SPIN can also be activated by quickly clicking/tapping the SPIN BUTTON three times. AUTO PLAY Tap the AUTO PLAY BUTTON to start playing. You can select the number of spins triggered in AUTO PLAY mode by changing the SPIN settings. The following advanced settings are also available: LOSS LIMIT AMOUNT: Stop AUTO PLAY if the total amount of losses exceeds the specified LOSS LIMIT amount. SINGLE WIN LIMIT AMOUNT: Stop AUTO PLAY if any win exceeds the specified SINGLE WIN LIMIT amount. Note: AUTO PLAY options can be different for some jurisdictions.
