Wild Survivor Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Symbol wins pay for any winning payline combination without any gaps. Only the highest win is paid per active win line. Game Rules WILD SURVIVOR is a 5-reel, 3-row game with 10 paylines. 3 or more SCATTER symbols on any reel trigger FREE SPINS mode. 3 X SCATTER awards 10 FREE SPINS. 4 X SCATTER awards 15 FREE SPINS. 5 X SCATTER awards 20 FREE SPINS. SCATTER symbols can’t land during FREE SPINS. WILD PRIZES are attached to Animal symbols and their values are random at every spin. WILD PRIZES can be collected during WILD RE-SPIN and FREE SPINS. The possible prize values are 1x, 2x, 5x total bet during the WILD RE-SPIN. During FREE SPINS the possible prize values are 1x, 2x, 5x, 10x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 250x total bet. Landed WILD symbols collect WILD PRIZE values during the WILD RE-SPIN and FREE SPINS. WILD RE-SPIN can trigger randomly during the base game. WILDS substitute for all other symbols, except SCATTERS. At the beginning of FREE SPINS, one of four FREE SPIN BOOSTERS is randomly selected. The Calls & Lures BOOSTER increases the frequency of landing Animal symbols. The Mug BOOSTER increases the frequency of landing WILD symbols. The Night Vision BOOSTER increases the multiplier for the WILD PRIZE on the Animal symbols by x1. The Knife BOOSTER awards 3 extra FREE SPINS. Landed WILD symbols are added to the WILD CHECKPOINTS panel during FREE SPINS. Each 4th WILD symbol landing during the FREE SPINS feature awards 5 extra FREE SPINS and increases the multiplier for the WILD PRIZES on the Animal symbols by x1. WILD CHECKPOINTS has a total of 12 reachable checkpoints. The WILD PRIZE multiplier will be x2 by reaching the 4th WILD CHECKPOINT, x3 by reaching the 8th WILD CHECKPOINT, and x4 by reaching the 12th WILD CHECKPOINT. In the case of starting the FREE SPINS with the Night Vision BOOSTER the WILD PRIZE multiplier will be x2 by default, x3 by reaching the 4th WILD CHECKPOINT, x4 by reaching the 8th WILD CHECKPOINT, and x5 by reaching the 12th WILD CHECKPOINT. The average RTP is 96.20%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Minimum Bet: 0.10 USD Maximum Bet: 50.00 USD Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 3000 times total bet. Actions Paytable - Toggles the display of the paytable. Auto Play - Click the AUTO PLAY button to enable/disable the Auto Play feature. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Hyper Spin - Pressing and holding down the SPACE button activates Hyper Spin mode after 1.5 seconds. The spins speed up immensely but slow down if a feature is activated. Spin / Start / Spacebar - Starts the game round with the currently selected bet. Press Spin to begin. Stop - Stops the reels more quickly. Fast Play - Toggle on for a significantly faster gameplay. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet and will be lower than the selected bet size when autospins was initiated.
