Fiesta Pop HOW TO PLAY Commit a wager for a chance to award prizes. All awarded prizes are identified by a confetti border and sparkling background until the next wager is committed. On any winning play, 3 - 20 prizes may be awarded. Prizes range from 2-5,000 credits times bet multiplier. All prizes displayed have already been multiplied by bet multiplier. The total award for any win during normal play ranges from 12-7,602 credits times bet multiplier. The piñata, stick, candy, and confetti are for entertainment purposes only and have no effect on the odds of winning. PIÑATA POP FEATURE The Piñata Pop Feature may be activated on any winning play. During the Piñata Pop feature, all winning prize spots show a confetti border around their boxes. All winning prize values are then multiplied by a multiplier between 2-7. Up to 3- 10 prizes can be awarded. All prizes are reverted back to their original values upon initiation of the next wager. The total award for any win during the Piñata Pop Feature ranges from 60-35,140 credits times bet multiplier. Additional Information MENU Press to open and close game settings and view game information. BET Press to access Bet Settings without opening the Menu. AUTO PLAYS Press to access Auto Play settings without opening the Menu. PLAY Press to start playing with the current bet settings. Or, if applicable, press Space Bar to start playing with the current bet settings. SOUND Press to enable or disable Sound. SETTINGS Press to access game Settings. BET SETTINGS Press to access Bet Settings. PAYS/HELP Press to view game pay and help information. Game will automatically complete after 24-48 hours if left in a feature.
