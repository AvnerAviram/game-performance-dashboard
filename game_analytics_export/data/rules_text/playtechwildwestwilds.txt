Wild West Wilds 5-reel, 25-line slot. The objective of Wild West Wilds™ is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. To play the game: Press START on the entry screen to enter the main game. You can also press on Don’t show next time to skip the entry screen the next time you play. This game is played with a fixed number of 25 active paylines. The total bet per game round is the line bet × 25. Press the '+' and '-' buttons to select the total bet. Pressing the Turbo Mode button switches on or off the Turbo Mode thus turning on or off some of the win animations and sounds, and making the reels spin faster or slower. Press the SPIN button to spin the reels with the current bet. During the reel spin the SPIN button changes into STOP . Pressing STOP ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin result. In the case of a winning spin, the WIN field displays the accumulating winnings. Autoplay: The reels are spun automatically in autoplay. - To display the list of options, press AUTO PLAY , and then select the number of spins to be played automatically. When you want to start autoplay, you must first set limitations for the autoplay session. The set limitations apply for one autoplay session in the current game. You can set the following options: Loss limit (mandatory) : The amount of money you can spend in the following autoplay session. If you reach the set limit, autoplay will stop. Note that bets reduce and wins increase the remaining limit. Single win limit (optional): Autoplay will stop if you win more than the set amount. Pressing START starts the autoplay session with your settings, pressing CANCEL returns you to regular gameplay. During autoplay, the number of remaining spins is displayed. Autoplay ends when: The reels have spun the number of times specified. You do not have sufficient funds for the next spin. The Free Games feature has been triggered. You can end autoplay by pressing STOP AUTOPLAY . Paytable: To open the paytable, press i. To navigate the paytable, press the arrow buttons. Payouts are listed on the HIGH-WIN SYMBOLS and LOW-WIN SYMBOLS screens. To find the possible win amount for any given symbol combination, the line bet must be multiplied by the payout. To close the paytable and return to the game, press the X button. Paylines: This game is played with a fixed number of 25 active paylines. The total bet per game round is the line bet × 25. Paylines are represented by lines that appear over the reels as illustrated in the paytable. Winning combinations must start from the leftmost reel, and the same symbol has to be on consecutive reels and appear on the same payline. There is a difference between the line bet and the total bet. The line bet shows how much is being bet on a single payline. The total bet shows how much is being bet in total on a single spin. Payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet. Maximum win limit: The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information, see the Terms and Conditions. The maximum win from a single game is £250000 Wild Symbol: There are 2 types of WILD symbols in this game. The brown symbol displaying the word “WILD�? is a normal WILD symbol that appears in the main game, the Respin feature and the Free Games feature. If a silver sheriff badge lands anywhere on the reels during the Respin or Free Games feature, it will turn into a golden sticky WILD symbol. The WILD symbols can stand for any symbol except the SCATTER and silver sheriff badge symbols to make the best possible winning combination. Scatter Symbol: The revolver with the word “SCATTER�? around it is the SCATTER symbol in the game. When 2 SCATTER symbols simultaneously land on reels 3, 4 or 5, the Respin feature is triggered. When 3 SCATTER symbols simultaneously land on reels 3, 4 and 5, the Free Games feature is triggered. Respin Feature: When 2 SCATTER symbols simultaneously land on reels 3, 4 or 5, the Respin feature is triggered. The reels will respin once for free. Any silver sheriff badge symbols that land on the triggering spin, or any subsequent respin, will turn into sticky WILD symbols. If 2 SCATTER symbols land on the reels during the Respin feature, an extra respin is awarded. If 3 SCATTER symbols land on the reels during the Respin feature, the Free Games feature will trigger. All sticky WILD symbols spin off the reels after the feature. Free Games: When 3 SCATTER symbols simultaneously land on reels 3, 4 and 5, the Free Games feature is triggered. You will win from 5 to 9 free games with a ×1, ×2 or ×3 win multiplier. Any silver sheriff badge symbols that land on the triggering spin, or any subsequent Free Games spin, will turn into sticky WILD symbols. 2 SCATTER symbols on reels 3, 4 or 5 during Free Games will award 1 extra free game. 3 SCATTER symbols on reels 3, 4 and 5 during Free Games will award from 5 to 9 extra free games and increase the win multiplier by up to 3. The win multiplier in Free Games is capped at ×10. Free Games are played automatically with the same bet that was placed on the spin that triggered them. Free Games end after the number of SPINS LEFT reaches 0. All sticky WILD symbols spin off the reels after the feature. Return to Player: The theoretical percentage return to player (RTP) is 96.14%.
