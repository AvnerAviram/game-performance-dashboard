To make winning combinations SYMBOL TRIGGERED PROGRESSIVE WITH JACKPOT JUMP Each progressive tier shares a contribution pool between all bet levels. The reset value for the tier increases as bet is increased. When between 5 and 10 Jackpot Inferno symbols land scattered in the reels, the corresponding progressive tier is locked-in but not yet awarded. The player is given 3 picks in a field of 20 icons. Selections can reveal credit prizes ranging between 10 and 200 times bet multiplier, +2 Picks which awards 2 additional picks, or Jackpot Inferno symbols. If a Jackpot Inferno symbol is found, the Jackpot Jump feature activates, causing the locked-in progressive tier to advance one tier higher. Once all picks have been mad, the current locked-in progressive tier is awarded. The top two tiers are available through Jackpot Jump. FREE SPIN BONUS 3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols scattered anywhere on the reels trigger the Free Spin Bonus. 3 Bonus symbols award 6 spins and 2x total bet. 4 Bonus symbols award 10 spins and 5x total bet. 5 Bonus symbols award 20 spins and 10x total bet. During Free Spins, an alternate set of reels are used: only the four types of 7 symbol and WILD appear in the reels. Retriggers are not possible. STACKS Wilds are 20-high stacks and are wild for all symbols except for the Jackpot Inferno and Bonus symbols. Wilds are present on all five reels.
