Blazin Hot 7s Blazin’ Hot 7s is a 3-reel slot game. The game has 5 pay lines which are active at all times. Wins are awarded for 3 of a kind symbols when on adjacent reels (left-to-right). The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is a minimum 93.99%. To win you must match 3 symbols on an active payline. At random, you may be awarded a “streak�? feature. In the “Spin Streak Bonus�?, symbols on the reels are locked and the reels spin again. Matching symbols are locked, and this continues until a win is achieved. In the “Cherry Streak Bonus�?, all cherry symbols are locked and the reels spin again, until a cherry symbol win is obtained. Obtaining 3 "BONUS" symbols in any position will trigger the free games bonus. Choose a symbol to reveal the number of free games, which will play automatically. Free games cannot retrigger. GAME RULES Payouts are made according to the Paytable All wins are multiplied by the "Total Bet", except in "Fortune Spins" where wins are multiplied by the "Total Bet" and divided by "Fortune Spins Bet Level" multiplier. Highest win paid per winning combination Coinciding wins are added Wins are paid left-to-right Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol 3 of a kind wins are paid once only WILD substitutes for all symbols except BONUS Free Games are played at the lines and bet of the triggering game Malfunction voids all pays and plays Fortune Spins To play the Fortune Spins game; click the Fortune Spins button and select a bet; Fortune Spins will be active until you select 'Reel Game' from the Adjust Fortune Spins menu. Press the "Play" button to return to the reels, and then press the "Spin" button to start a Fortune Spins game. Each level of Fortune Spins awards additional benefits; select the highest level to remove lower paying symbols and add an extra wild symbol for more wins!
