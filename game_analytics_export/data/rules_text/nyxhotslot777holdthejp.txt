Hot Slot: 777 Hold the Jackpot Hot Slot™: 777 Hold the Jackpot™ The game features 10 fixed bet lines. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Symbols pay from leftmost to right on consecutive reels. Wins from different lines are added together. All Bonus symbols pay only in the Bonus Game. Drawing at least 6 Bonus symbols activates the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. When a Sticky symbol appears, the outcome of remaining nonsticky symbols is entirely random. The maximum win amount is 1500x bet. SPECIAL FEATURES CASH INFINITY™ Cash Infinity™ symbols may randomly appear in the base game and stick to the reels until the end of the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. All sticky Cash Infinity™ symbols are locked in place while other symbols are spun. Cash Infinity™ symbols award prizes ranging from 5x-10x player's bet. However, the prize will be paid at the end of the Bonus Game. If the Cash Infinity™ symbol is drawn on the reels, it increases the chances of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. HOLD THE JACKPOT™ BONUS GAME The game is played on 15 reels. All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-5x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols may randomly appear during the Bonus Game and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash and Cash Infinity™ symbols and multiplies them randomly by 1-20x. Mystery symbol can transform into any other Bonus symbol, apart from Cash Infinity™ symbols. Jackpot Mystery symbol can only transform into MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols. Mystery and Jackpot Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Bonus symbol values, including MINI, MINOR, MAJOR Jackpots, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 15 Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 1500x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Bonus symbol values are not added to it. CHANCE LEVEL™ The game is played with the base bet set by default. However, the Chance Level™ feature can be enabled during the base game and you can select one of 3 levels: Chance Level™ x2 – the base bet is multiplied by 2, giving you at least a 2x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x5 - the base bet is multiplied by 5, giving you at least a 6x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x10 - the base bet is multiplied by 10, giving you at least a 12x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. The Chance Level™ feature does not affect the paytable, thus symbol values remain the same as for the base bet. Extreme and Double Extreme Volatility Using the Buy Feature, Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game can be activated on Extreme and Double Extreme Volatility levels, characterized by even bigger wins than other Volatility Levels™. GAMBLE FEATURE The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. The Gamble feature can be activated only with the current win value during the ongoing game session. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the current win counter and returns to the main game. The Gamble feature can be played for a total of 7 rounds. The number of rounds in the Gamble feature depends on the max win available in the game. The win value from doubling up in the next round cannot be higher than: 150,000 Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
