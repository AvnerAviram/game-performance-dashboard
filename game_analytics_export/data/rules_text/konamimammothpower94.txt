Mammoth Power BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credit(s), which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. CAVE changes to [RUNNING ANIMALS] when part of a winning combination. CAVE appears on reels 2, 3 & 4 only. RUNNING ANIMALS substitutes for WOOLLY MAMMOTH, SMILODON, ANDREWSARCHUS, WOOLLY RHINO, DINOHIPPUS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLS Some reels contain a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: WOOLLY MAMMOTH, SMILODON, ANDREWSARCHUS, WOOLLY RHINO, DINOHIPPUS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 STONE trigger 8, 20 or 30 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 STONE award additional 2, 8, 20 or 30 free games respectively during the free games. CAVE will change to the following when part of a winning combination during the free games: Reel 2 Reel 3 Reel 4 RUNNING ANIMALS 3x RUNNING ANIMALS 3x RUNNING ANIMALS 3x or or or RUNNING ANIMALS 2x RUNNING ANIMALS 2x RUNNING ANIMALS 2x If more than 1 CAVE appears on a reel, then each CAVE on that reel, when part of a winning combination, will change to the same multiplier. RUNNING ANIMALS 2X and RUNNING ANIMALS 3X substitute for WOOLLY MAMMOTH, SMILODON, ANDREWSARCHUS, WOOLLY RHINO, DINOHIPPUS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. Each RUNNING ANIMALS 2X in a line win will multiply the pay for that line win X2. Each RUNNING ANIMALS 3X in a line win will multiply the pay for that line win X3. Different reels are used during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 6/7/2022
