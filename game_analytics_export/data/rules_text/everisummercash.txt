Summer Cash GAME RULES FREE SPIN BONUS Three Free Spin Bonus symbols landing on payline 7 trigger the bonus, awarding 8 free spins. Free spins can retrigger, awarding 8 additional spins. Maximum number of spins is 32. During the free spins, reels are weighted differently and the Summer Cash symbol appears more frequently. SYMBOL TRIGGERED JACKPOTS Summer Cash symbol is a scatter symbol. At a bet multiplier of 3 or above, progressive jackpots are available. Progressive jackpot prizes increase as the bet is increased. Each progressive jackpot tier shares a contribution pool between all bet levels. An increasing flat pay is added to each tier by bet level. When 3 to 9 Summer Cash symbols land scattered on the reels, the corresponding progressive jackpot tier is awarded. Only the highest triggered progressive jackpot tier is paid per spin. At a bet multiplier of 1 or 2, when 3 to 9 Summer Cash symbols land scattered on the reels, the corresponding flat pay jackpot is awarded. The Free Spin Bonus cannot be awarded on the same spin as a jackpot or progressive jackpot win. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers.
