Diamond Supernova 40 Diamond Supernova 40 is a 5-reel slot game with 3 symbols per reel. How to Play To select a bet, press the button and select a bet among the Available Bets. To start a game round, press the Spin button. Alternatively, press the keyboard space bar. Quick Play To skip certain animations during a game round, press anywhere on the screen. Alternatively, press the keyboard space bar. Payout Rules A line win is awarded when a number of instances of the same symbol appear consecutively from the leftmost reel. Only one line win may be awarded on a single win line. The highest line win value is always awarded. All wins occur on win lines except for Scatter prizes. Scatter prizes are added to pay line wins or equivalent. Wins on separate win lines are added. The paytable reflects the current bet configuration, based on the bet per line. Symbols Scatter Symbols Scatter Symbols can appear exclusively on reels 1, 3 and 5. There are 3 types of Scatter Symbols, each with a respective prize. Landing 3 matching Scatter Symbols grants the corresponding prize value. 3 Blue Scatters award x25 the bet 3 Red Scatters award x100 the bet 3 Gold Scatters award x1000 the bet Features Mixed Scatter Prize Landing any combination of Scatter Symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 will award a Mixed Scatter prize of x5. Paytable The paytable reflects the current bet configuration, based on the Base Bet. Base Bet The Base Bet is the bet selected for each new spin or game round. Additional Information Incomplete games will be automatically resolved after 48 hours. Any winnings resulting from automatic resolve shall be paid to the player's account. Where an incomplete game cannot be resolved, the stake will be returned to the player. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 94.23%.
