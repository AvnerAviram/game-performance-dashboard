Aztec Chief Aztec Chief is the second game in the Ultimate Choice Jackpots game family, featuring a 3-level multi Jackpots. The game features short term persist WILD REEL in the base and free games where entire reel becomes WILD for a limited period. Jackpot pick bonus is the true pick bonus where player can win either credit prize or one of jackpots. 1. Base game • During Base game, collecting 2 COINS on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel. • WILD REEL turns all symbols in WILD for next 2 spins (Bonus symbol turns into Bonus wild). • 7 Free Spins are awarded by 3 or more Bonus symbols and/or Bonus Wild. • During base game, any COIN on the screen may trigger JACKPOT PICK BONUS randomly. 2. Bonus Game(s) Red Silk has 2 Bonus; the Free Spins Bonus and Jackpot Pick Bonus 2.1 Free Spins Bonus • 7 Free Spins are awarded by 3 or more Bonus and/or Bonus Wild symbols. • During Free Spins 2, 3, 4 and 5 Bonus and/or Bonus Wild symbols awards extra 2, 3, 5 or 10 free spins respectively. • During Free Spins, collecting 1 COIN on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel and WILD REEL will stay rest of the free spin. 2.2 Jackpot Pick Bonus • During base game, any COIN on the screen may trigger JACKPOT PICK BONUS randomly. • During Jackpot Bonus, player may select 1 of 10 objects. • Objects may reveal and award credit prizes or JACKPOT which reveal and awards one of jackpot prizes. • Jackpot Pick bonus is not available during free spins bonus.
