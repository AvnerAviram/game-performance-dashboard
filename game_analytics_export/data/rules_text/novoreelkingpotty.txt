Reel King Potty Rules Round, jolly, and in high spirits: How could it be otherwise - our merry monarch will lead you to the royal treasuries with Reel King Potty. You can have a taster of your large winnings first on 5 reels and 20 win lines - but it gets really exciting once the reels change colour: Then you either win the Reel King Feature or one of 3 sparkling jackpots with Reel King Potty-Feature. A royal audience! Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along a win line. The win lines run in different patterns from left to right the reels and form specific winning patterns. To achieve a win line, you must land several identical symbols along a win line from the left without interruption. The WILD symbols only appear on reels 3, 4 and 5 and act as a substitute for all other symbols. Reel King Feature The Reel King Feature starts as soon as you land 5 yellow reels in a spin. Once triggered, either one or several windows open in which a Reel King character appears. The Reel Kings spin and any combination of 3x the "7" provides whopping winnings. If the diamond is part of the combination, the affected Reel King is disabled. Reel king Potty Feature With 5 red reels you win the Reel King Potty Feature in which the Pot King ensures royal winnings. He spins his reels and with a little luck, according to the winning combination, he pays out the King-Pot, Gold Pot, Silver Pot or Bronze Pot! You can find all of the winning combinations directly in the game under "Paytable". RTP The theoretical return to player for this game is 95% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
