Green Lantern Game Rules Play 1 to 50 lines LINES can be changed by using the left and right arrows BET amount can be changed by using the left and right arrows SPIN begins the game with the current BET and LINES Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE Payline wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins except when playing the Bonus Bet Feature wins are added to scatter and payline wins Win combinations pay from left to right only except Scattered Ring Green Lantern appears on Reels 2 and 4 only and substitutes for all symbols except Scattered Ring The prize is doubled when Green Lantern substitutes in a winning combination Bonus Bet The Bonus Bet is activated by playing 50 lines plus 10 extra coins The Ring Feature is only available when the Bonus Bet is activated When the Bonus Bet is activated, scatter prizes are multiplied by 50 and the bet per line LANTERN Free Games 24 Free Games are awarded when Green Lantern appears on Reels 2 and 4, and Battery appears on Reel 3 During the Free Games, the prize is quadrupled when Green Lantern substitutes in a winning combination Every Battery that appears adds an additional Green Lantern to Reels 2 and 4 for the remaining Free Games The Lantern Free Games cannot be retriggered Free Games are played at the lines and bet of the trigger game Free Game wins are added to payline and scatter wins RING Feature The Ring Feature is triggered when the Bonus Bet is activated and 3 or more Scattered Rings appear Sinestro challenges the Green Lantern to a Power Duel A Ring Battle ensues with the two energy beams clashing onscreen If Green Lantern is unable to defeat Sinestro, a prize is awarded based on where the green beam reaches If Green Lantern is unable to defeat Sinestro, a prize is awarded based on where the green beam reaches If Sinestro is defeated in the Ring Battle, you win up to 100 times the triggering bet!
