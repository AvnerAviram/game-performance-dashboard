Fishing Bob Slingo How to Play Select your stake and press SPIN to begin. You have 10 spins to match numbers on the reel with numbers on the grid. Complete Slingos to move up the Payladder. Fishing Bob Slingo Jackpots Jackpots are awarded by landing 3, 4 or 5 JACKPOT overlays on adjacent reels. 3 JACKPOT overlays awards Minor Catch. Minor Catch awards x50 initial game stake. 4 JACKPOT overlays awards Major Catch. Major Catch awards X150 initial game stake. 5 JACKPOT overlays awards Monster Catch. Monster Catch awards X1000 initial game stake. Only one Jackpot can be awarded per spin. Extra Spins Extra Spins can be purchased after each game. The price of each spin is dependent on the grid position and potential prizes. Prices may be in excess of your base stake. The price of the next spin is shown on the SPIN FOR button. Bonus Spin symbols are removed from the reels during Extra Spins. Rules All symbols occur on all reels. Bonus Spin symbols award an additional spin. Extra Spins are available after each game. Based on best strategy the standard game has a theoretical RTP of 95.04%. Based on best strategy each Extra Spin has a minimum theoretical RTP of 94.98%. Prices are rounded to the nearest whole value which may alter the RTP. Malfunction voids pays and plays. In Extra Spins some potential prizes may require Super Wilds. Pending Games Incomplete games will be automatically completed 48 hours after launch. Any remaining spins will be played out with a random number generator used to make any player decisions.
