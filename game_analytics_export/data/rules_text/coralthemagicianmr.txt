Crystal Illusions Feature The feature begins with 2 waves of crystals being awarded. Different coloured crystals rise up to fill the hexagonal grid. When the grid is full, wins are paid for any combination of 3 or more matching adjacent crystal symbols. Wins are paid according to the number of matching symbols in the winning combination. As winning combinations are processed they are removed from the grid. Once all the wins have been processed, any remaining crystals then move up to the fill any gaps above and new crystals rise up to fill the grid again. The sequence continues until there are no more winning combinations in the grid. If there are any waves remaining, the grid is cleared and new crystals rise up to fill the grid and the sequence continues. During the feature, if any appear, the number of waves remaining is incremented by 1. The feature ends when no more winning combinations are available and there are no waves remaining.
