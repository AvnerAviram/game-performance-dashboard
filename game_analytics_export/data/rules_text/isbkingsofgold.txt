Wild The Wild symbol is stacked and you can get a Wild in one, two or three positions on the same reel. The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Bonus symbols and Gold Coins, to form winning combinations. 3.2. Free Spins During normal spins, three Bonus symbols anywhere on reels 1, 3 and 5 trigger 8 Free Spins. During Free Spins, at the start of each spin, between 2 and 5 reels are selected, randomly, to spin in sync (same symbols on each reel). Note: Free Spins cannot be re-triggered. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. During Free Spins you cannot change the bet. 3.3. Pharaoh's Gold Respins 5 or more Gold Coins symbols anywhere on the reels trigger 3x Pharaoh's Gold Respins - these are played on a special set of reels where only Gold Coins and Golden Pharaoh symbols appear. The re-spins start with the initial Gold Coins symbols in place. During Pharaoh's Gold Respins when either a Gold Coins symbol or Golden Pharaoh symbol, that collects Gold Coins, lands on the reels, the number of re-spins is reset to 3. All Gold Coins symbols that land during re-spins remain in place until all re-spins end. In addition, during re-spins, if the Golden Pharaoh symbol lands on the reels, the values of all Gold Coins symbols that are currently present on the reels are accumulated on the position of the Golden Pharaoh and the re-spins are reset to 3. The re-spins end when no additional Gold Coins symbols appear on the special set of reels or when all the positions on the special set of reels are filled. The sum of the values from each position on the special set of reels is added to your balance. The values displayed on Gold Coins symbols are shown in the selected currency/coin. Pharaoh's Gold Respins cannot be triggered during Free Spins. During Pharaoh's Gold Respins you cannot change the bet. When the Pharaoh's Gold Respins end all Gold Coin values in view will be paid. 4. Other game buttons 4.1.
