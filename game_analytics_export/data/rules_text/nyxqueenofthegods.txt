Queen of the Gods RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: 23- VERY HIGH GAME RULES This game is played between 729 and 1,000,000 ways. All 7 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to ways wins. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. BASE GAME 4 or more Jackpot Symbol appearing in any position on the reels after all tumbling is complete awards a jackpot prize. 4 Jackpot Symbol awards 100 times the total bet. 5 Jackpot Symbol awards 1000 times the total bet. 6 or more Jackpot Symbol awards 10000 times the total bet. Jackpot Symbol appears only on reels 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. Jackpot Symbol is a scatter symbol. Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Bonus Symbol and Jackpot Symbol. 1 Wild Symbol substitutes for 3 High Pay 1 Symbol, High Pay 2 Symbol, High Pay 3 Symbol, High Pay 4 Symbol, or High Pay 5 Symbol symbols. 1 Wild Symbol substitutes for 1 Low Pay 1 Symbol, Low Pay 2 Symbol, Low Pay 3 Symbol, Low Pay 4 Symbol, Low Pay 5 Symbol, or Low Pay 6 Symbol. Wild Symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7. At the start of each game a random number of symbol positions will appear on reels 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. Between 3 and 10 symbol positions will appear on reels 1 and 6. Between 2 and 9 symbol positions will appear on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. Reel 7 is a sideways reel located above reels 2, 3, 4, and 5, and always displays 4 symbol positions. Symbols appearing in positions 1, 2, 3, and 4 on that reel count as being on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5, respectively, for purposes of forming winning combinations. All symbols except Bonus Symbol and Jackpot Symbol in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above on reels 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, or from the right on reel 7. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels.
