Sonia Monroy en el Planeta Navidad PRIZES MAIN GAME 20 Prize Lines. Line wins are paid from left to right. By getting 3 Free Spins symbols in a prize line, 10 Free Spins are awarded for each winning line. The Wild symbol replaces all symbols, except Bonus symbols. Maximum prize is 2373 * bet MULTIPLIERS Each winning drop increases the multiplier, up to a maximum of 'x5' in the main game and 'x15' in Free Spins, and a new drop is obtained. When no prize is obtained, the counter is reset. FREE SPINS If a player gets 3 Free Spins symbols in a prize line are awarded 10 Free Spins. 10 Free Spins are awarded for each winning line. These spins do not consume balance and all the prizes obtained are accumulated. More Free Spins can be obtained within Free Spins GIFT MINI-GAME Three 'Bonus' symbols give access to this minigame. In this mini-game 5 gifts will appear. The player must select gifts until all have been selected or EXIT appears. The final prize is the sum of the accumulated prizes.
