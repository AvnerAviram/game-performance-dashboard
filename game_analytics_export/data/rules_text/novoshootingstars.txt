Shooting Stars Main Game Shooting Stars is played on 5 reels and 10 win lines. Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along a win line. Winning combinations start on the first reel on the left or right and run along an active win line to the last reel on the right or left. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other without any other symbols between them. The Wild substitutes all symbols and actives the shooting stars bonus. Shooting Star Bonus One or more star symbol appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, or 4 activates the shooting star bonus. During the shooting star bonus, the star(s) expands to fill the entire reel and remain in place while the other reels re-spin. If a new star appears during a re-spin, it will expand and holds in place together with any previously expanded s star for another re-spin. The shooting star bonus ends when no new stars appear during a re-spin. RTP The theoretical return to player for this game is 96.1% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
