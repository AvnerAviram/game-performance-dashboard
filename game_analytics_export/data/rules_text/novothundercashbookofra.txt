THUNDER CASH - Golden Book Of Ra Game Theme and Graphics Enter the awesome mysterious scene and try to collect as many coin symbols as you can. Collect coin symbols on all positions and the Grand Jackpot is yours. While you are collecting, you may find other Jackpots as well. Main Game Thunder Cash™ - Book of Ra™ is played on 5 reels, each 3 rows in height, with 10 lines always in play. Your goal is to land 5 Explorer symbols on a win line, which will award you an immediate win of 500x total bet. All winning combinations run from left to right on win lines, except Scatter symbols. Lock & Spin Feature Do you want to win one of the amazing Jackpots in Thunder Cash™ - Book of Ra™? Then you have to collect 6 or more coin symbols in the main game. If you manage to collect them, you will enter the Lock & Spin Feature with 3 free spins to find the next coin symbols. The feature will lock down all your collected coin symbols and will check for any new coin symbols appearing during free spins. Each time you manage to find a new one, your free spins will reset back to 3 and you will be able to search for more. If you are the ONE, then you will find all 15 coin symbols and win the biggest prize in this game- THE GRAND JACKPOT. Rules All pays are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. All wins are on selected lines, except Scatters. Scatters pay out in any position. Only the highest win is paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by bet per line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. 6 or more collected coins trigger the Lock & Spin Feature, which may result in winning all of the jackpots. Gamble Feature The gamble option is disabled by default. When offered, it is limited to the max possible gamble amount defined by the operator. Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
