Eternal Dusk Paytable Eternal Dusk is a 5x4 video slot with 30 Bet Lines. The game features the Wild Surge feature, a Free Spins Bonus, and a Super Free Spins Bonus. The maximum win is 34759x bet! Wild Surge During any base game spin, the Wild Surge feature can randomly be triggered, adding 3 to 10 Wild symbols with multipliers to the reels. Each Wild symbol can appear during the Base Game with a multiplier value of x2, x3, x4, x5, or x10. When substituting in a win, Wild symbols with multipliers apply their multiplier value to the win. If two or more Wild symbols with multipliers are on a winning bet line, their multiplier values will add together before being applied to the win. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered by landing 3 or more Free Spins Bonus symbols in the base game, awarding 5 Free Spins, and introducing a Golden Roaming Wild symbol that moves to a random position on the reels before each spin. Triggering the Free Spins Bonus with 3, 4, or 5 Bonus Symbols will start the Bonus collection at 3, 4, or 5 Bonus Symbols, respectively. Landing 3 or more Bonus symbols during the Free Spins Bonus retriggers the feature, awarding 5 additional Free Spins at that stage. Wild symbols that land become Roaming Wilds, that move to new positions between each spin but they do not carry multipliers. The Multiplier Meter tracks progression through the Free Spins stages. Each stage is activated by collecting a specified number of Bonus Symbols and awards 5 additional Free Spins along with upgraded stage multipliers. Stage 2: Collect 6 Bonus Symbols for 5 additional Free Spins with a x2 Stage Multiplier. Stage 3: Collect 9 Bonus Symbols for 5 additional Free Spins with a x4 Stage Multiplier. Stage 4: Collect 13 Bonus Symbols for 5 additional Free Spins with a x8 Stage Multiplier. Stage 5: Collect 17 Bonus Symbols for 5 additional Free Spins with a x16 Stage Multiplier. All Free Spins of the current stage are completed before advancing to the next stage. When a stage is completed, the Stage Multiplier is added to all Golden Roaming Wild symbols on the reels. The Golden Roaming Wild symbol collect all Roaming Wilds on the reels, and for each Roaming Wild collected, the stage multiplier is added to the Golden Roaming Wild symbols multiplier. All Roaming Wilds are then removed from the screen, while the combined multiplier of the Roaming Wild carries over into the next stage. Landing a Super Bonus Symbol during Free Spins adds two Free Spins and awards an additional Golden Roaming Wild. Super Free Spins Bonus The Super Free Spins Bonus is awarded by triggering Free Spins with a Super Bonus symbol or by upgrading the Free Spins Bonus. During Super Free Spins, two Golden Roaming Wild symbols are present and share the same multiplier. Both Golden Roaming Wild symbols move to random positions before each spin. Super Bonus symbols do not appear during Super Free Spins. However, landing 3 or more Bonus symbols retriggers the feature and awards 5 additional Free Spins in the current stage. Fast Track gains direct access to the game's features. Fast Track features can only be activated in the base game. All game rules apply for Fast Track features.
