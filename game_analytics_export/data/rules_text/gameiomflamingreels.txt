Flaming Reels Flaming Reels Flaming Reels is a 3x3 slot game with 9 paylines, featuring classic symbols and innovative features. Wild and 2X symbols substitute for Seven and Bar symbols, with 2X symbols multiplying wins by 2x or 4x. Landing 3 2X symbols awards the top jackpot, 3 Wild symbols trigger the second-tier jackpot, and 3 mixed 2X and Wild symbols grant the third-tier jackpot. The Cash on Reels Feature activates with 3 Cash on Reel, 2X, and Wild symbols on a payline, paying the sum of the Cash on Reel values, doubled or quadrupled with 1 or 2 2X symbols. GAME FEATURES Wild Feature [Wild] Symbol is wild for Seven and Bar Symbols except when a combination of 3 [2X] Symbols and [Wild] Symbols appear on an active payline. [2X] Symbol is wild for Seven and Bar Symbols except when a combination of 3 [2X] Symbols and [Wild] Symbols appear on an active payline. 1 [2X] Symbol in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 2x the win. 2 [2X] Symbols in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 4x the win. 3 [2X] Symbols on an active payline awards the highest tier jackpot. 3 [Wild] Symbols on an active payline awards the second-tier jackpot. 3 mixed [2X], [Wild] Symbols on an active payline awards the third-tier jackpot. 3 [2X] Symbols or [Wild] Symbols do not substitute for the third-tier prize. Cash on Reels Feature The Cash on Reels Feature is awarded when a combination of 3 [Cash on Reel], [2X], [Wild] Symbols appear on an active payline and will pay the sum of the numbers on the [Cash on Reel] Symbol(s). If the combination involved 1 [2X] Symbol it will pay 2x the win. If the combination involved 2 [2X] Symbols it will pay 4x the win. Possible [Cash on Reel] values are 250, 150, 100, 60, 40, 25, 20, 15, 10. Not all [Cash on Reel] values appear on each reel.
