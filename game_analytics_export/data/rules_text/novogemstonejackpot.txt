Gemstone Jackpot Rules These gems at Gemstone Jackpot will make your eyes sparkle. Tidy up the treasure chest by lining up the colourful-glistening gems in the right order. The 'Lock & Spin' Feature will support you because as soon as you have landed a winning combination the jewels will be fixed at their positions and you will bag brilliant winnings! The aim of Gemstone Jackpotis to land 5 matching symbols side by side along the 10 lines. The winning patterns run from left to right. Lock and Spin Feature Land a winning combination on one or more lines to trigger the ‘Lock & Spin’ Feature. The symbols of the winning combination will be fixed in their positions and the remaining symbols will be spun again. If symbols land, which complement the current winning pattern, they will also be fixed and the remaining positions will be spun again. This will be repeated until no more symbols land that complement the win lines. Then the total win will be awarded, the feature ends and the regular game will be started again. You can call up the Paytable during the game by clicking on the ‘Paytable’ button in the bottom left corner of the playing field.
