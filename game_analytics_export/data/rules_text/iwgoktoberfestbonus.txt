Oktoberfest Bonus Introduction Flip the 25 face beer mats. Find 3, 4 or 5 like symbols in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line to win a prize! Find a Gold Card to unlock the GOLD BEER MAT BONUS or find 3 "waitress with beer" symbols anywhere to unlock the BONUS GAME. Start the game Select the stake amount, then to start the game press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game, any winnings are displayed. To play again press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. How to play At the start of the game there are 25 face down beer mats. Select each mat to turn the mat to reveal a symbol. Or press the REVEAL ALL button to flip all beer mats. Find a Gold Beer Mat to unlock the GOLD BEER MAT BONUS or find 3 "waitress with beer" symbols anywhere to unlock the BONUS GAME. GOLD BEER MAT BONUS GAME Pick beer mats. Every beer mat adds to your prize. Keep going until you find the COLLECT card. At this point all prizes revealed during the game are win. BONUS GAME Select 1 of the 3 beer taps. Beer will then fill the glass. Win the prize indicated at the point that the beers stops pouring. Winning Wins are achieved by: - Finding 3 or more like symbols in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line. Win the corresponding prize on the prize table. - GOLD BEER MAT BONUS game - win all prizes revealed during the game. - BONUS GAME - win the prize indicated on the glass when the beer stops pouring. General information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by player do not affect the outcome of the game.
