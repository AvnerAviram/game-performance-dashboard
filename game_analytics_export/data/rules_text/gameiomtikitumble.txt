Tiki Tumble Tiki Tumble is a 5-reel slot with 20 win lines. Features include Wild symbols, Free Games Bonus Feature, and Nudging Wilds Feature. Base Game Land 3 or more symbols that aren’t a Scatter Symbol on a win line. Nudging Wilds This is triggered when 1 or more Wild Symbols appear in the top 3 symbol positions on 1 or more reels before the reels spin. These then nudge down by 1 symbol. After all spins and nudges are complete, the reels are evaluated for wins. Free Games Land 3 or more Gold Mask Scatter Symbols anywhere on the reels and the free games will start! Reels 2 and 4 are filled with Wild symbols. For every spin, the wilds will nudge by 1, adding a multiplier to the wins. Changing Bet Size Change your bet size and the symbols on the reels will change to show the result from the last spin at the selected bet size. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
