Super Reel - Spin It Hot 1. Overview Note: This game is available in portrait mode only. The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 20 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win Yes RTP 96.00% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Scatter and Spin it Hot symbols, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2. Scatter Symbol Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols will trigger Free Spins . Note : Free Spins can be retriggered. 3.3. Gold Spin it Hot symbol Landing 6 or more Gold Spin it Hot symbols will trigger Spin it Hot Respins . 3.4. Hot Prizes Hot prizes are seen cycling through during the game. These prizes can only be won during the Spin it Hot Respins round. Note : Mini, Major and Mega prizes offer more ways to win big. The following Hot Prizes are available: 0.5x Total Bet 1x Total Bet 2x Total Bet 3x Total Bet 4x Total Bet 5x Total Bet 6x Total Bet 7x Total Bet 8x Total Bet 9x Total Bet Mini - 10x Total Bet Major - 25x Total Bet Mega - 50x Total Bet 3.5. Spin it Hot Respins 6 or more Gold Spin it Hot symbols trigger the Spin it Hot Respins feature. Spin it Hot Respins awards 3 spins on 10 reels, with respins reset to 3 every time a Gold Spin it Hot symbol lands. During Respins, only Spin it Hot symbols are on the reels and only gold ones can award a prize. Gold Spin it Hot symbols can only land in the middle position on any reel. Each Gold Spin it Hot symbol will freeze the reel and guarantee a prize at the end of the feature. Trigger all Gold Spin it Hot symbols in Spin it Hot respins to activate the super spin with all possible winning positions covered. Spin it Hot Respins can be triggered in F ree Spins . 3.6. Free Spins Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols will trigger Free Spins : 8 Free Spins for 3 Scatter symbols 12 Free Spins for 4 Scatter symbols 15 Free Spins for 5 Scatter symbols Free Spins will feature upgraded Mini, Major and Mega prizes which can be won during Spin it Hot Respins . One of the following upgraded Mini, Major and Mega prizes are active: 25x, 50x, 100x Bet 50x, 100x, 250x Bet 100x, 250x, 500x Bet 250x, 500x, 1000x Bet Free Spins offers more chances to trigger Spin it Hot Respins . Note : Free Spins can be retriggered. Pot Boost: upgraded Mini,Major, Mega prizes in Free Spins. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play. Loss limit : click + or - to select the value of the loss limit; Stop if a win reaches : click + or - to select the value of the maximum win; Stop if JACKPOT is won (only for games with Progressive Jackpots): to enable this setting, tick the checkbox ; to disable it, remove the tick. Click the ACTIVATE button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature. Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time. 5.2.
