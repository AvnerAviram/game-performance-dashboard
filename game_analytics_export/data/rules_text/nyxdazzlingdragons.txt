Dazzling Dragons RTP 95.0% VOLATILITY: 7.2 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Select Bet Multiplier Click the Spin button to spin the reel. Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to line wins. High Pay symbol 1, High Pay symbol 2, High Pay symbol 3, High Pay symbol 4, or High Pay symbol 5 appearing in all positions on 2 or more reels awards a scatter pay. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 or more Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on the reels starts the Free Games Bonus. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols awards 7 free games, 4 Free Games Bonus symbols awards 15 free games, 5 Free Games Bonus symbols awards 50 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games High Pay symbol 1, Free Games High Pay symbol 2, Free Games High Pay symbol 3, Free Games High Pay symbol 4, or Free Games High Pay symbol 5 appearing in all positions on 2 or more reels awards a scatter pay. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain.
