Trigger the Wheel Bonus and spin the wheel to win prizes, a Ca$hLink Deluxe multiplier, or the Cool Catch award! How to Bet Cool Catch! features 20 paylines. Bet Settings Menu Press the cog or TOTAL BET to open the Bet Settings menu. Lines Displays the current number of paylines. Coins Displays the current number of coins being played. Betting is fixed at 20 paylines. Coin Value Displays the current coin value shown in currency. Total Bet Displays the current total bet shown in currency. Change by selecting the desired total bet. On desktop devices, optionally press the minus button (–) to decrease the total bet or press the plus button (+) to increase the total bet. Press OK to confirm and close the Bet Settings menu. Spin Press the circular arrow to submit the bet and spin the reels. Auto Spin Feature Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of spins at the current coin value. For jurisdictions where Auto Spin is enabled: Select the number of auto spins and where available, the loss limit, and the (optional) single win limit. After selecting the desired bet configuration, press AUTO SPIN, and choose how many spins to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until STOP is pressed, a bonus is triggered, the balance is insufficient to spin, or any enabled win/loss limit settings are reached. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions. Ca$hLink Deluxe Feature If a Treasure Chest Scatter Symbol appears on reel 5 and a Coin Scatter Symbol appears on reel 1, 2, 3 or 4, the Ca$hLink Deluxe feature is triggered. All Coin Scatter Symbols that do not contain an award are assigned a prize value. All revealed prize values and awards are awarded to the player. See the paytable for details. If a Treasure Chest Scatter Symbol appears on reel 5 and a Helm Coin Scatter Symbol appears on reel 1, the Ca$hLink Deluxe feature and the Wheel Bonus are triggered. Reveal an award symbol to win the corresponding award: Grand Symbol awards the Grand Award. Mega Symbol awards the Mega Award. Major Symbol awards the Major Award. Minor Symbol awards the Minor Award. Prize values and awards are shown in currency. More than 1 award may be awarded in the Ca$hLink Deluxe feature. Wheel Bonus A Helm Coin Scatter Symbol appearing on reel 1 and a Treasure Chest Scatter Symbol appearing on reel 5 triggers the Wheel Bonus. When the Wheel Bonus is triggered, a Ca$hLink prize value is determined. The Ca$hLink prize value is the sum of all Coin Scatter Symbol and Helm Coin Scatter Symbol values on the reels of the triggering spin. 7 pointers are available on the wheel. The number of active pointers is based on the triggering bet amount. Prize values and multipliers are available on the wheel. See the paytable for details. After the wheel stops spinning, all prize values indicated by active pointers are awarded to the player. If the Cool Catch wedge is indicated by an active pointer, the Cool Catch prize is awarded. Prize values are not multiplied by the triggering coin value. Prize values are displayed in currency. When a multiplier of 5x, 10x, or 20x is indicated by an active pointer, the Ca$hLink value for that bonus is multiplied. More than 1 multiplier slice may be indicated by active pointers, in which case the Ca$hLink value for that bonus is multiplied by the sum of all indicated multipliers. Prize values are not multiplied by this multiplier. Free Spins Bonus 3 Free Spins Scatter Symbols appearing anywhere on reels 1, 3, and 5 trigger the Free Spins Bonus and award 10 free spins. During the bonus, 3 Free Spins Scatter Symbols appearing anywhere on reels 1, 3, and 5 retrigger the Free Spins Bonus and award an additional 10 free spins. If the Ca$hLink Deluxe feature is triggered during the Free Spins Bonus, the total of the prize values displayed on all Coin Scatter Symbols appearing on the reels is awarded. The bonus can be retriggered multiple times, up to a maximum of 200 free spins per bonus. Free spins are played at same total bet and same number of paylines as the triggering spin. Options All Sound Toggle game sounds on or off. Sound Effects Toggle sound effects on or off. Music Toggle music on or off. Rules Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. All line win symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels, beginning with the far-left reel. Only the highest winner is paid on each payline. Line wins are multiplied by the coin value on the winning line. Coinciding line wins on different paylines are added. Free Spins Scatter Symbol appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 only in the base game and the Free Spins Bonus. Bonus and Feature wins are independent from line wins and are added to the total amount paid. Wild Symbol appears on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 in the base game and the Free Spins Bonus. Treasure Chest Scatter Symbol appears on reel 5 only in the base game and the Free Spins Bonus. Helm Coin Scatter Symbol appears on reel 1 only in the base game. Coin Scatter Symbols appear on reels 1, 2, 3, and 4 only in the base game and the Free Spins Bonus. More than 1 Grand, Mega, Major, or Minor award can be won in the base game and the Free Spins Bonus. Wins are shown in currency. In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable. In some jurisdictions, regardless of bet size, there may be an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the bonus, plus the outcome which launched the bonus.
