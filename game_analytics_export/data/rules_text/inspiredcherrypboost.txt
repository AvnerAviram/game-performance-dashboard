WIN LINES ALL GAMES ARE PLAYED WITH 20 WIN LINES. WINS CAN OCCUR ACROSS ANY OR ALL OF THE WIN LINES. ONLY THE HIGHEST AWARD IS PAID PER WINNING COMBINATION. WINS ON DIFFERENT PLAYED LINES ARE ADDED. ALL WINS PAY LEFT TO RIGHT ACROSS ADJACENT REELS, STARTING WITH THE LEFTMOST REEL. THE BONUS WIN IS ADDED TO ANY COINCIDENTAL LINE WINS. WILD SYMBOLS SUBSTITUTES FOR ALL SYMBOLS AS PART OF A LINE WIN. APPEARS ON REELS 2, 3 AND 4 IN BOTH THE BASE GAME AND THE FREE GAMES BONUS. POWER BOOST THE POWER BOOST GRAPHIC DISPLAYS THE CURRENT POWER BOOST THAT CAN BE AWARDED IN THE FREE GAMES BONUS. THE CURRENT POWER BOOST IS AWARDED ONCE AT THE END OF THE FREE GAMES BONUS WHEN 30X BET OR MORE HAS BEEN AWARDED INTO THE BONUS WIN METER. THE POWER BOOST CAN AWARD ONE OF THE FOLLOWING, PROGRESSING FROM TOP TO BOTTOM: +5 FREE GAMES X2 MULTIPLIER OF THE BONUS WIN AWARD VALUE. X3 MULTIPLIER OF THE BONUS WIN AWARD VALUE. X4 MULTIPLIER OF THE BONUS WIN AWARD VALUE. X5 MULTIPLIER OF THE BONUS WIN AWARD VALUE. X10 MULTIPLIER OF THE BONUS WIN AWARD VALUE. IF THE AWARD IS +5 FREE GAMES, ANY ADDITIONAL WINS FROM THOSE SPINS ARE ADDED TO THE BONUS WIN METER. IF THE AWARD IS A MULTIPLIER, THE AWARD VALUE IN THE BONUS WIN METER WILL BE MULTIPLIED BY THAT MULTIPLIER. IF THE POWER BOOST METER DOESN'T REACH 30X OR MORE THE POWER BOOST AWARD IS PROGRESSED ON TO THE NEXT AWARD ON THE LIST ON THE PREVIOUS PAGE, UP TO THE MAXIMUM OF X10 MULTIPLIER. IF THE CURRENT POWER BOOST IS AT X10 AND THE 30X BET OR MORE IS NOT ACHIEVED THEN THE MULTIPLIER REMAINS AT X10. AFTER THE POWER BOOST HAS BEEN AWARDED, THE POWER BOOST AWARD WILL BE RESET TO +5 FREE GAMES. THE VALUE OF THE POWER BOOST IS SAVED ACROSS GAMES AND GAME LOADS. THIS VALUE IS ALSO SAVED FOR EACH BET INDEPENDENTLY. FREE GAMES BONUS LANDING 5 OR MORE GOLD CHERRY SYMBOLS DURING THE BASE GAME AWARDS THE PLAYER 5 FREE GAMES IN THE FREE GAMES BONUS WHEN THE FREE GAMES BONUS IS ACTIVATED, THE VALUES ON ALL THE GOLD CHERRIES IN VIEW ON THE REELS ARE ADDED TOGETHER AND AWARDED TO THE PLAYER BEFORE STARTING THE FREE GAMES BONUS. ALL COINCIDENTAL LINE PAYS ARE PAID. COINCIDENTAL LINE WINS FROM THE TRIGGERING BASE GAME ARE NOT ADDED TO THE BONUS WIN METER, NOR THE POWER BOOST METER IN THE BONUS. WINS FROM GOLD CHERRIES ON THE TRIGGERING BASE GAME ARE ADDED TO BOTH THE BONUS WIN METER AND THE POWER BOOST METER IN THE BONUS. ONCE AWARDED, THE TOTAL AWARD VALUE FROM ALL THE GOLD CHERRY SYMBOLS IN VIEW ON THE REELS IS USED AS THE VALUE ON ALL THE GOLD CHERRIES IN THE FREE GAMES BONUS. DURING THE FREE GAMES BONUS, THE VALUES ON ALL THE GOLD CHERRY SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE BONUS WIN METER ALONG WITH ANY COINCIDENTAL LINE PAYS. Spin Chance offers the player an opportunity to wager their remaining credit in an attempt at winning an additional base game spin at the current bet. To play Spin Chance, select the 'PLAY' button to begin moving the pointer across the meter. Alternatively, select the 'NO THANKS' button to exit Spin Chance with the remaining credit. If the pointer stops on the green segment, a spin at the current bet is awarded and plays automatically. If the pointer stops on the red segment, the player loses any remaining credit and exits to the base game. The red and green segments are proportional to the amount of credit in relation to the current bet. The chance of winning a spin is determined by this ratio. GENERAL INFORMATION GAME RECOVERY In the event of an unexpected interruption / termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) for this game is 94.50%. The outcome of any play during the game is pre-determined.
