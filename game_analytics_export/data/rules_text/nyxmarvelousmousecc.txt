Marvelous Mouse Coin Combo HOW TO PLAY WILDS The House symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except for the ELEMENT COIN symbols. In base game and in all Free Spins features, the symbol appears on reel 2, 3 and 4. ELEMENT COINS Each ELEMENT COIN that lands during the base game will fly into the corresponding coloured bowl. Any ELEMENT COIN may trigger the corresponding bowl. More than one bowl can be triggered on a single spin If bowl/s are triggered the corresponding feature will start. JACKPOT PICK FEATURE Any red ELEMENT COIN landing in the RED bowl may trigger the JACKPOT PICK feature. 15 coins will be presented, coins are revealed by clicking on them. If 3 UPGRADE symbols are revealed before a JACKPOT is awarded, the JACKPOT won is upgraded to the next level. If Grand is awarded, it will be awarded twice. COLLECTION BOWLS BOWLS TRIGGERED FEATURES AWARDED BLUE BOWL MIGHTY Free Spins (4 rows) GREEN BOWL MEGA Free Spins (5 rows) RED BOWL JACKPOT PICK BLUE BOWL + GREEN BOWL SUPER Free Spins (6 rows) BLUE BOWL + RED BOWL ULTRA Free Spins (4 rows) + JACKPOT GREEN BOWL + RED BOWL SUPREME Free Spins (5 rows) + JACKPOT BLUE BOWL + GREEN BOWL + RED BOWL ULTIMATE Free Spins (6 rows) + JACKPOT Triggering the BLUE or GREEN bowl in base game awards 6 Free Spins. In Free Spins the ELEMENT COINS that land will have either +1 Free Spin, +2 Free Spins or a cash prize with up to x100 total bet. If +1 or +2 Free Spins is revealed, the total Free Spin meter is increased. If a cash prize is revealed it is added to the Win meter. In ULTRA, SUPREME and ULTIMATE Free Spins, Mini or Minor Jackpots may be awarded by ELEMENT COINS. If ULTRA, SUPREME or ULTIMATE Free Spins are awarded, the JACKPOT PICK feature will be played first. ELEMENT COINS may also award the JACKPOT PICK feature. Only RED ELEMENT COINS are available in Free Spins. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. Free Spins will end if the maximum win has been achieved. The theoretical return to player of this game is 96.26% Malfunction Voids All Pays and Plays. Last modification date: 8/3/2021
