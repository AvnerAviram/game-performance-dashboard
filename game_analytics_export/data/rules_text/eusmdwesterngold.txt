Western Gold About this Game Welcome to Western Gold. This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. It features Free Spins, Wild and Scatter pays. General Win on a line pays if in succession from the leftmost to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols and Scatters. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Free Spins and Scatter pays cannot trigger in the same spin. Scatters In the base game, landing at least 4 Scatters in one spin pays out. In the base game, Scatters pay when the spin is complete. Only the highest paying count of the Scatters pays out. The Scatter payout is awarded as a multiple of the total bet according to the PAYTABLE. Scatter payouts are added to line wins. Free Spins Free Spins symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game. 3 Free Spins symbols awards 3 Free Spins. In Free Spins, Scatters do not pay when they land, instead they are collected. Collected Scatters are tracked on a meter. A maximum of 12 Scatters can be collected. When one or more Scatters are collected in a spin, and the total number of Scatters collected is less than 12, the Free Spins counter is reset to 3 at the end of that spin. When the maximum of 12 Scatters has been collected, any other Scatters remaining on the visible reel area will not be collected, and the Free Spins counter will not reset. When the maximum of 12 Scatters has been collected, all Scatters are removed from the reel sets for the remaining spins of Free Spins. The Scatter pay is only awarded when the feature is complete and is based on the total number of Scatters collected. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount.
