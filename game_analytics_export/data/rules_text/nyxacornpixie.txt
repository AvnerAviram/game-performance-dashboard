Acorn Pixie Game Introduction Acorn Pixie is a 5 reel game with 30 win lines available on all bets. A cluster of 4 wilds, 2 wide by 2 high may be added at random to the reels in any position during a game 3 scattered symbols, appearing anywhere in reels 2, 3 and 4 triggers Acorn Pixie Bonus. Acorn Pixie Bonus 3 scattered symbols, appearing anywhere in reels 2, 3 and 4 triggers Acorn Pixie Bonus. 7 free games are awarded, with no retriggers. Acorn Pixie Bonus is played on a reel layout as shown here. Wilds substitute for all symbols. Acorns (pictured above) transform symbols on which they land The symbols disappear and reveal the wild background tree Once a symbol position becomes wild, it remains wild for the remainder of the bonus. The first game reveals the bottom position of the middle reel. For all subsequent games, any position that connects to any revealed wild is eligible to become wild. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 30 win lines. The free spins game contains 50 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as cash values. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. Buy a Bonus Press Buy Bonus to enter Buy A Bonus selection screen. Select one of the three options: 6 Games 8 Games 12 Games and press Start to start the bonus. Buy A Bonus games uses bonus reels that differ from the base game reels A bet in Buy A Bonus buys number of bonus games featured under chosen option and all pays on paylines Line pays are multiplied by 1 All pays in currency Buy A Bonus ends when no games remain Buy A Bonus games use the same reels as Acorn Pixie Bonus Optimal strategy is to select 12 bonus games The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96%.
