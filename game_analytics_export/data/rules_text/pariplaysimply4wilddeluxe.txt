Simply 4 Wild Deluxe RULES Simply 4 Wild Deluxe is a highly animated slot machine game that brings the fun of Simply Wild to the next level; with five different game modes to choose from! Try your chances at a BIG WIN or a MEGA WIN in this hot new 10 to 25 win line, 4 to 7 reel, 3 row video slot game. The ful reels are filled with retro fruit symbols, sevens, bells and special star and crown symbols. Choose from five different game play modes that each have a different number of reels, win lines and bet options. The game mode options are: GAME 1: 4 Reels / 10 Win Lines / 2 Coin Bet GAME 1+2: 5 Reels / 15 Win Lines / 3 Coin Bet GAME 1-3: 6 Reels / 20 Win Lines / 4 Coin Bet GAME 1-4: 7 Reels / 25 Win Lines / 5 Coin Bet GAME 1-4 +SUPERBET: 7 Reels / 25 Win Lines + MYSTERY WIN / 10 Coin Bet Click the SPIN or AUTO PLAY BUTTONS to start playing. Click MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. You can also use these buttons to choose to play GAME 1, GAME 1+2, GAME 1-3, GAME 1-4 or GAME 1-4 + SUPERBET. The theoretical minimum payback percentage for this game is 94.85% (GAME 1, GAME 1+2, GAME 1-3, GAME 1-4) or 95.49% (SUPERBET mode). Malfunction voids all pays and plays. FEATURES STAR SYMBOL The STAR symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols, except the CROWN symbol. Three (3) STAR symbols on a win line awards the STAR prize. STAR symbols appear on reels 1 to 7 and are displayed on special symbols that feature both a STAR symbol and a FRUIT (ORANGE, PLUM, PEAR) or BELL symbol. CROWN SYMBOL Three (3) CROWN symbols scattered anywhere on three (3) adjacent reels awards a CROWN prize. In SUPERBET mode, this combination of symbols awards a MYSTERY WIN prize. CROWN symbols appear on reels 1 to 7 and are displayed on special symbols that feature both a CROWN symbol and a FRUIT (ORANGE, PLUM, PEAR) or BELL symbol. SUPERBET In SUPERBET mode, all seven (7) reels are active. Three (3) CROWN symbols scattered anywhere on three (3) adjacent reels awards a MYSTERY WIN prize instead of a CROWN prize. A MYSTERY WIN awards a prize that is equal to or larger than five (5) times the value of a CROWN prize. When changing from GAME 1-4 MODE to SUPERBET MODE, the bet level also doubles. MYSTERY JACKPOT – PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT Every time any player plays the game, 0.5% of every bet is added to the MYSTERY JACKPOT (PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT). The minimum guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout is 500 EUR. There is a guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout when it hits the maximum jackpot value of 5,000 EUR. Higher stakes increase the chances of winning the MYSTERY JACKPOT. Bets made with bonus money do not qualify for MYSTERY JACKPOT payouts. Although we strive for complete accuracy and transparency at all times, the jackpot counter display may be affected by multiple players playing at the same time and other external factors including Internet connectivity issues. Such factors may cause discrepancies between the jackpot amount shown on the jackpot counter and the actual jackpot payout amount. The jackpot win amount displayed on the jackpot win message always supersedes the jackpot amount displayed on the jackpot counter. The jackpot amount displayed on the jackpot win message shall be binding. The jackpot is always calculated and awarded in euros. If a jackpot winner was playing in any other currency (not euros), the jackpot win amount will be converted from euros into the currency the winner was playing with at the time of the win, using the applicable exchange rate on the date of the jackpot win.
