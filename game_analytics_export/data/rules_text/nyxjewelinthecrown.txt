Jewel in the Crown Game Help Game Introduction This game has 10 Win lines with an extra 3 free! The game has a unique layout with 5 reels in a 43334 presentation Crown Scatter symbols trigger Free Spins with Wilds and Mega Stacks Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. 3 Crown Scatter symbols on reels 2 3 and 4 trigger Free Spins The Symbol in the red crown on Reel 5 shows which symbol is Stacked in Free Spins The Symbol in the blue crown on Reel 1 shows which symbol is Wild in Free Spins The same symbol can be both Wild and Mega Stacked Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. Big Bet games can contain the Crowning Glory Trail. In the Crowning Glory Trail, there are chances for additional Big Bet Spins and an additional opportunity to enter Free Spins. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 13 win lines. At lower stakes the Win Lines pay left to right At higher stakes, 'All Wins Poay Adjacent'. In Free Spins this means 1 line can have 2 wins. All line win amounts for the Standard game are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. All line win amounts for the Big Bet game are shown in the games pay table as cash values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 Last modification date: 6/5/2020
