Bet Deﬁnitions Current Bet: The current bet is the value that appears above . Minimum Bet: The minimum bet is the lowest possible bet value, which is selected by pressing until the bet value no longer decreases. Bet Multiplier: The bet multiplier is defined as the current bet divided by the minimum bet. Autoplay Options Autoplay will allow game rounds to be played automatically. Click on the Autoplay button and select the number of Autoplay game rounds. Additional Information. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wins A winning play occurs when the ball goes through the hoop. 3 to 18 Currency Wins are awarded on a winning play. Multiplier Feature If 10 or fewer Currency Wins are awarded on any winning play, the Multiplier Feature may randomly be activated. If the Multiplier Feature is activated, a random multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, or x7 will appear. All Currency Wins will be multiplied by the random multiplier. Last modification date: 1/9/2025
