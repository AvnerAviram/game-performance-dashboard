Dynamite Chickens Base Game Rules The base game offers up to 243 ways to win. Winning combinations must occur on adjacent reels starting from the leftmost reel. Only the highest win per winning combination is paid, and all wins across multiple ways are added together. The paytable reflects the currently selected bet configuration. Wilds The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Dynamite, Mega Dynamite, Plunger, and Coin symbols. Wilds appear only on reels 2, 3, and 4. Dynamite Symbols There are three colored Dynamite symbols: Blue, Yellow, and Red. These can appear on all reels in the base game. Each Dynamite symbol transfers to a matching colored nest below the reels. When 6 Dynamite symbols are collected in a nest, the corresponding Chicken Bonus is triggered. The nests have a persistent state that resets only when the corresponding Chicken Bonus is triggered using regular Dynamite symbols. Dynamite symbols appearing during bonus rounds do not contribute to this state. Mega Dynamite symbols can also appear and instantly trigger the corresponding Chicken Bonus. Only one Mega Dynamite can appear per spin, and it cannot appear together with regular Dynamite symbols. Triggering a bonus with Mega Dynamite does not reset the nest’s persistent state. The state is tied to the current stake level. Multiple bonuses can be triggered in the same spin and will be awarded in the following order: Jackpot, Blue, Yellow, and Red. Dynamite symbols act as Scatter symbols, while Mega Dynamite symbols act as Bonus symbols. A maximum of one of each colored Dynamite symbol can appear per spin. Chicken Bonuses There are three Chicken Bonus types: Blue, Yellow, and Red. During a Chicken Bonus, only the matching colored Dynamite symbols appear. Blue Chicken Bonus Awards 6 free spins. Dynamite symbols appear only on reel 5 and add +1 free spin. A multiplier between 2x and 5x is applied to wins on each spin. Yellow Chicken Bonus Awards 6 free spins. Dynamite symbols appear on reel 5 and add +1 free spin. Coin symbols can appear and award instant prizes between 1x and 10x the bet. Red Chicken Bonus Awards 6 free spins. Dynamite symbols appear on reel 5 and add +1 free spin. A multiplier between 2x and 5x is applied. Coin symbols can award prizes between 2x and 20x or trigger a jackpot. The Coin symbol acts as a Scatter symbol. Jackpot Plunger Bonus The Plunger symbol is a 3x3 symbol and must land fully visible to trigger the Jackpot Plunger Bonus. During the bonus, 15 plungers are displayed. Selecting a plunger reveals either a jackpot (Mini, Minor, Major, Grand) or an Upgrade icon. Matching 3 identical jackpot symbols awards the corresponding prize. Matching 3 Upgrade icons increases the jackpot level to the next tier. If 3 Grand Jackpot symbols are matched, the Grand prize is doubled. Jackpot values: Mini – 25x Minor – 100x Major – 250x Grand – 1250x Eggcellent Respin The Eggcellent Respin may randomly trigger during the base game, respinning the reels to produce a winning outcome. Jackpot and Dynamite symbols cannot be awarded during this respin feature. Turbo Mode Turbo Mode increases the speed of gameplay and can be toggled on or off at any time. This feature is disabled during free spins and does not affect game outcomes. Availability may vary depending on jurisdiction. Legal Notice Each game outcome is independent, and the probability of any result remains the same for every spin. The theoretical Return to Player (RTP) is 93.97%. If a malfunction occurs, all pays and plays are void. Malfunction Voids all pays and plays.
