BUZZ SAW FEATURE • Before the free spins begin, each BUZZ SAW symbol will move to the right until off-screen, starting with the rightmost BUZZ SAW symbol. • Each position that BUZZ SAW symbol touches while moving to the right, including its initial position, is evaluated as if HARD HAT symbol had landed there during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, one at a time. • During the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 free spin. • An alternate set of reels is used during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol does not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. MEGA HAT FEATURE • Before the free spins begin, 1 Mega Hat spin is awarded and played. • During the Mega Hat spin, all reels spin together and Mega Hat symbols MEGA HAT 2x2, MEGA HAT 3x3, MEGA HAT 3x5 are the only active symbols. • The Mega Hat spin cannot stop with Mega Hat symbols partially revealed • After the Mega Hat spin, STRAW FRAME is added to each position covered by Mega Hat symbols. • During the MEGA HAT FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 free spin. • An alternate set of reels is used during the MEGA HAT FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol does not appear. During the feature, credits are awarded as shown. MANSION FEATURE • Only BRICK FRAME will appear during the MANSION FEATURE. • BRICK FRAME is added to the position of each BUZZ SAW symbol. • During the MANSION FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 free spin. • An alternate set of reels is used during the MANSION FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol does not appear. During the feature, credits are awarded as shown. FREE SPINS FEATURE • 6 or more scattered HARD HAT symbols during the base game triggers the FREE SPINS FEATURE and award 6 free spins. • STRAW FRAME is added to the position of each triggering HARD HAT symbol. • During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 free spin. • An alternate set of reels is used during the FREE SPINS FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol does not appear. During the feature, credits are awarded as shown. SIMULTANEOUS FEATURE TRIGGER • If both the WHEEL FEATURE and the FREE SPINS FEATURE are won in a single spin, the WHEEL FEATURE will play out first.
