Fortune Cookie Fortunes How To Play Min bet = $1.00. Max bet = $200.00. Press to make a bet. Press to start. A fortune cookie is cracked open and one of these messages is revealed: Multiplier: You are awarded your bet amount times the multiplier. Free Cookies: You will crack open five cookies that each have a multiplier. Fun Fact: Your bet is forfeited. Multipliers Potential multipliers: 1.2x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 10x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 500x, and 1000x. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A new Free Cookies round cannot be triggered within an active Free Cookies round. Last modification date: 7/30/2024
