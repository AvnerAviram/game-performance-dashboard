Cash Cascade 100 Charming 7's Game Theme and Graphics Cash Cascade - 100 Charming 7's is a 5x4, 100 lines slot game with glossy fruits and scattered Stars and Coins. Main Game Wins pay from left to right – Scatters pay in any position. Star symbols are Scatter symbols. The Seven (Wild) symbol substitutes for all symbols on the same reel except Scatters and Coins. Cash Cascade Feature 8 Coin symbols in view will trigger the Cash Cascade Feature. Your goal is to collect as many coins as possible. Each coin will carry a prize value or bonus label (Mini, Minor or Major). At the end of the feature you will receive all collected prizes and bonuses. Two-level progressive Jackpot Both jackpots (Grand and Major) can be won in the Cash Cascade Feature of the game. The Major Jackpot is won when a coin is collected with the "Major" label. The Grand jackpot is won when all 20 coins have been collected and will be paid at the end of the feature on top of all other collected prizes and bonuses. Rules Seven (Wild) substitutes for all symbols except Stars (Scatters) and Coins. All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on selected lines excluding Scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per Scatter combination.
