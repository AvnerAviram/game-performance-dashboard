Secrets of the Queen Queen's Secret Powers The Queen holds a secret flame, which can burst into a colour to activate any of the following 3 secret powers during any spin, in the base or bonus. She may activate just one, or any combination of 2, or all 3, in any order. Expanding Reels The Queen expands the reels vertically, up to 5, 6, 7 or 8 high, which also increases the number of paylines to 75, 100, 125 or 150 respectively. Enhanced Reels The Queen removes most of the lower paying symbols, leaving the reels loaded with the higher paying ones. Colossal Symbols The Queen inserts 2x2, 3x3 & 4x4 colossal versions of the WILD and higher paying symbols into the reels, to spin on top of the normal reels. Queen's Secret Bonus 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8 BONUS symbols, landing on any reels in the base game, trigger the Queen's Secret Bonus, awarding 15, 20, 25, 30, 40 or 50 free spins respectively. Before free spins can start, a Key Multiplier game takes place with a chance of being awarded a multiplier, up to x20, for all wins during free spins. Any Key that lands will lock onto the grid. The multiplier grows by connecting Keys directly next to each other. The number of Keys in the largest connected cluster equals the multiplier. The Key Multiplier game starts with 3 lives, and blank reels. If no Key lands in a spin, a life is lost. After all lives are lost, or all positions on the grid are filled with Keys, the free spins can begin with any multiplier that was awarded. 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8 BONUS symbols, landing on any reels during free spins, award an additional 15, 20, 25, 30, 40 or 50 free spins respectively, with the same multiplier that was initially awarded. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 95.00% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $95.00 of wins. Rules Total bet is 25 x coin size. 50 paylines are played, but can expand up to 150 lines during the Expanding Reels feature. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
