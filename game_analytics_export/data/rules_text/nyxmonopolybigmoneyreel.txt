Monopoly Big Money Reel PAYTABLE WILD is wild for all symbols except NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200, NOTE 500. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. All line pays are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. MYSTERY BILLS FEATURE On any wagered spin, additional NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200 or NOTE 500 may be added to the reels. On any wagered spin where additional NOTES are added to the reels one of the following may occur: All NOTES change into one of NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200 or NOTE 500. OR ALL NOTES change into NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200 or NOTE 500. A full screen of NOTE 20 awards 5x Total Bet A full screen of NOTE 50 awards 10x Total Bet A full screen of NOTE 100 awards 15x Total Bet A full screen of NOTE 200 awards 20x Total Bet A full screen of NOTE 500 awards 50x Total Bet MONOPOLY BIG MONEY REEL SPIN BONUS Any combination of 5 or more scattered NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200, NOTE 500 awards a MONOPOLY Big Money Reel Spin. NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200 and NOTE 500 on the reels are collected for NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200 and NOTE 500 respectively on the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel. For each type of NOTE collected, the associated MONOPOLY Big Money Reel sections receive a multiplier equal to 1+ the number of that respective NOTE collected. Press SPIN REEL to spin the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel. If the reel lands on UTILITY BONUS, the Utilities Bonus is triggered. If the reel lands on GO FREE SPINS BONUS, the Go Free Spins Bonus is triggered. All cash values on the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel are already multiplied by the bet multiplier. Go Free Spins Bonus 8 spins are initially awarded during the Go Free Spins Bonus Before each spin pick a GO symbol to reveal NOTE 20, NOTE 30, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200, NOTE 500 or NOTE 1000 Only one of NOTE 200, NOTE 500 or NOTE 1000 is available for selection before each spin. All NOTES on the reels change into the revealed NOTE for that spin. For every NOTE on screen, a cash award equal to the value of the NOTE x bet multiplier is awarded 3 scattered GO EXTRA SPIN award extra spins 4 scattered GO EXTRA SPIN award 5x Total Bet + extra spins 5 scattered GO EXTRA SPIN award 50x Total Bet + extra spins If extra spins are awarded, the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel spins to determine the number of extra spins (3-50). During the extra spins, the number on all NOTES remains the same as the spin that awarded the extra spins. An alternate set of reels is used during the Go Free Spins Bonus. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except the Mystery Bills Feature and the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel Spin Button cannot be triggered. WILD is wild for all symbols except NOTE 20, NOTE 50, NOTE 100, NOTE 200, NOTE 500. WILD is not WILD for NOTE 30, NOTE 1000 or GO EXTRA SPIN. The Go Free Spins Bonus is guaranteed to award a minimum of 10x total bet. Any bonus ending with less than 10x total bet is automatically increased to 10x total bet at the end of the bonus. UTILITY BONUS A bankroll is awarded to begin the Utilities round. Pick a hand from Mr. MONOPOLY to reveal SPIN, COLLECT or COLLECT x2. If Mr.MONOPOLY reveals SPIN, the MONOPOLY Big Money Reel spins. The Utilities Bonus use an alternate MONOPOLY Big Money Reel. The reel can land on a section displaying either a credit award or a multiplier. If the reel lands on a section with a credit award (20-500), the credit award on that section is added to the bankroll. If the reel lands on a section with a multiplier (2x or 3x), the multiplier on that section is applied to the bankroll. The maximum number of multipliers awarded during the Utilities Bonus is 3. After the spin is complete, another pick is awarded. If Mr. MONOPOLY reveals COLLECT, the Utilities Bonus ends. If Mr. MONOPOLY reveals COLLECTx2, the bankroll is multiplied by 2 and the Utilities Bonus ends. The total number of picks during the Utilities Bonus is 2-10. All credit awards will be multiplied by the bet multiplier. The Utilities Bonus is guaranteed to award a minimum of 5x total bet. Any bonus ending with less than 5x total bet is automatically increased to 5x total bet at the end of the bonus. Rules Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wins are limited and cannot exceed €250,000.00 per wager. Bet multiplier is total bet divided by 40 credits. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.95%.
