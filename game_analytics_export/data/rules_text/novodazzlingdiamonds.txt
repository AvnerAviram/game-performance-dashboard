Dazzling Diamonds Main Game In order to win at Dazzling Diamonds the symbols must land consecutively on a line to form a pattern. Scatter symbols win in any position. Game Theme and Graphics Everything sparkles and glitters in our slot Dazzling Diamonds. The reels of this machine are filled with gems, jewels, diamonds, jewelry and large piles of money. With the right spin of the reels and a little luck the symbols can land in the right order and bring you huge winnings! Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations of adjacent matching symbols of a kind on selected lines, except Scatters. All prizes pay on selected lines, except Scatters. Scatter symbols pay at any position on the screen. Highest win only paid on each line and per Scatter combination. Scatter wins and line wins are added.
