Thunder Cash Sizzling Hot Game Theme and Graphics Enter the awesome fire scene and try to collect as many Disc symbols as you can find. Manage to collect Disc symbols on all positions and the Grand Jackpot is yours. While you are collecting, you may find other Jackpots as well. Main Game Thunder Cash™ - Sizzling Hot™ is played on 5 reels, each 3 rows in height, with 5 lines always in play. Your goal is to land 5 Seven symbols on a win line, which will award you an immediate win of 1000x the total bet. All winning combinations run from left to right on win lines, except Scatter symbols. Lock & Spin Feature Do you want to win one of the amazing Jackpots in Cash Connection™ - Sizzling Hot™? You have to collect 6 or more Disc symbols in the main game. If you manage to collect them, you will enter the Lock & Spin Feature with 3 free spins to find the next Disc symbols. The feature will lock down all your collected Disc symbols and will check for any new Disc symbols appearing during free spins. Each time you manage to find a new one, your free spins will reset back to 3 and you will be able to search for more. If you are the ONE, then you will find all 15 Disc symbols and win the biggest reward in this game - the GRAND JACKPOT. Rules Malfunction voids all plays and pays. All pays are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. All wins are on selected lines, except Scatters. Scatters pay out in any position. Only the highest win is paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by bet per line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. 6 or more collected Discs trigger the Lock & Spin Feature, which may result in winning all of the jackpots.
