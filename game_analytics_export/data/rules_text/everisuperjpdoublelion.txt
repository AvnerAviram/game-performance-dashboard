Super Jackpot Double Lion GAME RULES SPECIAL SYMBOLS Lion, Tiger and Panther symbols are wild and substitute for Seven and Bar symbols that also land on the payline. One Lion symbol in a winning Sevens or Bars combination pays 2x prize value. Two Lion symbols in a winning Sevens or Bars combination pays 4x prize value. Only highest winner paid per line. SUPER JACKPOT PROGRESSIVE When a combination of 3 of any Lion, Tiger, or Panther symbols land on an active payline, the associated Progressive tier is awarded. See Progressive paytable for prize values. Only highest winning Progressive awarded per play. Top Progressive is available only at max lines bet.
