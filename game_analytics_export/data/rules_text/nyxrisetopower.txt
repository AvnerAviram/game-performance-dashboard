Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 4 Human Soldier, Elf Wizard, or Troll Warrior symbols. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Yellow Ace, Blue King, Purple Queen, Green Jack, or Red Ten symbol. Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the game level increases. Double Troll Warrior appears only at or above game level 25. Double Elf Wizard appears only at or above game level 50. Double Human Soldier appears only at or above game level 75. Triple Troll Warrior appears only at or above game level 100. Triple Elf Wizard appears only at or above game level 150. Triple Human Soldier appears only at or above game level 250. Quadruple Troll Warrior appears only at or above game level 500. Quadruple Elf Wizard appears only at or above game level 750. Quadruple Human Soldier appears only at or above game level 1000. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the total amount bet increases. The total amount bet on this game is counted across all sessions played on the current user account. As the total amount bet increases, the following symbols will be unlocked and appear on the reels in order: Double Troll Warrior – Bet 3000 credits to unlock, Double Elf Wizard – Bet an additional 7000 credits to unlock, Double Human Soldier – Bet an additional 20000 credits to unlock, Triple Troll Warrior – Bet an additional 70000 credits to unlock, Triple Elf Wizard – Bet an additional 200000 credits to unlock, Triple Human Soldier – Bet an additional 700000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Troll Warrior – Bet an additional 2000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Elf Wizard – Bet an additional 7000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Human Soldier – Bet an additional 40000000 credits to unlock. When the final symbol is unlocked, the meter will remain visible and display 100% complete. FREE GAMES BONUS During the Base Game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any positions on reels 1, 2, and 3 starts the Free Games Bonus. 11 or 12 free games are awarded. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Bonus symbols appears only on reels 1, 2, and 3. Free Games Wild symbol is wild substitutes for all symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 4 Free Games Human Soldier, Free Games Elf Wizard, or Free Games Troll Warrior symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Free Games Yellow Ace, Free Games Blue King, Free Games Purple Queen, Free Games Green Jack, or Free Games Red Ten symbol symbol. Free Games Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Free Games Bonus symbol uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus symbol. The Free Games Bonus symbol does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus symbol ends when 0 free games remain. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels.
