Monopoly Hot Property GAME DESCRIPTION MONOPOLY HOT PROPERTY is a key number match-style game. Game play features a chance to win an instant cash prize and a chance to activate the BONUS GAME. HOW TO PLAY Select the plus or minus buttons to choose the PLAY AMOUNT from $0.50 to $30 (default play is $1). Select the WINNING NUMBERS and YOUR NUMBERS individually to reveal a number or select the REVEAL ALL button to reveal all numbers at once. When any of YOUR NUMBERS match any WINNING NUMBER, win prize shown under the matching number. HOW TO PLAY Reveal a FREE PARKING symbol to win the prize shown under that symbol automatically. Reveal a CHANCE or COMMUNITY CHEST symbol to win the prize shown on the card automatically. Reveal a GO symbol to activate the BOARD BONUS GAME. With the current configuration of PLAY AMOUNT, you can win a total prize amount ranging from $[MIN] to $[MAX]. BOARD BONUS GAME The BOARD BONUS GAME will activate when a GO symbol is revealed during main game play. Five Property, Station and Utility cards will be randomly selected and highlighted on the PRIZE CHART at the beginning of the BOARD BONUS GAME. The BOARD BONUS GAME will start with four dice rolls. Select the ROLL button to automatically move the Game Token around the MONOPOLY game board. As the Game Token stops on a Property, Station or Utility, a SOLD sign will appear and that Property, Railroad, or Utility will highlight in the PRIZE CHART. If the Game Token stops on CHANCE or COMMUNITY CHEST, the corresponding card will be drawn from the pile to reveal an instant cash prize or direction to move the Game Token to a specified area on the game board. BOARD BONUS GAME An extra dice roll will be awarded if doubles are rolled and when the Game Token passes GO. If the Game Token stops on a Property, Station, or Utility that is already SOLD or stops on the JUST VISITING or FREE PARKING squares, nothing will happen. When a Property set, all four Stations, or both Utilities are collected, multiply the original PLAY AMOUNT by the multiplier shown for the corresponding match and win that amount With the current configuration of PLAY AMOUNT, the BOARD BONUS has a minimum prize of [MIN] and maximum prize of MAX BOARD BONUS GAME – PRIZE CHART Prizes are awarded for collecting a full set of properties of the same colour (a Property Set), a set of four Stations or two Utilities. END OF GAME Any winnings will be displayed at the end of game play in the TOTAL WON box. To play again, select the PLAY button. To change the amount to play, select the plus or minus buttons. Once the PLAY button has been selected, the amount played will be deducted from the current balance and a new game will begin. GENERAL INFORMATION System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by player do not affect the outcome of the game. Last modification date: 6/14/2024
