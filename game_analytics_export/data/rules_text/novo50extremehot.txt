Main Game 50 Extreme Hot is played on 50 lines (fixed). The Jokers can be found in stacks on the reels. Jokers substitute for all symbols except stars (scatters). Free Game Feature 3, 4 or 5 scattered star symbols pay 10, 20 or 30 Free Games. During Free Games there is an increased amount of Jokers on the reels. Top Prize : 26.920 times bet per line per Free Game. Feature hit rate : ~300 Jackpot Diamonds Feature The Jackpot Diamonds Feature uses the four-level Diamond Mystery Jackpot. Each mystery level has specific gems. The base win of each jackpot depends on your stake. The base win is 5.000 times your stake for the Diamond Jackpot, 1.250 times your stake for the Red Gem Jackpot, 200 times your stake for the Blue Gem Jackpot, and 50 times your stake for the Green Gem Jackpot. Green Gem - 1st level (lowest Jackpot value) Blue Gem - 2nd level Red Gem - 3rd level Diamond - 4th and highest level Once triggered, the player is taken to the Jackpot Cards game where he/she can pick one of 12 face-down cards. The player picks cards until 3 matching gem symbols have been revealed. The Jackpot Diamonds Mystery level corresponding to the revealed gem will be awarded. Gamble Wins can be gambled in the red/black card gamble. Rules Jokers substitute for all symbols except stars (scatters). All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per scatter combination.
