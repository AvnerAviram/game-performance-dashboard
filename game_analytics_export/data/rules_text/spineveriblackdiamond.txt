Black Diamond Basic Game Rules WIN PROGRESSIVE JACKPOTS BY PLAYING MAX BET Progressive prizes are available at max bet only. Jackpot triggering symbols must land on active paylines to award jackpots. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 9 awards the MAJOR Progressive jackpot. 3 Black Diamond symbols on paylines 1 to 8 awards the MINOR Progressive jackpot. At less than max bet, jackpots pay flat credit prizes which are multiplied by bet per line. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 9 awards 5,000 credits. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 1 to 8 awards 300 credits. BLACK DIAMOND SYMBOL IS WILD! The Black Diamond Symbol is wild and substitutes for any symbol except multipliers to make winning combinations. BONUS MULTIPLIERS Multipliers multiply winning combinations except progressives. Two or more multipliers on a payline multiply together and multiply the winning combination. The maximum total multiplier for a winning combination is 30X. 2X multipliers are available on 777, 77, 7 Double Bar, and Single Bar symbols. 3X multipliers are available on 7 and Single Bar symbols. 5X Multipliers are available on 777, 77, and Double Bar symbols. PRESS “SPIN” OR “MAX” TO START “SPIN” will also rebet and start game. Maximum number of credits bet is 27. QUICK STOP BEHAVIOR Pressing the “SPIN” button while the reels are spinning may cause the reels to stop immediately. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins.
