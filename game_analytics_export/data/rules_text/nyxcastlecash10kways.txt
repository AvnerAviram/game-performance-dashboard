Castle Cash 10K Ways Castle Cash 10K Ways™ is a 10K Ways™ slot game with up to 10,000 ways to win. The game has 6 reels. It has 4 rows on reels 1 and 6; and 5 rows (inclusive of top reel) on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. The Average Theoretical Return to Player is: 94.47% The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of spins by numerous players over an extended period of time and cannot be affected by the player's strategy. Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed 0. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration. RULES WILD KNIGHT is wild and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. KNIGHT appears on top reel only during base game. SCATTER Bonus Respins are triggered when 6 or more SCATTER appear. BONUS RESPINS Bonus Respins are triggered when 6 or more SCATTER appear. 3 respins are awarded. All SCATTER symbols are held, and all remaining positions spin. DRAGON, MINOTAUR, TROLL and GOBLIN are MONSTER symbols. MONSTER, KNIGHT and SCATTER symbols are PRIZE symbols. Each spinning position will land a PRIZE or BLANK symbol. Only a single KNIGHT can appear on any respin. KNIGHT only appears if there are MONSTER symbols on the screen. MONSTER and SCATTER symbols display a non-bonus prize or bonus prize value. MONSTER symbols have a life value. DRAGON starts with a life value of 4. MINOTAUR starts with a life value of 3. TROLL starts with a life value of 2. GOBLIN starts with a life value of 1. When a KNIGHT appears, its value is determined by adding the bonus/non-bonus prize value of all MONSTER symbols on the display that have a life value greater than 0. When a KNIGHT appears, all visible MONSTER symbols' life values are reduced by 1 (down to a minimum of 0). A red layer is shown over the MONSTER symbol with height (relative to symbol height) representing the percentage of life lost by that MONSTER. When any new PRIZE symbols appear, they are held, and respins are reset to 3. In each respin after the first, all PRIZE symbols are held, and all remaining positions spin. Respins are completed when no more respins remain, or when all visible positions on the reels are filled with PRIZE symbols. Upon completion of respins, each PRIZE symbol awards a non-bonus prize or bonus prize. Mini and Minor bonus prizes may be awarded more than once per feature. Grand and Major bonus prizes may only be awarded once per feature. Grand bonus prize is 2000 x total bet. Major bonus prize is 250 x total bet. Minor bonus prize is 50 x total bet. Mini bonus prize is 10 x total bet. Non-bonus prizes on SCATTER as multiples of total bet are: 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Non-bonus prizes on DRAGON as multiples of total bet are: 2.5x, 2.75x, 3.25x, 3.5x, 3.75x, 4x, 4.25x, 4.5x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Non-bonus prizes on MINOTAUR as multiples of total bet are: 2x, 2.25x, 2.5x, 2.75x, 3x, 3.25x, 3.5x, 3.75x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Non-bonus prizes on TROLL as multiples of total bet are: 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x, 2.25x, 2.5x, 2.75x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Non-bonus prizes on GOBLIN as multiples of total bet are: 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Chance of KNIGHT appearing during Bonus Respins reduces when total winnings reach 4000, 5000, 6000 and 8000 times total bet. Bonus prizes are not progressive. Bonus Respins are played at the bet of the triggering spin. Symbol pays shown in the paytable are not awarded during Bonus Respins. GAME RULES Player selects a Total Bet size to play 10,000 ways. Payouts are made according to the paytable. All wins are multiplied by total bet. Non-bonus prizes displayed over SCATTER symbols and symbols during the Respins Feature have already been multiplied by total bet amount. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels. KNIGHT is wild and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. KNIGHT appears on the top reel only during base game. The top reel adds a symbol to the reel it is above for reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Every winning symbol (except SCATTER) explodes and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the reels and from the right in the top reel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. How to play Pick your bet amount and push spin button. Spin Button Places a bet then spins the reels. Menu Button Opens a window where the player can view info pages; go to lobby; change bets; configure autoplay (if available) and adjust game settings. It defaults to info pages. Balance Meter Display the player's current balance. Total Bet Meter Display the total wager staked. Win Meter Display the current win amount. Info Button Display info pages. Return to Game Button Go back to base game. Home Button Go to Lobby. Configurable per operator. Autoplay Button (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) Opens Autoplay configuration window, where the player can select the desired number of autoplay and start autoplay. Stop Autoplay Button (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) Stops autoplay. Number of autoplays remaining will be shown over the button. Bet Settings Button Opens Bet configuration window, where player can select the desired bet amount. Game Settings Button Open Game settings configuration window, where the player can adjust the following: Turn sounds On or Off. Turn background music On or Off (keep sound effects On). Sound Buttons Turn sounds On or Off respectively. Bonus Meters Show Bonus amounts. Keyboard Shortcuts Space bar: Pressing the Space bar will initiate Spin. Autoplay (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays for a certain amount of spins. This is game rules version 2, dated May 20, 2025.
