Poison Eve Game Rules Poison Eve is a video slot with 3 features; Expanding Wild, Liquid Magic and Flower Power. A 5-reel, 3-row video slot with 12 symbols. 20 win lines (see pay table for more info). A Wild symbol substitutes for any other symbol except Bonus. A Wild symbol substitutes for the highest possible winning combination on a win line according to the pay table. A winning combination pays from left to right. Coinciding wins on multiple win lines are paid out. Winning combinations and pays are made according to the pay table. The highest win per activated win line is paid. Diferent reel confgurations are used, depending on the game mode (main game or any of the bonus mode). Hot zone: Appears on the middle position on last reel both in main game and Flower Power feature. Flower Power: Trigger up to 3 portals on frst 4 reels. The theoretical return to the player for this game is 96.08%. A malfunction voids all winnings. Malfunction in gaming hardware/software; all afected bets are refunded. All game states will be saved for 6 months. Autoplay automatically plays the game for selected number of rounds or when any of the advanced autoplay setting criterias are fulflled. When changing autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take efect upon completion of the game round or feature. Some autoplay features may be mandatory for some jurisdictions. When playing with Cryptocurrency, unfnished game rounds and states will automatically reset after 24 hours. Any accumulated wins during this round will be paid out.
