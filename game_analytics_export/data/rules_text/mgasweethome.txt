Sweet Home STANDARD GAME RULES Bingo-based draw in which 30 balls are drawn from a drum containing 60 balls. The player has up to four cards with 15 numbers on each one. If a win figure is matched on a card, the prize associated will be awarded from the paytable. EXTRA BALL RULES Sometimes the player is offered the option of buying extra balls at additional cost. JACKPOT The top payout will be awarded if the card is complete within the first 30 balls drawn. PRIZE TABLE Prizes specified in credits. When bonus labelled figure in prizeplan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS 1 The game is about pressing "PLAY" when you want to stop the selection. You will get the highlighted prize. BONUS 2 Consists of selecting apples. Each one hides a prize or EXIT word. BONUS 3 In each round you will obtain a random number of steps. The final prize is the reached value when STOP appears or when you reach the last prize on the path. Bet Range Min Bet : €0.08 Max Bet : €30 The overall theoretical return to player is 91.11%.
