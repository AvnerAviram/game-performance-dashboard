Venecia de la Suerte PRIZE PLAN MAIN GAME 243 prize combinations. The Wild symbol Works as a wild card, replaces all symbols except the Scatter symbols, and doubles their value. In addition to being combined with the rest of the symbols, it will give its own prize indicated in the prize plan. The maximum prize is 6000 * the bet. WILD NIGHT At random up to a maximum of 4 reels can turn into Wild symobols. You cannot get Free Spins when playing Wild Night. 243 PRIZES COMBINATIONS A prize is given to the longest combination of symbols from left to right. The prize is multiplied by the number of times the symbol is repeated on each reel. FREESPINS WITH X2 AND X3 Each time a player gets a winning line, a wild symbol ‘x2’, ‘X3’ or both will appear in a random position. The maximum posible prize is 6000 * bet. If the total prize accumulates in a Free Spins round reaches this figura, the round will end inmediately and the prize of 6000 * bet is awarded. The rest of the Free Spins are discarded.
