Paradise Ticket Paradise Ticket is a 40 line slot with Sticky Wilds free spin feature. Paradise Ticket Free Spins 3,4 or 5 Wild Scatters trigger 7, 14 or 21 free spins. During the feature Wild Scatters become Sticky Wilds. When Sticky Wild lands on the screen 1 additional free spin is awarded. Wild substitutes for all symbols. How to Play Choose your bet, press the SPIN button and check for any win in the WIN Info Display. Payout Rules All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, unless explicitly stated otherwise. All wins count on active pay lines only. Payline wins for selected bet level are displayed in the paytable. All displayed prizes are prize totals with all applicable multipliers already taken into account.
