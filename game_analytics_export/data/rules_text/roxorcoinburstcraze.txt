Coin Burst Craze Fixed Jackpot Respins Feature Land two Bonus Coin symbols in the highlighted positions on reels 1 and 5 to trigger the Fixed Jackpot Respins Feature. During this Feature, the main reelset transitions to a 3 columns by 4 rows size, leaving a total of 12 spaces. Only Bonus Coin symbols and Blanks can land during this Feature. The player starts the Feature with three lives. When a Bonus Coin lands on the grid it locks in place. Every time no new Bonus Coins land on the grid, the player loses a life. The Fixed Jackpot Respins Feature finishes when zero lives are left or a full screen of Coin symbols is reached. Once that happens, the player is awarded a cash prize proportional to the amount of Bonus Coin symbols landed, according to the following table: 12 Bonus Coins: 1,000 x Total Bet 11 Bonus Coins: 250 x Total Bet 10 Bonus Coins: 100 x Total Bet 9 Bonus Coins: 75 x Total Bet 8 Bonus Coins: 50 x Total Bet 7 Bonus Coins: 25 x Total Bet 6 Bonus Coins: 20 x Total Bet 5 Bonus Coins: 15 x Total Bet 4 Bonus Coins: 10 x Total Bet 3 Bonus Coins: 5 x Total Bet 2 Bonus Coins: 3 x Total Bet 1 Bonus Coin: 1 x Total Bet Bonus Free Spins Feature Land three or more Bonus Coin symbols anywhere on the reels during the Base Game to trigger the Bonus Free Spins Feature. Land extra Bonus Coin symbols during Free Spins to award Free Spins re-triggers as shown below: 5: 10 extra Free Spins. 4: 5 extra Free Spins. 3: 2 extra Free Spins. The Fixed Jackpot Respins Feature can be triggered during Bonus Free Spins Feature. Increasing Multiplier mechanic Starting at x1, every time two of the same symbols are matched on the highlighted positions on reels 1 and 5, the total multiplier is increased by 1. Wild symbol is excluded. The total multiplier applies to both Winning Ways and Fixed Jackpot Respins amounts. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 95.00% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $95.00 of wins. Game Rules Total bet is 20 x Coin Size. 576 All Ways Pays are played. All wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest paying combination for each symbol is paid per spin. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
