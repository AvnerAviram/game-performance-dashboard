Silver Lion Game Rules Play bet 40 for 1024 ways. Win 8, 15 or 20 Free Games with a 3,4 or 5 DIAMOND symbol win respectively. During Free Games each WILD TREE that appears on reels 2,3 or 4 will MULTIPLY any prize it is substituting in by either x2 or x3 up to total maximum of x27. 5 Additional Free Games are awarded if Any 2 scattered DIAMOND Symbols occur during any Free Game. Free Games can be retriggered. Free Games are played at the triggering bet. Different reel strips are used in Free Games. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays 1024 Ways For all symbols except scatters, winning combinations pay through any position on reels 1,2,3,4 & 5 respectively. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. All wins shown at 1 credit bet. All wins multiplied by multiplier bet. How to Play Choose your BET SPIN MAIN GAME SCREEN BUTTONS: Button Function SPIN : Spins the reels AUTOPLAY : When more than zero AUTOPLAYs is selected the SPIN button will display the number of AUTOPLAYs that will be played. STOP AUTOPLAY: Once auto play begins the SPIN button turns into a STOP AUTOPLAY button and the number of AUTOPLAYs remaining will be displayed in the STOP button. HOME : Accesses casino menu SETTINGS : Accesses the SETTING page for BET and AUTOPLAY options SETTINGS PAGE BUTTONS: Button Function Ways: This game is fixed for 1024 ways BET : Adjusts the BET. The slider adjusts the BET up or down. AUTOPLAY : Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays. The slider increases or decreases the number of autoplays. The available of autoplays are 0, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45 and 50. INFO : Accesses pay awards for winning symbol combinations. Also contains the rules for the game. MUTE : Mutes all in-game sound effects. The game displays all your details in the following display fields: Balance : Displays your total balance Total Bet : The total wager staked.
