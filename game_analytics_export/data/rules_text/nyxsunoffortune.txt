Sun of Fortune The game has 16 independent reels on a 4x4 grid. Wins are paid for at least 8 identical symbols on any of the reels. When two symbol combinations of the same value are drawn, the win will be paid only for the combination with the symbol that has the higher position on the paytable. Only the highest win is paid. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbol. Wins for Bonus symbols are paid out only during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Drawing 3, 4 or 5 Bonus symbols in the base game triggers a paid re-spin with the Bonus symbols blocked on the reels, increasing the chances of activating the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. SPECIAL FEATURES Hold the Jackpot Bonus A draw of at least 6 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Bonus symbols that activated the Hold the Jackpot Bonus and those drawn during the bonus stay on the reels. 3 respins are granted at the beginning of the bonus round. Every time a Bonus symbol is drawn, it resets the number of respins to 3. The bonus ends when all respins are finished or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. When Hold the Jackpot Bonus ends, the prize is paid according to the values of the Bonus symbols. Prizes for the Bonus symbols equal bet value multiplied by x1-x10, x12 or x15. Prizes for the MINI, MINOR and MAJOR symbols are paid according to the paytable. The GRAND prize is paid when all 16 reels are filled with Bonus symbols. The bonus is played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated it. SPECIAL SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOL Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbol. BONUS SYMBOL A draw of at least 6 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols.
