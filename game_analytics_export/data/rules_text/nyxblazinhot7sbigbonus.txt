Blazin' Hot 7s Big Bonus How To Play This game is a 5-reel, 40-line game with BLAZIN’ HOT 7s BONUS, which is triggered when 3 or more BONUS symbols appear on any of the reels during the base game. WILD SYMBOL The BLAZIN’ 7 symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except for the BONUS symbol. The WILD symbol can land on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. During the FREE SPINS bonus, WILD symbols are sticky and will remain for the duration of the bonus. Any WILD symbols visible when the FREE SPINS bonus is triggered are not sticky. FREE SPINS BONUS The FREE SPINS bonus is triggered when 3 or more BONUS symbols are visible anywhere on the reels. Triggering the bonus with 3 BONUS symbols awards 8 Free Spins, triggering with 4 awards 10 Free Spins, and triggering with 5 awards 12 Free Spins. On each Free Spin, you are guaranteed to win a minimum of your bet value. If a reel win is obtained which exceeds the bet value, the minimum win value will be set to the highest reel win value achieved so far. It is not possible to retrigger the bonus whilst inside the bonus. LEGAL NOTICE Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed 250,000. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 10/6/2022
