Fu Lai Cai Lai Wild Destiny FU LAI CAI LAI WILD DESTINY GAME DESCRIPTION FU LAI CAI LAI WILD DESTINY™, enriching variety of gameplay and features delivering both instant and surprising wins. Get access to a special paytable with improved pays for a greater number of symbols when choosing a higher bet per play. Increasing the bet level played further amps up the excitement in the features, such as increasing the number of extra wild on the reels during free games and the number of jackpot levels that are available. Choosing to play the highest bet level triggers the free game feature in that very game. Malfunction void all pays and plays. This game has 5 reels, 3 rows and 243 ways to win every way.
