Beat The Bobbies Join Penny on her quest to steal the crown jewels and ‘Beat The Bobbies’ in the process! It’s not going to be easy, she will have to escape not only London’s finest but also the butler, the cleaning lady, the postie and even a British Bulldog. A successful heist is not guaranteed, but one thing for sure is that it will give you an action packed ride and truly exhilarating gameplay. It’s a high-speed 243-way slot where players can not only win big but can also choose the volatility of their free spins (a feature pioneered on Stampede which has become a true success story). So get yourself ready, it’s a robbery and the chase is on! How to play Are you ready to assist Penny on her quest to get rich, and beat the bobbies in the process? Let’s get started! Choose your initial bet amount and then hit ‘Spin’ to get the reels rolling! At first it may seem like an ordinary slot game, but ‘Beat The Bobbies’ has a rewarding 243 ways to win every time you spin! Though the Bobbies are trying to stop Penny from stealing the jewels, they can help you win big as they’re the game’s Wild Substitute – which can replace all reel symbols except the Penny Scatter to help you complete a winning combination. The Bobbies Wild can only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4, but when it does, you’re in luck! Free Games Beat The Bobbies’ is packed with FREE Spins! This bonus feature will begin when 3 or more Penny Scatters appear on the reels. You can enjoy 20 free spins with a 2x multiplier, 15 with a 3x multiplier, 10 with a 4x multiplier, 8 with a 5x multiplier or 5 free spins with a 10x multiplier.
