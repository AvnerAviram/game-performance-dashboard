Bet Deﬁnitions Current Bet: The current bet is the value that appears above . Minimum Bet: The minimum bet is the lowest possible bet value, which is selected by pressing until the bet value no longer decreases. Bet Multiplier: The bet multiplier is defined as the current bet divided by the minimum bet. Autoplay Options Autoplay will allow game rounds to be played automatically. Click on the Autoplay button and select the number of Autoplay game rounds. Additional Information. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wins During each bet, players shoot two Balls simultaneously. A winning bet occurs when at least one basket is made by a balls going through a hoop. 3 to 18 credit prizes are awarded on a winning bet. The Scoring Streak feature is triggered when both balls go through a hoop on a single bet. Scoring Streak Feature During the Scoring Streak feature, every successful basket adds x2 to the multiplier. A missed basket ends the feature at the current displayed multiplier. The multipliers available in the feature are: 2x - x2 4x - x4 6x - x6 8x - x8 10x - x10 12x - x12 14x - x14 16x - x16 18x - x18 20x - x20 During the Scoring Streak feature, up to 10 credit prizes can be awarded.
