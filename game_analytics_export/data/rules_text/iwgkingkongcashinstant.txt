King Kong Cash Instant Introduction 5 games to play with 13 chances to win! Start the game Press the PLAY button to start the game. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game, any winnings are displayed. To play again press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. How to play Game 1 Press each of the 6 crown icons to reveal a prize amount. Reveal 3 matching prize amounts to win one of those amounts. Game 2 Press each of the barrel icons to reveal a symbol and prize amount. Find the "King Kong Cash" symbol to win the prize for that row. Game 3 Reveal 2 numbers and a prize amount in a row. In each row the left hand number is YOUR number and the right hand number is THEIR number. In a row if YOURS is larger than THEIRS, win the prize for that row. Game 4 Reveal numbers and prize amount in a row. If the 2 numbers in a row add up to 10, win the prize for that row. Game 5 Press the King Kong Cash head icons to reveal a symbol. Reveal 2 like symbols in a row to win the prize for that row. General information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by player do not affect the outcome of the game. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 86.50%.
