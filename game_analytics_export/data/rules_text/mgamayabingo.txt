Maya Bingo STANDARD GAME RULES Bingo-based draw in which 44 balls are drawn from a drum containing 75 balls the player has up to four cards with 24 numbers on each one. If a win figure is matched on a card, the prize associated will be awarded from the paytable. EXTRA BALL RULES Sometimes the player is offered the option of buying extra balls at additional cost. JACKPOT The top payout will be awarded if the card is complete within the first 44 balls drawn. PRIZE TABLE Prizes specified in credits. When bonus labeled figure in prizeplan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS 1 Consists of a race between 4 characters of different colors. In each round a character chosen randomly moves a random number of positions. The resulting award is the value contained in the display reached. BONUS 2 The goal is to discover prizes. Each selection gets you a prize. The game ends when you discover EXIT or click on every element. When bonus labeled figure in prizeplan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS 3 The goal is to get the biggest prize in the pyramid. On each floor of the pyramid there are prizes and arrows, the arrow gives access up to play at the top level, and the prizes increase as you scale up the pyramid. The game ends when a prize and not an arrow is selected. RTP is 89.77% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 7/14/2020
