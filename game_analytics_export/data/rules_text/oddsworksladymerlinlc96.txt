Lady Merlin Lightning Chase Game Rules Play 432 ways for $1.25. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Scatter wins are added to way wins. Coinciding wins are added. Each symbol in the win can only be used once per winning combination. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. WILD appears on reels 2 and 4 only. WILD substitutes for all symbols except COLLECT. Each WILD in a win multiplies the prize by the Wild Multiplier. Wild Multiplier is 2 in the base game. COLLECT appears on reel 3 only. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. BOOM+ Bet 25 credits in total to unlock Wild Multiplier and Contagious Wild features. If a Wild appears it triggers the Expanding Wild, Wild multiplier or Contagious Wild feature as shown on the Wild Feature indicator. WILD+ Bet 25 credits to unlock extra wilds on reels 2, 3 and 4. Bet 40 credits for both BOOM+ and WILD+ features to be activated. Expanding Wild During an Expanding Wild feature spin, when a wild appears it covers all positions of that reel. Wild Multiplier During a Wild Multiplier Feature Spin, one or more Wild in a win multiplies the win by 2 to 10 times as shown on the Wild Feature indicator. Contagious Wild During a Contagious Wild Feature Spin, any DIAMOND, CLUB, HEART, or SPADE that touches the Wild that is spun in also becomes Wild. FREE SPIN CHOICE FEATURE 3 Scatters trigger Free Spin Choice Feature. Choose between the following features: 13 expanding wild free spins 13 wild multiplier free spins (only available when BOOM+ is activated) 10 contagious wild free spins (only available when BOOM+ is activated) Free Spins can be retriggered, awarding the same Free Spins Choice as was chosen upon initial trigger. Free Spins are played at the same lines and bet as the spin that triggered the Free Spins. HOLD & RESPIN FEATURE 3 Free Respins are triggered when 6 or more COIN appear. All COIN are held and other positions spun. Respins reset to 3 if another COIN or COLLECT appears. COIN prizes can be 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 10x, 20x, 25x or 888x the total bet(excluding side strike bet). During HOLD & RESPIN, COLLECT appears on one reel only. All prizes are immediately awarded and not held if COLLECT appears during HOLD & RESPIN. COLLECT FEATURE COLLECT appears on reel 3 only during base game. When a COLLECT appears, all COIN prizes are immediately awarded, and are not held for any Hold & Respin features triggered on that spin. SIDE STRIKE Only applicable in some jurisdictions. Red chip only appears on reel 1. Green chip only appears on reel 5. If your selection appears 2 more times than other selection 5x total strike bet is awarded. If your selection appears 1 more times than other selection 2x total strike bet is awarded. If your and other selection appear 2 times, 1x total strike bet is awarded.
