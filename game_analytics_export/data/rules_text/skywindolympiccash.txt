Olympic Cash HOW TO PLAY PLAY MODE AND BALANCE You can play the game in either FUN or REAL mode. In fun mode, you are playing just for the fun of the score. In real mode, you are playing with money from your real money balance. Your balance is shown in the BALANCE box both in fun and real mode. PLACING BETS The BET is the COIN value multiplied by 30. To set the bet amount, use the '-' and '+' buttons. CONTINUOUS SPIN Clicking and holding the SPIN button for continuous spinning. To stop the continuous spin session, release the SPIN button. When CONTINUOUS SPIN is on, some win animations and sound are skipped, the reels spin faster and the game results are displayed faster. AUTOPLAY MODE To select the number of autospins, use the AUTOPLAY options panel. Clicking the OK button initiates the autoplay session. Clicking the CANCEL button closes the panel. You can end the autospins session earlier by clicking the AUTOPLAY button. The number of remaining spins is displayed on the AUTOPLAY button. TURBO MODE When TURBO Mode is on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster. Clicking the TURBO button switches Turbo Mode on or off. PAYTABLE PAYOUTS Matching symbols on adjacent reels from left to right form winning combinations regardless of the symbol position on the reel. Regular wins are multiplied by the COIN value. SCATTER wins are multiplied by the BET value. SCATTER wins are added to ways wins. When 2 or more Wildx2 symbols appear on a single way, only one x2 win multiplier is applied to the way win. All Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Scatter. When 3 or more Wilds plus 1 or more identical paying symbols land on the same way, the wins from both the Wild-only win combination and the win combination with Wild substituting are paid. This rule applies to all Wild symbols in the game. The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information see the TERMS AND CONDITIONS section. FREE GAMES During FREE GAMES the reels are spun automatically using the same COIN value as per the spin that won the FREE GAMES. DISCONNECTION POLICY NOTE ON DISCONNECTION If you are disconnected from the Internet in the middle of the game, log in to the Casino again. Once you have reopened the game, you will be able to continue playing from the point where the game was disconnected. NOTE ON MALFUNCTIONS Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
