Lucia Martino Lucky Wave PRIZES MAIN GAME 25 pay lines Line winnings are paid from left to right. The Wild figure acts as a wild and substitutes for all figures except the Bonus figure. Getting 3 Wild figures gives 15 Free Spins. The maximum prize is 523.20 * bet. FREE SPINS Obtaining 3 Wild figures gives 15 Free Spins. These spins do not consume balance and all the prizes obtained are accumulated. More Free Spins can be obtained within Free Spins. All prizes are multiplied x3. DESTINATION MINIGAME Three or more 'Bonus' figures in any position give access to the 'Destiny' minigame. The game consists of a draw between various prizes. The player must stop the draw at the time he wishes. The final prize will be the corresponding prize that is lit. If the prize obtained has an associated 'surfboard' symbol, it will give access to the next minigame. The maximum prize is 36 *bet. SURF BOARDS MINIGAME If in the first minigame a prize is obtained with the 'surfboard' symbol, access to this minigame will be given. The game consists of a draw of 3 amounts to be hidden. The player must choose a possibility and will get the prize shown. If the prize obtained has a 'surfboard' symbol associated with it, it will give access to the next minigame. Maximum prize is 36 * bet. SURFING MINIGAME If in the second minigame a prize is obtained with the 'surfboard' symbol, access to this minigame will be given. Four surfers compete to get to the wave first. Each time you press play, the surfers will move the number of positions indicated on their marker. The final prize will be the one corresponding to the first surfer to cross the finish line. Maximum prize is 36 * bet. RTP:- 80.13% Last modification date: 1/24/2022
