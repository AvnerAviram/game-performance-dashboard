Wild The Wild symbol will always land fully stacked and can land on all 5 reels. The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Purple, Gold and Special Coins symbol, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2. Gold Coin symbol Landing 5 or more Gold Coin symbols anywhere in view will award the Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins. 3.3. Purple Coin symbol Any Purple Coin symbol that forms the trigger will add a multiplier to the Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins . Starting Multiplier is 1 (to which +1 is added for each Purple Coins triggering the feature). Purple Coins are not in play during the Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins . 3.4. Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins Triggered by 5 or more Gold Coin symbols anywhere in view, Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins will award 3 respins with all cash values remaining in place. Can also be triggered with 5 Purple Coins or a combination of Gold & Purple Coins. During Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins only Coin symbols are in play and every time a new one lands, the respins will reset to 3. Minor, Major and Grand special Coins can land as part of the trigger or in Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins. Minor - 20x total bet. Major - 200x total bet. Grand - 1000x total bet. If the respins run down to zero, the feature will end and all cash values in view will be collected. Values displayed on the Gold Coin symbols are shown in the selected currency/Coin. Gold Coin symbol values are random values from 1x to 1000x times bet. Possible Values during Respins are as follows in times bet values: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 8x, 10x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 50x, 100x, 200x, 250x, 1000x. 3.5. Coin Modifiers Randomly on any spin, Sparkles can fall over the reels and announce a special win. During the game Sparkles can fall over the reels and award either a Big Win (20 x Total Bet or more) or Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins . It can also transform Gold Coins into Purple multiplier Coins as part of the feature trigger. 3.6. Brilliant Bucks Bonus Buy The Bonus Buy costs 50 x Total Bet ( 1,250 coins). The Brilliant Bucks Bonus Buy spin is guaranteed to trigger the Brilliant Bucks Hold & Win Respins . Note: History application keys.
