Bet Configuration Menu Press the cog or TOTAL BET to open the Bet Configuration menu. Coins Displays the current number of coins being played. Coin Value Displays the current coin value shown in currency. Total bet Displays the current total bet shown in currency. Change by selecting the desired total bet. On desktop devices, optionally press the minus button (–) to decrease the total bet, or press the plus button (+) to increase the total bet. Press OK to confirm and close the Bet Configuration menu. Spin Press the circular arrow to submit the bet and spin the reels. Auto Spin Feature Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of spins at the current coin value. For jurisdictions where Auto Spin is enabled: Select the number of auto spins and where available, the loss limit, and the (optional) single win limit. After selecting the desired bet configuration, press AUTO SPIN , and choose how many spins to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until STOP is pressed, a bonus is triggered, the balance is insufficient to spin, or any enabled win/loss limit settings are reached. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered when 3 Bonus Scatter Symbols appear anywhere on the centre 3 reels. These symbols are only found on reels 2, 3, and 4. Triggering the Free Spins Bonus awards 15 free spins. The reels in the Free Spins Bonus are richer than the base game reels. The Free Spins Bonus is retriggered when 3 Bonus Scatter Symbols appear anywhere on the centre 3 reels. These symbols are only found on reels 2, 3, and 4. The bonus can be retriggered multiple times, up to a maximum of 225 free spins per bonus. The Diamond Symbol is wild in the Free Spins Bonus and is only found on reel 5. Every time a Diamond Symbol appears, it is saved in a special on-screen accumulator. Collect 3 Diamonds and all Female Thief Symbols become wild on reels 2 through 5. The Female Thief Symbols become wild on the spin when the third Diamond is collected and for all subsequent spins in the bonus. Furthermore, every additional set of 3 Diamonds upgrades an additional symbol: 3 Diamonds: Female Thief Symbol is wild on reels 2 through 5 6 Diamonds: Male Thief Symbol is also wild on reels 2 through 5 9 Diamonds: Security Symbol is also wild on reels 2 through 5 12 Diamonds: Banker Symbol is also wild on reels 2 through 5 After 12 Diamond Symbols have been collected, no more Diamonds can be collected, but the Diamond Symbol remains wild. Once the Free Spins Bonus ends, all Diamond accumulators are emptied. Free spins are played at same coin value as the triggering spin. Options Sound On Toggle game sounds on or off. Rules Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. All line win symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels, beginning with the far-left reel. Only the highest winner is paid on each payline. Line wins are multiplied by the coin value on the winning line. Coinciding line wins on different paylines are added. Only the highest scatter win is paid. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter wins are independent from line wins and are added to the total amount paid. Bonus wins are independent from line wins and are added to the total amount paid. Wins are shown in currency. Wild Symbol is wild and appears on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 only. In the Free Spins Bonus , symbols that have become wild are only wild on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. When enough Diamonds have been collected to make a given symbol wild, that symbol becomes wild on that spin immediately and for all subsequent spins in the bonus. Diamond Symbol is wild and appears on reel 5 only in the Free Spins Bonus . All Wild Symbols substitute for all symbols except the Bonus Symbol. In some jurisdictions, regardless of bet size, there may be an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the bonus, plus the outcome which launched the bonus.
