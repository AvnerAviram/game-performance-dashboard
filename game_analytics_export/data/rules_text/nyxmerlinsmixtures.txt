Merin's Mixtures The average theoretical return to player (RTP) is: Regular Game : 94.0% Buy Bonus : 94.17% The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of spins by numerous players over an extended period of time and cannot be affected by the player's strategy. RULES BONUS GREEN, BLUE and RED are BONUS symbols. Each BONUS symbol that lands is collected from the symbol, to the top of the reels. BONUS symbols have a random chance to trigger RESPINS. The more BONUS symbols collected in a spin the higher the chance of triggering the RESPINS. WILD BONUS symbols that have been collected become a WILD symbol. WILD substitutes for all symbols except BONUS symbols. All wins including WILD are 2x, this multiplier does not stack if more than 1 WILD symbol is present.WILD will substitute for the symbol that creates the highest possible win. PRIZE PRIZE symbols appear during RESPINS only. PRIZE values can be 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 6.25x, 12.5x, 62.5x, 125x total bet. MULTIPLIER MULTIPLIER symbol appears during RESPINS triggered by RED. MULTIPLIER symbols appear on a separate reel and multiply prize values in their row. MULTIPLIER values can be 1x, 2x, 3x, or 5x total bet. RESPINS RESPINS can be randomly triggered from any spin where a BONUS symbol lands. 3 respins are awarded. Only PRIZE symbols appear on the reels during RESPINS. When a PRIZE symbol lands it is held. Respins remaining are reset to 3 when any PRIZE symbol lands. If all positions are filled, PRIZE values are awarded, cleared from the reels and respins remaining is reset to 3. The RESPINS end when no respins are remaining. Upon completion of RESPINS each PRIZE symbol awards the value shown. No prizes in the RESPINS are progressive. RESPINS are played at the bet of the triggering spin. Symbol pays shown in the paytable are not awarded during RESPINS. The RESPINS may be triggered by 1, 2 or 3 BONUS types. The RESPINS are improved by all BONUS types that were involved in the trigger. GREEN If a GREEN was in the trigger, 4 respins are awarded instead of 3. Respins are reset to 4 when any PRIZE symbol lands. BLUE If a BLUE was in the trigger, an additional row appears on top of the existing reels. The respins play out on 25 positions instead of 20 positions. RED If a RED was in the trigger, an additional MULTIPLIER reel appears on the side of the reels. MULTIPLIER symbols appear on a separate reel and multiply all held symbols in their row. MULTIPLIER values can be 1x, 2x, 3x, or 5x. This MULTIPLIER is immediately applied to all PRIZE symbols on that row. If triggered with BLUE, the MULTIPLIER reel will include an additional position to match the additional row. PRIZE symbols may be affected by more than one multiplier during the RESPINS. MULTIPLIER values are not held and do not reset the respins. BUY BONUS (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) The player may opt to buy a RESPINS Feature. The cost to buy is 50x bet per spin. A spin is played that will trigger the RESPINS with 2 or 3 BONUS types. GAME RULES All wins occur on the 40 paylines. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels. Coinciding wins are added. Only highest win paid per line. All wins are as shown in the paytable at selected stake.
