Olympus 4 Game Features Olympus 4 is a classic 5 reels, 3 rows, and 10 paylines game with one highly volatile Free Spins Feature. The WILD in this game is also a SCATTER and will pay anywhere on the reels. With five of the scattered WILDs in view, players will be rewarded with 200x Total Bet. Free Spins If you land 3 or more Scatters on the reels in Base Game, you will receive 10 Free Spins. A symbol will be randomly chosen before the Free Spins Feature starts to act as a special expanding symbol. Whenever the special symbol comes into view on a reel, it will expand to cover the entirety of the reel, thus creating new winning combinations. Furthermore, if all 5 Zeus symbols land in view, you could win a total 50000 coins . Any time during Free Spins, if 3 or more Scatters come into view, the game will add an extra 10 Free Spins on top of your current total remaining Free Spins. In fact, you can retrigger infinitely for Unlimited Free Spins . Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 96.02% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $96.02 of wins. Rules Total bet is 10 coins x coin size. 10 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right adjacent symbols on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/3/2024
