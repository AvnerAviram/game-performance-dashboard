Reindeer Royale Game description Hunters in the mountains have killed the leader of the weirdest reindeer gang the world has ever seen. In the eternal struggle between humans and reindeer, it is hard to know who is the prey and who is the hunter.The revenge drives the reindeer wild and they plan to break into the hunting lodge and kill the humans. Reindeer Royale is a 5 reels, 5 rows slot with 259 connecting paylines. The game's main features are collectors and coins, represented by reindeer andammunition caches. The reindeer symbol is the driver of the game and awards respins when it collects ammunition caches or steals from other reindeer.Wild symbols reveal ammunition caches when they are part of a win and can then trigger a collect. Three bonus symbols trigger the free spins bonus game which kicks off with a sticky reindeer. Triggering the bonus game with more bonus symbols guarantees additional sticky reindeer. Game view Bet amount and balance are always displayed in the info bar at the bottom of the game view in the currency that the player has chosen. This information is alwaysavailable throughout the gaming session. Winnings are displayed in the selected currency in the middle of the info bar at the bottom of the game view when playing. Paylines Reindeer Royale has 259 connecting paylines. The RTP for all paylines is the same and the RTP of the game is defined in the game rules. Bonus game Three or more bonus symbols trigger the bonus game with free spins. Three bonus symbols award 3 free spins and 1 initial reindeer in the bonus game, four bonus symbols award 4 free spins and 2 initial reindeer, five bonus symbols award 5 free spins and 3 initial reindeer. The reindeer which are in view when the bonus game is triggered are brought into the bonus game, in addition to the reindeer awarded by the bonus trigger. The bonus game starts immediately after potential payline wins and collections, ending any ongoing respin. All reindeers are sticky during the bonus game. Game rules Reindeer Royale is a 5 reel, 5 rows slot with spinning reels and 259 connecting paylines. The minimum bet is $ 0.20, and the maximum bet is $ 100.00. Each bet is 100 coins. Wins begin with the first leftmost reel and only pay from left to right on consecutive reels. No gaps between the reels are allowed. Only the longest payline per active payline is paid out. A game round starts on the selected bet level when the player hits the spin button. The game can be played automatically by activating Auto Spin. Hold down the spin button or use the Auto Spin button to set the number of game rounds to be played automatically. Winnings and winning combinations are paid out according to the paytable. Winnings are presented in the player's currency or in coins. The player can change preference in Settings. Winnings are presented in the player's currency.Reindeer Royale is played in two modes; normal mode and bonus mode with free spins.The wild symbol substitutes for any symbol except the bonus, reindeer and ammunition cache symbols. A payline of all wilds pays as the highest value symbol.A wild that contributes to a win reveals an ammunition cache with a random value. The reveal occurs after the payline win, and only if there is at least one reindeer in view. Ammunition cache symbols are blockers. All reindeer in view individually collect the values of the ammunition caches after the potential payline wins. The collections award one respin regardless of the number of ammunition caches and reindeer involved. Reindeer symbols are blockers. They collect values from all ammunition caches in view, and from vertically and horizontally adjacent reindeer. The collect awards a respin, during which the reindeer may move to new positions. Any additional reindeer that appear without ammunition caches during a respin award one respin regardless of the number of reindeer. The collection of ammunition caches and between reindeer occur after potential payline wins. One respin is awarded regardless of the number of reindeer and ammunition caches involved, as long as some value has been collected. The values of the reindeer are paid out at the end of the game round when there are no more respins. All reindeer in view individually collect all ammunition caches in view prior to any potential collection from each other. Reindeer do not interact with each other if neither of them has any value. The value of a reindeer can never decrease and it is kept until the end of the game round. The ghost feature is triggered randomly during a respin or free spin when there are at least two reindeer in view. It triggers a collect between a random number of reindeer that are currently in view. The selected reindeer do not need to be adjacent to each other, and the collection takes place over spinning reels where the selected reindeer are currently located. A combination of three or more bonus symbols during normal mode triggers the bonus mode with free spins. 3 bonus symbols award 3 free spins and 1 reindeer in the first free spin 4 bonus symbols award 4 free spins and 2 reindeer in the first free spin 5 bonus symbols award 5 free spins and 3 reindeer in the first free spin Three or more bonus symbols during bonus mode retrigger additional free spins and additional reindeer. The number of additional free spins and reindeer is determined in the same way as when the bonus game was initially triggered. Single bonus symbols award an additional free spin The bonus game starts immediately after potential payline wins and collections if a bonus game is triggered during a respin. The reindeer which are currently present in view are brought into the bonus game, in addition to the reindeer awarded by the bonus trigger. There can never be more than 5 reindeer simultaneously in view in any mode. Bonus triggers and retriggers only award additional free spins when the maximum number of reindeer is reached. The bonus game and super bonus game end when no free spins remain or when the win cap has been reached. Winnings are paid out at the end of the bonus mode. No bets can be changed during an ongoing game round. A game round is immediately ended when the win cap is reached, and the wins collected so far will be paid out. The theoretical payout (RTP) in Reindeer Royale is 94.0%. The game will be capped at 25 000x the placed bet. In the event of malfunction all bets and wins are void.
