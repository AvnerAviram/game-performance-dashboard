Oink Country Love Game Payouts Winnings paid out on the online slot game are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination. Line Payouts Line wins are calculated by multiplying the line combination payout by the coins bet . For a combination of regular symbols to produce a winning line combination, the following criteria must be met: Winning line combinations pay out from left-to-right, so a winning line combination must start with the left most reel. The symbols in the winning line combination must be on adjacent reels on a payline. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Scatter Symbol Payouts Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the Scatter symbol combination payout by the total bet . Scatter wins are added to line wins. In the event of a scatter win and a line win, both wins are paid out, as the Scatter symbol does not need to appear on a payline to win. For a combination of Scatter symbols to produce a winning scatter combination, the following criteria must be met: Three or more Scatters appear on the reels. Scatters cannot be substituted by the Wild symbols. Game Rules See sections below for information about the game rules. Betting and Payout Rules You can select the number of coins bet and the coin size . Total bet is 50 multiplied by coins bet by coin size . Winning line combinations pay out from left-to-right, so a winning line combination must start with the left most reel. The symbols in the winning line combination must be on adjacent reels on a payline. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Winnings are paid out only on the highest combination on each payline. Line wins are calculated by multiplying the line combination payout by the coins bet . All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination. The values displayed are determined by the number of coins bet . All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. Scatter pays are determined by three or more Scatter symbols in any position on the reels. Free spins wins are added to regular wins. Pays during Free Spins are the same as in base game. Malfunctions void all plays and pays. Wild Symbol Rules The Red Pig and Blue Pig symbols are Wild symbols. The Red Pig wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol and the Blue Pig wild symbol. The Blue Pig wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol and the Red Pig wild symbol. The Red Pig and the Blue Pig wild symbols create their own line winning combination when mixed Wild symbols appear on a payline. Only the highest combination is paid out per payline. Scatter Symbol Rules The Ticket/Scatter symbol is a scatter symbol. Cannot be substituted by any Wild symbol. Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the Scatter symbol combination payout by the total bet . Scatter wins are added to line wins. If you have a scatter win and a line win, you are paid out for both wins, as the Scatter symbol does not need to appear on a payline to win. Ticket/Scatter symbols on 3, 4, or 5 adjacent reels starting from the leftmost reel trigger 5, 10, or 20 free spins respectively. 2 Ticket/Scatter symbols on reel 2 OR reel 3 multiplies the number of spins awarded by 2. 2 Ticket/Scatter symbols on reel 2 AND 2 Ticket/Scatter symbols on reel 3 multiplies the number of spins awarded by 4. Free Spins can be retriggered. Only the longest combination applies. Playing the Game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on an active payline. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. To play: To select your bet amount: Desktop Click the Bet area. Move the Coin Size slider to the right to increase or to the left to decrease the coin size you want to bet. Move the Coins slider to the right to increase or to the left to decrease the number of coins you want to bet. Click Max Bet to bet the maximum permitted. This converts the coin size and number of coins bet to the maximum bet permitted. Mobile Open the Options Panel by tapping the (>) button. In Bet Settings , swipe the Coin Size values left or right to adjust the coin size you want to bet. In Bet Settings , swipe the Coins values left or right to adjust the number of coins you want to bet. Tap Max Bet to bet the maximum permitted. This converts the coin size, number of coins bet to the maximum bet permitted. Click or Tap Spin to start spinning the reels. Once the reels stop, if you have a winning combination lined up on a payline, the win is highlighted on the reels. You are paid out the win amount as displayed in the Win box. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is completed and you can play again. Options and Settings The Options panel provides access to multiple game settings and options. Autoplay You can spin a set number of times in succession without interacting with the game, using Autoplay. About Autoplay Autoplay and some of the related options may not be available in the game you are playing. A bet amount must be set before starting the Autoplay session, as Autoplay plays with the last bet value. Autoplay can be stopped at any time. Triggering any feature will stop Autoplay.
