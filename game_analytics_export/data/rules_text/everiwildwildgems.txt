Wild Wild Gems GAME RULES RUBY AND SAPPHIRE SYMBOLS ARE WILD! Ruby and Sapphire symbols are wild and substitute for Seven and Bar symbols that also land on the payline. One Ruby symbol in a winning Sevens or Bars combination pays 2x prize value. Two Ruby symbols in a winning Sevens or Bars combination pays 4x prize value. Only highest winner paid per line. WIN TOP JACKPOT OF 10,000 CREDITS Three Ruby symbols on the 9th payline with max credits bet wins the top jackpot.
