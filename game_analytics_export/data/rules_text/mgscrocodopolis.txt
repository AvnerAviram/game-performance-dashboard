Crocodopolis About Crocodopolis Crocodopolis is a five reel and twenty-five payline slot machine, with: A Slide-a-Wild feature. A scatter symbol. A Free Spins bonus game. A Gamble feature. Game Payouts Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. Regular Payouts All payout tables display the number of coins that are won for each combination. The total number of coins paid out depends on the number of coins you play per payline. Regular wins are calculated by taking the number of coins won, multiplied by the number of coins played per payline. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size bet. For a combination of regular symbols to produce a winning regular combination, the following criteria must be met: The symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. • Scatter Symbol Payouts Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the scatter symbol combination payout by the total number of credits played. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on an enabled payline to win. Only appears on reels 2, 3 and 4. For a combination of scatter symbols to produce a winning scatter combination, two or more must appear on the reels. Free Spins Bonus Game Payouts You are awarded twelve free spins. All wins are doubled during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. Gamble Feature Payouts A correct guess for the card colour doubles your win. A correct guess for the card suit quadruples your win. You can play your winnings until the gamble limit is reached. Betting and Payout Rules Winnings are paid out on the highest combination on each enabled payline only, except for scatter wins. Winning scatter combinations can appear in any position on the reels. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. The symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. All payout tables display the number of coins that are won for each combination. The total number of coins paid out depends on the number of coins you play per payline. All payouts are in credits. Regular wins are calculated by taking the number of coins won, multiplied by the number of coins played per payline. Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the scatter symbol combination payout by the total number of credits played. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size bet. The formula to calculate the conversion of your coins to credits is: number of coins x coin size played = amount in credits. Malfunctions void all plays and pays. Reel 3 The Croc symbol is an expanding wild symbol. The Croc symbol only expands if it completes a winning combination. The expanded Croc symbol can complete multiple winning combinations. You win payout's for all winning combinations Reel 4 The Croc symbol is an expanding wild symbol. Triples the payout of any combination it completes as a wild symbol. The Croc symbol only expands if it completes a winning combination. Reel 5 The Croc symbol completes a wild symbol stack. Triples the payout of any combination it completes as a wild symbol. Free Spins bonus game Three Pyramid symbols scattered on the reels 2, 3 and 4 activate the Free Spins bonus game. In this game: You are awarded twelve free spins. All wins are doubled during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. So, if three or more Pyramid symbols are scattered on the reels during the free spins, you are awarded additional free spins. The extra free spins are added to the remaining number of free spins. To play the bonus game: The reels automatically start spinning the free spins. The free spins are complete. Your total bonus win amount is counted in the bonus Win box. You are automatically returned to the regular slot game. Gamble Feature Any win activates the Gamble feature. Playing the game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on the payline. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. To play: Select the number of paylines you want to play. How? Click Lines (+) or (-) to select the number of paylines you want to play. To bet the maximum number of lines permitted, click Max . Place a bet. How? Click Bet (+) or (-) to select the amount you want to bet. To bet the maximum number of credits permitted, click Max . The number of credits bet increases to the maximum and the game automatically starts playing. Click Spin . The reels start spinning. If you have a winning combination lined up on the payline, the win is highlighted on the reels. You are paid out the win amount as displayed in the Win box. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again. In some slot games the Spin button becomes a Stop button. Click Stop to display your spin results quicker.
