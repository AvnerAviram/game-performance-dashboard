Super Triple Fortune Game Theme and Graphics Super Triple Fortune brings you the traditional 3x3 slot machine with outstanding diamond wins up to x20000 bet. See the golden tree of fortune and let it shower you with glistening golden coins of good luck. This dynamic and active game enables you to choose between two interactive lucky bonus games, offering you the chance to win the Triple Diamond Win or multiply your fortune. Dive into America’s Chinatown and the golden tree of fortune will pave the way to the gold. Main Game Super Triple Fortune is a brand new 3x3 slot where you can play with 5 win lines and remember… the more win lines you place a bet on, the higher the wins will be. All symbols pay when 3 matching symbols appear on a win line from left to right. The coin symbols can pay for 1, 2, or 3 symbols when they appear anywhere on a win line. If 3 Diamond Wild symbols land on win line 5 when playing on 5 lines, they grant a prize of 20000 x total bet . There is also an "Any Bar" pay out that occurs when any 3 bar symbol combination lands on a win line. Wild Multiplier The game has 2 Wild Multiplier symbols - Wild and Diamond Wild. Land Wild Multiplier symbols in a winning combination and watch your win multiply by 2 or 4 with 1 or 2 Wild Multiplier symbols respectively. The Wild symbol will substitute for all symbols except Scatter or Diamond Wild. The Diamond Wild will substitute for all symbols except Scatter. Bonus Games Landing a Scatter anywhere on the reels will trigger the Bonus Game Choice. The player can choose the Bonus Game they wish to play – Golden Pick Bonus or The Wheel of Fortune Bonus Game. GOLDEN PICK BONUS The Bonus Game consists of 16 Chinese golden coins. You just pick 4 coins to reveal your luck. Behind each coin hides: a girl, boy, tiger, or panda, each character having its own value, multiplying the bet. Boy - 2000 x total bet. Girl - 1200 x total bet. Tiger - 500 x total bet. Panda - 300 x total bet. Bonus payout: 4 characters of the same kind grant the character value. 3 characters of the same kind grant 3% of its value. 2 characters of the same kind twice (2-2) grant 1% of each character. 1 character of each kind (1-1-1-1) grants 15 x total bet. 2 of the same kind + 2 different kinds (2-1-1) grants 1% of the character value that appears twice. Characters appearing only once have no payout in this combination. The win value seen on top of the reels presents the character's top value if 4 characters of the same kind were picked during the Bonus. THE WHEEL OF FORTUNE The wheel is divided into 22 wedges each containing a prize. Spin the wheel and land the pointer on your slice of fortune. You will then receive the value of the wedge that the pointer lands on. Prize values are 4, 5, 6, 8,10,12,15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50, 80, 100, 200. All wins are then multiplied by the total bet. Game Rules The game can be played with 1 to 5 win lines across the 3x3 grid. 3 Matching symbols on the same win line pay. All pays are for wins of left to right except Coin symbols, which pay any. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. The Coin symbols can pay for 1, 2, or 3 symbols when appearing on any win line and don’t need to align from left to right.
