Fairy Fantasy Exotic Wilds GENERAL Fairy Fantasy Exotic Wilds is a 5 reel by 3 row, 243 'ways pays' video slot featuring Exotic Wilds. During each reel spin, 1, 2, 3, or 4 reels will transform into a [lvy Leaves], [Waterfall], or [Flames]. When the reels stop, all transformed wild reels that contribute to a win will morph into their corresponding exotic wild fairy and given an exotic multiplier corresponding to the appearing fairy. In other words, the [Ivy leaves], [waterfall], or [flames] will transform into a [Earth Fairy], [Water Fairy], or [Fire Fairy] respectively if they are part of a win. Transformed wild reels that do not contribute to any winning ways will not morph into an exotic wild. At the end of each spin, every [Earth Fairy] that appears on the reels will have a random multiplier assigned from 2x to 7x. Specifically 2x,3x,4x,5x,6x, or 7x. At the end of each spin, every [Water Fairy] that appears on the reels will have a random multiplier assigned ranging from 3x to 15x. Specifically 3x,5x,7x,10x,12x or 15x. At the end of each spin, every [Fire Fairy] that appears on the reels have a random multiplier assigned ranging from 5x to 50x. Specifically 5x,10x,15x,20x,30x, or 50x. If more than one exotic wild appear during the same spin, wins that include multiple Exotic Wilds take the sum of all the Exotic Multipliers included in the spin. [Earth Fairy] Substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Water Fairy], and [Fire Fairy]. [Water Fairy] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Earth Fairy], and [Fire Fairy]. [Fire Fairy] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Earth Fairy], and [Water Fairy]. FREE SPINS AND CHOOSE YOUR VOLATILITY OPTIONS Landing 3 [free spins] during the base game triggers the free spins. Choose 1 of 3 Free Spin options from Medium to Extreme Volatility: Medium Volatility option awards 10 free spins with 2 reel transformations per spin. High Volatility option awards 5 free spins with 3 reel transformation per spin. Extreme Volatility option awards 1 free spin with 4 reel transformations. It is not possible to retrigger for more free spins during the free spins bonus. Free Spins can only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Spins are played on the bet of the triggering spin. BUY FREE SPINS Press the [Buy Free Spins] button to enter the bonus buy confirmation screen. Choose the stake of the bonus and press [Buy] to confirm a bet of 96x stake and automatically trigger the free spins. The RTP of the Buy Free Spins is 93.94%, 94.1%, and 94.08% respectively when choosing the option with 10, 5, or 1 spin respectively. Buy Feature may not be available on all markets Double Free Spins Chance Press [Double Free Spins Chance] to double the probability of triggering the free spins bonus in exchange for an extra ante bet equal to 30% the amount already bet. The RTP when Double Free Spins Chance is activated is 93.99%, 94.11%, and 93.92% when choosing the option with 10, 5, or 1 free spins respectively. Buy Free Spins option is not available when Double Free Spins Chance is activated.
