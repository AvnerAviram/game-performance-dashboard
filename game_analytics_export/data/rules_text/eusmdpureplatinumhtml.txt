Pure Platinum Pure Platinum General In this game all 40 paylines are permanently enabled. You can bet up to 10 coins per payline. Your total bet amount is the number of coins you bet per payline, multiplied by the number of paylines. Regular wins are created when the same symbols appear next to each other on a payline, with one of the symbols displayed on the first slot reel. One regular win is paid per payline. If you have more than one combination, you are paid the win that has the highest value. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. Regular wins are displayed in coins on the pay table. Regular wins are calculated by multiplying the amount on the pay table, by the number of coins you bet per payline. To convert your win amount in coins to credits, multiply the number of coins won by the coin size you bet. Wild symbol Substitutes for other symbols to complete wins. Does not substitute for the scatter symbol. Creates its own win when multiple wild symbols appear next to each other on a payline, with one of the symbols displayed on the first slot reel. Multiple wild symbols can appear on the same reel, creating a symbol stack. Regular win rules apply. Scatter symbol Does not need to appear next to each other on a payline to win. Creates a scatter win when two or more appear anywhere on the reels. Is not substituted by the wild symbol. Activates free spins when three or more appear on the reels. Scatter wins are displayed as a multiplier value on the pay table. Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the amount on the pay table by the total number of coins bet. Free spins Spin for free! Activates when three or more scatter symbols appear on the reels. You are awarded up to 50 free spins. The maximum multiplier value is 5. You can choose the number of free spins and multiplier values. The last bet you placed in the game is used as the free spins bet amount. Free spins cannot be re-activated. Quick spin Quick spin increases the reel spin speed to display your results quicker. This does not influence the results of the spin. To turn quick spin on in a game: Slide right to Settings. Tap quick spin Off. To disable quick spin, tap quick spin On. This feature may not be available in the game you are playing.
