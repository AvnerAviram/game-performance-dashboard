Saint Nicked 3 COLLECT FEATURE WHEN INITIALLY ENTERING THE GAME, THE JOY BRINGER COLLECT SYMBOL IS ACTIVE ON REELS 1 AND 6. A JOY BRINGER COLLECT SYMBOL APPEARING IN VIEW TOGETHER WITH ONE OR MORE CASH/CASHPOT SYMBOLS DURING ANY SPIN, TRIGGERS THE COLLECT FEATURE. IN THE FEATURE, THE JOY BRINGER COLLECT SYMBOL EXPANDS TO COVER THE ENTIRE REEL 1 OR 6 AND COLLECTS THE VALUES SHOWN ON THE CASH PRIZE SYMBOLS AFTER ANY INITIAL WINS ARE EVALUATED. THE CORRESPONDING CASH PRIZES ARE AWARDED IN ADDITION TO ANY WIN WAYS. IF A VAC & STACK SYMBOL APPEARS ON THE REELS DURING THE COLLECT FEATURE, IT ACCUMULATES THE VALUES OF ANY CASH PRIZE SYMBOLS IN VIEW BEFORE BEING COLLECTED BY A COLLECT SYMBOL ALONG WITH ALL OF THE CASH PRIZE SYMBOLS. LANDING ANY CASHPOT SYMBOLS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR OR MEGA) WITH A COLLECT SYMBOL IN VIEW AWARDS THE CORRESPONDING POT VALUE. 5 UPGRADE LEVELS IN THE COLLECT FEATURE IN THE MAIN GAME: 1. TINSELATOR CASH LOCK: ONCE A JOY BRINGER COLLECT SYMBOL COLLECTS ANY CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS, THE TINSELATOR CASH LOCK COLLECT SYMBOL IS UNLOCKED ON REELS 1 AND 6. IF YOU LAND IT, THE CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS THAT ACTIVATED THE FEATURE WILL BE HELD IN POSITION WHILE ACTIVE REELS RE-SPIN. THIS WILL CONTINUE UNTIL NO NEW CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS LAND IN VIEW. 2. NUTCRACKER NUDGE: ONCE A TINSELATOR CASH LOCK COLLECT SYMBOL COLLECTS ANY CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS, THE NUTCRACKER NUDGE COLLECT SYMBOL IS UNLOCKED ON REELS 1 AND 6. IF YOU LAND IT, ANY INITIATING CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS WILL STEP EITHER UP OR DOWN, FILLING THE WHOLE REEL WITH CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS. 3. BARGAIN BOOST: ONCE A NUTCRACKER NUDGE COLLECT SYMBOL COLLECTS ANY CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS, THE BARGAIN BOOST COLLECT SYMBOL IS UNLOCKED ON REELS 1 AND 6. IF YOU LAND IT, ALL CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL MATCH THE CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOL OF THE LARGEST VALUE. 4. JOY BRINGER ULTRA: ONCE A BARGAIN BOOST COLLECT SYMBOL COLLECTS ANY CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS, THE JOY BRINGER ULTRA COLLECT SYMBOL IS UNLOCKED ON REELS 1 AND 6. IF YOU LAND IT, ALL CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL BE COLLECTED UP TO 5 TIMES. 5. TREE-SURE TROVE: ONCE A JOY BRINGER ULTRA COLLECT SYMBOL COLLECTS ANY CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS, THE TREE-SURE TROVE IS UNLOCKED IN THE MAIN GAME AND SANTA SPINS. ONCE ACTIVE, ANY NON-COLLECTED CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL BE ADDED TO THE POT. ANY UNLOCKED COLLECT SYMBOLS REMAIN ACTIVE IN THE MAIN GAME, SANTA SPINS AND SUPER SANTA SPINS FROM THAT POINT ONWARDS. TREE-SURE TROVE VAC & STACK, CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS THAT APPEAR ON THE REELS WITHOUT A COLLECT SYMBOL IN VIEW ARE COLLECTED TO THE POT. ON ANY SPIN WITH NO WINS, THE POT CAN BE RANDOMLY ANIMATED, TRIGGERING THE TREE-SURE TROVE FEATURE. IN THE FEATURE, YOU ARE ASKED TO RANDOMLY PICK ONE OF THE FOLLOWING MODIFIERS: SANTA SPINS: AWARDS SANTA SPINS WITH A CHANCE OF A RANDOM CASH PRIZE (20X BET, 100X BET OR 500X BET). SUPER SANTA SPINS: AWARDS SUPER SANTA SPINS. CASHPOT WHEEL: A WHEEL SPINS TO AWARD ONE OF THE FOUR CASHPOTS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR OR MEGA). WIN SPIN: SEVERAL TOP PAYING SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE REELS TO GUARANTEE A WINNING COMBINATION. SANTA COLLECT: COLLECTS ALL CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOLS ON THE REELS, PAYING THE COMBINED VALUE OF THE SYMBOLS IN VIEW. JOLLY DROP: ADDS A COLLECT SYMBOL TO REEL 1, REEL 6, OR BOTH (DEPENDING ON THE TYPES OF COLLECT SYMBOLS ACTIVE), VAC & STACK, CASH, OR CASHPOT SYMBOLS TO THE REELS TO GUARANTEE A WIN. ADDITIONAL SPINS: UP TO 5 ADDITIONAL SPINS ARE AWARDED (ONLY ACTIVE IN SANTA SPINS OR SUPER SANTA SPINS). SANTA SPINS 3 OR MORE SCATTER SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL TRIGGER THE SANTA SPINS: 3 SCATTER SYMBOLS = 10 SANTA SPINS 4 SCATTER SYMBOLS = 10 SANTA SPINS + 20X BET 5 SCATTER SYMBOLS = 10 SANTA SPINS + 100X BET 6 SCATTER SYMBOLS = 10 SANTA SPINS + 500X BET DURING THE SANTA SPINS, ALL UNLOCKED COLLECT SYMBOLS REMAIN ACTIVE. ON ANY SPIN WHERE A COLLECT SYMBOL LANDS ON THE REELS, THE CASH PRIZE IS AWARDED FOR EACH CASH OR CASHPOT SYMBOL IN VIEW. DURING THE SANTA SPINS, ACCUMULATING COLLECT SYMBOLS AWARDS ADDITIONAL SANTA SPINS AND CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOL MULTIPLIERS AS FOLLOWS: 3 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SANTA SPINS + X2 MULTIPLIER 6 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SANTA SPINS + X3 MULTIPLIER 9 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SANTA SPINS + X5 MULTIPLIER 12 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SANTA SPINS + X10 MULTIPLIER THE AWARDED MULTIPLIERS WILL BE APPLIED TO THE CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOLS DURING THE COLLECT FEATURE. SANTA SPINS CONTINUE UNTIL ALL SANTA SPINS ARE USED, WHERE THE PLAYER IS AWARDED THE AMOUNT OF THE BONUS WINNINGS ACCUMULATED. SUPER SANTA SPINS THE SUPER SANTA SPINS CAN BE TRIGGERED ONLY VIA TREE-SURE TROVE. ONCE TRIGGERED, A PICK AN UPGRADE IS AWARDED BEFORE YOU ENTER SUPER SANTA SPINS, WHICH CAN AWARD ONE OR MORE OF THE FOLLOWING UPGRADES: BOOST TRAIL: BOOSTS THE PROGRESS TRAIL ABOVE THE REELS TO X2 LEVEL OF SUPER SANTA SPINS. EXTRA SPINS: AWARDS 5 ADDITIONAL SUPER SANTA SPINS. + VAC & STACK SYMBOLS: ADDS EXTRA VAC & STACK SYMBOLS TO THE REELS. + CASHPOT SYMBOLS: ADDS EXTRA CASHPOT SYMBOLS TO THE REELS. + COLLECT SYMBOLS: ADDS EXTRA COLLECT SYMBOLS TO REELS 1 AND 6. IN THE BOOST TRAIL UPGRADE, THE X2 LEVEL WILL BE UNLOCKED WITHOUT AWARDING 5 ADDITIONAL SUPER SANTA SPINS. DURING THE SUPER SANTA SPINS, ACCUMULATING COLLECT SYMBOLS AWARDS ADDITIONAL SUPER SANTA SPINS AND CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOL MULTIPLIERS AS FOLLOWS: 3 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SUPER SANTA SPINS + X2 MULTIPLIER 6 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SUPER SANTA SPINS + X3 MULTIPLIER 9 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SUPER SANTA SPINS + X5 MULTIPLIER 12 COLLECT SYMBOLS = 5 EXTRA SUPER SANTA SPINS + X10 MULTIPLIER IF THE BONUS STARTS ON THE RETRIGGER POSITION, YOU WILL ONLY BE AWARDED RETRIGGERING SPINS. THE AWARDED MULTIPLIERS WILL BE APPLIED TO THE CASH AND CASHPOT SYMBOLS DURING THE COLLECT FEATURE. SUPER SANTA SPINS CONTINUE UNTIL ALL SUPER SANTA SPINS ARE USED, WHERE THE PLAYER IS AWARDED THE AMOUNT OF THE BONUS WINNINGS ACCUMULATED. WILD SYMBOL THE WILD SYMBOL CAN APPEAR ON REELS 2-6. WILD SYMBOLS SUBSTITUTE FOR ALL SYMBOLS EXCEPT SCATTER, VAC & STACK, CASH/CASHPOT, AND COLLECT SYMBOLS. WIN WAYS EACH SPIN CAN HAVE UP TO 4,096 WAYS TO WIN! AUTOPLAY (subject to operator configuration and jurisdictional requirements) There is an Autoplay function in the game that will start each spin automatically. By selecting 'AUTOPLAY' you can choose to bet automatically, one after the other. Select your Loss Limit (maximum amount willing to lose for the autoplay session) and then press PLAY to begin your autoplays (other options are available). Select your stake as normal then press the 'AUTOPLAY' button to select the number of 'Autoplay' bets you want to play. You can stop Auto Play from continuing onto the next spin by clicking the 'STOP' button. Auto Play will turn off if you reach the set Loss Limit, Win Limit, or if a Progressive pot is awarded (where applicable). Auto Play will pause if you get into any bonus round and resume on returning to the Main Game. CLICK THE TOTAL BET BUTTON TO INCREASE OR DECREASE THE AMOUNT OF YOUR TOTAL BET. SELECT FROM THE LIST OF AVAILABLE STAKES FROM THE TOTAL BET OPTIONS (DEPENDING ON OPERATOR CONFIGURATION AND CURRENCY EQUIVALENT). THE REELS WILL SPIN AND EVENTUALLY STOP AT RANDOM. IF THE COMBINATION OF SYMBOLS APPEARING ON CONSECUTIVE REELS FORMS MATCHING SYMBOLS STARTING FROM THE LEFTMOST REEL ON ANY OF THE WAYS, THEN YOU WIN. ANY WINS YOU HAVE WILL BE HIGHLIGHTED AND YOUR WINNINGS WILL BE CREDITED TO YOUR ACCOUNT BALANCE. MULTIPLE SCATTER FEATURES CAN BE WON IN A SINGLE GAME. THE VISUAL APPEARANCE OF CASH/CASHPOT SYMBOLS DIFFERS BETWEEN THE MAIN GAME AND BONUS FEATURES. IN SANTA SPINS/SUPER SANTA SPINS, SYMBOLS CHANGE WHEN PROGRESSING TO DIFFERENT LOCATIONS IN THE PROGRESS TRAIL. CASH SYMBOLS DISPLAY VALUES BETWEEN 0.5X and 15X BET. CASHPOT SYMBOLS AWARD THE FOLLOWING CASHPOTS: MINI - 20X, MINOR - 50X, MAJOR - 200X, MEGA - 1000X BET. WIN FREQUENCY, VOLATILITY AND LEVEL PROGRESSION MAY VARY DEPENDING ON THE NUMBER OF GAMES PLAYED. ALL WAY PAY SYMBOLS MUST APPEAR ON CONSECUTIVE REELS BEGINNING WITH THE LEFTMOST REEL (4096 WAYS ARE ALWAYS ACTIVE). SCATTER WINS ARE PAID IN ADDITION TO REEL WINS. THE BONUS FEATURE IS PLAYED ON THE SAME BET AS THE TRIGGERING GAME. THE COLLECTION FEATURE IN THE GAME IS FOR VISUAL PURPOSES ONLY AND DOES NOT INFLUENCE GAME OUTCOMES.
