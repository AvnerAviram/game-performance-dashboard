Indian Spirit Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right except Scatters. Scatter symbols may only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4. All prizes are on selected lines, except Scatters. Scatter symbols pay at any position on screen. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per Scatter combination. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Free Games can be won again during Gree Games. Free Games played at trigger bet and lines. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Feature If three or more 'Eagle' symbols appear on the reels, you will receive 10 Free Games. If the 'Dream Catcher' symbol appears in a Free Game, huge winnings will call to you from the mystical skies above! Trigger: Scattered 'Eagle' on reels 2, 3 and 4 triggers 10 Free Games Top prize: 1000 times bet per line times lines played Feature hit rate: 160-200 games Main Game The aim of Indian Spirit™ is to get the same symbol showing on all 5 reels. In the regular game, winning combinations run left to right across the paylines. To land a win, a complete winning combination must occupy a payline, without any other symbols coming between the winning symbols. The Wild 'Dream Catcher' symbol acts as a Joker and replaces all symbols, apart from the Scatter symbol ('Eagle'). Game Theme and Graphics Follow the path of the wise American Indian chiefs and let your dreams show you the way. The eagle will keep its eye on you whilst you step up to the challenge of finding the treasures of the Injuns. RTP-92.40% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
