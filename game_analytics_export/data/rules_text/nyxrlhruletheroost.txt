Rich Little Hens Rule the Roost PAYTABLE Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Multiple line wins are added together. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. WILD SYMBOL WILD substitutes for all symbols except RED COIN, SILVER COIN, PURPLE COIN and their combinations. MYSTERY REVEAL During the base game, before pays are evaluated, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, QUEEN HEN, KING HEN, KNIGHT HEN, CROWN, TRUMPET, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN. In addition, MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a RED COIN, SILVER COIN, PURPLE COIN or their combination on top of a replaced symbol before pays are evaluated. During the Free Spins Feature, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, QUEEN HEN, KING HEN, KNIGHT HEN, CROWN, TRUMPET, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN before pays are evaluated. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with PURPLE COIN, WILD only appears on reels 2-4 through replacement. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except RED COIN, SILVER COIN, and PURPLE COIN do not appear. The distribution of Free Spins Feature prizes changes with bet option. The size of the Red, Silver, and Purple Hens has no effect on triggering the Free Spins Feature. FREE SPINS FEATURE Any combination of RED COIN, SILVER COIN, PURPLE COIN may trigger the Free Spins Feature and award 1x total bet. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered by RED COIN, SILVER COIN, or PURPLE COIN, then, for each coin involved, "WINNER!" appears by the BROWN HEN, WHITE HEN, or PURPLE HEN respectively. If a RED COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that RED COIN may add 1 free spin to the BROWN HEN meter for the active bet option. If a SILVER COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that SILVER COIN may add 0.25x-6.75x Current Bet to the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI meter for the active bet option. If a PURPLE COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that PURPLE COIN may increase the Wild Multiplier by 1 in the PURPLE HEN meter for the active bet option. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with SILVER COIN, then each MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a Jackpot Symbol for the MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI Jackpots. Jackpot Symbols are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. The MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI Jackpots have 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, and 2 spaces respectively. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, and the corresponding jackpot is awarded and then reset before any additional jackpots are awarded. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with SILVER COIN , the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI Jackpot meters are reset. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with RED COIN, then the number of free spins displayed in the BROWN HEN meter are awarded. If the Free Spins Feature was not triggered with RED COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded when Free Spins Feature is awarded. The BROWN HEN meter can display a maximum of 100 Free Spins. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with RED COIN, the BROWN HEN meter is reset to 9 free spins. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with PURPLE COIN, then all winning combinations involving WILD MULTIPLIER are multiplied by the Wild Multiplier. The PURPLE HEN meter can display a maximum Wild Multiplier of 7x. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with PURPLE COIN, the PURPLE HEN meter is reset to a Wild Multiplier of 2x. WILD MULTIPLIER appears only during Free Spins Feature triggered with PURPLE COIN. WILD MULTIPLIER appears only on reels 2-4. WILD MULTIPLIER is WILD for WILD.
