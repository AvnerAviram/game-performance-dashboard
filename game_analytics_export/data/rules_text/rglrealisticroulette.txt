Realistic Roulette Introduction Roulette is a game of chance. It has a spinning wheel, with numbers ranging from 0 to 36. The purpose of the game is to predict where the ball will land on the wheel. The player makes one or more bet selections on the betting layout to reflect their where the ball will land on the wheel. The player makes one or more bet selections on the betting layout to reflect their prediction, then spins the wheel to see if their prediction is correct Chip Selection Select a chip value using the Chip Selector button. Placing Chips Once you have selected your desired chip value, you can place that chip on the betting layout. Hover your mouse over the betting layout to highlight the available betting positions, then click with the primary mouse buttonto place the chip. Keep clicking the primary mouse button to place more chips to place the chip. Keep clicking the primary mouse button to place more chips. The x2 button will double all bets on the betting layout. The Undo button will undo your last action. The Clear button will clear all bets on the betting layout. Spin the Wheel Once you are happy with your bet selections, press the Spin button to place your bets and spin the wheel. The wheel will spin with the ball spinning in the opposite direction to the wheel. Upon landing in a cup, the result is then displayed on-screen and any winnings are paid. Next Game Any winning chips from the previous game will remain on the betting layout. If you wish, you can press Spin to place another bet using those chips. If you want to place the previous bet selection in full, press the Rebet button. You can also place other chips if you wish. Return to Player This game is random.
