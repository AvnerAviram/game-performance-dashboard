50 Lions 50 lines are played for line cost of 25 Coins. Bet is 25 Coins x Coin Size. All wins on played lines only except Scatters. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. RTP: 94.71%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
