Turbo Mode Game Recovery General Information Getting Started: Roulette Six is a roulette game with 37 potential outcomes for each spin of the wheel. Several different bet types are available. How to play: You must place at least 10p in chips on the betting table before selecting BET to start a game. The Total stake is displayed at all times. Set your bet Place chips on the table by selecting the position on the table that represents the bet you wish to place. Chip values can be changed using the chip panel below the table. Bets can be placed on numerous positions on the table. For example, to place a split bet, select the position in-between two numbers on the table. Pressing Repeat Bet will repeat the chip placement from the previous game. Pressing Clear All Bets or Clear Last Bet will remove any bets placed in error. Start the game Select 'BET' to start your game. The SPACE BAR key on the keyboard can often be used as an alternative for pressing the primary on screen button. How do you win? A win is awarded if the ball lands on a number on the wheel which corresponds to any bets placed on the table. Once the ball has landed, any winnings are paid and the chips are cleared. The previous results of the roulette wheel spin are displayed above the betting table. Awards are detailed in the pay table. Bets and Payouts Your winnings will be credited to your account immediately. You will lose your bet amount if you lose the game. Game Recovery: Bet Recovery Due to the nature of the communications medium for the game, the player may experience interruptions, delays or termination to the game play cycle. In the event of an unexpected interruption/termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. If for example, the game was started but not fully completed due to closure of the browser or connectivity problems, this will not affect the final result of the game after the game has recovered, and any winnings will be correctly paid to your account balance, and will be exactly according to the winning or losing result produced prior to the game being prematurely terminated. Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. General Information: The player's odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) for this game is 97.3%. The RTP reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays over an extended period of time, and represents the long-term expected payback of the game which has been calculated by an independent testing company, in accordance with the required regulations. The outcome of any play during the game is pre-determined and player choices do not affect the return of the game. This game has been independently fairness tested by GLI, a leader in Testing and Auditing with over 40 years of experience serving the gambling sector as an ISO17025 accredited testing facility.
