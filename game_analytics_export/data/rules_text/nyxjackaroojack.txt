Jackaroo Jack Jackaroo Jack is a Bet 40, 1024 way 4x5 Reel slot game with a 2nd Chance Respin and Free Game bonus. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1, 2 and 3. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.6236% with any bet. The Won field displays winnings in credits. All 1024 ways always played for 40 credits. Spacebar can be used to spin. Select Bet Multiplier x 40 bet for ways stake. Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Free Game wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. Game Rules Play bet 40 Coins for 1024 ways. Payments are made according to the pay table. All wins shown at 1 credit bet.All wins multiplied by the multiplier bet. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. For all symbols including scatters, winning combinations pay through any position on reels 1, 2, 3, 4 & 5 respectively. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels including scatter. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. 6 or more MAN/WILD triggers Free Games. When 2 Free Games are triggered from a base game, the second Free Games is queued and played at the end of Free Games. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. 2nd Chance Respin 6 or more MAN/WILD triggers Free Games. 2nd Chance Respin can be triggered both in Base Games and Free Games. Any Wild with no win “locks” the reel containing the wild and the remaining reels are respun. If you improve by obtaining another Wild, your respin does not start again. Free Games Select 1 of the 5 Free Games game options. Free Games re-triggered during Free Games are added to the remaining number of Free Games. Multipliers are applied to the WILDS (different multiplier on Reel 2 and Reel 4). Free Games are played at the same multiplier bet as the triggering game.
