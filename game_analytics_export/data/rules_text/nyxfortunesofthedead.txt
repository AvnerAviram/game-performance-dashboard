Fortunes of the Dead Fortunes of the Dead is a 5 reel slot game. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols on any of the 50 lines. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.79%. ONCE IN A WILD BONUS Once in a Wild Bonus is a mystery feature that can be awarded any time When awarded, the player will have one re-spin with 4 WILD symbols randomly placed Once in a Wild Bonus is not available in the Free Games Once in a Wild Bonus awards guaranteed prize of 50% of player bet SKULL BONUS The SKULL Bonus is awarded when 3 Skull symbols appear on reels 1, 3 and 5 only Choose one of the 3 Skulls to reveal a prize All prizes are awarded as displayed SIMULTANEOUS BONUSES Once in a Wild Bonus and Skull Bonus can be awarded simultaneously Once in a Wild Bonus and Free Games Feature can be awarded at the same time FREE GAMES FEATURE The Free Games Feature is awarded when 3 SCATTER occur on reels 2, 3 and 4 5 Free Games are awarded On each spin a WILD is randomly placed on the reels and remains locked until the free games are completed The feature cannot be re-triggered Free Games are played at the lines and bet of the trigger game Free Games wins are added to payline wins GAME RULES Play 50 lines The Once in a Wild feature will appear randomly awarding the player a re-spin.
