Wildfire Wins About this Game Welcome to Wildfire Wins. This is a 5 reel 5 row video slot game. It features sticky Wilds, Wildfire Respins, and Wildfire Wheels. This game has 50 lines. General Win on a line pays if the same symbol appears in succession on adjacent reels, from the leftmost position to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Bonus. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Wild symbols can create their own winning combination when landing in a payline combination. The maximum cumulative total win in Wildfire Wheels is capped at 10 000x. When the cap is reached the feature ends. Wildfire Respins In the base game, any win with at least one special symbol triggers Wildfire Respins. The special symbols are fiery versions of low symbols. When a special symbol is part of a win, it is activated. Activated special symbols are tracked on a collection meter. When a special symbol is activated, all special symbols of the same kind on the visible reel area become sticky Wilds. This special symbol will not appear for the remainder of Wildfire Respins, but normal low symbols can still appear. The sticky Wilds remain in place for the duration of the feature. The feature continues if at least one more of the special symbols is part of a win. Activating all special symbols awards a final Wildfire Respin. All wins on the final spin are multiplied by a random spin multiplier. The multipliers are x2, x3, x5, and x10. Wildfire Wheels In the base game or in Wildfire Respins, 3 Bonus symbols trigger Wildfire Wheels. During Wildfire Respins, landing a cumulative total of 3 Bonus symbols throughout the duration of the feature also triggers Wildfire Wheels. Bonus symbols appear on 1, 3, and 5 only. Wildfire Wheels is comprised of 3 spinning wheels, starting at level 1. Each wheel has 8 different segments. The first 2 levels have cash prizes on 7 wheel segments. The 8th segment grants an upgrade to the next level. The 3rd and final level has a 10 000x segment instead of an upgrade segment. When the wheel stops on a segment with a cash prize, the cash prize is collected and the segment turns into a collect segment. The feature ends when the wheel stops on a collect segment. The possible cash prizes on level 1 are 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x bet. The possible cash prizes on level 2 are 3x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x, 25x, 100x bet. The possible cash prizes on level 3 are 5x, 10x, 20x, 30x, 50x, 100x, 250x, 10 000x bet. On level 3, if the wheel stops on the 10 000x segment, a cash prize of 10 000x bet is awarded and the feature ends.
