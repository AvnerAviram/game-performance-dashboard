Lotus Land with Quick Strike Online BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 30 credits. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by total credits wagered. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. JEWEL appears on reels 2, 3, & 4 only. JEWEL substitutes for TIGER, WOMAN, ELEPHANT, SNAKE, UMBRELLA, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: TIGER, WOMAN, ELEPHANT, SNAKE, UMBRELLA, A, K, Q, J, 10, 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 FLOWER trigger 8, 12 or 20 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 FLOWER award additional 5, 8, 12 or 20 free games respectively during the free games. All JEWEL change to the following during the free games: Reel 2 Reel 3 Reel 4 JEWELx3 OR JEWELx2 JEWELx4 OR JEWELx2 JEWELx5 OR JEWELx2 JEWELx2, JEWELx3, JEWELx4 and JEWELx5 substitute for TIGER, WOMAN, ELEPHANT, SNAKE, UMBRELLA, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If each JEWELx2, JEWELx3, JEWELx4 and JEWELx5 substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pays x2, x3, x4 and x5 respectively. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Quick StrikeTM Online Quick StrikeTM Online is a two-level linked mystery progressive that is won randomly during game play. The mystery progressive win will occur within the range indicated below the progressive amount. The more a player bets the higher the chance of hitting a progressive. The chance of hitting the progressive is proportional to the amount wagered. Last modification date: 10/3/2023
