Cleopatra Queen of the Desert Win Over 3500X: Stacks Turn Wild Free spins: Any stack of 4 Ra, Bastet, Scarab or Eye of Ra symbols in a reel will expand into a Wild and fill the reel. Stacks Turn Wild: If any of the above land as a stack fully in view, they will turn wild, pay and award a respin. Substitutes for all paying symbols. If any further stacks land on the respin they will also turn wild, pay and award another respin; however, the first wild stack will now be removed. Free Spins: If Free Spins symbols land on 3 or more reels, 7 Free Spins will be awarded +1 for each Free Spins symbols. During Free Spins respins can be awarded like in the base game. Free Spins can be retriggered in the same way as the original trigger. Paytable: Values are based on current stake multiplied by the win per line for each symbol. Win Lines: This is a 10 winline game, paying from left to right for all symbols, except Cleopatra symbols. Pays on adjacent reels. A win consisting only of wilds pays left to right. Game Information: The overall payout percentage of the game is 96.30%. This game is completely random. Malfunction voids all pays. Only the highest win per line is paid. The maximum win from any spin cycle is: £250,000. If multiple wins are achieved on different winlines on any spin, the total win will be the sum of all individual wins on separate winlines.
