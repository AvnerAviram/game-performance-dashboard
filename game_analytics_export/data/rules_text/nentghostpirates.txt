Ghost Pirates™ Game Rules Ghost Pirates™ is a 5-reel, video slot that offers 243 ways to win. In addition, the game has scatter wins, Free spins and wild substitutions. There are no bet lines. You can play 3, 9, 27, 81 or 243 bet ways at 1 to 5 bet levels. The spin button plays the game at the current bet way, bet level and coin value. The bet level is set using the LEVEL selector. The coin value is set using the COIN VALUE selector. MAX BET plays the game at the maximum bet way, highest bet level, and the pre-selected coin value. AUTO automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. Win calculations are based on consecutive left to right symbol combinations. The first symbol in the win combination must be on the first reel. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE . A bet way win in coins is equal to the value shown in the PAYTABLE multiplied by the bet level. A bet way win in currency is equal to the win in coins multiplied by the coin value. In both the main game and Free Spins, Wild symbols only appear on reels 2 and 4. Wild symbol substitutes for any symbol. Coin wins on Scatter symbols are calculated by multiplying the total bet for the round by value shown in the PAYTABLE . Simultaneous wins on different bet ways are added. All wins pay on selected bet ways only, except for wins with Scatter symbols. Some features may not be available in PLAYING FOR FUN mode. Free Spins 3 or more Scatter symbols appearing anywhere on the reels in Free Spins activates additional Free Spins according to the PAYTABLE . Scatter symbols must appear in succession, starting from the leftmost reel. Free spins are played at the same bet as the round activating Free spins. Additional Free Spins can be won during Free Spins. Bet line wins in Free Spins pay win amount x3. At the end of Free Spins, the total win from Free Spins is added to any wins from the round that activated Free Spins.
