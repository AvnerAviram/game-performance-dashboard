Monopoly Travel World Tour WILD SYMBOL The WILD symbol appears only in the base game. The WILD symbol can appear on any reel and substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS symbol. BONUS SYMBOL The BONUS symbol is a Scatter symbol and can appear on any reel. Landing 2 BONUS symbols will award a random CASH prize, either 2x, 3x, 5x, 6x, 8x or 10x the total bet. Landing 3 or more BONUS symbols will trigger the BOARDING CALL feature. The BONUS symbols may also appear in some features. The outcome will then be different and as specified for those features. BOARDING CALL FEATURE From the BOARDING CALL feature screen one of the following BONUS rounds must be selected: NEW YORK - MONEY IN HAND CANCÚN - MONTEZUMA CAPE TOWN - RAGING RHINO KENTUCKY - RACE DAY NEXT FLIGHT ANYWHERE Over time, BONUS rounds may be added or removed from the above list. MONEY IN HAND MR. MONOPOLY BONUS The MR. MONOPOLY symbol is a Scatter symbol and can appear on any reel. If one or more MR. MONOPOLY symbols appear on the reels with any CASH symbols, each MR. MONOPOLY symbol will collect the multipliers displayed on each CASH symbol. They will be multiplied with the stake and added together, then any win lines will be awarded. MONEY IN HAND CASH BONUS The CASH symbols can land on any reel. They are represented by three different symbols. While they are all visually different symbols, they all act as the same symbol. Each CASH symbol is assigned a multiplier that can only be triggered by the MR. MONOPOLY symbol. CASH Blue - 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x CASH Green - 6x, 8x, 10x CASH Yellow - 15x, 20x MONTEZUMA MONTEZUMA WILD SYMBOL The MONTEZUMA WILD symbol can appear on any reel during the MONTEZUMA BONUS and substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS symbol. MONTEZUMA BONUS WHEEL On selecting CANCÚN - MONTEZUMA from the BOARDING CALL feature, the MONTEZUMA BONUS WHEEL spins and awards the initial number of Free Spins. The wheel does not reflect the odds of a prize being awarded. MONTEZUMA MULTIPLIER At the start of each normal spin, the Multiplier Wheel spins above the reels and reveals a spin multiplier. This is applied to any wins on that spin. The available multiplier values are 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x. The wheel does not reflect the odds of a prize being awarded. The multiplier value on retrigger will remain the same for the awarded amount of additional Free Spins. The Multiplier Wheel will not spin. RAGING RHINO WILD SYMBOL RAGING RHINO WILD symbol can appear on reel 2-5 during the RAGING RHINO BONUS and substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS symbol. During the RAGING RHINO feature, each WILD symbol is assigned either 2x or 3x multiplier. The multiplier is multiplicative for each WILD symbol included in that win line. WAGERS Before the start of Free Spins select how you would like to distribute the wager amount amongst the 5 horses. HORSE WILD The Horse WILD symbol can appear on any reel during the Race Day BONUS and substitutes for all symbols. For each Horse Wild that lands the corresponding horse will move along one place in the Horse Race above the reels. Once a horse has won the race the Free Spins will stop and an award for that horse will be added to your winnings in Free Spins. It is possible for more than one horse to win the race. In this case multiple horse race winnings will be awarded to your total win. BUY PASS The BUY PASS feature allows direct access to the BOARDING CALL feature. The BOARDING CALL feature is played at the selected bet level when BUY PASS™ was triggered. No trigger win is awarded when the BOARDING CALL feature is triggered via BUY PASS™. The cost of the BUY PASS™ feature is 45.8x bet value. TURBO MODE Turn ON Turbo Mode button to speed up each spin, it can be turned OFF at any point during the game. When Free Spins is awarded, Turbo Mode will automatically be disabled. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins. LEGAL NOTICES In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent. The chances of getting a particular outcome are always the same at the start of every game. The expected return for this game is between 94.01% and 94.21%. This reflects the theoretical return across many plays. BUY PASS feature has an expected return of between 93.82% and 94.39%. This is the theoretical return across many plays.
