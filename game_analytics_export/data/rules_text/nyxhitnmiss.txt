Hit 'N' Miss GAME DESCRIPTION HIT ‘N’ MISS is a tap game where the player will press the HAMMER button to try to hit the moles for a chance to win cash prizes. HOW TO PLAY The game screen consists of 9 holes and 18 unique prize panels. Tap the HAMMER button and the moles will randomly pop out of their holes. A hammer will appear on the screen and attempt to hit one or more of the moles on the screen. A prize panel will highlight for every mole that is hit and be awarded to the player. A maximum of 5 unique prizes can be won in a game round. If the hammer misses the moles, the game round will end as a non-winning game. With the current configuration of STAKE, you can win a total prize amount ranging from {MIN} to {MAX}. END OF GAME Any winnings will be displayed at the end of game play in the WIN panel. To play again, select the HAMMER button. To change the STAKE, select the plus or minus buttons. Once the HAMMER button has been selected, the STAKE will be deducted from the current balance and a new game will begin. AUTOPLAY FEATURE Select the AUTO PLAY button to choose how many games to automatically play in a row (5, 10, 15, or 20). Select the START button to begin the AUTO PLAY session with the current selected PLAY AMOUNT. Select the STOP button at any time to stop any AUTO PLAY games once the current game round has completed.
