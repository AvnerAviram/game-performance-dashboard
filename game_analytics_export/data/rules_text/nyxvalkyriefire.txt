Valkyrie Fire How to Play Game Introduction This is a 10 win line game that features a wild multiplier, fire stacked wilds and a Free Spins Bonus. The Free Spins Bonus scatter symbol also pays in the base game. Valkyrie Wild Symbol The Valkyrie symbol is both a wild symbol and a line win symbol. When featuring as a substituting wild symbol, it doubles any wins in which it participates. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered when 3 or more bonus symbols are in view at the end of a spin. The Free Spins Bonus awards 10 Free Spins. The Free Spins Bonus contains Fire Stacked Wild symbols that can appear on all reels. Fire Stacked Wilds The Fire Stacked Wilds are only available in the Free Spins Bonus. Present on every reel, these symbols substitute for all symbols. Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button.
