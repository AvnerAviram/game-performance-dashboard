Gangsta City Slot Reloaded Main Game Screen Game Objective: Gangsta City Slot is a 25-payline, 5-reel slot game with 3 symbols on either side and 4 symbols on the middle reels. When 3 or more bonus symbols appear on the reel view, the bonus game is activated and players have a chance to get any one of 4 bonus games. Free spins cannot be re-triggered within free spins. Wanted levels will award one extra re-spin to the player during regular game. Paytable Paylines Game Information: The game is played with 25 fixed lines. When you enter the game, a default total bet is set. The total BET is the number of lines multiplied by the selected BET Value. The BET VALUE can be changed by using the and buttons. SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the currently selected BET. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Payline wins are multiplied by the amount ‘bet per line’ and added to other payline wins. The highest win amount is paid on each payline. Coinciding wins on different bet lines are all added to your total. All symbols pay from left to right, except for the bonus symbol. Payouts for the highest paying symbol (Car) will be paid if 5 Wilds appear on a payline. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols, except for the bonus symbol and only highest win amount will be paid. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will update and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at the bottom of the game. The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and play-money modes, except that auto spin option will not be available in play-money mode. Wanted Level Feature: Players can get a phone call after completing a spin and the possible outcomes are: 1) Let’s Go: 1 extra re- spin is awarded to the player. 2) Not Today : No re-spin is awarded. During a re-spin, the reels are modified based on the current Wanted level. Wanted level progress can be viewed by clicking the stars under the wanted text on top left corner of the game. Wanted level progress has 5 levels: Captain, Thug, Hood Rat, The Don, Mafia Boss. Wanted levels are persistent across the player sessions. Captain: After a re-spin, a Machine Gun shoots 1 or 2 symbols to Wild on reel s 1, 2 and 3 in random positions . All wins during Captain Level are multiplied by 3. The player then moves to the next level after the box/bar is filled in current level. Thug: After a re-spin, a Baseball Bat changes one complete reel to Wilds. All wins during Thug level are multiplied by 3. The player then moves to the next level after all the boxes/bars are filled in current level. Hood Rat: After a re-spin, a Cigarette burns the symbols (A, K, Q, J) to wild. The player then moves to the next level after all the boxes/bars are filled in current level. The Don: During a re-spin, Roses will lock on reels 1 and 2 and acts as super wild symbols. Payouts will definitely be awarded according to the paytable during this level. The player then moves to the next level after all the boxes/bars are filled in current level. Mafia Boss: After a re-spin, a Revolver shoots randomly any one of the Q, K, A, Dog and Thief Symbols which will be changed to gigantic symbols on reels 2, 3 and 4 . All wins during Mafia Boss level are multiplied by 3. Wanted levels will be reset to the initial position once the boxes/bars are filled in the current level. Bonus Feature: 3 or more symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the bonus feature. After clicking the start button, a bonus is awarded based on the stop position of the navigator. The bonus round cannot re-trigger within any bonus round. Bonus games (features) are played at the lines and bet of the triggering spin. All winnings during bonus games are added to your total winnings. South Side Spins: Choose one of the briefcases to reveal 6, 9 or 12 free spins. For every free spin, an additional re-spin is awarded. During this re-spin, reels 1 & 5 are locked and reels 2, 3 and 4 will spin. West Side Turf wars: A city map is shown with eight “?” marks for player to choose one by one until collect appears. All region wins add up to total winnings. Helicopter Wilds: 10 free spins are awarded to the player. For every free spin, a Helicopter will appear and shoot 1 or 2 symbols to Wild on reels 1, 2 or 3. All payouts in this bonus game are multiplied by 3. Free Spins Town: 5 free spins are awarded. Reels 2 ,3 and 4 will be locked with any one gigantic symbol per spin. All payouts in this bonus game are multiplied by 3. Car& Wild symbols will not be gigantic symbol in this bonus game .
