Captain Payback 2 RTP: 96.5% VOLATILITY: 9.2 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Click the Spin button to spin the reels The game automatically plays max lines times the bet per line. All 6 reels are always in play. Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild Symbol 1 can appear with the following credit values: Reel 2 – 25 to 5000 credits, Reel 3 – 25 to 5000 credits, Reel 4 – 25 to 2000 credits, Reel 5 – 25 to 1000 credits. Credit values have been pre-multiplied by the bet per line. For each game, all credit values on a reel are the same. At the end of each game, win the sum of the displayed credit values. Wild Symbol 1 and Wild Symbol 2 are wild and substitute for all other symbols except the Free Games Bonus Symbol. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 or more Free Games Bonus Symbol appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 starts the Free Games Bonus. 3 Free Games Bonus Symbol awards 8 free games. 4 Free Games Bonus Symbol awards 15 free games. Free Games Bonus Symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. Free Games Bonus Symbol is a scatter symbol. Each Free Games Wild 1 Symbol on the reels may appear as Free Games Wild 2 Symbol with a credit value. Free Games Wild 2 Symbol can appear with the following credit values: Reel 2 – 25 to 5000 credits. Reel 3 – 25 to 2000 credits. Reel 4 – 25 to 2000 credits. Reel 5 – 25 to 5000 credits. Credit values have been pre-multiplied by the bet per line. For each free game, all credit values on a reel are the same. At the end of each free game, win the sum of the displayed credit values. Free Games Wild 2 Symbol with a credit value contributes to Scatter Bucks pays. Free Games Wild 2 Symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Wild 1 Symbol does not contribute to Scatter Bucks pays. Free Games Wild 1 Symbol and Free Games Wild 2 Symbol are wild and substitute for all other symbols. Free Games Wild 1 Symbol or Free Games Wild 2 Symbol appearing on reels 3, 4, or 5 expand left and substitutes for all symbols in the same position on each reel from the initial symbol, up to and including reel 2. Free Games Wild 2 Symbol with a credit value will duplicate its credit values on each symbol position to which it expands. If a position has two or more credit values on it, that position will display the sum of those credit values. Wins are evaluated after all Free Games Wild 1 Symbol and Free Games Wild 2 Symbol expand. Free Games Wild 1 Symbol and Free Games Wild 2 Symbol appear only on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain Last modification date: 7/8/2022
