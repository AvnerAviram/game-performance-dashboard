Dolphins Pearl deluxe Main Game Your goal is to land 5 identical symbols along one of the 10 paylines. In a regular game the symbols in a winning pattern will start on the left side and continue on to the right. In order to win, the symbols must be directly next to each other without any other symbols between them. The dolphin (joker) substitutes for all other symbols except for scatter symbols (pearl). Scatter symbols count regardless of their position on the reels, once they appear twice Free Spins Landing 3 or more Pearl symbols in any location triggers 15 free games. You can increase your winnings many times over. The stake used in the last regular game will be used in the free games and winnings are tripled. Furthermore, you can win additional free games by landing 3 or more Pearl symbols during a free game. Trigger: Any 3, 4 or 5 scattered PEARL trigger 15 free games. Top Prize:27000 times bet per line.
