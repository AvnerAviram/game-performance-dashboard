Firelight II TWIN SPINS FEATURE A win of 3, 4 or 5 awards 6, 9 or 12 free games respectively. Press the PLAY button to start the TWIN SPINS FEATURE. Twin reels are a pair of reels that display the same symbols in adjacent positions when spun. During the free games, reels 1 and 2 pair up to become twin reels and reels 4 and 5 pair up to become twin reels. Bonus reels are in play during free games. TWIN SPINS FEATURE can be triggered again during the TWIN SPINS FEATURE. Bet is the same as the game that triggered the TWIN SPINS FEATURE. GAME RULES All Pays XTRA REEL POWER cost is 40 Coins. Bet is 40 Coins x Coin Size. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Scatter wins are added to XTRA REEL POWER wins. RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 4/2/2024
