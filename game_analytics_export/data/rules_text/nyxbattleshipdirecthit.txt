Battleship Direct Hit HOW TO PLAY MISSILE is a scatter symbol. WILD is wild for all symbols except for scatter symbols. Game Introduction BATTLESHIP Direct Hit! is an exciting immersive Any Ways game based on the classic Hasbro game. Win up to 117,649 ways in this thrilling slot game. Sink ships to win free spins and jackpot prizes! When MISSILE symbols land, they are launched towards the ships above the reels. Sink entire ships to win the award associated with each ship. This game has 6 reels with up to 7 rows. The number of ways is randomised every spin. Select your bet: To adjust the BET, use the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons to increase or decrease the size of your bet. Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Cascading Wins Throughout the game, symbols that are part of winning combinations are destroyed and sunk. Symbols from above will drop down to fill empty spaces and new symbols will fall to fill any remaining spaces. MISSILE and WILD symbols will also undergo cascading mechanics. Turret Fire At the end of any base game spin, before the pays are evaluated, turrets can unleash a barrage onto the reels, replacing low paying symbols with higher paying symbols. Free Spins Win free spins by sinking ships in the base game. Land MISSILE symbols during free spins to win jackpot prizes and additional free spins. Legal Wins occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far left reel. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Wins on multiple ways are added together. Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
