Speed Cash Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules This is a 3-reel slot machine with one payline. Only the highest win is paid. The wild symbol substitutes for any other symbol to help comprise winning combinations. Win the progressive jackpot by betting the max bet and hitting three wild symbols on the payline. The RTP is 94.12% including progressive jackpot. The RTP is 87.92% when not playing with max bet. Click SPIN to start the game. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the prize table. 1. Review the paytable to see how the jackpot can be won. 2. A percentage of the bet is contributed to the progressive jackpots. 3. The displayed value of the jackpot might differ from the actual value due to delays in the communication with the server. 4. If two players win the jackpot at approximately the same time the first winner will receive the full value of the jackpot and the second winner will receive the re-seeded value of the jackpot. Actions Increase Denomination - Click + to increase the denomination of each coin. Decrease Denomination - Click - to decrease the denomination of each coin. Bet One - Bets one coin.
