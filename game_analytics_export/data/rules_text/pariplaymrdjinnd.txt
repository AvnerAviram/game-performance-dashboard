Mr. Djinn Wonders General Mr. Djinn’s Wonders is a real money Match 3 game. The game features three different bonuses. All wins are multiples of the bet. The theoretical payout of the game (RTP) is 95.38%. How to Play Blast tiles clusters by pressing on one of the tiles in the cluster to fill the symbols bars, win bonus games or jackpot prize. Tile Clusters include 3 or more identical tiles connected horizontally or vertically. Blasted tiles are removed from the board and are being replaced by new tiles from above. Choices of which clusters to blast while playing the game do not affect the win result. Bonus Features Free Games Blasting a cluster of Free Games tiles awards 5 always-winning free games. There are no special tiles during the free games. Mystery Lamp Blasting a cluster of Bonus tiles triggers the bonus game. Choose one of three genie lamps to reveal a prize. Sultan Treasure Blasting a cluster of Sultan Treasure tiles grants a grand prize of x1000. Disconnection Policy Due to the game’s communication medium nature, the player may experience interruptions, delays, or a termination of gameplay. In the event of an unexpected interruption or termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. Should a disconnection occur after a bet has been placed, but before the current game is complete, in your next game session you will be prompted to start the game you played before the interruption. Any malfunction in the game voids all payouts. Last modification date: 9/4/2025
