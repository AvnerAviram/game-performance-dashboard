Lucha Loot RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: 11.8 - MEDIUM GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play. Select Bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels. Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits stated. All 5 reels are always in play. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except the Pick Bonus symbol. The Loot Line is a separate bonus payline composed of Loot Line symbols appearing in any position on the reels from left to right. Wins on the Loot Line are subject to multiplier. Loot Line Symbols are indicated by [Loot Overlay]. 5 of a kind Wild wins are not available in Loot Line wins. PICK BONUS 3 Pick Bonus symbols appearing in any positions on reels 1, 3, and 5 starts the Pick Bonus. The Pick Bonus Symbol is a Scatter Symbol. The Pick Bonus awards 1 pick. Pick Bonus symbols appear only on reels 1, 3, and 5. The Pick Bonus features 3 options Choose a Pick Bonus Option to reveal an award. Each Pick Bonus Option awards 5 to 25 times the total initiating bet. Last modification date: 10/6/2022
