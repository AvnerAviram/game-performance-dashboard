The player can skip the gamble feature and collect their initial winnings by pressing the “Collect” button, Spin button or the spacebar. If the gamble option is selected, a pop-up will appear with a playing card, face down: The player then has two options: Guess the color of the card (black or red). A correct guess awards a 2x multiplier. Guess the suit of the card. A correct guess awards a 4x multiplier. After each correct guess, the player can collect their winnings or re-gamble their winnings. There is no limit to the number of times the player can re-gamble. If the player does not guess correctly, the gamble feature ends and all winnings from the initial round that triggered the feature and the gamble feature winnings are forfeited. --> Triple Bonus Wheel The bonus wheel is triggered by landing three bonus symbols anywhere on the reels or with the Buy Bonus feature. First, the player gets to spin the outer wheel which displays cash prize values, some of which have an arrow above the value. After spinning the wheel, if it comes to rest on a value without an arrow, the player wins the amount shown and the bonus round ends. The player can then collect their winnings or initiate the gamble option. If the wheel stops on a value that has an arrow on it, the player then advances to the second wheel with higher winning amounts. Once again, some values on the second wheel have arrows too. If the wheel stops on a value without an arrow, the player wins the amount shown and can collect the prize or opt to play the gamble option. If the value shown has an arrow, the player advances to the third and final wheel. The third wheel contains only cash prizes. When the wheel comes to rest, the player wins the amount shown which is then added to the prizes won from the 1st and 2nd wheel. The player then has the choice to collect their total winnings or to play the gamble option. --> Buy Bonus The Buy Bonus option allows the player to activate the Triple Bonus Wheel feature at any time during the base game by paying a certain amount. A “Buy Bonus” button is active in the base game. Selecting the “Buy Bonus” button opens a pop-up that shows the amount that needs to be paid to immediately initiate the Triple Bonus Wheel. The amount of credits to be payed is a multiplier of the current bet amount. --> Playing the Game To play a round, configure your bet using the Bet Plus / Minus buttons, and press or the “Spacebar” to submit the bet and initiate the round. A second click on the or the “Spacebar” will fast-forward the round to the round results, or in the case of multiple lines winning, will skip the win animation and start the next round. The round can also be initiated using the Autobet / Autoplay . Payout Rules There are different coin values that can be set by using the COIN VALUE + and - buttons. COIN VALUE represents the value in currency of each coin. There are different bet amounts that can be set by using the BET + and - buttons. BET represents the number of coins that the player bets per round. Winning combinations are paid per line in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. A line win in credits or currency is equal to the value shown in the PAYTABLE, multiplied by bet per line amount. Winning combinations and payouts are calculated according to the game's “Paytable”, which can be accessed through the Info button . All symbol multipliers can be found in the Symbol screen in the Info pages. The prizes are calculated using the following formula: Bet amount, multiplied by the symbol multiplier, divided by the number of paylines (10). Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added up.
