Aztec Gold Game Objective: Aztec Gold is a five-reel, 50 payline slot game with a sixth reel incorporated on the left side of the screen to denote the possible “Free spins and multiplier”. The sixth reel (The Aztec Gold Free Spins Reel) spins along with the other 5 reels and will display the number of free spins and multiplier if the feature game is triggered. The Aztec Gold feature is invoked when three or more scattered Temple symbols appear anywhere in view. The free spin feature can re-trigger within free spin. Game Information: Game is played with 50 variable lines. SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the current selected BET and LINES. Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE. Payline wins are multiplied by the amount ‘bet per line’ and gets added to other pay line wins. Only the highest winning combination made with at least one non-wild symbol is awarded on each selected lines. Coinciding wins on different bet lines are all added to your total. Scatter (represented by Temple symbol) wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins. All symbols pay from left to right, except the scatter symbol which pays anywhere in view. On the winning line the winning symbols will slightly animate with bounce in animation and the win line will be colored and remaining will be grayed out. The Ticker messages will appear on bottom of game screen. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will increase, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at the bottom of the game. You can increase the bet per line by clicking the button to the right of 'BET', or you can decrease the bet by clicking the button to the left. Bet and lines will not get adjusted to the next possible bet, if your balance drops below total bet placed, then click on button to deposit. Setting 'LINES': Fifty lines are selected by default. You can decrease the number of lines by clicking the button to the left of 'LINES', or select more lines by clicking the button to the right. MAX BET: To play with MAX BET, click on . If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled. The number of selected lines are multiplied by the 'bet per line'. The total bet amount is displayed beside the 'BET' label, which can be found at the bottom of the game screen. Paytable: The Paytable can be viewed either by clicking the button located in bottom of game screen beside ‘BET’ label or by clicking button located at the bottom of the screen beside the Line meter. The prizes displayed in the Paytable are per unit bet, and are shown as multipliers. To get the effective payout per line, multiply the respective prize with the bet per line. You can go back to the game by clicking on the button on the Paytable screen. Clicking on the button will open the rules screen along with Paytable and allow you to read the game rules. Spin: Click on SPIN icon to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations on the selected lines and any scatter wins will be paid according to the Paytable. Result: Wins are indicated by colorized paylines animated symbols, and in case of a scatter win, by animated symbols, with the total win amount being shown at the bottom of the screen beside 'WIN'. Game Rules : Player can play the game from 1 to 50 lines. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Only the highest winning combination made with at least one non-wild symbol is awarded on each selected lines. SPIN begins the game with the currently selected BET and LINES. MAX BET selects 50 lines and maximum bet value. Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE. Payline wins are multiplied by the amount in bet meter. Highest win amount is paid on each selected pay line. Coinciding wins on different lines are all added to your total. Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins. All symbols pay Left to Right, except scattered Temple symbol, which pay anywhere in view. Aztec Gold Logo substitutes for all other symbols except the scattered Temple symbol. Aztec Gold Logo appears only on reels 1, 2, 3, and 4. The game and game rules are identical both in real-money and play-money mode. Feature game play: The Aztec Gold feature is triggered when 3 or more scattered Temple symbols appear anywhere in view. If 3 or more Scattered Temple symbols are in view, then the number of free spins and multiplier denoted against the indicator (Arrow) on the sixth reel are awarded and the free spins are played. The number of free spins and free spins multiplier are denoted at random. Number of free spins awarded with each trigger, range from 8 to 25 and the multiplier awarded with each trigger, ranges from 2 to 10. All prizes during free spins are multiplied by the denoted free spins multiplier. Free spins can retrigger free spins and award the same number as originally awarded when triggered. Free spins are played at the same number of lines and bet per line of the triggering game. Free spin wins are added to payline and scatter wins.
