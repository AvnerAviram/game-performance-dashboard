Steinhaus Game Theme and Graphics The beer is always flowing and the steins are always full in Stein Haus, this new 50-line video slot. The 'Bonus' symbols will lead you to the Free Games where the beers are even bigger and better. Large 'Steins' will move up and down the reels during the feature, stopping in random positions for each game, and substituting to create more chances to win. Experience the fun and festivities of Oktoberfest all year round. Main Game Play up to 50 lines, where all prizes are for combinations left to right, except scattered 'Bonus'. Scattered 'Bonus' symbols pay in any position on screen. 3, 4 or 5 'Bonus' symbols award 10 Free Games with large floating Wild symbols. Wild beer 'Steins' substitute for all symbols except 'Bonus'. Moving Wilds Feature Trigger: 3, 4 or 5 scattered 'Bonus' symbols trigger 10 Free Games. During the Free Games the triggering 'Bonus' symbols expand to be a large Wild 'Steins', each covering 2 positions on the reels. These Wilds move up and down the reels, stopping in random positions for each free game, and substitute for all reel positions they cover. Feature hit rate: 170-230 games. Feature top prize: 1000x total bet. Rules 'Stein' substitutes for all symbols, except 'Bonus' All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines, excluding scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. RTP is 95.26% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
