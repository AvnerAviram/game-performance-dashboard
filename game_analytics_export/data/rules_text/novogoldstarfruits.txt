Gold Star Fruits Main Game The game starts with the base game. The base game is played on 10 lines (fixed). Wins pay left to right and right to left. The super game is triggered when you win 4 x your bet or more. Super Game The default (super) bet is 4 x base bet. The bet is taken from the super meter. Max. 50 super spins can be played. Jokers expand and substitute for all symbols Rules All prizes for combinations of a kind. Highest prize only per pay direction. 5 of a kind prizes paid once. RTP:95% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/18/2020
