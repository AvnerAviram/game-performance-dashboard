Cluedo Spinning Detectives The objective of the game is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. How To Play Select Your Total Bet To adjust the total bet, use the '+' and '-' buttons. WIN LINES The default win lines is 40. The win lines are not adjustable in this game. TOTAL BET This meter displays the total amount wagered per spin. START THE GAME Click the SPIN (the button showing an arrow) button to start your game. AUTOPLAY If this game contains an AUTOPLAY function that allows the player to select a number of spins to be played out autonomously, without any further player input. All necessary options, as determined by jurisdictional requirements, need to be configured by the player prior to setting AUTOPLAY in motion. To begin AUTOPLAY, select the AUTOPLAY button from the game screen. Once the AUTOPLAY menu has opened, the player will configure AUTOPLAY to their desired specifications. The selection “NUMBER OF AUTOSPINS” will determine the number of spins to be played automatically. When the spins configured by the player’s selection in “NUMBER OF AUTOSPINS” are completed, AUTOPLAY will stop. In certain jurisdictions, players will be required to set the options “STOP WHEN LOST MORE THAN …” and “STOP ON A SINGLE WIN OVER …” in addition to the desired number of spins played automatically. The selection “STOP WHEN LOST MORE THAN …” will stop AUTOPLAY after a player has lost the total amount selected. The selection “STOP ON A SINGLE WIN OVER …” will stop AUTOPLAY after a player has won the total amount selected on a single spin. AUTOPLAY will stop when the conditions met in any of “NUMBER OF AUTOSPINS”, “STOP WHEN LOST MORE THAN …”, and/or “STOP ON A SINGLE WIN OVER …” are met. Once the player has configured AUTOPLAY to their desired specifications, they will select the START button, which will set the reels into motion. At any time after this point, the player can select the STOP button to stop the remaining AUTOPLAY spins. Once stopped, AUTOPLAY will need to be reconfigured through the AUTOPLAY menu in order to take advantage of this feature. The player can exit the AUTOPLAY menu at any time by selecting the CANCEL button. A bonus event will stop the Autoplay. WIN This meter displays the total amount won for any single spin. HELP All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiples of the bet per line. PAYS LOGO is wild for all symbols except JACKPOT, CONSERVATORY, BILLIARD ROOM, LIBRARY, KITCHEN, DINING ROOM, MANSION, and CARDS. Line wins must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Scatter pays are paid for symbols appearing in any position on the reels. Only the highest win paid per line. Scatter pays are paid for symbols appearing in any position on the reels. ROOM FEATURES Room Reel Sets are shown beside the Main Reel Set. Room Reel Sets are triggered when the respective Room symbol appears on the Main Reel Set. When triggered, the Room Reel set will move to center and replace the Main Reel set. One spin on the triggered Room Reel Set is awarded. Each Room symbol awards 1x total bet. 5 scattered MAGNIFYING GLASS award that Room’s Jackpot. Conservatory Feature GOLD WHITE, GOLD PEACOCK, and GOLD GREEN replace MRS. WHITE, MRS. PEACOCK, and MR. GREEN respectively, and pay mixed on the Conservatory Room Reel Set. Billiard Room Feature If WHITE BALL WILD appears on reel 3, 2 to 12 ORANGE BALL WILD, BLUE BALL WILD, PURPLE BALL WILD, RED BALL WILD, and YELLOW BALL WILD will spew across reels 1, 2, 4, and 5. Library Feature While the book moves across the reels, 1 or 2 full wild reels of BOOK WILD are added. Kitchen Feature 2-15 KNIFE WILD are randomly added. Dining Room Feature 2 to 5 full reels of POISON WILD are added. FREE SPINS BONUS 3 scattered MANSION on reels 1, 3, and 5 of the Main Reel Set trigger the Free Spins Bonus and award 7 Free Spins. Free Spins begin with the Conservatory Room Reel Set. If ENVELOPE WILD lands scattered on Reel 5 of the Conservatory Room, Billiard Room, or Library Room Reel Sets, then pick one of the two SECRET CLUE cards to receive an additional Free Spin and the chance to advance one or more rooms from the current position. SPINNING DETECTIVE BONUS 3 scattered CARDS on reels 1, 2, and 3 of the Main Reel Set trigger the SPINNING DETECTIVE BONUS. Pick 7 cards to reveal suspects, weapons, rooms, 2x multipliers, or the Master Slot Detective until all empty card berths in the Clues Found area are filled. There are two 2x multipliers in the pick field that, if selected, award 2 times the total accumulated bonus win. If both are selected, it awards 4 times the total accumulated bonus win.
