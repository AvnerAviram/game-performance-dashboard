Fonsi Nieto Deluxe Racing Life PRIZES MAIN GAME 10 pay lines. Line wins are paid from left to right. Wild figure acts as a wild and substitutes for all figures except Bonus figures. Maximum prize is 5500* bet. Higher amounts can be accumulated in the case of Free Spins. FREE SPINS By obtaining 3 or more Wild figures, 10 Free Spins are awarded. These Free Spins do not consume balance and all the prizes obtained accumulate. You can obtain more Free Spins within Free Spins. EXPANDING SYMBOL A special EXPANDING SYMBOL is randomly chosen to help you win more on free spins. ¡Watch it expand to cover the reel when more prizes are in store! ¡Symbols can appear anywhere along active line to win! BONUS GAMES SELECT BONUS GAME Three 'Bonus' figures, or more, give access to the mini-game selection screen. Several entries to the mini-game selection screen will unlock, one after the other, accesses to bonus games. RACING BONUS GAME If the first minigame is selected, the racing minigame will be accessed. 4 drivers are shown, each one has their own lane. Each lane has an assigned prize. Each turn the runners will move the number of positions indicated on the marker of their color. The final prize will be the prize corresponding to the runner who crosses the finish line. Maximum prize is 120 * bet. ROUTE BONUS GAME Selecting the second minigame will access the tour minigame. A tour of different cities is shown. In each round you will get a number of cities to advance through or the word EXIT. The final prize will be the one corresponding to the end of the tour or to the city where EXIT appears. Maximum prize is 140 * bet. TOKIO BONUS GAME Selecting the third minigame will access the Tokyo minigame. Three traffic lights appear in each round. The player must select one of the traffic lights to get a prize or EXIT. The final prize will be the accumulated value for each round until the word EXIT appears. The maximum prize is 150 * bet.
