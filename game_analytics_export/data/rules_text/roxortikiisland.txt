Tiki Island Game Features Puffer Fish Bonus In each round you're presented with 3 puffer fish. Simply click on a fish to win a prize. But watch out - if you choose a fish without a prize the bonus ends. All wins are multiplied by the total number of bonus initiating lines. Coconut Bonus Pick coconuts and win prizes! Continue picking until you reveal "Collect" and collect 2 coins for each remaining coconut. All wins are multiplied by the total number of bonus initiating lines. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 96.29% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $96.29 of wins. Rules Total bet is the coin size multiplied by the number of lines played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 6/19/2024
