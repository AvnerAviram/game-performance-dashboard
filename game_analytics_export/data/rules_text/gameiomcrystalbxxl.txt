Crystal Burst XXL Crystal Burst XXL is an action-packed Fruit Slot with special Wild- and Respin-Feature, which promises explosive excitement. An extended 5x4 reel set and a 2-way-winning-Feature create a maximum of tension and additional chances of winning. PLAY NOW Game Reels: 5 Paylines: 10/20/30/40 selectable "Gem" is Wild and substitutes for all symbols. "Gem" is Expanding Wild and expands on the reel. "Gem" appears on reel 2/3/4 only. "Gem" triggers a respin. The maximum number of respins is 3. Gamble Card gamble The card gamble can be entered by clicking the gamble button with the cards symbol. The win can be gambled by betting on the of the next card. If the of the drawn card matches the choice, the win is doubled. In case the s don’t match, the risked win is lost. Ladder gamble The ladder gamble can be entered by clicking the gamble button with the ladder symbol. In case of win, you will land on the highlighted step above your current one, in case of loss on the highlighted lower one. Gambling is not possible for high wins or in autoplay mode. Rules All wins begin with the leftmost or rightmost reel and pay left to right or right to left for identical symbols on adjacent reels. All wins count on payed lines only. Wins on different lines are added. Return to Player (RTP) This game has a return to player 96.16%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Wins and winning combinations are shown in the paytable. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
