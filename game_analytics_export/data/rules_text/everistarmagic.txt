Star Magic Game Rules REEL MATCH(TM) On random base game spins, 2-5 reels are randomly selected to sync up and stop with their reels symbols matching across the selected reels. Free Spin Bonus can be triggered during Reel Match. FREE SPIN BONUS Bonus symbol is a scattered symbol. 3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols landing scattered on the reels awards a prize of 2x, 5x, or 20x total bet in credits respectively plus 6 free spins. The Bonus symbol credit award is added to payline wins. The Reel Match feature is active and triggers on every free spin. Retriggers are available with the same triggers, and award as outlined above. Maximum number of free spins is 90. The Free Spin Bonus uses an alternate set of reels. PAYLINES ADDITIONAL INFORMATION MENU Press to open and close game settings and view game information.
