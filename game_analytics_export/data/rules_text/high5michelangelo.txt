Michelangelo VOLATILITY: 5.2 GAME RULES • Malfunction voids all pays and plays. • Select Bet • Click the Spin button to spin the reel. • Line wins must appear on played lines. • Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. • Only the highest win is paid on each line. • Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. • Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. • Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. • All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. • Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels. FREE GAMES BONUS • 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position starts the Free Games Bonus. • The Free Games Bonus uses the same reels. • Free Games Wild and Free Games Wild 2 symbol are wild and substitute for all symbols. • Winning combinations must contain at least one symbol other than Free Games Wild and Free Games Wild 2 symbols. • Free Games Bonus symbol appears only on reels 1, 2, and 3. • Free Games Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. • Free Games Wild 2 symbol appears only on reels 1, 2, and 3. • Free games automatically play on max lines at the same bet as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. • Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. • Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. • All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. • Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels.
