Chiquito Halloween MAIN GAME To access the upper game, it is necessary to have a sufficient number of bonuses and credits.\nwhen a prize is won, except the blue seven, portions of this prize may be risked in the game of risk which, is automatically accessed. When risking a portion, we can get double that portion or an equivalent value in bonuses. If the player does not want to risk that portion, they can collect it by pressing collect, and if they hold down the collect button, they can collect the rest of the prize without subjecting it to risk. UPPER GAME In this game, the player decides if they want to claim the prize by pressing the collect button or risk it by pressing the play button. By risking the prize, we can obtain a higher prize in the prize plan or an equivalent amount in bonuses. Once the limit for the three lemons symbols prize is exceeded, the game changes to a raise-lower type game, where the possible prizes in the prize plan can be higher or lower. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. AUTO When nudges are activated, it allows you to rotate the reels automatically, always resulting in the best option for the player. If the button is permanently lit, this means that the auto option is activated. Otherwise, if the button is flashing, this indicates that the auto option is not activated. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. AUTO PLAY Activate the risk function in main and upper game. The maximum prize that can be obtained in a minigame is the maximum prize of the bet in which the game has been made. THE MINI-GAMES ARE ACCESSED AT RANDOM WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PRIZE LINES, BY ACCUMULATING ALL THE GHOSTS, OR VIA THE GRIJANDER CEMETERY GAME.
