Eagle Ridge CashLink 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 1- per line(cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes (Bonus symbols) Bonus round No Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 94.00% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the CashLink Collect & Bonus , to form winning combinations. 3.2. CashLink Collect The CashLink Collect symbol can land on any spin and will collect all Cash Coins & Jackpot Coins in view. The CashLink Collect symbol can also act as an extra Bonus symbol . Cash Coin values are: 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x and 10x. Jackpot Coins values are: 20x ( Mini ), 50x ( Maxi ), 100x ( Major ) and 200x ( Grand ). 3.3. Eagle Modifiers Soaring Eagle : The Eagle can multiply each Cash Coins value by x5. If a CashLink Collect symbol is on screen, all Cash Coins values are collected. Wild Eagle : The Eagle can drop 3-5 Wild symbols onto the reels. Bonus Eagle : The Eagle can drop extra Bonus symbols to trigger Free Spins . 3.4. Free Spins Any 3 or more Bonus symbols awards Free Spins . 3 Bonus symbols = 10 Free Spins 4 Bonus symbols = 15 Free Spins 5 Bonus symbols = 20 Free Spins A CashLink Collect symbol can also act as an extra Bonus symbol to trigger Free Spins . During Free Spins , CashLink Collect symbols are sticky and will stay on screen. Multiple CashLink Collect symbols can land during Free Spins , and each will inidually collect any Cash Coins or Jackpot Coins in view. During Free Spins , every Bonus symbol on screen with a CashLink Collect symbol adds +2 extra Free Spins . 4. Other game buttons 4.1. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play.
