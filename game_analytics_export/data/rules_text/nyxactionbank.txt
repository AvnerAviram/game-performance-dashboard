Action Bank Games Rules Game Introduction Action Bank is a new five reel slot that features freespins and a Big Bank Bonus. How to play Select your stake - If you wish to adjust this, click the STAKE/LINE '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Win line s The default win line selection is 20 win lines. Start the game Press SPIN to initiate the game. The game contains an AUTOPLAY function that allows the player to select a quantity of games for continual play. Select your desired number of spins by clicking on the numbered buttons that are revealed when you press AUTOPLAY. Click 'Stop' to cancel auto play. Autoplay is stopped when a bonus event occurs. Help All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the HELP button. Free Spins Bonus Each reel that contains a losing 'O' symbol has a chance of transforming in a vault scatter symbol. Five vault scatter symbols awards a quantity of free spins and the chance to gamble those free spins or a chance to win the Big Bank Bonus. Big Bank Bonus Select a vault to reveal a Sapphire, Diamond or Ruby. Keep choosing gems until you have 3 of a kind to win the associated multiplier value. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.16%. This RTP represents the long-term expected payback of the game which has been calculated and verified by an independent testing company.
