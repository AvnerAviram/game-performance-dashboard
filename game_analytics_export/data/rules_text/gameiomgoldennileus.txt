Golden Nile Golden Nile has the Diamond District Bonus, featuring 3 Jackpot prizes. The game utilizes a cash on reels concept that is awarded via 5-of-a-kind DIAMOND symbol. During Free Spins, a DIAMOND multiplier symbol may appear on reel 5, providing explosive wins. This Egyptian theme has a strong chase in the DIAMOND symbol. Royals are Streamline A standard and used as the low pay symbols. Malfunction voids all pays and play. Diamond District Bonus DIAMOND symbol on reel 3 contains either various CREDIT prizes or one of the JACKPOT prizes. 5-of-a-kind DIAMOND symbols award the CREDIT/JACKPOT prizes appeared on Reel 3. Free Spins Bonus 3, 4, or 5 BONUS on consecutive reels, starting with the leftmost reel, awards 7, 12, or 20 Free Spins, respectively. A scatter prize is awarded upon entry into Free Spins, dependent on the number of triggering BONUS symbols. During the Free Spins, a DIAMOND symbol on reel 5 may appear with a 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x, or 10x multiplier, which multiplies the Diamond District Bonus award for that combination.
