Wonder Rose BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. FLOWER appears on reel 3 only and substitutes for WOMAN, STATUE, JEWEL, CAT, BIRD, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM 1st, 2nd, 4th and 5th reels contain a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: WOMAN, STATUE, JEWEL, CAT, BIRD, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3 or more HEART trigger the feature. Select one of the following free game features. 20 free games: All FLOWER change to FLOWER2X, FLOWER3X or FLOWER5X when part of a winning combination. 10 free games: All FLOWER change to FLOWER5X, FLOWER8X or FLOWER10X when part of a winning combination. 5 free games: All FLOWER change to FLOWER10X, FLOWER15X or FLOWER30X when part of a winning combination. FLOWER2X, FLOWER3X, FLOWER5X, FLOWER8X, FLOWER10X, FLOWER15X and FLOWER30X substitute for WOMAN, STATUE, JEWEL, CAT, BIRD, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If either FLOWER2X, FLOWER3X, FLOWER5X, FLOWER8X, FLOWER10X, FLOWER15X or FLOWER30X substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X, 5X, 8X, 10X, 15X or 30X respectively. Different reels are used during the free games. Any 3 or more HEART re-trigger the selected free game feature during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
