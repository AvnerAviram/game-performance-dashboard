Diamond Mystery - 40 Charming 7's Game Theme and Graphics 40 Charming 7's is a 5x4, 40 lines slot game with glossy fruits, scattered stars and a fourlevel Diamond Mystery™ Jackpot Feature. Main Game Wins pay from left to right on win lines, scatters pay in any position. The Diamond Mystery™ Feature is triggered randomly on any non-winning spin. Seven (Wild) symbols substitute for all symbols except scattered Stars. Diamond Mystery™ Feature The Diamond Mystery™ Feature uses four mystery levels. Each mystery level has specific gems. The base win of each jackpot depends on your stake. The base win is 5.000 times your stake for the Diamond Jackpot, 1.250 times your stake for the Red Gem Jackpot, 200 times your stake for the Blue Gem Jackpot, and 50 times your stake for the Green Gem Jackpot. Green Gem - 1 st level (lowest jackpot value) Blue Gem - 2 nd level Red Gem - 3 rd level Diamond - 4 th and highest level Once triggered, the player is taken to the Jackpot Cards game where he/she can pick one of 12 face-down cards. The player picks cards until 3 matching gem symbols have been revealed. The Diamond Mystery™ level corresponding to the revealed gems will be awarded. Rules Seven (Wild) substitute for all symbols except stars (scatter). All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per scatter combination Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Last modification date: 5/2/2025
