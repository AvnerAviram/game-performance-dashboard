Lion Carnival BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most column to the right on adjacent columns except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. a player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. FLOWER [WILD] appears on columns 2, 3, 4 and 5. Substitutes for GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION, BLUE LION, A, K, Q, J and 10. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION and BLUE LION before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION and BLUE LION can only appear on the reels when selected as the Action Stacked Symbol. MYSTERY MULTIPLIER FEATURE Mystery Multiplier Feature occurs randomly during the primary game. At the beginning of the feature, the multipliers and their positions are selected randomly. The randomly selected multipliers are only applied to GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION or BLUE LION and will only be displayed if GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION or BLUE LION lands in any of the randomly selected positions. All multipliers appearing after the reels stop will be multiplied together. Any GOLD LION, GREEN LION, PURPLE LION or BLUE LION wins are multiplied by the total multiplier. The range of the total multiplier is x1 to x144. Different reels are used during the feature. FLOWER [WILD] does not appear during the feature. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 BALL [SCATTER] trigger 5, 8 or 12 free games respectively. Any 6 or more BALL [SCATTER] trigger 20 free games. Mystery Multiplier Feature is always active during the free game feature. Additional free games can be won during the free games. Different reels are used during the feature. FLOWER [WILD] does not appear during the feature. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Theoretical Return to the player is 94%.
