Main Game Your aim in Chilli El Toro™ is to land 3 or more matching symbols on adjacent reels. Chill i El Toro™ has a Multiplier Feature at the top of the reels, 3 random Multipliers are displayed above 3 random symbols, and change on a per round basis. The symbols are awarded the multiplier shown above them for that game only. Multiplier values can be up to 25x. Any symbols can be displayed below a multiplier except BONUS and WILD. The Multiplier Feature is active for all games. Free Games Feature The BONUS (Scatter) symbol is the key to the Free Games round. Land three scattered BONUS (Scatter) symbols and the Free Games Feature is triggered with 10 Free Games and a multiplier starting at x2 for all wins. Free Games can be re-triggered. During the Free Games Feature 2, 3, 4 or 5 Scatter triggers 2, 3, 4 or 5 more Free Games, respectively, and increases the Free Games multiplier by 1. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except BONUS (Scatter) symbols. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per Scatter combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
