Dragon Stone Game Rules 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications: Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 1 - per line (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes (Dragon Stone Symbols) Bonusround No Free spins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.00% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note: In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: 1.Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value and the number of coins to bet per line. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. 2.Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES 3.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Dragon Stone symbols, to form winning combinations. Note: If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. The Wild symbols can change during the Fire Dragon or the Earth Dragon into Fire Wilds or Rock Wilds. Note: The normal Wild symbols land only during spins that have no other modifiers, The Fire Wilds land during the Flaming Wilds feature, and the Rock Wilds land during the Earthquake Reels feature. 3.2. Dragon Stone Symbols Three or more Dragon Stone symbols anywhere on the reels will trigger Free Spins according to the paytable. 3.3. Elemental Power Spins During any spin one of the Elemental Dragons can randomly unleash their powers and alter the symbols on the reels according to their powers. Note: Free Spins cannot be triggered during Elemental Power Spins. 3.3.1. Flaming Wilds The Fire Dragon will rain fireballs down a random amount of Fire Wilds. Note: The amount of Fire Wilds can be between 3 and 9. 3.3.2. Ice Storm Spins The Ice Dragon will freeze into place winning symbols and will award respins as long as the win is improved. Note: A maximum of two symbols can be frozen at a time. 3.3.3. Earthquake Reels The Earth Dragon will raise rocks from the Earth that will turn the entire reels Wild. Note: The amount of extended Wilds can be between 1 and 3. 3.3.4. Winds of Winnings The Wind Dragon's stormy winds will blow in a random amount of mystery symbols to replace 3 to 15 other symbols positions to create wins. Note: The mystery symbols only appear during the shuffle screen and they have no payout. 3.4. Free Spins At the start of the Free Spins one of the four Elemental Dragons will be randomly selected and triggered. This will increase the chance on any spin. Note: Free Spins cannot be retriggered. 4.
