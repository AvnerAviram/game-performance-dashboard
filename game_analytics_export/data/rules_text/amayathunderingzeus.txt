Thundering Zeus GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and plays All wins pay left to right The Won field displays winnings in credits. All 20 lines always played Spacebar can be used to spin Clicking SPIN or space bar repeats the last bet. Select Credits Per Line multiplier (1, 2, 3, 5, 10) x 20 lines Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current Credits Per Line selected Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits Per Line staked Only the highest winning combination is paid per line Scatter wins (if available) are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter WILD substitutes for any symbol except BONUS Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. FEATURE WILD on middle reel awards 3 free spins WILD expands to cover entire 3rd reel during free spins BONUS cannot be activated during FEATURE BONUS BONUS only located and appearing anywhere on 2nd, 3rd, and 4th reels triggers BONUS Bonus anywhere on reels 2, 3, 4 triggers bonus feature BONUS award is free spins To determine number of free spins and win multiplier, pick icons in BONUS game until you win 3 same 20, 15, 10, 7, 5 Free Spins can be won If 20 Free Spins are won, all wins are multiplied by 3 If 15, 10, 7, 5 Free Spins are won, all wins are multiplied by 2 Bonus can be re-triggered multiple times Bonus re-triggers automatically win the same spins and multiplier as the initial Bonus and are immediately added to the Free Spin tally on the info bar. Free spin win amounts are based on the same Credits Per Line bet as the triggering spin amount Feature can be awarded during Bonus AUTO SPIN Click the AUTOSPIN button and select the number from the pop-up menu, then click START Click on STOP to cancel Autospin Autospin is deactivated after a bonus or feature Last modification date: 5/16/2018
