Blazin' Gems Deluxe WILD SYMBOLS The Diamond Jackpot, Ruby Jackpot, and Citrine Jackpot symbols are Wild and substitute in winning Seven or Bar symbol combinations when appearing on an active payline. Only highest winner paid per line. Wild symbols do not substitute for Multipliers. DOUBLE NUDGE Ruby Jackpot symbols appear with an arrow pointing up and Citrine Jackpot symbols appear with an arrow pointing down. When one or more Ruby Jackpot and/or Citrine Jackpot symbols appear on a reel stop, wins in the initial reel stop are paid first, then each Ruby Jackpot and Citrine Jackpot symbol may nudge up or down one position to create a new reel stop combination to be evaluated. The nudge direction is dictated by the arrow on the Jackpot symbol. All applicable Jackpot symbols nudge at the same time, no matter the direction. If applicable, wins for the initial nudge combination are paid, then each applicable Jackpot symbol may nudge a second time up or down one position to create a new reel stop combination to be evaluated. Applicable Jackpot symbols will not nudge above payline 2 or below payline 3. Applicable Jackpot symbols can nudge one or two positions depending on their initial landing location. It is possible to award a progressive before or after the nudge has taken place. Progressive tiers funded by a percentage of all wagers. MULTIPLIERS Certain Seven and Bar symbols display a 2X, 3X,4X, or 5X Multiplier. Multipliers multiply winning combinations they appear in, except Jackpots. Two or more Multipliers on an active payline multiply together and multiply the winning combination. 2X Multiplier appears on a 7 symbol and a Double Bar symbol. 3X Multiplier appears on a 77 symbol and a Single Bar symbol. 4X multiplier appears on a Double Bar symbol. 5X Multiplier appears on a 777 symbol and a Single Bar symbol.
