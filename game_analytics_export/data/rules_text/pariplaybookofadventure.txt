Book of Adventure Game Rules Play Book of Adventure and join an intrepid explorer searching for great riches in ancient Egypt. The reels of this highly animated slot machine are packed with Egyptian imagery, and a mystical book awards a free spins bonus game complete with expanding symbols and retriggers. Spin the highly animated reels and get ready for the game of your life in Book of Adventure! Try your chances at a BIG WIN or MEGA WIN in this hot new 10 win line, 5 reel, 3 row video slot game. The colorful reels are flled with Egyptian symbols including pharaohs, Egyptian gods like Anubis, explorers, and a mystical book. Game features include WILD SCATTER SYMBOLS that award SCATTER PRIZES and a FREE SPINS BONUS GAME. The FREE SPINS BONUS GAME awards 10 FREE SPINS with EXPANDING SYMBOLS that expand to cover an entire reel if winning combinations can be created. Click/Tap the SPIN or AUTO PLAY BUTTONS to start playing. Activate MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. The theoretical minimum playback percentage (RTP) is 96.21% Disclaimer: Only the highest winning combination per active win line pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. Features Wild Scater Symbols Three (3) or more WILD SCATTER SYMBOLS visible anywhere on the reels award a SCATTER PRIZE and a FREE SPINS BONUS GAME. WILD SCATTER SYMBOLS substitute for all symbols. Free Spins Bonus Game The FREE SPINS BONUS GAME awards 10 FREE SPINS played on special reels that feature EXPANDING SYMBOLS. During the FREE SPINS BONUS GAME, WILD SCATTER SYMBOLS substitute for ALL symbols to complete winning combinations. The FREE SPINS BONUS GAME can be RETRIGGERED. During the FREE SPINS BONUS GAME, three (3) or more WILD SCATTER SYMBOLS visible anywhere on the reels awards a SCATTER PRIZE and 10 EXTRA FREE SPINS. Expanding Symbols When the FREE SPINS BONUS GAME is triggered, one of the symbols on the reels will be randomly chosen to become an EXPANDING SYMBOL. The Book will reveal which symbol will become an EXPANDING SYMBOL before the FREE SPINS start. The EXPANDING SYMBOL will automatically expand to cover the entire reel (or reels) only if a winning combination can be created with that EXPANDING SYMBOL. An EXPANDING SYMBOL pays like a regular symbol on all win lines. Gamble Feeling lucky? Each time you win, you’ll be given the option to gamble your winnings. Choose a Color – Red or Black – to double your winnings (x2). Choose a Suit – Heart, Club, Diamond, or Spade to quadruple your winnings (x4). You may gamble up to 5 times or pay 2,500 coins.
