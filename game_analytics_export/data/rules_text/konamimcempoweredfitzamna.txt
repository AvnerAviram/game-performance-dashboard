Mayan Chief Empowered Reel of Itzamna BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels. Highest win only on each lit line. Wins on different lit lines are added. All wins are shown in currency. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLS DOOR will transform into one of the following symbols: RELIEF, CHEST, CUP, JAGUAR, BIRD, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9, at the end of reel spin. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. MAN appears on reels 2, 3, 4 & 5 only and substitutes for RELIEF, CHEST, CUP, JAGUAR, BIRD, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. RELIEF (TRIGGER) pays are on activated lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels XTRA REWARD™ RULES The 3rd reel has 2 symbols in 1 reel. For each lit line of the 3rd reel, the symbols in the 2 symbols in 1 position are evaluated separately when part of a winning combination. FREE GAME FEATURE 3, 4 or 5 RELIEF wins trigger 8, 10 or 15 free games respectively. All wins during the free games are 2X. Additional free games can be won during the free games. During the feature, all pays are on 30 lines. Credits bet remain the same as the game that triggered the feature. RTP - 94.1% Last modification date: 1/6/2026
