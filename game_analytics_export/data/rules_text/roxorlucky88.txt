Lucky 88 Game Rules: 25 lines are played for line cost of 30 Coins. Bet is 30 Coins x Coin Size. All wins on played lines only except scatters and DICE FEATURE wins. Highest win only on each line. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only for scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels, except scatters. RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 2/11/2025
