Asgards Thunder Game Theme and Graphics Join Thor, the god of lightning, in this new 5 reel 20 line slot, Asgard's Thunder™. With his mighty hammer Mjolnir, this mighty Norse warrior has the power to release epic wins. Main Game Your aim with Asgards Thunder™ is to land five matching symbols along one of the 20 win lines. All winning combinations start on the leftmost reel and continue to the right on adjacent reels. The 'Mjolnir' symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the bonus "Hand of Thor". Feature Land 3 or more 'Hand of Thor' symbols adjacent, from left to right, and be rewarded with 8 Free Games. During the free games Thor will strike the reels and replace each picture symbol with a wild 'Mjolnir' for electrifying wins. Free Games can be re triggered Feature trigger : 3 or more adjacent 'Hand of Thor' symbols starting at the leftmost reel. Feature hit rate : 100 - 140 games. Feature top prize : 2000 x bet per line Rules 'Mjolnir' substitutes for all symbols except scatter 'Hand of Thor'. All pays are for wins of left to right. All prizes are on selected lines, excluding scatter 'Hand of Thor'. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet. Free Games are played at trigger bet and lines. Free Games can be retriggered. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to player of this game is 95.19%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
