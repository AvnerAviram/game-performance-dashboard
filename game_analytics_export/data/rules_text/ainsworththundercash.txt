Thunder Cash Game rules 'Lightning Bolts' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Lightning Bolts' appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. 'Thunder Cash' is a scatter. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 94.07%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games Feature 8 free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Thunder Cash'. During the Free Games Feature, wherever 'Lightning Bolts' appears it is held in position until the completion of the Free Games Feature. During the Free Games Feature, the Feature paytable is applied. During the Free Games Feature, 5 additional free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Thunder Cash'. During the Free Games Feature, up to 15 scattered 'Thunder Cash' can be awarded. During the Free Games Feature, 6 or more scattered 'Thunder Cash' pays the same as 5 scattered 'Thunder Cash'. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games.
