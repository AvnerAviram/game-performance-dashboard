Fishin Frenzy Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 96%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Rules FISHIN' FRENZY is a 5-reel 3-row game with 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 or 10 selectable win lines. Stakes range from €0.01 to €20.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. All wins and winning combinations are shown in the pay table. WILDCARD Symbol The MAN symbol substitutes for all symbols except the BOAT scatter symbol. MAN only appears in the free games. Scatter Symbol The BOAT is a scatter symbol. Free Games 3, 4 or 5 BOAT awards 10, 15 or 20 free games respectively. During the free games each MAN that appears catches the prize from all the fish tags on screen. Free Games cannot be retriggered. Stakes do not have to be paid for the free games. Your effective bet for these free games is your actual bet when you won the free games. The resulting winnings will be shown in the pay table. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
