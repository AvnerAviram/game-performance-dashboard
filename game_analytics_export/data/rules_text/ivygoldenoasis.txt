Golden Oasis Game Objective: Golden Oasis is a five-reel 9-paylines Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot slot game. You win based on the combination of the various symbols displayed on the selected winning payline(s). The ‘PAYTABLE’ below shows how much you win for each combination. For each bet amount placed, the house adds a percentage of the bet amount to the running jackpot total. Game Information: The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and play-money modes, except that play-money games do not contribute to the jackpot amount and auto-spin option will not be available in play-money mode. You can bet on up to nine lines. The paylines are shown in the PAYLINES table. Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot: Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot is a jackpot that grows progressively as bets are made. For each spin, on any of the four Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot games, the house adds a small credit to the running jackpot total. The Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot can be won irrespective of hitting any paying or non-paying combination, while betting any amount. How to play: Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet per payline is set for all 9 lines on the slot machine. You can increase the bet per line by clicking the button to the right of 'BET', or you can decrease the bet by clicking the button to the left. Bet and lines will not get adjusted to the next possible bet, if your balance drops below total bet placed, then click on button shown on bottom of game screen. Setting 'LINES': Nine lines are selected by default. You can decrease the number of lines by clicking the button to the left of 'LINES', or select more lines by clicking the button to the right up to 9 lines. When the number of lines is increased, all the line numbers and the line path will be colored to represent the selected paylines. These lines and their path are decolored when the number of paylines is decreased. MAX BET: To play with MAX BET, click on . If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled. BET: The number of selected lines are multiplied by the 'bet per line'. The total bet amount is displayed beside the 'BET' label, which can be found at the bottom of the game screen. Paytable: The Paytable can be viewed either by clicking the button located in bottom of game screen beside ‘BET’ label or by clicking button located at the bottom of the screen beside the Line meter. The prizes displayed in the Paytable are per unit bet, and are shown as multipliers. To get the effective payout per line, multiply the respective prize with the bet per line. You can go back to the game by clicking on the button on the Paytable screen. Clicking on the button will open the rules screen along with Paytable and allow you to read the game rules. Spin: Click on SPIN icon to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations on the selected lines and any scatter wins will be paid according to the Paytable. Result: Wins are indicated by colorized paylines animated symbols, and in case of a scatter win, by animated symbols, with the total win amount being shown at the bottom of the screen beside 'WIN'. Volume: icon will play the game music. Click on icon will mute the volume . 'AUTOSPIN': You can use this feature to choose all or any of the various options for 'hands free' game play. The available options under this feature are: Number of spins: The game will spin for the number of rotations selected from the drop-down menu. You can choose any number of games and play until your balance is less than the total bet placed. Spin till Win equals or exceeds: The game will spin until the win amount is equal to or exceeds the amount selected from the drop down menu. Spin till Loss not exceeds: The game will automatically spin until the loss limit is reached. Loss will be calculated with (Loss = Total bets - Total wins) Total bets=accumulated total bets Total wins=accumulated total wins (((Total bets- Total wins) + current game bet) <= Loss limit target) then next auto spin will be allowed. Spin till feature trigger: The game will spin until the next feature is triggered. Autospin game is terminated whenever a Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot is triggered in-between the Autospins. To make use of the autospin feature, click on the 'AUTOSPIN' button in the bottom right of the game screen. When the autospin window opens, check the boxes next to the option(s) you'd like to play with. Select from the drop-down menu where applicable and click ‘Ok’ button. Click on 'START' to activate the selected options and start the spins. Similarly, click on ‘X’ button to skip the AUTOSPIN' feature and return to the game screen. When playing with multiple options and conditions selected, the condition that is satisfied first will take precedence over the others, and the remaining selected conditions will be disabled. You can then choose to either select the same set of options again or opt for new option(s) and proceed with the game. 'AUTOSPIN' option is not available in the play-money mode. Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. ­To change your bet, increase or decrease the Bet shown on the game screen. Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen. Browser Panel Commands: Click on ‘ Responsible Gaming ’ to navigate to the Responsible gaming page. Click on ' Game Rules ' to view the current version number & game rules of the game. Click on ' Game logs ' to view the logs of the games you've played. Click on ' Deposit ' button to add funds to your account. Click on ‘ Real/Play ’ to switch between real-money or play-money game modes. The ‘ Game logs ’ functionality is applicable only for the real money game play. Game Rules : Play 1 to 9 lines. All symbols pay left to right, except scattered Camel symbol, which pays when appeared anywhere on the reels. Only the highest win on each selected payline is paid. Coinciding wins on different lit lines are added together. Line wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter wins are added to payline wins. All wins are paid on selected paylines only, except scatter symbols, which pays anywhere in view. The Wild (Golden Oasis) symbol substitutes for all other symbols except the scattered Camel. All jackpot wins are credited to your account balance immediately. Features: Jackpot feature round: Features: Jackpot feature round: A Ca$h Fla$h jackpot can be triggered anytime, either during normal spin, autospin or after the feature game. The reset value of Ca$h Fla$h Jackpot is $1,000. Any bet is eligible for the jackpot. The larger is your wager on each game, the more chances you have of winning the jackpot. You will be informed of any jackpot wins by a pop-up message, and the win amount will be credited to your account immediately. The current value of the jackpot is shown at the top right-hand corner of the game screen. Feature game play: The 'Treasure Cave' bonus feature is triggered when the 3 or more Camel symbols appear anywhere in view on the reels. The main game screen will change, and the feature game screen will open. You must then select from the pots shown in the ‘Treasure Cave’ to reveal gold coins. The gold coins determine your prize multiplier, which is displayed at the bottom left of the feature screen. The total feature win is the prize multiplier multiplied by the triggering bet. The feature is repeated until no gold is collected. Click on the ‘COMPLETE’ button to restore the main game screen. Wins are transferred to the BALANCE at the end of the feature.
