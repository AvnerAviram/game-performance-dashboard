9 Tigers The aim of the game is to land three matching symbols on any of 8 paylines as described in the paytable. It is possible to win on up to 8 paylines at the same time (but not possible to win on 7 paylines at the same time, due to the way the game is structured). The prize is awarded for all winning combinations caught simultaneously on the reels, not for each winning line separately. Each new game costs one stake. Tiger Bonus Drawing a wall of identical symbols activates Tiger Bonus. Tiger symbols that created the wall stay locked on the reels. Player needs to draw as many opposite symbols as possible during 9 bonus spins. When two opposite symbols meet on the reel, they become a Yin and Yang symbol. After 9 spins, the win is paid for the number of Yin and Yang symbols according to the Tiger Bonus paytable. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. GAMBLE FEATURE • The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. • After activating the gamble feature, a player can: • take the win and end the gamble feature, • continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. • After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. • The player can end the gamble feature after each round. • In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the Current win counter and returns to the main game. • The gamble feature has seven rounds. • Current win – amount of win that can be taken. • Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
