This 5 reel 25 line slot has much in store with revealing doors, a Hold & Spin Feature and a Free Games Feature. Main Game Your aim in Gems of Giza™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 25 win lines running from left to right. During the base game when landing Door symbols on reels, they will open to reveal mystery symbols. 3 or more Pyramids trigger the Free Games Feature where appearing Locked Doors are held in place each spin resulting in some huge wins. Gems of Giza Jackpots Feature 6 or more Coin symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Gems of Giza Jackpots Feature. The triggering Coin symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Coin lands on the reels, the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on screen are filled with Coins. Coin prizes can award Major, Minor and Mini Jackpots or up to 20x total bet. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Door, Locked Door, Coin and Pyramid. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. PAYTABLE SYMBOL 5x 4x 3x SPECIAL FUNCTION WILD Substitutes for all symbols except Door, Locked Door, Coin and Pyramid.
