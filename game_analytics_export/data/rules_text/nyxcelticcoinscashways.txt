Celtic Coins Cashways Welcome to CELTIC COINS CASHWAYS™ Multipliers can appear on the following symbols: Triple Horseshoe, Horseshoe, Triple BAR, Double BAR, Single BAR and the Celtic Coin 5x does not appear on reel 2. 3x does not appear on reels 1 and 3. 2x can appear on all reels. More than one multiplier on a pay line will multiply the win by the product of those included, for example 2x3x5 = 30x CASHWAYS™ Celtic Coins award their cash prizes when they land in any positions on reels 1, 2, and 3. ALL Coin symbol wins are multiplied by the product of ALL multipliers appearing on ANY Coin symbol. Each individual coin cash prize is awarded as a multiplier of the total bet. Possible values are: 0.4x, 0.6x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.2x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 4.5x, 5x, 6x, 8x, 10x, and 20x. CELTIC CASH MODE CELTIC CASH MODE may activate when coins land on at least reels 1, 2 and last 6-12 spins. During CELTIC CASH MODE all coin cash prizes are awarded regardless of how many coins are landed. HOW TO CALCULATE PAYLINE WINS To calculate your total payline win, calculate all payline wins and add them together. To calculate a single payline win, count the number of identical symbols lined up in sequence on the line starting from the left most reel. If three identical symbols are lined up, find the win value in the Pay Table. There you can find the value for all symbols. Only the highest winning combination per line is paid out. WILD symbols are wild for HORSESHOE, TRIPLE BAR, DOUBLE BAR and BAR. RETURN TO PLAYER The RTP cannot be affected by player strategy. The overall theoretical RTP is 94.04%. MAX WIN Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed $250,000.00. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration.
