Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Wins subject to verification. Values displayed in the [Game Rules/Help Screens] have not been pre-multiplied, except where stated. Base Game Wild Symbols are wild and substitutes for all Multiplier Wild Symbols. Multiplier Wild Symbols are wild and substitutes for all symbols. Multiplier Wild Symbols appear displaying a multiplier of 1. Multiplier Wild Symbols landing on the played line on reel 2 will lock that reel in place and award between 2 and 10 respins. Reel 2 remains locked until all respins have been played. On each respin, the multiplier displayed on a Multiplier Wild Symbol will increase by 1. All wins including a multiplier wild symbol receive the currently displayed multiplier. Last modification date: 1/5/2023
