Super Prize Punch Lucky Tap User Interface ‘+’ / ‘-’ buttons increase / decrease the bet value — the bet value is the total bet per game round. Starts a game round at the current bet value. Displays the autoplay options. Stops the autoplay session. The numerical value displayed within the button shows the number of autoplay game rounds remaining. Bet Definitions Current Bet: The current bet is the value that appears above . Minimum Bet: The minimum bet is the lowest possible bet value, which is selected by pressing until the bet value no longer decreases. Bet Multiplier: The bet multiplier is defined as the current bet divided by the minimum bet. Autoplay Options Autoplay will allow game rounds to be played automatically. Click on the Autoplay button and select the number of Autoplay game rounds. Additional Information The overall return to player is 96.70%. The game outcomes are determined by a random number generator that has been certified by an approved test house. Misuse and malfunction voids all pays and plays. The above 2 points may be subject to further terms, conditions and processes of the gaming site operator. Wins A winning play occurs when the player lands a punch during the game round. 3 to 15 prizes can be awarded on a winning play, plus a chance to trigger the Free Games feature, if lights up completely Multiplier Feature If Free Games are not awarded on any winning play, the Multiplier Feature may randomly be activated. If the Multiplier Feature is activated, a Random Multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, or x7 will appear. All Currency Wins will be multiplied by the Random Multiplier. Free Games 12 Free Games are awarded if lights up completely on a winning play. After all Free Games are played, the Multiplier Feature is activated and all Currency Wins awarded during Free Games and the play that initiated Free Games will be multiplied by the Random Multiplier. Free Games can not be retriggered. Last modification date: 9/26/2023
