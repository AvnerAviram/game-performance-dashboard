Roulette x5 About Roulette x5 Roulette x5 is an adaptation of European Roulette, with the same rules and payouts, but with the addition of a dice roll bonus that can multiply your winnings up to x5. General Game Payouts How to play Game controls Game Features Game statistics Game settings Return to Player(RTP) General Roulette x5 plays like a classic European Roulette, but after every winning spin there is a twist - a chance to multiply your winnings with the dice bonus! The total bet is shown at all times and this is what will be debited from your balance for each spin. This game does not use Coins. Win amount is based on the total bet amount and type of bet placed. Total bet is the sum of all bets placed for the spin. Bets can be placed on the classic Roulette table as well as on the Racetrack which allows to place alternative bets. Different types of bets can be combined and different value chips can be used in one spin. After a winning spin the player is presented with the dice roll bonus, which can multiply the total winnings. The payouts for all market types are fixed and the payout amount is based on the total bet. On desktop, the payouts are displayed by hovering over the market type. On mobile, the payouts are displayed in the game menu. The dice roll bonus consists of six dice being rolled. If at least four dice present the same number, the total win for the corresponding spin is multiplied. If the outcome of the spin does not match your prediction and the spin is not winning, the game is over and the player can start a new round. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. How to play The wheel has 37 bays, numbered 0 to 36. The payouts for all the classic bets match classic Roulette odds. The goal is to predict on which number the ball will land. If the outcome matches your prediction, player wins. Select the chips to use for placing bets. Various chips and bet types can be combined for each spin. Bets can be placed on the classic Roulette table as well as on the Racetrack which allows to place alternative bets. On desktop, click and drag, to place multiple chips in one go using our Quick Chips function. On mobile, slide your finger across the chosen numbers to place multiple chips in one go using our Quick Chips function. Multiple chips can be placed on one number, the colours will change to the highest possible colour of the chip denomination. After bet placement Undo, Clear and Spin buttons become active. Confirm the bet by clicking Spin button, or amend the bet. After the Spin button is clicked, the wheel spins and the ball drops into a numbered bay. The result is shown and is entered in the winning numbers box and also highlighted on the table. If the spin is winning, six dice are presented over the wheel display and are rolled. If at least 4 dice match, winnings are multiplied. After a winning spin the player is presented with the dice roll bonus, which can multiply the total winnings. Match any 4 dice to activate 10% bonus. Match any 5 dice to activate 50% bonus. Match any 6 dice to activate x5 bonus. If the spin is not winning, the game is over and the player can start a new round. After the spin has ended the Rebet button becomes active. Previously played game rounds can be seen in Bet History. Return to Player(RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time. This game has an RTP of 97.66-98.00%.
