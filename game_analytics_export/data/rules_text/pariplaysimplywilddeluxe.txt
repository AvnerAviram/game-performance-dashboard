Simply Wild Deluxe RULES Simply Wild Deluxe is a quintessential classic slot game that features TWO side-by-side games, with double reels that are packed with cool retro symbols, special double symbols and wild symbols. Try your chances at a BIG WIN or a MEGA WIN in this hot new 10 win line, 4 reel, 3 row (per game) video slot game. The colorful reels are filled with retro fruit machine symbols, special STAR symbols and WILD symbols. Stakes range between 1 and 250 per game and you can play on three (3) or four (4) reels per game, depending on your stake. A stake of 10 COINS or higher activates the fourth reel, for more chances at hitting winning combinations. The special STAR symbols also become WILD symbols, depending on your stake. Bet a stake of 5 COINS and the special STAR symbols also become WILD symbols on the three (3) active reels. Bet a stake of 20 COINS, or higher, and the special STAR symbols become WILD symbols on all four (4) reels. Play the left set of reels, the right set of reels, or both sets of reels at the same time, complete with synchronized spinning action. Click the SPIN or AUTO PLAY BUTTONS to start playing. Click MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. The theoretical minimum payback percentage for this game is 96.50%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. FEATURES STAR SYMBOL These reels feature special STAR SYMBOLS that display both a FRUIT (ORANGE, PEAR, or PLUM) or a BELL symbol AND a STAR together on the same symbol. A combination of any three (3) FRUIT/BELL and STAR symbols on a win line awards a special STAR prize. These special FRUIT/BELL and STAR symbols can also complete a winning combination with regular FRUIT/BELL symbols when the fruits/bells match. Mixed STAR and FRUIT/BELL combinations award the value of three (3) FRUIT/BELL symbols. For example: ORANGE-STAR/ ORANGE / ORANGE makes a winning combination that awards the same value as an ORANGE / ORANGE / ORANGE winning combination. The special STAR SYMBOLS also become WILD symbols, depending on your stake. Bet a stake of 5 COINS and the special STAR SYMBOLS also become WILD symbols on the three (3) active reels. Bet a stake of 20 COINS, or higher, and the special STAR SYMBOLS become WILD symbols on all four (4) reels. MYSTERY JACKPOT – PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT Every time any player plays the game, 0.5% of every bet is added to the MYSTERY JACKPOT (PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT). The minimum guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout is 500 EUR. There is a guaranteed MYSTERY JACKPOT payout when it hits the maximum jackpot value of 5,000 EUR. Higher stakes increase the chances of winning the MYSTERY JACKPOT. Bets made with bonus money do not qualify for MYSTERY JACKPOT payouts. Although we strive for complete accuracy and transparency at all times, the jackpot counter display may be affected by multiple players playing at the same time and other external factors including Internet connectivity issues. Such factors may cause discrepancies between the jackpot amount shown on the jackpot counter and the actual jackpot payout amount. The jackpot win amount displayed on the jackpot win message always supersedes the jackpot amount displayed on the jackpot counter. The jackpot amount displayed on the jackpot win message shall be binding. The jackpot is always calculated and awarded in euros. If a jackpot winner was playing in any other currency (not euros), the jackpot win amount will be converted from euros into the currency the winner was playing with at the time of the win, using the applicable exchange rate on the date of the jackpot win.
