Leonidas King of the Spartans Game Rules Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. All 40 lines always played. All credits staked are multiplied by the credit value. All pays left to right on consecutive reels starting with the leftmost reel. All symbols pay once in any position on the reel, except substituted wins which may pay multiple times. Select credits per spin (40, 80, 120, 160 or 200). Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current credits selected. The Won field displays winnings in Credits. Only the highest win on a pay line pays. Wins On Different Paylines Are Added Together. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON field. Features are played at the same credits per spin as the triggering spin. Minimum wager: 40 Credits. Maximum wager: 200 Credits. Game Features/Bonus Refer to Paytable for more information Spin Tapping SPIN repeats last bet. Auto Spin Auto Spin mode is cancelled and must be activated after a Big Win occurs.
