Mariachis STANDARD GAMES RULES Bingo-based draw in which 30 balls are drawn from a drum containing 60 balls the player has up to four cards with 15 numbers on each one. If a win figure is matched on a card, the prize associated will be awarded from the paytable. EXTRA BALL RULES Sometimes the player is offered the option of buying extra balls at additional cost. PRIZE TABLE When bonus labeled figure in prize plan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS GAME 1 Involves a chase between two characters. In each round the they move a random number of places. The second character must not catch the first one. BONUS GAME 2 Consists of selecting hearts in each round. Each heart hides a prize or EXIT word. The resulting prize will be the accumulated values. When bonus labeled figure in prize plan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS GAME 3 Select an object and reveal the random prize hidden behind it. The final prize will be the accumulated values. BONUS GAME 4 Spin the roulette with eight possible prizes. The obtained prize will be the one pointed by the arrow when the roulette stops.
