Smoking Hot 7's Game Theme and Graphics Smoking Hot 7's is a 5x3 5 lines slot game with glossy fruits, scattered stars and the Jackpot Diamonds Feature. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along a win line. Winning combinations begin on the first reel to the left. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines, excluding Scatters. Feature The Jackpot Diamonds Feature has the four-level Diamond Mystery™ Jackpot. Each mystery level has specific gems. The base win of each jackpot depends on your stake. The base win is 5.000 times your stake for the Diamond Jackpot, 1.250 times your stake for the Red Gem Jackpot, 200 times your stake for the Blue Gem Jackpot, and 50 times your stake for the Green Gem Jackpot. Green Gem - 1st level (lowest jackpot value) Blue Gem - 2nd level Red Gem - 3rd level Diamond - 4th and highest level Once triggered, the player is taken to the Jackpot Cards game where he/she can pick one of 12 face-down cards. The player picks cards until 3 matching gem symbols have been revealed. The Jackpot Diamonds Mystery level corresponding to the revealed gem will be awarded. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per Scatter combination. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Last modification date: 7/13/2023
