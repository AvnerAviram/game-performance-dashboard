Triple Threat GAME RULES FIXED MULTIPLIER SYMBOL Triple Threat symbol is wild and pays 3x for one and 9x for two in winning Sevens and Bars combinations. Only the highest line win or scatter pay is awarded per symbol combination. Only 3 Triple Threat symbols can award a scatter pay.
