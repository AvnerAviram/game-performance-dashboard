Power Keno Power Keno Power up your Keno game with 4x the excitement! Power Keno offers the classic Keno play you know and love, with 2 to 10 available spots, and a 4x Power Win if the last ball hits a marked spot! How to Bet Press a number on the play area to mark spots. Press a marked spot to cancel that mark. The following commands are available during the game: BET UP – Press to increase the bet. BET DOWN – Press to decrease the bet. QUICK PICK – Press to automatically mark spots. Quick Pick defaults to 6 marked spots. ERASE – Press to erase marked spots. START – Press to start the game at the current bet. How to Play Press the screen to mark 2 to 10 spots or press the Quick Pick button to randomly select spots. Press a marked spot to cancel that mark. To start the game, place a bet and press START. 20 numbers are drawn. If a drawn number matches the number of a marked spot, a hit is counted. Wins are based on the number of marks, the number of hits, and the bet amount. If the last ball drawn hits a marked spot, the total win is multiplied by 4. RTP - 94.97% Options Sound Press to turn game sounds on or off. Paytable Press to see the award value for each winning outcome. Speed Press to change how fast the game plays. Rules Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. Only the highest winner is paid. Wins are shown in currency and are based on the size of the bet. For jurisdictions where spacebar functionality is enabled it can be used as an alternative to the Start Button for a faster, smoother game experience.
