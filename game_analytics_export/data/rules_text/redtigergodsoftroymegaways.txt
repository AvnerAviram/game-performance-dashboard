Winners LIVE Feature buy toggle Game Features Golden Shields and Wilds Wilds only appear on the 10th spin, placed on a reel based on the shields collected there during the first 9 spins. Wilds substitute for all paying symbols. Every spin is part of a 10-spin cycle, shown by a counter above the reels. Golden Shields can land on the middle 4 reels during the first 9 spins and are stored above the reel where they land. On the 10th spin, all collected Golden Shields drop onto their reels as Wilds. If multiple shields land in the same spot, they increase the Wild’s multiplier by +1. Helen Helen is a regular paying symbol. When 2 or more land stacked, they merge into a super symbol. The number on the super symbol shows how many 1x1 symbols it represents. Troy Spins Troy Spins scatters can only land on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 during the first 9 spins of the cycle. - 3 scatters: 8 Troy Spins, with 3 - 8 random wilds placed on the reels each spin. - 4 scatters: 12 Troy Spins, with 5 - 10 random wilds placed on the reels each spin. Shield scatters do not appear during Troy Spins. Any Troy Spins scatter that lands during the bonus round adds 1 extra spin. Feature Buy 1.
