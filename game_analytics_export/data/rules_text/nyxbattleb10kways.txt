Battle Bucks 10K Ways BATTLE BUCKS 10K WAYS™ is a 10K Ways™ slot game with up to 10,000 ways to win. The game has 6 reels. It has 4 rows on reels 1 and 6; and 5 rows (inclusive of top reel) on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. The Average Theoretical Return to Player is: 94.72% RULES GOLD TANK GOLD TANK is wild and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. During the base game, GOLD TANK only appears on the top reel. SCATTER Battle Spins Feature is triggered when 5 or more SCATTER appear. SCATTER BONUSES Each SCATTER awards a bonus or non-bonus prize in the Battle Spins Feature. BATTLE SPINS FEATURE Battle Spins Feature is triggered when 5 or more SCATTER appear. SCATTERs are converted to SPANNERs. The prize on each SPANNER is paid. Each SPANNER awards Gold Tank 1 Life point up to a maximum of 10. The first OPPONENT is revealed and his Life points are displayed. Order of OPPONENTS is GREEN TANK, BLUE TANK, PURPLE TANK then RED TANK. GREEN TANK, BLUE TANK, PURPLE TANK and RED TANK start with 3, 4, 5 and 6 Life points respectively. ENEMY symbols are GREEN TANK, BLUE TANK, PURPLE TANK and RED TANK. Only the ENEMY symbol that matches the current OPPONENT appears in any Battle Spins Feature spin. A Battle Round is triggered. In each Battle Round, 3 spins are awarded. The reel display is cleared at the start of each Battle Round. All positions are spun and will display either a GOLD TANK, ENEMY or BLANK symbol. GOLD TANK symbols will display a prize value. If a GOLD TANK or ENEMY symbol appears in a position then that position is locked and doesn't spin for the remainder of the Battle Round. When all spins are complete, or all symbol positions on the reel display are filled, the Battle Round ends. After the Battle Round ends, the reel display is processed from left-to-right and top-to-bottom. When an ENEMY symbol is processed it reduces Gold Tank's LIFE points by 1. When a GOLD TANK symbol is processed, it reduces the OPPONENT's LIFE points by 1 and awards the prize on the symbol. If Gold Tank's LIFE reaches 0 before all symbols are processed then symbol processing stops. If Gold Tank's LIFE reaches 0 then the feature is concluded. If the OPPONENT's LIFE reaches 0 before all symbols are processed, any remaining unprocessed GOLD TANK symbols change to SPANNERs. When a SPANNER is processed, it raises Gold Tank's LIFE by 1 point up to a maximum of 10, and awards the prize on the symbol. If the OPPONENT's life reaches 0 then the next opponent is revealed and its LIFE points are displayed. If Gold Tank's life is greater than 0 then a new Battle Round is triggered. If the RED TANK OPPONENT's life reaches 0, then a Victory Prize is paid and the feature ends. The Victory Prize is guaranteed to be a Grand, Major, Minor or Mini bonus. SCATTER/SPANNER and GOLD TANK (during Battle Spins Feature) display a Grand, Major, Minor or Mini Bonus, or a Non-Bonus Prize. Grand bonus prize is 2000 x total bet. Major bonus prize is 250 x total bet. Minor bonus prize is 50 x total bet. Mini bonus prize is 10 x total bet. Non-bonus prizes as multiples of total bet are: 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 75x, 100x. Bonus prizes are not progressive. Battle Spins Feature is played at the bet of the triggering spin. Symbol pays shown in the paytable are not awarded during the Battle Spins Feature. GAME RULES Player selects a Total Bet size to play 10,000 ways. Payouts are made according to the paytable. All wins are multiplied by total bet. Non-bonus prizes displayed over SCATTER/SPANNER symbols have already been multiplied by total bet amount. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels. GOLD TANK is wild and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. During the base game, GOLD TANK only appears on the top reel. The top reel adds a symbol to the reel it is above for reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Every winning symbol (except SCATTER) explodes and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the reels and from the right in the top reel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
