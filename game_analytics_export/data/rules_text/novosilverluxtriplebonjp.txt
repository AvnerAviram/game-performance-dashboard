Silver Lux - Triple Bonus Gems Game Theme and Graphics Silver Lux - Triple Bonus Gems is a 3 reel stepper game themed with glittering gemstones. Let these spinning gemstones make you win BIG! Main Game The main game is played on 3 reels and 25 win lines. Watch the multipliers! They are not only Wild but multiply your winnings as well. Whilst playing the main game, 3 progressive Free Games Meters will increase once in a while. The Free Games Feature will start when 3 scattered Bonus symbols of the same color appear in the reel window or when one or more Free Games counters hits 15. Free Games Feature During the Free Games Feature you really start to win big. The games are not only for free but will be multiplied as well. 2x for the Red Bonus, 5x for the Blue Bonus and 10x for the White Bonus. However, the Grand and Major Jackpot can be won during the Free Games Feature but will not be multiplied Hit 3 scattered Green Bonus symbols and 5 extra Free Games will be added! Silver Lux Linked Progressive Jackpot At the top of the game two progressive jackpots are presented, a Major Jackpot and a Grand Jackpot. Both jackpots are linked to the jackpots of other Silver Lux games. A mixed combination of multiplier symbols on any of the 25 win lines will award the Major jackpot. If the combination consists only of 3x multiplier symbols the Grand Jackpot will be yours! Rules All wins are presented in credits, except the Grand and Major Jackpot which are presented in the actual currency. Scatter and line wins are added. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per scatter combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 3/18/2025
