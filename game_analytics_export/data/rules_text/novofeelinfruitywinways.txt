Look out for those Free Spins symbols to reveal the Increasing Win Multiplier! Main Game and Win Ways ™ Feelin' Fruity™: Win Ways™ is played across a main reel set and a top reel. The main reel set has 6 reels. The top reel contains 4 symbols. Each symbol is added to the reel directly below on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Each main reel with or without the top reel symbols can contain up to 7 symbols. Each spin can have a maximum of 117649 Win Ways™ to win. Wins are awarded from left to right on adjacent symbols. Awards are multiplied by stake. Every winning symbol is part of a reaction and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the main reels and from the right on the top reel.
