Hot Safari Characteristics and features All winning combinations pay from left to right only All wins are multiplied by bet per line, only the highest win is paid per line Bets are made in coins per line To control bets, user is able to change the value of a coin in real currency and also increase the number of coins bet per line Balance, bets, wins are all displayed in both coins and real currency at all times The game has 12 symbols 5 high symbols, representing royals 5 low symbols, representing dice faces 1 wild symbols, substitutes for all symbols except for bonus and scatter. If the wild symbol hits on the middle row, it always expands vertically to fill the whole reel 1 scatter symbol that triggers the free spins round The game also has a 6 th reel, visually containing multipliers from 1x to 10x and a super wild feature. At each spin, the the 6 th reel spins also and awards either a multiplier for the current spin win or the Super Wild feature. The symbols on the 5 th reel are only a visual representation, created on the client. The server does not send the symbols on that reel, instead sending only one reponse per spin saying which random multiplier or if super wild was won Super Wild feature Super Wild feature is triggered when the Super Wild hits on the 6th reel When it hits, one wild is added to all of the 5 reels, in a random position. It is guaranteed that one wild will be added to each reel When, during Super Wild feature, a wild symbol is awarded in the middle row of a reel, that symbol expands to fill the whole reel Scatters still trigger the Free Spins feature if they are under an expanded wild Free spins When three or more Scatter symbols hit anywhere on the reels,the Free Spins round is triggered User is awarded 10 free spins More free spins can be won during the free spins round if more scatter symbols hit Paytable Artwork English Paytable Artwork English
