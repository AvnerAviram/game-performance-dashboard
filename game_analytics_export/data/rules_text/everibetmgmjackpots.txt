BetMGM Jackpots Game Rules Fixed Multipliers Green Lion symbol is wild and substitutes for any non-jackpot symbol to make winning Seven and Bar combinations. One Green Lion symbol pays 3X the winning combination, except for progressives. Two Green Lion symbols pay 9X the winning combination, except for progressives. The Orange Lion and Black Lion symbols are wild and substitute for any non-jackpot symbol to make winning Seven and Bar combinations. SYMBOL-TRIGGERED PROGRESSIVES 3 Green Lion symbols on an active Payline 9 awards the top progressive jackpot. 3 Green Lion symbols on an active Payline 1-8 awards the 2nd tier progressive jackpot. 3 Orange Lion symbols on an active Payline awards the 3rd tier progressive jackpot. 3 Black Lion symbols on an active Payline awards the 4th tier progressive jackpot. 3 of any Lion symbols on an active Payline awards the 5th tier progressive jackpot. Only the highest progressive achieved is awarded. Progressive jackpots are not multiplied by the Green Lion symbol. 3 Jackpot symbols on an active payline will pay only the progressive tier for the respective combination. The odds of winning the progressive jackpots improve as Lines Bet increases, Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers.
