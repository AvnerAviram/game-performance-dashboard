SYMBOLS TO LOOK OUT FOR Pig symbols can display jackpot or cash values and award wins in mixed combinations. 5 symbols awards 20x your stake 4 symbols awards 5x your stake 3 symbols awards 1x your stake The collector (hammer) symbol is Wild. This symbol acts as a cash collector and Wild substituting for all symbols except the bonus symbol during Free spins and Fortune Spins. FREE SPINS BONUS Bonus symbols can land on any reel. 3 bonus symbols awards10 Free spins 4 bonus symbols awards 15 Free spins 5 bonus symbols awards 20 Free spins During Free spins, each collector symbol accumulates cash values from all pig symbols in view and adds them to a 12-stage trail above the reels. The trail has 12 positions, with 3 enhanced stages that award 10 extra Free spins and increases the multipliers for pig symbol values. Multipliers increase to 2x, 3x, and 10x at each stage, applying after the current spins are completed. Pig symbols can display jackpot or cash values. The available jackpots are mini, minor, major or mega. Mini awards 20x base game stake Minor awards 30x base game stake Major awards 50x base game stake Mega awards 100x base game stake Cash values on the pig symbols can be 1x, 2x, 5x or 10x the base game stake. FORTUNE SPINS For a different spin on the game, play Fortune Spins! You can activate this alternative mode via the Fortune Spins button. All symbols except the pig, bonus and collector are removed from the reels increasing your chances of triggering big wins and the bonus. Once you activate the bonus, all regular symbols return to the reels. The collector symbol acts as a Wild and cash collector. If the collector and pig symbols appear on the reels at the same time, the value on the pig symbols will be collected for each collector symbol in view. Pig symbols display jackpot and cash values. The available jackpots are mini, minor, major or mega. Mini awards 20x base game stake, minor awards 30x base game stake, major awards 50x base game stake and mega awards 100x your stake. Cash values on coin pot symbols can be 1x, 2x, 5x or 10x the base game stake. Fortune spins is played at £2 stake. GAMBLE The gamble feature can be turned on, off or for wins of 5x stake or more.
