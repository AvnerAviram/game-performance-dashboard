Alaskan Husky Alaskan Husky GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Plays. Beginning with the left-most reel, pays left to right on consecutive reels. The Won field displays winnings in credits. All games are played with 100 paylines. Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet. In the flash version space bar can be used to spin. Select Multiplier Bet Level (1x to 5x). Only highest winner paid per line. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. Scatter wins (if available) are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter. WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. A DOUBLE HIGH symbol is treated like 2 stacked symbols each equivalent to the other. Five RED HUSKY symbols on payline 2 at maximum multiplier bet level award the Jackpot, however Jackpot cannot be won on a free spin. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. Mobile only Tapping SPIN repeats last bet Free Spins Feature 3 SCATTER symbols in any position on reels 2, 3 and 4 award 10 free spins and a SCATTER pay. Jackpot cannot be won on a free spin. Free spins are played at the same Multiplier Bet Level as the triggering spin. Additional free spins can be won during the Free Spins Feature. Different Reels are used during Free Spins. Expanding and Random Wilds The WILD symbol may randomly expand 2, 3 or 4 symbols upward on the reel of origin. The WILD symbol may randomly appear over a symbol and turn that symbol to WILD. Auto Spin: In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for a selectable number of spins. In settings, choose Auto Spin "ON" and select the desired number of autospins. Select the STOP button to cancel Autospin. Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus.
