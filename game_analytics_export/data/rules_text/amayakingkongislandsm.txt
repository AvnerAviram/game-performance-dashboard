King Kong Island SM GAME RULES Malfunctions void all pays and play. All credits staked are multiplied by the credit value. All pays left to right on consecutive reels, starting with the leftmost reel. Players can select credits per spin (25, 50, 75, 100 or 125). Only the highest combination is paid per way. Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current credits selected. The Won field displays winnings in Credits. GATE BONUS wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. There are 243 ways to win. Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet. Scatter wins (if available) are added to reel wins. WILD Substitutes for all symbols, except additional GATE BONUS symbols. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1,000, 1M=1,000,000, 1B=1,000,000,000. GATE BONUS Rules: The gate symbol can appear on any reel. Under the gate symbol, all symbols except wild can appear. During a game round, the symbol behind is the same for all occurrences of Auto Spin In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. Flash only Click the AUTO SPIN button and select the number from the pop-up menu. Click on STOP to cancel Auto Spin. Mobile only Press and hold the Spin button to activate the Auto Spin function or access Auto Spin from the Game Settings menu. Last modification date: 1/31/2017
