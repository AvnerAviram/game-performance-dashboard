Geisha Game Rules All wins shown in credits. 50 lines are played for 60 credits. Minimum bet 60 credits. Choose your TOTAL BET. TOTAL BET is 60 credits multiplied by the bet multiplier. Select +, - or to choose your TOTAL BET. The PLAY button will immediately commence play. While the reels are spinning you may press the PLAY button to stop them. Touch CREDIT, TOTAL BET or WIN meter to show in currency or credits. All wins on played lines only except scatters. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Line wins multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier and the number of lines played. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. FOR A FULL LIST OF ARISTOCRAT PATENTS PLEASE CHECK WITH YOUR LOCAL PATENT OFFICE. Auto play AUTO PLAY AUTOMATICALLY INITIATES A SPECIFIED NUMBER OF BOUGHT GAMES AT THE CURRENT TOTAL BET. CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET AND PRESS AUTOPLAY PRESS EXIT TO RETURN TO GAME WITHOUT INITIATING AUTO PLAY SPINS. SELECT A NUMBER OF AUTO PLAY SPINS. PRESS START TO IMMEDIATELY COMMENCE PLAY. THE SELECTED NUMBER OF GAMES WILL BE PLAYED WHILE THERE IS SUFFICIENT BALANCE ON THE CREDIT METER OR UNTIL FREE GAMES ARE TRIGGERED. DURING AUTO PLAY, PRESS stop TO CANCEL REMAINING AUTO PLAY SPIN FREE GAMES FEATURE 15 FREE GAMES ARE WON WITH 3, 4 OR 5 PRESS THE PLAY BUTTON TO START THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. THE FREE GAMES WILL START AUTOMATICALLY AFTER 2 MINUTES. ALL WINS DURING THE FREE GAMES ARE MULTIPLIED BY 3. FREE GAMES FEATURE CAN BE TRIGGERED AGAIN DURING THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. TOTAL BET IS THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. Last modification date: 10/13/2023
