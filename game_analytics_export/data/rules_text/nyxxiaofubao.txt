Xiao Fu Bao Payouts Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. Scatter wins are added to Any Ways™ pays. WILD SYMBOL WILD substitutes for all symbols except GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, RED COIN, WHITE COIN, MEGA JACKPOT COIN, MAJOR JACKPOT COIN, MAXI JACKPOT COIN, MINOR JACKPOT COIN and MINI JACKPOT COIN. WILD only appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. CASH FALLS FEATURE During the base game, all COINS will display a bet multiplier value based on the table below. REEL COIN VALUES AND AWARDS 1 & 5 0.5x - 10x total bet represented by GOLD COIN 2 & 4 0.5x - 25x total bet, represented by GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN 3 0.5x - 100x total bet, represented by GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN If any COIN lands on a reel, then all COINS on that reel are held for the next 3 wagered spins at the same bet level. The number of wagers that a reel's COINS will remain held is shown at the bottom of the associated reel. If every position on a reel is filled with a COIN then that reel awards the Cash Falls Feature. The Cash Falls Feature awards the prizes displayed on each of the corresponding reel's COIN's. After a reel awards the Cash Falls Feature, the COINS currently on that reel are no longer held. If the Cash Falls Feature is awarded, and the associated reel contains a BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN, then the free spin variant associated with the BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN is awarded to the player in addition to any values on GOLD COINs included in the Cash Falls Feature award. Symbols that land behind a GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN are not evaluated. All information on the Cash Falls Feature pages pertain to the base game. FEATURE COINS BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN each behave the same as a GOLD COIN and can attribute to a Cash Falls Feature. On the wagered spin in which BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN lands on the array, the associated free spins may be awarded. On each spin in which a BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN are locked into a position, the associated free spins may be awarded. Any Feature Coin included in a Cash Falls Feature award will also award the associated free spins. If the free spins are triggered by BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN, 'WINNER' appears next to the BLUE FU BABY, WHITE FU BABY, or RED FU BABY respectively. BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN will remain on the reel for the next three wagered spins, and may trigger the associated feature of the BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN. MORE FREE SPINS FEATURE If the More Free Spins Feature is triggered with BLUE COIN, then the player is awarded 9-40 free spins. At the beginning of the More Free Spins Feature, the number of free spins is randomly awarded. If a Free Spins Feature is not triggered with BLUE COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded. JACKPOT FREE SPINS FEATURE If the Jackpot Free Spins Feature is triggered with WHITE COIN, then on each free spin MINI JACKPOT COIN, MINOR JACKPOT COIN, MAXI JACKPOT COIN, MAJOR JACKPOT COIN, or MEGA JACKPOT COIN may land on the reel array. Jackpot coins are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. MEGA, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI have 6, 5, 4, 3, and 2 spaces respectively. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, the corresponding jackpot is awarded and its meter is reset to its base value. At the beginning of the Jackpot Free Spins Feature, the jackpot meters may be updated to the range of values in the table below. All RED COINS shown during the Scatter Free Spins feature are of the same value during the feature, and the value of the RED COINS will be shown to the player at the beginning of the Scatter Free Spins feature. The value of the RED COINS during the Scatter Free Spins feature are between 1x-7x. At the beginning of the Scatter Free Spins Feature, the multiplier is randomly awarded. GAME RULES Only highest win paid per winning combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The appearance of the BLUE FU BABY, WHITE FU BABY, and RED FU BABY has no effect on feature outcome. The size and animation are for enterainment purpose only. There is no possibility of retriggering the feature or activating any other features during the free spins. ANY WAYS™ PAYS Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. The maximum number of ANY WAYS™ is 720. Turbo Mode Turn on Turbo Mode to speed up each spin, it can be turned off at any point during the game. When FREE SPINS Feature is awarded, Turbo Mode will automatically be turned OFF. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins. Legal Notices A stable Internet connection is required. In the event of a disconnection, please relaunch the game to complete any outstanding transactions. Unfinished game rounds: Any game rounds that for some reason have not been completed are stored on the server. For example, an unfinished game round may be due to poor internet connection. Unfinished game rounds either automatically resume after a short delay or by restarting the game. An unfinished game round is stored on the server along with the current bet. If a round of betting is not continued within 24 hours, the player will receive any winnings for bets that have been settled. Where the game is not settled, the bet will be returned to the player.
