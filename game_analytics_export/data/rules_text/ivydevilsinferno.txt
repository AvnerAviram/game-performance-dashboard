DEVIL'S INFERNO GAME OBJECTIVES: DEVIL’S INFERNO is a 5-reel, 50 payline slot game. Landing 3, 4 or 5 BONUS symbols anywhere in view will award up to 20 FREE SPINS and 10x BET amount in the DEVIL’S HOT SPINS feature. The game features 8 jackpots on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER which consists of 8 ascending JACKPOT VALUES. There is a chance to increase each JACKPOT VALUE on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER at the start of every game. When 5 to 8 FIRED UP! symbols land anywhere in view during the base game, the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER is triggered and the FIRED UP! feature is awarded. 3 PICKS are awarded in the FIRED UP! feature with a chance of revealing CASH VALUES, EXTRA PICKS and FIRED UP! symbols, which advance up the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER positions. HOW TO PLAY: BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘ +’ or ‘ -‘ buttons either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. C licking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET. REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game. PLAY: The PLAY button in the centre right position starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the Centre bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode. PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the HAMBURGER icon and selecting the PAYTABLE option. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game. VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF. In play-money mode, the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Player's current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen. AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play. To configure AUTOPLAY: Click the AUTO PLAY button then select the required number of plays. AUTOPLAY begins automatically. The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time. MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP): Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information. Click the ‘i’ icon to view game information and rules. Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game play . GAME RULES: DEVIL’S INFERNO is a 5-reel, 50 payline slot game. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the current selected BET. The paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET. All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive order on a payline, starting from the left-most reel, except for the BONUS and FIRED UP! symbols. All wins are paid according to the paytable and are added to the player’s BALANCE. Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid. The BONUS symbol acts as a scatter. When 3 or more BONUS symbols land anywhere in view the DEVIL’S HOT SPINS feature is awarded. If DEVIL’S HOT SPINS and FIRED UP! features are triggered on a single spin, then the FIRED UP! feature will take precedence. The WILD symbol has no payout. BONUS(Scatter) symbol wins are added to other payline wins. The WILD symbol substitutes for all other symbols except for the BONUS and FIRED UP! symbols. The maximum win in this game is capped to 140,000 GBP or its equivalent amount in your currency during the base game, DEVIL’S HOT SPINS or the FIRED UP! feature. All further wins will be forfeited if the maximum win is reached. The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency during the base game, DEVIL’S HOT SPINS or the FIRED UP! feature. All further wins will be forfeited if the maximum win is reached. The win display count-up animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing SPACEBAR (desktop). Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “GAME LOGS” inside any game will allow you to access your gameplay history. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. DEVIL’S HOT SPINS: 3, 4 or 5 BONUS symbols landing anywhere in view during the base game will trigger the DEVIL’S HOT SPINS feature, awarding 6, 10 or 20 FREE SPINS in addition to the line win. 3 x BONUS symbols = 6 FREE SPINS + BONUS symbol payout. 4 x BONUS symbols = 10 FREE SPINS + BONUS symbol payout. 5 x BONUS symbols = 20 FREE SPINS + BONUS symbol payout. DEVIL’S HOT SPINS are played with the high paying symbols (TRIDENT, PLAYING CARD, DICE and DEVIL) and the WILD symbol only. Symbols J, Q, K and A will not appear during DEVIL’S HOT SPINS. DEVIL’S HOT SPINS are played at the same BET as the triggering game. DEVIL’S HOT SPINS cannot be re-triggered. The FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER will not be active during DEVIL’S HOT SPINS. DEVIL’S HOT SPINS will end when there are no more FREE SPINS left. The TOTAL WIN amount will be added to player's BALANCE. FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER: When 5 to 8 FIRED UP! symbols land anywhere in view during the base game, the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER will be activated and lock the corresponding position on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER. 5 x FIRED UP! symbols will activate position 5 on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER. 6 x FIRED UP! symbols will activate position 6 on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER. 7 x FIRED UP! symbols will activate position 7 on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER. 8 x FIRED UP! symbols will activate position 8 on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER. No more than 8 FIRED UP! symbols can be awarded in the base game. The JACKPOT values shown on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER are seeded per BET level. JACKPOT LADDER will be activated and lock the corresponding position on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER and the FIRED UP! feature will be triggered. On entering the FIRED UP! feature, the player will be awarded with 3 PICKS of the 20 DEVIL’S COINS displayed on screen. The number of PICKS remaining are shown in the PICKS LEFT display. Each selection of a DEVIL’S COIN icon reveals either a CASH VALUE, +2 PICKS or a FIRED UP! symbol. Revealing a CASH VALUE awards a random multiplier from the range of 0.2x, 0.4x, 0.6x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.2x, 1.4x, 1.6x and 2x which will multiply the players BET. Accumulated CASH VALUES are displayed in the TOTAL WIN display. Revealing a +2 PICKS will award 2 EXTRA PICKS which are added to the PICKS LEFT display. Revealing a FIRED UP! symbol activates the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER and advances the current locked ladder position by 1. The new position is then locked in. The top 4 positions on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER (positions 9, 10, 11 and 12) can only be activated during the FIRED UP! feature and cannot be activated from the base game. Once all PICKS have been completed, the accumulated TOTAL WIN and the locked FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER position will be paid into the player's BALANCE. The locked Jackpot value on the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER will be reseeded at the start of the following game. If player gets disconnected in the middle of the FIRED UP! feature, player will be prompted to complete it after logging back in. The theoretical return to player from the FIRED UP! JACKPOT LADDER is 31.38%. FIRED UP! feature is played at the same BET as the triggering game.
