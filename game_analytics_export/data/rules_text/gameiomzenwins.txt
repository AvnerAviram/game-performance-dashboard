Zen Wins Zen Wins™ Zen Wins™ is a vibrant 3x3 game featuring a unique blend of symbols and mechanics, with 9 selectable lines and dynamic jackpots that increase with the bet per line. This game stands out with its combination of Double Jackpot, mid-tier, and low-tier WILD symbols that unlock various winning possibilities, alongside innovative multiplier symbols that can exponentially increase line wins. Jackpots are secured by landing three WILD symbols of the same kind on an active payline, each representing different tiers of rewards.
