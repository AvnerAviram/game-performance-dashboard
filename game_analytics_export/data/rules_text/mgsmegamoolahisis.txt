Mega Moolah Isis About Mega Moolah Isis Mega Moolah Isis is a five reel, twenty-five payline, and five hundred coin slot machine. Regular symbol payout rules: Matching symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. All payout tables display the number of coins that are won for each combination. Regular payouts depend on your bet per payline. Only one winning combination is paid out per payline. If there is more than one possible winning combination on a payline, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size bet. Wild, multiplier symbol The Isis symbol is a wild, multiplier symbol. This symbol: Substitutes for other symbols to complete wins. Doubles the payout of any combination it completes as a wild symbol. Creates its own winning combination when multiple symbols appear in a line on an enabled payline. These payouts are not multiplied. Wild symbol substitution rules: Does not substitute for the scatter symbol. Only one winning combination is paid out per payline. If there is more than one possible winning combination on a payline, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. Scatter symbol The Hawk symbol is a scatter symbol. Scatter symbol rules: Two or more symbols must appear anywhere on the reels to create a win. This symbol cannot be substituted by the wild, multiplier symbol. Three or more Hawk symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. Scatter symbol payout rules: Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed, once the reels have come to a stop. Scatter wins are displayed as a multiplier value on the payout table. Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on consecutive reels to win. Free Spins bonus game Three or more Hawk symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. In this game: You are awarded up to thirty free spins. All wins are multiplied by 6 during the free spins. All wild, multiplier symbol wins are also multiplied by 6 during the free spins. Free Spins bonus game rules: The number of symbols that activated the bonus game indicates the number of free spins you win. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. The Free Spins bonus game can be re-activated during the free spins. So, if three or more Hawk symbols are scattered on the reels during the free spins, you are awarded additional free spins. The extra free spins are added to the remaining number of free spins. The Mega Moolah Progressive Jackpot bonus game cannot be activated during the free spins. Winnings are automatically collected during the bonus game. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. In this game: Depending on where the wheel stops, you can win one of the progressive jackpots. Progressive Jackpot rules: The current value of the progressive jackpot is displayed in the game.
