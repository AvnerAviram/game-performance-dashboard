Cobra Hearts BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 50 credits. All wins pay from the left most reel to the right in any positions on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only for each combination. Only highest scatter win pays. All wins except scatters are multiplied by the bet level. Scatter wins are multiplied by 50 credits times the bet level. All wins are added. This game supports 243 ways. Reels 2, 3 and 4 each contain a number of positions that are randomly replaced with [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] before the reel spin is initiated. If each [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X or 5X respectively. All wins are shown in currency. Total credits wagered is the bet level multiplied by 50 credits. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] and [HEART 5X] substitute for DANCER, INCENSE BURNER, HEADDRESS, DRUM, ZILLS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] and [HEART 5X] appear on reels 2, 3 & 4 only. The theoretical return to player for this game is 96% FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 [SWORD] trigger 8, 15 or 25 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 [SWORD] award an additional 3, 8, 15 or 25 free games respectively during the free games. Reels 2, 3 and 4 each contain a number of positions that are randomly replaced with [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] before the reel spin is initiated. Any [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] landing one symbol outside of the reel window will replace the adjacent symbol in the reel window with [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] respectively. [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] and [HEART 5X] substitute for DANCER, INCENSE BURNER, HEADDRESS, DRUM, ZILLS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If each [HEART 2X], [HEART 3X] or [HEART 5X] substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X or 5X respectively. Different reels are used during the free games.
