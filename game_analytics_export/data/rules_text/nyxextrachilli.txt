Extra Chilli Introducing the game Extra Chilli is a six-reel video slot with reacting wins featuring Big Time Gaming’s unique Megaways™. How to play Select your stake by clicking the left and right arrow buttons on the stake. The stake value is displayed in the stake display. All payouts are multiplied by the stake of each individual spin. Auto play To set up Auto play press the Auto button. This will display Auto play panel which the player can use to modify the number of spins, set a loss limit or set a win limit. When a loss limit has been set the player can select Start to commence auto play. When Auto play is on, the Play button will change to a Stop button. The remaining Auto plays will be displayed in the Stop button. The player can cancel Auto play by clicking the Stop button. Auto play will stop automatically if any of the limits set is reached or anything occurs that requires player intervention. Feature Drop™ Feature Drop symbol may appear in any position in the reels to reduce the price of the feature. Feature Drop symbols do not appear in the extra reel or during a feature. If the price is reduced to a zero balance the feature will automatically trigger. Start the spin Press the Play button to start the spin at the stake displayed. The 6 reels will spin and then come to stop, displaying the result. Extra Reel The extra reel adds a symbol to the reel it is above for reels 2,3,4, and 5. Reactions Every winning symbol is part of a reaction and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the reels and from the right in the extra reel. Symbols Wins A win is matching consecutive symbols, regardless of height, from left to right starting from left most reel. Highest win paid per winning combination. All winning combinations are added together. See PAYS for further information. Wild Wilds substitute every symbol except scatter. Wilds are only present in the extra reel. Scatter Get three HOT scatters to win eight Free Spins. Any extra scatter adds an extra four Free Spins. Free Spins Gamble After triggering the Feature, the Free Spins Gamble Wheel is displayed, giving you the chance to win up to 24 Free Spins. You can choose to COLLECT or GAMBLE. Press COLLECT to start the Free Spins with the current number of Free Spins awarded. Press GAMBLE to spin the wheel. A successful gamble will award four extra Free Spins, as well as the opportunity to play another Free Spins Gamble. An unsuccessful gamble awards the number of Free Spins displayed in black. If the number in black is zero the feature ends. Free Spins During Free Spins there is an unlimited win multiplier which starts at 1 and is increased by 1 after each reaction. Free Spin Scatter icons can appear in the extra reel and will award extra free spins. The stake that was used to trigger the free spins will be used for the free spins duration. At the end of the free spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited into the players account and regular play then resumes. Free Spin Scatter The free spin scatter icon can appear in the extra reel during free spins. 3 Scatters = 4 extra Free Spins. 4 Scatters = 8 extra Free Spins. General Information The game without buying Feature Drop has an RTP of 96.15% - 96.41%. The game when buying Feature Drop has an RTP of between 96.26% - 96.82%. In all cases, the optimal strategy is to always spin the Feature Gamble Wheel. Incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity.
