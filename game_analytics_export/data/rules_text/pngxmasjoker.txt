Xmas Joker Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules Xmas Joker is a 3-reel video slot game with 5 fixed paylines, decked with holly, bells and holiday cheer. A combination of three matching symbols along any payline constitutes a win. The elfish Xmas Joker is a scatter symbol. Three Xmas Joker symbols that appear anywhere on the reels trigger a sequence of ten free spins. There is a special Xmas gift waiting to be unwrapped when at least two Xmas Gift symbols appear on a free spin. This Xmas gift is equal to 1-100 times the total bet! On any free spin, three Xmas Gift symbols that appear anywhere on the reels extend the free-spin sequence by an additional ten free spins for a total of up to 50 consecutive free spins. Each of these offers the chance to win more wonderful Xmas surprises. The RTP is 93.99 %. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Actions Paytable - Toggles the display of the paytable. Auto Play - Click the AUTO PLAY button to enable/disable the Auto Play feature. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings . The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Spin - Starts the round by spinning the reels. Stop - Stops the reels more quickly.
