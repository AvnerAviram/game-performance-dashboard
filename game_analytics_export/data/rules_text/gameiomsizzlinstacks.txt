Sizzlin' Stacks Sizzlin' Stacks™ is a vibrant 3x3 game with 9 lines that are always active. This game combines classic slot symbols, a double jackpot symbol that acts as a 2x multiplier, and a persistent wild symbol. Any time a wild symbol lands on the reels, that reel will have 2 spins where that reel becomes a stack of wilds, where every symbol is either a wild or double jackpot symbol. GAME FEATURES Double Jackpot Feature Double Jackpot is wild for seven and bar symbols, except when three Double Jackpot symbols appear on a payline 1 Double Jackpot symbol in a non-jackpot winning combination will double the win 2 Double Jackpot symbols in a non-jackpot winning combination will quadruple the win WILD is wild for seven and bar symbols, except when 3 WILD symbols appear on a payline Persistent Wild Feature During the base game, WILD appearing on the reels will fill the two wild spaces above the reel that it appeared on. The corresponding reel will be turned into a wild reel for the new 2 base games played with the same bet option. All symbols on the Wild Reel will be either WILD or Double Jackpot symbol Wild symbols above the reels and Wild Reels are saved separately for each bet option GAME INFORMATION RTP: 92.06%, 93.96%, 95.04% Reel Configuration: 3x3 Pay Evaluation: 9 Lines Total Bet: $0.45 - $900.00 Volatility: Medium Technology: HTML5 Channel: Mobile & Desktop Malfunction voids all plays and pays.
