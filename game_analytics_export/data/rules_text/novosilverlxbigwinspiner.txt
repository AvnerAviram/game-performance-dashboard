Silver Luxe Big Win Spinner Game Theme and Graphics Silver Lux - Big Win Spinner is a straight forward 3 reel stepper game with a classic look. Main eye catcher in the game is the slow spinning Big Win Spinner wheel. Let this slot have you spin the wheel and win BIG. Main Game The game is played for 10 credits and you can select the value of a credit of your choice. Winning combinations will be awarded on 9 win lines. The "Big Win Spinner" Wild multiplier symbols will help you to increase your winnings even more. Double Seven symbols not only substitutes for Single Seven symbols of the same color but will double the win as well. 3 scattered Wheel symbols will let you spin the Big Win Spinner wheel. Big Win Spinner Feature Spin the Big Win Spinner wheel for big prizes. Did the wheel land at the segment with the arrows. Than you are lucky! The inner wheel will grow and you may spin again for even bigger wins or jackpots. Silver Lux Linked Progressive Jackpot At the top of the game two progressive jackpots are presented, a Major Jackpot and a Grand Jackpot. Both jackpots are linked to the jackpots of other Silver Lux games. The jackpots can be won in the Big Win Spinner Feature. When the inner Big Win Spinner wheel lands on a "grand" segment or on a "major" segment the respective jackpot is yours. Rules All wins are presented in credits, except the Grand and Major Jackpot which are presented in the actual currency. Scatter wins and line wins are added. More than 1 Multiplier symbol may be included in a winning combination, when this happens, each multiplier will multiply the winnings. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
