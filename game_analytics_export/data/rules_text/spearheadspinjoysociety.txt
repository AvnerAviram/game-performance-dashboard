Spin Joy Society General Information SpinJoy Society is video slot which has 5 reels, 3 rows and 15 paylines. The game is played on 15 paylines, the TOTAL BET consists of LINE BET multiplied by the selected line count. The theoretical RTP for this game is 94.17%. Symbols There are 12 symbols in the game, comprising 6 high-paying, 3 low-paying, a Wild symbol, Bonus symbol and the Free Spins symbol Wild symbol substitutes any paying symbol in the winning combination. Wild doesn’t substitute Bonus or Free Spins symbols. Wild only appears on reels 2, 3 and 4. 3 Bonus Symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Bonus Game. Bonus game can't be triggered during Free Spins. 3 or more Free Spins symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Free Spins session. 10, 12, or 15 free spins are awarded for landing 3, 4 or 5 free spins symbols respectively. Wins are paid based on combinations of identical symbols and wilds on a payline. Winning combinations start from the leftmost reel and the symbols must be consecutive along a payline. Wins are multiplied by the Line Bet. Only the highest win pays on each payline. Coinciding wins on different paylines are added together. Free Spins During the FREE SPINS the reels spin automatically with the same line bet as the spin that won the FREE SPINS. The bets are not subtracted from your balance during the FREE SPINS. When this limit is reached, further appearances of 3 or more Free Spins symbols on the reels won't result in additional FREE SPINS. Bonus Game During the BONUS GAME, During the jackpot pick bonus, 15 available picks that will reveal the mini, minor, major, mega, or grand prizepots as pick choices are made. Picks are made until 3 matching prizepots reveal which will end the bonus and award the prizepot that has 3 reveals. The BONUS GAME prize amounts depend on your TOTAL BET amount.
