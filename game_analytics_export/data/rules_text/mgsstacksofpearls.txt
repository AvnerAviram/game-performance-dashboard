Stacks of Pearls Stacks Of Pearls™ is a Bet 40, 30 Line 3x5 reel slot game with Pearl Respin feature. Return to Player (RTP) General Bet Controls Bonus symbol Pearl Respin Feature Autoplay Return to Player (RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time. The RTP is fixed and does not change. This game has an RTP of 95.41%. General Game has fixed 30 lines. 40 Coins buy 30 paylines and eligibility for the bonus. Winning line/s are highlighted on the reels. Regular wins are created when the same symbols appear next to each other on a payline, with one of the symbols displayed on the first slot reel. (All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive ) One regular win is paid per line. If you have more than one combination, you are paid the win that has the highest value. Your Total bet is available for selection on screen and in settings panel. All wins are multiplied by the Bet multiplier.If your total bet is 0.40 Credits per spin, your Bet multiplier = (Total bet / 40 Coins * 100) i.e (0.40 / 40 * 100 = 1x). (x = values displayed on the paytable) Regular wins in paytable are displayed in coins. Your total payout amount in Coins is calculated as (Bet multiplier * values displayed on the paytable). So, if your Total bet is 0.40 Credits per spin, your Bet multiplier = Total bet / 40 Coins * 100 i.e. 0.40 / 40 * 100 = 0.01 * 100 = 1. For example, a 5 of a kind win of Fish symbol is calculated as (1 * 80 coins) = 80 Coins. Your total payout amount in Credits is calculated as (Payout amount in Coins / 100) i.e (80 / 100) = 0.80 Credits. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again. Bonus Pearl appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 during Base Game and Pearl Respin feature. Bonus Pearl converts into Blank Pearl or Wild Pearl. Wild Pearl substitutes for all symbols. 2 full stacks of Bonus Pearls trigger 5 Respins with 2 locked stacks. 3 full stacks trigger 7 Respins with 3 locked stacks. When starting with 2 full stacks, lock in another full stack to win an extra 2 respins. During Pearl Respin feature, triggering reels are locked and spun with the addition of X2 and X3 Wild Pearl Multipliers on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Substituted wins with three X3 multipliers are combined for an X27 multiplier. Pearl Multipliers are multiplied together per line. Pearl Respin feature is played at the triggering lines and bet. Autoplay You can also spin a set number of times in succession without interacting with the game, using Autoplay either in base game or inside menu AUTOPLAY option. You must select a bet amount before you start Autoplay, as it plays with the current bet settings. Tap/Click Autoplay On in base game. Tap/Click the number of spins you want to play. The Autoplay session automatically starts playing. To stop Autoplay, Tap/Click Stop . You can also start the Autoplay from the option available inside menu option. Start button will remain disabled until you select any number of spins. Tap/Click the number of spins you want to play and again Tap/Click on Start , The Autoplay session automatically starts playing. To stop Autoplay, Tap/Click Stop in base game. Custom Mode Autoplay is also available with Custom Mode where some Autoplay Settings are available. Autoplay Settings 1. Coin Size You can select a coin size before you start Autoplay, as it plays with the last bet value. 2. Coins Bet Coins Bet is the number of coins allowed per line. Coins Bet differ between games. 3. Total Bet Total Bet displays the Total Bet value. Total Bet = (Coin Size * Coins Bet * 40 credits). 4. Spins You can set the number of times the slot reels spin during your Autoplay session. 5. Win Limit Stop spins if the number of credits won on any spin exceeds or equals the amount selected. 6. Loss Limit Stop spins if Autoplay exceeds the loss limit value with the next bet. Until Stop Continue Autoplay until you Tap/Click the Stop button or stops if Free Spins or another bonus feature is triggered. Important: This feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Tapping/Clicking on the Stop button will not influence the results of the Spin. Keyboard Shortcut On Tapping/Clicking the Spin button, reels will start spinning and button changes to Stop button. Tapping/Clicking the Stop button will stop the reels. Spin/Stop Button can be selected by pressing Space bar on keyboard. Disclaimer: This feature is applicable for Desktop version only. This feature may not be available in the game you are playing.
