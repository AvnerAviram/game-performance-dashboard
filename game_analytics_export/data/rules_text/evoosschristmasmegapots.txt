Christmas Megapots Introducing the game Christmas Megapots is a 6 reel Megaways™ game with up to 117,649 ways to win, featuring a Hold and Spin with Megapots™. How to play Select your stake by clicking the left and right arrow buttons on the stake. The stake value is displayed in the stake display. All payouts are multiplied by the stake of each individual spin. Auto play To set up Auto play press the Auto button. This will display Auto play panel which the player can use to modify the number of spins, set a loss limit or set a win limit. When a loss limit has been set the player can select Start to commence auto play. When Auto play is on, the Play button will change to a Stop button. The remaining Auto plays will be displayed in the Stop button. The player can cancel Auto play by clicking the Stop button. Auto play will stop automatically if any of the limits set is reached or anything occurs that requires player intervention. Auto play may not be available in your jurisdiction. Start the spin Press the Play button to start the spin at the stake displayed. The reels will spin and then come to stop, displaying the result. Turbo Turbo mode removes unnecessary delays. Turbo can be switched on and off in the OPTIONS menu. Turbo may be limited or unavailable in your jurisdiction. Bonus Buy Press the Bonus Buy button to access the Bonus Buy menu. You may buy 12 Free Spins for 100 times the Free Spins Stake. You may buy a Hold and Spin for 35 times the Free Spins Stake. The Free Spins Stake can be changed by pressing the plus and minus buttons on the Free Spins Stake display. The Free Spins Stake will apply to each Free Spin. Press the price to buy the bonus for that price. The price will be deducted from your balance. Bonus Buy may be limited or unavailable in your jurisdiction. Symbol Wins A win is matching consecutive symbols, regardless of height, from left to right starting from the leftmost reel. Highest win paid per winning combination. All winning combinations are added together. See PAYS for further information. Wild Wild substitutes for all symbols except Coin and Scatter. Wild occurs on reels 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 only. Coin Six or more Coins in any position trigger the Hold and Spin. Scatter Three or more Scatters in any position award 12 Free Spins, plus 2 additional Free Spins for each Scatter beyond the third. Megapots™ Mini, Midi and Mega Megapots are visible above the reels, and contain random prizes which change every spin. Megapots may be awarded during the Hold and Spin only. Mini Megapots can have values from 100 to 300 times stake. Midi Megapots can have values from 1000 to 8888 times stake. Mega Megapots can have values from 20888 to 88888 times stake. Hold and Spin 3 respins are awarded. Every symbol onscreen becomes an individual reel which may reveal additional Coins. All Coins reveal a prize amount, a multiplier, or a Megapots icon. Every time one or more additional Coins are revealed, the number of remaining respins resets to 3. At the end of the respins, the total prize amount is multiplied by the total of all revealed multipliers and awarded as a bonus prize. In addition, every three matching Megapots icons awards the associated Megapots amount, as shown. If every position has a Coin, then the Hold and Spin ends, and the total bonus prize amount is doubled. Free Spins The Free Spins are played with an unlimited Free Spin Win Multiplier that starts at 1 and is increased by 1 for each Coin. The Free Spin Win Multiplier does not apply to Hold and Spin wins and is not affected by Coins that occur during Hold and Spin. If the Hold and Spin is not triggered, then every Coin that occurred in that Free Spin is held in place until the next Free Spin. Three or more Scatters in any position award 4 additional Free Spins, plus 2 Free Spins for each Scatter beyond the third. The stake that was used to trigger the Free Spins will be used for the Free Spins duration. At the end of the Free Spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited into the player's account and regular play then resumes. Return to Player (RTP) The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by dividing the total payouts by the total bets from a simulation of numerous game rounds. Values reflect the maximum expected RTP and do not change. The RTP is defined at different stages or modes of the game.
