Xiao Fu Bao 2 Payouts Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. Scatter wins are added to Any Ways pays. WILD SYMBOL WILD substitutes for all symbols except GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, RED COIN, WHITE COIN, MEGA JACKPOT COIN, MAJOR JACKPOT COIN, MAXI JACKPOT COIN, MINOR JACKPOT COIN and MINI JACKPOT COIN. WILD only appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. WHEEL WILD WHEEL WILD substitutes for all symbols except GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN. WHEEL WILD only appears on reels 2, 3, or 4. WHEEL WILD is only available in base game. WHEEL BONUS FEATURE During the base game, a WHEEL WILD may trigger the Wheel Bonus Feature. If a WHEEL WILD does not trigger the Wheel Bonus Feature, then that WHEEL WILD may add 1x - 8x Total Bet Multiplier to a multiplier prize on the wheel for the active total bet option or may upgrade the Free Spins Feature for the active total bet option based on the table below. If the Wheel Bonus Feature is triggered, then a wheel spin is awarded. The wheel awards a multiplier prize, MEGA, MAJOR, or a Free Spins Feature. If the wheel awards a Total Bet Multiplier, MEGA, or MAJOR, then 1 additional spin is awarded. A fifth spin of the wheel only awards the Free Spins Feature. At the end of the Wheel Bonus Feature, the Total Bet Multipliers are reset to 1x - 10x Total Bet Multiplier and the Free Spins Feature options are reset to the Current Free Spins Feature options based on the table below for the active bet option. If the MEGA or MAJOR jackpot meter is awarded, then the value displayed in the corresponding meter is awarded and reset before any additional prizes are awarded. The wheel does not reflect the odds of a prize being awarded. CASH FALLS FEATURE If any COIN lands on a reel, then all COINS on that reel are held for the next 3 wagered spins at the same bet level. The number of wagers that a reel's COINS will remain held is shown at the bottom of the associated reel. If every position on a reel is filled with a COIN then that reel awards the Cash Falls Feature. The Cash Falls Feature awards the prizes displayed on each of the corresponding reel's COIN's. After a reel awards the Cash Falls Feature, the COINS currently on that reel are no longer held. If the Cash Falls Feature is awarded, and the associated reel contains a BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN, then the free spin variant associated with the BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN is awarded to the player in addition to any values on GOLD COINs included in the Cash Falls Feature award. Symbols that land behind a GOLD COIN, BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN are not evaluated. All information on the Cash Falls Feature pages pertain to the base game. FEATURE COINS BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN each behave the same as a GOLD COIN and can attribute to a Cash Falls Feature. On the wagered spin in which BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN lands on the array, the associated free spins may be awarded. On each spin in which a BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN are locked into a position, the associated free spins may be awarded. Any Feature Coin included in a Cash Falls Feature award will also award the associated free spins. If the free spins are triggered by BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN, 'WINNER' appears next to the BLUE FU BABY, WHITE FU BABY, or RED FU BABY respectively. BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, and RED COIN will remain on the reel for the next three wagered spins, and may trigger the associated feature of the BLUE COIN, WHITE COIN, or RED COIN. MORE FREE SPINS FEATURE If the More Free Spins Feature is triggered with BLUE COIN, then the player is awarded 9-40 free spins. At the beginning of the More Free Spins Feature, the number of free spins is randomly awarded. If a Free Spins Feature is not triggered with BLUE COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded. JACKPOT FREE SPINS FEATURE If the Jackpot Free Spins Feature is triggered with WHITE COIN, then on each free spin MINI JACKPOT COIN, MINOR JACKPOT COIN, MAXI JACKPOT COIN, MAJOR JACKPOT COIN, or MEGA JACKPOT COIN may land on the reel array. Jackpot coins are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. MEGA, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI have 6, 5, 4, 3, and 2 spaces respectively. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, the corresponding jackpot is awarded and its meter is reset to its base value. At the beginning of the Jackpot Free Spins Feature, the jackpot meters may be updated to the range of values in the table below. GAME RULES Only highest win paid per winning combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The appearance of the BLUE FU BABY, WHITE FU BABY, and RED FU BABY has no effect on feature outcome. The size and animation are for entertainment purpose only. There is no possibility of retriggering the feature or activating any other features during the free spins.
