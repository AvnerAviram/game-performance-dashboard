The Nutcracker Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20(cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 20 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Autospin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 95.97% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Winnings on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. On the info bar(s) located under the main game screen, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. Special Symbols and Features Wild The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Scatter and Mystery Symbols to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. Scatter During normal spins, three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels trigger the Free Spins round. Note: Free Spins cannot be retriggered. During Free Spins you cannot change the bet value. Fairy Free Spins Colossal Symbols appear anywhere on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5, only during Free Spins and Dragon Colossal Spins. These are high paying symbols that take over 3x3 or 4x4 positions on the reels. Note : Wilds cannot become Colossal Symbols. Nutcracker Bonus During normal spins, if a Wild appears on the whole third reel (stacked wild), it triggers the Nutcracker Bonus round where you have to pick one of the gifts in order to randomly win 5, 7, 8 or 10 Nutcracker Free Spins. Reel three is Wild and remains in place during Nutcracker Free Spins. In addition, during each Nutcracker Free Spin, every time you get a winning combination, the symbols that form that combination remain in place while the reels respin (the respin is also free) - the winning combination is not yet paid. Also, any other winning symbols from the next respins are held. Note : A respin does not decrease the remaining number of Nutcracker Free Spins. If after a respin you get more winning combinations, the previous winning symbols as well as the new winning symbols remain in place while the reels respin again - the winning combinations are not yet paid. This continues until the final win (formed by all the held symbols) cannot be improved any further (no other symbols that can be held appear) at which point the final win is paid and the Nutcracker Free Spins resume. Note : Fairy Free Spins cannot be triggered during Nutcracker Free Spins. 4.
