Founding Fortunes Wins RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: 21.4 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play. Choose the amount you want to bet. Click the Spin button to spin the reels. This game is played at 720 ways. All 5 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel or rightmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. BONUS WHEEL During the base game, at least one bonus symbol appearing in any position on 5 consecutive reels starts the Bonus Wheel. The wheel will award one of the following: 12, 14, or 16 Free Games Bonus spins per winning combination of bonus symbols. Minor Award, which awards the Minor Jackpot or 50 times the total bet. Major Award, which awards the Major Jackpot or 500 times the total bet. Grand Award, which awards the Grand Jackpot or 5000 times the total bet. FREE GAMES BONUS The Free Games Bonus begins with the number of free games awarded during the Wheel Bonus. A bonus symbol appearing in any position on 5 consecutive reels awards 8 additional free games per winning combination. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. SCATTER BUCKS FEATURE (BASE AND BONUS) Each scatter wild on reels 2, 3, and 4 appears with one of the following credit values times the bet multiplier: Reel 2 – 10 to 1000 credits Reel 3 – 10 to 5000 credits Reel 4 – 10 to 1000 credits All Scatter Bucks values are pre-multiplied by the bet multiplier.
