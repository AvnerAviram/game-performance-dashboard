King of Babylon Action Spins PAYOUTS Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far left reel. All wins that occur on multiple paylines are added to the total win, except for the ACTION SPINS POT wins. The paytable reflects the current bet configuration. WILD SYMBOLS Both WILD and GOLDEN WILD symbols can land on the reels. WILD symbols may randomly turn into GOLDEN WILD symbols on landing. WILD, GOLDEN WILD, expanded WILD and GOLDEN WILD symbols substitute for all other symbols except scatter symbols and each other. All types of WILD symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. WILD and expanded WILD symbols do not appear in Free Spins. When a single WILD symbol lands, it may randomly expand to fill the whole reel, this symbol can also change into an expanded GOLDEN WILD symbol. When a single GOLDEN WILD symbol lands, it may randomly expand to fill the whole reel, this can happen in the base game and in Free Spins. GOLDEN WILD and expanded GOLDEN WILD symbols are used to activate the ACTION SPINS Feature. SCATTER SYMBOL DICE is a scatter symbol and can appear on all reels in both the base game and in Free Spins. Up to 3 DICE symbols can land on each reel. Land 6 or more DICE symbols in a single spin to win Free Spins. ACTION SPINS The Action Spins feature will be activated on any spin where a GOLDEN WILD or an expanded GOLDEN WILD symbol is part of a winline. The Action Spins Feature can award: Multipliers or Extra Action Spins. When the Action Spins feature is activated, it can award: SPINS +3 = 3 Extra Action Spins POT x1 = 1x Action Spins Pot POT x5 = 5x Action Spins Pot BET x25 = 25x total bet + 1x Action Spins Pot BET x100 = 100x total bet + 1x Action Spins Pot BET x250 = 250x total bet + 1x Action Spins Pot BET x1000 = 1000x total bet + 1x Action Spins Pot After the first Action Spin, the remaining Action Spins are played automatically. Any wins which are won using GOLDEN WILD symbols will not be awarded to the player, instead any wins will go into the ACTION SPINS POT. The amount displayed in the ACTION SPINS POT will only be awarded to the player if the wheel lands on any of the multipliers. The amount added to the ACTION SPINS POT will be displayed above the reel set. The ACTION SPIN WIN box will only appear in the Action Spins Feature and will be displayed above the reel set. The wins from this feature will be accumulated and added to the total win. FREE SPINS Land 6 or more DICE symbols in a single spin to win Free Spins. The number of Free Spins won is equivalent to the number of DICE symbols which land in a single spin. WILD and expanded WILD symbols do not appear in Free Spins. All WILD symbols are replaced with GOLDEN WILD symbols for the duration of Free Spins. If the Action Spins Feature is won during Free Spins, it will be activated when Free Spins has finished. Any wins awarded with a GOLDEN WILD or an expanded GOLDEN WILD symbol will be added to the ACTION SPINS POT throughout Free Spins. Additional GOLDEN WILD or expanded GOLDEN WILD wins will not award additional Action Spins. The number of Action Spins awarded per feature trigger is always 3, regardless of how many spins contributed to the ACTION SPINS POT. Land 6 or more DICE symbols during the feature to win additional Free Spins. The number of Free Spins won is equivalent to the number of DICE symbols which land on a single spin. Any additional Free Spins won are added to the total number of Free Spins remaining. Free Spins are played with the same total bet, awards and winning combinations as the base game spin which triggered the feature. Free Spins are played automatically. The feature ends when there are no Free Spins remaining, or when the maximum win has been achieved in Free Spins or in the ACTION SPINS POT.
