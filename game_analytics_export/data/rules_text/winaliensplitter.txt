Alien Splitter Objective: Alien Splitter is a five-reel slot machine with 40 variable paylines. All symbols pay from left to right in any consecutive combinations, except for the scatter symbol that appears anywhere in view. The free spins feature is triggered when 3 or more scatter symbols appear anywhere in view. Information: The game rules are identical in both real-money and play-money modes. The theoretical return to player of this game is 96.02%. How to play: When you enter the game, a default total bet is set. BET : You can change the bet per line by clicking the “+” or “-” buttons between BET PER LINE field. The TOTAL BET is the lines played multiplied by the bet per line and is shown near BET in the lower left of the screen LINES : 40 lines are selected by default. Lines can be changed by clicking the “-“ and “+” buttons between LINES . SPIN : Click on Spin to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations will be paid according to the paytable. Click on MAX BET to select the maximum possible bet. “ AUTO SPIN ”: You can make use of this feature to choose amongst the various options for 'hands free' game play. Options available under this feature are: Number of spins: the game will automatically spin for the number of spins selected from the drop-down menu. You can choose any number of games and play until your balance is less than the total bet placed. Spin till Win equals or exceeds: the game will automatically spin until the win amount is equals or exceeds the amount entered. Spin till Loss not exceeds: the game will automatically spin until the loss amount does not exceed the amount entered. Spin till feature trigger: the game will automatically spin until the feature is triggered. Click the on STOP button to deactivate AUTOSPIN and the normal game is restored. Click the on X button to close the AUTOSPIN dialogue window. All winning symbols will be animated. The total win amount is shown in the WIN window at the bottom right of the screen. Your remaining game balance is shown near the BALANCE meter at the bottom of the screen. Malfunctions void all pays and plays. Browser Button Panel Commands Click on Game Rules to view the detailed rules of the game as web page. Click on Game Logs to view the logs of the games you've played. Click on Responsible Gaming to open Responsible Gaming page. Click on Real\Play to switch to real- or play-money mode. Click on Star to add or remove game from your favourites. Click on Deposit button to deposit money into your account. Rules: Click on the ? button at the bottom of the screen to navigate to the Game Rules. Use scroll to navigate through all game help information. “Alien Split” feature: During the main game, in the alien feature after the wins are awarded, if there are high value symbols on selected lines which could pay on splitting, a flying UFO randomly appears, splitting the high value symbols, after which only the split symbols are paid. A random wild multiplier X1 or X2 is used if wilds form any combinations of split symbols (irrespective of the number of wilds participating). In the free spins feature, during each free spin, after the wins are paid, the alien feature happens always if there are high value symbols on selected lines which could pay on splitting. A flying UFO appears, splitting the high value symbols after which only the split symbols are paid with a random wild multiplier of X1 or X2 or X3 or X4 (irrespective of the number of wilds participating) Free spins feature rules: When 3,4 or 5 scatter symbols appear anywhere on the screen, players are awarded with 8, 12 or 15 free spins respectively. During this feature, players are awarded with an alien split feature along with wild multiplier. All wins during free spins are played with same bet and lines of the triggering game. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Simultaneous or coinciding wins on different paylines are added and paid. All wins occur on selected lines only. The free spins feature cannot be retriggered during free spins. At the end of the free spins, the total amount won will be presented to the player. Free spins feature wins are added to the game balance. Game rules: Play 1 to 40 lines. SPIN begins the game with the currently selected BET and LINES. Payline wins are multiplied by the bet per line. All payouts are made according to the paytable. All line wins are paid left to right, except for the scatter that pays in any position. Only the highest win is paid per line. Wild substitutes for all symbols, except for the scatter. Wild does not substitute for split symbols. It appears on reel 2, 3 and 4 only. PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the “i” button. Wild The wild symbol substitutes for all symbols, except for the scatter symbol. Appears on reel 2, 3 and 4.
