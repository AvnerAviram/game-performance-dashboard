Buffalo All wins shown are multiplied by the active Coin Size. SCATTER pays in any position. 3 or more Scatters trigger FREE GAMES FEATURE. WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. Wild appears on reels 2, 3 & 4 only. FREE GAMES REPEAT WIN FEATURE 8, 15 OR 20 FREE GAMES ARE AWARDED WITH 3, 4 OR 5 SCATTERED WIN RESPECTIVELY. PRESS THE PLAY BUTTON TO START THE FREE GAMES FEATURE. DURING THE FREE GAMES EACH THAT APPEARS ANYWHERE ON REELS 2, 3 OR 4 WILL MULTIPLY THE TOTAL WIN FOR THAT SPIN BY EITHER 2 OR 3. Free Games Feature can be triggered again during the Free Games Feature. IN ADDITION, 5 EXTRA FREE GAMES ARE AWARDED IF ANY 2 SCATTERED OCCUR DURING ANY FREE GAME. Bet and reels played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. GAMERULES All Pays XTRA REEL POWER cost is 40 coins for 1024 ways. Bet is 40 coins x Coin Size. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. Scatter wins are added to XTRA REEL POWERTM wins. RTP: 94.72% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game.
