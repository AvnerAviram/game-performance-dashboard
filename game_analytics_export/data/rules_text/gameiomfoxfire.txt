Fox Fire RTP: 96.0% Volatility: 15 Game Rules This game is played at between 729 and 117649 ways. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. BASE GAME By default each reel consists of 3 symbol positions. Landing a fox activator symbol on an inactive reel will cause that reel to activate. Activated reels expand to include one additional symbol position on each subsequent bet, up to a maximum of 7 symbol positions. After a bet where a reel expanded to 7 symbol positions, that reel is deactivated and resets to having 3 symbol positions. Activated reels are different from the default reels. Above each reel is a meter consisting of a number of segments. By default no segments are filled on these meters. After a bet where a reel expanded to 7 symbol positions, that reel’s meter fills one empty segment. After a bet where the last segment of a reel’s meter is filled, that reel will remain consisting of 7 symbol positions for the subsequent 5 bets before deactivating and resetting to having 3 symbol positions. The meter above reels 1 and 6 has 6 segments. The meter above reels 2 and 5 has 7 segments. The meter above reels 3 and 4 has 8 segments. Placing a bet at a different bet multiplier will deactivate each reel, reset each reel to having 3 symbol positions, and remove all filled segments from the meters above each reel. The wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the fox activator symbol. The wild appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. The wild appears on reel 5 only when that reel has been activated by a fox activator symbol. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/4/2020
