Diamond Mystery: 100 Imperial Crown deluxe Game Theme and Graphics Diamond Mystery™ - 100 Imperial Crown deluxe is a 5x4 100 lines slot game with glossy fruits, expanding Crowns and scattered Stars. Main Game Wins pay from left to right – Scatters pay in any position. Star symbols are Scatter symbols. Blue Stars only appear on reels 1, 3, and 5. The Crown (Wild) symbol appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. The Crown (Wild) symbol expands and substitutes for all symbols on the same reel except Scatters. Jackpot Diamonds Feature The Jackpot Diamonds Feature uses the four-level Diamond Mystery™ Jackpot Feature Each mystery level has specific gems. The base win of each jackpot depends on your stake. The base win is 5.000 times your stake for the Diamond Jackpot, 1.250 times your stake for the Red Gem Jackpot, 200 times your stake for the Blue Gem Jackpot, and 50 times your stake for the Green Gem Jackpot. Green Gem - 1 level (lowest jackpot value) Blue Gem - 2 level Red Gem - 3 level Diamond - 4 and highest level Once triggered, the player is taken to the Jackpot Cards game where he/she can pick one of 12 face-down cards. The player picks cards until 3 matching gem symbols have been revealed. The Jackpot Diamonds Mystery level corresponding to the revealed gems will be awarded. Rules Crown (Wild) substitutes for all symbols except Stars (Scatters). All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on selected lines excluding Scatters. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per Scatter combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 2/1/2024
