Triple Double Patriot High Limit BONUS MULTIPLIERS Multipliers multiply winning combinations except progressives. Two or more Bonus Multiplier symbols on an active payline multiply together and multiply the winning non-progressive combination. 2X multipliers are available on Red 7 and Double Bar symbols. 3X multipliers are available on White 7 and Single Bar symbols. 4X multipliers are available on Blue 7 and Single Bar symbols. 5X multipliers are available on Red 7, Single Bar, and Double Bar symbols. MULTIPLIER WILD SYMBOLS The Patriot, Eagle, and Bell Wild symbols are Wild and substitute for any non-Wild symbol to make winning combinations with Seven and Bar symbols. Wilds do not substitute for Bonus Multipliers on symbols. The Patriot Wild symbol pays 3X winning non-progressive combinations. The Eagle Wild symbol pays 2X winning non-progressive combinations. Two Wild symbols on an active payline multiply together and multiply the winning non-progressive combination. Multiplier Wild symbols and Bonus Multipliers on an active payline multiply together to multiply the winning non-progressive combination. SYMBOL-TRIGGERED PROGRESSIVES The highest progressive jackpot prize is available at max bet only. 3 Patriot Wild symbols on an active payline 9 awards the top progressive jackpot. 3 Patriot Wild symbols on an active payline 1-8 awards the 2nd tier progressive jackpot. 3 Eagle Wild symbols on an active payline awards the 3rd tier progressive jackpot. 3 Bell Wild symbols on an active payline awards the 4th tier progressive jackpot. 3 of any Wild symbols on an active payline awards the 5th tier progressive jackpot. Only the highest progressive tier achieved is awarded. Progressive prizes are not multiplied by the Wild symbol multipliers. 3 Wild symbols on an active payline will pay only the progressive tier for the respective combination. The odds of winning the progressives improve as Lines Bet increases. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers for the progressive games.
