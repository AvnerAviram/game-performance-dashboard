Mustang Money Rings Of Fortune How to Play SPIN / PLAY — Click the ▶ / 🔄 button or press the spacebar to start the game. After the reels stop, any winning combinations on active lines are paid according to the paytable. STOP — Once the game starts, the SPIN button becomes a STOP (⏹) button. Clicking it stops the reels visually but does not affect the final outcome. AUTO PLAY — Click the 🔁 / ⏩ / ➕ button to enable autoplay. Choose the number of automatic spins using the + / ▲ or - / ▼ controls. You can stop autoplay anytime using the STOP button. MAXIMUM BET — Click the MAX button to place the highest possible bet. GAME HISTORY — View previous results using the clock (🕓) button. RESULT — Winning combinations are highlighted on the reels and displayed in the message area. The rules are identical in both real-money and demo modes. All winnings are paid in the same currency as your balance. Ensure your bet is correct before spinning. Incomplete games will resume upon reopening. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules All available bet options are multiplied by a fixed multiplier of 75. Wins occur from left to right on adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel, except for scatter and bonus symbols. Only the highest win per line is paid, while all wins across different lines are added together. Scatter symbols can pay in any position where applicable. The theoretical Return to Player (RTP) for this game is 96.08%. Paytable The payout depends on symbol combinations appearing across 5, 4, 3, or 2 reels. Higher-value symbols offer payouts such as $40.00 for 5 matches, decreasing for fewer matches. Lower-value symbols provide payouts ranging from $25.00 to $10.00 for 5 matches, with reduced values for 4 and 3 matches. All payouts follow the structure defined in the game paytable and are multiplied based on the bet size. Special Symbols & Features Wild Symbol — Substitutes for all regular symbols except scatter symbols. Appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. Scatter Symbols — Trigger bonus features and can appear anywhere on the reels. They also award payouts independently of paylines. Coin Symbols — Appear with random prize values such as 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 12, 15, 20, or 25 multiplied by 50x the bet per line. They may also contain special prize indicators. Bonus Feature The bonus feature is triggered by landing 6 scatter symbols. Initially, 3 free spins are awarded. Scatter symbols that trigger the feature remain locked in position. Any additional scatter resets the free spins back to 3. Coin symbols appear during the feature with random prize values, which are collected and awarded at the end of the feature. No line wins are awarded during the bonus feature. The feature may transition into additional bonus rounds when specific symbol conditions are met. Advanced Bonus Mechanics During bonus rounds, multiple special symbols may appear and remain locked until the feature ends. Coins collected may trigger further bonus stages when enough are accumulated. Multipliers may apply to coin values depending on special symbol interactions: 2x multiplier when specific symbols land inside designated areas 3x multiplier under enhanced conditions 5x multiplier for premium symbol combinations Collecting 15 special symbols awards an additional prize. Feature Rules The total bet during bonus features remains the same as the triggering spin. All wins are accumulated and paid at the end of the feature. Additional bonus triggers may occur based on symbol combinations. Game Controls Balance — Displays your current account balance. Total Bet — Shows the current bet amount. Win — Displays winnings from the current spin. Sound — Toggle game sounds on or off. Help — Opens the help and rules section. Fast Mode — Enables quicker reel spins. Important Information All prizes are awarded based on the current bet value. Game outcomes are random and independent for each spin.
