Power of Gods: Medusa The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and 10 paylines. Wins are paid for combinations of 3, 4 and 5 identical symbols on an active bet line, from left to right on consecutive reels. Only the highest win per line is paid. Wild substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbols. Bonus symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-10x, 12x and 15x player’s bet. Wins for Bonus symbols are paid out only during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. SPECIAL FEATURES STICKY BONUS SYMBOLS During the base game, Bonus symbols may appear as a Sticky symbol. Sticky Bonus symbols have a countdown with a random value from 1 to 9 which decreases with each spin. When the countdown reaches 0, the symbol becomes a standard Bonus symbol. Hold the Jackpot Bonus A draw of at least 6 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. All symbols are removed from the reels, with the exception of the Bonus symbols that activated the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Sticky Bonus symbols are converted to standard Bonus symbols. The Bonus symbols that activated the Hold the Jackpot Bonus and those drawn during the bonus stay on the reels. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR symbols may randomly appear during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Bonus symbols during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus include: Medusa, Mystery and Medusa Mystery. Hold the Jackpot Bonus is played on 15 independent reels. 3 respins are granted at the beginning of the bonus round. Every time any Bonus symbol is drawn, it resets the number of respins to 3. When a Medusa symbol is drawn, it accumulates all values from standard Bonus symbols and Medusa symbols on the reels. Mystery symbol can transform into standard Bonus symbols, MINI, MINOR and MAJOR symbols. Medusa Mystery symbol can transform into the MINI, MINOR and MAJOR symbols. Mystery and Medusa Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Games. The bonus ends when all respins are finished or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. When Hold the Jackpot Bonus ends, the prize is paid according to the value of the Bonus symbols. The GRAND prize is paid when all 15 reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Hold the Jackpot Bonus is played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated the bonus. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the Current win counter and returns to the main game. The gamble feature has seven rounds.
