3 Fortune Wishes Game Overview 3 Fortune Wishes is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring 20 fixed paylines. Set in a magical world guided by a mystical Genie, the game includes interactive features such as the 3 Lucky Lamps, Cash & Collect mechanics, and high-value Cash Diamond prizes. Wins are paid from left to right for matching symbols on adjacent reels. Reels and Lines The game is played on a 5x3 grid with 20 fixed paylines. Wins are awarded for three or more matching symbols on a payline starting from the leftmost reel. Wild Symbols Wild symbols substitute for all other symbols except for Scatters, Cash Diamonds, and Treasure Collect Boxes. Cash & Collect Feature During the base game and Free Games, Cash Diamonds can appear on the reels. These symbols hold prize values up to 200x the total bet. When a Treasure Collect Box lands, it collects the value of all visible Cash Diamonds on the screen, instantly awarding the total as a win. If more than one Collect Box appears, the total collected amount is multiplied accordingly. Lucky Lamps Feature There are three Lucky Lamps in the game: Blue, Green, and Red. Each is linked to a specific Free Game mode and is triggered by collecting three matching colored Butterflies. Blue Lucky Cash Lamp When this feature is triggered, one symbol is randomly selected. During the five Free Games awarded, every time the selected symbol lands, it transforms into a Cash Diamond, which can then be collected for additional wins. Green Lucky Collect Lamp This mode awards five Free Games with a permanently active Collect Box. Any Cash Diamonds appearing on the reels during these spins are automatically collected, significantly increasing win potential. Red Lucky Multiplier Lamp This feature activates five Free Games with a random multiplier applied on each spin. The multiplier can reach up to 10x, boosting all win amounts for that spin. Free Game Mechanics All three Lucky Lamp Free Game types can be triggered separately or together. It is possible to retrigger Free Games without limit. When multiple types are active, their effects stack, creating even greater opportunities for large wins. Game Rules Summary The Cash & Collect feature is available in both the base game and Free Games. All wins are paid from left to right on selected paylines. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatters, Cash Diamonds, and the Collect Box. Feature Availability Table Cash Diamonds, Treasure Collect Boxes, and the Lucky Lamp features are active during Free Games, while Cash Diamonds and Collect Boxes can also appear during base gameplay. Additional Information Multiple Free Game features may be active at once. Retriggering is unlimited. The game is optimized for various regions and supports automatic localization for language and settings.
