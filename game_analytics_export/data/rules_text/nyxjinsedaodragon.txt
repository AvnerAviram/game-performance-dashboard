EXPANDING REELS FEATURE If 1 or 2 GLOWING ORB symbols appear on reel 1, then before the reels evaluate, GLOWING ORB symbols will nudge up or down to fill all 3 positions of that reel. 3 GLOWING ORB symbols on reel 1 will trigger Expanding Reels Feature and up to 3 rows will be added above reels 2 – 5. GLOWING ORB symbols on reels 2 – 5 displays a prize value of 1x - 20x the Total Bet, or a jackpot. During this feature, each GLOWING ORB symbol on adjacent reels beginning from reel 2 will award the prize displayed on it. Prize values appearing on GLOWING ORB symbols have already been multiplied by the Total Bet. MINI jackpot can be awarded on GLOWING ORB symbols only on reels 4 & 5. MINOR and MAJOR jackpots can be awarded on GLOWING ORB symbols only on reel 5. MINI, MINOR or MAJOR awards the value displayed in the corresponding meter. ANY WAY™ pays are not evaluated during Expanding Reels Feature. EXPANDING REELS FEATURE – SECOND CHANCE The Expanding Reels Feature may be randomly triggered on a base game spin if reel 2 contains at least one GLOWING ORB symbol and there are no like symbols on reels 1 and 2. If the feature is triggered, before the reels evaluate, all 3 positions on reel 1 will be filled with GLOWING ORB symbols. WHEEL BONUS 3 scattered YIN-YANG symbols on reels 3, 4 and 5 trigger the Wheel Bonus. 1 spin of the wheel is awarded. The wheel will award either a Free Spins Bonus or a Jackpot. Award Type Awards Jackpot MEGA, MAJOR, MINOR or MINI award the value displayed in the corresponding meter Free Spins Bonus 5 – 20 free spins FREE SPINS BONUS If Expanding Reels Feature is triggered during Free Spins Bonus, up to 5 rows will be added above reels 2 – 5. Expanding Reels Feature cannot be triggered via Second Chance during Free Spins Bonus. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Bonus. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except YIN-YANG and MAJOR do not appear during Free Spins Bonus. During the Free Spins Bonus the Total Bet remains the same as the spin that triggered the Bonus. ANY WAY™ PAYS Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination, but may create separate winning combinations. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. 243 Ways pays are multiplied by the Total Bet. 243 Ways pays must occur on the adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel.
