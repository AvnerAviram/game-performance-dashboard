WILDS The Diamond Double Wild Jackpot symbol is wild and substitutes for all non-wild symbols to make winning non-progressive combinations. One Diamond Double Wild Jackpot symbol landing in a winning non-progressive combination multiplies prize by 2x. Two Diamond Double Wild Jackpot symbols landing in a winning non-progressive combination multipliers prize by 4x. Only highest winner paid per line.
