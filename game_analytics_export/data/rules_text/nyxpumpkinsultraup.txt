Pumpkins Ultra Up Pumpkins UltraUp is a Bet 40, 40 Lines 3x5 Reel slot game with UltraUp Respins. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 94.04% with any bet. Game Rules Pumpkin UltraUp Rules Play Bet 40 for 40 lines. Wild Witch substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. All wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. All wins on selected paylines only except SCATTER. Wins on different paylines are added. Highest win only on each selected payline. Payouts are made according to the Paytable. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. UltraUp Respins Appears during UltraUp Respins only. 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12 or 13 {Scatter Pumpkin} triggers 3 UltraUp Respins. Triggering {Scatter Pumpkin} are held as {Pumpkins} throughout Respins. Additional {Pumpkins} are held and reset Respins to 3. Land {UltraUp} on {Level 1 Pumpkin} or {Level 2 Pumpkin} to increase its level. Level 1 {Pumpkins} may contain: 20, 40, 80, 120 or 160 credit prizes. Level 2 {Pumpkins} may contain: 200, 240, 280, 320 credit prizes or Mini. Level 3 {Pumpkins} may contain: 800, 1200 credits prizes or Mini, Minor, Major or Grand. When no Respins remain, prizes are revealed and then awarded. Only one prize revealed per symbol held. 10 collected Pumpkins of any level unlocks 5 additional positions starting from the next Respin. 15 collected Pumpkins of any level unlocks 5 additional positions starting from the next Respin. Maximum of 25 positions. {UltraUp} and {Pumpkins} cannot land on the same position and during the same Respin. {UltraUp} cannot land on a level 3 {Pumpkin}. Mini, Minor, Major or Grand Jackpot prizes are fixed, are not progressive, and are not multiplied by the bet multiplier. During the reveal, Mini, Minor and Major Prizes may be won more than once. During the reveal, Grand Prize may be won only once. Up to 12 level 2 {Pumpkins} may be held at any one time. Up to 6 Level 3 {Pumpkins} may be held at any one time. How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. The game displays all your details in the following display fields. Balance: Displays your total balance. Win: Displays the amount won in the spin. Total Bet: Displays the total wager staked. Total Win: Displays the accumulated wins during Respins.
