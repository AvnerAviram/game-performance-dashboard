Moon Temple Moon Temple is a 5 reel slot game with a Collection Games bonus and Moon Spins. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 97.02% with any bet. Game Rules Moon Temple is a 3x4x5x5x4x3 reel, 80-line video slot. The game feature has two parts Collection Games and Moon spins. Moon Spins are accumulated during Collection Games and Played at the conclusion of the Collection Game Spins. Paytable amount initially displayed are based on current credits selected All 80 lines always played. Wins on different bet lines are added. Select Credits per spin (50, 100, 150, 250 or 500). All credits staked are multiplied by the credit value. Only highest win on a pay line pays. The won field displays winnings in dollars. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. Scatter wins (if available) are paid in addition to reel wins. WILD PRINCESS substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. 3 Scattered BONUS TEMPLE symbols triggers 8 Free Collection Games. BONUS TEMPLE appears during the base game. The base game, and each stage of the feature, use different reel sets. Collection games cannot be retriggered. Further moon spins cannot be collected during moon spins. Collection games and moon spins are played at the triggering spin bet and credit value. MOON SPIN+1 appears and valid only in collection games. 1 MOON SPIN is collected for each scattered MOON SPIN +1 appearing during each Collection Game. Moon Spin Game Rules Moon Spins are accumulated during 8 Collection Games and played at the conclusion of the Collection Game Spins During Moon Spins, RED PANTHER becomes WILD PANTHER. WILD PANTHER substitutes for all symbols Moon Spin Games used different reel sets. Moon Spin Meter shows the number of Moon Spins collected during Collection Games Moon Spin Meter reduces by one for each Moon Spin played, until there are no Moon Spins remaining.
