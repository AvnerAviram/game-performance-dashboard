Golden Wins Deluxe has a symbol initiated Free Spin Bonus and Wheel Bonus. Free Spins 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols starting on the leftmost reels or more triggers the free spins selection bonus. Player can choose 1 of 4 selections; 15 Free Games with 243 ways 10 Free Games with 1024 ways 5 Free Games with 3125 ways 3 Free Games with 7776 ways During free spins, 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols starting on the leftmost reels or more triggers additional free games. Number of additional free spins are same as number of free spins selected initially. Wheel Bonus Wheel Bonus can be triggered randomly when any WILD symbol landing on the base game screen. Possible Main Wheel prizes are GRAND, MAJOR, MINOR, MINI, BONUS, or credit prize of 88, 188, 288, 388 , 588, 788, 888, 1888 OR 3888. After main wheel stops, the inner wheel opens. Prizes on the inner wheel changes depending on which prize is won from the main wheel. 1.Credit prize Wedge Credit prize wedges awards the credit shown on the wedge then, spin the inner wheel to be awarded a multiplier of 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x or a respin of the wheel bonus. If a multiplier is awarded, the credit award will be multiplied by the amount shown. Multiplier awards only apply to the current spin and do not affect credit awards from previous spins of the wheel bonus. If the respin is won, one spin of the wheel bonus is awarded. Respin is given after initial credit prize spun up is awarded. The respin can be won again during respin of the wheel bonus. 2.Bonus Wedge The bonus wedge awards coin grab bonus. then, spin the inner wheel to be awarded 18, 28, 38, 58 or 88 coin grab bonus picks. For the last pick, INGOT will be awarded. Touch INGOT to reveal credit prize between 788 and 1888 credits. Credit prizes are multiplied by the bet multiplier as shown on the screen. 3.Bonus Wedge GRAND, MAJOR, MINOR and MINI wedges award the corresponding bonuses. then, spin the inner wheel to be awarded 1, 2, 3, 5 or 8 boost bonus. Boost bonus is a single random credit prize in additional to the bonus won. Boost bonus prize is multiplied by the bet multiplier as shown on the screen.
