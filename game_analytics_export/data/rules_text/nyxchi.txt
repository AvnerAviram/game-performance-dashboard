Chi Game Rules 1. Chi is a slot with 5 reels and 3 vertical columns with 27 paylines paying both ways resulting in a total of 54 paylines. 2. The game offers 12 bet levels for each currency. 3. Each bet is 100 coins. 4. Bet lines are paid from top to bottom and from bottom to top. Only the highest paying payline per active bet line is paid out. 5. A game round starts on the selected bet level when the player hits the Spin button. 6. The game can be played automatically by activating Auto Spin. Hold down the Spin button or use the Auto Spin button to set the number of game rounds to be played automatically. 7. Winnings and winning combinations are paid out according to the paytable. 8. Winnings are presented in the player's currency or in coins. The player can change preference in settings. 9. Chi is played in three modes; Normal mode, Free spins mode and Bonus mode. 10. Three or more free spins symbols in Normal mode trigger free spins. 3 free spins symbols award 10 free spins, 4 free spins symbols award 15 free spins, and 5 free spins symbols award up to maximum of 20 free spins. Three or more free spins symbols during Free spins mode retrigger additional free spins. 11. A hit on the fire spirit Chi to the left awards 1-6 wilds on the reels. In Free spins mode, wilds generated by Chi become sticky for the remaining spins. 12. A hit on the wind spirit Mow to the right awards a multiplier of x2 to x5. In Free spins mode, the first hit awards a multiplier of x2, and every additional hit increases the multiplier with x1 up to the maximum of x5. 13. Three bonus symbols trigger the Bonus mode. 14. If Bonus mode is triggered during the Free spins mode, the free spins are paused to be resumed after the end of the Bonus mode. 15. The first part of the Stairway to Fortune bonus game consists of 17 increasing multipliers ranging from x2 to x200. The second part, the Fortune Reels, consists of a tower of 5 horizontal reels, with multipliers ranging from x10 up to a maximum of x1,500. 16. In the first part of the bonus game, the fire spirit Chi draws a random number between 1 and 6 to be given to the wind spirit Mow. Mow then moves the corresponding number of steps towards the Fortune Reels. If Collect is drawn, the multiplier value currently held by Mow is collected and the Bonus mode ends. 17. The second part of the bonus game starts off with a collected x200 multiplier. One spin on each of the 5 reels is awarded. Every hit is added to the multiplier total. Hitting the arrow on the final reel also awards the top x10,000 multiplier. 18. The Bonus mode ends when no spins remain on the Fortune Reels, Collect is drawn or when the maximum win has been reached. 19. Winnings are paid out at the end of a game round. 20. No bets can be changed during an ongoing spin or in any Bonus mode. 21. The theoretical payout (RTP) in Chi is 96.0% 22. The game has a maximum win of 1,250,000 coins. 23. In the event of malfunction all bets and wins are void. Last modification date: 6/4/2020
