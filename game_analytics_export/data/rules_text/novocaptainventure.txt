Captain Venture Rules Set sail with Captain Venture on your quest for treasure and get ready to claim your bounty. Countless legends surround burried gold but we're not spinning any seaman's yarn: Treasure island does really exist. Set sail and steer straight in the direction of the bonus feature! Your aim at Captain Venture is to land 5 matching symbols side by side on up to 10 win lines . The winning patterns run from left to right. Bonus Symbol If at least 3 Steering Wheel symbols (Scatter symbols) land on any screen position the bonus games will be triggered. The Captain (Joker symbol) substitutes all symbols except for the Scatter symbols. Bonus Feature 3 or more Scatter symbols (Steering Wheel) in any position on the reels trigger the bonus feature with 10, 12, 14, 16, 18 or 20 free games. The Steering Wheel determines how many free games you have won. All winning combinations quadruple your wins during the free games. The Captain replaces any symbol except for the Scatter symbol and complements your winning combinations. The Captain symbol also doubles the value of your winning combinations (a line win with a Captain pays eightfold compared to a regular game). 3 or more Scatter symbols during the current free game win more free rounds. You can call up the Paytable during the game by clicking on the ‘Paytable’ button in the bottom left corner of the playing field.
