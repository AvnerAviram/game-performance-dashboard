Rumble Rumble Rules 'Wild' substitutes for all symbols except scatters, 'Wild Coin' and 'Fire Bull'. 'Fire Bull' appears on reels 2 and 4 only. 'Coin' and 'Wild Coin' are scatters. 'Wild Coin' appears on reels 1, 3 and 5 only. 'Wild Coin' substitutes for all symbols except 'Fire Bull'. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Wins on different lines are added. Wins are added to Cash after Gamble, Spin or transfer win. The game has a return to player of 94.08%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Game Features 8 free games are triggered by any 3 or more scatters. All reels expand to nine positions. 'Fire Bull' can only appear on the top three positions of reels 2 and 4 only. 'Coin' can only appear on the top three positions of each reel. Any 2 or more scatters trigger 5 more free games. The Feature paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games. Fire Bull Feature When 'Fire Bull' appears in a blue bordered reel position on reels 2 or 4, all symbols on that reel are transformed to 'Wild' symbols. When 'Fire Bull' appears in the red bordered position on reel 2 only, all symbols on reels 1, 2 and 3 are transformed to 'Wild' symbols except 'Coin' which is transformed into 'Wild Coin'. When 'Fire Bull' appears in the red bordered position on reel 4 only, all symbols on reels 3, 4 and 5 are transformed to 'Wild' symbols except 'Coin' which is transformed into 'Wild Coin'. When 'Fire Bull' appears in both the red bordered positions on reels 2 and 4, all symbols on all reels are transformed to 'Wild' symbols except 'Coin' which is transformed into 'Wild Coin'.
