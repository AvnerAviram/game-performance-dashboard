Buster Hammer Carnival Buster Hammer Carnival is the sequel to the popular Buster Hammer gameboasting a NEW 3125 WAYS, 5x5 reel layout in the base game. The game presents exciting and unique features that will entertain players; culminating during the Mighty Free Spins where the reels expand to a 5x8 grid with a MASSIVE, dare we say MEGA, 32,768 WAYS to win! The Theoretical Return to Player(RTP) is: Buster Bet Level 1 (50 Credits): 95.970% Buster Bet Level 2 (75 Credits): 96.061% Buster Bet Level 3 (100 Credits):96.338% Rules Game Rules Each bet plays 3125 Ways. During Free Spins all reels expand to 8 positions high for 32,768 Ways. Total Bet is the Base Bet multiplied by the Total Credits. Total Credits is either 50 credits, 75 credits or 100 credits based on the selected BUSTER BET level. Payouts are made according to the payable. All wins are multiplied by the Base Bet, except SCATTER wins which are multiplied by the Total Bet. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Winning combinations can occur anywhere on all reels. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels, except SCATTER which pays any. WILD HAMMER substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. At BUSTER BET level 1 (50 credits), WILD HAMMER appears on Reel 3 only. At BUSTER BET level 2 (75 credits), WILD HAMMER appears on Reel 3 and 4 only. At BUSTER BET level 3 (100 credits), WILD HAMMER appears on Reel 2,3 and 4 only. GOLD WILD HAMMER only appears during the WILD STRIKER Feature. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wild Striker The WILD STRIKER Feature is awarded whenever 1 or more WILD HAMMER appears in a winning spin. All winning combinations on the triggering spin are awarded Buster will hit the WILD STRIKER once for each reel that WILD HAMMER has appeared on. After each hit, starting from bottom position, up to 5 symbols on the corresponding reel change to GOLD WILD HAMMER. All WILD HAMMER symbols on the corresponding reel change to GOLD WILD HAMMER. After all WILD STRIKER hits are complete, all winning combinations are awarded except for SCATTER combinations. If Free Spins and the WILD STRIKER Feature are triggered simultaneously on the same spin, the WILD STRIKER Feature plays first, before commencing Free Spins. Free Spins are only awarded once on the initial spin, and no additional Free Spins are awarded after each WILD STRIKER hit. The WILD STRIKER Feature can also be awarded during Free Spins. During Free Spins, after each WILD STRIKER hit, up to 8 symbols on the corresponding reel change to GOLD WILD HAMMER. Buster Bet Adjust the BUSTER BET level to add WILD HAMMERS to more reels: Level 1 – Bet 50 credits for WILD HAMMERS on Reels 3. Level 2 – Bet 75 credits for WILD HAMMERS on Reels 3 and 4. Level 3 – Bet 100 credits for WILD HAMMERS on Reels 2,3 and 4. Mighty Free Spins Feature The Mighty Free Spins Feature is triggered when 3 or more SCATTER appear. 10 Free Spins are awarded. All reels expand to be 8 positions high for the duration of the Free Spins, with 32,768 ways to win. Free Spins are played at the bet of triggering spin. SCATTER symbols do not appear during Free Spins. Free Spins cannot be retriggered. Extra symbols that appear during reel expansion from 5 to 8 symbols on the triggering spin, do not award any extra wins. How to Play Pick your bet amount and push spin button. Spin Button Places a bet based on current lines selected and bet per line; then spin the reels. Menu Button Opens a window where the player can view info pages; go to lobby; change bets; configure autoplay and adjust game settings. It defaults to info pages. Balance Meter Display the player's current balance. Total Bet Meter Display the total wager staked Win Meter Display the current win amount. Info Button Display info pages. Return to Game Button Go back to Base Game. Home Button Go to Casino Lobby. Configurable per operator. Autoplay Button Opens Autoplay configuration window, where the player can select the desired number of autoplay and start autoplay. Stop Autoplay Button Stops autoplay. Number of autoplays remaining will be shown over the button. Bet Settings Button Opens Bet configuration window, where player can select the desired bet amount. Game Settings Button Open Game settings configuration window, where the player can adjust the following: - Turn sounds On or Off - Turn background music On or Off (keep sound effects On) - Turn full screen mode On or Off - Turn left hand mode On or Off Sound Buttons Turn sounds On or Off respectively. Buster Bet Button Exclusive to Buster Hammer Carnival game, it adjusts the Buster Bet (ante bet) from the following: - Level 1 : Wild Hammers are added on reel 3 (default) - Level 2: Wild Hammers are added on reel 3 and 4 - Level 3 : Wild Hammers are added on reel 2, 3 and 4 Keyboard Shortcuts Space bar: Pressing the Space bar will initiate Spin.
