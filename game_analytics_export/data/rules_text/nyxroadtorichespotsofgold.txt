Road to Riches Gold Pots WILD and GOLD POT symbols can appear on all reels. WILD substitutes for all symbols except GOLD POT. GOLD POT symbols are scatter symbols. GOLD POTS BONUS Land three or four GOLD POT symbols to be awarded the GOLD POTS BONUS. Five GOLD POT symbols awards the GOLD POTS SUPER BONUS. Greater prizes can be won by triggering the bonus features with more GOLD POT symbols. Spin the wheel to win a cash prize. Bronze, Silver and Gold cash prizes are available. Prizes on the wheel are displayed as multipliers of the total bet which triggered the feature. To win the GOLD POTS BONUS: Land 3 GOLD POT symbols to win between 30x and 1000x total bet. Land 4 GOLD POT symbols to win between 40x and 2000x total bet. To win the GOLD POTS SUPER BONUS: Land 5 GOLD POT symbols to win between 50x and 5000x total bet. One spin of the wheel is awarded per feature trigger. Bonus wins are added to line wins. RTP:- Theoretical return to the player for this game is 96.40%. Malfunction voids all pays and play.
