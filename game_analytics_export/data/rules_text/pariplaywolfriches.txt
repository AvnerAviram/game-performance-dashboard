Wolf Riches Game Overview Game Description Through the ages, many stories and legends have been about wolves. Renowned for their tenacity, strength, sense of community and impeccable hunting skills, these majestic creatures have captured the attention and imagination of man for generations. Now is your opportunity to walk and play with the wolves as you hunt down big WINS in this thrilling 5-reel, 3-row slot. Wolf Riches is a 50 payline adventure slot that is overflowing with features. The Full Moon random rewards feature places a special prize above reels 1,3 and 5 that is awarded when the Full Moon symbol lands on the respective reel during the base game. The random win multiplier can appear at any time during a spin to award a multiplier betwen x2 and x5. Wolf Riches also boasts Free Spins with extra rewards, a jackpot wheel, stacked wilds and for even more chances to win, a reel tilt feature. Sink your fangs into Wolf Riches slot for some howling good spins and wins! Game Information Game winning lines: There are 50 winning lines in Wolf Riches. The wins are counted from left to right. During the base game, the rewards above reels 1,3, and 5 are active. The prizes above reels 2 and 4 are active during the Free Spins. Random Multiplier – The random multiplier can increase the cash prizes above the reels. Jackpot Wheel – The player gets to spin the Jackpot Wheel if the Full Moon symbol lands on the reel with the Jackpot prize above it. Stacked Wilds and Reel Tilt – Wilds come in stacks of 3 and a random tilt might occur on some reels to reveal additional Wilds. During the Free Spins, the prizes above reels 2 and 4 are unlocked. An additional 2 free spins can be won if the Full Moon lands on reels 1, 3 or 5 again and the prize above the reel is Free Spins. Jackpots – The player wins the chance to spin the Jackpot wheel. This guarantees that one of four types of jackpots will be won - Mini, Minor, Major or Grand. * The Full Moon symbol also acts as a Wild in both the Base game and Free Spins. Random Multiplier There is a random multiplier in both the base game and the free spins which can increase the cash prizes above the reels by x2, x3 or x5. Stacked Wilds and Reel Tilt The Wilds in the game come in stacks of three. The reels tilt at random and reveal additional Wilds, giving the player a chance of higher winnings. Playing the Game To play a round, configure your bet using the Bet Plus / Minus buttons, and press the Spin button or the Spacebar to submit the bet and initiate the round Once the round is started, the player can fast-forward it to the round result or skip the animations by pressing the Skip/Stop button that replaces the Spin button. Payout Rules There are different coin values that can be set by using the COIN VALUE + and - buttons. COIN VALUE represents the value in currency of each coin. There are different bet amounts that can be set by using the BET + and - buttons. BET represents the number of coins that the player bets per round. Winning combinations are paid per line in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. A line win in credits or currency is equal to the value shown in the PAYTABLE, multiplied by the line bet amount. Winning combinations and payouts are calculated according to the game's Paytable, which can be accessed through the Info button All symbol multipliers can be found in the Symbol screen in the Info pages. The prizes are calculated using the following formula: Bet amount, multiplied by the symbol multiplier, divided by the number of paylines (50). Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added up. All wins occur on the selected paylines, excluding bonus wins. Payout rules in Features Free Spins are played at the same bet amount as the round that activated the Free Spins. Free Spins wins are added to any initial wins. Jackpot wins use the bet amount that was used in the round that activated the Jackpot feature. The cash prizes above the reels are linked to the bet amount. Increasing or decreasing your bet will have a corresponding effect on the cash prizes. The prize awarded will be multiplied by the random multiplier (x2, x3, x5) if it appears. Autobet / Autoplay Enables playing the game automatically for a predetermined number of spins at the current number of lines and bet configuration. The autobet / autoplay menu is accessible through the Autobet / Autoplay button. Autobet / Autoplay Limits The player can set various autobet / autoplay limits: Loss Limit (mandatory in UK) – Allows you to control your combined losses by entering a numeric value that will stop the autobet function and trigger a popup message when the loss limit is reached. Calculated as follows: (Sum of bets) - (Sum of wins) >= (Loss Limit amount). Single Win – Will stop the autobet function and a popup message will be displayed when a single win payout is >= the 'Single Win' value entered. Win Limit – Allows you to control your combined wins by entering a numeric value that will stop the autobet function and trigger a popup message when the win limit is reached. Calculated as follows: (Sum of bets) - (Sum of wins) >= (Win Limit amount). On a Win – The autobet will stop and a popup message will appear on any win within a single round. A free spins feature is considered as one round for this limit setting and the autobet will only stop after all the free spins are finished. Stop on a Feature – The autobet will stop and a popup message appear when the game reaches any feature round, including but not limited to: free spins round, bonus games and mini games, with exception to random wilds features. When the limit's conditions are met, the autobet will stop and the relevant popup message will appear in the middle of the screen. Malfunction Voids All Pays and Plays.
