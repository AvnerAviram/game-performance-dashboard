Wild Dodo Wild Dodo is a 5 reel slot game with Wild Dodo Re-spins and Dodo’s Nest Free Games to increase your winnings. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols on any of the 20 lines. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 97.015%. WILD DODO RE-SPINS 3 Re-Spins Awarded when a Wild Dodo appears on reel 3. During Re-Spins reel 3 will expand to make the entire reel Wild. Re-Spins cannot be retriggered. Re-Spins are played at the payline and bet of the trigger game. DODO'S NEST FREE GAMES DODO'S NEST Free Games are awarded when 3 Nest scatter symbols appear on reels 2, 3 and 4. In the Dodo's Nest, select Eggs until you have matched 3 identical prizes. Win one of the following prizes in the Dodo's Nest: 5 Free Games with a 2x multiplier 7 Free Games with a 2x multiplier 10 Free Games with a 2x multiplier 15 Free Games with a 2x multiplier 20 Free Games with a 3x multiplier Wild Dodo Re-Spins can be won during DODO'S NEST Free Games. DODO'S NEST Free Games can be retriggered at the same number of Free Games and Multiplier currently being played at. DODO'S NEST Free Games are played at the paylines and bet of the trigger game.
