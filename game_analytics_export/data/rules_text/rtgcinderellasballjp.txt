How to play Cinderella's Ball Total Stake The Total Stake is the total of stakes per line. You can adjust it at any time, using the + or – buttons. Spin the Reels If you are playing on desktop, click the SPIN button or use your mouse or touchpad to swipe down on the reels to spin. Speed up each reel spin by pressing spacebar, or by clicking on the reels. For TURBO SPIN, hold down spacebar. If you are playing on a mobile device, tap the SPIN button or swipe the reels to spin. Speed up each reel spin by tapping the same button or the reels again. Autoplay Press the AUTO button to commence automatic play of the game at the currently selected stake.
