Cash of Kingdoms General Your bet is dependent on the number of paylines, the number of coins per payline and coin size played. All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination. All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. Only one regular winning combination is paid out per payline and you are paid out the value of the highest win. Only one scatter winning combination is paid out per spin and you are paid out the value of the highest win. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. If you have a winning combination, the win is highlighted on the reels. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again. Regular symbols Matching symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right and right-to-left, starting from the first or fifth slot reel. Regular payouts depend on your bet per payline. Wild symbol This symbol only appears on reels 2, 3 and 4. This symbol creates a wild symbol stack on reels 2 and 4. This symbol does not substitute for the scatter symbol. Scatter symbol This symbol only appears on reels 2, 3 and 4. This symbol creates its own winning combination when three symbols appear on the reels. This symbol does not need to appear on an enabled payline to win. This symbol cannot be substituted by the wild symbol. Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Invading Wilds™ feature A wild symbol stack on reels 2 or 4 turns an adjacent reel into a wild reel. During a regular game, a wild symbol stack on reels 2 and 4 turns reel 3 wild. During free spins, a wild symbol stack on reels 2 and 4 turns reel 3 wild and doubles your win for that spin. Free Spins bonus game The last bet you placed in the game is used as the free spins bet amount. During free spins each scatter symbol that appears on the reels awards 1 free spin. Scatter symbol rules apply. Winnings are automatically collected during the free spins. Paytable Achievements feature This feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Tracks your win status by highlighting symbol combinations you have won. When you win all combinations for the same symbol it is highlighted. Achievements are permanently highlighted on the payout table, even after you exit the game. Regular game wins are logged as achievements. Wild symbol substitute wins are not logged as achievements. Free spins wins are not logged as achievements. Game Controls Item Description Balance box Displays your Casino balances. Bet box Displays your total bet amount per spin. Return to Player (RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time. The RTP is fixed, and does not change. This game has an RTP of 96.28%. Last modification date: 10/14/2020
