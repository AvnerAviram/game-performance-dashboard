MONOPOLY Bring The House Down Game Introduction Monopoly Bring The House Down is a 5 reel game with 40 win lines available on all bets. Wilds spin in over the reels to deliver increased wins. 3 or more Scatter Symbols awards Free Spins Malfunction voids all pays and play Hot Zones In each game, hot zone wild patterns will spin in addition to the reels. If hot zone lands on a scatter symbol they become a combined wild scatter symbol. Chance Card Feature Choose 1 of 3 chance cards to reveal a hot zone. The hotzone is dropped into a random reel position and wins are awarded. Bonus Selector A random Hot Zone pattern drops on the reels The player has 3 seconds to accept, else another drops. Bonus Drop The Bonus drop is an extra chance if the Hot Zone appears outside the Reel window The Pattern will drop in before any wins are awarded. Big Bet A Big Bet game initially consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. (Additional Big Bet Spins can be won from the Community Chest Cards) Up to 4 Community Chest cards are awarded before the game. These award a number of different Bonuses to be used in the game. The Cards award a number of different Bonuses to used in the Big Bet Spins. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per 2 lines. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 40 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. All Big Bet line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as cash values.
