Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Golden Egg symbols, to form winning combinations. Wild only appear on reels 2 ,3 and 4. 3.2. Wild Barn Multipliers Modifier On any paid spin, single Wild can be assigned a random multiplier of x2, x3 or x5. Multipliers will be added together and applied to all wins from this spin. Maximum of 3 Wilds with multipliers can appear during the modifier. 3.3. Wild Abduction Modifier On any paid spin, reels 2, 3 & 4 can be covered by Wild reel. 3.4. Chicken or the Egg Modifier On any paid spin, Chicken Symbol can lay Golden Egg to trigger the Hold & Winfinity Bonus . 3.5. Hold & Winfinity Bonus Landing 5 or more Golden Eggs symbols trigger the Hold & Winfinity Bonus . The feature starts with Golden Eggs and row multipliers of x1. Each Golden Egg has the value of the row multiplier. When 3 Golden Eggs fill a horizontal row they are collected in the basket. Each time you collect Golden Eggs the row multiplier is increased. Up Booster immediately increases the row multiplier upon landing. Collect Booster collects all Golden Eggs on screen but do not increase multipliers. Hold & Winfinity Bonus ends when 5 Golden Eggs fill a vertical row. Horizontal rows are always counted first. 3.6. Golden Bet Golden Bet is a base game Side Bet which will increase the chance of all modifiers and Hold & Winfinity Bonus trigger. The Side Bet will add 50% to the cost of each spin. 3.7. Bonus Buy Buy the Bonus Buy for 80x stake for guaranteed entry into the Hold & Winfinity Bonus . The Bonus Buy will guarantee 5 or more Golden Egg symbols with no additional wins. 4.
