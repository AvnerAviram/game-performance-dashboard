Quickhit Ultra Pays Sun Dragon Game Introduction Quick Hit Ultra Pays™ Sun Dragon™ is a 5x6 Ultra Pays slot game which features a Free Games Bonus with an exciting Pick Feature! SUN DRAGON is wild for all symbols except for scatter symbols and appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. SCROLL and QUICK HIT are scatter symbols. The top 3 rows of the reel set are covered by curtains. During any spin, curtains may rise and increase the number of ways for that spin. Land QUICK HIT symbols to be awarded jackpot prizes. The available prizes are displayed in the paytable. Bets Selecting your bet: To adjust the BET, use the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons to increase or decrease the total bet.Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Free Games Bonus Land 3 scattered SCROLL symbols to trigger the Free Games Bonus. SCROLL symbols appear on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Choose and match fireworks to determine the number of ways to play the Free Games Bonus with. Up to 7,776 ways may be selected. Ways In the base game there are 243 ways by default and up to 7,776 ways possible to play with. The Free Games Bonus has between 576 and 7,776 ways depending on the curtain configuration. Jackpots The jackpots available in this game are awarded for landing 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9 or more QUICK HIT symbols. The value of each jackpot is dependent on the initial bet. Disclaimer Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Only active ways can register wins. Like symbols on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination, but may create separate winning combinations.
