Wild Wild Nugget GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play. All wins shown in credits. Game starts with 3 symbol positions in height on all 5 reels with 243 ways. All reels are played for a reel cost of 50 credits. Minimum bet 50 credits. Choose your TOTAL BET. TOTAL BET is the reel cost multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Select +,_ or MAX to choose your TOTAL BET. The PLAY button will immediately commence play. While the reels are spinning you may press the PLAY button to stop them. Touch CREDIT, TOTAL BET or WIN meter to show in currency or credits. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Scatter wins and WILD BONUS prizes are added to REEL POWER wins. All wins multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. WILD BONUS 2 WILD triggers WILD BONUS on any bought or free game. Reels 3, 4 and 5 may randomly grow to a height of 4 symbol positions with 576 ways or 5 symbol positions with 1125 ways. At the start of each spin, a WILD BONUS prize is randomly selected for each BONUS WILD BONUS prizes are shown multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Reel 3 prizes are 100, 150 or 250 credits multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Reel 4 prizes are 250, 500, 1000 or 5000 credits multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Reel 5 prizes are 1000, 2500, 5000 or 10000 credits multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Each WILD BONUS prize is awarded once only for each from left to right on adjacent reels starting with reel 3. BONUS appears on reels 3, 4 and 5 only. WILD BONUS cannot be triggered again during the WILD BONUS. TOTAL BET is the same as the game that triggered the feature. FREE GAMES 3 or more FREE GAMES triggers 15 FREE GAMES. Press the PLAY button or touch the start feature banner to start the free games. The free games will start automatically after 2 minutes. During the FREE GAMES each WILD that appears anywhere on reels 1 or 2 will multiply the total win for that spin by either 2 or 3. Bonus reels are in play during the feature. *2 and *3 substitute for WILD 15 additional FREE GAMES are won with 3 or more FREE GAMES TOTAL BET is the same as the game that triggered the feature.
