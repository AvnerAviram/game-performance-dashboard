Jewel Twist Introduction Jewel Twist is a 5-reel 10-line slot machine game with a diamonds and gems theme. Get a win for matching 3 or more symbols on a winline, with all wins paying adjacent. Each symbol is stacked on the reel giving the player more chances to win big! How To Play Instructions Click the Total Bet panel to increase or decrease the amount of your Total Bet. Select your Bet from 10p up to £500 (or currency equivalent). Click 'SPIN' to start your game. There is an Autoplay function in the game that will start each spin automatically. If you wish to cancel this during your auto spins then simply press the Stop Autoplay button which will appear at the top of where the Autoplay button was previously along with the remaining number of spins. Reel Wins Line wins are multiplied by the value bet on the winning line. All Wins across all different lines are added together to form the total win. All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive adjacent reels. Only the highest line win is paid on each line. Twist Mystery Symbol During any spin if any Twist symbols land on the reels they will animate and reveal a symbol at random, which will be displayed from every Twist symbol in view. Payout Information and Game Rules Line wins are multiplied by the value bet on the winning line. Wins on different paylines are added. All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive adjacent reels. Only the highest line win is paid on each line. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PLAYS AND PAYS. This game has a theoretical return to player of 95.488%. This is based on long term payback. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
