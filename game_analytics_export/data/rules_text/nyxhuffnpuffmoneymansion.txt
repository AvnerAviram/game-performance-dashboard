FRAMES During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, TOUCH-UP FEATURE, HOME IMPROVEMENT FEATURE, and MANSIONS FEATURE, for each HARD HAT symbol that lands on a position without BRICKFRAME, a new frame is added to the position, or the existing frame is upgraded, based on the table below. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, TOUCH-UP FEATURE, HOME IMPROVEMENT FEATURE, and MANSIONS FEATURE, for each HARD HAT symbol that lands on a position with BRICKFRAME, an existing frame on a different position is upgraded, a new frame is added to a different position or 7.5x total bet is awarded. A frame appearing on a position is held for the remainder of the feature. Each position may have only 1 frame at a given time. HOUSE PRIZES At the end of the FREE SPINS FEATURE, TOUCH-UP FEATURE, HOME IMPROVEMENT FEATURE, and MANSIONS FEATURE, each position with STRAWFRAME, STICKFRAME, or BRICKFRAME is replaced with STRAWHOUSE, STICKHOUSE, or BRICKHOUSE, respectively, and each house reveals and awards a prize, based on the table below. If BONUS symbol is awarded, then the MONEY MANSION FEATURE will be triggered after all houses on the grid have revealed and awarded their prizes. A maximum of one BRICKHOUSE may award the BONUS symbol at the end of each feature. MONEY MANSION FEATURE During the base game, 3 or more scattered BONUS triggers the MONEY MANSION FEATURE. The MONEY MANSION FEATURE may award the GRAND, GOLD ZONE multiplier prize meters ranging from 750x - 4000x total bet, MAJOR, a BLUE ZONE multiplier prize meters ranging from 250x - 500x total bet, TOUCH-UP FEATURE, HOME IMPROVEMENT FEATURE, MANSIONS FEATURE, MINOR, MINI or one of the bottom 8 multiplier prize meters ranging from 5x - 75x total bet. If the MONEY MANSION FEATURE awards TOUCH-UP FEATURE, HOME IMPROVEMENT FEATURE, or MANSIONS FEATURE, then that feature is triggered with 6 free spins. If both MONEY MANSION FEATURE and FREE SPINS FEATURE are triggered during the base game spin, then the MONEY MANSION FEATURE will be played first. The distribution of JACKPOT prizes in the MONEY MANSION FEATURE changes with bet.
