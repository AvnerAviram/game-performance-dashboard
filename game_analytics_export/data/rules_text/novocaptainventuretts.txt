Captain Venture: Treasures of the Sea Game Theme and Graphics The Captain is back on the seas! Join Captain Venture for a follow up to the ori ginal adventure! Main Game Captain Venture™: Treasures of the Sea is played on 10 lines across 5 reels. Wilds Wilds substitute all symbols except Scatter Bonus Symbol. It does not replace Anchor Symbol during Anchor Drop Re-Spins, or Chest Symbol during Treasure Chest Bounty. Sea Change After a spin, the reels may sink into the sea and rise again with different symbols, any wins are paid. Anchor Drop Re-Spins Anchor Symbol wins may be followed by a re-spin of some reels. Treasure Chest Bounty Chest Symbol wins may add extra Gem Symbols to the reels. X Marks The Slot Re-Spins During a spin, the Treasure Map may appear. Captain Venture Symbols trigger the feature and re-spins are awarded. Captain Venture Symbols will roam around the reels and highlight positions. Highlighted positions will add to the overall multiplier. Yellow positions will reveal a multiplier of x1. Orange positions will reveal a multiplier of x2. Red positions will reveal a multiplier of x3. Mystery Multiplier Free Spins 3x Scatter Bonus Symbols awards Free Spins. After each Free Spin, the wheel will apply a win multiplier and all wins are added. Captain Venture Symbols may enhance the win multiplier. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Symbols pay from left to right and must be consecutive. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. All prizes are on selected lines, except scatters. Scatter symbols in any position trigger the feature game. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are added. The paytable always shows the prizes for the currently selected bet and number of lines. Free spins can be won again during the Free Spins. Free spins are played at trigger bet and lines.
