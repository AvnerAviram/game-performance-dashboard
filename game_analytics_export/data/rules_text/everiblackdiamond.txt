Black Diamond WIN PROGRESSIVE JACKPOTS BY PLAYING MAX BET Progressive prizes are available at max bet only. Jackpot triggering symbols must land on active paylines to award jackpots. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 9 awards the major jackpot. 3 Black Diamond symbols on paylines 1 to 8 awards the minor jackpot. At less than max bet, jackpots pay flat credit prizes which are multiplied by bet per line. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 9 awards 5,000 credits. 3 Black Diamond symbols on payline 1 to 8 awards 300 credits. Progressive tiers are funded by a percentage of all wagers. BLACK DIAMOND SYMBOL IS WILD! The Black Diamond symbol is wild and substitutes for any symbol except multipliers to make winning combinations. BONUS MULTIPLIERS payline multiply together and multiply the winning combination. The maximum total multiplier for a winning combination is 30X. 2X multipliers are available on 777, 77, 7, Double Bar, and Single Bar symbols. 3X multipliers are available on 7 and Single Bar symbols. 5X multipliers are available on 777, 77, and Double Bar symbols.
