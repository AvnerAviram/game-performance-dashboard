BELL SYMBOLS SILVER BELL SYMBOLS and GOLD BELL SYMBOLS are wild and substitute for all symbols, except the SUPER4BELLS Deluxe SYMBOL. When a SILVER BELL SYMBOL or GOLD BELL SYMBOL lands on a reel but it is not part of a winning combination, that reel stays locked into place for the next spin (one spin only) to provide more chances at hitting winning combinations. Three (3) SILVER BELL SYMBOLS, GOLD BELL SYMBOLS or a mixed combination of GOLD BELL SYMBOLS and SILVER BELL SYMBOLS on a win line awards a prize equal to the BAR prize. SILVER BELL SYMBOLS and GOLD BELL SYMBOLS appear on reels 1 through 7. GOLD BELL MULTIPLIER FEATURE When a GOLD BELL SYMBOL is part of a winning combination, it will activate a MULTIPLIER FEATURE. A multiplier value will be randomly selected and that multiplier will be applied to all of the winning combinations that contain the GOLD BELL SYMBOL. If more than one GOLD BELL SYMBOL is part of a winning combination, only one multiplier will be applied to a winning combination. If a jackpot winner was playing in any other currency (not euros), the jackpot win amount will be converted from euros into the currency the winner was playing with at the time of the win, using the applicable exchange rate on the date of the jackpot win.
