Ace of Spades Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules This is a 3-reel slot machine with one payline. Only the highest win is paid. Aces are wild and substitute for any other reel symbol to help comprise winning combinations. One ace doubles the normal prize for a winning combination. Two aces quadruple the normal prize for a winning combination, except when the combination is 3 aces. The RTP is 95.94%. The RTP is 95.87% when not playing with max bet Click SPIN to start the game. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the prize table. Actions Increase Denomination- Click + to increase the denomination of each coin. Decrease Denomination- Click - to decrease the denomination of each coin. Bet One- Bets one coin. Bet Max- Bets the maximum. Spin- Starts the round by spinning the reels. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
