Age of the Gods Ruler of the Sky 5-Reel 40-Line Slot The objective of Age of the Gods™: Ruler of the Sky is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. TO PLAY THE GAME Click CONTINUE on the entry screen to enter the main game. This game is played with a non-fixed number of 40 active paylines. Only active paylines can register line wins. Line bet per game round = Total bet divided by the number of active paylines. Choose your lines by clicking '–' or '+'. Choose your total bet by clicking '−' or '+'. Click the TURBO MODE button to switch Turbo Mode on or off. When turned on reels spin faster. Clicking the SPIN button spins the reels with the currently selected total bet. During the reel spin, the SPIN button changes into the STOP button. Clicking STOP ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin result. Mouse over the AUTOPLAY button and then click on an Autoplay option to start Autoplay mode. During Autoplay mode the AUTOPLAY button changes into a smaller STOP button. The number of auto spins left is displayed above the smaller STOP button. Click this smaller STOP button to stop Autoplay mode. To enter a triggered feature, click the CONTINUE button or anywhere on the screen. After the end of the feature, click CONTINUE or anywhere on the winnings summary screen to return to the main game or resume the Autoplay mode (if there are any auto spins left to play). The paytable can be accessed via the INFO button or the Menu. ABOUT PAYOUTS Wins are calculated according to the paytable, which can be accessed via the INFO button or the Menu. Line bet = total bet divided by the number of active paylines. Scatter payouts are multiplied by the total bet. If more than 1 active payline has a winning combination, the winnings are added up. If 2 winning combinations occur on the same line, you will be paid the value of the higher winning combination. Winning combinations must start from the leftmost reel, and the symbols have to be consecutive along a payline. The SCATTER symbol is an exception to these rules. More information about the SCATTER symbol can be found below. The accumulating winnings and all payline wins are shown for each winning spin. In the case of large wins, a win pop-up opens displaying the total spin revenue. WILD SYMBOL The WILD symbol can stand for any other symbol (except SCATTER ) to make the best possible winning combination. WILD symbols are always stacked, meaning they appear one on top of the other. More WILD symbols are added to the reels during the Mighty Pegasus Free Games feature. There is also a separate payout for 3 or more WILD symbols on an active payline. This is paid out instead of the regular symbol win if the win amount from WILD symbols is larger than the win from the regular symbols (by WILD standing in). SCATTER SYMBOL The SCATTER symbol appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. If 3 SCATTER symbols appear in any position on these reels during the main game, the payout earned is multiplied by the total bet and added to any payline winnings. In addition, 5 Mighty Pegasus Free Games are triggered. MIGHTY PEGASUS FREE GAMES If SCATTER symbols appear on reels 2, 3 and 4 simultaneously during the main game, 5 Mighty Pegasus Free Games with additional stacked WILD symbols are triggered. If SCATTER symbols appear on reels 2, 3 and 4 simultaneously during the Free Games feature, 5 more free games are added to any remaining ones. During the Free Games feature the reels are spun automatically using the same bet per line as on the spin that triggered the feature. The total win for the Free Games round is displayed in the WIN field below the reels. The number of Free Games remaining is displayed above the reels. At the end of the round a result board appears that summarizes the winnings. Click CONTINUE or anywhere on the screen to return to the main game reels.
