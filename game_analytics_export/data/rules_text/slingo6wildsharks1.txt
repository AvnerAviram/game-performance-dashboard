INTRODUCTION 6 WILD Sharks is a 6-reel 4,096 Ways slot created by 4ThePlayer.com. Ways removes paylines making it easier to see where the win is. A win is formed from consecutive symbols starting from the first reel. 6 WILD SHARKS features WILD CHOICE™ allowing the player to choose where to place locked wilds. The game also features 4ThePlayer’s unique BIG REEL Portrait Mode™. HOW TO PLAY The player chooses their bet amount using the + and – symbols above or below the spin button. All wins are multiplied by the bet. For details on win payouts see the game paytable. To play the game, the player presses the spin button - the largest button with 4 arrows. If playing on PC and if enabled from the game options menu, the space bar can be used to spin. When the spin stops winning symbols are highlighted. Wins pay from left to right except in the BIG REEL Portrait Mode™ where a win pays from top to bottom. BIG REEL Portrait Mode™ When playing on mobile in portrait, the player has the option of playing classic small reels or BIG REEL Portrait Mode™. This option enlarges the reels, stacks them on top of each other and spins them right to left and pays top to bottom. This maximizes the visual experience on the mobile without changing the game experience. This mode is only available in portrait mobile play, payouts and game features are unchanged. WILD ZONES There are two WILD ZONES, as marked on reels 1,2 and 5,6 as WILD ZONE. Shark Wilds and Locked Wilds can only exist in the WILD ZONES. CLASSIC and ADVANCED MODES The default mode on entry to the game is CLASSIC MODE. The player can tap the ADVANCED MODE button to enter the ADVANCED MODE. Inside ADVANCED MODE they can tap the CLASSIC MODE button to return to CLASSIC MODE. ADVANCED MODE - WILD CHOICE™ In advanced mode the player can add Locked Wilds into the WILD ZONES on the base game using the WILD CHOICE™ feature! Locked Wilds remain in place for all spins. Tap symbols in the WILD ZONES to create Locked Wilds. Each Locked Wild has a set price that is added to the Total Bet cost to spin. As Locked WILDs are added or removed costs for other Locked WILDs may change. Locked Wilds are priced differently depending on the position of the Locked Wild and number of Locked Wilds on other reels. A minimum of 1 Wild and up to 6 Wilds can be placed. Tap a Locked Wild to remove it. Place Locked Wilds on reels 1 and 2, for frequent wins, place Locked Wilds on reels 5 and 6 for bigger wins. If Free Spins are won, all Locked Wilds carry over into the Free Spins feature. The RTP remains the same for CLASSIC and ADVANCED MODES and for all possible wild configurations. SYMBOLS Buoy Scatter 2 Buoy Scatters, on reels 3 and 4 will trigger the Free Spins bonus. Shark Wild The Shark Wild symbol substitutes every symbol except for Buoy Scatter. Appears only in WILD ZONES (reels 1,2,5,6) Locked Wild A Locked Wild is a Shark Wild that is Locked in place for future spins. Surfer The Surfer is the top paying symbol. FREE SPINS BONUS Free Spins bonus is triggered by two Buoy Scatters on reels 3 and 4. The bet for the free spins will be set at the value that the free spins were triggered. During any FREE SPINS bonus Shark Wilds that land on the reels will become Locked Wilds for the remainder of Free Spins. Also two Buoy Scatters on reels 3 and 4 will retrigger to award 3 extra Free Spins. Retriggers can occur multiple times during FREE SPINS. CLASSIC MODE FREE SPINS BONUS - WILD CHOICE™ Free Spins can be traded for Locked Wilds. You will receive between 15-1 initial Free Spins based on the Locked Wilds setup you create. Your Locked Wilds will remain in place for all Free Spins. You can also play Free Spins with no Locked Wilds selected. Tap ADD WILD in the WILD ZONES to add Locked Wilds using the WILD CHOICE™ feature. The number of initial FREE SPINS you will receive depends on the position and number of Locked Wilds Up to 6 wilds can be placed. Tap a Locked Wild to remove it. When Locked Wilds are placed or removed the number of Free Spins shown on the START FREE SPINS button automatically updates. Place Locked Wilds on reels 1 and 2, for frequent wins, place Locked Wilds on reels 5 and 6 for bigger wins. To start Free Spins tap the START FREE SPINS button which shows the number of initial FREE SPINS that you will receive. ADVANCED MODE FREE SPINS BONUS 10 Free Spins are awarded for all base game Locked Wild configurations. In ADVANCED MODE all Locked Wilds that are present on the triggering spin will remain locked for all Free Spins. HOW TO CALCULATE A WIN WITH 4096 WAYS If any of the reels contain more than 1 instance of the symbols, the win will be multiplied. Multiply the number of symbols on each of the reels to get the final payout multiplier. For example, if the first reel contains 2 symbols, second reel 3 symbols and third reel 2 symbols, the paytable value is multiplied by 2*3*2=12. AUTOPLAY Autoplay allows the player to play without pressing spin. To open autoplay, press the autoplay button – the small button with 4 arrows and a play icon in the middle. The player has the option of choosing the number of spins, loss limit, and single win limit. The spins will automatically stop when the chosen number has completed or the loss limit or single win limit is hit. When the autoplay is active, the spin button becomes a stop button which displays the number of spins remaining. To stop autoplay spins, press the stop button.
