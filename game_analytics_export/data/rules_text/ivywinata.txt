Winata Game Objective: Winata is a five-reel slot game with 35 paylines, a Piñata feature game and a Pure 5-of-a-Kind Bonus. The feature game is triggered when three or more Piñata symbols appear anywhere on the reels. The Chilli symbol is the wild substitute. Paytable Paylines Game Information: Game is played with 35 fixed number of lines. SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the current selected BET. Payouts are displayed on the Paytable. Payline wins are multiplied by the amount ‘bet per line’ and gets added to other payline wins. Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline. Coinciding wins on different bet lines are all added to your total. Scatter (represented by Piñata symbol) wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins. All symbols pay from left to right, except the scatter symbol which pays anywhere in view. On the winning line, the winning symbols will slightly animate with bounce in animation and the win line will be colored and remaining will be grayed out. The Ticker messages will appear on bottom of game screen. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will update, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at the bottom of the game. The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and play-money modes and Autospin option will not be available in play-money mode. How to play: Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet per payline is set for all 35 lines on the slot machine. You can increase the bet per line by clicking the button to the right of 'Bet per line', or you can decrease the bet by clicking the button to the left. Bet per line will not get adjusted to the next possible bet, if your balance drops below total bet placed, then click on button shown at the bottom of the game screen. LINES: Thirty-five lines are selected by default. Lines are fixed and lines count cannot be changed. MAX BET: To play with MAX BET, click on . If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled. The number of lines are multiplied by the 'bet per line'. The total bet amount is displayed beside the 'BET' label, which can be found at the bottom of the game screen. Paytable: The Paytable can be viewed either by clicking the button located in bottom of the game screen beside ‘BET’ label or by clicking button located at the bottom of the screen beside the Lines text. The prizes displayed in the Paytable are per unit bet, and are shown as multipliers. To get the effective payout per line, multiply the respective prize with the bet per line. You can go back to the game by clicking on the button on the Paytable screen. Spin: Click on SPIN icon to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations on the selected lines and any scatter wins will be paid according to the Paytable. Result: Wins are indicated by colorized paylines animated symbols, and in case of a scatter win, by animated symbols, with the total win amount being shown at the bottom of the screen beside 'WIN'. Volume: icon will play the game music. Click on icon will mute the volume . CREDITS: Your remaining game balance, after you have placed the desired bet, is shown in ‘BALANCE’ meter i.e. the current game balance minus the total bet placed.'AUTOSPIN': You can use this feature to choose all or any of the various options for 'hands free' game play. The available options under this feature are: Number of spins: The game will spin for the number of rotations selected from the drop-down menu. You can choose any number of games and play until your balance is less than the total bet placed. Spin till Win equals or exceeds: The game will spin until the win amount is equal to or exceeds the amount selected from the drop down menu. Spin till Loss not exceeds: The game will automatically spin until the loss limit is reached. Loss will be calculated with (Loss = Total bets - Total wins) Total bets=accumulated total bets Total wins=accumulated total wins (((Total bets- Total wins) + current game bet) <= Loss limit target) then next auto spin will be allowed. Spin till feature trigger: The game will spin until the next feature is triggered. Autospin Panel To make use of the Autospin feature, click on the 'AUTOSPIN' button in the bottom right of the game screen. When the Autospin window opens, check the boxes next to the option(s) you'd like to play with. Select from the drop-down menu where applicable and click ‘Ok’ button. Click on 'START' to activate the selected options and start the spins. Similarly, click on ‘X’ button to skip the AUTOSPIN' feature and return to the game screen. When playing with multiple options and conditions selected, the condition that is satisfied first will take precedence over the others, and the remaining selected conditions will be disabled. You can then choose to either select the same set of options again or opt for new option(s) and proceed with the game. Click on the STOP SPIN to deactivate AUTOSPIN and the normal game will be restored. 'AUTOSPIN' option is not available in the play-money mode. Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. ­To change your bet, increase or decrease the Bet shown on the game screen. Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen. The ’ Game logs ’ functionality is applicable only for the real-money game play. GAME RULES: Winata is a five-reel slot game with 35 paylines. Line wins multiplied by bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet. All payouts are made according to the Paytable. All symbols pay left to right, starting with the left-most reel, except for the scattered Chilli symbol, which pays anywhere in view. Only the highest win on each selected payline is paid. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Coinciding or simultaneous wins are added and paid according to the Paytable. FEATURES: PIÑATA FEATURE: The feature game is triggered when three or more Piñata symbols appear anywhere on the reels. When the PIÑATA FEATURE is triggered, the message "Choose a Piñata to win!" appears. One of the following prizes is hidden behind each piñata: 5 free games with all wins multiplied x2. 8 free games with all wins multiplied x3. 10 free games with all wins multiplied x4. 15 free games with all wins multiplied x6. 25 free games with all wins multiplied x10 During free games, the bet per line is the same as the game that triggered the SCATTER FEATURE. During free games, if we get 3 or more scatters, feature game is retriggered and awards the original number of free games and multipliers. Free spin wins are added to lines and scatter wins. PURE 5 OF A KIND BONUS: Any 5-of-a-Kind of Girl, Bandito, Band, Chihuahua or Cactus without Chilli substituting payline win is multiplied by x3, x4, x6, x11 or x21 randomly.
