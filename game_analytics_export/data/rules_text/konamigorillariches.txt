Gorilla Riches BASIC GAME RULES MINIMUM BET IS 60 CREDITS. ALL WINS ARE SHOWN IN CURRENCY EXCEPT FOR CREDIT PRIZES. ANY 3 OR MORE [COIN SYMBOL] PAY THE TOTAL CREDIT PRIZES OF EACH CREDIT PRIZE ON [COIN SYMBOL]. THE CREDIT PRIZE SHOWN ON THE [COIN SYMBOL] ARE EITHER 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 90, 150, 200, 300, 400, 500, 750, 1000, 2000, 3000 OR 5000 MULTIPLIED BY THE BET PER LINE. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. A PLAYER CANNOT INFLUENCE THE RESULT OF A GAME BY STOPPING THE REEL SPINS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING CORRECT CREDITS ARE REGISTERED BEFORE PLAY. THE GAME DISPLAY DOES NOT INDICATE HOW CLOSE YOU WERE TO WINNING, AND CANNOT NECESSARILY BE USED TO DETERMINE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING OR LOSING IF YOU CONTIUE TO PLAY. FREE GAME FEATURE ANY 3, 4 OR 5 [MASK SCATTER SYMBOL] TRIGGER 8, 12 OR 20 FREE GAMES RESPECTIVELY. ANY 2, 3, 4 OR 5 [MASK SCATTER SYMBOL] AWARD AN ADDITIONAL 3, 8, 12 OR 20 FREE GAMES RESPECTIVELY DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. 20, 30, 40 OR 50 [COIN SYMBOL] ARE ADDED TO THE REEL BEFORE FREE GAME START. ANY 3 OR MORE [COIN SYMBOL] TRIGGER THE PRIZE FEATURE. DIFFERENT REELS ARE USED DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. CREDITS BET AND LINES PLAYED ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FEATURE. PRIZE FEATURE WHEN THE PRIZE FEATURE IS TRIGGERED, THE CREDIT PRIZES SHOWN ON EACH [COIN SYMBOL] ARE ADDED TO THE TOTAL [COIN SYMBOL] WON METER. EACH CREDIT PRIZE SHOWN ON [COIN SYMBOL] IS CHANGED AT THE SAME TIME, AND THE TOTAL OF EACH CREDIT PRIZE CHANGED IS ADDED TO THE TOTAL [COIN SYMBOL] WON METER AGAIN. THIS CHANGE MAY OCCUR UP TO 20 TIMES. ALL WINS ARE PAID AT THE END OF THE PRIZE FEATURE. THE PRIZE FEATURE CANNOT BE RE-TRIGGERED DURING THE PRIZE FEATURE. CREDITS BET ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE PRIZE FEATURE. Last modification date: 6/19/2024
