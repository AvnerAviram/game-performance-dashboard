Rumble Roo K.O. Rumble Roo K.O.! is a Bet 40, 40 line slot game. It has 5 reels, 4 rows, and the following features: Respins Mystery Spin The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of spins by numerous players over an extended period of time and cannot be affected by the player's strategy. Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed 0. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration. Game Rules Wild Wild substitutes for all symbols except {Scatter Bell}. Appears on all reels during Base Game. Appears in Base Games only. Scatter Bell 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, or 15 Scatter Bell trigger Respins. In Respins, each Scatter Bell increments all {Respin Roo} once. Mystery Symbol May appear on any spin to reveal {Wild}, {KOALA}, {COCKATOO}, {PLATYPUS}, {GYM BAG}, {BOXING GLOVES}, {Scatter Bell}. Appears in Base Game only. Respins Roo Initial prizes of 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50, 60, 80, 100, 120, 200, 400 credits are available at min bet. Prizes can then be incremented upon landing {Scatter Bell} or {Respins K.O.!}. Appears in Respins only. Respins K.O.! Respins K.O.! will increment all {Respin Roo} 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, or 10 times. Appears in Respins only. Respins 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, or 15 {Scatter Bell} trigger Respins. 3 Respins are initially awarded. Upon entering Respins, each triggering {Scatter Bell} transforms into a {Respin Roo} with an initial prize. Initial prizes of 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50, 60, 80, 100, 120, 200, 400 credits are available at min bet. Any {Scatter Bell} will increment the amount on each {Respin Roo} by the initial prize displayed. {Respins K.O.!} will increment the amount on each {Respin Roo} by the initial prize displayed 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, or 10 times. After incrementing other prizes, {Scatter Bell} and {Respins K.O.!} will transform into {Respin Roo} and be allocated an initial prize. {Scatter Bell} and {Respins K.O.!} will not increment a {Respin Roo} which first appears on the same spin. Any {Scatter Bell} or {Respins K.O.!} will reset Respins to 3. Respins end when no respins remain or all reel positions have been filled. Respins are played at the triggering bet. Mystery Spins On any Base Game Spin, Roo and Boxing Bag may appear during reel spin. If Roo breaks Boxing Bag, 6 - 10 {Mystery Symbols} will appear randomly on reels to award the Mystery Spin. Once reel spin is complete, all {Mystery Symbol} will disappear, revealing identical symbols. {Mystery Symbol} may reveal {Wild}, {KOALA}, {COCKATOO}, {PLATYPUS}, {GYM BAG}, {BOXING GLOVES}, {Scatter Bell}. Rules (For Line Games) Play bet 40 for 40 lines. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. All wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. All wins on selected paylines only except SCATTER. Wins on different paylines are added. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win only on each selected payline. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RTP: 94.08% How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. The game displays all your details in the following display fields. Balance: Displays your total balance. Win: Displays the amount won in the spin. Total Bet: Displays the total wager staked. Total Win: Displays the accumulated wins during Respins. Respin: Displays the number of respins played, together with the total number of respins awarded. MAIN GAME BUTTONS: ADJUST BET Total Bet menu to adjust bet. SPIN Spins the reels. The SPACE BAR can also be used to spin when playing on Desktop. AUTOPLAY View and set AUTOPLAY options. STOP AUTOPLAY Displays the remaining amount of AUTOPLAY spins, click this button to STOP AUTOPLAY and return to normal play mode. STOP/SKIP During Base Game button will STOP the reel spin. During Free Games button will STOP the reel spin or SKIP to the next spin. MENU View the MENU, giving access to PAYTABLE, BET, AUTOPLAY, SETTINGS, HOME and CASHOUT tabs. MUTE Mutes sound and ambient volumes. UNMUTE Unmutes sound and ambient volumes. MENU TAB BUTTONS: PAY TABLE View game rules and pay awards for winning symbol combinations. AUTOPLAY View available AUTOPLAY AUTOPLAY options allows automatic consecutive normal game plays. TOTAL BET View available bet options. TOTAL BET equals the Bet Multiplier x the coin value.
