Old Mac Dollars GAME RULES All 5 reels are always in play. All symbols are scatter symbols. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Scatter Symbol 1, Scatter Symbol 2, Scatter Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 1, , Jackpot Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 4, or Jackpot Symbol 5 appearing in any position on a played reel with a currency value awards a scatter pay. The values that Scatter Symbol 1, Scatter Symbol 2, Scatter Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 1, Jackpot Symbol 2, Jackpot Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 4, and Jackpot Symbol 5 appear with depend on the Spin-crease feature. Jackpot Symbol 1 appears only on reel 1. Jackpot Symbol 2 appears only on reel 2. Jackpot Symbol 3 appears only on reel 3. Jackpot Symbol 4 appears only on reel 4. Jackpot Symbol 5 appears only on reel 5. Jackpot Symbol 1, Jackpot Symbol 2, Jackpot Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 4, and Jackpot Symbol 5 do not appear during the Free Games Bonus. Win the sum of the displayed currency values on all played reels. During the base game, each Wheel Symbol appearing in any position on the reels awards a wheel spin. Each wheel spin awards one of the values that Scatter Symbol 1 can appear with or the highest value that Scatter Symbol 2 can appear with. Wheel Symbol does not appear during the Free Games Bonus. At any time during the base game the player is allowed to make a Power Bet at a cost of 15X, 30X, or 45X the currently selected total bet to activate the Power Bet feature for 15, 30, or 45 bets, respectively. The player can make this bet by clicking the Power Bet button and then clicking the option of their choice on the confirmation screen. After making a Power Bet, the subsequent affected bets have an increased chance to win. At the start of the Power Bet the size of the reels increase by 1 row. If the Power Bet feature was activated for 15 bets, then the reels will increase by an additional row after each of the first two sets of 5 bets during the Power Bet feature. If the Power Bet feature was activated for 30 bets, then the reels will increase by an additional row after each of the first two sets of 10 bets during the Power Bet feature. If the Power Bet feature was activated for 45 bets, then the reels will increase by an additional row after each of the first two sets of 15 bets during the Power Bet feature. When the Power Bet feature completes the reels return to their original size. Each set of bets on different numbers of rows are played on different reels. Placing a bet at a different bet multiplier will forfeit the Power Bet effect on any remaining bets for that Power Bet feature. The total cost to make a Power Bet as well as play the affected bets is equal to 30X, 60X, or 90X the currently selected total bet for selecting 15, 30, or 45 affected bets, respectively. A Power Bet cannot be made while a Power Bet is already in effect. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as Spin-crease points are accumulated. Each bet placed awards 20 Spin-crease points times the bet multiplier. The total amount of Spin-crease points accumulated is counted across all sessions played on the current user account. Initially, Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.15, 0.3, or 0.75 times the total bet. Initially, Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 1.5, 3, or 7.5 times the total bet. Initially, Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 15, 75, or 150 times the total bet. Initially, Jackpot Symbol 1 appears with 20 times the total bet. Initially, Jackpot Symbol 2 appears with 40 times the total bet. Initially, Jackpot Symbol 3 appears with 200 times the total bet. Initially, Jackpot Symbol 4 appears with 400 times the total bet. Initially, Jackpot Symbol 5 appears with 1000 times the total bet. The Spin-crease meter fills as Spin-crease points are accumulated. When the Spin-crease meter fills completely, the values that Scatter Symbol 1, Scatter Symbol 2, Scatter Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 1, Jackpot Symbol 2, Jackpot Symbol 3, Jackpot Symbol 4, or Jackpot Symbol 5 appear with increase. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.2, 0.3, or 0.75 times the total bet after 1000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.2, 0.4, or 0.75 times the total bet after 2000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.2, 0.4, or 1 times the total bet after 3000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2, 3, or 7.5 times the total bet after 5000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2, 4, or 7.5 times the total bet after 10000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2, 4, or 10 times the total bet after 17500 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 20, 75, or 150 times the total bet after 30000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 20, 100, or 150 times the total bet after 50000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 20, 100, or 200 times the total bet after 100000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.25, 0.4, or 1 times the total bet after 150000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.25, 0.5, or 1 times the total bet after 275000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 1 appears with 0.25, 0.5, or 1.25 times the total bet after 500000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2.5, 4, or 10 times the total bet after 900000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2.5, 5, or 10 times the total bet after 1500000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 2 appears with 2.5, 5, or 12.5 times the total bet after 2750000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 25, 100, or 200 times the total bet after 5000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 25, 125, or 200 times the total bet after 8500000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Scatter Symbol 3 appears with 25, 125, or 250 times the total bet after 15000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. Jackpot Symbol 1 appears with 25 times the total bet after 25000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated. After 250000000 total Spin-crease points have been accumulated the meter will remain visible and display 100% complete. The rate of the Spin-crease progress is automatically adjusted based on the exchange rate of the currency in play. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 1 or more Free Games Symbol appearing in any position on the reels starts the Free Games Bonus. During the base game, Free Games Symbol may appear with 3 or 5 free games displayed. Win the sum of the displayed free games on all played reels. Scatter Symbol 1, Scatter Symbol 2, or Scatter Symbol 3 appearing in any position on a played reel with a currency value awards a scatter pay. The values that Scatter Symbol 1, Scatter Symbol 2, or Scatter Symbol 3 appear with depend on the Spin-crease feature. Win the sum of the displayed currency values on all played reels. Free Games Bonus use different reels. Free Spins automatically play at the same bet multiplier with the same number of rows as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. 1 or more Free Games Symbol appearing in any position on the reels awards additional free games. Free Games Symbol may appear with 1, 3, or 5 free games displayed. Win the sum of the displayed free games on all reels. The Free Games Bonus end when 0 free games remain.
