Fortune Stacks BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 30 credits. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. MAN appears on reels 2, 3, 4, & 5 only. MAN substitutes for CAT, DRAGON, PIG, TREE, BAG, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLS TM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: CAT, DRAGON, PIG, TREE, BAG, A, K, Q, J, 10, and 9, before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 INGOT trigger 5, 15 or 30 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 INGOT award additional 3, 5, 15 or 30 free games respectively during the free games. At the start of every spin, Action Stacked Symbols on 1 or 2 reels will be upgraded with multipliers 2x, 3x or 5x. The randomly selected multiplier will be the same for all Action Stacked Symbols on that reel. Each CAT_5x, DRAGON_5x, PIG_5x, TREE_5x, BAG_5x, A_5x, K_5x, Q_5x, J_5x, 10_5x or 9_5x in a line win will multiply the pay for that line win x5. Each CAT_3x, DRAGON_3x, PIG_3x, TREE_3x, BAG_3x, A_3x, K_3x, Q_3x, J_3x, 10_3x or 9_3x in a line win will multiply the pay for that line win x3. Each CAT_2x, DRAGON_2x, PIG_2x, TREE_2x, BAG_2x, A_2x, K_2x, Q_2x, J_2x, 10_2x or 9_2x in a line win will multiply the pay for that line win x2. Different reels are used during the feature. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
