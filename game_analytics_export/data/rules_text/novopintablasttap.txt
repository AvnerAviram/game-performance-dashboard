Pinata Blast - Tapper Game Theme and Graphics Welcome to the Piñata Blast! Each Piñata is filled with tantalizing prizes, from colorful candy cash to juicy multipliers, but you have to smash them open to find out. Grab a stick and start swinging for your chance to win big with Piñata Blast- Tapper! Main Game Piñata Blast- Tapper is a single click game with a Piñata that is surrounded by 17 candy symbols, each with a unique prize value. Every click of the Spin button causes the Piñata to be hit with a chance to burst the Piñata open, causing at least 3 unique prize values to be highlighted and won. Feeling lucky? If yes, then all 17 prize values can be yours! Multiplier Additionally, winning game rounds also have a chance to reveal a prize Multiplier. If a Multiplier appears after the Piñata bursts, then all winning prizes are multiplied by the displayed Multiplier value. The Multiplier can appear as 2x, 3x or 5x. Rules The aim of this game is to burst the Piñata and win the prize values displayed on the candies. When the Piñata bursts the winning candies and prizes are highlighted. The player can win anywhere from 3 to 17 unique prizes. Any win that results in less than 10 prizes has a chance to gain a multiplier of either 2x, 3x or 5x.
