Dragon Treasure Pearls BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 50 credits. All pays shown are based on minimum bet. Only highest winner paid for each winning combination. Pays occur in any position on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel. All wins are added. All wins are shown in currency. Reels 2, 3 and 4 each contain a number of positions that are randomly replaced with PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X before the reel spin is initiated. PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X and PEARL 5X substitute for KIRIN, FISH, CRANES, PEONY, COINS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If each PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X or 5X respectively. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X and PEARL 5X APPEAR on reels 2, 3 & 4 only and substitute for all pay table symbols except CHARACTER. CHARACTER pays in any position. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 CHARACTER trigger 8, 15 or 25 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 CHARACTER award an additional 3, 8, 15 or 25 free games respectively during the free game feature. Reels 2, 3 and 4 each contain a number of positions that are randomly replaced with PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X before the reel spin is initiated. Any PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X landing one symbol outside of the reel window will replace the adjacent symbol in the reel window with PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X respectively. PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X and PEARL 5X substitute for KIRIN, FISH, CRANES, PEONY, COINS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If each PEARL 2X, PEARL 3X or PEARL 5X substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X or 5X respectively. Different reels are used during the free game feature. Credits bet are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 11/16/2022
