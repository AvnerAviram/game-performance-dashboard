Main Game and Win Ways Strike It Gold™: Win Ways™ is played across a main reel set and a top reel. The main reel set has 6 reels. The top reel contains 4 symbols. Each symbol is added to the reel directly below on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Each main reel with or without the top reel symbols can contain up to 7 symbols. Each spin can have a maximum of 117649 Win Ways™ to win. Wins are awarded from left to right on adjacent symbols. Awards are multiplied by stake. Every winning symbol is part of a reaction and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the main reels and from the right on the top reel. Scatter and Wilds 4 Scatter symbols award 12 Free Spins. Every extra Scatter awards an additional 5 Free Spins. Wild substitutes all symbols except Scatters. Free Spins In Free Spins, Scatter symbols only appear on the top reel. During Free Spins: x3 Scatters award 5 additional Free Spins. x4 Scatters award 10 additional Free Spins. Increasing Win Multiplier Free Spins includes an Increasing Win Multiplier. The Increasing Win Multiplier starts at one and increases by one after each reaction. Wins from each reaction are multiplied by the Increasing Win Multiplier. Rules All prizes are for combinations left to right on adjacent symbols, except Scatters. Awards are multiplied by stake. Scatter symbols in view trigger Free Spins.
