Crabbin' Crazy Hold and Win Crabbin' Crazy Game Rules 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 25 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 25 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes(Let's Go Crabbin') Free s pins Yes (Frenzy) Instant win No The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Bonus and Crabs symbols, to form winning combinations. 3.2. Bonus symbol Landing a Bonus symbol on reels 1, 3 and 5 will trigger Free Spins Frenzy . 3.3. Crabs symbol Landing 5 or more Crabs symbols on the reels will trigger Let's Go Crabbin' Respins . 3.4. Free Spins Frenzy Bonus symbols exist only on reels 1, 3 and 5 during normal spins. Landing all 3 in view will trigger Free Spins Frenzy . 3.5. In Sync Reels During Free Spins Frenzy up to 5 reels can spin in sync on any spin. Note: Free Spins Frenzy can not be retriggered. 3.6. Let's Go Crabbin' 5 or more Crabs symbols in view will trigger 3 Let's Go Crabbin' Respins . This will turn them from Catch Me into Caught. Every time a new Catch Me crab lands in view during Let's Go Crabbin' Respins , the number of respins will reset to 3. A paytable is displayed during the feature displaying the minimum reward for each Catch Me crab. Wins are displayed in Catch Me Posters in the selected currency/coin. When the Let's Go Crabbin' Respins end, all crabs bounties will be collected. If the Fisherman lands during Respins, the Fisherman will gather them up to create extra spaces to catch more Crabs. 3.7. Pick a Crab On any spin, Fisherman can trigger a random modifier. Pick 1 of 3 Crabs, picked from a possible 4 outcomes, to reveal the following: Let's Catch Crabs – The Fisherman will land in view and pay the rewards of 3 or 4 Crabs . Bonus Add – Bonus symbols are added to reels 1, 3 and 5 for an increased chance of triggering Free Spins . This modifier does not guarantee Free Spins. Stacked Wilds – Super Stacked Wilds are added onto the reels to guarantee 2 or more stacked Wilds. Win Spin – Boxes are added to the reels. Any in view transform into the same paid symbol.
