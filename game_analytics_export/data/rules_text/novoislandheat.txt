Island Heat GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Soak up the sun in this 30-line slot game, Island Heat™. Escape to the tropics with a beautiful lady and with 7 or more flaming Bonus symbols on screen get ready to turn up the heat and set to party. Up to 30 Free Games can be played. Adjacent symbol stacks can become Mega Stacks and award full screen wins ! Main Game In Island Heat™ your perfect winning combination consists of 5 matching symbols along one of the up to 30 win lines that run from left to right. The Tiki is Wild and will act as a substitute for all symbols in the game except for the bonus and therefore increase your chances of winning. If the Magic Flame (Bonus) appears 7 to 9 times on reels 2, 3 or 4, 8 up to 32 free games are triggered. The Tiki and the Magic Flame symbol are stacked on the reels. Additionally, each game has a randomly chosen MEGA STACKS symbol, which appears stacked on all reels. Feature If you land 7, 8 or 9 Magic Flame (Bonus) on reels 2, 3 and 4, you receive 8, 16 or 32 free games, respectively. MEGA STACKS symbols also appear during free games. Rules All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. All prizes are on selected lines except scatters. Scatter symbols pay at any position on screen. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per scatter combination. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Each game a random MEGA STACKS symbol is chosen, which appears stacked on all reels. RTP-95.04 The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
