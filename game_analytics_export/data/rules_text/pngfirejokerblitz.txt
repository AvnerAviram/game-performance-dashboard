Fire Joker Blitz Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules Fire Joker Blitz is a 6-reel video slot machine with 4096 payways. Cash Coins can land on the reels with the following bet values: 25x, 20x, 15x, 12x, 10x, 8x, 5x, 4x, 3x, 2.5x, 2x, 1.6x, 1.5x, 1.4x, 1.2x, 1x, 0.8x, 0.7x, 0.6x and 0.5x. The Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coins have the following bet values: 100x, 250x and 1000x respectively. The Cash Coins are collected by landing up to 2 Fire Joker Collector symbols on reels 1 or 6. Only 1 Fire Joker Collector can land on reels 1 and 6. Landing two Fire Joker Collectors will collect all bonus Cash Coins present on the reels twice. The Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coins are collected with Fire Joker Collectors and can also be collected twice. If in the base game 2 Fire Joker Collectors land on the reels but no Cash Coins land, a re-spin (Blazing Re-Spin) will be triggered, awarding a second chance where only Cash Coins may land on the reels. There is a Golden Fire Joker above the reels. When Cash Coins land with no Fire Joker Collector, they will be collected in the Golden Fire Joker. This can grow in size when Cash Coins are collected. The Golden Fire Joker can award an instant prize randomly in the game round. The size of the Golden Fire Joker does not relate to the prize, it is completely random. The bonus prizes are: 10x, 20x, 50x, 100x, 200x, 500x, 2000x, 5000x. Golden Fire Joker is not active in Fire Blitz or Free Spins. Land 3 – 6 Scatters to choose your spin. Three options will be shown – Fire Blitz, Free Spins, and Mystery Spins. If Fire Blitz is chosen, the game will proceed into Fire Blitz. If Free Spins is chosen, the game will proceed into Free Spins. If Mystery Spins is chosen, the game will choose between Fire Blitz or Free Spins and proceed onto the game. For the Fire Blitz - 3 Scatters award 5 Fire Blitz spins, 4 Scatters award 10 Fire Blitz spins, 5 Scatters award 15 Fire Blitz spins, 6 Scatters award 20 Fire Blitz spins. If Fire Blitz is chosen, only Cash Coins and Fire Joker Collector can land. All Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coin are active. A special 150x Cash Coin can land only in the Fire Blitz. The maximum amount of Fire Blitz spins is 20. For the Free Spins, 3 Scatters award 10 Free Spins, 4 Scatters award 15 Free Spins, 5 Scatters award 20 Free Spins, 6 Scatters award 25 Free Spins. Scatters are collected in the meter on the reels, every 4 Scatters collected, a new stage is unlocked. The player will begin in stage 1 - Stage 1: x1, x2, x3 + 10, 15, 20 or 25 initial Free Spins. Stage 2: x2, x4, x6 + 10 extra Free Spins. Stage 3: x8, x10, x15 + 10 extra Free Spins. Stage 4: x20, x30, x50 + 10 extra Free Spins. A mulitplier from the current will be chosen on every spin and will be applied to the win. Only the Fire Joker, Gold Bell, X Symbol and all of the Fruit Symbols are present in the Free Spins. The maximum amount of Free Spins is 55. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatters, Fire Joker Collector, and Cash Coins. The average RTP is 94.27%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. There are 4096 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 6000 times total bet. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated. The bet may only be decreased and will not return to its original amount following any wins that would allow the initial bet size to be resumed. Last modification date: 10/22/2025
