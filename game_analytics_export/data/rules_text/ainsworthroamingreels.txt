Roaming Reels Rules 'Lion' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Tree' is a scatter. 'Lion' appears on reels 3, 4, and 5 during paid games only. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 94.14%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Game Features 20, 12 or 8 free games are triggered by any 5, 4 or 3 scattered 'Tree' respectively. During the Free Games Feature 'Lion' appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 only. During the Free Games Feature any 3 or more scattered 'Tree' will trigger an additional 5 free games. During the Free Games Feature, the Feature paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games.
