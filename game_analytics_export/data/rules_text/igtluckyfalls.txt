About the Game The object of Lucky Falls is to drop three coins into the same bucket to win a prize. Dropping a single coin into the center pot triggers the bonus round where the player picks one of three instant win prizes. Game Screen The game reveals a touchable drop zone area above the waterfall, a waterfall game board, and a meter displaying the number of coins remaining. At the bottom of the falls, there are six distinctly-colored buckets, each marked with a shamrock-shaped meter with prize labels, one central bonus pot, and REVEAL ALL and DROP buttons. To play, press the DROP button to randomly select a drop zone. Or, for more direct control, press the highlighted drop zone area above the waterfall to release a coin over the falls. Press REVEAL ALL to play the game automatically. (Best experienced in portrait orientation). Winning a Prize When a coin lands in a bucket, a leaf of the shamrock meter for that bucket is highlighted. If three coins land in the same bucket, the shamrock meter fills completely and animates to indicate a win. If a coin lands in the bonus pot, the game transitions to a bonus round behind the waterfall featuring three gems. Select one of the gems to reveal its prize value. Bonus round instant wins are immediately added to the total prize winnings. Game Completion When all seven coins have been dropped down the waterfall, the game ends.
