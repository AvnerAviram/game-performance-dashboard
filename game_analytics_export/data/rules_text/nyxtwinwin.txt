Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the scatter symbol. It substitutes for 2 of the high pay symbols or 1 of the low pay symbols. 1 Split symbol substitutes for 2 single symbols.
