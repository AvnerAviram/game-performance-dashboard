Dr Jekyll Goes Wild Game Introduction Dr Jekyll Goes Wild! is a 5x3 high slot featuring Jekyll Free Spins and Unlimited Hyde Spins. 3 or more Scatter Symbols awards Jekyll Free Spins with a special Potion Symbol on Reel 1 to trigger Unlimited Hyde Spins. Jekyll Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. 3 or more Scatter symbols activate Free Spins. 2 or more Scatter symbols can add extra Free Spins. Hyde Spins The player can win Hyde Spins during ever Jekyll Free Spins game. Hyde Spins are triggered from within Jekyll Free Spins. Hyde Spins are unlimited and will end when a Sand Timer appears on Reel 5. The Sand Timer will either STOP Hyde Spins or CONTINUE within Hyde Spins. When Hyde Spins end the player returns to complete any remeining Jekyll Free Spins. Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. A Dr Jekyll Trail will be available that awards up to 75 Jekyll Free Spins. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96.2%. Malfunction voids all play and pays. Last modification date: 12/29/2021
