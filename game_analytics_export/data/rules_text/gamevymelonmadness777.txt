Melon Madness 777 To Play Select how many lines you want to play and how much you want to bet per line Press the ‘Spin’ button. Match 3 identical symbols in a row to win the prize. See below for the prizes. Separate wins will be added together Your total win amount will be added to your game balance. Prizes Available Symbol Prize (multiple of stake) Bar X500 7 X200 Melon X40 Grape X20 Pear X10 Banana X5 Apple X4 Orange X3 Lemon X2 Cherry X1 Bell FREE SPINS (2-20 spins on all 7 lines) The Big One BONUS GAME Free Spins When you match 3 bells in a row, then you will trigger a free spin round. Select one of the 3 bells and reveal how many free spins you have won – it could be from 2 free spins up to 20! These will then play automatically with any wins being added up for you. Free game wins will be added at the end to main game winnings. You may also win even more free spins (up to an additional 20 each time) if you match another 3 bells and – of course – you could always trigger the bonus round! Your free spins will be played at the bet amount which triggered the win, but will always spin all 7 lines. Bonus When you match ‘The Big One’ three times, you will enter the special bonus game to win one of the 5 special jackpots. On a bonus round screen, you can spin the jackpot wheel. See which colour the wheel will land on to reveal which jackpot you will win! The jackpot amounts are pre-set and do not depend on your bet amount. The amount offered for each of the 5 jackpots is shown in rotation on the main screen and on the bonus screen as well – so you can always check how much you might win. If you trigger both Free Spins and the Big One bonus round in the same game, the bonus round will play first, before returning to see the Free spins play out.
