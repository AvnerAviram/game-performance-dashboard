Main Game Dolphin's PearlTm deluxe 10 is played on 2 reel sets: . Reel set 1, 4 x 5 reels, 25 win lines . This continues until there are no more winning combinations in view. Wilds Get four consecutive vertical wild symbols on reel set 1 (left) to convert the entire corresponding reel on reel set 2 (right) to a stacked wild. Free Spins Three or more Pearl scatter bonus symbols in view across both reel sets awards Free Spins.
