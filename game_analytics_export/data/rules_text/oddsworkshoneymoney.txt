Honey Money GENERAL RULES Honey Money is a 3-row, 5-Reel, 3 x 5 Game with 25 paylines. This game has a fixed number of 25 pay lines, you cannot change the number of your active paylines. When identical matching symbols appear consecutively on an active payline from leftmost to right, you win (see paytable for more details). Only the highest win on an active payline will be paid. If you have more than one win on a single spin, the total amount of your winnings is added and displayed in the win field, then it will be added to your balance. Total bet is the product of the bet per line multiplied by the number of lines. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RTP - 95% Last modification date: 2/10/2026
