Treasures of the deep Mobile Main Game screen Game Objective: Treasures of the Deep is a five-reel, nine payline slots game, with a feature game that gives you chances to win outside the normal game. You win based on the combination of the various symbols displayed on the selected winning payline(s). The 'Paytable' in game shows how much you will win for each combination. Free Game feature with 5 free spins will be triggered when at least 3 Helmet symbols appear anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4. The game also features, Helmet symbol which substitutes for all other symbols including the scatter Pearl symbol. Paytable Pay lines Game Information: Game is played with 9 variable lines, which are displayed on either sides of the game screen. SPIN can be triggered by tapping on the spin button with the current selected BET and LINES. Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE. Payline wins are calculated based on coin value * multiplier for symbol occurrences. Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline. Coinciding wins on different bet lines are all added to your total. Scatter (represented by Pearl symbol) wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins. All symbols pay from left to right, except the scatter symbol (represented by Pearl) which pays left to right and right to left. On the winning line the winning symbols will slightly animate with bounce in animation and the win line will be colored and remaining will be grayed out. The ticker messages will appear on bottom of game screen. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will increase, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN bar at bottom of the game. How to play: SPIN: Tap on SPIN to start spinning the reels. After the reels stop spinning, any winning combinations on all the selected no of lines will be paid according to the paytable. To calculate the line payout amount, multiply the respective symbol prize with the coin value. Remember only highest win in a line will be paid. To calculate the payout for scattered symbol, multiply the respective scattered symbol prize with the total bet. Result: Any wins are indicated by their paylines highlighted and symbols animating or, in the case of a scatter win, by their symbols animating the individual payline wins are shown at the end of each winning line. Game Rules : Play 1 to 9 lines. All symbols pay left to right, except scattered Pearl symbol, which pays left to right and right to left. Only the highest win on each selected payline is paid. Coinciding wins on different paylines are added. Line wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter wins are added to payline wins. Wild symbol (Helmet) appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4 and substitutes for all other symbols including the scatter Pearl symbol. Feature game play: A free games feature is triggered when 3 Helmet symbols appear anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4. Five free games are awarded, during which prizes are doubled except scatter and bonus wins. Each free game also awards a bonus prize equal to the triggering bet. The free game feature can be re-triggered and 5 free spins will be added to the current free spins. Free games are played at the lines and bet of the triggering game.
