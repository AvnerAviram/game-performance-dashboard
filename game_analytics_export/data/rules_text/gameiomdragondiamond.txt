Dragon Diamond Dragon Diamond is a 3x3 slot featuring exciting Wild Symbols, Wild Multipliers, and four Jackpot tiers. Wild Symbols substitute to complete winning combinations, while Wild Multipliers not only substitute but also boost line wins with multipliers up to 15x. Jackpots are triggered by specific Wild combinations on active paylines, with the top Jackpot offering a thrilling fixed prize. Higher bet levels significantly increase the odds of landing the required Wilds for the top Jackpot, creating an engaging and rewarding gameplay experience. WILD SYMBOLS FEATURE [5X] is Wild for Seven and Bar symbols, except when three Wilds of any type appear on an active payline. [5X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 5X the win. [3X] is Wild for Seven and Bar symbols, except when three Wilds of any type appear on an active payline. [3X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 3X the win. [2X] is Wild for Seven and Bar symbols, except when three Wilds of any type appear on an active payline. [2X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 2X the win. [3X] and [5X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 15x the win. [2X] and [5X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 10x the win. [2X] and [3X] in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 6x the win. [2X] appears only on reel 1, [3x] appears only on reel 2, and [5x] appears only on reel 3. [Blue Diamond] and [Green Diamond] are wild for Seven and Bar symbols, except when three wilds of any type appear on an active payline. JACKPOT WILDS FEATURE [2X], [3X], and [5X] on an active payline awards the highest tier Jackpot. 3 [Blue Diamond] on an active payline awards the second tier Jackpot. 3 [Green Diamond] on an active payline awards the third tier Jackpot. A combination of any three mixed wild symbols on an active payline awards the fourth tier Jackpot. Trigger combination for top tier, second tier or third tier Jackpot does not substitute for the fourth tier Jackpot. Increasing bet level improves the odds of [2X], [3X], and [5X] appearing together for the top tier Jackpot.
