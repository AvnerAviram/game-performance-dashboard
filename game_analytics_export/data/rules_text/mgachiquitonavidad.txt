Chiquito Navidad MAIN GAME In this game, each prize could either be risked, by pressing the play button, or to be collected, by pressing the collect button. In case the prize is risked, it will either be given the amount of a one level higher prize or the equivalent amount of bonus. Once the line of the three lemons is reached, the game becomes an up down, where the possible prizes go up or down in the prize plan. UPPER GAME In order to play this game it will be required to have enough bonus and credits. Whenever a prize is obtained, unless it is a blue seven prize, the player will be offered to either risk a portion of the prize or to collect it, until there are no more portions left. Choosing to risk a portion may allow the player to either obtain the double of that portion or the equivalent value of that portion in bonus. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. AUTO When nudges are activated, it allows you to rotate the reels automatically, always resulting in the best option for the player. If the button is permanently lit, this means that the auto option is activated. Otherwise, if the button is flashing, this indicates that the auto option is not activated. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. AUTO PLAY Activate the automatic game mode. The games will be launched automatically if the play button is kept pressed. The maximum prize that can be obtained in a minigame is the maximum prize of the bet in which the game has been made. A MINI-GAMES ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES, ACCUMULATING ALL THE ORNAMENTS OR THROUGH THE BARRIO GRIJANDER GAME. CUIDADÍN BONUS GAME 3 ornaments give access to game, which consists of drawing a prize. ESCALERA SERSUÁ BONUS GAME 3 snowy can give access to game, which consists of improving the prize through of prize stair. CALCETÍN CONDEMOR BONUS GAME 3 socks can give access to game, which consists of choosing a sock to win a prize. RULETA FISTRAL BONUS GAME 3 links can give access to game, which consists of stopping a roulette with prizes. ÁRBOL DIODENARRL BONUS GAME Is accessed through the minigame draw. Consists of stopping bonus. The bonus obtained may be multiplied by a random number. BARRIO GRIJANDER BONUS GAME Is accessed through the minigame draw. The square will move to as many positions as chiquito indicates, prizes will be accumulated, until it falls into an exit. ADDITIONAL FEATURES VERTICAL LINES Get the jaarl figures and you can play 8 lines in top game. CUMULATIVE GAME Accumulate the ornaments figures and give access to draw minigame.
