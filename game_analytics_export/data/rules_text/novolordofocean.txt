Lord of the Ocean Main Game Restock your chest of winnings with 10 paylines and 5 reels at your disposal. Winning paylines run from left to right. 'Scatter' symbols act as wildcards, replacing each and every symbol in regular games. Receive 10 free games if 3 or more 'Scatter' symbols appear on your playing field. Feature You can dramatically increase your winnings with this feature. A special symbol will be selected before you commence with a set of 10 free games. If this bonus symbol appears anywhere on the playing field, it will take over all positions on the reel on which it appears. Your current stake is used to calculate your winnings. Trigger: Any 3, 4 or 5 scattered GATE symbols trigger 10 Free Games. Top Prize: 50000 times bet per line per Free Game. Feature hit rate: 150 - 200 games. Rules GATE substitutes for all symbols except scattered SPECIAL EXPANDING symbol. All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet.
