Jewel of the Dragon: Valley of the Tiger PAYTABLE Before the reel spin initiates, the base game reel set is chosen from multiple possible reel sets. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Scatter pays are paid in addition to line pays. Winning pays per symbol per winning lines. Wins on multiple lines are added together. BASE GAME - BONUS In a base game spin where the Hold & Spin Feature is not triggered, the mystery GRAND jackpot may be awarded. In a base game spin where the Hold & Spin Feature is not triggered, the mystery MAJOR jackpot may be awarded. During the base game, SCATTER COIN displays MINOR, MINI or a bet multiplier value of x1-x200. WILD SYMBOL WILD is wild for DRAGON, TURTLE, CLAW, LOTUS, KING, QUEEN, JACK, TEN and NINE. WILD only appears on reels 2 to 5. SCATTER GEM only appears on reels 2 to 4. HOLD & SPIN FEATURE During the base game, SCATTER COIN displays MAJOR, MINOR, MINI or a bet multiplier value of x1-x200. Any combination of 6 or more SCATTER GEM or SCATTER COIN triggers the Hold & Spin Feature. 3 spins are awarded. All SCATTER GEM and SCATTER COIN appearing on the feature triggering spin are held during the feature. During each spin, any additional SCATTER COIN that lands will be held for the remainder of the feature. Additionally, if COIN lands, the number of spins remaining is reset to 3. If there was no MAJOR on the feature triggering spin, then during the feature, COIN displays MAJOR, MINOR, MINI or a bet multiplier value of x1-x200. If all 15 positions are held, then the GRAND jackpot is awarded, and no additional spins will be played. After all spins are complete, the value displayed on each SCATTER COIN is awarded. Any COIN containing MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI awards the value displayed in the corresponding meter. An alternate set of reels is used during the feature. SCATTER GEM does not appear on the reels used during the feature. Only COIN are evaluated during the feature. The Hold & Spin Feature cannot be retriggered during the feature. 3 SCATTER GEM displayed during the Hold & Spin Feature indicates that the Free Spins Feature was also triggered during the base game and will be played after the Hold & Spin Feature. If any combination of 15 SCATTER COIN or SCATTER GEM lands on reels during the spin, both the GRAND JACKPOT and the prizes displayed on each Coin and Scatter Coin are awarded without entering the Hold & Spin bonus. Maximum of only one MAJOR JACKPOT may be awarded in the Hold & Spin feature. FREE SPINS FEATURE During the base game, 3 SCATTER GEM on reels 2, 3 and 4 award 5 free spins. Additionally, the prize displayed on each GEM is also awarded and the prize displayed on each GEM is shown directly above the corresponding reel for the duration of the Free Spins Feature. Before the reel spin initiates, a number of positions on the reels 1 and 5 will be randomly replaced with a symbol based on the table below. REEL 1 : DRAGON, TURTLE, CLAW, LOTUS, KING, QUEEN, JACK, TEN or NINE REEL 5 : DRAGON, TURTLE, CLAW, LOTUS, KING, QUEEN, JACK, TEN, NINE or WILD During the Free Spins Feature, each Free Spin GEM that lands on reels 2, 3, and 4 will award the value displayed directly above the corresponding reel. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except SCATTER COIN does not appear. Free Spins Feature can not be retriggered during Free Spins Feature. BUY PASS™ BUY PASS™ allows direct access to the Hold & Spin Feature. BUY PASS™ allows direct access to the Free Spins Feature. Entry into Free Spins can be purchased using the BUY PASS button for 32.8x of the current selected bet. This will trigger 5 Free Spins, no additional free spins may be awarded in the bonus. BUY PASS™ cannot be activated during the Hold & Spin Feature. BUY PASS™ cannot be activated during the Free Spins Feature. BUY PASS™ cannot be activated during Autoplay.
