Abigail Ratchford's Treasure Quest PRIZE PLAN MAIN GAME 243 award combinations. Abigail's figure acts as a wild card and replaces all figures except the scatter figures, doubling its value. If three or more wildcard figures are obtained and cannot be replaced by a figure, you will give your own prize indicated in the winning plan. The maximum prize is 6350 * bet 243 AWARD COMBINATIONS The longer symbol combinations are awarded from left to right. The prize is multiplied by the number of times the symbol is repeated on each roller. WILD JUNGLE Randomly the game can turn up to a maximum of 4 reels into a wild card. You can't get freespins during the Wild Jungle. FREESPINS FREESPINS MEXICO Three 'Scatter' symbols or more gives access to the freespins selection screen. Each entry in the freespins increase the top counter, when you fill the gaps, you unlock a freespins. If you have two or more freespins unlocked you can choose a freespins. FREESPINS MÉXICO If the first game is chosen, the freespins Mexico will start. Play each game with at least two identical rollers, up to a maximum of 5. FREESPINS EGYPT If the second game is chosen, the freespins Egypt will start. Each winning fall increases the multiplier, up to a maximum of 'x15', and a new fall is obtained. When no reward is obtained, the counter is reset. FREESPINS THAILAND If the third freespins is chosen, the freespins Thailand will start. Each time you get a winning line you get a wildcard figure 'x2', 'x3' or both in a random position. FREESPINS JAPAN If the fourth freespins is chosen, the freespins Japan will start.
