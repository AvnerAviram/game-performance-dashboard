Secret Trail Match 3 Main Game Try your chance in this hot 7-rows, 8-column match game. Create a match of 3 or more symbols of the same color by switching two non-diagonal neighboring symbols. Symbols in match explode and new symbols fall from the top into the game board. There are win values from 0,5 to 300 times bet. Win Symbols: After every move, there is a chance for a win symbol to drop onto the board. You win as soon you successfully remove one of those symbols from the board. Collect the win value of a win symbol by moving it to the bottom of the board or by clearing it via a special symbol. Every win symbol gathered by you has its value added to your Credits. Matching Symbols: Symbols fall onto the board from above. If 3 identical symbols form a vertical or horizontal match, they are cleared from the board. If 4 or 5 symbols can be aligned in one of the formations, they are cleared from the board and depending on the formation - additional special symbols are created. Feature Cross Special Symbols: resemble the basic symbols in colour and shape, but are marked with a special golden frame. If they are combined with 2 or more symbols of the same colour, they cause an explosion across the entire vertical column and horizontal line. It doesn't matter if the target location is occupied by a basic, win or special symbol. The detonation of a bomb special symbol is caused simply by moving it - it clears up to 12 surrounding symbols. This is how they are created: If a match of 5 identical symbols in a L - or T - form is cleared from the board then a bomb special symbol is created in its wake. Combine Bomb Special symbols by switching it with other Special Symbols for an additional effect. Bell Special Symbols: can only be moved by one position in any direction. The swap can be with an adjacent basic, special or win symbol. When a bell special symbol is swapped with another basic symbol, all symbols the same colour turn into bell special symbols and immediately explode. This is how they are created: If a horizontal or vertical match of 5 identical symbols is cleared from the board, a bell special symbol is created in its wake. Match 3 or more of the same symbol to explode them. Every move costs a bet. After every move Win Symbols might be added to the board. If 4 symbols of the same colour are matched (in line horizontally or vertically), a Cross Special Symbol is created. The Cross Special Symbol needs to match with 2 or more symbols of the same colour in order be cleared. If 5 symbols of the same colour are matched in a L or T shape, a Bomb Special Symbol is created. Bomb Special Symbols can be swapped in any direction, with any symbol or special symbol. If 5 symbols of the same colour in a row or column are matched, a Bell Special symbol is created. Bell Special Symbols can be swapped in any direction, with any symbol or special symbol. Combine Special symbols by switching them with each other for an additional effect. After every move, there is a chance for win symbols to drop onto the board. You gather win symbols by moving them to the bottom of the board or by hitting them with the explosion of a special symbol. Every symbol gathered by you has its value added to your Credits. The player can change bet before every move (except tutorial). RTP is 95.32%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
