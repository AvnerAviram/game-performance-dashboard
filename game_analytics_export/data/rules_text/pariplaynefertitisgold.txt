Nefertiti's Gold Nefertiti’s Gold is a 5x3 video slot with 20 paylines featuring a Scarab Wild Replacement feature, Nefertiti’s Magic Flame Multiplier, Phoenix Stacked Wilds with respins, and free spins. Wins are awarded for 3, 4, and 5 of a kind symbols from left to right. The return to player (RTP) is 95.93%. This is a medium to high volatility game. Nefertiti’s Gold 'WILD' Symbol Wilds replace all symbols except the scatter symbol Scatter symbol 3 scatters landing on reels 1, 3 and 5 trigger 12 free spins Nefertiti’s Magic Flame Randomly triggered, the Magic Flame feature can award multipliers. Here, Nefertiti will appear at the right of the reels to cast a spell and fill up the Multiplier Meter on the left of the reels. This meter will fill up to reveal a random multiplier, which will remain in place until the next win. Phoenix Feature The Phoenix Feature is also triggered randomly, and sees the phoenix fly across the screen with magic dust and randomly assign at least 1 stacked wild. Scarab Feature If a Scarab symbol lands in the middle of reels 2 or 4, it will trigger the Scarab feature. Here, a set of scarabs will begin crawling up and onto the reels to eat and replace random symbols with wilds. If the scarab appears on 1 reel, between 2 and 5 wilds will appear. If scarabs appear on 2 reels, between 5 and 9 wilds can be awarded. Free Spins Three scatter symbols landing on reels 1, 3 and 5 will pay double the total bet and award 15 free spins. GAME RULES Payline wins pay if in succession from the left most reel to the right most reel. Only the highest win per payline is paid. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the Paytable. Max bet plays the game at the highest bet level. A payline win is equal to the values shown on the Paytable multiplied by the bet per line. Simultaneous wins on different paylines are added. Balance displays money available to wager.
