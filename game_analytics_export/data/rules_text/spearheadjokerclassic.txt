Joker Classic Joker Classic is a 5 reel 10 line video slot featuring stacked wilds pay both ways and respins. The Paylines are first evaluated left-to-right on adjacent reels starting on the leftmost reel and then evaluated right-to-left on adjacent reels starting on the rightmost reel. Only the longest matching combination per winning line is paid for each direction. 5 of a kind combinations are only paid out from left to right. Simultaneous or coinciding wins are added together. The actual payouts equals the sum of the dynamic paytable values corresponding to each line won. Game plays on total bet. Wilds Wild Substitutes for all symbols except Scatter Coins. Wild appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 only. Respin Feature At the end of any spin, landing one or more reels with a full stack of wild awards exactly 1 respin with all full wild stack reels locked in place. Landing any additional full stack of wilds on any unlocked reels during a respin awards exactly 1 more respin. The maximum number of respins possible is 3. Scatter Coins Special scatter coin symbols show prize amounts visible when landing on the reels. Land at least 3 Scatter Coin on the same spin to be paid the sum of all prize amounts visible. Scatter Coin symbols do not appear during the respins. Scatter coin prize amounts for each coin range from 1 times the bet amount to 1000 times the bet amount. Scatter Coins appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 only. Game RTP The regular game has a theoretical RTP of 94.1% Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Last modification date: 11/21/2023
