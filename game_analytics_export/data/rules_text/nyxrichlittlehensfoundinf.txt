Rich Little Hens Founding Feathers PAYTABLE Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Multiple line wins are added together. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. WILD SYMBOL WILD substitutes for all symbols except RED COIN, SILVER COIN, BLUE COIN and their combinations. MYSTERY REVEAL During the base game, before pays are evaluated, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, JOHN HEN, GEORGE HEN WASHINGTON, UNCLE HEN, BELL, INK BOTTLE, ACE, KING,QUEEN, JACK, or TEN. In addition, MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a RED COIN, SILVER COIN, BLUE COIN or their combination on top of a replaced symbol before pays are evaluated. During the Free Spins Feature, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, JOHN HEN, GEORGE HEN WASHINGTON, UNCLE HEN, BELL, INK BOTTLE, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN before pays are evaluated. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except RED COIN, SILVER COIN, and BLUE COIN do not appear. The distribution of Free Spins Feature prizes changes with bet option. The size of the Red, White, and Blue Hens over time has no effect on triggering the Free Spins Feature. FREE SPINS FEATURE Any combination of RED COIN, SILVER COIN, BLUE COIN may trigger the Free Spins Feature and award 1x total bet. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered by RED COIN, SILVER COIN, or BLUE COIN, then, for each coin involved, "WINNER!" appears by the RED HEN, WHITE HEN, or BLUE HEN respectively. If a RED COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that RED COIN may add 1 free spin to the RED HEN meter for the active bet option. If a SILVER COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that SILVER COIN may add 0.25x-6.75x Current Bet to the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI meter for the active bet option. If a BLUE COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that BLUE COIN may increase the Wild Multiplier in the BLUE HEN meter for the active bet option. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with SILVER COIN, then each MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a jackpot symbol for the MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI. Jackpot symbols are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. The MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI have 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, and 2 spaces respectively. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, and the corresponding jackpot is awarded and then reset before any additional jackpots are awarded. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with SILVER COIN, the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI Jackpot meters are reset. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with RED COIN, then the number of free spins displayed in the RED HEN meter are awarded. If the Free Spins Feature was not triggered with RED COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded when Free Spins Feature is awarded. The RED HEN meter can display a maximum of 100 Free Spins. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with RED COIN, the RED HEN meter is reset to 9 free spins. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with BLUE COIN, then all winning combinations involving CREDIT WILD award a Wild Bet Multiplier. The BLUE HEN meter can display a maximum Wild Bet Multiplier of 8x.
