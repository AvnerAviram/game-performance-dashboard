Wild Rainbows GAME OBJECTIVES: WILD RAINBOWS is a slot game with 5x4 reel set which will be expanded to 5x6 reels and paylines increasing from 20 to 40 paylines. 3 different Sweet symbols will be placed randomly on the reels: BLUE, GREEN, and PINK. Each Sweet symbol that lands in view has a chance to trigger its associated feature: Pink Sweet Symbol: JACKPOT FEATURE Blue Sweet Symbol: ACCELERATOR FEATURE Green Sweet Symbol: EXPANDING REELS FEATURE Multiple features can be triggered simultaneously, resulting in a combination of the features based on the Sweet symbols present in view when the feature is triggered. The colored jars above the reels are purely cosmetic and do not affect the player's chance of triggering a feature. HOW TO PLAY: BET VALUE: When the player enters the game, the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the '+' or '-' buttons on either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. Clicking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the minimum and maximum BETS. REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game. PLAY: The PLAY button in the center-right position starts the game in desktop and mobile landscape mode. The PLAY button in the center-bottom position starts the game in mobile portrait mode. PAYTABLE: The PAYTABLE can be viewed by clicking on the HAMBURGER icon and selecting the PAYTABLE option. Click on the 'X' icon to close the menu and return to the game. VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF. In demo-money mode, the AUTOPLAY option will be disabled. CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under 'BALANCE'. Player's current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen. AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play. To configure AUTOPLAY: Click on the AUTOPLAY button, then select the required number of spins. AUTOPLAY begins automatically. The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time. MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP): Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information. Click the 'i' icon to view game information and rules. Click the 'PAYTABLE' icon to view the game awards. Click the 'X' icon to close the menu and return to the gameplay. GAME RULES: WILD RAINBOWS is a 5x4 to 5x6 reel slot game with 20 to 40 paylines. The BET value can be adjusted using the '+' and '-' buttons. Click on the PLAY button or press SPACEBAR (desktop) to begin the game with the currently selected BET. The paytable always displays the win amounts for the currently selected BET. All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive order on a payline, starting from the left-most reel except for the Sweet symbols, SPECIAL COIN (CASH, JACKPOT), ACCELERATOR CASH, COLLECT UNICORN, and SPIN symbols. All wins are paid according to the paytable and are added to the player's BALANCE. Coinciding wins on different paylines are added to the player's BALANCE. Only the highest win amount on each payline is paid. The BLUE, GREEN, and PINK Sweet symbols are pseudo symbols with no payout. Sweet symbols added to their respective jars are purely cosmetic and do not affect the player's chance of triggering a feature. If a feature is triggered, the combination of Sweet symbols in view determines the feature triggered (e.g., if BLUE and PINK Sweet symbols are in view, the JACKPOT + ACCELERATOR feature is triggered). The WILD RAINBOW symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Sweet symbols (BLUE, GREEN, and PINK) and the SPECIAL COIN (CASH, JACKPOT), ACCELERATOR CASH, COLLECT UNICORN and SPIN symbols. The COLLECT UNICORN symbol appears only on reels 1 and 5 and does not appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Win animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere on the screen or pressing SPACEBAR (desktop). Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your gameplay history. MIN BET is $0.20, and MAX BET is $25.00. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 94.54%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. All pays are shown in USD currency. EXPANDING REELS FEATURE: EXPANDING REELS FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more GREEN sweet symbol(s) land in view. The player is awarded with 5 FREE SPINS, and the reel set will be expanded from 5x4 to 5x6, increasing paylines from 20 to 40. EXPANDING REELS FEATURE uses the same symbols as the base game. If WILD RAINBOW symbols land in view during FREE SPINS, the WILD RAINBOW symbols expand to fill the entire reel with WILD RAINBOW symbols. EXPANDING REELS FEATURE is played at the same BET as the triggering game. EXPANDING REELS will not be re-triggered and ends when no more FREE SPINS remain. JACKPOT FEATURE: JACKPOT FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more PINK Sweet symbol(s) lands anywhere in view. In JACKPOT FEATURE, the player will be awarded with 5 FREE SPINS. Along with the base game symbols, SPECIAL COIN symbols will also appear on the reels. SPECIAL COIN symbols can reveal either a CASH or a JACKPOT symbol. Each SPECIAL COIN with CASH has a random BET MULTIPLIER range from: 0.5x, 1x, and 1.5x. During the JACKPOT FEATURE, when 1 or more CASH symbols appear anywhere in the reel view, the prize values shown on the CASH symbols are added and at the end of the feature, all CASH symbol wins will be added to the player's balance. Each SPECIAL COIN with JACKPOT symbols can be either MINOR (10x BET), MAJOR (50x BET), MEGA (500x BET), or GRAND (5000x BET). To win a JACKPOT, collect 4 corresponding JACKPOT symbols across all FREE SPINS in that round. Example: To win MEGA JACKPOT the player needs to collect 4 MEGA JACKPOT Special Symbols across all the FREE SPINS. If more than 4 JACKPOT symbols of the same type are collected, additional JACKPOTS are awarded for each set of 4 symbols collected. Example: If the player collects 9 MEGA JACKPOT Special symbols across all the FREE SPINS, the player will be awarded 2x MEGA JACKPOT for the first 8 MEGA JACKPOT Special Symbols collected. If the player collects 9 MEGA JACKPOT Special symbols till the 4 th FREE SPIN, the player will be awarded 2X MEGA JACKPOT and the remaining 1 MEGA JACKPOT Special symbol will be carried forward to the 5 th FREE SPIN. MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA, and GRAND are fixed JACKPOTS and do not take contributions from player bets. JACKPOT FEATURE is played at the same bet as the triggering game. JACKPOT FEATURE cannot be re-triggered and ends when no more FREE SPINS remain. ACCELERATOR FEATURE: ACCELERATOR FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more BLUE Sweet symbol(s) lands anywhere in view. When triggered, the player is awarded 2 FREE SPINS. Only COLLECT UNICORN, ACCELERATOR CASH, BLANK, and SPIN symbols appear in the reels. Each CASH Symbol has a random BET MULTIPLIER range from 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 3.5x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 250x, 2500x. The COLLECT UNICORN symbol will appear only on reels 1 and 5 and will not appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. The COLLECT UNICORN symbol collects all the ACCELERATOR CASH symbols available in view. All ACCELERATOR CASH symbols are collected at least once. Each COLLECT UNICORN symbol awards all ACCELERATOR CASH symbols in view. Example: When the player lands COLLECT UNICORN symbols on reels 1 and 5, the payout is 2x the sum of the value of ACCELERATOR CASH symbols that landed in view. If a SPIN symbol lands in view 1, 2, or 3, additional FREE SPINS will be awarded randomly. SPIN symbol will land only on reels 2, 3, and 4 and do not appear on reels 1, 5. ACCELERATOR FEATURE is played at the same bet as the triggering game. ACCELERATOR FEATURE cannot be re-triggered and ends when no more FREE SPINS remain. MIXED FEATURES: JACKPOT + EXPANDING REELS FEATURE: FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more PINK and GREEN Sweet symbols land anywhere in view. The player will be awarded with 5 FREE SPINS, and the reel set expands from 5x4 to 5x6, increasing paylines from 20 to 40. In addition to base game symbols, SPECIAL COIN symbols appear on the reels. If WILD RAINBOW symbols land in view during FREE SPINS, WILD RAINBOW symbols will expand to fill the entire reel. When all symbols (WILD RAINBOW, SPECIAL COIN, CASH SYMBOLS, and JACKPOT symbols) land in a single spin, SPECIAL COIN symbols are unveiled first, followed by the CASH collection, JACKPOT symbol collection, WILD RAINBOW symbol expansion and line payout will happen last. EXPANDING REELS + ACCELERATOR FEATURE: FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more GREEN and BLUE Sweet symbols land anywhere in view. When triggered, the player is awarded 5 FREE SPINS, and the reel set expands from 5x4 to 5x6, increasing paylines from 20 to 40. In addition to base game symbols, COLLECT UNICORN, ACCELERATOR CASH, and SPIN symbols appear on the reels. If WILD RAINBOW symbols land in view during FREE SPINS, the WILD RAINBOW symbol will expand to fill the entire reel. If a SPIN symbol lands in view 1, 2, or 3, additional FREE SPINS will be awarded randomly. SPIN symbol will land only on reels 2, 3, and 4 and do not appear on reels 1, 5. Landing a COLLECT UNICORN symbol collects all ACCELERATOR CASH symbols in view. When all symbols (ACCELERATOR CASH, COLLECT UNICORN, SPIN, and WILD RAINBOW) land in a single spin, ACCELERATOR CASH symbols are collected via COLLECT UNICORN first, followed by SPIN collection, WILD RAINBOW symbol expansion and line win payout will happen last. JACKPOT + ACCELERATOR FEATURE: FEATURE will be triggered randomly when one or more PINK and BLUE Sweet symbols land anywhere in view. In addition to base game symbols, SPECIAL COIN, COLLECT UNICORN, ACCELERATOR CASH, and SPIN symbols appear on the reels. SPECIAL COIN in JACKPOT + ACCELERATOR FEATURE always unveils a JACKPOT symbol. SPIN symbol will land only on reels 2, 3, and 4 and do not appear on reels 1, 5. When all symbols (ACCELERATOR CASH, SPECIAL COIN, SPIN, and COLLECT UNICORN) land in a single spin, SPECIAL COIN symbols are unveiled first to show the JACKPOT symbol, followed by JACKPOT symbol collection, ACCELERATOR CASH collection via COLLECT UNICORN, and SPIN collection happens last. JACKPOT + EXPANDING REELS + ACCELERATOR FEATURE: FEATURE triggers randomly when one or more PINK, GREEN, and BLUE Sweet symbols land anywhere in view. The player will be awarded with 5 FREE SPINS, and the reel set expands from 5x4 to 5x6, increasing paylines from 20 to 40. In addition to base game symbols, SPECIAL COIN, COLLECT UNICORN, ACCELERATOR CASH, and SPIN symbols appear on the reels. SPECIAL COIN in JACKPOT + EXPANDING REELS + ACCELERATOR FEATURE always unveils a JACKPOT symbol. If WILD RAINBOW symbols land in view during FREE SPINS, WILD RAINBOW symbols will expand to fill the entire reel. If a SPIN symbol lands in view 1, 2, or 3, additional FREE SPINS will be awarded randomly. Landing a COLLECT UNICORN symbol collects all ACCELERATOR CASH symbols in view. When all symbols (WILD RAINBOW, SPECIAL COIN, SPIN, ACCELERATOR CASH, and COLLECT UNICORN) land in a single spin, SPECIAL COIN symbols are unveiled first to show the JACKPOT symbol, followed by JACKPOT symbol collection, ACCELERATOR CASH collection via COLLECT UNICORN, SPIN collection, WILD RAINBOW symbol expansion and line wins are paid last.
