Gem Splitter The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and a minimum of 243 ways to win. All wins are paid for combinations of at least 3 identical symbols on any positions on subsequent reels, starting from the first reel. Only the highest win per way is paid. Wild substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and special symbols. Wild is not a paying symbol. Scatter symbols pay at any position on the reels. SPECIAL FEATURES SPLITTER - WAYS OF SPLIT In each spin, two additional elements are drawn: a type of a symbol that will split if it appears on the reels and a type of the split. When the splitting symbol is drawn, it will split according to the given split type into several independent, full-value symbols that occupy one position on the reels. There are five possible split types: a split into 2 symbols on top of each other; a split into 2 symbols side by side; a split into 3 symbols on top of each other; a split into 3 symbols side by side; a split into 4 symbols in a 2x2 layout. Only regular symbols can be split. The splitting mechanism may result in extending the number of winning symbols up to 15 or increasing the number of ways applicable to a given spin. FREE SPINS A draw of 3, 4 or 5 Scatters triggers Free Spins. Special symbols can increase the number of Free Spins by 3. The winnings from Free Spins accumulate until the end of the feature. Free Spins are played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated the feature. The 2x2 split type remains active throughout Free Spins. SPECIAL SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOL Wild substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and special symbols. Wild is not a paying symbol. SCATTER SYMBOL Scatter symbols pay at any position on the reels.
