The Theoretical Return to Player is: 96.313% (without BUY PASS) 96.848% (with BUY PASS) KING’S RE-SPIN Triggered whenever 3 or more WILD symbols appear anywhere on screen. Any wins from the initial spin are paid and 1 RE-SPIN is awarded. SCATTERS that appear on the triggering spin turn into WILDS before RE-SPIN commences. During RE-SPINS, KING REELS spin over all triggering symbol positions as an overlay. RE-SPIN is retriggered if one or more additional WILD symbols appear on the reels after a RE-SPIN. Any new WILD symbols spun up during RE-SPINS are HELD for the duration of the RE-SPINS and supersede all symbols appearing beneath them. RE-SPINS continue until no additional WILD symbols appear or all reel positions are occupied by WILD symbols. KING’S RE-SPIN wins are paid once only after all RE-SPINS have completed. KING’S RE-SPIN is played at the lines and coin value of the trigger game. KING’S RE-SPIN wins are added to pay line and SCATTER wins. RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE 8, 9 or 10 Free Games are triggered whenever 3, 4 or 5 SCATTER symbols appear respectively. A COLLECT METER appears above the reel window during Free Games. COLLECT METER indicates the SCATTER COLLECT COUNT throughout the Free Games. Before Free Games commence, each SCATTER that appears in the triggering combination is added to the COLLECT METER. Every SCATTER that appears during Free Games increases the SCATTER COLLECT COUNT by 1. 1 EXTRA Free Game is also awarded for every SCATTER that appears during Free Games. SCATTER COLLECT COUNT is cumulative throughout RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE. Once SCATTER COLLECT COUNT reaches its maximum value, COLLECT METER ceases to increment for the remainder of the Feature. 1 EXTRA Free Game continues to be awarded for each SCATTER that appears after COLLECT METER increment ceases. All wins in which one or more WILD symbols appear during Free Games are multiplied by the active WILD MULTIPLIER. WILD MULTIPLIER initially starts with a value of x5. WILD MULTILIER permanently INCREASES during the RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE once SCATTER COLLECT COUNT reaches certain thresholds: LEVEL 2 (SCATTER COLLECT COUNT = 6): WILD MULTIPLIER = x10 LEVEL 3 (SCATTER COLLECT COUNT = 9): WILD MULTIPLIER = x15 LEVEL 4 (SCATTER COLLECT COUNT = 12): WILD MULTIPLIER = x25 LEVEL 5 (SCATTER COLLECT COUNT = 15): WILD MULTIPLIER = x50 LEVEL 6 (SCATTER COLLECT COUNT = 18): WILD MULTIPLIER = x100 All wins are calculated once only per Free Game after COLLECT METER has updated. In the case that SCATTER COLLECT COUNT surpasses multiple thresholds in the same spin, WILD MULTIPLIER immediately increases to the value of the highest LEVEL achieved and the cumulative total of additional Free Games is awarded. KING’S RE-SPIN can be triggered during RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE. RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE cannot be retriggered. RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE is played at the lines and coin value of the trigger game. RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE wins are added to pay line and SCATTER wins. WILD MULTIPLIER value resets to x1 once RISE OF THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE completes. All wins on specified pay lines only except SCATTERS. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTERS which pay any.
