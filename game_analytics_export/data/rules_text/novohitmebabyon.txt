Hit ME Baby Game Theme and Graphics Have you got the power to fight your way to a Jackpot? If so, Hit ME Baby! is the game for you, it really packs a punch. Main Game Hit ME Baby! is simple, click or tap the bag to punch. Each punch will display a Power, which is shown in the Power Gauge, the higher the power, the higher the prize, that's how winning is done! Jackpot Punch If you've got the eye of a tiger, you might be powerful enough to land a jackpot punch by hitting in the 976-994 power range. If you do, one Jackpot Punch is awarded! In this punch you are guaranteed a Mini, Minor, Maxi, Major or championship winning Grand Jackpot that pays 999x your bet. Rules The player clicks or taps on the punching bag and the player's power is shown on the Power Gauge. Based on this result, the Power Meter displays and awards the corresponding prize. If the player manages to get between 976-994 as their power, they get an extra punch that awards them a guaranteed jackpot win. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Last modification date: 12/17/2024
