Book of Ra Classic Rules 'Book' substitutes for all symbols, except 'Special Expanding Symbol'. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines, excluding Scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins Free Games The feature consists of 10 Free Games played at trigger bet and lines. Free Games can be retriggered. During the feature, one symbol is allocated as the 'Special Expanding Symbol'. This symbol will expand to cover all three positions on the reel, and pays for any on selected lines. Trigger: 3, 4 or 5 scattered 'Book' symbols trigger Free Games. Top prize: 45000 times line bet per Free Game. Feature hit rate: 150 - 200 games. Main Game The aim with Book of Ra™ is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. In the regular game, every winning combination begins on the left and runs along the win line to the right. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other without any other symbols between them. The 'Book' symbol (Joker) substitutes all symbols. Game Theme and Graphics Play the classic version of the popular Book of Ra™ and discover hidden treasures. 9 win lines on 5 reels in the regular game, as well as numerous bonus games provide an adventure with mysterious hieroglyphics.
