Luxor Fortunes SYMBOL PAYOUTS All REEL WAYS™ pays are multiplied by current total bet divided by 20. WILD is wild and sbstitutes for all symbols. WILD only appears on reel 2,3 and 4. Only the highest winner paid per winning combinations. There are 243 ways to win which must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. All wins on multiple ways are added together. All pays in currency. JACKPOT FEATURE When one or more WILD appears on reels, the player may be awarded JACKPOT feature. If the JACKPOT feature is awarded, a field of 12 pick options is presented to the player. Touching or clicking a coin will reveal the symbol of any one of the four Jackpots: GRAND, MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI. When 3 matching symbols have been revealed, the corresponding jackpot will be awarded and the feature ends. Jackpot values are proportional to bet per spin. FREE SPINS FEATURE 3, 4, OR 5 scattered FREE SPINS symbols on adjacent reels, starting with the leftmost reel, award 10 free spins and initial credit award of 5x, 10x, or 50x total bet respectively. ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, 10 and 9 symbols do not appear during FREE SPINS. The JACKPOT feature may be triggered during the FREE SPINS. Additional free spins may be awarded during the FREE SPINS feature and are added to the remaining number of free spins. FREE SPINS are played on alternate set of reels. WILD substitute for FREE SPINS in any left to right winning combination. FREE SPINS are played with the same bet as the BASE GAME which triggered the feature. The awards and winning combinations in the FREE SPINS are same as the BASE GAME. The FREE SPINS ends when no free spins remain. GAME RULES Only the highest win paid per winning combination. Scatter pays are paid in addition to REEL WAYS™ pays. LEGAL NOTICES A stable Internet connection is required. In the event of a disconnection, please relaunch the game to complete any outstanding transactions. In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
