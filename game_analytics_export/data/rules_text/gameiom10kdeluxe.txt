10k Deluxe How to play £10k Deluxe is an action-packed 5 panel scratch card with a set stake value of £1. Game Three - Reveal three matching symbols on the 3x3 board to win the amount displayed in the paytable - up to £10,000! Game Four - Match any three cash values to bag an instant win of that amount. Bonus Game - Reveal a bonus multiplier for the total winnings of your card, an instant cash prize or a free scratch card.
