Mega Multi Diamonds RTP 96.0% VOLATILITY: HIGH GAME RULES Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Malfunction voids all pays and play. BASE GAME RULES There are three multiplier meters, each corresponding to two symbols. Multiplier Meter 1 corresponds to High Pay Symbol 1 and Low Pay Symbol 1. Multiplier Meter 2 corresponds to High Pay Symbol 2 and Low Pay Symbol 2. Multiplier Meter 3 corresponds to High Pay Symbol 3 and Low Pay Symbol 3. All three multiplier meters start with a value of 1x. Each line win with Low Pay Symbol 1, Low Pay Symbol 2, or Low Pay Symbol 3 increases the value of the corresponding multiplier meter by 1x, to a maximum of 100x. After all increases have been applied to the multiplier meters, all wins with High Pay Symbol 1, High Pay Symbol 2, or High Pay Symbol 3 are multiplied by the value of the corresponding multiplier meter. After all wins have been paid, if the value of a win was multiplied by the value of a multiplier meter, then that meter is reset to a value of 1x.
