G.R.O.W WILD substitutes for all symbols except BONUS, GOLDEN BEE and G.R.O.W. symbols. The BONUS, GOLDEN BEE and G.R.O.W. are all scatter symbols. The GOLDEN BEE and G.R.O.W. symbols appear on all the reels in the Base game and Free Spins. The BONUS symbols appear on all reels in the base game and only on reels 1, 3 and 5 in Free Spins. GOLDEN BEE and G.R.O.W. symbols also act as WILD symbols and payout accordingly. BEANSTALK TREASURE At the top of the reels there are 4 CHESTS. Each CHEST contains a CASH VALUE. To win a CASH VALUE, the corresponding BEANSTALK must reach the CHEST. BEANSTALKS will start at the bottom of the reels and must grow 10 times in total to reach the corresponding CHEST. If the BEANSTALK grows more than 10 times, the extra growth will be added to the new BEANSTALK. Total in Chests is the total of all cash values contained in the 4 CHESTS. Each CHEST has a unique cash value which can change throughout the game. The cash value per CHEST is calculated based on the average of all contributing bets for that CHEST, in addition to any amount awarded from GOLDEN BEE symbols. A contributing bet is defined as the current total bet of a spin, in which a G.R.O.W. symbol lands and causes the corresponding BEANSTALK to grow towards the CHEST. With larger contributing bets, greater cash values can be won from CHESTS. The Total in Chests value persists between play sessions. Landing G.R.O.W. symbols on the reel set will cause one or more BEANSTALK(S) to grow. Each G.R.O.W. symbol will cause the corresponding BEANSTALK(S) to grow by the same amount. If a G.R.O.W. symbol lands on reels 1 or 5, it will increase the height of the first or last BEANSTALK, respectively. If a G.R.O.W. symbol lands on reels 2, 3 or 4, it will increase the height of the BEANSTALK(S) adjacent to it. When one or more GOLDEN BEE symbol(s) land on the reel set, an additional cash amount will be added to the corresponding CHEST(S), which will be reflected in the combined total of all four CHESTS. When a GOLDEN BEE symbol lands on the reels, one or more GOLDEN BEE(S) will fly up to the corresponding CHEST(S) and award 2x total bet per GOLDEN BEE. If a GOLDEN BEE symbol lands on reel 1 or 5, it will increase the value contained in the first or last CHEST respectively. If a GOLDEN BEE symbol lands on reel 2, 3 or 4, it will increase the value contained in the two CHESTS which are adjacent to the top of the reel. FREE SPINS Land 3 or more BONUS symbols to be awarded 10 Free Spins. WILD symbols appear naturally on the reels in Free Spins in addition to the MYSTERY symbol replacements. During Free Spins, land 3 BONUS symbols to be awarded 10 extra Free Spins. In Free Spins, BONUS symbols only land on reels 1, 3 and 5. Any extra Free Spins won are added to the number of Free Spins remaining. All Free Spins are played with the same bet per line and winning combinations as the game which triggered the feature. The feature uses a different reel set to the base game. The Free Spins will be played automatically. The feature will end when there are no Free Spins remaining or the maximum win is achieved.
