Fortune Rangers Fortune Rangers™ is a 5-reel, 76-line (fixed) video slot featuring Lucky Wild Reels, Free Spins and Linked Reels. The game is played with 76 bet lines (fixed), 1-10 bet levels (20 coins per level) and different coin values. The bet level is set using the LEVEL selector. The coin value is set using the COIN VALUE selector. COINS displays the number of coins available to wager. MAX BET plays the game at the highest bet level and the current coin value. When playing at any lower bet level, MAX BET must be clicked twice to play the round at the selected level. AUTOPLAY automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the Paytable. A bet line win in coins is equal to the value shown in the Paytable multiplied by the bet level. A bet line win in currency is equal to the win in coins multiplied by the coin value. Bet line wins pay if in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added. All coin wins pay on bet lines only. Wild Symbol Wild symbols can appear in the main game and substitute for all symbols except for Scatter symbols. Wild symbol substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a bet line according to the Paytable. Lucky Wild Reels Lucky Wild Reels randomly appear in the main game as an overlay reel with only Wild symbols. Between 1 and 5 reels can turn into Lucky Wild Reels. Free Spins 3 or more Scatter symbols appearing anywhere on the reels in the main game activate Free Spins. 3 Scatter symbols = 8 Free Spins 4 Scatter symbols = 12 Free Spins 5 Scatter symbols = 16 Free Spins Free Spins wins are added to any bet line wins. At the end of Free Spins, the game returns to the round that activated Free Spins. Free Spins are played at the same bet level and coin value as the round that activated Free Spins. Additional Free Spins cannot be won during Free Spins. Linked Reels The Linked Reels feature can be randomly activated in Free spins only. At the beginning of each spin, identical linked reels appear adjacently on any of reels 1-4. The right reel is a copy of the left reel, with reel 1 linked to reel 2 and/or reel 3 linked to reel 4.
