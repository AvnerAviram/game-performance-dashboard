Respin King HOW TO PLAY PLAY MODE AND BALANCE You can play the game in either FUN or REAL mode. In fun mode, you are playing just for the fun of the score. In real mode, you are playing with money from your real money balance. Your balance is shown in the BALANCE box both in fun and real mode. PLACING BETS This game is played with a fixed number of active paylines. The BET is the COIN value multiplied by the number of active paylines. To set the bet amount, use the '-' and '+' buttons. CONTINUOUS SPIN Clicking and holding the SPIN button for continuous spinning. To stop the continuous spin session, release the SPIN button. When CONTINUOUS SPIN is on, some win animations and sound are skipped, the reels spin faster and the game results are displayed faster. AUTOPLAY MODE To select the number of autospins, use the AUTOPLAY options panel. Clicking the OK button initiates the autoplay session. Clicking the CANCEL button closes the panel. You can end the autospins session earlier by clicking the AUTOPLAY button. The number of remaining spins is displayed on the AUTOPLAY button. TURBO MODE When TURBO Mode is on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster. Clicking the TURBO button switches Turbo Mode on or off. PAYTABLE PAYOUTS Winning combinations start from the leftmost reel and the symbols have to be consecutive along a payline. Regular wins are multiplied by the COIN BET. Only the highest win pays on each payline. Coinciding wins on different paylines are added. The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information see the TERMS AND CONDITIONS section. FREE GAMES During FREE GAMES the reels are spun automatically using the same COIN BET as per the spin that won the FREE GAMES. PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT The Progressive Jackpot receives a contribution from each bet made across the game. The Respin King game includes 5 Jackpots. Land 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9+ Crown symbols on reels 2, 3 and 4 during the Respin feature to activate the respective Jackpot. Progressive Jackpot status is saved per bet. Changing the bet will change the Progressive Jackpot pool. Returning to the previous bet amount restores the corresponding Jackpot pool. After a progressive jackpot is won, it is reset and begins to accumulate again. If a Player triggers a Jackpot immediately after another Player has triggered the same Jackpot, the Player who triggered it first will win the Jackpot, and the other Player will get the start-up amount plus any contributions made since the Jackpot was reset.
