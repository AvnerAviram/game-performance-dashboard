Clicking the AUTO button toggles between Auto ON and Auto OFF. When Auto ON, game will play for 99 spins or until player turns Auto OFF. --> TURBO Mode TURBO button is located on the Control Panel. Clicking the TURBO button toggles between Turbo ON and Turbo OFF. When Turbo Mode is on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster.
