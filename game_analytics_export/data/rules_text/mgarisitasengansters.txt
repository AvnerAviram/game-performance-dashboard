Risitas en Gansters MAIN GAME IN THIS GAME, EACH PRIZE COULD EITHER BE RISKED, BY PRESSING THE PLAY BUTTON, OR TO BE COLLECTED, BY PRESSING THE COLLECT BUTTON. IN CASE THE PRIZE IS RISKED, IT WILL EITHER BE GIVEN THE AMOUNT OF A ONE LEVEL HIGHER PRIZE OR THE EQUIVALENT AMOUNT OF BONUS. ONCE THE LINE OF THE THREE LEMONS IS REACHED, THE GAME BECOMES AN UP DOWN, WHERE THE POSSIBLE PRIZES GO UP OR DOWN IN THE PRIZE PLAN. RTP: 90% UPPER GAME IN ORDER TO PLAY THIS GAME IT WILL BE REQUIRED TO HAVE ENOUGH BONUS AND CREDITS. WHENEVER A PRIZE IS OBTAINED, UNLESS IT IS A BLUE BARS PRIZE, THE PLAYER WILL BE OFFERED TO EITHER RISK A PORTION OF THE PRIZE OR TO COLLECT IT, UNTIL THERE ARE NO MORE PORTIONS LEFT. CHOOSING TO RISK A PORTION MAY ALLOW THE PLAYER TO EITHER OBTAIN THE DOUBLE OF THAT PORTION OR THE EQUIVALENT VALUE OF THAT PORTION IN CUNAOS. NUDGES THE ADVANCE FUNCTION ALLOWS THE PLAYER TO MOVE REELS FORWARD ONE PLACE SEEKING A WINNING COMBINATION. THE NUMBER OF ADVANCES CAN BE BETWEEN 1 AND 4. HOLDS ALLOWS UP TO TWO REELS TO BE HELD UP DURING A GAME TO INCREASE THE PROBABILITY OF GETTING PRIZE. AUTO PLAY ACTIVATE THE AUTOMATIC GAME MODE. AUTO RISK ACTIVATE THE RISK FUNCTION IN MAIN AND UPPER GAME. A MINI-GAMES ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES. THE REFORM BONUS GAME 3 SACKS IN MAIN GAME GIVE ACCESS TO THE REFORM GAME. DRAW A PRIZE. SOMETIMES AFTER A PRIZE OF CARDS, CAR, OR STRONGBOX FIGURES, THE PLAYER WILL HAVE ACCESS TO THE LIFT WHERE WILL BE ABLE TO CHOOSE BETWEEN 5 DIFFERENT OPTIONS. CHOOSE THE MINIGAME THE PLAYER CAN SELECT THE MINIGAME PRESSING THE CORRESPONDING ICON. CASINO BONUS GAME SELECT A CARD. RESTAURANT BONUS GAME DRAW A PRIZE. BAR BONUS GAME SELECT DRINKS TO GET PRIZES. CLUB BONUS GAME OBTAIN THE BEST PRIZE COMBINATION. VAULT BONUS GAME SPIN THE ROULETTE. ADDITIONAL FEATURES VERTICAL LINES GET THE HAT FIGURES AND YOU CAN PLAY 8 LINES IN TOP GAME. BAR MIXED IN HIGH BETS YOU GET COMBINATIONS OF DIFFERENT BAR FIGURES AND GET A RANDOM PRIZE.
