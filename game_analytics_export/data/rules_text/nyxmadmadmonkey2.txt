Mad Mad Monkey II Mad Mad Monkey II™ Mad Mad Monkey II is a Bet 50, 50 Lines slot game. It has 5 reels, 4 rows, and the following features: RESPIN MONKEY MAD MONKEY LUCK During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The Theoretical Average Return to Player (RTP) is 94.22% Game Rules RULES Play bet 50 for 50 lines. Highest win only on each line. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Wins on different lines are added. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels. WILD WILD substitutes for all symbols except COIN. WILD appears on reel 2,3 and 4 only. All wins with WILD substituting are 2x. RESPIN SYMBOLS COIN symbol 6 or more COIN triggers the RESPIN feature. BANANA symbol BANANA upgrades all surrounding COIN symbols. Each BANANA increments BANANA Counter. RESPIN 6 or more COIN symbols trigger the RESPIN feature. 3 respins are awarded. All triggering COIN symbols are held, and other positions spun. During RESPIN, only COIN and BANANA symbols are active. Each BANANA is not held, and upgrades all surrounding COIN symbols by 0.2x, 0.4x or 1x the total bet. Each BANANA increments BANANA counter by one, to be used in the MONKEY MAD feature. Respins reset to 3 if new COIN or BANANA symbols appear. COIN prizes can be 0.4x, 1x, 1.4x, 2x, 2.4x, 5x the total bet, or a MINOR or MAJOR bonus. Any new COIN is held. Respins are completed when 0 respins remain, or if all reel positions are filled with COIN symbols. MINOR bonus is 10 times the total bet. MAJOR bonus is 25 times the total bet. GRAND bonus is 250 times the total bet. MINOR and MAJOR bonuses may be awarded more than once per RESPIN feature. GRAND bonus is awarded if all reel positions are filled with COIN symbols. GRAND bonus may be awarded only once per RESPIN feature. Paytable prizes are not awarded during RESPIN feature. Respins are played at the triggering bet. “Surrounding” means in any position 1 space to the left, right, above, below, and diagonal to the Banana symbol (up to 8 positions total) MONKEY MAD At the end of the RESPIN feature, if any BANANA symbols were collected, MONKEY MAD feature begins. Each BANANA may reveal a prize. BANANA prizes can be: Multiplier : a random COIN prize will be multiplied by 2x, 3x or 5x. New COIN : a COIN will appear on an empty reel position. New COIN prizes can be 3x, 5x of total bet, or MINOR or MAJOR bonus. If no BANANA symbols are collected during RESPIN feature, MONKEY MAD feature is skipped, and all COIN prizes are paid immediately. New COIN awarded during the MONKEY MAD feature does not restart the RESPIN feature. MONKEY LUCK During the base game, any COIN that lands may increase the Banana Tree meter. Any base game spin that does not trigger the RESPIN feature can trigger the Monkey Luck feature. When Monkey Luck feature is triggered, all line wins are paid. Then, Banana Tree meter empties, and up to 6 COIN symbols from the meter are placed in random positions on the reels. Emptying the Banana Tree meter triggers the RESPIN feature. The accumulation of COIN symbols in the Banana Tree meter has no effect on triggering the Monkey Luck feature. How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. The game displays all your details in the following display fields. Balance: Displays your total balance. Win: Displays the amount won in the spin. Total Bet: Displays the total wager staked. Banana Tree Meter: Displays accumulated COIN symbols. MAIN GAME BUTTONS: SPIN Spins the reels. The SPACE BAR can also be used to spin when playing on Desktop. STOP/SKIP During Base Game button will STOP the reel spin. During Free Games button will STOP the reel spin or SKIP to the next spin. SETTINGS View available sound settings and paytable buttons. CLOSE SETTINGS Close the Settings display. PAYTABLE View game rules and pay awards for winning symbol combinations. MUTE Mutes sound and ambient volumes. UNMUTE Unmutes sound and ambient volumes. ADJUST BET Total Bet menu to adjust bet. TOTAL BET MENU Click states from left to right: Lower the current Total Bet. Close the Total Bet Menu. Increase the current Total Bet. AUTOPLAY View and set AUTOPLAY options. STOP AUTOPLAY Displays the remaining amount of AUTOPLAY spins, click this button to STOP AUTOPLAY and return to normal play mode. TURBO Enables fast play where reels spin faster and stop earlier or as early as possible. TURBO can be turned off at any point during the game.
