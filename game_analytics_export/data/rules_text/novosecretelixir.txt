Secret Elixir It's mystical, secretive and fantastic! Secret Elixir™ will whisk you away to the world of the Alchemists! This intricately detailed game features gargoyles, wise owls, the philosopher's stone and of course, the alchemist himself! It's all about finding the magic formula which will turn your wager into gold, literally! Main Game The aim of the Secret Elixir™ is to get the same symbol showing on all 5 reels. In the regular game, winning combinations run left to right across the paylines. To land a win, a complete winning combination must occupy a payline, without any other symbols coming between the winning symbols. The 'Alchemist' symbol acts as a Joker and replaces all other symbols apart from the Scatter symbol ('Philosopher's Stone'). Multiplier Bonus Win Multipliers appear on reel 5 and multiply the respective line win by the value shown. Base Game: x2, x3, x5 Free Games: x2, x3, x5, x10, x20 Free Games If 3 or more Scatter symbols ('Philosopher's Stone') appear on your reels, 12 Free Games will be unlocked. Each win you make during the Free Games will be multiplied by a factor up to20! Top prize: 20000 times bet per line in a Free Game Feature hit rate: 170-180 games. Rules 'Alchemist' substitutes for all symbols except 'Philosopher's Stone'. All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All pays are for wins from left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by credits bet. Return to Player The theoretical return to player for this game is 96.02%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
