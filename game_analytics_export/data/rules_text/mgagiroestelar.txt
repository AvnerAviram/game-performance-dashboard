Giro Estelar PRIZES MAIN GAME 25 Prize Lines. Line winnings are paid from left to right. The Wild symbol (Astronaut) substitutes all symbols except the Scatter symbol and the “Lift-off” symbol The maximum prize is 1140 * bet SPECIAL RESPIN MINI-GAME To access the mini-game players need to get 6 or more “Lift-off” symbols in the main game or obtain the “Lift-off” symbol on the giant reel in Free Spins. Each “Lift-off” symbol has a prize associated with it. The reels will be replaced by special reels that only contain 'Lift-off' symbols and blank spaces. The 'Lift-off’ that appear on the screen will remain fixed in their position SPECIAL RESPIN MINI-GAME The game will award 3 respins and in each of them the symbols other than 'Lift-off' symbols will spin and may award another 'Lift-off' or nothing. Every time a 'Lift-off' is awarded the respin counter will reset to 3. The mini-game ends when 'Lift-off' symbols are obtained in all positions or the respin counter reaches 0. MINI - MAYOR - MEGA The 'Lift-off' symbols may have special prizes associated with them (MINI, MAJOR and MEGA). The value associated with each prize appears both in the main game and in Free Spins and varies depending on the bet. The prize can only be awarded during the Special Respin mini-game. FREE SPINS Obtaining 3 Scatter symbols gives 5 Free Spins. These spins do not consume balance and all the prizes obtained are accumulated. More Free Spins can be obtained within Free Spins. Reels 2, 3 and 4 spin together like a giant symbol. PRIZE LINES RTP = 95.76% Last modification date: 12/18/2023
