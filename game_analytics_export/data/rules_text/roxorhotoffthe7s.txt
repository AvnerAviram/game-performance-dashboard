Hot Off the 7s Game Features Bonus Reel Features Win 1 of 4 exciting features on any spin! Hit a win on the payline and simultaneously land a feature symbol on the bonus reel to trigger the corresponding feature. There are 4 available features: Win multipliers The line win will be multiplied by x2, x3, x5, x8, x10 or x18. Cash Wheel Spin the Cash Wheel for a cash prize of up to 2000 coins! RESPINS You are awarded a respin, with a chance to retrigger the feature again and again! Only the Respins symbol and blank can land on the bonus reel during the feature. Landing the Respins symbol awards you another respin. Landing a blank ends the feature. Each respin guarantees a win! The Respins feature uses the same bet as on the spin that triggered the feature. SUPER RESPINS You are awarded a super respin, with a chance to retrigger the feature again and again! The win multiplier starts from x2 and grows by 1 after each super respin. Only the Super Respins symbol and blank can land on the bonus reel during the feature. Landing the Super Respins symbol awards you another super respin. Landing a blank ends the feature. Each super respin guarantees a win! The Super Respins feature uses the same bet as on the spin that triggered the feature. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 96.07% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $96.07 of wins. Rules Total bet is 30 coins x coin size. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 4/10/2024
