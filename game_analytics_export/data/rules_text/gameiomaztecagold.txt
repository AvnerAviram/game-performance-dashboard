Azteca Gold This game has 10 winlines. All symbols pay left to right on consecutive reels starting from the leftmost reel. Only the highest win per line is paid. The pay values below are proportional to the stake per line. SYMBOLS 5 Jackpot symbols anywhere in view awards a JACKPOT of total bet x5,000! The JACKPOT cannot be won during FREE DROPS or the FORTUNE WHEEL BONUS. 5 - 20 FREE DROPS 4 - 12 FREE DROPS 3 - 10 FREE DROPS Each cascading free drop continues until there are no further wins on that free drop. 5 – 6 FREE FORTUNE WHEEL SPINS 4 – 4 FREE FORTUNE WHEEL SPINS 3 – 2 FREE FORTUNE WHEEL SPINS The maximum win for this bonus game is total bet x600. 5 – 1000 4 – 200 3 – 50 5 – 1000 4 – 200 3 – 50 5 – 500 4 – 100 3 – 25 5 – 320 4 – 80 3 – 20 5 – 240 4 – 60 3 – 15 5 – 120 4 – 30 3 – 10 5 – 100 4 – 25 3 – 8 5 – 80 4 – 20 3 – 5 5 – 60 4 – 15 3 – 5 Substitutes for all symbols except FORTUNE WHEEL BONUS 3 or more BONUS symbols anywhere in view awards the FORTUNE WHEEL BONUS game. For each FORTUNE WHEEL spin a multiplier is awarded. These are added together when no spins are left to give a final multiplier of the total bet. FREE DROPS FEATURE 3 or more SCATTER symbols anywhere in view awards the FREE DROPS BONUS game. FREE DROPS have cascading wins: When a win is awarded the winning symbols are removed and new symbols drop in to replace them for futher possible wins. For each free drop this continues until there are no further wins. The next free drop will then start. PAY LINES Malfunction voids pays and plays.
