X Wheel Chili X WHEEL CHILI™ GAME DESCRIPTION X WHEEL CHILI™ is a colorful game with Stacked Reels, Multiplier Wheel, Free Spins and Retriggers. It is dedicated to the culture of Mexico, so on the reels, you will see traditional musical instruments, residents in sombreros and chili peppers, unchanged for Mexican cuisine. It is equipped with 4 fixed Jackpots. “X WHEEL CHILI™”, has a 3x5 matrix layout, with fixed 30 lines. HOW TO PLAY Select your bet. Press the Spin button to start a game cycle. UI:- BUTTONS Title Icon Action Spin Initiates a reel spin. Spin Stop Stops the reel spin immediately (wherever applicable). Autoplay Displays the Autoplay settings screen to show all available options to initiate multiple spins consecutively : 10,25,50,100,200,500,1000 (wherever applicable). Autoplay Stop Press to stop Autoplay session manually (wherever applicable). Bet Displays the bet per line options for the player to select the level of their Bet. Turbo Spin Turn on/off to change the speed of the Spin to a faster/normal spin (wherever applicable). Gamble Start the gamble game - Press the GAMBLE button at a win. Collect Collect the win or go back to the base game. SETTING BUTTONS Title Icon Action Paytable Choose to view the Paytable and feature descriptions for the game. Bet Setting Displays the Bet per line options available for the player to select and change the level of their bet. Help Displays the detailed information about the game. METERS AND BUTTONS Bet – Shows the bet value of the current setting. Bet per line – Shows the current bet per line. Spin Button – Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Stop Button. Press the Stop Button to stop the reels immediately. Max – Displays the maximum bet. Autoplay (wherever applicable) – Player can set the game to spin without the need to press the spin button every time. You can choose the number of spins to play automatically by selecting through the various options available in the menu. The loss limit (wherever applicable) may avert you from losing above set limit during autoplay session. Autoplay can be manually stopped by pressing Stop Autoplay button. Win – Displays the win for the current or last win paid. Credits – Displays the credit amount of the player. All credit, bet and win meter would be shown in currency FREE GAMES Any 3 or more in a bought game trigger the FREE GAMES. FREE GAMES consists of 6 free games. Bonus reels containing only , , , , , , and are used during the FREE GAMES. During the FREE GAMES, any 3 or more award 3 additional free games. The prize displayed on any appearing in a free game is added to the BONUS PRIZE meter. If 1 or more appeared during a free game, at the end of the FREE GAMES the MULTIPLIER WHEEL is spun once to award a multiplier that is applied to the BONUS PRIZE total which is then won. If the FREE GAMES and COLLECT FEATURE are triggered in the same game, the FREE GAMES start after the COLLECT FEATURE ends. If the FREE GAMES and JACKPOT WHEEL are triggered in the same game, the FREE GAMES start after the JACKPOT WHEEL ends. All bets and lines played during the FREE GAMES are the same as the game that triggered the FREE GAMES. COLLECT FEATURE Any 6 or more scattered appearing in a bought game only, trigger the COLLECT FEATURE and are held for the duration of the COLLECT FEATURE. COLLECT FEATURE consists of 6 free spins. Different reels are used during the COLLECT FEATURE. Any new appearing during the COLLECT FEATURE is also held for the duration of the COLLECT FEATURE. After all free spins have been played or 15 are held, all prizes displayed on held are added to the BONUS PRIZE meter. 15 held add an ALL STACK AWARD of 1000 credits multiplied by the bet multiplier to the BONUS PRIZE meter. After all prizes for held have been added to the BONUS PRIZE meter, the MULTIPLIER WHEEL is spun once to award a multiplier that is applied to the BONUS PRIZE total which is then won. If 15 are held, any remaining free spins are not played. Any symbol stopping behind is invalid. If the COLLECT FEATURE and the JACKPOT WHEEL are triggered in the same game, the COLLECT FEATURE starts after the JACKPOT WHEEL ends. All bets during the COLLECT FEATURE are the same as the game that triggered the COLLECT FEATURE. BONUS PRIZE METER AND MULTIPLIER WHEEL The BONUS PRIZE meter and MULTIPLIER WHEEL are used during the FREE GAMES and the COLLECT FEATURE only. During the FREE GAMES and COLLECT FEATURE, the BONUS PRIZE meter is displayed at the center of the MULTIPLIER WHEEL. The MULTIPLIER WHEEL is spun once at the end of the FREE GAMES and COLLECT FEATURE to award a multiplier that applies to the BONUS PRIZE total. If no appears during the FREE GAMES, the MULTIPLIER WHEEL is not spun at the end of FREE GAMES. After the MULTIPLIER WHEEL is spun, 1 multiplier is awarded as indicated by the red pointer at the top of the wheel. The MULTIPLIER WHEEL contains the following multipliers: 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 8x, 10x, 12x, 15x and 20x. JACKPOT WHEEL There is a chance that the JACKPOT WHEEL may be triggered when 1 or more appear in bought games only. When the JACKPOT WHEEL is triggered, a wheel appears on the screen and is spun once only. After the wheel is spun, 1 jackpot is awarded as indicated by the red pointer at the top of the wheel. The JACKPOT WHEEL contains 4 JACKPOT LEVELS: GRAND, MAJOR, MINOR and MINI. TITLE VALUES GRAND 250000 MAJOR 50000 MINOR 2500 MINI 1000 (Currency unit and amount vary according to jurisdiction.) GAMBLE FEATURE To enter the gamble game after a win, Press the GAMBLE button. Select RED/BLACK or a SUIT to gamble the total win. Win is quadrupled if suit selection is correct. Win is doubled if red/black selection is correct. Gamble feature is not available for any feature win. Win may be gambled up to 5 times. Gamble will not be offered for main game wins part of feature trigger and feature wins. All wins cannot be gambled if the Jackpot is triggered. Gamble will not be offered during Autoplay. GAME RULES This game requires a minimum of 75 CREDITS to play. This game is intended for all play lines. All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel except scatters. All wins on win lines only except scatters. Wins on different lines are added. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter and Jackpot wins are added to line wins. Free Games wins are added to line wins. Only highest win is paid on each line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. All open game rounds (pending state) will be automatically settled after 30 days. LOBBY BAR Home: - Player can return to lobby by selecting this button. Sound: - Choose to enable/disable the game sounds. AUTOPLAY SETTINGS Number of spins: Select number of Auto spins as per available choice : 10,25,50,100,200,500,1000. Autoplay Advanced Settings (wherever applicable): Loss Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Win Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Single win limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Stop on any win: Stops on any wins. NET POSITION Net position (wherever applicable): The Net position will display customer’s net position, in the currency of their account or product (e.g., pounds sterling, dollar, Euro) since the session started. Net position is defined as the total of all winnings minus the sum of all losses since the start of the session. REALITY CHECK Reality check (wherever applicable): The Reality check feature may display as a periodic in-game notifications for the real money players. Game gets suspended whenever the check appears during game play and only resumes once the player has acknowledged the check and has taken displayed actions. “Back to game”:- Notifying player to resume the game. “To Lobby”:- Quits the current game (after a final prompt) and back to the Operators Main Game Menu Screen. MALFUNCTION In case of any malfunction of hardware or software during the game play, all current game bets and payouts are considered void and all bets affected are refunded.
