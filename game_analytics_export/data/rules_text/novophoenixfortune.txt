Phoenix Fortune Main Game The Wild symbols substitute for all other symbols to complete win lines, however, they will not substitute for each other. The Blue Phoenix Wild Medals appear only on reels 2 and 4, and the Red Phoenix Wild Medals appear only on Reel 3. The Red Phoenix Wild Medals on reel 3 will even double winnings when appearing in a substituting win. Gong symbols in any reel position (left to right) trigger the free games feature. There are no win lines in this game. That means: All winning combinations with at least 3 identical symbols landing from left to right generate winnings provided they start on reel 1. Free Games Feature During the 10 free game rounds the symbols Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10 and 9 are removed from the reels, which increases your winning chances. Further free games can be won during the free spins. Feature trigger: 3, 4 or 5 GONG symbols, left to right. Feature hit rate: 110-140 games. 4 Level Jackpot Feature The Red and Blue Wild symbols might randomly trigger the jackpot game. Then 12 Medallions appear on the screen which you can uncover one by one by clicking on them. If you come upon 3 matching Phoenix on the screen, you hit the respective jackpot and return to the main game. Win either a Grand Jackpot, Major Jackpot, Minor Jackpot or Mini Jackpot. The Grand Jackpot and Major Jackpot are progressive, and increase as the game is played until they are won. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Highest win only paid per combination on screen. Free Games are played at trigger bet. Free Games can be won again during the Free Games. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to player of this game is 95.00%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
