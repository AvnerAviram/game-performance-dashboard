Wins A winning bet occurs when the football goes through the goal posts causing it to light up. 3 to 18 credit prizes are awarded on a winning bet. Multiplier Feature The multiplier feature is awarded when the football turns gold. A random multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, or x7 is awarded. All credit prizes will be multiplied by the random multiplier, and then up to 10 credit prizes will be awarded.
