Master of Fortune Master of Fortune Game Rule By playing our games, you agree that you do not find them offensive, unfair or indecent. How to Play Slots: Choose the amount you would like to bet with. Control the bet amount by clicking the arrows next to the Coin Value or choose Max Bet (Max Coin Value). Your winnings are automatically added to your account balance. You can see your Total Win displayed in the WIN section of the screen after each spin. Game Rules: The game is played with 27 fixed paylines and each game costs a minimum of 27 fixed coins. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Payline prizes are multiplied by coin value All symbols pay from left to right in consecutive order, excluding the Bonus symbol. Highest win only paid per selected line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For this game, the long-term expected payback is 95.82%. Bonus Symbol: Landing 3 Bonus symbols anywhere on the reels in the main game awards 12 Free Spins. Bonus symbol wins are multiplied by the total cash bet. Bonus symbols cannot re-trigger during Free Spins. Free Spins: During Free Spins 1,2 or 3 Wild symbols will land on non-adjacent reels with each spin. Wild symbols can land on reels 1, 3 and 5 only, and all positions are equally probable. Wild symbols deliver a x3, x5 and x7 multiplier to a win combination depending on the number of Wilds that are present. Wild Symbol: Wild symbols substitute for all symbols, excluding the Bonus symbol. When Wild symbols land in a winning combination, they expand to cover the entire reel. Wild symbols deliver a x3 multiplier to all winning combinations. Bonus symbol wins are not affected by the multiplier. You can place restrictions on gameplay by following any of these options: Stop on any win: Auto-bet function is stopped when you get any winning combination. Stop on single win limit: Based on the limit value selected, the Auto-bet function is stopped when your total win in a single game reaches the selected value. Stop on session loss limit: Based on the limit value selected, the Auto-bet function is stopped when your total loss in the session reaches the selected value, which is derived by bet minus wins. All games are tested to level 3 standards in accordance with the UKGC Testing Strategy for Compliance with the Remote Gambling and Software Technical Standards. If the game is interrupted due to connection loss during Free Spins or Bonus Rounds, the game will resume the remaining Free Spins or the pending Bonus Round upon re-opening. Auto Spin Game Rules: Auto Spin is similar to regular spins; you can choose Auto Spin for a selected number of spins. As soon as Auto Spin begins, the Auto Spin button is replaced by a counter displaying the remaining Auto-bet rounds. The Auto Spin button changes to the Stop Auto Spin icon. Auto Spin automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. Auto Spin can be stopped at any time by clicking the Auto Spin (Auto Stop) button. Auto Spin settings will return to default when you get any feature round (Free Spins/Bonus Round). If you are disconnected while playing, all Auto-play setting will return to default when you reload the game. Hence the actual payouts will be coin value * paytable factor when bet lines are kept constant.
