Spin Bonanza Spin Bonanza Spin Bonanza features a thrilling slot experience with a 3x5 reel configuration and 243 ways to win. The game includes multipliers that enhance wins by being counted as multiple matching symbols. Landing three Wheel Symbols activates a bonus wheel, awarding exciting free games or a jackpot prize. Retriggers during free games enhance the thrill, making each spin a high-stakes adventure. GAME FEATURES Multiplier Feature 2X Symbol and 3X Symbol appear on reels 2, 3, and 4, and are wild for all symbols except the Wheel Symbol. When a 2X Symbol appears on a reel in a win, it is considered as 2 of any matching symbols in that win. When a 3X Symbol appears on a reel in a win, it is considered as 3 of any matching symbols in that win. Wheel Feature 3 Wheel Symbols in any position during base game play awards 1 Wheel Spin. 4 Wheel Symbols in any position during base game play awards 2 Wheel Spin. 5 Wheel Symbols in any position during base game play awards 3 Wheel Spin. The player spins the wheel and is awarded either Free Games or Jackpot Prize. During Free Games, the player can retrigger additional wheel spins: 2 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 1 Wheel Spin. 3 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 3 Wheel Spins. 4 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 4 Wheel Spins. 5 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 5 Wheel Spins. The odds of the wheel bonus prizes are not equal in value.
