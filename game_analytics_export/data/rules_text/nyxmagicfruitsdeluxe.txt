Magic Fruits Deluxe All wins are paid for combinations of the same symbol on active line. Each new game costs one stake. There are 9 symbols in the game and the 4 lowest symbols may include an additional JOKER marker. The arrangement of 3 symbols with JOKER tags in the winning line is the best paid system. Symbols with JOKER does not substitute for any other symbol. GAMBLE FEATURE The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the Current win counter and returns to the main game. The gamble feature has seven rounds. Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess. BIG SCREEN MODE Play Button Click to start playing at the current bet level.
