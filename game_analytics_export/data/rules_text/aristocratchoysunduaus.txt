Choy Sun Doa GAME RULES All wins shown in credits. 20 lines are played for line cost of 20 credits. Choose your TOTAL BET. Minimum bet 20 credits. TOTAL BET is the line cost multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. SELECT +,- OR MAX TO CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. The PLAY button will immediately commence play. While the reels are spinning you may press the PLAY button to stop them. Touch CREDIT, TOTAL BET or WIN meter to show in currency or credits. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Highest win paid per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Scatter wins and random prize wins are added to REEL POWER™ wins. REEL POWER™ wins multiplied by the Bet Mutiplier. Game R MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE TO CONFIRM CREDITS REGISTERED BEFORE GAME START.
