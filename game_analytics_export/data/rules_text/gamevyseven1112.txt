7 11 21 Rules 7 11 21 is a number game scratch with 3 chances to win. Select coins to reveal numbers – if the three coins selected add up to 7, 11 or 21, you will win the prize revealed. Set Your Stake The stake value is shown on the ‘play button’. You will also see it appear in the bottom panel as ‘Bet’. Start The Game To start the game, tap the ‘Play’ button. This will then change to a Reveal All button. You can choose to reveal the coins by touching them, swiping several at once or press the Reveal All button to do it automatically. You can also tap each of the mini game icons to automatically select three coins for that game. To Play Choose your coins, which will reveal the numbers (from 0-7 and 11) to be added together. If you hit the lucky numbers 7, 11 or 21 you will win the prize revealed. In the event of a win (once the card is complete), the total win value from all 3 games will be awarded and added to your account balance. If there is no win, the button will change to ‘Play’ again for you to buy another card. Tapping the ‘Play’ button will reset the card for a new game.
