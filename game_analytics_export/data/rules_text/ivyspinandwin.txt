Spin And Win Game Objective: Spin And Win is a 5x3 reels 10 fixed paylines slot game with TheBigOne jackpot. There are only four symbols: two that pay, a blank, and a wheel scatter symbol. 3 or more Wheel symbols appear anywhere on the Reels triggers a Wheel Feature round. Game Information: Game is played with 10 fixed paylines. SPIN can be triggered by clicking on the spin button with the current selected BET and LINES. Payouts are displayed on the PAYTABLE. Whenever there is a win, the total BALANCE will update, and the amount won in the spin is shown in the WIN meter at the bottom of the game. The game and game rules are identical in both the real-money and play-money modes, except that autospin option will not be available in play-money mode. The Big One jackpot: The Big One jackpot consists of five jackpots at different levels; the levels are - from the lowest to the highest – the Blitz Cash Jackpot, the Quick Cash Jackpot, the Super Cash Jackpot, the Mega Cash Jackpot and the Colossal Cash Jackpot. All five jackpots grow progressively as bets are placed. For each spin, the house adds a small credit to each of the five running jackpots. All five levels in The Big One jackpot can be won irrespective of hitting any winning combination, while betting any amount. Jackpot is triggered randomly. The larger the wager, the higher the chances to win a jackpot. Free spins cannot trigger jackpots. If there is a jackpot which has triggered during the main game which also triggers Free spins, then the jackpot feature is played and shown after the Free spins feature play. How to play: Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet is set. You can change the bet per win by clicking the or buttons available on either side of the BET meter. LINES: Ten lines are selected by default. Lines are fixed and lines count cannot be changed. The Total bet is 10 times the bet per line and is shown near BET in the lower left of the screen. MAX BET: Click on MAX BET to select the maximum possible bet. If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled. Paytable: The Paytable can be viewed by clicking button located at the bottom of the screen beside Lines. The prizes displayed in the Paytable are per unit bet, and this game has dynamic Paytable and payouts shown in the Paytable are multiplied with the bet. You can navigate to all screens of Paytable by clicking on and buttons. You can go back to the game by clicking on the button on the Paytable screen. Spin: The SPIN button in the lower right corner starts the game. Volume: icon will play the game music. Click on icon will mute the volume . Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. ¬To change your bet, increase or decrease the Bet shown on the game screen. Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen. Game Settings : Volume: Player can set the volume of the sound effects and ambience by sliding through the Volume button. Sound effects: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game win animation sounds. Ambience sound: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game background sound. Spacebar to spin: By checking this option, Spacebar key can be used to trigger a spin. Game Rules: Spin And Win is a 5 reel slot game with 10 paylines. All payouts are made according to the Paytable. Paytable will always show the win amount for the currently selected bet on 10 fixed paylines. MAX BET selects 10 lines and maximum bet value. Only the highest win on each selected payline is paid. Coinciding wins on different paylines are added to your total balance. Bar and Cherry symbols pay when 3 or more identical symbols appear adjacent to one another on selected paylines. Wheel and O symbols do not have any payouts. When 3 or more Wheel symbols appear anywhere on the Reels triggers a Wheel Feature round. All jackpot wins are credited to your account immediately. Theoretical return to player is 93.19%. Jackpot Contribution RTP is 3.004%. Feature Game Rules: Wheel Feature round is triggered when three or more Wheel symbols appear anywhere on the reels. Player needs to click on Spin button in order to spin the wheel. Player will be awarded with one spin for each wheel symbol appeared on Reels. The number on which wheel stops is the prize multiplier which will be multiplied by the triggering bet. Player will be awarded with any of these multipliers randomly. X1, X2, X3, X4, X5, X7, X10, X15, X20, X25, X50, X200.Wheel Bank counter will be displayed on top of the reels during the Feature game, which shows the cumulative total of the feature game win amounts. Jackpot game: The Big One jackpot can be triggered anytime, either during normal spin or after the feature game. The main screen then changes to jackpot screen after the user has won jackpot game in which all the jackpots and their values are listed on the right-side area and a wheel is displayed on the left side area. There are 5 different jackpots, each of which has a unique color. All five colors are shown on the wheel: The orange color represents the Blitz Cash Jackpot. The red color represents the Quick Cash Jackpot. The blue color represents the Super Cash Jackpot. The green color represents the Mega Cash Jackpot. The lavender color represents the Colossal Cash Jackpot. The spin button is shown in the middle of the bottom area. By clicking on the SPIN button, the wheel rotates and stops on a color and the respective jackpot is awarded. You can see the current values of all five jackpots on the main game screen.
