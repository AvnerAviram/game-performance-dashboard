Odin Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 95.84%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Rules ODIN is a 5-reel 3-row game with 20 fixed win lines. Stakes range from €0.10 to €10.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. Each will be replaced by one and only one symbol. In addition to the cash winnings from the pay table, free games can be won with 3x . All wins and winning combinations are shown in the pay table. WILDCARD Symbol The wildcard symbol substitutes any symbol to create the highest win on a win line. Scatter Symbol The scatter symbol is exempt from the win line rule. ’s can win independently of their position on the reels; only the number of symbols counts for a win. Free Games Free games can be won with 3x . Stakes do not have to be paid for the free games. Your effective bet for these free games is your actual bet when you won the free games.
