Chili Poppers WIN LINES All games are played with 10 win lines. Wins can occur across any or all of the win lines. Only the highest award is paid per winning combination. Wins on different played lines are added. All wins pay left to right across adjacent reels. Starting with the leftmost reel. The Bonus win is added to any coincidental line wins. WILD SYMBOL This symbol is WILD and substitues for all symbols except coins and the Mini, Minor, Major and Grand fixed prizes as part of a line win. It appears on the centre reel in the base game and the Free Spin Bonus. It is locked in place on the centre reel for the duration of the Free Spin Bonus. CHILI POT BONUSES There are 3 pots above the reels. When the WILD Symbol is on the centre reel, it may activate one or more of them. The size of the pots does not affect the activation chance. Left Pot - Coin Boost Middle Pot - Six Free Spins Right Pot - Coin Multiply Coin Boost and Coin Multiply can only be activated if there is at least one Coin Symbol on the reels. Fixed Prizes and Line Wins are not boosted or multiplied. In the Base Game, Coin Boost and Coin Multiply are only available during the game that activated them and in any Free Spins activated within that Base Game. If Coin Boost or Coin Multiply are activated during Free Spins, they remain active for the duration of Free Spins. COINS These Can Appear On All Reels Except The Centre Reel In The Base Game And The Free Spins Bonus. They Are Only Awarded If The Wild Symbol Lands On The Centre Reel. Coins Will Have A Monetary Value On Them. These Values Are Proportionate To The Base Game Bet. Please Consult In-Game Help Page For The Possible Initial Values. The Value May Be Boosted Or Multiplied If The Relevant Bonus Is Activated. Coins Are First Boosted And Then Multiplied If Both Bonuses Are Active. Please Consult In-Game Help Pages For The Range Of Boost Values. Coin Multiply Amounts Range From 2 To 25. FIXED PRIZES These Can Appear On All Reels Except The Centre Reel In The Base Game And The Free Spins Bonus. They Are Only Awarded If The Wild Symbol Lands On The Centre Reel. Please Consult In-Game Help Page For The Values Of Each Prize. NUDGE UP MODIFIER After A Reel Spin, If There Is No Wild Symbol On The Centre Reel And No Line Win, Then The Centre Reel May Step Backwards Until A Wild Symbol Is Revealed. This Can Only Happen In The Base Game. In The Base Game And The Bonus Game If There Is No Line Win And There Is A Wild On The Centre Reel, From Either A Reel Spin Or A Nudge, Then One Or More Of The Other Reels May Step Backwards Until A Fixed Prize Is Revealed. One Or More Of These Modifiers May Be Activated If These Criteria Are Met. Any Coins Or Fixed Prizes, Already In View, Will Be Awarded As Normal. FREE SPINS BONUS When The Middle Pot Is Activated By The Wild On The Centre Reel, 6 Free Spins Are Awarded. Any Coin Boost Or Coin Multiply Activated At The Same Time Will Persist Throughout The Free Spins Bonus. The Wild Symbol Is Fixed In Position On The Middle Reel For The Duration Of Free Spins. During Free Spins The Wild Symbol May Activate One Or More Of Coin Boost, Free Spins And Coin Multiply. If Free Spins Are Activated During This Bonus, Another 6 Free Spins Are Awarded. If Coin Boost Or Coin Multiply Are Awarded During Free Spins, They Remain Active Until The End Of The Free Spins Bonus. FORTUNE BET Fortune Bet Is An Alternative To Standard Spins. Activating Fortune Bet Allows The Player To Select A New Bet Multiplier Of 1.5x The Current Bet. Doing So Increases The Frequency Of The Free Spins Bonus And Increases The Average Win From The Free Spins Bonus. The Awards And Big Win Thresholds Remain The Same As The Base Bet. The Average Payback Over Multiple Game Cycles In Fortune Bet Mode Is 94.50%. BONUS BUY (If Available) Bonus Buy Allows The Player To Purchase Direct Entry To The Free Spins Bonus And Choose If The Bonus Starts With Coin Boost Or Coin Multiply Active, Or Both. When The Bonus Buy Button Is Pressed, The Player Is Presented With 4 Options: 6 Free Spins 6 Free Spins With Coin Boost Active 6 Free Spins With Coin Multiply Active 6 Free Spins With Coin Boost And Coin Multiply Active The Base Bet Can Also Be Changed And Changing This Alters The Cost Of Each Option. All Awards Are Determined By The Current Bet. The Average Payback Over Multiple Game Cycles In Bonus Buy Mode Is 94.50%. MAIN GAME CONTROLS SPIN Press to start playing at the current bet value. BET Pressing once will open bet window for you to adjust your bet amount. AUTOPLAY (If available) In the Autoplay options panel, select the number of autoplay and loss limit amounts. Autoplay countdown is automatic. FORTUNE BET(If available) Press to enable Fortune Bet mode on/off. SETTINGS Press to access game settings and game information. GAME HISTORY Press yo see your play history. SOUND Press to enable sound on/off. TURBO Speeds up reel spins. Press to enable Turbo mode on/off. SPACE BAR The SPACE BAR key on the keyboard can often be used as an alternative for pressing the primary on-screen button. SLAM STOP (If available) During a regular spin, pressing anywhere on the screen whilst the reels are spinning will cause them to land quicker. THis does not impace the outcome of the game. GENERAL INFORMATION GAME RECORVERY In the event of an unexpected interruption/termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. PAYBACK INFORMATION The player's odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) for this game is 94.50%. The outcome of any play during the game is pre-determined. Player choices do not affect the outcome of the game unless indicated. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
