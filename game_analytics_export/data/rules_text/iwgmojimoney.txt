Moji Money Introduction Pick 8 mojis from a choice of 40. 10 WINNING MOJIS are then drawn. Match 3 or more to win a prize! Start the game Select the stake amount, then to start the game press the PLAY button. Once the PLAY button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game, any winnings are displayed. To play again press the PLAY AGAIN button. Once the PLAY AGAIN button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance and the game starts. How to play At the start of the game 8 different random YOUR MOJIS are displayed to the player on the panel in the middle of the screen. These can be changed by pressing the CHANGE MOJIS button on the left. When changing mojis the player picks 8 from a choice of 40. Once PLAY is pressed, the game starts and 10 WINNING MOJIS are drawn and display along the top pf the screen. If any WINNING MOJIS match any of YOUR MOJIS then the moji highlight, and this counts as 1 match on the prize table. Pressing QUICK PLAY during the game will speed up the rate at which the WINNING MOJIS are drawn. Winning Wins are achieved by: Matching 3 or more of YOUR MOJIS symbols with the WINNING MOJIS symbol. General information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by player do not affect the outcome of the game.
