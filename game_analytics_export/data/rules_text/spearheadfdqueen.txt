Fortune Dragon Queen Fortune Dragon Queen - Exotic Wilds Fortune Dragon Queen - Exotic Wilds is a 5 reel by 3 row, 243 ‘ways pays’ videoslot featuring Exotic Wilds frequently occurring and rewarding different multipliers each spin as well as a chance at the prizepot bonus and the fortune collector. There are three different Exotic Wilds in this game, [Dragon], [Queen], and [Dragon Queen]. At the end of each spin, every [Queen] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel and have a random multiplier assigned from 3x to 7x. At the end of each spin, every [Dragon] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel and have a random multiplier assigned from 2x to 5x. At the end of each spin, any reel that contains both a [Queen] and [Dragon] that lands on the reels will expand the entire reel as a [Dragon Queen] and have a random multiplier assigned from 5x to 50x. If exotic wilds land on multiple reels during the same spin, wins that include multiple Exotic Wilds take the sum of all the Exotic Multipliers included in the spin. [Queen] Substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Dragon], and [Dragon Queen]. [Dragon] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Queen], and [Dragon Queen]. [Dragon Queen] substitute for all symbols except for [Scatter], [Queen], and [Dragon]. Free Spins and choose your volatility options Landing 3 or more [Scatter] during the base game triggers the free spins Choose 1 of 3 Free Spin options for Medium to Extreme Volatility Multiplier Boost, Extra Exotic, Extreme XXX The Multiplier Boost option awards 12 Initial Free Spins with Medium Volatility 3 [Scatter] Award an additional 12 Free Spins. Each [Special symbol] awards an extra free spin and increases the multiplier range of all Exotic Wild types from 1 to 3. An increase range is done by adding the random amount from 1 to 3 to all the multipliers. For example, if the multiplier range of the Exotic Wilds is increased by 3, then the range of [Dragon] increases from 2x to 5x to a new range of 5x to 10x, the range of [Queen] increases from 3x to 7x to 6x to 10x, and the range of [Dragon Queen] wilds are increases from a range of 5x to 50x to 8x to 53x. The increased range of the Exotic Wilds apply to all wilds for all remaining free spins, including the spin that the [Special Symbol] has landed. As more [Special Symbol] land during free spins the Exotic Wild multipliers will continue to increase further. The Extra Exotic option awards 10 Initial Free Spins with High Volatility 3 [Scatter] Award an additional 10 Free Spins. Collect [Queen Tokens] to convert more symbols to [Queen] wilds Collecting 3 [Queen Tokens] will convert all [SH1] Symbols into [Queen] Collecting 3 more [Queen Tokens] will convert all [H1] Symbols into [Queen] Collecting 3 more [Queen Symbol] will convert all [H2] Symbols into [Queen] Collect [Dragon Tokens] to convert more symbols to [Dragon] wilds Collecting 3 more [Dragon Tokens] will convert all [H3] Symbols into [Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Dragon Tokens] will convert all [H4] Symbols into [Dragon] Collecting 3 more [Dragon Tokens] will convert all [H5] Symbols into [Dragon] The Extreme XXX option awards 8 Initial Free Spins with Extreme Volatility 3 [Scatter] award an additional 8 Free Spins. [Queen] during the Extreme XXX free spins option always pay from 5x to 10x. [Dragon] during the Extreme XXX free spins option always pay from 7x to 20x. [Dragon Queen] during the Extreme XXX free spins option always pay from 10x to 100x. Furthermore, landing multiple exotic wilds on the same spin have compounded win potential as the total win multiplier is calculated by taking the product of all multipliers included in the win together rather than the sum. Every time at least one [Queen], [Dragon], or [Dragon Queen] lands on the reels, in addition to the multiplier and prizepots award feature, there is a chance that the prizepot bonus will trigger. An animation is shown to show the chance of triggering, and if randomly triggered the game will transition to the prizepot pick bonus. The prizepot pick bonus can only be triggered in the base game. During the prizepot pick bonus, 15 available picks will reveal the mini, minor, major, mega, or grand prizepots as pick choices are made. Picks are made until 3 matching prizepots reveal which will end the bonus and award the prizepot that has 3 reveals. Fortune Collectors Special Credit symbols show prize amounts visible when landing on the reels. In addition to the multiplier ranges on the multiplier wheel, a fortune collector can also land which will award the player the sum of all special credit symbol prize amounts on the reels, including any prizepots. After the credit prizes and/or prizepots are awarded, the multiplier wheel will respin.
