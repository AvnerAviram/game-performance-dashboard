Cash Gusher Game Rules Cash Gusher™ is a 3-reel, classic stepper online slot game played with 27 Ways featuring the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature. Base Game RTP is 96.12%. All RTPs represent the long term theoretical payback. Minimum bet: 0,50$. Maximum bet: 375,00$. The game is always played with 27 ways. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. All wins pay left to right on adjacent reels beginning with the leftmost reel. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Highest win only for each winning combination. Wins on different combinations are added. Wins from Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature are added to base game wins, if any. All wins shown in currency. Animations in the background are visual only and do not represent increased chances of winning. Paytable win values are multiplied by the currently selected bet. Game outcome is random and cannot be influenced by the actions of the player. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. WILD SYMBOLS There are two types of WILD symbols: Teal WILD - does not have a multiplier. Gold WILD - multiplies wins for combinations that it’s a part of, by a randomly drawn multiplier. WILD symbols substitute for all non-WILD symbols except for RED, GREEN and BLUE DERRICK SCATTER SYMBOLS. All WILD symbols appear only on the second reel both in base game and Cash Gusher Bonus Spin Feature. If a Gold WILD lands as part of a winning combination in the base game, one multiplier is randomly selected from 2×, 5×, 10×, 25×, 50×, or 75×. Once the multiplier is drawn, its value appears on the Gold WILD symbol and is applied to the payout of the winning payway in which the Gold WILD participates. If a winning reel combination includes both ways with a Gold WILD and ways without a Gold WILD, they are presented as a single win. Prizes are combined and displayed as a total sum. CASH GUSHER BONUS SPINS FEATURE Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature consists of 7 initial Bonus Spins with additional features depending on the triggering SCATTER. Landing one or more SCATTER symbols can randomly trigger the feature with different modifications. However, getting a SCATTER does not guarantee the feature will activate. All types of SCATTER symbols can appear on any reel during the base game only. Oil DERRICKS presented above the game reels do not represent actual chances of hitting the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature. Depending on the reel combination, the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature can include one to three additional modifications. For example, if the feature is triggered by the BLUE DERRICK SCATTER with no other SCATTER on the reels, the player receives extra spins added to the 7 initial Bonus Spins. If the feature is triggered by a combination of the BLUE and RED SCATTER, the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature game will include both, extra spins and play across three separate reel sets. An individual alternate set of reels is used during Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature. The feature ends when there are no Bonus Spins remaining. At the end of the feature, all wins accumulated during the Bonus Spins are summed up and displayed as the total feature win, which is then added to any base game win. It is not possible to land two or more SCATTERS of the same type during single spin in base game. SCATTERS appear in the base game only. EXTRA SPINS Landing the BLUE DERRICK SCATTER on any reel during the base game may increase the number of Extra Spins awarded in the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature by 1. The Extra Spins number starts at a base value of 5 and can increase up to a maximum of 10. The current amount of Extra Spins is continuously displayed on the BLUE DERRICK meter above the reels. When the BLUE DERRICK SCATTER triggers the Cash Gusher Bonus Spin Feature +1 Extra Spin is added, if possible. When the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature that includes Extra Spins starts, the Extra Spins are added to the initial 7 spins. After the round ends, the Extra Spins meter is reset to the base value of 5. Moreover, each distinct bet level maintains its own spins at one bet does not affect the number at another. TRIPLE REELS When the RED DERRICK SCATTER triggers the Cash Gusher Bonus Spin Feature, the feature is played on 3 separate sets of reels. GIANT MULTIPLIERS When the GREEN DERRICK SCATTER triggers the Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature, the feature includes Giant Multipliers. During this feature, two types of WILD symbols may appear: Teal WILD, that does not have a multiplier Gold WILD, whose multiplier is randomly drawn from the pool: 2x, 5x, 10x, 25x, 50x, 75x, 100x, 150x, or 200x. The last three multipliers (100x, 150x, and 200x) are displayed in green. If a WILD symbol is part of a winning combination within the 27 ways, it multiplies the prize by its assigned or drawn value. When the triggered Cash Gusher Bonus Spins Feature does not include Giant Multipliers, the gold WILD draws its multiplier from the same pool as in the base game: 2x, 5x, 10x, 25x, 50x, 75x.
