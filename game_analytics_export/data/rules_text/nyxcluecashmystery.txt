Clue Cash Mystery How to play Winlines This game contains 20 win lines. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far-left reel. Wins on multiple lines are added together. Wild The Wild symbol substitutes for all other paying symbols in both Base Game and Free Spins. Wild symbols can appear on all reels. Scatters The game contains 17 scatter symbols of 3 different types. Scatter symbols only appear in reels 1, 3 and 5 in the Base Game. Landing 3 different types of scatter symbols awards Free Spins. Scatters - Weapon There are 5 different Weapon scatters that can appear in reel 1. The scatter that lands determines the multiplier that will affect all wins during Free Spins. The possible weapons and the multiplier they award are as follows: Lead Pipe – 2x Spanner – 3x Candlestick – 4x Dagger – 5x Revolver - 6x Scatters – Room There are 6 different Room scatters that can appear in reel 3. The scatter that lands determines the number of Free Spins awarded. The possible rooms and the number of Free Spins they award are as follows: Lounge – 5 spins Kitchen – 8 spins Study –10 spins Hall – 12 spins Conservatory – 15 spins Library – 25 spins Scatters – Character There are 6 different Character scatters that can appear in reel 5. This determines the Free Spins modifier. The possible characters and their modifiers are as follows: Mrs Peacock – Infinite Retriggers Colonel Mustard – Expanding Wilds Professor Plum – Multiplier Wilds Reverend Green – Growing Symbols Dr. Orchid – Both Directions Pay Miss Scarlett – Persistent Wilds Free Spins All Free Spins will be played with the same total bet as the spin that triggered the Feature. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. Free Spins will end when the maximum win has been achieved.
