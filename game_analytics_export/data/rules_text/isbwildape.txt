How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. For the reels set on the left, all winning ways pay from left to right. For the reels set on the right, all winning ways pay from right to left. For the Extended Wins feature, winning ways pay from left to right or from right to left. During Free Spins, winnings are paid from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. Note: The number of adjacent winning symbols (ways) is calculated by multiplying the number of winning symbols on each reel. For example, if there is 1 winning symbol on reel 1, 1 winning symbol on reel 2 and 2 winning symbols on reel 3, then the number of adjacent winning symbols is 1 x 1 x 2 = 2. In this case, the number of reels involved in the win is 3 – in the pay table, you can view the gain for 3 reels for the winning symbol. During normal spins, for each reel set, only payouts for up to 3 reels apply. During Free Spins and for the Extended Wild feature, payouts for up to 6 reels apply. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Bonus symbol, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2. Wild Ape Respins When 3 consecutive reels land stacked Gorilla symbols, they will become a giant symbol and will trigger Wild Ape Respins. At the beginning of the Wild Ape Respins the Wild Ape will smash to extend the reels and will begin the 3 Respins. Note: During the Wild Ape Respins all the Gorilla symbols will become Wild symbols. During the Wild Ape Respins the number of win lines is doubled (40). 3.3. Bonus When a Bonus symbol lands on reels 1, 3 and 5 it will trigger the Bonus Wheel. Note: During the wheel spin you can win up to 20 free spins with up to 50 added Wilds. Press the Spin button to spin the wheel to win Free Spins and Added Wilds . 3.4. Free Spins After being awarded the Free Spins from the Bonus Wheel the extended 4x5 reel set will appear, and the Free Spins will start with the added Wilds. Note: During the Free Spins the number of win lines is doubled (40). Free Spins can't be re-triggered. 4.
