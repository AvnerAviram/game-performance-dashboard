Eye of the Queen Game Theme and Graphics Eye of the Queen is an Egyptian themed slot from 707 Games / Novomatic. The symbols range from Egyptian hieroglyphs to scarabs, and other classical egyptian themed symbols and decorations in this slot. The main feature is the Free Games feature with expanding wilds and symbol upgrades Main Game Eye of the Queen is quite a simple and straightforward game. The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and 10 winlines and you'll find special features like expanding wilds and a Free Games feature that will award you with 12 free spins and exciting symbol upgrades. Free Games Feature Trigger 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols trigger 12 Free Games. Description Bet staked and lines played are the same as in the game that started the feature. The feature can be retriggered.
