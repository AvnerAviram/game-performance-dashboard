Oktoberfest GAME RULES • 5 reels and 3 rows video slot with 15 symbols • 20 bet lines (see paytable for more info) • Wild symbols substitutes for any other symbol except the scatter • Coinciding wins on multiple betlines are paid out • Winning combinations pays from left to right • Winning combinations and pays are made according to the pay table • The highest win per activated bet line is paid, multiplied by bet per line • The maximum winning from any spin in this game is set to: € 50 000 • Depending on game mode (main game or any of the bonus modes) different reel configurations are used • 3 scatters in main game awards 10 Bierspins • No scatters appear on the reels during the Prost Spins mode, Pretzel Party or the Party Spins • Scatters acts as collectibles in the Bierspins and awards additional spins and increased multiplier according to the meter • Three collected scatters in Bierspins awards a new level on the meter. Each level will award a higher win multiplier and three additional free spins • Bierspins always starts on level 1 • Level 1: x1 multiplier • Level 2: x2 multiplier • Level 3: x3 multiplier • Level 4: x4 multiplier • Level 5: x5 multiplier • Each new level awards +3 additional free spins • Collectible scatters in Bierspins can award up to 5x multiplier • Collectible scatters in Bierspins can award up to 12 additional free spins • Landing fully stacked wilds on reel 2 and reel 4 awards Prost Spins feature • Prost Spins feature awards two additional re-spins with guaranteed wins with a wild on reel 3. This wild moves down one row before each respin • Party Spins can be triggered on any spin in main game and will have one super-stacked symbol during that spin • Pretzel Party can be triggered on any spin in main game and will turn all Pretzel symbols into wilds • The theoretical return to player for this game is 96.75% • The simulated maximum payout is 500 times the bet (10.000.000.000 rounds). • Malfunction voids all winnings • Malfunction in gaming hardware/software; all affected bets are refunded • All game states will be saved for 6 months. • When playing with Cryptocurrency, unfinished game rounds and states will automatically reset after 24 hours.
