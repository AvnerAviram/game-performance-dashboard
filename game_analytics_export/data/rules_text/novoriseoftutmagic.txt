The symbols range from Egyptian hieroglyphs to scarabs, and other classical egyptian themed symbols and decorations. The main feature is the Free Games Feature with expanding wilds and symbol upgrades. Main Game The aim in Rise of Tut™ Magic is to line up 5 matching symbols along one of the 10 win lines that all run from left to right. A minimum combination of three matching symbols on a line starting on the leftmost reel pays out. 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols trigger 12 Free Games with a symbol upgrade feature. Feature Trigger : 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols trigger 12 Free Games. Description: Free Games are played using the same bet as the spin which triggered it. Du ring the feature, different reel strips are used. At the start of the feature, a random number (0-5) of picture symbol upgrades is awarded. The feature can be retriggered. WILD Retrigger Bonus: 3, 2 or 1 scattered WILD symbols trigger 5, 3 or 1 additional Free Games respectively and upgrade picture symbols, which stay upgraded during the remaining games of the Free Game Feature. Top prize: 322 × total bet in a single game (10 lines played) Feature hit rate: Approx. 120 - 140 games. Rules WILD substitutes for all symbols, except Scatters. All pays are for wins of left to right except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding Scatters. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. PAYTABLE All prizes are for one credit bet per line.
