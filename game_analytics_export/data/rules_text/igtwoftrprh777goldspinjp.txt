How to Bet Wheel of Fortune Gold Spin Triple Red Hot 7s features 5 paylines. Bet Settings Menu Press the cog or TOTAL BET to open the Bet Settings menu. Total Bet Displays the current number of paylines, bonus eligibility, and total bet shown in currency. Change by selecting the desired total bet. On desktop devices, optionally press the minus button (–) to decrease the total bet or press the plus button (+) to increase the total bet. Press OK to confirm and close the Bet Settings menu. Spin Press the circular arrow to submit the bet and spin the reels. Auto Spin Feature Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of spins at the current total bet and number of paylines selected. For jurisdictions where Auto Spin is enabled: Select the number of auto spins and where available, the loss limit, and the (optional) single win limit. After selecting the desired bet configuration, press AUTO SPIN , and choose how many spins to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until STOP is pressed, a bonus is triggered, the balance is insufficient to spin, or any enabled win/loss limit settings are reached. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions. Multiplier Feature If 1 Triple Red Hot 3x Symbol appears in a line win, that win is multiplied by 3. If 2 Triple Red Hot 3x Symbols appear in a line win, that win is multiplied by 9. Wheel of Fortune Bonus The Wheel of Fortune Bonus is triggered when 3 Spin Scatter Symbols appear on a played line. Triggering the Wheel of Fortune Bonus awards 1 spin of the Wheel of Fortune wheel. After the wheel stops, the prize value indicated by the wheel pointer is awarded and the bonus ends. Gold Spin Bonus The Gold Spin Bonus is triggered when 2 Spin Scatter Symbols appear on reels 1 and 2 and the Gold Spin Scatter Symbol appears in any active position on reel 3, on a played line, with a $7 or $10 bet. Triggering the Gold Spin Bonus awards 1 spin of the Gold Spin wheel. If the Gold Spin wheel pointer indicates a 2x, 3x, 5x, or 10x multiplier, 1 spin of the Wheel of Fortune wheel is awarded. After the Wheel of Fortune wheel stops, win the value indicated by the wheel pointer, times the multiplier awarded from the Gold Spin wheel. A different Wheel of Fortune wheel is used at different multipliers. The Gold Spin Bonus ends after all awarded wheel spins have been played. A different Gold Spin wheel is used when playing $7 or $10. Winning the Progressive Jackpot Wheel of Fortune Gold Spin Triple Red Hot 7s offers 2 ways to win the Progressive Jackpot. At a $5 or higher bet, 3 Wheel of Fortune Symbols on payline 1 awards the Progressive Jackpot. At a $7 or $10 bet, the Progressive Jackpot may be awarded during the Gold Spin Bonus . The Progressive Jackpot value is shown in currency and is not multiplied by any value. The odds of winning the Progressive Jackpot are proportional to the amount wagered. The higher the bet, the better the odds of winning. For example, a player playing at a $10.00 total bet has twice the chance of winning the Progressive Jackpot as a player playing at a $5.00 total bet in the same currency.
