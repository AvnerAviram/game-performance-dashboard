The Voice Do you have what it takes to make it on The Voice? Press the red button to spin the judges’ chairs and watch the musical notes fill in the prize columns. If a prize column fills at any time during the game’s 7 rounds, you win! Watch out for The Voice logo…it could appear at any time during the game, awarding you an instant prize! How to Bet How to Play Rules Additional Information How to Bet Ticket Cost: Displays the current ticket cost. Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost. Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost. Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game. How to Play Press PLAY in each round to turn 1 to 4 chairs. Each chair releases a single or double musical note, or an instant win prize. A single note lights 1 step and a double note lights 2 steps in the chair’s prize column. After 7 rounds, if the chair’s prize column is fully lit, or if the chair releases an instant prize, you win! After all 7 Rounds have been played, a closing message appears, indicating the game outcome. Rules Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. Instant wins are added to total winning prize values. Wins are shown in currency.
