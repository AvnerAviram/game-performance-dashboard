Celestial Sun Riches BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 200 credits. This game uses a special reel area during primary and free game play that consists of all positions on Reels 2, 3, and 4. All wins pay from the left most reel to the right in any positions on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only for each combination. Only highest scatter win pays. All wins except scatters are multiplied by the bet level. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total credits wagered. All wins are added. All wins are shown in credits except jackpot amount shown. Total credits wagered is the credit cost of playing a special reel area multiplied by the bet level. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. WILD appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. WILD substitutes for BEAR, FALCONS, STAGS, ACORN, PINECONE, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. SPECIAL REEL AREA Each WILD appearing in the special reel area changes to 2XWILD or 3XWILD. 2XWILD and 3XWILD substitute for BEAR, FALCONS, STAGS, ACORN, PINECONE, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. Each 2XWILD is equivalent to 2 WILD in that position. Each 3XWILD is equivalent to 3 WILD in that position. Each SUN appearing in the special reel area changes to SPECIAL SUN. SPECIAL SUN substitutes for SUN. WHEEL FEATURE The wheel feature is triggered if one or more SPECIAL SUN appear when the free game feature is triggered or retriggered. The number of wheel spins awarded is equal to the number of SPECIAL SUN that appear when the free game feature is triggered. Press the spin button to activate the feature. Available prize awards in this feature are the following: 500, 750, 1000, 2000 or 5000 credits times the bet level An additional 3, 5, 8 or 15 free games A 3X or 5X wild multiplier applied to all wilds appearing in the special reel area during the free games Jackpot If the 3X or 5X wild multiplier prize is awarded, the 3X or 5X wild multiplier wedges on the wheel change to one (1) 15 additional free games prize wedge and one (1) additional credit prize wedge during the free games session. The odds of landing on each prize are not equal. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 SUN and/or SPECIAL SUN trigger 7, 12 or 20 free games respectively. All wins during the free games are doubled. During the free games, reels 2, 3 and 4 each contain a number of positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: WILD, SUN, BEAR, FALCONS, STAGS, ACORN, PINECONE, A, K, Q, J, 10 or 9, before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. If the 3X or 5X wild multiplier prize is won during the wheel feature, each WILD appearing in the special reel area changes to 3XWILD or 5XWILD during the free games. 3XWILD and 5XWILD substitute for BEAR, FALCONS, STAGS, ACORN, PINECONE, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. Each 3XWILD is equivalent to 3 WILD in that position. Each 5XWILD is equivalent to 5 WILD in that position. Credits bet remain the same as the game that triggered the feature. Different reels are used during the free games. Additional free game features can be won during the free games. The Theoretical Return To Player (RTP) is 94%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
