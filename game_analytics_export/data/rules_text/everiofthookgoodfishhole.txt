Off The Hook Good Ol' Fishin' Hole HOW TO PLAY Commit a wager to cast a line and have a chance to catch a fish worth a particular credit prize value shown in the Paytable above. Every bet will be 10 credits. The fishing rod used is for entertainment purposes only and has no effect on the odds of winning. Non-Winners can be displayed as junk items or no item shown after a line snap. Additional Information MENU Press to open and close game settings and view game information. BET Press to access Bet Settings without opening the Menu. AUTO PLAYS Press to access Auto Play settings without opening the Menu. PLAY Press to start playing with the current bet settings. Or, if applicable, press Space Bar to start playing with the current bet settings. SOUND Press to enable or disable Sound. SETTINGS Press to access game Settings. BET SETTINGS Press to access Bet Settings. PAYS/HELP Press to view game pay and help information. Game will automatically complete after 24-48 hours if left in a feature. Payoffs on registered credits only. Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 11/19/2024
