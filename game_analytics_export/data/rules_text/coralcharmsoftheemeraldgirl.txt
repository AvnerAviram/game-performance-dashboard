Charms Of The Emerald Girl Help /Paytable Rules / How To play: Charms Of The Emerald Girl is played over 5 reels. Press the Spin button to begin a spin. Once a spin cycle has started, press spin button to turbo the spin. The Stake is the stake per line multiplied by the number of lines. Bonus Feature: The bonus feature is activated when 3 or more of the Crystal Ball Symbol land in view. The Crystal Ball Symbol Will trigger at any position in view. Triggering the Bonus Feature will always award 15 free spins. During free spins all prize amounts are multiplied by x3. The Bonus Feature can be re-triggered during an existing bonus round. Re-triggering the Bonus Feature during an existing bonus round will also award 15 free spins. The total winnings shown at the end of the free spins bonus feature also includes the initial amount won when initialising the bonus feature. Wilds The Emerald Girl Symbol is available on all reels. The Emerald Girl Symbol can substitute for all other symbols except the Crystal Ball Symbol. Only the highest win will pay per active win line. The Emerald Girl Symbol cannot trigger the Free spins feature. Win Lines There are 10 Adjustable independent win lines. The user can choose any number of win lines between 1 and 10 before starting a spin. The Crystal Ball Symbol will pay in any position on screen. Auto Play Select your number of spins, a single win limit and a loss limit to start Auto Play. Auto Play will stop when the number of spins is complete, the single win limit is reached or the loss limit is reached. Additionally you may click the Cancel Auto Play button to stop auto play. Information The expected RTP is at least 94%. Not all stakes may be available. Malfunction voids game. No prize greater than £250,000 can be won in any one game (this includes the total amount that can be won during the free spins bonus feature). The outcome of any game or feature is not necessarily that shown by the odds displayed.
