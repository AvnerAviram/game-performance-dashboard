Safari Spirit Game rules 'Elephant' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Landscape' is a scatter. 'Elephant' appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 94.32%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games Feature 20, 12 or 8 free games are triggered by any 5, 4 or 3 scattered 'Landscape' respectively. During the Free Games Feature, any 3 or more scattered 'Landscape' will trigger an additional 5 free games. During the Free Games Feature, Paw wins are multiplied by 2. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games.
