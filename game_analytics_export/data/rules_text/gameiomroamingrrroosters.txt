Roaming Reels Raging Roosters Game Features Awards The Raging Roosters Reloaded Feature is triggered when Raging Roosters symbol appear on fifth reel of any paid game. The player selects 1 of 5 available fighting cocks. The selected fighting cock will oppose up to 4 other cocks in five BOUTS. The player is awarded 5 chances to knock out as many of the 4 opposing cocks as possible. If all 4 opposing cocks are defeated using the first 4 chances, then the 5th chance is not required. After each bout win, a new opponent will appear for the following bout. The number of successful bout wins will determine the bonus prize awarded. Free Spins 20, 12 or 8 free games are triggered by any 5, 4 or 3 scattered 'Tree' respectively. During the Free Games Feature 'Lion’ appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 only. During the Free Games Feature any 3 or more scattered 'Tree' will trigger an additional 5 free games. During the Free Games Feature, the Feature paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games. Win Lines 'Lion' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Tree' is a scatter. 'Lion’ appears on reels 3, 4, and 5 during paid games only. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 96.15%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/8/2020
