Frogs and Flies GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. Pays left to right on consecutive reels. The Won field displays winnings in credits. 1024 Ways to Win. Touch SPIN to spin or repeat the last bet. Select Multiplier Bet (1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x). All symbols pay once in any position on the reel, except substituted wins which may pay multiple times. Paytable amounts only display with the multiplier bet at 1. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. Scatter wins (if available) are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter. WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. Free Games Bonus 3, 4, or 5 SCATTERS triggers the Free Games Bonus and awards 5, 10, or 20 Free Games respectively. 3 or more SCATTER anywhere on reels 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 triggers free games and pays according to paytable. Before Free Games, BONUS WILDS are added to reels 2,3 and 4. Free Games are played at the same Multiplier Bet as the triggering game. Bonus can be retriggered during the Free Games. Different Reels are used during Free Games. Auto Spin: In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. Tap the Auto Spin button "ON" from setting and select the appropriate radio button to start autospins. Tap on spin button to cancel Autospin. Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus. Autospin is deactivated after a bonus or feature. Last modification date: 1/31/2017
