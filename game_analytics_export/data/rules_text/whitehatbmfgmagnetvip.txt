STEP 1: GETTING STARTED PRESS THE TOTAL BET BUTTON TO INCREASE OR DECREASE THE AMOUNT OF YOUR TOTAL BET. SELECT FROM THE LIST OF AVAILABLE BETS FROM THE TOTAL BET OPTIONS (DEPENDING ON OPERATOR CONFIGURATION AND CURRENCY EQUIVALENT). PRESS 'SPIN' TO START THE GAME. STEP 2: HOW TO PLAY PRESS 'SPIN' TO START THE GAME (DEPENDING ON JURISDICTION AND OPERATOR CONFIGURATION, SPACEBAR MAY ALSO BE USED TO START A GAME). THE REELS WILL SPIN AND EVENTUALLY STOP AT RANDOM. EACH REEL POSITION SPINS INDIVIDUALLY. 1 OR MORE MONEY SYMBOLS IN A CHAIN ON A CONNECTING WAY CAN AWARD A WIN BASED ON THE TOTAL OF THE CASH VALUES ON THE MONEY SYMBOLS INCLUDED IN THE CONNECTING WAY. ANY WINS YOU HAVE WILL BE HIGHLIGHTED AND YOUR WINNINGS WILL BE CREDITED TO YOUR ACCOUNT BALANCE. THE GAME FEATURES 5 REELS WITH 17 REEL POSITIONS UNLOCKED IN THE MAIN GAME, 25 REEL POSITIONS UNLOCKED IN FRENZY SPINS BONUS, AND 6 REELS WITH 32 REEL POSITIONS IN THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS. IN THE MAIN GAME, DYNAMITE SCATTER SYMBOLS IN VIEW CAN UNLOCK UP TO 8 REEL POSITIONS. CONNECTING WAYS 1 OR MORE SYMBOLS THAT ARE IMMEDIATELY OR PARTIALLY ADJACENT TO EACH OTHER STARTING FROM A SYMBOL(S) ON THE LEFTMOST REEL FORMS A CONNECTING WAY. CONNECTING WAYS PAYOUTS ARE MADE BASED ON THE CASH VALUES OF ALL MONEY SYMBOLS INCLUDED IN THE CONNECTING WAY. MONEY SYMBOLS A MONEY SYMBOL AWARDS A WIN BASED ON ITS CASH VALUE ONLY IF IT IS A PART OF A CONNECTING WAY. THERE ARE 4 TYPES OF MONEY SYMBOLS: BLANK MONEY SYMBOLS WITH NO CASH VALUE. REGULAR MONEY SYMBOLS WITH CASH VALUES BETWEEN 0.4 X BET AND 8 X BET. GOLDEN MONEY SYMBOLS WITH CASH VALUES BETWEEN 10 X BET AND 100 X BET. JACKPOT SYMBOLS WITH THE FOLLOWING CASH VALUES: MINI (15 X BET), MAJOR (50 X BET), OR GRAND (1000 X BET). MONEY SYMBOLS OF CERTAIN VALUES CAN APPEAR ON CERTAIN REELS IN THE MAIN GAME AND IN THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS AS BELOW: REEL 1: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0 (BLANK MONEY SYMBOLS) AND 0.6 X BET REEL 2: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0 (BLANK MONEY SYMBOLS) AND 1 X BET REEL 3: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0.6 AND 4 X BET REEL 4: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 1 AND 20 X BET REEL 5: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 3 AND 100 X BET MONEY SYMBOLS OF CERTAIN VALUES CAN APPEAR ON CERTAIN REELS IN THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS AS BELOW: REEL 1: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0.4 AND 0.6 X BET REEL 2: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0.4 AND 1 X BET REEL 3: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 0.6 AND 4 X BET REEL 4: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 1 AND 20 X BET REEL 5: MONEY SYMBOLS BETWEEN 3 AND 100 X BET REEL 6: JACKPOT SYMBOLS MINI, MAJOR AND GRAND DYNAMITE SCATTER SYMBOLS, CASH ZONES, AND DYNAMITE RESPINS FEATURE DYNAMITE SCATTER SYMBOLS CAN ONLY LAND ON REELS 1, 2, AND 3 IN THE MAIN GAME, WITH A MAXIMUM OF 1 SYMBOL PER REEL. EACH DYNAMITE SCATTER SYMBOL IN VIEW UNLOCKS A RANDOM NUMBER OF LOCKED REEL POSITIONS, A MINIMUM OF 1 PER SYMBOL. ANY NEWLY UNLOCKED REEL POSITIONS BECOME CASH ZONES. CASH ZONES ARE GUARANTEED TO LAND REGULAR OR GOLDEN MONEY SYMBOLS AT THE END OF A RESPIN. A LOCKED SYMBOL POSITION CANNOT BE UNLOCKED IF ALL ITS ADJACENT POSITIONS ARE ALSO LOCKED. WHEN THE NEW REEL POSITIONS ARE UNLOCKED, THE RESPINS FEATURE IS TRIGGERED. DURING THE FEATURE, ALL EMPTY REEL POSITIONS WILL RE-SPIN. THE DYNAMITE SCATTER SYMBOL IS THE ONLY SYMBOL IN THE GAME THAT DOES NOT NEED TO BE PART OF A WINNING CONNECTED CHAIN IN ORDER TO BE ACTIVATED. ANY PREVIOUSLY UNLOCKED POSITIONS WILL BE RELOCKED ON THE NEXT MAIN GAME SPIN. IT IS NOT POSSIBLE TO ACTIVATE THE GOLDEN MAGNET AND A DYNAMITE RESPIN IN THE SAME SPIN. GOLDEN MAGNET THE GOLDEN MAGNET CAN BE RANDOMLY ACTIVATED IN THE MAIN GAME, FRENZY SPINS, AND IN THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS ROUND. THE GOLDEN MAGNET CAN MOVE A NUMBER OF SYMBOLS FROM ANY REEL TO A PLAYER BENEFICIAL POSITION. IN THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS, AN ACTIVE GOLDEN MAGNET CAN ALSO MOVE AROUND A NUMBER OF JACKPOT SYMBOLS ON THE 6TH REEL TO A PLAYER BENEFICIAL POSITION. MULTIPLIER SYMBOLS MULTIPLIER SYMBOLS CAN ONLY LAND ON REEL 1 IN THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS. THE MULTIPLIER SYMBOL WILL MULTIPLY THE TOTAL OF ALL CONNECTING WAYS WINNINGS ON THE CURRENT SPIN. IF THERE ARE 2 OR MORE ACTIVE MULTIPLIER SYMBOLS IN A SPIN, THEIR VALUES WILL BE ADDED TOGETHER BEFORE APPLYING TO THE TOTAL SPIN WIN. THE SYMBOL MULTIPLIER VALUE IS X2 KEY SYMBOLS KEY SYMBOLS CAN LAND ON REELS 1 TO 5 IN THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS ROUND. FRENZY SPINS BONUS TRIGGER FRENZY SPINS SYMBOL CAN LAND ON REELS 3, 4, AND 5 IN THE MAIN GAME AND WITH A MAXIMUM OF 1 SYMBOL PER REEL. A FRENZY SPINS SYMBOL IS ACTIVATED IF IT IS A PART OF A CONNECTING WAY. 1 OR MORE ACTIVE FRENZY SPINS SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL TRIGGER THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS. THE NUMBER OF FRENZY SPINS A SYMBOL CAN OFFER DEPENDS ON THE REEL IT LANDS ON: REEL 3: 5 FRENZY SPINS REEL 4: 10 FRENZY SPINS REEL 5: 15 FRENZY SPINS IF MULTIPLE FRENZY SPINS SYMBOLS ARE ACTIVE, THEIR TOTALS ARE ADDED TOGETHER TO FORM THE TOTAL NUMBER OF TRIGGERING FRENZY SPINS FOR THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS. FRENZY SPINS BONUS IN THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS, ALL 25 REEL POSITIONS ARE ALWAYS UNLOCKED. 2 TO 5 CASH ZONES ON ANY RANDOM REELS ARE GUARANTEED EVERY FRENZY SPIN. EXTRA FRENZY SPINS SYMBOLS CAN LAND ON REELS 2, 3, 4, AND 5 WITH A MAXIMUM OF 1 SYMBOL PER REEL. THE EXTRA FRENZY SPINS SYMBOL IS ACTIVATED IF IT IS A PART OF A CONNECTING WAY. THE ACTIVE EXTRA FRENZY SPINS SYMBOL WILL AWARD ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS DEPENDING ON THE REEL IT LANDS ON: REEL 2: 1 OR 2 ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS REEL 3: 3 ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS REEL 4: 4 ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS REEL 5: 5 ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS IF MULTIPLE EXTRA FRENZY SPINS SYMBOLS ARE ACTIVATED, THEIR TOTALS ARE ADDED TOGETHER TO FORM THE TOTAL NUMBER OF ADDITIONAL FRENZY SPINS AWARDED. JACKPOT VAULT BONUS TRIGGER THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS SYMBOL CAN ONLY LAND ON REELS 3, 4, AND 5 IN THE MAIN GAME AND ON REELS 4 AND 5 IN THE FRENZY SPINS BONUS. THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS SYMBOL IS ACTIVATED IF IT IS A PART OF A CONNECTING WAY. ANY ACTIVE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL TRIGGER THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS. JACKPOT VAULT BONUS DURING THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS, A 6TH REEL IS ADDED, WHERE ONLY JACKPOT SYMBOLS CAN LAND. ON REELS 1-5, ONLY KEY SYMBOLS OR EMPTY POSITIONS CAN BE DRAWN. THE JACKPOT VAULT BONUS STARTS WITH 3 SPINS WHERE ONLY REELS 1 TO 5 WILL SPIN. ANY KEY SYMBOLS IN VIEW RESETS THE NUMBER OF REMAINING SPINS TO 3. ALL KEY SYMBOLS IN VIEW WILL BE HELD IN POSITION WHILE EMPTY REEL POSITIONS RE-SPIN. IF NO NEW KEY SYMBOLS LAND IN VIEW 3 TIMES IN A ROW, THEN BONUS WILL RUN A FINAL JACKPOT SPIN, RE-SPINNING ALL SYMBOLS POSITIONS THAT CONTAIN KEYS – GUARANTEEING A MONEY SYMBOL IN EACH OF THOSE POSITIONS AND FINALLY SPINNING THE 6TH REEL. ANY 6TH REEL POSITION SPIN CAN RESULT IN ONE OF THE FOLLOWING OUTCOMES: EMPTY, OR THE JACKPOT SYMBOLS - GRAND CAN ONLY SHOW A MAXIMUM OF 1 SYMBOL PER BONUS ROUND. IF A JACKPOT SYMBOL IS ADJACENT TO A REGULAR OR GOLDEN MONEY SYMBOL WHICH IN TURN FORMS PART OF A CONNECTING WAY THEN THE CORRESPONDING JACKPOT WILL BE AWARDED ALONG WITH THE TOTAL OF ALL MONEY SYMBOL CASH VALUES IN THE CONNECTING WAYS IN VIEW. MULTIPLE JACKPOTS CAN BE WON IN THE SAME JACKPOT VAULT BONUS ROUND EXCEPT FOR THE GRAND WHICH CAN ONLY BE WON A MAXIMUM OF ONCE PER BONUS ROUND. AUTOPLAY THERE IS AN AUTOPLAY FUNCTION IN THE GAME THAT WILL START EACH SPIN AUTOMATICALLY. (AVAILABILITY OF AUTOPLAY IS SUBJECT TO OPERATOR CONFIGURATION AND JURISDICTIONAL REQUIREMENTS.) BY SELECTING 'AUTOPLAY' YOU CAN CHOOSE TO BET AUTOMATICALLY, ONE AFTER THE OTHER. SELECT YOUR STAKE AS NORMAL THEN PRESS THE 'AUTOPLAY' BUTTON TO SELECT THE NUMBER OF 'AUTOPLAY' BETS YOU WANT TO PLAY. (THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL FUNCTIONS ARE ALSO APPLICABLE DEPENDING ON JURISDICTION) SELECT YOUR LOSS LIMIT (MAXIMUM AMOUNT WILLING TO LOSE FOR THE AUTOPLAY SESSION) AND THEN PRESS PLAY TO BEGIN YOUR AUTOPLAYS (OTHER OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE). AUTO PLAY WILL PAUSE IF YOU GET INTO ANY BONUS ROUND INCLUDING FRENZY SPINS AND RESUME ON RETURNING TO THE MAIN GAME. AUTO PLAY WILL STOP IF YOU REACH ANY SET WIN LIMIT, LOSS LIMIT OR IF A PROGRESSIVE POT IS AWARDED (WHERE APPLICABLE). YOU CAN STOP AUTO PLAY FROM CONTINUING ONTO THE NEXT SPIN BY CLICKING THE 'STOP' BUTTON. PAYOUT INFORMATION AND GAME RULES ALL WINNING CASH VALUES ARE ADDED TOGETHER.
