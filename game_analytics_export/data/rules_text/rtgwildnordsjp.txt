Wild Nords SPECIAL SYMBOLS AND FEATURES BJORN, WILD NORD OF THE BEARS When Bjorn appears next to the reels, he turns all Bear Symbols into WILDS! When Bjorn appears on the reels, he turns any Bear symbols into Wilds, locks them and triggers a respin! If new Bear symbols appear during the respin, another respin is triggered! ROLF, WILD NORD OF THE WOLVES When Rolf appears next to the reels, he turns all WOLF Symbols into WILDS! When Rolf appears on the reels, he turns any Wolf symbols into Wilds, locks them and triggers a respin! If new Wolf symbols appear during the respin, another respin is triggered! ASTRID, WILD NORD OF THE EAGLES When Astrid appears next to the reels, she turns all Eagle symbols into WILDS! When Astrid appears on the reels, she turns all Eagle symbols into Wilds, locks them and triggers a respin! If new Eagle symbols appear during the respin, another respin is triggered! ANIMALS When an animal appears next to the reels, it will summon high paying animal symbols of its own kind to the reels! If one or more new matching animal symbols appear on the reels during a RESPIN, then they will lock and another RESPIN is triggered! WILD SYMBOLS Wild symbols substitute for all paying symbols. HOW TO PLAY WILD NORDS TOTAL STAKE The Total Stake is the total of stakes per line. You can adjust it at any time, using the + or – buttons. SPIN THE REELS Click the Spin button to spin the reels. Speed up each reel spin by pressing spacebar again, or by clicking on the reels. For TURBO SPIN, hold down the spacebar or the Spin button. AUTOPLAY Press the AUTO button to commence automatic play of the game at the currently selected stake. Select how many spins to play via the Total Spins Dropdown menu. MORE AUTOPLAY SETTINGS Select the loss limit: The Autoplay will stop at the last spin before the loss limit is reached. The Аutoplay loss limit should be higher than your total stake. Expand the AUTOPLAY dialog by choosing MORE AUTOPLAY SETTINGS. Here you can select additional criteria for the AUTOPLAY stop including - Stop if single win exceeds certain amount; Stop on a Jackpot Win; Stop on Bonus Feature. For the best gaming experience, use of the latest versions of software is recommended. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
