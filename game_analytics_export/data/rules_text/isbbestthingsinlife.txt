Best things in life How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different selected lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each selected line is paid. Winnings from Scatter and bonus rounds are added to the total win. Note : On the info bar located under the main game buttons, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WINNINGS, in your currency. To play, follow these steps: Use the coin value select button to choose a coin value. The coin value select button is located in the bottom-left side of the main game screen and shows the currently selected coin value. Use the LINE BET button to choose the amount of coins you want to bet on each active line. Use the LINES button to choose the number of active lines. Note : The current bet amount, in your currency, is displayed in the BET box. Click SPIN to place the bet and spin the reels once. You can click the BET MAX button to place the maximum bet (all lines active and maximum number of coins per line) for the currently selected coin value and spin the reels once. The total amount won, in your currency, according to the paytable, is shown in the WINNINGS box. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the paytable for that winning combination. Special Symbols and Features Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols except Scatter. During normal spins and during free spins, a Wild symbol can expand on the whole reel, no matter where it appears on the reels, if, by expanding, it forms at least one winning combination. Scatter Scatters pay anywhere on the reels, once per spin. Scatter winnings for normal spins and for free spins are shown in the paytable. Note : Scatters and Wilds cannot appear on the same reel. Free Spins Free spins are awarded by three or more Scatters anywhere on the reels, during normal spins and by one or more Scatters anywhere on the reels during other free spins. During free spins, any winnings are multiplied by the current multiplier (X1, X2, X3, X4 or X5). During free spins, each spin with no winnings increases the current multiplier by one, up to maximum X5. The current multiplier is reset to X1 after a winning spin. Example: The current multiplier for Spin 1 is X1 and, at the end of this spin, there are no winnings - the multiplier is increased to X2 for the next spin (Spin 2). The current multiplier for Spin 2 is X2 and, at the end of this spin, there are winnings which are multiplied by X2 and are added to your balance - the multiplier for the next spin is reset to X1. Autospin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the autospin feature automatically when the selected limit plus your cumulative winnings minus your cumulative bets is less than 0. Note : The autospin feature does not automatically start free spins - player input is required. Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window where you can set up the autospin feature. On the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window, you can set up the following: Loss limit - enter a number for the loss limit value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the loss limit value Stop if WIN reaches - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side and then enter a number for the WIN value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the WIN value; click the button on its left side again to disable it Stop if JACKPOT is won - (only for games with Progressive Jackpots or with a specific Jackpot value displayed) - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side; click the button on its left side again to disable it Number of AUTO SPINS to play - use the left and the right arrow buttons to select the number of autospins Click START to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window and start the autospin feature with the currently selected bet and number of autospins or click CANCEL to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window without starting the autospin feature. Click the STOP xx/yy button to stop the autospin feature at any time; xx represents the remaining number of autospins and yy represents the number of autospins to play that you selected on the AUTO SPINS SETTINGS window.
