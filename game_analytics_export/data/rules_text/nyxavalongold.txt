The game can be played automatically by activating Auto Spin (if available in the applicable jurisdiction). Hold down the Spin button or use the Auto Spin button to set the number of game rounds to be played automatically. Winnings and winning combinations are paid out according to the Paytable. Winnings are presented in the player's currency or in coins. The player can change preference in Settings. Avalon Gold is played in two modes; Normal mode and Bonus mode with free drops. All symbol drops in Normal mode start with 4 rows and 4,096 ways to win. All low, mid, high and Mystery Box symbols exist in four sizes; standard 1x1, Super 2x2, Mega 3x3 and Epic 4x4. All big symbols are counted as consisting of the number of 1x1 symbol instances it covers, e.g. 1 Super 2x2 is counted as 4 standard 1x1 symbols. Empty spaces occurring below big symbols are filled with 1x1 sized symbols of the same type as the big symbol to reach a stable horizontal resting state. The wild symbol substitutes for any symbol except the bonus and Mystery Box symbols. The Expanding Wild symbol substitutes for any symbol except the bonus and Mystery Box symbols. The Expanding Wild symbol expands upwards, filling all vacant positions up to the current maximum number of rows when the payout symbol in front of it is part of a winning combination. The Expanding Wild symbol is only removed in case it is the only symbol on its reel that contributes to a winning combination. The Mystery Box symbol is a blocking symbol that can drop in on any reel in any drop. If there are 5 or more Mystery Boxes on the reels, the Mystery Boxes will activate and either reveal a payout symbol, a wild symbol or trigger the Avalon features. The activation occurs when there are no more winning combinations. If the Mystery Box reveals a payout or wild symbol that leads to a win, a new chain of Avalanches will start, and all Mystery Boxes will go back to a blocking state. When the Avalanche cycle is over and no additional wins appear, the Mystery Boxes will be activated again. If the Mystery Boxes reveal the Avalon features, the Mystery Boxes will reveal either a coin win or a feature. There are 4 different features; multiplier, +1 Swiper, Collect and Redrop. The Mystery Boxes are revealed one at a time, top to bottom one reel at a time. Coin win: The value of the coin win symbol is multiplied by the placed bet. Unopened Mystery Boxes are not affected. +1 Swiper: Swipes in a 1 in front of the most recently awarded coin value prior to the +1 Swiper.
