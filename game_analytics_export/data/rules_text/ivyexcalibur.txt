Excalibur GAME OBJECTIVES: EXCALIBUR is a five reel, ‘ALL WAYS SLOT’ game with 243 possible ways to win in the base game and FREE SPINS. Symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive combinations (WAYS). The FORTUNE SPINNER and TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature games can also be triggered. When 3 or more FORTUNE SPINNER symbols land anywhere in view the FORTUNE SPINNER feature is triggered. FREE SPINS can be triggered during the feature. Any CASINO CHIP symbols which land in view will reveal a cash value or a VEGAS POTS title. CASINO CHIP symbols are then locked in place. The reels will RESPIN until no further CASINO CHIP symbols land in view. When 6 or more CASINO CHIP symbols land anywhere in view the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature is triggered. The VEGAS POTS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and GRAND) are active during the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature. The WILD symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 and substitutes for all symbols except the FORTUNE SPINNER and CASINO CHIP symbols. GAME RULES: EXCALIBUR is a five reel, ‘ALL WAYS SLOT’ game with 243 possible ways to win. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the current selected or default BET amount. All payouts are made according to the Paytable. The Paytable will always display the win amounts for the current selected BET. All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in any position on adjacent reels starting from the left most reel, except for the FORTUNE SPINNER and CASINO CHIP symbols. Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid. All possible wins are added to the players BALANCE. FORTUNE SPINNER and CASINO CHIP symbols act as scatter symbols and do not have a direct payout. When 3 or more FORTUNE SPINNER symbols land anywhere in view, the FORTUNE SPINNER feature will be triggered. Any CASINO CHIP symbols in view will be locked in position and the RESPINS will be awarded. 6 or more CASINO CHIP symbols landing anywhere in view will trigger the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature with the chance to win VEGAS POTS. When the FORTUNE SPINNER and TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS features are triggered during the same game, the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature will be completed first, followed by the FORTUNE SPINNER feature. The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the FORTUNE SPINNER and CASINO CHIP symbols. The WILD symbol appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only in both base game and FREE SPINS. Wilds can form part of multiple wins. The CASINO CHIP symbol is only available in base game and the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS Feature and appears on all reels. The FORTUNE SPINNER symbol is only available in base game and FREE SPINS. MIN BET is $0.10 and MAX BET is $20. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) is 95.67%. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. Win count up animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (desktop). All pays are in USD. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. RESPINS: A RESPIN is awarded to the player when 1 or more CASINO CHIP symbols land in view. RESPINS will re-trigger if further CASINO CHIP symbols land on subsequent spins. The CASINO CHIP symbols lock and hold until no further CASINO CHIP symbols are added. If a minimum of 6 CASINO CHIP symbols land during the RESPINS feature the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature will be triggered. The values displayed on the CASINO CHIP symbols will carry forward to the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature. During the RESPINS feature, the value displayed on the CASINO CHIP symbols will not award any payout. During the RESPINS feature, the player has the chance to trigger both the FORTUNE SPINNER and TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature games. The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature takes precedence followed by the FORTUNE SPINNER feature. If the FORTUNE SPINNER and RESPINS features trigger in the same game, then the FORTUNE SPINNER feature takes precedence followed by the RESPINS feature. FORTUNE SPINNER: The FORTUNE SPINNER feature is triggered when 3 or more FORTUNE SPINNER symbols land anywhere in view. The player must click the PLAY button to spin the wheel. The FORTUNE SPINNER spins randomly awards 1 of 5 prizes: TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS 2 FREE SPINS with 50 EXTRA WILDS and 2x MULTIPLIER 10 FREE SPINS with 50 EXTRA WILDS 10 FREE SPINS with 100 EXTRA WILDS 10 FREE SPINS with 150 EXTRA WILDS The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature can also be triggered from the FORTUNE SPINNER but starts with zero seeded CASINO CHIP symbols. When the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS is triggered from the FORTUNE SPINNER feature, any CASINO CHIP symbols displayed on the base game reels are not carried into the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature. The feature continues until all spins are complete or all 15 CASINO CHIP symbol positions have been filled. FREE SPINS: FREE SPINS commence with the awarded prize from the FORTUNE SPINNER . FREE SPINS play automatically. During FREE SPINS, if 3 or more FORTUNE SPINNER symbols land anywhere in view then the introductory award of FREE SPINS is retriggered. The additional FREE SPINS are added to the remaining FREE SPINS. Any introductory WILD and MULTIPLIERS are kept for the remaining FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS are played at the same BET as the triggering game. TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS: The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature is triggered when 6 or more CASINO CHIP symbols land anywhere in view. The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature starts with the same number of chips seeded in their landing positions. The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature is be played with 15 independent reels spinning individually. The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature starts with 3 spins awarded to the player. Each additional CASINO CHIP symbol/s which land in view resets the spins counter to 3. Each CASINO CHIP symbol that lands in view will have a random BET MULTIPLIER applied from the following range - 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 4x, 4.5x, 5x, 7.5x, 15x, 20x, 60x, 75x. The TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature will continue until the player reaches zero spins or has filled all 15 CASINO CHIP positions. Once the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature is completed, all the CASINO CHIP values are cumulated and awarded as winnings to the player. The VEGAS POTS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and GRAND) can be won only during the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature: If a CASINO CHIP symbol labeled with MINI lands anywhere in view, the MINI pot (10x) will be awarded to the player. If a CASINO CHIP symbol labeled with MINOR lands anywhere in view, the MINOR pot (50x) will be awarded to the player. If a CASINO CHIP symbol labeled with MAJOR lands anywhere in view, the MAJOR pot (500x) will be awarded to the player. If a CASINO CHIP symbol labeled with GRAND lands anywhere in view, the GRAND pot (10000x) will be awarded to the player. More than 1 VEGAS POTS award can be won during the TOURNAMENT OF CHAMPIONS feature. The VEGAS POTS value is in ratio with the BET wagered.
