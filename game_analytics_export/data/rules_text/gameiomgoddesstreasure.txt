Goddess Treasure Goddess Treasures is a Greek goddess themed game containing the Fortune Blast feature, Pick Feature as well as a Free Spins Bonus. Pays are left-to-right and use line evaluation method. Linked jackpots are awarded through the Fortune Blast feature. Jackpots and credit prizes appear on the scatter symbols. The Mini and Minor jackpots are static and can be won multiple times in one bonus. The Major and Grand jackpots are static and can only be won once per bonus. Base game 3 or more Bonus symbols triggers the Pick Bonus. The Pick Bonus can trigger an entry into the Fortune Blast Feature, or a Free Spins. A wild that lands on the reels can trigger the Fortune Blast Feature. Fortune Blast Bonus Can be triggered by the Choose-Your-Fortune Bonus, or mysteriously when a Wild lands on the reels During the Bonus, credit prizes are locked in place and Jackpots can be won until spins run out. The Bonus begins with 3 spins and an additional spin is awarded for each symbol locked.
