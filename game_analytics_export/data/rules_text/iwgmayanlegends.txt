Mayan Legends Introduction Roll the dice to collect the masks. Fill a row on the prize table, collect an instant prize or collect 3 or more crystal masks to win Set Your Stake Choose your stake by pressing the Stake '+' and '-' buttons. Start of Game To start the game, press the ‘Play’ button located below the wager amount. Once the ‘Play’ button has been selected the stake is deducted from the account balance and the game starts. End of the game At the end of the game the outcome is displayed on a message panel. You can play the game again at the same stake amount as the previous game by pressing the 'Play' button located on the message panel. Once the 'Play' button has been selected the stake is deducted from the current balance. Pressing the 'Set Bet' button will cause the 'Set Stake' interface to appear where you can alter the stake. How to Play You have 5 rolls of the dice. Click the dice to select a co-ordinate to collect the mask at that position. Any matching adjacent symbols will also be collected. All collected symbols will be marked in the prize table. Fill any row, collect 3 or more crystal masks or collect an instant prize to win. Winning Ways to win Filling a row on the prize table. You win the prize amount shown for that row. Collect an instant prize from the game board Collect 3 or more crystal masks General Information System malfunction voids all pays and all plays. This game is a game of chance. The outcome of a play in the game is pre-determined. Actions or choices made by the player do not affect the outcome of the game. The theoretical average return to player(RTP) is 85.16 % Last modification date: 6/9/2017
