WHEN A PRIZE, EXCEPT FOR THE RED SEVEN, IS AVAILABLE, PORTIONS OF THIS PRIZE CAN BE RISKED IN THE AUTOMATICALLY ACCESSED RISK GAME. BY RISKING A PORTION WE CAN GET DOUBLE THAT PORTION OR AN EQUIVALENT VALUE IN BONUSES. IN CASE THE PLAYER DOES NOT WANT TO RISK THAT PORTION, HE/SHE CAN COLLECT IT BY PRESSING COLLECT, AND IF HE/SHE KEEPS PRESSING COLLECT, HE/SHE WILL BE ABLE TO COLLECT THE REST OF THE PRIZE WITHOUT RISKING IT. MAIN GAME IN THIS GAME, THE PLAYER DECIDES WHETHER TO COLLECT THE PRIZE BY PRESSING THE COLLECT BUTTON OR THE PLAY BUTTON TO RISK IT. BY TAKING A RISK WE CAN EITHER GET THE TOP PRIZE IN THE WIN PLAN OR AN EQUIVALENT AMOUNT OF BONUSES. ONCE THE PRIZE BARRIER CORRESPONDING TO THE FIGURES OF THE THREE BELLS HAS BEEN PASSED, THE GAME BECOMES A SEESAW GAME WHERE THE POSSIBLE PRIZES ARE TO GO UP OR DOWN IN THE WINNINGS PLAN. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. AUTO When nudges are activated, it allows you to rotate the reels automatically, always resulting in the best option for the player. If the button is permanently lit, this means that the auto option is activated. Otherwise, if the button is flashing, this indicates that the auto option is not activated. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. AUTO PLAY Activate the automatic game mode.
