Vampires Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 96.00%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Rules VAMPIRES is a 5-reel 3-row game with 5 win lines fixed. Stakes range from €0.05 to €10.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins There are a total of five win lines. Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. VAMPIRE is scattered and pays in any position and is wild and substitutes for all Symbols. Free Games Three or more VAMPIRE symbols trigger 15 free games. During free games, any substitute VAMPIRE win changes all winning symbols to WILD for the remainder of the feature. In free games all scatter wins are omitted. The resulting winnings will be shown in the pay table. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
