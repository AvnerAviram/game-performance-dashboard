Rainbow Riches Reels of Gold Game Introduction This game is a Colossal Reel game with the classic Irish theme! Play over 100 lines and Win Fairy Bonuses and Free Spins! Fairy Bonus The Fairy can appear at any time in the game. She will turn the game purple. She can award a number of different Reel bonuses and upgrades. Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. The player can gamble the win for extra Free Spins, up to 100 Free Spins. The Fairy Bonuses are available in Free Spins too. Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. Big Bet games can contain the Balloon Bonus. In the Balloon Bonus, there are extra chances to enter Free Spins. The reels will also turn Rainbow Riches Symbols on the main reel into stacked reels and transfer to the equivalent Colossal reel. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains up to 100 win lines. The number of win lines in the game increases with stake up to 100 lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 Last modification date: 6/4/2020
