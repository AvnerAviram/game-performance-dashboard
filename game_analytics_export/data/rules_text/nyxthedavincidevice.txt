The Da Vinci Device Rules This game has an RTP of 96% You play a fixed 25 coins per spin with 243 win ways The value of the coin is: Bet value/25 Only the highest win on any win way is paid Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the paytable Malfunction voids all pays and plays Features This game features Wilds, x2-4 Wilds, and Scatter symbols Normal Wilds substitute for any basic symbols and will pay as the highest win. x2-4 Wilds work like normal Wilds, but also multiplies the payout of any win they are part of Only one multiplier wild can appear at any time Each one of the special scatter symbols appears only on their specific reel, they do not look identical but assemble to make a cryptex. Collecting 3 parts to this will unlock 8 Freespins collecting more parts will award 4 additional Freespins each.
