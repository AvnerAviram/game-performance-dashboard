FREE SPINS FEATURE Any combination of BLUE COIN, YELLOW COIN, RED COIN may trigger the Free Spins Feature and award 1x total bet. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered by BLUE COIN, YELLOW COIN, or RED COIN, then, for each coin involved, "WINNER!" appears above BLUE PIG, YELLOW PIG, or RED PIG, respectively. If a BLUE COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that BLUE COIN may add 1 free spin to the BLUE PIG meter for the active bet option. If a YELLOW COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that YELLOW COIN may add 0.25x-6.75x total bet to the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI meter for the active bet option. If a RED COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that RED COIN may increase the Wild Multiplier by 1 in the RED PIG meter for the active bet option.If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with BLUE COIN, then the number of free spins displayed in the BLUE PIG meter are awarded. If the Free Spins Feature was not triggered with BLUE COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded when Free Spins Feature is awarded. The BLUE PIG meter can display a maximum of 40 free spins. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with BLUE COIN, the BLUE PIG meter is reset to 9 free spins. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with YELLOW COIN, then each MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a jackpot symbol for the MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI Jackpots. The MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI have 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, and 2 spaces respectively. Jackpot symbols are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, and the corresponding jackpot is awarded and then reset before any additional jackpots are awarded. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with YELLOW COIN , the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI meters are reset. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with RED COIN, then all winning combinations involving WILD MULTIPLIER are multiplied by the Wild Multiplier. The RED PIG meter can display a maximum Wild Multiplier of 7x. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with RED COIN, the RED PIG meter is reset to a Wild Multiplier of 2x. WILD MULTIPLIER appears only during Free Spins Feature triggered with RED COIN. WILD MULTIPLIER appears only on reels 2-4. WILD MULTIPLIER is WILD for WILD.
