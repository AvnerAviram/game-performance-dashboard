Cherry Surprise GENERAL INFORMATION Game Type 5 reel, 10 win line slot game Theme Fruit, Bagatelle GAME FEATURES Free Spins Cherry Pot Free Spins Gamble PAYOUT Default maximum Win x1402.5 Hit Frequency (%) 17.64% Jackpot Not Suitable Volatility Variations Variations (%) 90% 94% Return (%) 90.12% 94.53% GAME DESIGN Main Game Cherry Surprise is played over 5 reels, each reel containing 3 symbols. The Cherry Pot displays the total value of all Cherry Symbols in view. The Cherry values are x2, x5, x10, x15, x25, x50 and multiply the current total bet. The Cherry Pot can only be triggered in Free Spins. Free Spins 3x Bonus scatters award 10 Free Spins. 4x Bonus scatters award 15 Free Spins. 5x Bonus scatters award 20 Free Spins. During Free Spins, landing the Cherry Pot symbol anywhere in view awards the Cherry Pot. Free Spins Gamble When Free Spins are triggered, there will be an option to gamble the current number of Free Spins in the Free Spins Gamble. Selecting 'COLLECT' will collect the current number of Free Spins and start the Free Spins Bonus. Selecting 'GAMBLE' will activate the Free Spins Gamble feature, giving the player a 50 /50 chance of winning more Free Spins. Selecting 'CHANGE VALUES' will change the range of the gamble. This can be cycled through showing all of the available options. Rules Scatter wins and line wins are added. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per scatter combination. Scatter symbols in view trigger Free Spins. Free Spins are played at the trigger bet and lines. Wild symbols are only available in Free Spins. Wild symbol substitutes all symbols.
