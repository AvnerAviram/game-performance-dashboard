Nudging Wilds Feature At the end of a game, one or more Wild symbols appearing in the top 2 positions of the reels, will trigger the Nudging Wilds Feature. If the same happens on the next round, these reels will nudge down by 2 positions rather than spin to a random position while increasing the potential for big wins! Free Games Feature Three or more Scatter symbols will trigger the Free Games Feature. During the feature, each Scarab symbol will award with one or two Free Games. The total number of Free Games added is equal to the sum of values revealed by the Scatter symbols. Wild reels During the Free Games, first, third and fifth reels are always filled with Wild symbols meaning that every Free Game is a winner! Changing Bet Size The last spin result is tracked for each valid bet value. This is to prevent players from playing at a low bet size until they know that the Nudging Wild Feature will be triggered in the next game, and then increasing their bet size before playing the next game. After changing the bet, the symbols on the reels will change to display the result from the last spin at the selected bet size. In the case where the next spin will be the first play of the game at the selected bet size, a default result with no Wild symbols will be displayed.
