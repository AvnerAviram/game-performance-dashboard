Glitter Gems HOW TO PLAY The 'TOTAL BET' is the 'BET' multiplied by the number of 'LINES'. GAME HISTORY: This can be viewed by clicking on the 'clock' button. RESULT: Any wins are indicated by the highlighted lines and symbols animating wherever applicable. Wins are shown in the message box. The game rules are identical in both real play and demo play modes. The player is responsible for checking the correct bet has been registered before commencing play. Malfunction Voids all Pays and Plays. Game Rules 'Glitter Gems' substitutes for all symbols except 'Blue Diamond'. 'Glitter Gems' appears on reels 3, 4 and 5 only, except during the Free Games Feature. 'Blue Diamond' is a scatter. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 94.25%. FREE GAMES FEATURE 8 free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Blue Diamond'. During the Free Games Feature, 'Red Seven' and 'Purple Seven' pays 'Mixed Seven'. During the Free Games Feature, 'Glitter Gems' appears on all reels. During the Free Games Feature, if one or more 'Glitter Gems' substitutes in a win the pay for that win is DOUBLED (x2). During the Free Games Feature, 5 additional free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Blue Diamond'. During the Free Games Feature, the Feature paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games.
