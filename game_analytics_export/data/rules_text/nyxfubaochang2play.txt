Displays the autoplay options. Stops the autoplay session. The numerical value displayed within the button shows the number of autoplay game rounds remaining. Bet Deﬁnitions Current Bet: The current bet is the value that appears above . Minimum Bet: The minimum bet is the lowest possible bet value, which is selected by pressing until the bet value no longer decreases. Bet Multiplier: The bet multiplier is defined as the current bet divided by the minimum bet. Autoplay Options Autoplay will allow game rounds to be played automatically. Click on the Autoplay button and select the number of Autoplay game rounds. on any winning play, the Multiplier Feature may randomly be activated. If the Multiplier Feature is activated, a Random Multiplier of x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, or x7 will appear in front of the net. All Currency Wins will be multiplied by the Random Multiplier. Progressive Pick Feature Each time at least one lands on a grid, the Progressive Pick Feature could be randomly triggered. During the Progressive Pick Feature, objects are picked until 3 match. The progressive corresponding to the 3 revealed matching objects is awarded. Free Games The appearance of 3, 4, or 5 on a grid will initiate 7, 12, or 25 Free Games, respectively. The appearance of 3, 4, or 5 on a grid during Free Games will retrigger 7, 12, or 25 Free Games, respectively. During Free Games, only will appear on a grid. During Free Games, each time at least one lands on a grid, the Progressive Pick Feature could be randomly triggered. Progressive Jackpot The Progressive Pick Feature must be accessed in order to win 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. The 4 Progressive Jackpots are: Mini, Minor, Major, and Mega. If 3 Minis are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Mini Progressive is awarded. If 3 Minors are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Minor Progressive is awarded. If 3 Majors are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Major Progressive is awarded. If 3 Megas are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Mega Progressive is awarded. It is not technically possible to have two simultaneous wins of the same Progressive Jackpot. There are separate Progressive Jackpots per bet multiplier. Contributions made to Progressive Jackpots at a certain bet multiplier are added to Progressive Jackpots that can only be achieved by making a bet at that same bet multiplier. The following contributions are made to Progressive Jackpots on each base game spin: .33% of the bet is contributed to the Mini Progressive Jackpot. .26% of the bet is contributed to the Minor Progressive Jackpot. .21% of the bet is contributed to the Major Progressive Jackpot. .15% of the bet is contributed to the Mega Progressive Jackpot At a 1x bet multiplier, the start-up value for each Progressive Jackpot is: Mini: $5.00 Minor: $15.00 Major: $50.00 Mega: $300.00 The start-up values for the Progressive Jackpots at bet multipliers other than 1x are calculated by multiplying the bet multiplier by the start-up value at a 1x bet multiplier. There is no upper limit on Progressive Jackpot values. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 8/14/2024
