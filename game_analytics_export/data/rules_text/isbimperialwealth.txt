Imperial Wealth Game Rules 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 4 Number of lines 40 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 20 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.51% Jackpot 250 coins per line, during normal spins The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. On the info bar located at the bottom of the main game screen, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Mobile version To play, follow these steps: Tap to open the settings screen. On the settings screen, tap then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features 3.1. Wild The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Scatter, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2. Scatter Three or more Scatter symbols (Bonus) anywhere on the reels award Free Spins, according to the paytable, and trigger the Fortune Tree Bonus round. Note : Free Spins start when the Fortune Tree Bonus round ends. 3.3. Free Spins During Free Spins, two or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels award additional Free Spins, according to the pay table. Note : The maximum amount of Free Spins that you can play during one round is 100. 3.4. Fortune Tree Bonus To play the Fortune Tree Bonus round, click or tap envelopes to reveal a random prize: additional Free Spins - these are added to the current number of Free Spins you won from Scatters; a multiplier that applies to all winnings from the Free Spins that will start when the Fortune Tree Bonus round ends - the initial multiplier you can win is 2x; after you win it, the next multiplier you can win will be 3x and so on, up to the max multiplier you can win: 5x. one or more of the high paying symbols will act as Wild during the Free Spins that will start when the Fortune Tree Bonus round ends Note: As only the highest win on each line is always awarded, a high paying symbol transformed into Wild might pay its usual amount, according to the paytable, if the amount from the combination formed as Wild is less than the amount of the combination from the paytable for that high paying symbol. The Fortune Tree Bonus round ends when you reveal Start Free Spins . 3.5. Imperial Roar Bonus Randomly, at the start of any normal paid spin, the Imperial Roar Bonus can start and you can win one of the following: Wild reels: 1 to 3 reels can become Wild Extra Wilds: 1 to 10 positions on the reels can become Wild 5 of a kind: at least one line of "5 of a kind" with high paying symbols will form Fortune Tree Bonus round trigger (three or more Scatter symbols) Note : It is possible not to win any of the above awards from the Imperial Roar Bonus. 4.
