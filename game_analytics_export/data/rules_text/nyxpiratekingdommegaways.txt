Pirate Kingdom MegaWays Game Rules Pirate Kingdom Megaway™ is a 6-reel, 2 to 7-row video slot with win ways from 64 to 117649, featuring Wilds, x2 Wilds, Free Tumbles and Free Spins with incremental multipliers. You play a fixed 20 coins per spin, the total bet value is shown on the bet control. The spin button plays the game at the currently shown bet value. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYOUT SCREEN. Wins are calculated by: counting the number of consecutive reels with matching symbols and multiplying the relative multipler, shown in the payout values, by the product of the number of matching symbols on each reel. Wild symbols count as all other symbols. X2 Wild symbols count as all other symbols and multiply any payout they are part of by 2. Multiple X2 Wild multiply each other, ie: 3 X2 wilds combine to give an overall X8. Free Spins Freespins are reached by having multiple successive tumbles. A minimum of 4 tumbles is required to trigger 8 Free Spins. Each successive tumble after 4 awards 2 Free Spins, up to a maximum of 14 During Free Spins, reels with 7 symbols lock at that number of symbols for the entirety of Free Spins feature. When a reel locks it awards 1 additional multiplier. On the last Free Spin, locking reels award 1 additional Free Spin that can also re-trigger. Additional Free Spins are reached by having multiple successive tumbles. A minimum of 4 tumbles is required to trigger additional 2 Free Spins. Each successive tumble after 4 awards 2 Free Spins, up to a maximum of 14 The current multipler applies to any win earned while that multipler is in effect, increases will affect all subsequent wins. The Arrows either side of your bet value lets you change the stake The Max Bet button will increase your stake to the maximum allowed value The COINS and CASH option toggles the balance display and payout values, the bet value is always shown in CASH. The value of the coin is: Bet value/20. Buy Free Spins This option is not available for every operator and jurisdiction, therefore may not be available in every instance. You have to select both the number of Free Spins you want to buy and if you want a locked reel. Once you have selected both of these options, a price will appear for buying the feature.
