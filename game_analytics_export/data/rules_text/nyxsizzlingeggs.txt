Sizzling Eggs Sizzling EggsTM The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and an additional top row with Collector symbols. Wins are awarded for drawing 3 symbols of a kind from leftmost to right on consecutive reels. The game features 5 fixed bet lines. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and Special symbols. Drawing 3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels awards 10, 20 or 30 Free Spins respectively. Collector symbols can only be drawn on the top row of the reels. When a Collector symbol is drawn, it is assigned a random countdown value from 1 to 10. With each spin, the Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash and Jackpot symbols drawn on the reels below, until its countdown reaches 0. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-10x player's bet. MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and MEGA Jackpot symbols award corresponding Jackpots. Cash and Jackpot symbols’ values can only be won when collected by the Collector symbol. The maximum win amount is 2500x bet. Special symbols may randomly appear during the base game. These symbols may have additional features, which are active during the base game, e.g. collect prizes from other symbols or stick to the reels for up to 10 spins. However, all Special symbols pay only in the Free Spins feature. Wins from different lines are added together. SPECIAL FEATURES FREE SPINS 3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols drawn during the Free Spins feature award additional 10, 20 or 30 Free Spins. Collector symbols that are on the reels when the Free Spins feature is activated remain there with their current countdown and accumulated wins. Collector modifier symbols may randomly appear on the reels and affect the Collector symbol drawn on the reel above them. Multiplier symbols multiply the total value of prizes collected by the Collector symbol by 2x-10x. Countdown Booster symbols increase the value of the Collector countdown by 3. Collect to Infinity™ symbols change the countdown value of the Collector symbol to infinity, allowing it to accumulate all values from Cash and Jackpot symbols drawn on the reels below till the end of the Free Spins feature. During the Free Spins feature when the Collector symbols’ countdown reaches 0, the symbol stays on the reels as inactive. Inactive Collector symbols do not accumulate wins from Cash or Multiplier symbols. Inactive symbols can be reactivated with Countdown Booster and Collect to Infinity™ symbols. When the Free Spins feature ends, the prize is paid for the sum of bet line wins and Collector symbols’ values and all Collector symbols are removed from the reels.
