Spirits of Zen This game pays 243 ways. All symbols pay left to right on consecutive reels starting from the leftmost reel. Only the highest win per line is paid. Wild Hopper Hops onto random symbols and turns them wild The WILD HOPPER occurs randomly after a spin. It does not occur at the same time as other game features. However, it can occur in the FREE SPINS BONUS. Heli Flipper Flips random reel symbols and awards wins Randomly after a spin the HELI FLIPPER flies around the screen flipping symbols to reveal one or more winning symbols. It does not occur at the same time as other game features. However, it can occur in the FREE SPINS BONUS. Reel Shunt Re-spins random reels and awards wins Randomly after a spin the REEL SHUNT character grabs a reel and starts an instant re-spin of that reel to reveal a new set of symbols with one or more winning symbols. It does not occur at the same time as other game features. However, it can occur in the FREE SPINS BONUS. Free Spins Bonus 3 or more FREE SPINS symbols anywhere in view awards the FREE SPINS BONUS game. During Free Spins wins give an additional re-spin and increase the award multiplier starting from X1 up to a maximum of X5. Wins after the X5 re-trigger are capped at X5. However, when there is no win the award multiplier is reset to X1. Any of the game features can occur during Free Spins. The final free spins bonus win is added to the total win. 243 Ways Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations, starting from the leftmost reel. Malfunction voids pays and plays. The theoretical average return to player is 95.52%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/9/2020
