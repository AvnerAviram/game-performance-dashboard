Aztec Gold Megaways Game Rules 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 6 Number of rows 3-7 Number of ways Up to 117,649 Megaways Number of coins you can bet 20 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes Scatter symbols Yes, Sun Disc Scatters Bonus round Yes (Aztec Gold Cash Respins) Free spins No Instant win No RTP 95.99% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: 1.Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value and the number of coins to bet per line. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. 2.Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. 3.Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features 3.1. Megaways Megaways under license from Big Time Gaming. Each spin can have up to 117,649 ways to win. Wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbols, starting on reel 1, regardless of size. 3.2. Cascading Wins Symbols spin into position to form a number of Megaways. Any win in view will start a Cascade. Symbols in winning combinations will explode and new symbols will drop into position. Cascades will continue until no new wins are formed. Note: Sun Disc Scatters cannot be broken and will remain on screen until no more wins are formed. 3.3. Wild The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Sun Disc Scatters, to form winning ways. Wild symbols can only be found on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. 3.4. Max Megaways During any paid spin the Max Megaways modifier can be triggered. This will happen randomly with a unique background animation and the modifier title appearing, causing the reels to land the maximum amount of Megaways available (117,649). 3.5. Sun Discs Boost During any paid spin the Sun Discs Boost can be triggered. This will happen randomly with a unique background animation and the modifier title appearing for a chance of triggering the Aztec Gold Cash Respins. Note: An Aztec Gold Cash Respins trigger is not guaranteed with the Sun Discs Boost modifier. 3.6. Aztec Gold Cash Respins 5 or more Sun Disc Scatters anywhere on the reels will trigger the Aztec Gold Cash Respins – 3 initial respins are awarded and these are played on a special set of reels where only Sun Disc Scatters can appear. The respins start with the initial Sun Disc Scatters remaining in place and the number of Megaways locked at the triggering amount for the duration of the feature. During Aztec Gold Cash Respins, whenever at least one new Sun Disc Scatter lands on the reels, the number of respins is reset to 3. All Sun Disc Scatters that land during the respins will remain in place until the feature ends. If any Mini, Major or Mega Sun Disc Scatters land in view, the amount shown in the relevant top panel above the respins reels is awarded, the same as with any other Sun Disc Scatter. In addition, if an entire reel is filled with Sun Disc Scatters, the relevant multiplier (equal to the number of spaces on the reel and displayed above the reel) is awarded and all Sun Disc Scatters on that reel are multiplied when the winnings are paid. The Aztec Gold Cash Respins end when no additional Sun Disc Scatters appear on the special set of reels or when all the positions on the special set of reels are filled. At the end of the respins feature, the sum of the values from each Sun Disc Scatter in view is added to your balance. Note: The values displayed on Sun Disc Scatters are x Total Bet. During Aztec Gold Cash Respins you cannot change the bet. 4.
