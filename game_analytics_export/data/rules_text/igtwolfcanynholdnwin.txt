Wolf Canyon Scatter symbols Landing 3 Wolf Canyon Scatter symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 will trigger the Wolf Feature Pick. 3.2. Cash Moon symbols Landing 5 or more Cash Moon symbols anywhere will award the Hold & Win Feature. 3.3. Totem Wild symbols Landing 3 Totem Wild symbols on the same reel will extend to become a stacked Wild symbol. Stacked Wild symbols can appear only on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. The Stacked Wild will still count as 3 single Wilds . The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Cash Moon symbols and Wolf Canyon Scatter symbols, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.4. Dreamcatcher Triggered randomly on any paid spin, the Dreamcatcher will activate and reveal an instant cash prize of 8x, 88x, 888x or 8,888x Bet. The Dreamcatcher may shake during the game but it doesn't guarantee the Dreamcatcher feature. 3.5. Wolf Feature Pick Landing 3 Wolf Canyon Scatter symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 will trigger the Wolf Feature Pick . Pick a Wolf to reveal either the Free Spins or the Hold & Win Feature . If Hold & Win Feature is won, no Cash Moon symbols will be in view when the feature begins. There is a 60/40 probability in favour of the player winning the Free Spins Feature . 3.6. Free Spins When Free Spins are triggered you will be awarded either 8, 10 or 12 Free Spins with an extra 25, 50, 75 or 100 added Wild symbols. During Free Spins, Totem Wild symbols can become stacked on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. 3 Wolf Canyon Scatter symbols will re-trigger the initial amount of Free Spins. 3.7. Hold & Win Feature Triggered by 5 or more Cash Moon symbols landing anywhere in view, Hold & Win Feature will award 3 respins with all cash values remaining in place. Hold & Win Feature can also be won during the Wolf Feature Pick . During Hold & Win Feature only Cash Moon symbols are in play and every time a new one lands, the respins will reset to 3. Mini, Minor, and Major special Cash Moon symbols can land as part of the trigger or in Hold & Win Feature. Mini - 30x total bet. Minor - 100x total bet. Major - 500x total bet. Grand - 1000x total bet. If all the spaces are filled during the feature, the Grand prize will be awarded and added to the total win If the respins run down to zero, the feature will end and all cash values in view will be collected. Values displayed on the Cash Moon symbols are shown in the selected currency/coin. Cash Moon symbol values are random values from 1x to 500x times bet. Possible Values during Hold & Win Feature are as follows in times bet values: 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x, 25x, 30x, 40x, 50x, 100x, 250x, 375x, 500x. Note : History application keys.
