Spinning Dragons Game Features Free Spins Feature 2, 3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols trigger 2, 5, 10 or 20 free spins respectively. Free spins can be activated again during the feature. All bets and lines played are the same as the game that started the feature. Wild Multiplier All Wild substitution Wins Doubled! Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 94.84% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $94.84 of wins. Rules Total bet is 9 x coin size. 9 paylines are played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 7/2/2024
