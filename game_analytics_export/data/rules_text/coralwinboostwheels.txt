Winboost Wheels Feature • The Boost Wheel feature or Super Boost wheel feature is awarded when a or lands in view on reel 5 with a winning combination in view respectively. • The highlight will spin on the wheel and come to rest on one of the segments. The bonus shown in the segment is awarded and the segment is changed to a segment. • The highlight then spins again on the wheel to land on a wheel segment. If the segment is another bonus then this is awarded in addition to any bonuses already awarded. • The sequence continues until the highlight lands on a segment or the max win limit is reached. • All winnings are then calculated and transferred to the winnings meter and the feature ends. • The boost wheel feature and super boost wheel feature cannot be awarded together in a single spin. • Hold win and Respin: • Reels which are involved in the winning combination in view are held and remaining reels Respin to try and improve the win. • Win Boost: • One or more reels in view Respin to reveal a higher value winning combination. If the winning combination covers all 5 reels at any time then hold win and Respin segments change to become a Win Boost segment.
