Red Flag Fleet Base Game WILD is wild for all symbols except BONUS. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. All line pays are already multiplied by the bet multiplier. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. BONUS only appears on reels 2 through 6 in the base game. Free Spin Bonus 3 or more BONUS scattered on reels 2 through 6 trigger the Free Spin Bonus. 5 BONUS = 40 Free Spins + 50x Total Bet. 4 BONUS = 12 Free Spins + 10x Total Bet. 3 BONUS = 5 Free Spins. Free Spins are awarded from BONUS REELS, with the reels and lines 'flipped'. During the free spins, 2 to 3 reels will randomly change to WILD on every spin. During the Free Spins an alternate set of reels is used. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. During the free spins the bet multiplier and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the Bonus. Scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter pays are added to payline wins. During Free Spins, 3 or more scattered BONUS on reels 1 through 5 add additional free spins to the amount remaining. BONUS only appears on reels 1 through 5 in the free spins. Quikset Panel Clicking on the Total Bet meter opens and closes the Quikset panel. All values in the Quikset panel are equated with maximum paylines enabled. Click a value to immediately change the Total Bet value to that amount. The number of active paylines and the bet multiplier will be updated to reflect the Total Bet amount selected in the Quikset panel. Clicking on a value in the Quikset panel will not set the reels into motion. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is base bet multiplied by the bet multiplier. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
