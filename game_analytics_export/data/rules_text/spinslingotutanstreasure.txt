Tutan's Treasure Introduction Each game consists of 5 spins Gems landing on the reels are collected. Complete artefacts to win cash. How To Play Select you artefacts using the arrows. Choose a stake and press start to play. Collect Ingredients Gems on the reel are added to any recipes they apprear in. Complete an artefact to win the cash. Respins After 5 spins you can buy additional spins to collect more gems. The price is displayed on the button. Return To Player Each artefacts is paid once per game. malfunctions voids pays and plays. Wins are multiplied by the line bet. Each respin is priced with a margin of 5%. Prices are rounded up to the nearest whole value. Free spins are rounded up to the nearest whole value. Free spins are removed from the reels in extra spins.
