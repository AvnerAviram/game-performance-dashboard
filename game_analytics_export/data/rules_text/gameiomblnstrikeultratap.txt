Balloon Strike UltraTap Game Pays The game pays are as multiples of the bet: 500, 150, 50, 20, 10, 5, 3.5, 2, 1.5, 1, 0.8, 0.5, 0.3, and 0.2 Wins If the arrow does not hit and pop a balloon during a game round, the next round can be started before the arrow disappears. 3 to 19 prizes can be awarded when a winning play occurs. Free Games Bonus When 3 "Free Games" prizes are lit during a win, 10 free games are awarded. During the Free Games Bonus, when 3 "Free Games" prizes light up, an additional 10 free games are awarded. Multiplier Feature The multiplier feature may randomly activate by getting a win, awarding the 2x prize, the 3x prize, or both the 2x and 3x prizes. If the 2x multiplier is awarded and the 3x multiplier is not awarded, all credit wins are multiplied by 2x. If the 3x multiplier is awarded and the 2x multiplier is not awarded, all credit wins are multiplied by 3x. If both the 2x and 3x multipliers are awarded, all credit wins are multiplied by 6x. If the multiplier feature is active on a round where the Free Games Bonus is triggered, the number of free games awarded will be multiplied by the awarded multiplier(s). If the multiplier feature is active on a round where the Free Games Bonus is re-triggered, the number of free games awarded will be multiplied by the awarded multiplier(s). Rules Press the play button to play at your selected bet level. Press the spacebar key to play the game at the current selected bet level (if applicable). All pays are active at all times. All displayed wins have been multiplied by the bet multiplier. All wins will be added to total win. The displayed game RTP can be achieved at the minimum bet level. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 6/26/2025
