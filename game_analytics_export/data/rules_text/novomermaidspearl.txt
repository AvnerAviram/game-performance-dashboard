Mermaids Pearl Main Game Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along a win line. Winning combinations start on the first reel on the left and run along an active win line to the last reel on the right in the regular game. To win, the symbols in a winning combination must lie next to each other without any other symbols between them. Scatter symbols trigger a win, regardless of their position on the reels. The 'Mermaid' substitutes for all symbols except 'Treasure Chests' while doubling your line win. Free Games 3, 4 or 5 scattered 'Treasure Chests' trigger 40, 50 or 60 Bonus Games. Free Games can be re-triggered. Free Games are played at current bet. Top prize 10000 times bet per line times lines played Rules 'Mermaid' substitutes for all symbols except scattered 'Treasure Chest' and pays double. All pays are for wins of left to right except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding Scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins RTP The theoretical return to player for this game is 95.21% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
