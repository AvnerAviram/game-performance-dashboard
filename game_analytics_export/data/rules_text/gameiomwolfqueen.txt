Wolf Queen Wolf Queen is rich in golds, arctic and royal imagery, featuring the Wolf Queen and her loyal Wolf. During base-game play, three or more Bonus or Wild symbols in any position on reels one, two, or three triggers the Free Game Bonus, while additional symbols Bonus or Wild symbols on reels four or five can trigger an additional scatter pay. The scatter-triggered Free Game Bonus enables you to choose your reel layout, which determines your number of free spins up to 15. Additional free spins can be won again during the Free Game Bonus, so your wins can really add up! During the randomly awarded jackpot pick bonus, you are guaranteed to win one of four jackpots. Just touch the Xtreme Jackpot icons until you match three-of-a-kind to win a jackpot award. Wolf Queen features the Power XStream feature, which maximizes your number of ways to win by evaluating awards based on the number of symbols for big-win potential!
