Mega Meltdown GAME RULES MEGA PROGRESSIVE The MELTDOWN symbol is wild for any other symbol, except the MEGA symbol, to make winning combinations. The MEGA symbol on reel 1 is wild and substitutes for any symbol that also lands on the payline to make winning combinations. RESPIN BONUS Certain winning combinations may activate the Respin Bonus. Reels spin up to 5 times, paying different winning combinations. Progressive jackpots are only available on the last respin after the initial spin. Reels are weighted differently during Respin Bonus. PLAY 4TH CREDIT TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR PROGRESSIVE JACKPOTS When playing max bet, MEGA combinations pay the corresponding progressive jackpot prize instead of the line award. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION MENU Press to open and close game settings and view game information. BET Press to access Bet Settings without opening the Menu. AUTO SPIN Press to access Auto Spin settings without opening the Menu. SPIN Press to start playing with the current bet settings. Or, if applicable, press Space Bar to start playing with the current bet settings. Quick Stop: Press again to quick stop the reels. SOUND Press to enable or disable Sound. LOBBY Press to return to Lobby SETTINGS Press to access game Settings. BET SETTINGS Press to access Bet Settings. PAYS / HELP Press to view game pay and help information. Game will automatically complete after 24-48 hours if left in a feature. Payoffs on registered credits only. Line wins must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Different reels used at 3x and 4x bet. The odds of triggering a respin bonus are the same from 1x to 2x bet and vary from 2x to 4x bet. Only highest winner paid when centered on payline.
