Alice And The Mad Tea Party Base Game WILD FEATURE WILD is wild for all symbols except FEATURE. WILD only appears on reels 2-5. FEATURE only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Super Mad Respin Feature Description WILD LOGO LOGO + SPIN LOGO + END SPIN END 3 LOGO symbols appearing on reel 1 award the Super Mad Respin Feature. Select a Teapot above the reels. Teapots reveal one of the following: LOGO + SPIN awards up to 3 LOGO symbols that lock into random reel positions not already LOGO, followed by a respin of reels 2-5. LOGO + END awards up to 3 LOGO symbols that lock into random reel positions not already LOGO, followed by the end of the Super Mad Respin Feature. SPIN respins reels 2-5. END will end the Super Mad Respin Feature. Any WILD will change into LOGO and all LOGO are held for the duration of the Super Mad Respin Feature. Any LOGO that lands on reels 2-5 on the spin that triggers the Super Mad Respins will be held for the entire feature. Pays are evaluated only at the end of the feature. Any single Super Mad Respin Feature can have a maximum of 7 respins. Mad Tea Party Free Spin Feature Description FEATURE SPADE CLUB DIAMOND HEART 2X 2X + PICK AGAIN 3X RESPIN 3 scattered FEATURE symbols on reels 1, 3, and 5 trigger the Mad Tea Party Free Spin Feature. 6 free spins are awarded. Before each spin, select a Teapot above the reels. Teapots can reveal multipliers, or turn SPADE, CLUB, DIAMOND, or HEART wild for all symbols except FEATURE or award the Super Mad Respin Feature. The multipliers awarded during the free spin feature are 2X, 2X + PICK AGAIN and 3X. A Teapot that reveals RESPIN will trigger the Super Mad Respin Feature on that spin. Any single Super Mad Respin Feature can have a maximum of 7 respins. During free spins, 3 scattered FEATURE symbols add additional free spins to the amount of free spins remaining. Random Alice Features cannot be triggered during the Mad Tea Party Free Spin Feature. During Free Spins, the bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Alice Feature Description FEATURE WILD REEL Alice may appear during any spin that does not award another feature. Alice will change 1 to 5 of the reels into a WILD REEL. The WILD REEL is wild for all symbols except for FEATURE. Line pays are evaluated after the feature. Only one feature can be awarded on the same spin. FEATURE only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5. Cheshire Cat Feature Description FEATURE CAT WILD The Cheshire Cat may appear during any spin that does not award another Feature. Cheshire Cat will change 1 to 10 reel positions into CAT WILD. The CAT WILD is wild for all symbols except for FEATURE. Line pays are evaluated after the feature. Only one feature can be awarded on the same spin. FEATURE only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5. Queen Feature Description FEATURE SPADE CLUB DIAMOND HEART QUEEN WILD The Queen may appear after any spin that did not award another Feature. Pick a card to reveal a SPADE, CLUB, DIAMOND, or HEART and a monetary award of 50x or 250x times the line bet. Everywhere the chosen symbol appears on the reels will be replaced with QUEEN WILD. QUEEN WILD is wild for all symbols except FEATURE. Monetary awards appearing in the Queen Feature have already been multiplied by the line bet. Only one feature can be awarded on the same spin. FEATURE only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5. Caterpillar Feature Description The Caterpillar may appear during any spin that does not award another Feature. The Caterpillar awards a multiplier of 2x-10x or 15x. Only one feature can be awarded on the same spin. Mad Hatter Feature Description TEACUP MUFFIN DIAMOND WHITE RABBIT The Mad Hatter may appear after any spin that did not award another Feature. Pick a TEACUP appearing above the reels to reveal a MUFFIN, a DIAMOND, or the WHITE RABBIT. Accumulating MUFFIN increases the MUFFIN multiplier to 3x, 5x or 10x. MUFFIN multipliers apply only to cash prizes awarded in the Mad Hatter Feature. The DIAMOND awards 1x or 2x times the total bet and ends the Feature. The WHITE RABBIT awards 10x times the total bet and ends the Feature. Monetary awards appearing in the Mad Hatter Feature have already been multiplied by the total bet. Only one feature can be awarded on the same spin. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line plus the feature bet. The feature bet is an additional forced wager of 20 times the bet per line amount. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
