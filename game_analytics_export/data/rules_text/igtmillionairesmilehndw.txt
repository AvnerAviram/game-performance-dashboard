Millionaire's Mile: Hold And Win 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning way spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of ways 243 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 30 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes (Wild) Scatter symbols Yes (Hold & Win symbols) Bonus round Yes Free s pins No Instant win No RTP Normal game: 94.00% Bonus Buy: 94.25% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right, beginning with the far-left reel. All way win symbols must appear on consecutive reels. Wins on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. On the info bar, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except Hold & Win symbols to form winning combinations. Wild symbols only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4, and only one per reel. Wild symbols will multiply wins x2 and can multiply each other up to x8. All wins are multiplied by Wild multipliers. 3.2. Golden Vault Mystery Spin Any regular spin can randomly trigger a Golden Vault Mystery Spin . Stacked Mystery Symbols will spin through and any that land will turn into the same regular symbol at the end of the spin. 3.3. Millionaire's Mile Hold & Win Respins Landing 5 or more Hold & Win symbols on the reels will trigger Millionaire's Mile Hold & Win Respins . 3 Respins will be awarded and any Property symbol that lands will stick in place and reset the respins. Note : Possible win in this feature is x1 - x250. 3.4. Properties At the start of the Millionaire's Mile Hold & Win Respins , all Hold & Win symbols will turn into level 1 Properties . When a new Property lands, any adjacent ones (up, down, left, right) will be upgraded +1 level. When the Millionaire's Mile Hold & Win Respins end, the market trends will reveal the Properties prizes for each level: Green = level 1: x1, x2, x3, x4, x5 or Mini Awards. Purple = level 2: x6, x7, x10, x15, x20 or Major Awards. Red = level 3: x25, x30, x50, x100, x250 or Grand Awards. Note : The maximum possible level of the Properties is 3. 3.5. Awards In addition to the cash prizes, Awards can also be won by collecting Mini , Major or Grand Symbols . Mini awards 20 x bet; Major awards 100 x bet; Grand awards 1,000 x bet. Note : Player can win more than one Award in the same respin session. 3.6. Bonus Buy The Bonus Buy costs 70x Total Bet. A Bonus Buy spin is guaranteed to trigger the Millionaire's Mile Hold & Win Respins . 4. Other game buttons 4.1.
