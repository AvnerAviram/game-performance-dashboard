Wild Swarm A 97,03% RTP slot, the base game has a 5x4 reel with 20 win lines paying from left to right with the chance to win 3,100 x bet. Wilds substitute for all symbols except for the Scatter and Purple Flower symbol. Stacks of Honey Stacks of Honey Symbols which will pay through the reels randomly and these symbols have a significantly higher payout than any other symbol. Base Game Instant Prizes During the base game the Purple Flower may land, multiple hives will appear on the screen and the player selects one to reveal. Prizes can be instant wins up to 200 x bet or other amazing bonus features. Free Games 3 Hive Symbols on the reel will reward with 7 free spins, 4 Hive Symbols with 10 Free Spins and 5 Hive Symbols with 15 Free Spins. During free spins, bees are locked wilds and they are not collectable. Collectable Bees During the base game, Wild symbols may land with bees attached to them. If a bee is attached, it flies up to the hive at the top of the reels. The hive at the top of the reels will grow over time. Once the hive is at maximum size, each time a bee flies into it the player has the chance of triggering the Swarm Mode Feature. Swarm Mode Feature During the Swarm Mode, from 2 to 6 sticky Wilds with bees hit the reels throughout the Free Spins. At the end of the feature, 3 selectable hives will appear on the screen for the player to select a hive to reveal. The options are bet multipliers from 1 to 3 with a hive boost.
