Fu Lai Cai Lai Triple Festival GAMBLE FEATURE To enter the gamble game after a win, press the GAMBLE button. Select a Suit, Red/Black or Collect. Select RED/BLACK or a SUIT to gamble the total win. Win is doubled if red/black selection is correct. Win is quadrupled if suit selection is correct. Gamble feature is not available for any feature win. Win may be gambled up to 5 times. Gamble will not be offered for the wins awarded during the feature trigger.All wins cannot be gambled if the Jackpot is triggered. Gamble will not be offered during Autoplay. GAME RULES This game requires a minimum of 11 CREDITS to play. All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel. Each symbol appearing on a reel is counted once per winning combination. All wins are multiplied by coin value. Only the highest win is paid for the winning way pattern. Coinciding wins are added. wins multiplied by the coin value. Free Games wins are added to ways wins. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter wins are added to EVERY wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. EVERY WAY provides for 243 ways to win. EVERY wins are winning combinations that appear in any position on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel, except scatters.
