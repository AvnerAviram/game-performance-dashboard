Raging Dragons 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning way spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of ways 243 Number of coins 20 Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free S pins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.00 % The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins - player input is required. On the info bar, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). To toggle between credit or your real currency, click in the BALANCE area (Available only for specific jurisdictions). 2.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Scatter symbols, to form winning combinations. Only one Wild symbol can land per spin in base game. Wild can multiply all wins up to x3. Wild symbols can land on reels 2,3 and 4. 3.2. Scatter symbol Landing 3 ore more Scatter symbols on the reels will trigger Free Spins . 3.3. Lucky Envelope Triggered randomly on any paid spin, the red envelope will open and reveal an instant cash prize of 8x, 88x, 888x or 8,888x Bet. The red letter may shake during the game but it doesn't guarantee the Lucky Envelope feature. 3.4. Free Spins Landing 3 ore more Scatter symbols on the reels will trigger Free Spins and cash prize, according to the paytable. At the starting of the Free Spins you get to choose between: 25 Free Spins with Wild Multipliers of x2, x3 or x5 20 Free Spins with Wild Multipliers of x3, x5 or x8 15 Free Spins with Wild Multipliers of x5, x8 or x10 13 Free Spins with Wild Multipliers of x8, x10 or x15 10 Free Spins with Wild Multipliers of x10, x15 or x30 Mystery Choice - can award any combination of Free Spins (10, 13, 15, 20, 25) & Wild Multipliers ( x2, x3, x5 / x3, x5, x8 /x5, x8, x10 / x8, x10, x15 / x10, x15, x30) from the above choices (25 possible outcomes). During Free Spins , Wild symbols can land on all 5 reels and will multiply any wins they are part of, with random multiplier values as above for each Free Spins option. During Free Spins , landing one ore more Scatter symbols on the reels will award an additional Free Spins according to the paytable. freespins_trigger= Free Spins FS_CHOICE= Free Spin option selected FS with mult= Free Spins with multiplier random prize= Lucky Envelope 4. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play. Loss limit : click + or - to select the value of the loss limit; Stop if a win reaches : click + or - to select the value of the maximum win; Stop if JACKPOT is won (only for games with Progressive Jackpots): to enable this setting, tick the checkbox ; to disable it, remove the tick. Click the ACTIVATE button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature. Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time. 5.2.
