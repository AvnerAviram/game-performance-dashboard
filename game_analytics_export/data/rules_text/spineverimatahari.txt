Mata Hari STACKS Wild and Mata Hari symbols appear in stacks on all reels. Wild is Wild for all symbols except Free Spin. FREE SPIN BONUS 3 or more Free Spin symbols landing scattered on any reels triggers the Free Spin Bonus and award 10X total bet and 20 free spins. Four symbols awards 5X total bet and 12 free spins. Three symbols awards 2X total bet and 7 free spins. During free spins, the Mata Hari symbols is Wild for all symbols except Free Spin. When any portion of the Mata Hari symbol lands on a reel, it will NudgeTM to display a full stack on the reel. Retrigger credit prizes and additional spins are the same as the initial trigger prizes. During free spins, an alternate set of reels is used. Maximum number of free spins available is 70.
