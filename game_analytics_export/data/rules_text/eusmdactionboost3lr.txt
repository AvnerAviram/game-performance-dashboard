3 Lucky Rainbows Action Boost About this Game This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols land in different combinations. Winning combinations are highlighted on the reels and a payout is awarded. Bet Rules Each spin played costs a total bet amount. The initial bet is the current total bet pided by the payline cost of 20. The total bet is based on the initial bet placed, multiplied by the cost of the paylines played. Payline Rules This game includes 20 - 40 paylines. The number of paylines played is fixed. A payline is a pattern of adjacent symbol positions that runs in a line across the reels. A win is awarded when matching symbols land adjacent to each other on a payline. Payline wins run from left to right, starting from the first slot reel. Payout Rules Payline win payouts are based on the initial bet placed. The payout value is based on the winning combination formed. The total payout is the sum of all payline wins resulting from a spin. If multiple winning combinations land on a single payline, only the highest win is awarded. Only the highest winning combination is awarded per symbol combination. The Paytable displays the payout for each symbol combination based on the current bet. Autoplay This feature is available in base game only. Autoplay allows a selected number of spins to play out automatically. Spins are played at the current selected bet. The number of spins remaining in the Autoplay session is displayed in the game. Spins: Sets the number of spins for the Autoplay session. Autoplay ends when these spins have played out. Loss Limit: Sets a loss limit for the Autoplay session. Autoplay ends if the balance decreases by this amount. Win Limit: Sets a win limit for the Autoplay session. Autoplay ends if a single win reaches this amount. Total Bet: Sets the current bet value. Wild Symbols Hat symbols are Wild symbols. Wild symbols can substitute for all symbols except Coin symbols. Wild symbols can substitute for other symbols to complete winning combinations. Wild symbols can create their own winning combination when landing in a payline combination. Rainbow Spin Feature Whenever a Pot of Gold Coin appears there is a chance for the Rainbow Spin to be triggered. Each Pot of Gold coin collects to the Pot of Gold. All Rainbows are active when the Rainbow Spin feature is triggered. One Rainbow Spin is awarded. Whenever one or more coins land on rainbows, Action Boost™ Free Spins are triggered. The combination of coins that land determines the Action Boost™ Free Spins awarded. The Rainbow Spin can also award MEGA, MAJOR, MINOR or MINI. Action Boost™ Free Spins Whenever one or more Coins appear there is a chance for Action Boost™ Free Spins to be triggered. Each Coin collects to the bag of the same color above the reels. The combination of Coins that collect when the Action Boost™ Free Spins are triggered determines the Action Boost™ awarded. In the Action Boost™ Free Spins, active rainbows in the Rainbow Spin feature are determined by the Action Boost™ awarded. Bet multiplier is the same as the game that triggered the feature. Green Coin Action Boost™ 5 Free Spins are awarded. Every Gold Coin that appears during this feature awards a bonus credit prize or Rainbow Spin. One Rainbow Spin is awarded for each Gold Coin awarding a Rainbow Spin. The Rainbow Spin will award a bonus credit prize or upgrade a symbol to Clover for the remaining Free Spins. If a symbol upgrade is awarded, symbols will upgrade before any wins are paid. 40 lines are played during this feature. All reels grow to a height of 6 symbol positions. Any reel with a Wild symbol appearing is nudged so that all positions on that reel contain Wild symbols. All wins are paid after the reels have been nudged. Red Coin Action Boost™ 10 free spins are awarded. Every Gold Coin that appears during this feature awards a shamrock, bonus credit prize or Rainbow Spin. One Rainbow Spin is awarded for each Gold Coin awarding a Rainbow Spin. The Rainbow Spin will award shamrocks, a bonus credit prize or a jackpot boost. A jackpot boost will boost the respective jackpot value for the remaining Free Spins. Collect 6 Red Shamrocks to win the MEGA jackpot. Collect 5 Purple Shamrocks to win the MAJOR jackpot. Collect 4 Blue Shamrocks to win the MINOR jackpot. Collect 3 Green Shamrocks to win the MINI jackpot. If all shamrock prizes for a jackpot are collected, the corresponding jackpot is awarded and the collected shamrocks for that jackpot are reset. Purple Coin Action Boost™ 5 free spins are awarded. Every Gold Coin that appears during this feature awards a bonus credit prize, 1 free spin, 2 free spins or Rainbow Spin. One Rainbow Spin is awarded for each Gold Coin awarding a Rainbow Spin The Rainbow Spin will award a bonus credit prize, 1 free spin, 2 free spins or 3 free spins. Every spin a random multiplier of 2, 3 or 5 is awarded. Line wins and bonus credit prizes are multiplied by the random multiplier. Multiple Coin Action Boost™ Free spins are played with all Action Boost™ triggered on a single base spin. If a Red Coin Action Boost™ is triggered then 10 Free Spins are awarded, otherwise 5 Free Spins are awarded. The random multiplier from the Purple Coin Action Boost™ applies to all line wins and bonus credit prizes, and does not apply to jackpots. Game Terminology The following are translations of game terms that may appear in English in the game. MEGA = MEGA MAJOR = MAJOR MINOR = MINOR MINI = MINI Free Games A Free Games offer is a number of free game spins awarded to a player. Each offer has a unique reference number. The bet configuration is set by the game managers and cannot be changed. Wins are added to the cash or bonus balance, depending on the offer. An offer can be played now, played later, or discarded. Select Play Now to begin playing a Free Games offer. Only one Free Game offer can be played at a time. Select Play Later to postpone the offer. A postponed offer will be available again when re-entering the game. Select the Bin icon to discard the Free Games. A discarded offer can only be re-opened by contacting the game managers and providing the reference number. When an offer is complete, discarded, or expired the next available offer is displayed. Each offer has an expiry date and time; if an offer is not played before this time, it will no longer be available. Select the Information icon to view bet information and the offer expiry date and time. Additional Information Some settings and features may not be available in this game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Any changes to game rules will be conducted in accordance with regulatory requirements. In the event of a disconnection, the final game state is displayed on return to the game. The balance is updated with the amount won or lost. The results of the last game played are displayed. If a request does not reach the server before disconnection, the results of the previous game played are displayed. Any features in progress that require interaction or selection can be continued. Return to Player The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by piding the total possible payouts by the total possible outcomes that are available within the game. Values reflect the maximum expected return and do not change.
