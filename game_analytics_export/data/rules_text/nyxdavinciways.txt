Da Vinci Ways RTP 96.5% GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Select Bet Multiplier Click the Spin button to spin the reel. This game is played at 243 ways Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Free Games Bonus symbol. Wild Symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols fall in from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. The sequence repeats until no new winning combinations are created. Free Games Bonus Rules 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 10 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically pay on the same ways at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards 5 additional free games.
