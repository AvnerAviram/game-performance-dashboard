Road to Riches Free Spins Game Introduction Road to Riches Freespins is a 5 reel game with 3 symbols in view for each reel. How to play Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Win lines The default win line selection is 10 win lines. The win lines are not adjustable in this game. Start the game Press SPIN to initiate the game. Help All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. FREE SPINS BONUS BONUS SYMBOL ON REELS 1, 3 AND 5 INITIATES THE FREE SPIN BONUS. BONUS SYMBOLS ARE STACKED ON THE FIRST REEL AND CAN AWARD 10, 15 OR 20 FREE SPINS. THIS ALSO APPLIES TO THE FREE SPIN BONUS GAME PLAY AS WELL, MULTIPLE EXTRA SPINS CAN BE AWARDED FOR A RETRIGGER DEPENDING ON HOW MANY SYMBOLS ARE IN VIEW ON THE FIRST REEL. DURING THE FREESPINS BONUS, AN ALTERNATIVE SET OF REELS ARE USED. THE BET PER LINE REMAINS THE SAME AS THE SPIN THAT TRIGGERED THE FREE SPINS BONUS. ALL FREE SPIN GAMES USE THE SAME AWARDS AS THE BASE GAME. THE BONUS ENDS WHEN 0 FREE SPINS REMAIN OR AFTER 999 FREE SPINS HAVE BEEN PLAYED. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to player (RTP) is 95.17%. Last modification date: 12/15/2020
