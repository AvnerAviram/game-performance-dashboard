Progressive Jackpots The Sapphire Wheel Bonus or Ruby Wheel Bonus must be accessed in order to have a chance of winning 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. The 4 Progressive Jackpots are: Mini, Minor, Major and Mega. If the Wheel stops on the Mini progressive, the value of the Mini progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Minor progressive, the value of the Minor progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Major progressive, the value of the Major progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Mega progressive, the value of the Mega progressive is won. It is not technically possible to have two simultaneous wins of the same Progressive Jackpot. There are separate Progressive Jackpots per bet multiplier. Contributions made to Progressive Jackpots at a certain bet multiplier are added to Progressive Jackpots that can only be achieved by making a bet at that same bet multiplier. The following contributions are made to Progressive Jackpots on each base game spin: .4% of the bet is contributed to the Mini Progressive Jackpot. .3% of the bet is contributed to the Minor Progressive Jackpot. .25% of the bet is contributed to the Major Progressive Jackpot. .2% of the bet is contributed to the Mega Progressive Jackpot.
