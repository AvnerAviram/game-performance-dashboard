Silver Lux: Coin Stacks Main Game The game is played for 10 credits and you can select the value of a credit. Winning combinations will be awarded on 5 win lines. The “Coin Stacks” Wild Multiplier-symbols will help you to increase your chances in winning combinations. They do not only substitute for most other symbols but will multiply your winnings as well. Coin Stacks Wild Multiplier-symbols can have multipliers of 2x, 3x and 5x. Game Features Hold & Spin Feature 3 scattered Coin-symbols will drag you into the exciting Hold & Spin Feature. The values on the triggering Coin-symbols will be added and the result is your initial feature win. The reel will turn blue, except for the Coin-symbols, and 3 Free Spins are offered which are presented on top of the reel window. Not only will each value on a Coin-symbol which is spun in added to your winnings but it will reset the Free Spins counter to 3 as well. The feature ends when no Free Spins are left. Coin Stacks Feature Each Coin symbol landing on the reels can trigger the Coin Stacks Feature. The stacks of coins will collapse and the reels will spin to a 3 Coin-symbol scatter combination and the Hold & Spin Feature will start but now with even bigger prizes! Silver Lux Linked Progressive Jackpot At the top of the game two progressive jackpots are presented, a Major Jackpot and a Grand Jackpot. Both jackpots are linked to the jackpots of other Silver Lux games. The jackpots can be won in the Hold & Spin Feature. A Coin-symbol with a “grand” label and a Coin-symbol with a “major” label can appear. When this happens the respective jackpot is yours!
