Bad Bunny Money Game Rules Minimum bet is 25 credits, 1 credit wagered per line. Line wins for each line are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Line wins pay from the left to right in adjacent reels only, starting with the leftmost reel. Only the highest line win is paid on each active pay line. Free Spins and InstaSpins will be played at the bet amount of the initiating spin. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Incomplete games will automatically complete after 21 days. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before playing. Instaspins Feature can appear OVERLAID on top of any non-bonus symbols. in a winning combination activates InstaSpins feature for 3 free spins. Each in each winning combination adds 1x multiplier to the InstaSpins feature. cannot appear during the InstaSpins feature. Pay amounts collected from triggering spin and 3 free spins are multiplied by the final InstaSpins multiplier. InstaSpins feature ends when all 3 free spins are complete and the payout awarded. Free Spins Feature activates a bonus game with 10 free spins and a 1x multiplier. acts as a Scatter symbol and may appear on any spot in reels 2, 3, and 4 only. Each in a winning combination awards 1x multiplier to the Free Spins Feature. Pay amounts collected are multiplied by the final Free Spin multiplier and paid out. Free Spins feature ends when all 10 free spins are played and any payouts awarded. In the case where Free Spins feature and InstaSpins feature are triggered in the same spin, Free Spins feature will complete first; it will then complete the InstaSpins feature with credits bet and lines played as triggered.
