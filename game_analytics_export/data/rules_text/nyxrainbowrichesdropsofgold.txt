Rainbow Riches Drops of Gold How to Play Game Introduction This is a 5 reel game which features Free Spins and a new Drops of Gold bonus. The reels have up to 200 win lines. 3 or more BONUS symbols awards Free Spins. Free Spins Bonus Free Spins are awarded when 3 or more BONUS symbols are in view at the end of a spin. 3 BONUS symbols in view awards 8 Free Spins. Each additional BONUS symbol in view awards an extra 4 Free Spins. Bonus symbols are present in Free Spins and can re-trigger as described above. Drops of Gold Bonus If there is a "Drops of Gold" symbol in view at the end of a spin, all pot symbols in the drop zone view will fall on to the lower reel. Silver pot symbols are wild and substitute for all symbols except the BONUS symbol. Gold pot symbols are wild and substitute for all symbols. Bonus symbols are present in Free Spins and can re-trigger as described above. A Big Bet game initially consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. Additional benefits awarded include: The Rainbow of pot symbols upgrades to a Super Rainbow conaining more pots. The 'Drops of Gold' symbol extends to double height. Lower paying symbols are removed from the base game reels. More win lines. Additional information can be viewed in game by pressing the "?" button Select your stake- To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 50-200 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button.
