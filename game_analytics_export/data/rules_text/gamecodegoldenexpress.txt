Each symbol can be used only once per winning ways-combination. Highest win only for each winning combination. GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE wins, if any, are added to the base game wins. Displayed MEGA, MAJOR and MINOR jackpot prizes are proportional to the selected bet and shown in currency. Paytable values are displayed for the currently selected bet. All wins are shown in currency. The game outcome is random and cannot be influenced by the actions of the player. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. WILD SYMBOLS There are two types of WILD symbols in the Game: WILD and 2X WILD. WILD appears during the base game on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. 2X WILD appears during the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Each WILD and 2X WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols that are part of the winning combination, except any SCATTER symbols and other WILD symbols. Multiple 2X WILD Symbols can appear in a single GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE spin, following their appearance rules. The total win multiplier is the product of all 2X WILD multipliers in the winning combination. SCATTER SYMBOLS SCATTER WITH CREDIT PRIZE may appear on all reels during the base game and the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE, forming a SCATTER WINNING COMBINATION. When SCATTER WITH CREDIT PRIZE symbols land on three or more consecutive reels, starting from the leftmost reel, the sum of those SCATTER values is awarded and added to the base game win. If the sequence of consecutive reels is broken, only the SCATTERs in the uninterrupted sequence are summed and awarded. For example, if SCATTERS appear on reels 1, 2, 3, and 5, only the SCATTERS on reels 1, 2, and 3 will be awarded. The SCATTER WINNING COMBINATION logic applies the same way during the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE. SCATTER WITH MEGA, SCATTER WITH MAJOR, SCATTER WITH MINOR, SCATTER WITH 2X MULTIPLIER, SCATTER WITH 3X MULTIPLIER, and SCATTER WITH 5X MULTIPLIER follow their specific rules, but must be part of a valid SCATTER WINNING COMBINATION to activate their respective effects. GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE The GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE consists of 8 initial bonus spins. Additional modifiers may occur depending on the triggering SCATTERS. During the base game, any SCATTER WITH CREDIT PRIZE symbol may be overlaid with a green SCATTER WITH MULTIPLIERS COIN, yellow SCATTER WITH JACKPOTS COIN or red SCATTER WITH EXTRA SPINS COIN. In such cases, the underlying SCATTER WITH CREDIT PRIZE symbol is revealed once the spin ends. It is possible to land two or more yellow SCATTER WITH JACKPOTS COIN symbols or green SCATTER WITH MULTIPLIERS COIN symbols during a single base game spin, but only one red SCATTER WITH EXTRA SPINS COIN symbol may appear per spin. Landing a single green, yellow, or red SCATTER COIN—or any combination of these—may randomly trigger the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE, with different modifications depending on the coins. However, neither individual SCATTER COINS nor combinations guarantee triggering of the feature. Each type of SCATTER COIN may appear on all reels during the base game only. The yellow, green, and red trains displayed above the reels are visual representations only and do not reflect actual chances of triggering the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE. Depending on the triggering SCATTER COINS, the feature may include one, two, or all three of the following modifiers: Multipliers Jackpots Extra Spins For example: If only the green SCATTER WITH MULTIPLIERS COIN is present, the player receives Multiplier modifier added to the 8 initial bonus spins. If the feature is triggered by the green SCATTER WITH MULTIPLIERS COIN and yellow SCATTER WITH JACKPOTS COIN, the player receives Multiplier modifier and Jackpot scatter symbols added to the 8 initial bonus spins. If the feature is triggered by green, yellow, and red coins together, it will include all three modifiers: Multipliers, Jackpots, and Extra Spins. An alternate set of reels is used during the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE. The feature ends when no bonus spins remain. All wins collected during the feature are summed and presented as the total feature win and added to the base game win, if any. EXTRA SPINS Landing a red SCATTER WITH EXTRA SPINS COIN on any reel during the base game may increase the number of Extra Spins for the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE by +1. The Extra Spins counter starts at a base value of 3 and can increase up to a maximum of 10. Extra Spins are added on top of the 8 initial bonus spins, for the maximum of 18. The current value is displayed on the RED TRAIN meter above the reels. The player is awarded a number of Extra Spins equal to the value displayed on the RED TRAIN meter. If the red coin triggers the feature, an additional Extra Spin is added, if the maximum has not been reached. After a feature that included Extra Spins ends, the meter resets to 3. Each bet level maintains a separate Extra Spins count. Jackpot Values: MEGA = 250 × base bet MAJOR = 50 × base bet MINOR = 10 × base bet MULTIPLIERS When the green SCATTER WITH MULTIPLIERS COIN triggers the feature, the GOLDEN EXPRESS BONUS SPINS FEATURE includes: SCATTER WITH 2X MULTIPLIER SCATTER WITH 3X MULTIPLIER SCATTER WITH 5X MULTIPLIER These symbols may appear in any bonus spin on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. If the feature is triggered by both the green MULTIPLIER COIN and yellow JACKPOTS COIN, any MULTIPLIER that forms a SCATTER WINNING COMBINATION with any JACKPOT will also multiply the corresponding jackpot value.
