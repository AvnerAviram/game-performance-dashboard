Only one Progress Wild can land at a time, and it can only appear in the main game if there are empty spots remaining. Progress Blocks Five progress blocks are active, each linked to a specific feature. Collecting a star fills a block and activates its feature. The Chicken Feature requires 6 stars, Sheep needs 5, Pig needs 4, Cow needs 3, and Multiplier needs 2. Once a feature is completed, it can trigger randomly, with an increased chance of activation during Free Spins. Chicken Feature When activated, a random number of 1x1 Chicken Wilds will appear on the reels. Sheep Feature When activated, a 2x2 Sheep Wild will appear on the reels. Pig Feature When activated, a 3x3 Pig Wild will appear on the reels. Cow Feature When activated, a 4x4 Cow Wild will appear on the reel. Multiplier When activated, a x2 Multiplier applies to all wins until the chain reaction ends. On the second activation, a x5 Multiplier is awarded instead. Once fully unlocked, the Multiplier will be either x2 or x5. Free Spins Landing 3 Scatters awards 10 free spins. During Free Spins, any feature can activate randomly.
