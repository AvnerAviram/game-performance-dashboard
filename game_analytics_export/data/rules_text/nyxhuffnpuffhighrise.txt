BASE GAME Before the base game reel spin initiates, a base game reel set is chosen from multiple possible reel sets. HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT, VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT, HAZARD HARD HAT and SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbols are WILD for HARD HAT symbol. Before the base game reel spin initiates, a number of positions may be randomly replaced with HARD HAT, HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT and/or VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbols. The base game starts with the bottom 3 rows of the grid active. If HAZARD HARD HAT symbol lands on an active row during the base game, then 1-3 additional rows will become active. HAZARD HARD HAT symbol only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 during the base game. During the base game, 1-5 HARD HAT, HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT, VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT and/or SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbols may be placed on active rows. HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT, VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbols do not appear on reel 4 during the base game, except via placement. SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbol only appears via placement. GIRDER TRIGGER If 1-5 scatters land, then the difference needed to trigger the feature with 6 scatters will be added to the reels. During the base game if 5 or 6 scatters except SUPER SAW HARD HAT land, a SUPER SAW HARD HAT may be thrown onto the reels. FREE SPINS FEATURE Any combination of SCATTER symbols of 6 or more in active rows, except the SUPER SAW HARD HAT during the base game, triggers the FREE SPINS FEATURE and awards 6 free spins. Before the FREE SPINS FEATURE begins, STRAW FRAME is added to the position of each triggering SCATTER symbol. Before the FREE SPINS FEATURE begins and during each free spin, every HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT symbol in active rows generates a saw that moves left/right and every VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbol in active rows generates a saw that moves up/down until it exits the active grid. Each position without BRICK FRAME that a saw touches (excluding the initial position) will be evaluated as if HARD HAT symbol had landed there during the FREE SPINS FEATURE, one at a time. Saws will be generated only after all other frame updates have occurred. During free spin, before each reel spin initiates, a number of positions may be randomly replaced with HARD HAT, HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT, VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT or HAZARD HARD HAT symbols. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, if HAZARD HARD HAT symbol lands on an active row, then 1 or more additional rows will become active and remain active for the remainder of the FREE SPINS FEATURE. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, if 3 or more SCATTER symbols land on active rows then 1 additional free spin is awarded. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT, VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbols and HAZARD HARD HAT symbol only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, WILD symbol only appears on reels 2 and 3. An alternate set of reels is used during the FREE SPINS FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbol does not appear. Prizes are awarded as shown. SUPER SAW FREE SPINS Any combination of SCATTER symbols of 6 or more in active rows (with SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbol) during the base game triggers the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS and awards 6 free spins. Before the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS begin, each HARD HAT symbol, HAZARD HARD HAT symbol and SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbol in active rows is replaced with HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT and/or VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbol. Before the free spins begin, STRAW FRAME is added to the position of each triggering SCATTER symbol. Before the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS begins and during each free spin, every HORIZONTAL SAW HARD HAT symbol in active rows generates a saw that moves left/right and every VERTICAL SAW HARD HAT symbol in active rows generates a saw that moves up/down until it exits the active grid. Each position without BRICK FRAME that a saw touches (excluding the initial position) will be evaluated as if HARD HAT symbol had landed there during the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS, one at a time. Saws will be generated only after all other frame updates have occurred. During free spin, before each reel spin initiates, a number of positions may be randomly replaced with HARD HAT symbol, SAW HARD HAT symbol, or HAZARD HARD HAT symbol. During the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS, if HAZARD HARD HAT symbol lands on an active row, then 1 or more additional rows will become active and remain active for the remainder of SUPER SAW FREE SPINS. During the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS, if 3 or more SAW HARD HAT and/or HAZARD HARD HAT symbols land on active rows then 1 additional free spin is awarded. During the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS, WILD symbol only appears on reels 2-4. An alternate set of reels is used during the SUPER SAW FREE SPINS. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except SUPER SAW HARD HAT symbol does not appear. Prizes are awarded as shown.
