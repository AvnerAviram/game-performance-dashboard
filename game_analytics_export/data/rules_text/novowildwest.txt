Wild West Rules Howdy! Don your Stetson hat, put on your boots and spur your horse: ride into the sunset where the winnings are! In Wild West™ you paint the prairie red on one main and three auxiliary reel sets. If your gun is triggered you take home the Gunshot-Bonus - and your leather bags will be filled with gold nuggets! The aim with Wild West™is to land five matching symbols side by side along ten win lines. The winning patterns run from left to right and from right to left. Bonus Symbol The "Wild West" symbol serves as a Wild symbol, which substitutes for any basic symbols in the game and increases you winning chances. If it lands on the main reel set its position is transferred to the three smaller reel sets. Gunshot Bonus The Gunshot-Bonus can be triggered after every successful spin: a gun fires a shot at one or several reel sets with winnings and multiplies them (up to ten-times). Each reel set features its very own multiplier! Hi Roller You can activate Hi-Roller-Mode any time you like: Just click on the red "Hi Roller" button at the bottom of the screen. You can choose between two variants now - the Silver (five-time stakes) or the Gold Mode (ten-times stake). For each mode you receive three spins with Wild symbols that are frozen in their positions.
