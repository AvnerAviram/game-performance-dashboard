Jackpot Cherries Game Overview Jackpot Cherries is a three reel, 1 win line, simple play arcade style slot with holds and nudges. 3 Nudges may be awarded, at random, at the end of any non-winning game. The player may nudge the reels manually or use the auto nudge function. If the player doesn’t find the best win available, the auto nudge will kick in anyway and re-nudge the reels to the win. When no win is available from nudges, there’s a chance a winning re-spin may be offered. All wins can hold up to 9 times, giving a maximum payout of x250 per game. Formats This document provides information on Jackpot Cherries for the following game formats: Flash HTML5 tablet HTML5 phone The game Playing the game Bet value Select the total bet value. Start button Flash game: Press the start button to spin the reels. Tablet and phone games: Press the start button to spin the reels. The tablet game has the additional option to use thumb control start buttons – these buttons can be turned on / off by pressing the tool button and selecting the on / off toggle button. Auto button Flash game: Pressing the auto button will provide various auto play options. Once the desired options have been selected, press the begin button to start auto play. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Tablet game: Pressing the auto button will provide the option to select 10, 25 or 50 auto spins. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Phone game: Pressing the auto button will start 10 auto spins. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Nudges 3 nudges may be offered, at random, at the end of any non-winning spin. Flash game: Press the flashing nudge buttons to nudge the reels forward one symbol at a time. Tablet and phone games: Press the flashing reels to nudge them forward one symbol at a time. Auto nudge When lit, press auto nudge to automatically nudge to the best available position (the auto nudge button replaces the start button when active). During nudges, if the best available winning combination is missed, the auto nudge feature will kick in. The auto nudge feature resets the reels to the position before the nudges were offered and then automatically nudges them to the best available winning combination. Super spin feature The super spin feature may be offered, at random, after a losing nudge. When offered, the hold buttons will flash and the reels will automatically spin to a win. Holds A hold, or series of holds, may be offered, at random, after any win. When offered, the reels are held automatically and the win is added to the initial win. The hold chance is awarded in the same game as the original win, and as such, no credit is taken. The maximum number of possible holds is nine. If this is reached, no more holds will be offered. Displays Various displays are used to show important information to the player during the game. On phone, some of these displays appear as pop-ups, only when needed. Information display and Alphanumeric display At different points during game play these will show information about the game. Bet display This displays the total bet value charged per game. Win display At different points during game play this will display: Total win line payouts. Paid display This displays the total paid for a complete bet. Game views The game can be played in different views, as follows: Flash: Full cabinet view. Close-up view of the reels only. Press the Real 2 Reel button to toggle between the views. Tablet and phone: Both tablet and phone games can be played in landscape and portrait view. Payouts Win line payouts When a winning line is obtained: The winning symbols will be highlighted. The win value will be shown. Any holds awarded will be advertised. The total amount won will be shown. Payout values Jackpot Cherries has a maximum payout of 250 x the total bet value, per game. The RTP is expressed as the percentage returned to players in winnings of the total amount staked, e.g. if £1,000,000 was staked and £960,000 was returned, the RTP of the game for that period of play would be 96%. The expected RTP for this game is 95.01%. The actual RTP during a playing session can vary widely, in either direction, from the theoretical return.
