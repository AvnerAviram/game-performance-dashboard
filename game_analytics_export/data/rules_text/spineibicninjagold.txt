Ninja Gold Basic Game Rules Game is always played at 30 lines with a minimum bet of 30 credits. Line pays are on selected lines only on consecutive reels starting from the leftmost reel to the right. Only the highest win pay per played line. Wins on all lines are added. Only the highest scatter win pays. Scatter wins are multiplied by the wins played times the bet per line. Scatter wins are added to the line wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wild substitutes all symbol except bonus. All line wins substituted with Wild are 2X, except Wild line pays. All wins are shown in credits. Payoffs on registered credits only. Free Game Feature ANY 3,4,5 BONUS TRIGGER THE BONUS FEATURE. THE BONUS FEATURE BEGINS WITH A BONUS PICK GAME OF 10 SCROLLS. PLAYER KEEPS PICKING SCROLLS UNTIL BEGIN SCROLL IS PICKED. THERE ARE 7 DIFFERENT TYPE OF SCROLLS. EACH JACKPOT WILL HAVE A LAST COUNTDOWN METER THAT STARTS AT 18. MAXIMUM NUMBER OF FREE GAMES IS 30. WHEN THE FREE GAMES END, EACH ACTIVATED JACKPOT WILL BE MULTIPLIED BY ITS JACKPOT MULTIPLIER AND WILL BE ADDED TO THE TOTAL WIN. FREE GAMES CANOT BE RETRIGGERED. DIFFERENT REELS ARE USED DURING THE FREE GAMES. CREDITS BET AND LINES PLAYED ARE THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE BONUS FEATURE. Autoplay Press Auto to play the game for a selected number of wagers automatically. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 4/3/2019
