Amazon Kingdom About this Game Welcome to Amazon Kingdom. This is a 5 reel, 4 row video slot game. This game has 30 lines. It features Free Spins and Super Free Spins with multipliers, Queen's Bonus, Wild Scrolls, and Bonus Symbols. General Line wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win is paid per line. Multiple wins can be awarded per spin. It is not possible to trigger Queen's Bonus within Free Spins and Super Free Spins. Queen's Bonus and Free Spins of any type cannot trigger simultaneously.The Paytable displays the payout for each symbol combination based on the current bet. Wild Symbols Wild Symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins Symbols and Bonus Symbols. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Wild symbols can create their own winning combination when landing in a payline combination. Bonus Symbols Collection Bonus Symbols appear in the base game. Each reel has a Bonus Holder located above it. Each Bonus Symbol that lands is added to its respective Bonus Holder and also collected in the Queen's Bonus area. Collect 3 Bonus Symbols to activate the Wild Scrolls feature on that reel. Bonus Symbols do not appear on active Wild Scrolls reels. Bonus Holders, Wild Scrolls, and remaining reels persist for each bet level. Collecting 1 or more Bonus Symbols gives a random chance of triggering the Queen's Bonus feature. Only the Bonus Symbols collected on the current spin contribute to the chance of triggering the Queen's Bonus. The graphical collection pot in the game is purely for visual entertainment purposes and as such does not influence awarding the Queen's Bonus. Wild Scrolls Activate the Wild Scrolls in the base game, Free Spins, and Super Free Spins. Wild Scrolls add expanding sticky Wilds to the reel while active. When Wild Scrolls is activated in the base game, on the next spin a Wild Scroll Symbol is placed on the top position of the triggering reel. On every subsequent spin, the Wild Scroll Symbol expands down 1 position on the reel. Once the Wild Scroll Symbol has expanded to the bottom position, the feature ends and the Wild Scroll is removed on the following spin. Free Spins Symbols are visible through the Wild Scrolls. Free Spins, Super Free Spins and Queen's Bonus can be triggered during Wild Scrolls. Queen's Bonus There are 4 types of Bonus coins. Each Bonus type corresponds to a cash prize. In total, there are 12 Bonus coins. The cash prizes are: 15x, 40x, 200x, and 5000x bet. Match 3 of the same Bonus coin type to win the corresponding cash prize according to the PAYTABLE. Free Spins Free Spins symbols appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game. Land 3 Free Spins Symbols to trigger an indefinite number of Free Spins. Free Spins has a multiplier of x2. Every inpidual win in Free Spins is multiplied by the multiplier. At the start of Free Spins, Wild Scrolls are active on reel 3, starting from the top reel position. Land a regular Wild on any reel to trigger Wild Scrolls. Wild Scrolls start from the same reel position as the triggering Wild. Regular Wilds do not appear on active Wild Scroll reels. The feature ends when Wild Scrolls are no longer present on the visible reel area. All bets and lines played are the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Super Free Spins Collection Land 2 Free Spins Symbols in the base game to add 1 point to the Super Free Spins Upgrade Meter. Landing more than 2 Free Spins Symbols will not contribute any points to the Super Free Spins Upgrade Meter. Collect 20 points to upgrade Free Spins to Super Free Spins. The next time 3 Free Spins Symbols land, Super Free Spins will be triggered instead of Free Spins. The Super Free Spins Upgrade Meter cannot collect more than 20 points. The Super Free Spins Upgrade Meter persists for each bet level. Super Free Spins Super Free Spins has a multiplier of x5. At the start of Super Free Spins, Wild Scrolls are active on reels 2 and 4, starting from the top reel position. When the feature ends, the Super Free Spins Upgrade Meter is reset to 0. Return to Player The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by piding the total payouts by the total bets from a simulation of numerous game rounds.
