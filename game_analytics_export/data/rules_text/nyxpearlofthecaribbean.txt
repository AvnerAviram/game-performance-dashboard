Pearl of the Caribbean How to Play Game Introduction Pearl of the Caribbean features 5 sets of reels which can pay up to 250 win lines, up to 50 lines on each reel set. The additional upper reel sets become active when the Expanding Wild Pirate symbol appears in full view on the lower reel set. Pirate Symbol When the Wild Pirate symbol appears fully in view on the lower reel set, the symbol will expand to fill the reel. This activates the upper four reel sets and the expanded Pirate Symbol populates the same respective position on each of the upper reels. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered when 3 or more bonus scatter symbols appear anywhere in view at the end of a spin. During Free Spins, any Pirate Symbols partially in view will expand and activate the upper reel sets. Free Spins use the same award card as the base game spin that triggered the feature. 3 bonus scatter symbols award 8 Free Spins. 4 bonus scatter symbols award 12 Free Spins. Each additional bonus scatter symbol in view will award an extra 3 Free Spins. If 3 or more bonus scatter symbols appear on active reels during the Free Spins Bonus, additional Free Spins will be awarded as above. Big Bet Game The Big Bet Game game plays 5 linked spins with additional advantages at each stake. Wild Pirate Symbol expands when partially in view. Lower paying symbols are removed. Pirate Symbols include multiplier values up to x5. Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines There are 250 potential win lines over the 5 sets of reels. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 RTP:97.75% Last modification date: 6/4/2020
