Doubles GAME RULES ABOUT THE GAME Doubles is a Video Slot with 5 reels and 243 ways to win. The game has 9 regular symbols and 1 free spin symbol. The regular symbols can appear both as single and double. Double symbol counts as two single symbols, so a win can be up to 10 symbols long. Three or more free spin symbols trigger free spins where the player chooses which symbol that should convert all its single symbols to doubles. 3 Free Spin symbols award 10 Free Spins 4 Free Spin symbols award 20 Free Spins 5 Free Spin symbols award 40 Free Spins When entering the free spin mode, you may choose a symbol that will appear only as Double version in the free spin mode. The other symbols will appear as single and double versions, just like in the base game. HOW TO PLAY Select your coin denomination. Press the Spin button to start game HOW TO CALCULATE 243 WAYS WINS To calculate your total 243 ways coin win, add all your 243 ways symbol wins together. To calculate a single 243 ways symbol win, count the number of subsequent reels that contain the symbol, starting from left. If the number of subsequent reels containing the symbol is three or greater, find the corresponding win amount in the pay table. The above doesn't include any Free Spin symbols and Bonus symbols which pay scattered. Calculate the total win in your home currency by multiplying the total coin win with your coin value. HOW TO WIN You win when three or more identical symbols are shown in any of the 243 different ways,beginning in the leftmost position. All wins pay left to right on consecutive reels starting with the first reel. The winning symbols light up and show the number of coins won on each winning pay-line. When three or more Free Spin symbols appear anywhere on the screen, you win Free Spins. GAME PANEL A) Ways - Displays the number of ways to win in this game. The number of ways to win cannot be changed. The bet is 25 coins per spin. B) Coin Value - Change your bet size by changing your coin value. To transform any bet or win in coins to your home currency, multiply the coin amount (25) by your coin value. C) Cash Bet - The bet size is 25 coins per spin. D) Max Bet - Sets the coin value to the largest available. Press the Max Bet button again to go back to the coin value you had before. E) Spin Button - Start the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Stop Button. Press the Stop Button to stop the reels immediately. F) Autoplay - You can make the game play without pressing the spin button every time. Choose the number of rounds to auto play by pressing the Autoplay button. You can choose additional conditions for when to stop autoplay in the Game Settings. To stop autoplay manually, press the Autoplay button. G) Win - Displays the win for the current or last win payout step. H) Balance - Display your account balance. If cash view is chosen, field displays your balance in your home currency. If coin view is chosen, field displays your available coins based on the current coin value. Should you change the coin value, the balance field will update with your new amount of available coins. I) Cash/Coin view - You can choose to view Bet size and Balance in either Cash or Coins. If you choose cash, the game will transform all values in coins to cash by multiplying the coin amount with your coin value. You can switch between Cash and Coin view by pressing the Bet field or Balance field. CHOOSING THE BET If allowed by the operator, players may choose a different coin value in the coin selector. Coin value can e.g. vary from €0.01 to €2 or corresponding amounts in other currencies. The cost of each spin is always 25 coins for 243 ways. SELECTING WAYS In Doubles Video Slot the player must always play all 243 Ways. PLAYING To spin the reels the player presses the Spin button.
