300 Shields GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play Play 1 to 25 lines Payouts are made according to the Paytable Payline wins are multiplied by the amount staked per payline Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount staked Scatter wins are added to payline wins Only the highest win on a pay line pays Coinciding wins on different paylines are added All wins occur on selected lines except scattered SHIELD Pays left to right on consecutive reels except scattered SHIELD which pays any WARRIOR substitutes for all other symbols except scattered SHIELD FREE GAMES FEATURE A Free Games Feature is awarded when 3 or more scattered SHIELD symbols appear A series of 5 Free Games is won, with all WARRIOR wins doubled If you collect 2 or more SHIELDs, a second series of 5 Free Games is awarded, with all WARRIOR wins multiplied by 5 If you collect 6 or more SHIELDs, a third series of 5 Free Games is awarded, with all WARRIOR wins multiplied by 25 If you collect 12 or more SHIELDs, a fourth series of 5 Free Games is awarded, with all WARRIOR wins multiplied by 300 The feature can be re-triggered. Whenever 3 or more SHIELDs appear, 5 additional Free Games are awarded in the current series Free Games wins are added to payline and scatter wins The theoretical return to player of this game is 95.00% Last modification date: 3/7/2017
