5 Line Hold & Spin Game Theme and Graphics 5 Line Hold & Spin is a straight forward 3 reel slot filled with fresh and fancy fruits and lots of profitable coins. Let this slot drag you into the exciting Hold & Spin Feature and win BIG! Main Game The main game is played on 3 reels. The 5 win lines offer you 5 chances to win. If this is not enough, the Wild symbol will help to increase your chances and will substitute for almost any symbol to complete winning combinations! Hold & Spin Feature 3 or more Coin symbols in view will start the Hold & Spin Feature. Your goal is to collect as many coins as possible. Each coin will carry a prize value or "Mini", "Minor" or Major" label. Coins also can have an extra "BONUS" label, these coins will be awarded instantly but disappear the next spin. This way you can even collect more coins! At the end of the feature you will receive all collected prizes and bonuses. 9 coins in view? You will be awarded the Grand Bonus on top! Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are on 5 win lines, except scatters. Line wins and feature wins are added. Last modification date: 12/13/2023
