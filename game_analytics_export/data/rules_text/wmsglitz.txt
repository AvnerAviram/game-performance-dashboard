Glitz Base Game WILD FEATURE WILD is wild for all symbols except FEATURE. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Feature Description MONEY BURST REEL FEATURE WILD LOGO DIAMOND GREEN PURPLE PEARL TURQUOISE MARBLE RED ORANGE BLUE SMALL GREEN The free spin feature is triggered in 3 different ways. Method 1 If reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B contain 4 matching symbols (WILD included), and 3 scattered FEATURE are on reels 3, 4, and 5, 20 free spins are awarded. If the symbols on reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B are not LOGO or WILD, pick a jewelry box to reveal one of these symbols: LOGO, DIAMOND, GREEN, PURPLE, PEARL, TURQUOISE, MARBLE, RED, ORANGE, BLUE, or SMALL GREEN. If pays for the chosen symbol are better than the symbol currently on reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B, the chosen symbol takes its place. During free spins, 4 of that symbol are held on reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B. 3 scattered FEATURE on reels 3, 4, and 5 award 5 additional free spins to the amount of free spins remaining. During Free Spins, the bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. Method 2 If scattered FEATURE are on reels 3, 4, and 5 (and reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B don't contain 4 matching symbols), 5 free spins are awarded. Pick a jewelry box to reveal one of these symbols: LOGO, DIAMOND, GREEN, PURPLE, PEARL, TURQUOISE, MARBLE, RED, ORANGE, BLUE, or SMALL GREEN. 4 of that symbol replace the symbols on reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B and are then held during the free spins. 3 scattered FEATURE on reels 3, 4, and 5 award 5 additional free spins to the amount of free spins remaining. During Free Spins, the bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. Method 3 If reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B contain 4 matching symbols (WILD included), and 3 scattered FEATURE are not on reels 3, 4, and 5, 5 free spins are awarded with reels 1A, 1B, 2A, and 2B held. 3 scattered FEATURE on reels 3, 4, and 5 award 5 additional free spins to the amount of free spins remaining. During Free Spins, the bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. PAYLINES Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the bet amount added for every 2 lines. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/16/2018
