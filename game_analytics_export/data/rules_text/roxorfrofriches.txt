Frog of Riches WILD MULTIPLIERS WILDS have a chance to land with a win multiplier of up to x5. If WILD is involved in any payline win, the pay for that win is multiplied. If more than one WILD is involved in a win on the same payline, winnings are only multiplied with the multiplier shown on the first WILD on the payline. WILDS can land with or without a multiplier. Frog Free Spins Bonus 3, 4 or 5 BONUS FROG symbols trigger the Frog Free Spins Bonus. At the start of the bonus, stop the wheel to be awarded between 10-50 free spins. The greater the number of triggering BONUS FROG symbols, the greater the chance of getting more free spins. 3 or more BONUS FROG symbols landing during the Bonus award extra free spins, equal to the amount initially awarded. At the end of the bonus, select a coin to reveal a multiplier between 1x to 12x, to multiply the total bonus winnings. The greater the number of triggering BONUS FROG symbols, the greater the chance of getting a higher multiplier. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 95.55% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $95.55 of wins. Rules Total bet is the coin size multiplied by the number of lines played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 3/21/2024
