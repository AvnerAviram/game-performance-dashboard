Book of Fusion How To Play This game is a 5x3 reel video slot with 10 fixed lines and a ‘BOOK OF FUSION FREE SPINS’ bonus. The bonus is triggered when 3 or more WILD symbols appear on any of the reels during the base game. WILD SYMBOL The WILD substitutes for all symbols in the base game. WILD substitutes for any symbol in line wins; it also has a scatter payout. WILD does not substitute for growing symbols during the BONUS. BOOK OF FUSION FREE SPINS When 3 or more WILD symbols appear scattered in view the Free Spins bonus is triggered. Ten free spins are awarded. At the start of the bonus, two SPECIAL SYMBOLS are chosen randomly. Retriggering the bonus will award a further two SPECIAL SYMBOLS. Growing symbols do not need to appear adjacent to each other to result in a winning combination. If all 10 symbols are active during the FREE SPINS the bonus cannot be retriggered. WILD does not substitute as the SPECIAL SYMBOL(s) during the BONUS. BUY PASS ™ You can choose to play the FREE SPINS by pressing the BUY PASS™ button. This function may not be available due to the jurisdiction in which you are located. The cost of the BUY PASS™ is displayed on screen. Press CONFIRM to begin the game. The reels will spin to a position containing at least 3 WILD symbols, and the FREE SPINS BONUS will begin. RTP=93.98% Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 2/28/2023
