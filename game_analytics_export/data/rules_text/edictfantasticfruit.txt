Fantastic Fruit Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 95.89% using 10 win lines and 96.00% using 64 win lines. These RTPs represent the long term theoretical game payouts. Rules FANTASTIC FRUIT is a 5-reel 4-row game with 10 or 64 selectable win lines. Stakes range from €0.05 to €5.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins There is a choice of playing with 10 or 64 win lines. Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. All wins and winning combinations are shown in the pay table.
