Piggy Blitz: Disco Gold Piggy Blitz Disco Gold Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules Piggy Blitz Disco Gold is a 6-reel game machine with 4096 payways. Cash Coins can land on the reels with the following bet values: 25x, 20x, 15x, 12x, 10x, 8x, 5x, 4x, 3x, 2.5x, 2x, 1.6x, 1.5x, 1.4x, 1.2x, 1x, 0.8x, 0.7x, 0.6x and 0.5x. The Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coins have the following bet values: 100x, 250x and 1000x respectively. The Cash Coins are collected by landing up to 2 Piggy Bank symbols on reels 1 or 6. Only 1 Piggy Bank can land on reels 1 and 6. Landing two Piggy Banks will collect all Cash Coins present on the reels twice. The Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coins are collected with Piggy Banks and can also be collected twice. If 2 Piggy Banks land on the reels but no Cash Coins land, a Re-Spin will be triggered, awarding a second chance to land Cash Coins. There is a Gold Piggy above the reels. When Cash Coins land with no Piggy Bank, they will be collected in the Gold Piggy. The Gold Piggy can grow in size when Cash Coins are collected. The Gold Piggy can award an instant prize randomly in the game round when a Cash Coin is collected. The size of the Gold Piggy does not relate to the prize, it is completely random. The prizes are: 10x, 20x, 50x, 100x, 200x, 500x, 2000x, 5000x. Gold Piggy is not active in Blitz Spinz or Free Spinz. Land 3 – 6 Scatters to choose your spins. Three options will be shown - Blitz Spinz, Free Spinz and Mystery Spinz. If Blitz Spinz is chosen, the game will proceed into Blitz Spinz. If Free Spinz is chosen, the game will proceed into Free Spinz. If Mystery Spinz is chosen, the game will choose between Blitz Spinz or Free Spinz and proceed onto the game. For the Blitz Spinz - 3 Scatters award 5 Blitz Spinz, 4 Scatters award 10 Blitz Spinz, 5 Scatters award 15 Blitz Spinz, 6 Scatters award 20 Blitz Spinz. If Blitz Spinz are chosen, only Cash Coins and Piggy Banks can land. All Minor, Major and Mega Cash Coins are active. A special 150x Cash Coin can land only in the Blitz Spinz. The maximum amount of Blitz Spinz is 20. For the Free Spinz, 3 Scatters award 10 Free Spinz, 4 Scatters award 15 Free Spinz, 5 Scatters award 20 Free Spinz, 6 Scatters award 25 Free Spinz. Scatters are collected in the meter on the reels, every 4 Scatters collected, a new stage is unlocked. The player will begin in stage 1 - Stage 1: x1, x2, x3 + 10, 15, 20 or 25 initial Free Spinz. Stage 2: x2, x4, x6 + 10 extra Free Spinz. Stage 3: x8, x10, x15 + 10 extra Free Spinz. Stage 4: x20, x30, x50 + 10 extra Free Spinz. One multiplier from the current stage will be chosen on every spin and will be applied to the win. Only the highest paying symbol (diamond) and all royal symbols are present in the Free Spinz. The maximum amount of Free Spinz is 55. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatters, Piggy Bank and Cash Coins. The average RTP is 94.27%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. There are 4096 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 6000 times total bet. Any disabled or inactive button indicates that the option is not available in the jurisdiction and/or to the operator.
