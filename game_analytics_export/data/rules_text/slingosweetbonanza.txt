Slingo Sweet Bonanza SLINGO IS A MIX OF SLOTS AND BINGO. MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER BY COMPLETING SLINGOS. TRIGGER THE BOMB BONUS FROM THE REEL OR SLINGO LADDER. SELECT YOUR STAKE AND PRESS START GAME TO BEGIN. YOU HAVE 10 SPINS TO MATCH NUMBERS ON THE REEL WITH THE GRID. COMPLETE SLINGOS TO MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER. WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE COLUMN ABOVE. FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ADD AN ADDITIONAL SPIN. SUPER WILDS ALLOW YOU TO MARK ANY NUMBER IN THE GRID. BLOCKER SYMBOLS DO NOTHING. MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER TO WIN CASH OR A BONUS GAME. MORE PICKS AND BIGGER MULTIPLIERS ARE OFFERED AS YOU MOVE UP THE PAY LADDER. 3 OR MORE BONUS SYMBOLS IN VIEW TRIGGERS THE BONUS. 3 SYMBOLS = 2 PICKS 4 SYMBOLS = 4 PICKS, ALL BOMBS X2 5 SYMBOLS = 4 PICKS, ALL BOMBS X4 PICK A BOMB TO REVEAL A PRIZE. FINDING LOLLIPOPS ADDS MORE CASH PICKS. EXTRA SPINS EXTRA SPINS ARE AVAILABLE AFTER EACH GAME. THE PRICE OF EACH SPIN IS DEPENDANT ON THE GRID POSITION AND POTENTIAL PRIZES. PRICES MAY BE IN EXCESS OF YOUR BASE STAKE. YOU CAN CONTROL EXTRA SPINS LIMITS IN PLAY CONTROLS. COLLECT OR BUY MORE SPINS. THE PRICE FOR THE NEXT SPIN IS SHOWN ON THE SPIN BUTTON. WIN LINES THERE ARE 12 WIN LINES AND 11 AWARDS ON THE PAYTABLE BECAUSE THE LAST NUMBER ON THE GRID WILL ALWAYS AWARD AT LEAST 2 WINNING LINES. BEST STRATEGY THE BEST STRATEGY DICTATES THAT THE SELECTION FOR POSITIONS OF WILD AND SUPER WILDS IS ALWAYS IN THE POSITION THAT WILL MOVE THE PLAYER CLOSER TO A SLINGO. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA ARE AVAILABLE THE PREFERENCE IS GIVEN TO THE POSITIONS THAT ARE INCLUDED IN THE MOST SLINGOS (WIN LINES). FOR EXAMPLE THE CENTRAL SQUARE WOULD BE GIVEN PREFERENCE SINCE IT IS INCLUDED IN A HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL AND TWO DIAGONAL LINES. WHERE MULTIPLE POSITIONS WITH THE SAME CRITERIA EXIST AGAIN A RANDOM CHOICE IS MADE. PLAY CONTROLS PLAY CONTROLS CAN BE SELECTED FROM THE MENU. SET THE MAXIMUM PRICE TO BE OFFERED AS AN EXTRA SPINS. SET THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF EXTRA SPINS TO BE OFFERED. SET THE MAXIMUM STAKE FOR ANY SINGLE GAME. THE GAME WILL AUTOMATICALLY END IF ANY OF THE SET LIMITS ARE REACHED. SET THE MAXIMUM LOSS FOR ANY SINGLE GAME. GENERAL BASED ON BEST STRATEGY THE GAME HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 96.45% BASED ON THE BEST STRATEGY EACH EXTRA SPIN HAS A THEORETICAL RTP OF 95.08% FREE SPIN SYMBOLS ARE REMOVED FROM THE REELS IN EXTRA SPINS. IN EXTRA SPINS SOME POTENTIAL PRIZES MAY REQUIRE SUPER WILDS. EXTRA SPIN PRICES ARE ROUNDED TO THE NEAREST WHOLE VALUE WHICH MAY AFFECT THE RTP. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. FREE GAMES IF A PLAYER IS AWARDED A FREE GAME OF SLINGO SWEET BONANZA THEY WILL BE ABLE TO PLAY THE BASE GAME FOR FREE AT A PREDEFINED STAKING VALUE. WHEN THE BASE GAME IS COMPLETED, THE WINNINGS ACCRUED BY COMPLETING HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL OR DIAGONAL SLINGOS OR OTHER SPECIAL SYMBOLS BONUSES WILL BE DISPLAYED TO THE PLAYER AND COLLECTED. FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, THE FREE GAME IS CONSIDERED AS A BONUS CASH STAKE. PENDING GAMES INCOMPLETE GAMES WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY COMPLETED 3 HOURS AFTER LAUNCH.
