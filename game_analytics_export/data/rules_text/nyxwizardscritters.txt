Wizard's Critters RTP: 92.0% - 97.0% VOLATILITY: 16.4 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 4 Red Bird, Green Dragon, or Purple Chimera symbols. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Ace, King, Queen, Jack, or Five symbol. Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Spin-crease feature improves the game as the total amount bet increases. The total amount bet on this game is counted across all sessions played on the current user account. As the total amount bet increases, the following symbols will be unlocked and appear on the reels in order: Double Purple Chimera – Bet 3000 credits to unlock, Double Green Dragon – Bet an additional 7000 credits to unlock, Double Red Bird – Bet an additional 20000 credits to unlock Triple Purple Chimera – Bet an additional 70000 credits to unlock, Triple Green Dragon – Bet an additional 200000 credits to unlock, Triple Red Bird – Bet an additional 700000 credits to unlock Quadruple Purple Chimera – Bet an additional 2000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Green Dragon – Bet an additional 7000000 credits to unlock, Quadruple Red Bird – Bet an additional 40000000 credits to unlock. When the final symbol is unlocked, the meter will remain visible and display 100% complete. FREE GAMES BONUS During the Base Game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any positions on reels 1, 2, and 3 starts the Free Games Bonus. 11 or 12 free games are awarded. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free Games Bonus symbols appears only on reels 1, 2, and 3. Free Games Wild symbol is wild substitutes for all symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 4 Free Games Red Bird, Free Games Green Dragon, or Free Games Purple Chimera symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Free Games Ace, Free Games King, Free Games Queen, Free Games Jack, or Free Games Five symbol symbol. Free Games Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels.
