Way to Go Game Rules All pays REEL POWER cost is 25 Coins. Bet is 25 Coins x Coin Size. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Highest win paid per winning combination. Each symbol can be used only once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Scatter wins are added to REEL POWER wins. RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. This product or service may be protected by one or more patents.
