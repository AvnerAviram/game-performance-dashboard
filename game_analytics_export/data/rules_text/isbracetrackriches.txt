Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Scatter symbol, to form winning combinations. Note: If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2. Scatter symbol Landing a Scatter symbol on reels 1, 3 and 5 will trigger Megaboard . 3.3. Mystery symbol During Megaboard feature the Mystery symbols transform into any other symbols, excluding Scatter . 3.4. Jockey Wilds Randomly on any spin the Jockey can run across the reels to create random Wilds . 3.5. Win Spin Randomly after any losing spin the Jockey can run across the reels and spin losing symbols to create winning combinations. 3.6. Rainbow Respin Randomly after any missed feature trigger on reel 5, with Scatter symbols on reels 1 and 3, the Jockey can run across the reels and respin reel 5 for an extra chance to trigger Megaboard . 3.7. Megaboard Landing a Scatter symbol on reels 1, 3 and 5 will trigger Megaboard . The player will start with two rolls of the dice, causing the Jockey to move that many spaces around the board. Each time the Jockey lands on a space that item is triggered. The resulting possible options are: Multiplier Award – Awards a simple multiplier of stake. Wilds Added – Extra Wilds are added to the reels for the remainder of the feature. Mystery Added – Mystery symbols are added to the reels for the remainder of the feature. Symbol Removed – The symbol shown is removed from the reels for the remainder of the feature. All Wins x – The multiplier is applied to all reel wins for the remainder of the feature. Win Spin – A winning spin is guaranteed on the resulting free spin. Big Money – Big Money Bonus is awarded. For each Jockey dice roll, a Free Spin is triggered. Play through 3 possible laps, with increased prizes after each completed lap. The feature ends when the chasing jockey catches you, or you reach Big Money by completing the third lap. Note: Winnings from the Megaboard are cumulative, with reel based prizes enhancing the Free Spins experience. 3.8. Big Money Bonus Completing three laps of the Megaboard will trigger the Big Money Bonus . Spinning the trophies can give you the chance to multiply the total Megaboard winnings by up to x10. index 4.
