Gypsy Fire BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 30 credits. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by total credits wagered. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. CRYSTAL BALL appears on reels 2, 3 & 4 only and substitutes for WOMAN, RING, BOOK, CUP, CARDS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. The theoretical return to player for this game is 94% ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols WOMAN, RING, BOOK, CUP, CARDS, A, K, Q, J, 10, 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 LOGO trigger 8, 12 or 20 free games respectively. Any 2, 3, 4 or 5 LOGO award additional 5, 8, 12 or 20 free games respectively during the free games. All CRYSTAL BALL change to the following during the free games: Reel 2 Reel 3 Reel 4 CRYSTAL BALLx3 OR CRYSTAL BALLx2 CRYSTAL BALLx4 OR CRYSTAL BALLx2 CRYSTAL BALLx5 OR CRYSTAL BALLx2 CRYSTAL BALLx2, CRYSTAL BALLx3, CRYSTAL BALLx4 and CRYSTAL BALLx5 substitute for WOMAN, RING, BOOK, CUP, CARDS, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If each CRYSTAL BALLx2, CRYSTAL BALLx3, CRYSTAL BALLx4 and CRYSTAL BALLx5 substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay x2, x3, x4 and x5 respectively. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 11/11/2021
