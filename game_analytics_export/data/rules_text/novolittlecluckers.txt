Little Cluckers Game Theme and Graphics Little Cluckers™ is a 5 reel slot with 243 winning ways and 3 little chickens looking to grant you some 'egg-cellent' features! Main Game With 3 different coloured Eggs and their unique features combining into a crazy Free Games Feature containing Expanded Reels, Jackpot Prizes and Win Both ways mechanics, you will be entertained. In Little Cluckers™, your aim is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 243 winning paths running from left to right. Feature Any of the various coloured Egg Symbols landing on the reels have a chance to radnomly trigger the Free Games Feature for 10 Free Games. Multiple egg symbols of different colours allows for multiple features to be combined together in the Free Games. Triggering the BLUE EGG activates the Expanded Reels Feature (5x4 Reels) alongside the opportunity for additional Free Games. Triggering the YELLOW EGG activates the Jackpot Feature, where numerous Jackpot prizes can be won by collecting tokens in the Free Games. Triggering the PINK EGG activates the Win Both Ways Feature, where win-ways can be won from right to left as well as left to right. A prize multiplier is also in play here. Cash Prizes can also be collected from the Egg Symbols during the Free Games. Trigger all 3 Eggs at once to activate the Ultimate Feature with all of the features simulteanously coming into play! Feature Hit Rate: 110-140 Rules WILD substitutes for all symbols except for BLUE EGG, YELLOW EGG and PINK EGG All pays are for wins of left to right on selected win lines, except for scatters, which pay any. Highest win only is paid per selected line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 4/15/2025
