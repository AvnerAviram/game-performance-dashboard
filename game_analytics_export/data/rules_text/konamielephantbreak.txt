Elephant Break BASIC GAME RULES MINIMUM BET IS 60 CREDITS. ALL WINS ARE SHOWN IN CREDITS. THIS GAME SUPPORTS 1024 WAYS. 2X SUN AND 3X SUN CAN APPEAR INDEPENDENTLY OF SUN DURING THE BOUGHT GAMES ONLY. EACH 2X SUN OR 3X SUN WILL MULTIPLY ALL WINS 2X OR 3X RESPECTIVELY. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. A PLAYER CANNOT INFLUENCE THE RESULT OF A GAME BY STOPPING THE REEL SPINS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING CORRECT CREDITS ARE REGISTERED BEFORE PLAY. THE GAME DISPLAY DOES NOT INDICATE HOW CLOSE YOU WERE TO WINNING, AND CANNOT NECESSARILY BE USED TO DETERMINE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING OR LOSING IF YOU CONTINUE TO PLAY. SUN, 2X SUN AND 3X SUN APPEARS ON REELS 2, 3 & 4 ONLY AND SUBSTITUTES FOR ALL PAY TABLE SYMBOLS EXCEPT MARK. MARK PAYS IN ANY POSITION. ONLY HIGHEST SCATTER WIN PAYS. ALL PAYS SHOWN ARE BASED ON 60 CREDITS BET. ONLY HIGHEST WINNER PAID FOR EACH WINNING COMBINATION. PAYS OCCUR IN ANY POSITION ON ADJACENT REELS STARTING WITH THE LEFTMOST REEL. ALL WINS ARE ADDED. FREE GAME FEATURE ANY 3, 4 OR 5 MARK TRIGGER 8, 15 OR 20 FREE GAMES RESPECTIVELY. ANY 2, 3, 4 OR 5 MARK AWARD AN ADDITIONAL 5, 8, 15 OR 20 FREE GAMES RESPECTIVELY DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. EACH SUN CHANGES TO 2X SUN OR 3X SUN WHEN ANY WINS OCCUR DURING THE FREE GAMES. 2X SUN AND 3X SUN SUBSTITUTE FOR ALL PAY TABLE SYMBOLS EXCEPT MARK AND DIAMOND_ELEPHANT. DIAMOND_ELEPHANT APPEAR DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. COLLECT DIAMOND_ELEPHANT TO CHANGE HIPPOPOTAMUS, ZEBRA, MEERKATS OR MONKEY TO ELEPHANT FOR ALL REMAINING FREE GAMES. EACH DIAMOND_ELEPHANT THAT IS SPUN UP IS COLLECTED ON THE PANEL ABOVE THE REEL WINDOW. ONCE A DIAMOND_ELEPHANT HAS BEEN COLLECTED, IT WILL CHANGE TO ELEPHANT FOR ALL REMAINING FREE GAMES. PLAYER CAN COLLECT UP TO 15 DIAMOND_ELEPHANT DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. HIPPOPOTAMUS WILL CHANGE TO ELEPHANT WHEN 4 DIAMOND_ELEPHANT ARE COLLECTED. ZEBRA WILL CHANGE TO ELEPHANT WHEN 8 DIAMOND_ELEPHANT ARE COLLECTED. MEERKATS WILL CHANGE TO ELEPHANT WHEN 12 DIAMOND_ELEPHANT ARE COLLECTED. MONKEY WILL CHANGE TO ELEPHANT WHEN 15 DIAMOND_ELEPHANT ARE COLLECTED. IF THE 15TH DIAMOND_ELEPHANT IS COLLECTED ON THE LAST SPIN OF THE FREE GAMES AND NO ADDITIONAL FREE GAMES ARE AWARDED, THEN 1 FREE GAME IS AWARDED. DIFFERENT REELS ARE USED DURING THE FREE GAME FEATURE. CREDITS BET REMAIN THE SAME AS THE GAME THAT TRIGGERED THE FREE GAME FEATURE. Last modification date: 8/9/2023
