Scratch Ahoy Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules The scratch card is purchased for the selected price. (The higher the price you pay for your card, the greater your potential prize.) Each card includes 9 symbols hidden behind scratchable seals. The RTP is 92.95%. Press the BUY CARD button to start the round and enable the scratching feature. When all seals have been scratched off, the revealed symbols determine your prize according to the prize diagram. Match 3 symbols along any line to win. Prizes displayed in the paytable are multiplied by the denomination. Actions Increase Denomination- Click + to increase the denomination of each coin. Decrease Denomination- Click - to decrease the denomination of each coin. Buy Card- Starts the round, enabling the seals on the card to be scratched off. Reveal All- Automatically reveals all hidden symbols on the card. Last modification date: 5/16/2017
