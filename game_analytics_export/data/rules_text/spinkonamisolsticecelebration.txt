Solstice Celebration BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. SUN substitutes for GEM, HORSE, BUTTERFLY, FLOWER, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions with FRAME that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: GEM, HORSE, BUTTERFLY, FLOWER, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9, before the reel spin is initiated. All positions with FRAME are filled with the same symbol. SPARKLE FEATURE FRAME on all positions on 2 or more reels triggers the Sparkle Feature. Any reels with FRAME appearing on them are nudged until all positions on that reel have FRAME. All symbols in FRAME are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols: Full Reel-SUN, WOMAN, Full Reel-GEM, Full Reel-HORSE, Full Reel-BUTTERFLY, Full Reel-FLOWER, Full Reel-A, Full Reel-K, Full Reel-Q, Full Reel-J, Full Reel-10 or Full Reel-9. All positions with FRAME are filled with the same symbol. The initial symbol in FRAME can be selected for the replacement. The Sparkle Feature cannot be triggered again during the Sparkle Feature. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Full Reel-SUN, Full Reel-GEM, Full Reel-HORSE, Full Reel-BUTTERFLY, Full Reel-FLOWER, Full Reel-A, Full Reel-K, Full Reel-Q, Full Reel-J, Full Reel-10 and Full Reel-9 are equivalent to SUN, GEM, HORSE, BUTTERFLY, FLOWER, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9, respectively for line wins. FREE GAME FEATURE Free Game Feature can only be triggered during the Sparkle Feature. WOMAN on any 2, 3, 4 or 5 reels triggers 10, 15, 20 or 40 free games respectively. During the free games, reels with SUN appearing on them are nudged until all positions on that reel are SUN. Wins are paid after nudging. Sparkle Feature can be triggered during the Free Game Feature where additional free games can be won. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature.
