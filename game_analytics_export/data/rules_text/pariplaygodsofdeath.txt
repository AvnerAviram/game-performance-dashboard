Gods of death Gods of Death is a highly animated ten (10) win line, five (5) reel, three (3) row video slot game. Game features include EXPANDING WILD SYMBOLS and a special TORNADO FEATURE while three (3) or more PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award FREE SPINS with WALKING WILD SYMBOLS. SNAKE QUEEN SYMBOL When a SNAKE QUEEN SYMBOL lands on REEL 3 (three), it EXPANDS to cover the entire reel, making it a WILD REEL. After every spin, the SNAKE QUEEN SYMBOL automatically drops down one position until it leaves the reel. SNAKE QUEEN SYMBOLS substitute for all symbols except PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS. SNAKE QUEEN SYMBOLS are only active in the base game. TORNADO FEATURE The TORNADO FEATURE can trigger after every non-winning spin. When the TORNADO FEATURE is active, one of the reels is picked and one or more of the other reels transform into a copy of that reel. The TORNADO FEATURE can only trigger in the base game. PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOL Three (3) or more PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS in view awards the FREE SPINS FEATURE. Three (3) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award 10 (ten) FREE SPINS, four (4) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award twenty (20) FREE SPINS and five (5) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award fifty (50) FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS can RETRIGGER. Two (2) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award two (2) extra free spins, Three (3) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award ten (10) extra free spins, four (4) PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award twenty (20) extra free spins and five PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS award fifty (50) extra free spins. MUMMY SYMBOL When a MUMMY SYMBOL lands on the reels during FREE SPINS, it EXPANDS to cover the entire reel, making it a WILD REEL. After every spin, the MUMMY SYMBOL moves left one reel on each subsequent FREE SPIN until it leaves the game. MUMMY SYMBOLS are on reels one (1) to five (5) and substitute for all symbols except PHARAOH SCATTER SYMBOLS. MUMMY SYMBOLS are only active in the FREE SPINS FEATURE.
