Joker Poker Game Objective: The objective of JOKER POKER game is to obtain a five-card poker hand that contains a winning combination. The higher the ranking of the poker hand the higher the prize offered. Placing the BET and clicking on the DEAL button, deals the player 5 cards. The player can then choose to hold any of the 5 cards and DRAW the remaining cards. The cards that are not held will be replaced with the new cards when the DRAW button is clicked. After each round, used cards are returned to the deck and the deck is reshuffled. A single JOKER card is added to the pack of cards, which substitutes any other card and awards wins if any. Game Information: All suits (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, and Clubs) have equal rank. The game is played with a 53-card deck. This consists of 52 regular playing cards and a JOKER card. The JOKER card acts as wild and substitutes for all cards. The cards are shuffled at the start of every game. Up to 5 cards can be held for “DRAW”. How to play: Setting BETS: On entering the game, a default BET is set. Player can increase the BET by clicking the + button and decrease the BET by clicking the - button. All the available BETS are shown on the BET panel. The player can change the speed of the game by clicking on the SPEED button before the game round starts. PAYTABLE – The payouts are made as per the ‘PAYTABLE’ shown on the top. The total win amount is the payout in the paytable multiplied by the BET amount. Click on 'DEAL' button to reveal five face-up cards. Additionally, the player can 'DEAL' using the spacebar option on desktop. Player has the option to HOLD up to 5 cards that are dealt. Clicking on a card will place ‘HELD’ text on that card. Click on ‘HOLD’ text to hold the card and to remove the ‘HELD’ card, click on the card again or click on ‘HELD’ text. After making the selection, click on 'DRAW' button. The cards which are not HELD will be replaced with new cards. Final win is based on the combination of the final set of cards shown after the completion of second “DRAW”. Only the highest winning combination is paid. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to the player of this game is 98.09%. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option ' History ' inside any game will allow you to access your gameplay history. All pays are shown in USD currency. PAYTABLE: ROYAL FLUSH: 5 cards with the same suit and sequential face value of 10, J, Q, K, A will be considered as ROYAL FLUSH without any wild substitution. E.g.: 10, J, Q, K, A of Hearts, Spades, Clubs or Diamonds. FIVE OF A KIND: 4 cards of the same face value along with a JOKER card i.e. JOKER will be considered as the fifth card for FIVE OF A KIND. E.g.: 9, 9, 9, 9, JOKER 3, 3, 3, 3, JOKER 4, 4, 4, 4, JOKER etc. WILD ROYAL FLUSH: 5 cards with the same suit having sequential face value 10, J, Q, K, A substituted by Wild card i.e. JOKER will be considered as WILD ROYAL FLUSH. E.g.: 10, J, Q, K, JOKER 10, JOKER, Q, K, A 10, Q, JOKER, K, A etc. STRAIGHT FLUSH: 5 cards of same the suit and face value in a sequential manner will be considered as STRAIGHT FLUSH except 10, J, Q, K, A. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 of Hearts, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 of Clubs, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 of Spades, 3, 4, 5, 6 of Diamonds, JOKER etc. FOUR OF A KIND: 4 cards out of 5 having the same face value will be considered as FOUR OF A KIND. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 5, 5, 5, 5, 7 9, 9, 9, 9, 4 4, 4, 4, 9, JOKER. FULL HOUSE: In 5 cards, 3 cards with the same face value and the remaining 2 cards with different face value of any suit will be considered as a FULL House. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 5, 5, 5 of any suit and 2, 2 any suit. 3, 3, 3 of any suit and JOKER, 2 of any suit. FLUSH: 5 cards with the same suit and different numbers not in sequence will be considered as a FLUSH. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 2, 5, A, 7, K of Hearts 3, 6, 8, J, Q of Spades 2, 3, JOKER, Q, 9 of Diamonds etc. STRAIGHT: 5 cards with different suits (out of 4) and which are in sequence will be considered as STRAIGHT. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 4 of Hearts, 5 of Diamonds, 6 of Clubs, 7 of Spades, 8 of Hearts. JOKER, 7 of Hearts, 8 of Diamonds, 9 of Clubs, 10 of Spades. THREE OF A KIND: 3 cards of the same face value out of 5 cards will be considered as THREE OF A KIND. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well. E.g.: 3, 3, 3, 5, 6 8, 8, 8, 9, 5 JOKER, 4, 9, 4, A etc. TWO PAIR: Any 2 cards of the same face value and another 2 cards of the same face value will be considered as TWO PAIR. If you have 2 Ace or King cards along with 2 same face value cards are also considered as TWO PAIR. E.g.: 2, 2, 3, 3, 5 5, 5, 4, 4, 6 A, A, 3, 2, 2 9, K, K, 7, 7 etc. KINGS OR BETTER: 2 same cards which has a value greater than or equal to K i.e. K,K and A,A will be considered as KINGS OR BETTER. Payout will be paid with JOKER substitution and without JOKER substitution as well.
