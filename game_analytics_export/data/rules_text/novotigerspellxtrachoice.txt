Tiger Spell Xtra Choice Main Game Play 100 lines. Enter the Xtra Choice selection screen when you spell TIGER. Feature Players choose their volatility by selecting their number of Free Games or throw caution to the wind and chance the Mystery Selection where maximum Free Games with maximum number of WILDs are available. The feature is won with by spelling TIGER. You can choose between: 10 Free Games: All K symbols are replaced by wild TIGERS. 7 Free Games: All K and Q symbols are replaced by wild TIGERS. 5 Free Games: All K, Q and J symbols are replayed by wild TIGERS. 3 Free Games: All K, Q, J and 10 symbols are replaced by wild TIGERS. Mystery Choice: Win 10, 7, 5 or 3 Free Games with any one of the following sets of symbols replaced by wild TIGERS: K or K, Q or K, Q, J or K, Q, J, 10. During the free games, 3 or more SCATTER award an additional prize of 2, 5, 10 or 20 multiplied by the total bet. Feature can also be won again by spelling TIGER during the feature. The bet multiplier is the same as the game that triggered the feature. Rules Choose your number of paylines. TIGER substitutes for all symbols, except scatters. TIGER only appears during free games. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. All wins multiplied by bet multiplier, except Scatters and the feature trigger prize which are multiplied by the total bet. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per Scatter combination. Scatter wins and line wins are added. All prizes are on selected lines, except Scatters.
