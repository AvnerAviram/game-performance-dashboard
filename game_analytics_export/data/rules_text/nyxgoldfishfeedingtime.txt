Gold Fish Feeding Time How to Play FISH SYMBOLS The GOLD, RED, and GREEN FISH symbols are 4 rows in height. Up to 2 types of FISH symbols can appear on the reels each base game spin. WILD SYMBOLS WILD symbols substitute for all symbols except for GOLD, RED and GREEN FEEDER symbols and are 2 rows in height. SMALL WILD substitutes for all symbols except for GOLD, RED and GREEN FEEDER symbols and is only available in GREEN FISH BONUS, RED AND GREEN SUPER BONUS, GREEN AND GOLD SUPER BONUS and EXTREME BONUS games. SCATTER SYMBOLS The GOLD, RED and GREEN FEEDER symbols are Scatter symbols and are 2 rows in height. When a FEEDER symbol lands fully in view on the reels – the corresponding FISH in the bowl above the reels may grow bigger. Any combination of FEEDER symbols that land fully in view on the reels may award the corresponding FISH BONUS. The bigger the FISH gets over time in the bowls above the reels, has no effect on triggering Free Spins. The number of Free Spins, and types of coins available are shown in the table on the next page. There are coins along the top of the reels with a prize displayed on either side. If one or more FEEDER symbols land fully in view, a prize from the coin above the FEEDER symbol is awarded. Bet Multiplier Coins: Award x1, x2, x3, x4, x5, x6, x7, x8, x10, x15, x25, x30, or x40 of Current Bet. Bonus Spins Coins: 2 - 4 additional Free Spins are awarded. Wild Coins: 6 – 9 SMALL WILD symbols are added to the reels, before winning lines are evaluated. The number of WILDS is displayed on the coin. Jackpot Coins: Award the corresponding Jackpot. There are 4 Jackpots – MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and GRAND. Each Jackpot has a corresponding coin. The Jackpot coins only appear in the GOLD FISH BONUS, RED AND GOLD SUPER BONUS, GREEN AND GOLD SUPER BONUS and EXTREME BONUS. If a Jackpot COIN is awarded – the corresponding Jackpot value is added to the BONUS WIN meter. If a coin is awarded, it drops down into the relevant meter below the reels. If no coins are awarded, the coin above reel 1 is removed, the coins will move left to fill in the blank spaces and new coins will be added. When RED FISH BONUS is triggered, GREEN and GOLD FEEDER symbols do not appear on the reels. When GREEN FISH BONUS is triggered, RED and GOLD FEEDER symbols do not appear on the reels. When GOLD FISH BONUS is triggered, GREEN and RED FEEDER symbols do not appear on the reels. When RED AND GREEN FISH SUPER BONUS is triggered, GOLD FEEDER symbol does not appear on the reels. When RED AND GOLD FISH SUPER BONUS is triggered, GREEN FEEDER symbol does not appear on the reels. When GREEN AND GOLD FISH SUPER BONUS is triggered, RED FEEDER symbol does not appear on the reels. Free Spins are played with the same total bet, awards and winning combinations as the base game which triggered the Bonus. Free Spins game will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. TURBO MODE The Turbo Mode button is displayed on the bottom right of the reels with an "ON" and "OFF" option. Turn ON Turbo Mode button to speed up each spin, it can be turned OFF at any point during the game. When Free Spins is awarded Turbo Mode button will automatically be turned OFF but can be turned ON during the feature. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins.
