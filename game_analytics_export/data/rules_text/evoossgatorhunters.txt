EATER SYMBOL The Eater symbol appears one at a time and triggers only when there are no winning combinations. There are 2 types of Eater symbols: Normal Eater It removes a random number of symbols and all their instances, allowing new symbols to fall in. After that, it transforms into a Wild symbol. Super Eater It behaves same way as Normal Eater symbol but gets a multiplier when it turns into a Wild. If the Wild is part of a scatter win, its multiplier applies on win amount. Wild multipliers are additive. Possible multiplier values are x2, x3 and x10. REVOLVER Revolver symbols activate when at least one winning combination occurs, no Eater symbols are present, and no more winning combinations can occur. Each Revolver contains a set of bullets (with multiplier values) and empty positions. When triggered, the cylinder spins and randomly selects a position. If a bullet is selected, its multiplier is added to the total win multiplier. The total win multiplier is then applied to the current spin’s win. A maximum of six Revolvers of the same or different types can appear at once. Normal Revolver It spins only once, regardless of whether it selects a bullet or an empty position. However, it can spin again if activated by a Super Fire Revolver. Super Revolver If a bullet is selected, it spins one more time. Super Fire Revolver If a bullet is selected, the Super Fire Revolver forces all Revolvers with bullets to spin again. There are 5 types of bullets, each in a different color that give higher multiplier according to the hierarchy (from low to highest), Purple - Blue - Orange - Red - White. SWAMP SPINS Landing 3 Bonus Symbols will trigger 10 Swamp Spins. The total multiplier is kept during Freespins. The total multiplier is only applied to the wins from spins in which a Revolver lands and selects a bullet. Landing 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 Bonus symbols during Swamp Spins awards 3, 5, 7, 10, or 15 additional spins, respectively. During this feature, any 1 of the following 4 upgrades is randomly awarded: - Extra Bullet 2x : All Revolvers will have an additional 2x bullet. - Extra Bullet 10x : All Revolvers will have an additional 10x bullet. - Super Eater : All Normal Eaters are replaced by Super Eaters. - Super Revolvers : All Normal Revolvers are replaced by Super Revolvers. FRENZY SPINS Landing 4 Bonus symbols will trigger 12 Frenzy Spins. In addition to Swamp Spins feature, 2 upgrades are randomly awarded instead of one. GATOR SPINS Landing 5 Bonus symbols will trigger 15 Gator Spins. In addition to Swamp Spins feature, 3 upgrades are randomly awarded instead of one. APEX PREDATOR SPINS Landing 6 Bonus symbols will trigger 18 Apex Predator Spins. In addition to Swamp Spins feature, all 4 upgrades are awarded instead of one. Also the “Super Revolvers” upgrade is replaced by the following: - Super Fire Revolvers : All Revolvers are replaced by Super Fire Revolvers. NOLIMIT BOOSTER BONUS BOOSTER At a cost of 2.5 times the base bet, the player is guaranteed a Bonus symbol on reel 2. REVOLVER ROLL At a cost of 90 times the base bet, the player is guaranteed an active Super Revolver. The total multiplier applies to the base bet. Other symbols will not land. SUPER FIRE SPINS At a cost of 400 times the base bet, the player is guaranteed a Super Fire Revolver with 5 bullets of different colors. Bonus symbols will not land during Super Fire Spins. MASSACRE SPINS At a cost of 1200 times the base bet, all Revolvers are replaced by Super Fire Revolvers with 1000x and 2000x bullets. EXTRA SPIN Players might get the opportunity to purchase an additional spin when a round ends. The additional spin will keep the total multiplier and all Revolvers with any remaining bullet. The total multiplier is only applied to the wins from spins in which a Revolver lands and selects a bullet. The cost of the spin will be calculated based on the total multiplier and the remaining Revolvers. If the cost of the Extra Spin is less than or equal to the win on the previous spin, then the Extra Spin option will be presented.
