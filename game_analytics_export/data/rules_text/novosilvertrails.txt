Silver Trails Game Theme and Graphics Get ready for golden rewards in Silver Trails™. This 5 reel slot with 243 different ways to win, hosts some exciting Features. Firstly, a Hold and Spin Feature with Bonus Multipliers at the top of the Reels. Secondly, Multiplying stacked Silver Stars with instant prizes if you land a stack on reels 1, 3 or 5. Main Game Your aim in Silver Trails™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 243 winning paths running from left to right. During base game spins when Silver Stars fill reels 1, 3, or 5, the prize values on those Silver Stars is multiplied by the value shown at the top of that reel, then this prize is awarded. Multipliers will randomly change after each game. Silver Trails Jackpots Feature 6 or more Silver Star symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Silver Trails Jackpots feature. The triggering Silver Star symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Silver Star lands on the reels, the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on the screen are filled with Silver Stars. During the hold and spin feature when Silver Stars fill any reel, the prize values on those Silver Stars will be multiplied by the Bonus Multipliers shown at the top of that reel. The prize is awarded at the end of the hold and spin feature. Silver Star prizes can award Major, Minor or Mini Jackpots or up to 9x total bet. Possible Bonus Multipliers are up to 20x. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Silver Stars Scatter wins and all pay wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 6/28/2022
