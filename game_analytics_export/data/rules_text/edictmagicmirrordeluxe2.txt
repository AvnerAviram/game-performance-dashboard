Magic Mirror Deluxe II Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 95.63%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Rules MAGIC MIRROR DELUXE II is a 5-reel 3-row game with 10 fixed win lines. Stakes range from €0.10 to €20.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. In addition to the cash winnings from the pay table, 10 Feature Games can be won with at least 3x < MAGIC MIRROR >. All wins and winning combinations are shown in the pay table. WILDCARD Symbols The wildcard symbol substitutes any symbol to create the highest win on a win line. Free Games 10 Magic Mirror Feature Games can be won with at least 3x on screen. In the Magic Mirror Feature one special expanding symbol is randomly selected at the beginning of the Feature Games. During Feature Games the special symbol expands to cover 3 positions on the reel and pays in any position on lines played. For additionally triggered Free Games one additional special expanding symbol is randomly selected. Stakes do not have to be paid for the Feature Games. Your effective bet for these Feature Games is your actual bet when you won the free games. The resulting winnings will be shown in the pay table. Last modification date: 5/31/2017
