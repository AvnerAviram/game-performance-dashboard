If any winning combination is increased as a result, play continues and this process repeats. If no winning combinations are increased and the respin meter is 0, all winning combinations are awarded and the game ends. • When a winning scatter combination is formed of 5 or more symbols and only on reels 1 and 2,and there are no winning combinations that include a symbol on reel 3, then all wins are awarded and the game ends. • Each [Multiplier Symbol] collected during a winning spin adds +1 to the multiplier meter. All awards except Jackpots are multiplied by the number in the multiplier meter at the end of the spin. • Each [Respin Symbol] collected during a winning spin adds +1 to the respin meter. If the amount of respins in the meter is great than 0 and no winning combination is increased by the symbols landing on a new reel, the reel will respin and the respin meter will decrease by 1. • Scatter Symbols appearing in any position on the reels with a value awards a scatter pay if it is part of a winning combination. • Bonus Symbol, JACKPOT SYMBOLS, MULTIPLIER SYMBOL, RESPIN WILD SYMBOL, WILD SYMBOL, AND SCATTER SYMBOLS are wild and substitute for all non-wild symbols.
