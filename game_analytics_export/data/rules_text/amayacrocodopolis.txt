Crocodopolis GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play Play 1 to 25 lines Payouts are made according to the Paytable Payline wins are multiplied by the amount staked per payline Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount staked Scatter wins are added to payline wins Only the highest win on a pay line pays Coinciding wins on different paylines are added All wins occur on selected lines except scattered PYRAMID CROC substitutes for all symbols except scattered PYRAMID Scattered PYRAMID appears on Reels 2, 3 and 4 only Pays left to right on consecutive reels except scattered PYRAMID which pays any SLIDE-A-WILD Position the onscreen slider to move the Wild CROC to any reel The CROC will appear only on the reel selected WILD ON REEL 1 Prize is DOUBLED if CROC substitutes in a winning combination WILD ON REEL 2 Prize is TRIPLED if CROC substitutes in a winning combination WILD ON REEL 3 When CROC appears it expands to cover all three positions on Reel 3 Pays are now calculated with the CROC substitution CROC Expansion will only occur if it results in a winning combination WILD ON REEL 4 Prize is TRIPLED if CROC substitutes in a winning combination When CROC appears it expands to cover all three positions on Reel 4 Pays are now calculated with the CROC substitution CROC Expansion will only occur if it results in a winning combination WILD ON REEL 5 All symbols on Reel 5 are replaced with CROC Prize is TRIPLED if CROC substitutes in a winning combination FREE GAMES FEATURE A Free Games Feature is awarded when 3 scattered PYRAMID appear 12 Free Games are awarded during which all prizes are DOUBLED The CROC will perform the same function during Free Games as in the trigger game The Free Games Feature can be re-triggered Free Games are played at the lines and bet of the trigger game Free Games wins are added to payline and scatter wins Last modification date: 1/31/2017
