Super Bar-X Sequences of Wins Up to 3 wins may be awarded in each game by way of ‘Hold on Win’ or a ‘Free Spin’. Each win is added to the ‘Collect Bank’ display. At the end of the game, the total is transferred to the account balance and the game is over. No stake is taken for a second or third win in a sequence. Only the initial bet value is taken at the start of the game. Hold on Win 1 or 2 holds may randomly be offered after a nudged or straight in win. 1 hold may randomly be offered after a ‘Hold After Losing Nudge’ win. ‘Hold’ and ‘Cancel’ buttons flash. Holds may be pressed, but this is purely cosmetic. The game cannot be started without holding the win. Press the ‘Tick’ button to hold any un-held reels and continue the game. Free Spin 1 or 2 free spins may randomly be offered after a straight-in win. Press the ‘Tick’ button to spin the reels to a repeat of the initial win. Nudge Feature Awarded at random at the end of any losing game. 3 nudges are always offered. Press the flashing nudge buttons to nudge the reels forward by one symbol per nudge. Press the ‘Tick’ button to automatically complete the nudges. In auto-play, the nudges will be processed automatically. If a win is missed, the auto-play will rewind and automatically nudge the best combination. Up to 2 holds may be offered (in the same game) on any win obtained from nudges. Hold After Losing Nudge Feature Offered at random after losing Nudges. ‘Hold’ and ‘Cancel’ buttons flash. Holds may be pressed, but this is purely cosmetic. The reels cannot be held. Press the ‘Tick’ button to cancel any lit hold buttons and spin the reels. Reels will spin to a guaranteed random win. There is no link between the symbols nudged to the win line during the nudges and the resulting win. 1 hold may be offered on the win (in the same game). The maximum win from a single game is £36,000 The theoretical return to player of this game is 96.00% Last modification date: 11/4/2020
