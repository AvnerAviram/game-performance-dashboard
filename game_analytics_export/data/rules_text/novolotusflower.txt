Lotus Flower Game Theme and Graphics Experience the majesty of the world of Asia in Lotus Flower™. Riches await you in this new 5 reel, 10-line slot game. Immerse yourself in the stark beauty of Asian aesthetics, and seek the fabled 'Lotus' symbol, for it will guide your path to fortune and riches untold Main Game Your goal is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across active win lines in the normal game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on an active win line without any other symbols in between. The 'Lotus' symbol substitutes all other symbols and can therefore complete a winning combination. Landing 3x 'Lotus' symbol anywhere on reels 1, 3 and 5 will award 15 free games. Feature Free Games played at trigger bet and lines. Free games are played with only three symbols on the five reels. These three symbols are randomly selected from all symbols excluding the 'Lotus' symbol. Free games cannot be retriggered during the feature. Trigger: 3x 'Lotus' symbols anywhere on reels 1, 3 and 5. Feature Hit Rate: 140 - 180 Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. All prizes are on selected lines, except scatters. LOTUS symbols pay at any position on screen. LOTUS symbols may only appear on reels 1, 3 and 5. Highest win only paid on each selected line. Free games are played at trigger bet and lines.
