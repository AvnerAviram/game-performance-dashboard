T his game is played using Cluster bucks evaluation. Winning combinations are defined as groups of matching symbols that are horizontally or vertically adjacent to one another. Only the highest paying combination for each group is paid per spin. Win combination pays are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. All 5 reels are always in play. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels. Free Games Rules During the base game, 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 start the Free Games Bonus. Awards 7 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. The Free Games Feature uses different reels. Free games are automatically played at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Feature. At the end of the Free Games Bonus, the player is awarded 1x the total bet for each winning tumble that occurred during the Free Games Bonus. Additional free games cannot be awarded during the Free Games Feature. The Free Games Feature ends when 0 free games remain. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations.
