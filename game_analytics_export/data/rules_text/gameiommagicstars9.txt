Magic Stars 9 Magic Stars 9 is a 9-reel video slot featuring Stars Spins and Free Spins with Infinite Multiplier. All wins are paid for combinations of 4 or more same symbols at any position. Drawn symbols can be locked for another spin only during the main game. Bonus features are played at the same bet level and coin value as the round that activated them. Each new game costs one stake. After each draw of all 9 symbols on the reels the game automatically blocks the best set of symbols, but it may be changed by the player. Playing with blocked reels is a new game. RTP is 96.21% SPECIAL FEATURES Stars Spins A draw of three Stars Spins Symbols on the three middle horizontal reels activates the Stars Spins bonus. Stars Spins awards 9 Free Spins during which Stars Spins Symbols get automatically locked after appearing on any reel. A win is paid for the number of collected Stars Spins Symbols during the bonus. Free Spins with the Infinite Multiplier A draw of three Free Spins Symbols on the three middle horizontal reels activates 9 Free Spins with the Infinite Multiplier bonus. During the bonus, a +3FS Star Symbol is active. Each time it occurs, it adds 3 Free Spins. The initial value of the Infinite Multiplier counter is x1. Each winning spin increases the multiplier’s value by 1. SPECIAL SYMBOLS STARS SPINS BONUS SYMBOL A draw of three Stars Spins Symbols on the three middle horizontal reels activates the Stars Spins bonus. FREE SPINS BONUS SYMBOL A draw of three Free Spins Symbols on the three middle horizontal reels activates 9 Free Spins with the Infinite Multiplier bonus.
