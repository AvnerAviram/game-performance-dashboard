King Kong Game Rules LINES can be changed by using the up and down arrows BET amount can be changed by using the up and down arrows SPIN begins the game with the current BET and LINES Payouts are displayed within INFO Payline wins are multiplied by the amount bet per line Highest win amount is paid on each selected payline Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet and added to payline wins Feature wins are added to scatter and payline wins Win combinations pay from left to right including Scattered Logo King Kong appears on Reels 2, 3, and 4 only and substitutes for allsymbols except Scattered Logo System malfunction voids all pays and plays Bonus Feature Free Game Play 3 or more Logo scatter symbols anywhere in view triggers the King Kong Free Games Feature All Free Games are played with the same number of lines and the same bet per line as the triggering spin King Kong Goes Ape Special Feature The King Kong Goes Ape Feature is awarded when King Kong appears on Reel 3 All positions on reel 3 become substitutes Additional substitutes are added to Reels 2 and 4 Payouts are recalculated and awarded The King Kong Goes Ape Feature is played at the lines and bet of the triggering game King Kong Smash Feature The King Kong Smash Feature is awarded when 3 or more Scattered Logos appear left to right Select planes for King Kong to smash and keep selecting planes until King Kong misses 3 times Each smashed plane increases the number of Free Games awarded The player may choose to reject the Free Games and take one of three mystery prizes instead If a mystery prize is selected, all Free Games are forfeit If the Feature is retriggered during Free Games, the same number of Free Games are awarded without repeating the Feature Free Games are played at the lines and bet of the triggering game with all prizes doubled The mystery prize is multiplied by the triggering bet King Kong Smash Feature wins are added to payline and scatter wins The King Kong Goes Ape Feature
