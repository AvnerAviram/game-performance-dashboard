Xmas Bells Main Game With Wild symbols and a Bonus Spins Feature game, this slot is sure to keep you entertained. Your aim in Xmas Bells™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 576 winning paths running from left to right. Game Features Bonus Spins Feature Look out for Bell symbols, when 5 or more Bell symbols appear anywhere on screen they trigger the Bonus Spins Feature. The Bell symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Bell lands on the reels, the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. All prizes on Bell symbols are paid at the conclusion of the bonus spins, or if the screen is completely filled with Bells. The Grand Prize is awarded if all positions on the screen are opened and filled with Bells. Bell symbol prizes can award, Major, Minor or Mini Prizes or up to 40 x total bet. Getting a +1 Row Bell will add another row to the feature. Landing a Multiplier Bell will increase the multiplier on the left hand side of the reels, when the feature ends all Bell Prizes will be multiplied by this multiplier (except for the Grand Prize and the Major Prize).
