[top] Game Payouts Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed, once the reels have come to a stop. All pay ways are permanently enabled for every spin, so you cannot select or de-select pay ways. All bet amounts are automatically multiplied by a 30 bet multiplier. So, if you bet 1 credit, you are betting 1 credit x 30 = 30 total credits bet per spin. Regular Payouts All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination. Regular payouts depend on your total bet per spin. For each spin, the bet amount you choose is automatically multiplied by the bet multiplier. Multiple winning combinations are paid out per pay way. If there is more than one winning combination for different symbols on a pay way, you are paid out for each combination. If there is more than one winning combination for the same symbol on a pay way, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. If winning symbol combinations are displayed on multiple pay ways, you are paid out for each combination. For a combination of regular symbols to produce a winning regular combination, the following criteria must be met: Three or more matching symbols must be displayed in any position on consecutive reels. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Scatter Symbol Payouts Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on consecutive reels to win. For a combination of scatter symbols to produce a winning scatter combination, the following criteria must be met: Completes a winning scatter combination when three or more appear on the reels. Cannot be substituted by the wild symbol. Free Spins Bonus Game Payouts You are awarded ten free spins. The Brilliant Wilds™ feature activates when an Untamed Giant Panda symbol is displayed on a reel. All the bets and pay ways played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. The bonus game cannot be re-activated during the free spins. Your Gamble™ Feature Payouts You spin the compass needle to gamble your win. You can adjust your odds by up to 20x your win. You can choose to gamble your entire win amount or bank half. All your gamble amounts and any wins are in credits. You can play your winnings until the gamble limit is reached. [top] Game Rules Click the links for more information about the game rules. General Rules 243 permanently enabled pay ways. A bet multiplier of 30 applies per spin. Malfunctions void all plays and pays. Wild Symbol Rules The Untamed Giant Panda symbol is a wild symbol. Does not substitute for the scatter symbol. Creates its own winning combination when multiple symbols appear on a pay way. Activates the Collect-a-Wild™ feature. Multiple winning combinations are paid out per pay way. If there is more than one winning combination for different symbols on a pay way, you are paid out for each combination. If there is more than one winning combination for the same symbol on a pay way, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. Scatter Symbol Rules The Panda Eye symbol is a scatter symbol. Completes a winning scatter combination when three or more appear on the reels. Cannot be substituted by the wild symbol. Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on consecutive reels to win. Three or more Panda Eye symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. Free Spins Bonus Game Rules Three or more Panda Eye symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. You are awarded ten free spins. The Brilliant Wilds™ feature activates when an Untamed Giant Panda symbol is displayed on a reel. The Untamed Giant Panda symbol is held in its current position for the remaining free spins. The Untamed Giant Panda symbol is a wild symbol. All the bets and pay ways played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. The bonus game cannot be re-activated during the free spins. Winnings are automatically collected at the end of the bonus game. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. Lucky Nudge™ Feature Rules Two Panda Eye symbols scattered on the reels, and one Panda Eye symbol peeking on a reel could activate the Lucky Nudge™ feature. The peeking symbol can be nudged up or down to display on the reel. If the peeking symbols is nudged onto the reel, it completes a winning symbol combination. If there is more than one symbol peeking on the reels, only one symbol can be nudged. Three Panda Eye symbols scattered on the reels, activate the Free Spins bonus game. Collect-a-Wild™ Feature Rules Collect four wild symbols on a reel to activate the Collect-a-Wild™ feature. When a wild symbol is displayed on a reel, a wild icon is displayed on the counter below the reel. If you collect four wild icons for a reel, the reel is transformed into a wild reel for the next four spins. When the four spins are complete the counter is reset. Any collected wild icons or activated wild reels relate to the bet amount they were collected on. If you change your bet amount any collected wild icons are removed and the counter is reset. Any activated wild reels are disabled. If you change your bet amount back to a previous bet amount, any collected wild icons or activated wild reels are restored. The Collect-a-Wild™ feature is not available during free spins. Your Gamble™ Feature Rules Any win activates the Your Gamble™ feature. You can choose to play the feature or you can skip it and continue to play the regular game. However, the feature is not available if your win exceeds the gamble activation limit. You spin the compass needle to gamble your win. The compass is divided into two sections, win and lose. You can adjust your odds by up to 20x your win. Adjusting your odds changes how much of the compass is divided into win or lose. You can choose to gamble your entire win amount or bank half. All your gamble amounts and any wins are in credits. [top] Playing the game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on the pay way. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. To play: 1. Place a bet. How? Click the Bet button. Click the Coin Size arrows to increase or decrease the coin size you want to bet. Click the Coins arrows to increase or decrease the number of coins you want to bet. You can also click the Bet button arrows to select the number of coins you want to bet. 2. Click Spin . The reels start spinning. If you have a winning combination lined up on the pay way, the win is highlighted on the reels. You are paid out the win amount as displayed in the Win box. Click Win to view the top five wins for your current game session. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again. [top] AutoPlay, Options and Statistics You can change options and view statistics for games using the Options or Statistics buttons. You can also spin a set number of times in succession without interacting with the game, using AutoPlay or AutoSpin. In some games AutoPlay is available in Expert mode only. How do I turn on Expert mode? Click Expert . The Expert mode features are activated, such as AutoPlay. How do I use AutoPlay? Select a bet amount. You must select a bet amount before you start the AutoPlay session, as AutoPlay plays with the last bet value. If the game has Expert mode, click Expert . Click AutoPlay . In some games you cannot set the number of spins to play in the AutoPlay session, so clicking this button starts AutoPlay that continues until you click Stop . In other games this button enables you to choose the number of spins you want to play and your stop options. Once you have configured these options, click Start to begin your AutoPlay session. What options do I have in Autoplay? AutoPlay options differ between games. Your game may not have all of these options.
