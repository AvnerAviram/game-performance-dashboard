Aramis Fuster La bruja MAIN GAME In this game, each prize could either be risked, by pressing the play button, or to be collected, by pressing the collect button. In case the prize is risked, it will either be given the amount of a one level higher prize or the equivalent amount of owls. Once the line of the three lemons is reached, the game becomes an up down, where the possible prizes go up or down in the prize plan UPPER GAME In order to play this game it will be required to have enough owls and credits. Whenever a prize is obtained, unless it is a blue bar prize, the player will be offered to either risk a portion of the prize or to collect it, until there are no more portions left. Choosing to risk a portion may allow the player to either obtain the double of that portion or the equivalent value of that portion in owls. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. A MINI-GAMES ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES. CAULDRONS BONUS GAME Sometimes accessed with 3 potions. Bubbles come out of the cauldrons, press ‘play’ to sum up the best prize. TAROT BONUS GAME Sometimes accessed with 3 cards.
