Pinata Fiesta 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 1 per line (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.00% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different selected lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each selected line is paid. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Mobile version To play, follow these steps: Tap to open the settings screen. On the settings screen, tap then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features 3.1.Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Scatter symbol, to form winning combinations. Note : If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. 3.2.Scatter Three, four or five Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels will trigger 10, 15 or 20 Free Spins. 3.3. Free Spins During Free Spins , three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels award additional Free spins, according to the pay table. During Free Spins there is a chance to trigger one of the four random in-game Piñata Modifiers ( Popping Wilds , Mega Spiñata , Fiesta Frenzy or Grande Reels ). At the end of Free Spin three piñatas will drop down over the reels. Selecting one of them will either reveal a number of extra free spins (3-10) or "Fiesta Over". Free spins Winnings are added to the total WINNINGS. Note: You cannot change the bet during Free Spins . The Pick a Pinata Pop Up at the end of Free Spins will only ever show once per Free Spins round. It is also possible for the Pinata to swing across the entire screen as a teaser and not be broken so no feature will be awarded 3.4. Popping Wilds Floating balloons will appear over the reels. Any balloons that pop over the reels will reveal extra Wild symbols. 3.5. Mega Spiñata Reels 1, 2 and 3 will transform in one colossal reel. 3.6. Fiesta Frenzy During Fiesta Frenzy any royals (10, J, Q, K, A) that land in view will turn into one of the higher paying symbols or Wilds. 3.7. Grande Reels During Grande Reels a random number of reels will turn Wild 4.
