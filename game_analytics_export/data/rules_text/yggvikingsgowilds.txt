Vikings Go Wilds Welcome to Vikings Go Wild Video Slot with Freespin Mode with sticky wild and treasure chest bonus. ABOUT THE GAME Vikings Go Wild is a Video Slot with 5 reels and 25 paylines oriented from the left to right. The game has eight regular symbols that win if three or more are lined up in sequence on a payline, beginning from the leftmost position. The Wild Symbol substitute for all symbols except Freespin Symbols and Treasure chest. Three or more Freespin Symbols trigger the Freespin Mode. 3 freespin symbols award 8 freespins 4 freespin symbols award 16 freespins 5 freespin symbols award 24 freespins Free spins can't be retriggered. In the free spin mode, The Viking symbols in the game will try to fight of the sea creature when they land on the reels. If a Viking manages to fight of the sea creature it transforms into a wild that sticks on the reels and if not it will stay as a Viking symbol. The fight sequence happens before the wins are paid out for the spin. The wilds stick on the reel until the free spin mode is over. The "Viking symbols" are the four highest paying symbols, with characters is the imagery. There are no Wild symbols on reels in Free Spins. The free spin mode is played with the same bet that activated it. Wins from the free spin mode is added to the total win of the round that activated it. On the fifth reel there is a Treasure chest symbol that during main game can award Freespins or a coin win. In Freespins the treasure chest can also award a wild reel, making an entire reel turn into wild until the end of Freespins. The wild reel is randomly chosen between the first four reels. The coin win is either 50, 75, 100, 125, 150, 175, 200, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900 or 1000. The number of free spins awarded can be 8, 9, 10, 11 or 12. Treasure Chest can't award Free Spins if Free Spins were already awarded by free spin symbols in the spin. If a treasure chest lands on top of a sticky wild symbol, both the treasure chest and wild symbol are active that round. HOW TO PLAY Select your coin denomination. Press the Spin button to start game HOW TO CALCULATE PAYLINE WINS To calculate your total payline coin win, calculate all payline wins and add them together. To calculate a single payline win, count the number of identical symbols lined up in sequence on the line. If the payline pays from left, then start counting from the far left. If the payline pays from right, then start counting from the far right. If three or more identical symbols lined up, find the coin win value in the Pay Table. There you can for all symbols find the value for 3 in a row, 4 in a row, and 5 in a row. Only the highest winning combination per line is paid out. The above doesn't include any Freespin and Bonus symbols which pay scattered. If the winning combination starts with three wilds, there can be many ways to interpret the win. In this scenario, only the highest win combination is paid out. Calculate the total win in your home currency by multiplying the total coin win with your coin value.
