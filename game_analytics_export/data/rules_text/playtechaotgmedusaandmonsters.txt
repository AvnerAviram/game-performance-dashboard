Age of the Gods Medusa and Monsters 6-Reel 164-Line Slot The objective of Age of the Gods: Medusa & Monsters™ is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels. TO PLAY THE GAME Click START on the entry screen to enter the main game. This game is played with a fixed number of 164 active paylines. Your total bet per game round is your coin value × 50. Click '−' or '+' under TOTAL BET to choose the total bet. Click the TURBO MODE button to switch Turbo Mode on or off. When turned on, some win animations and sounds are skipped and the reels spin faster. Clicking the SPIN button spins the reels with the currently selected total bet. During the reel spin the SPIN button changes into STOP . Clicking STOP ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin result. Pressing the Spacebar key spins the reels with the currently selected total bet. Mouse over the AUTOPLAY button to see the Autoplay options. Click on an option to start the Autoplay function. During Autoplay mode the AUTO PLAY button changes into the STOP AUTO PLAY button. Click the STOP AUTO PLAY button to stop Autoplay mode. Select the number of spins to be played automatically or choose UNTIL FEATURE to spin until the Medusa's Gaze Free Games feature or Age of the Gods™ Jackpot are triggered. The number of auto spins left is displayed above the smaller STOP AUTO PLAY button, unless you've chosen the UNTIL FEATURE option. To enter the triggered feature, click the CLICK TO START button. After the end of the feature, click the CONTINUE button on the winnings summary screen to return to the main game or resume the Auto play mode (if there are any auto spins left to play). The paytable can be accessed from the Menu or the INFO page. ABOUT PAYOUTS Wins are calculated according to the paytable, which can be accessed via the Menu or by clicking on the INFO button. All payouts are the Coin Value multiplied by the value for the win on the Paytable . If more than 1 active payline has a winning combination, the winnings are added up. If 2 winning combinations occur on the same line, you will be paid the value of the higher winning combination. Winning combinations must start from the leftmost reel, and the symbols have to be consecutive along a payline. The SCATTER symbol is an exception to these rules. More information about the SCATTER symbol can be found below. The accumulating winnings and all payline wins are shown for each winning spin. In the case of large wins, a win pop-up opens displaying the total spin revenue. WILD SYMBOL The symbol with the Medusa image on it is the MEDUSA WILD symbol in the game. MEDUSA WILD symbols can stand for any other symbol, except SCATTER symbols to make the best possible winning combination. When the MEDUSA WILD symbol lands anywhere on the reels during the main game, it expands to fill the reel. During the Medusa's Gaze Free Games feature, the MEDUSA WILD symbol only appears as a stacked shifting wild symbol. SCATTER SYMBOL The Shield symbol is the SCATTER symbol in the game. SCATTER symbols are stacked 2 symbols high, only appear on reels #2, #4 and #6, and only appear during the main game. When SCATTER symbols land simultaneously on reels #2, #4 and #6 during the main game, the Medusa's Gaze Free Games feature is triggered. SCATTER symbols do not appear during free games, meaning the Medusa's Gaze Free Games feature cannot be retriggered. MEDUSA'S GAZE FREE GAMES The Medusa's Gaze Free Games feature is triggered when 3 SCATTER symbols land simultaneously on reels #2, #4 and #6 during the main game. You will be presented with a screen showing 3 shields. Pick a shield to reveal 1 of the 3 high-paying symbols (Siren, Minotaur or Cerberus), then click CONTINUE to begin the 6 free games. The high-paying symbol you picked will lock to the reels when it lands during free games. When the stacked MEDUSA WILD symbol moves to the same reel, the high-paying symbol will unlock from the reels and be removed. The 2 high-paying symbols that were not picked do not appear on the reels during this feature. During the Medusa’s Gaze Free Games feature, the MEDUSA WILD symbol only appears as a stacked shifting wild symbol. The stacked MEDUSA WILD symbol starts free games on reel #6, and shifts 1 reel left on every subsequent spin. The final free game takes place with the stacked MEDUSA WILD symbol on reel #1. During the Medusa’s Gaze Free Games feature the reels are spun automatically using the same bet per line as on the spin that triggered the feature. The win for each spin is displayed in the WIN field below the reels. The total win for the Free Games round is shown in the TOTAL WIN field. When all free games have been played, click CONTINUE on the winnings summary to return to the main game reels. Win requirements (what you need to do to be eligible for the jackpot): Play a slot game with the Age of the Gods™ Jackpot .
