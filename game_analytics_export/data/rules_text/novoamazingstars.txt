Amazing Stars Main Game Your goal is to land five identical symbols along a pay line. In a regular game the symbols in a winning pattern will start on the left side on the first reel and continue on to the right. In order to win, the symbols must be directly next to each other without any other symbols between them. Scatters win in any position regardless of the order on the line. Feature Trigger: 3x, 4x or 5x scattered STAR trigger 10 Free Games. Description: The 3x, 4x or 5x Star symbols (Scatter) which have started the Bonus Feature, are held for the duration of the Bonus Feature. During the Bonus Feature all Star symbols (Scatter) remain in the same position for the duration of the freespins. There is no payout during the free spins. At the end of the free spins, a single win is collected based on the number of Star Symbols (Scatter) landed in accordance with the Paytable. 15x Stars (Scatter) win the Star Jackpot and ends the Bonus Feature. No further free spins can be won during the free spins. Bonus Games are played at current bet. Jackpot Amazing Stars has a common progressive jackpot for all stakes and currencies. 5x ‘7’s on a win line triggers the ‘7 Jackpot’. With the maximum stakes the basic win for 5x ‘7’ and 20% of the Jackpot is paid out (equivalent to the ‘7 Jackpot’ sum shown in the game). 15x Stars (Scatter) wins the ‘Star Jackpot’. With the maximum stakes 100 percent of the Jackpot is paid out (equivalent to the ‘StarJackpot’ sum shown in the game). The ‘Star Jackpot’ can only be won in the Bonus Feature. At lower stakes the corresponding share of the progressive jackpot, depending on the stake amount, is paid out. Top prize: 10000 times bet per line times lines played + percentage of progressive Jackpot. Rules All pays are for wins of left to right except Scatter, Scatter pay anywhere.. All prizes are on selected lines. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
