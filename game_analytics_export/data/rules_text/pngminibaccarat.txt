Mini Baccarat Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you re-open the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days and will be forfeited to charity. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Placing Bets To place a bet, click on the desired chip and then click the game table in the location you wish to place the chip. The chip will be added to your total bet. All bets must conform to the table limits. To remove a bet, select the 'X' chip and then click the chip you wish to remove. To remove all bets, click CLEAR ALL. Game Rules In Baccarat, played with 6 decks, the player and the dealer are dealt two initial cards. They are then dealt one optional card. The winner holds the hand with a value closest to 9. Tens and face cards count as 0. Aces count as 1, and other cards count according to their face values. A bet can be placed on the dealer, the player, or a tie. In the event of a tie, bets placed on the player and the dealer are returned, bets placed on tie pays 8 to 1. Bets placed on the dealer pay even money minus a 5% commission payable to the house. Bets placed on the player pay even money. The player bet RTP is 98,76%. The banker bet RTP is 98,94%. The tie bet RTP is 85,56%. Cards are re-shuffled after each round.
