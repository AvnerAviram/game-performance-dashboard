Hercules High and Mighty Game Help Game Introduction Hercules High and Mighty is a 10 symbol high slot featuring Mighty Reels. During Mighty Reels play over 100 lines and win Free Spins! Mighty Reels The game plays over the bottom half of the reel for up to 50 lines. When a Hercules symbol appears, the portcullis will rise and the game is played over the full height reel. The game then awards wins up to 100 lines. Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. Any part of 3 or more Bonus symbols activate the Free Spins. If in Mighty Reels, Free Spins can be triggered from the top half of the reels Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. In Big Bet spins, any part of Hercules in view will award Mighty Reels. Big Bet games may remove low value symbols which will give bigger wins. Big Bet games may hold Mighty Reels until the end of the game or a win is achieved, increasing the chance of wins over 100 lines. How to Play Select your stake To adjust the STAKE , use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Press SPIN to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains up to 100 win lines. The number of win lines in the game increases with stake up to 50 lines. The number of win lines in the game increases up to 100 lines when in Mighty Reels. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 98% Last modification date: 12/29/2021
