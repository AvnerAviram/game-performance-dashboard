JACKPOTS Three 3X WILDs on payline 9 awards the value displayed in the corresponding Jackpot meter. Three 3X WILDS on paylines 1-8 awards the value displayed in the corresponding Jackpot meter. During the base game, the distribution of outcomes and the probability of winning combinations vary with the bet. Only highest winner paid per winning combinations. All line pays are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. RESPIN BONUS During the base game, if SINGLE BAR or BLUE 7 with a revolution badge forms a win line, then the Respin Bonus will be triggered and 1 respin is awarded. During the Respin Bonus, if SINGLE BAR or BLUE 7 with a revolution badge forms a win line, then 1 additional respin is awarded. A maximum of 5 respins may be played. Each respin is guaranteed to be a winning spin. During the Respin Bonus, the distribution of outcomes and the probability of winning combinations vary with the bet. An alternative set of outcomes is used during the Respin Bonus. Winning combinations are the same as base game spins except not all combinations are available during the bonus.
