PAYTABLE Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Multiple line wins are added together. Mystery Stacked Reels™ During the base game, before pays are evaluated, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, CAPTAIN, PILOT, ATTENDANT, CASH, WHISKEY, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN. In addition, MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a RED COIN, YELLOW COIN, PURPLE COIN, WHITE COIN or any combination of RED COIN, YELLOW COIN, or PURPLE COIN on top of a replaced symbol before pays are evaluated. WILD SYMBOL WILD is WILD for CAPTAIN, PILOT, ATTENDANT, CASH, WHISKEY, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, and TEN. WHEEL BONUS FEATURE If the Wheel Bonus Feature is triggered, then a wheel spin is awarded. The wheel awards a total bet prize, MEGA, GRAND, or a Free Spins Feature. If a Free Spins Feature is awarded, then 1x total bet is also awarded. If the wheel awards a total bet prize, MEGA, or GRAND, then 1 additional wheel spin is awarded. A fifth spin of the wheel only awards the Free Spins Feature. At the end of the Wheel Bonus Feature, the total bet prizes are reset to 1.00x - 10.00x total bet and the Free Spins Feature options are reset to the Current Free Spins Feature options based on the table below for the active bet option. If the MEGA or GRAND meter is awarded, then the value displayed in the corresponding meter is awarded before any additional prizes are awarded. During the base game, a WHITE COIN may trigger the Wheel Bonus Feature. If a WHITE COIN does not trigger the Wheel Bonus Feature, then that WHITE COIN may add 1.00x - 8.00x total bet to a total bet prize on the wheel for the active bet option or may upgrade the Free Spins Feature for the active bet option based on the table below. FREE SPINS FEATURE Any combination of RED COIN, YELLOW COIN, PURPLE COIN may trigger the Free Spins Feature and award 1x total bet. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered by RED COIN, YELLOW COIN, or PURPLE COIN, then, for each coin involved, "WINNER!" appears above RED HEN, YELLOW HEN, or PURPLE HEN, respectively. If a RED COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that RED COIN may add 1 free spin to the RED HEN meter for the active bet option. If a YELLOW COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that YELLOW COIN may add 0.25x-6.75x total bet to the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI meter for the active bet option. If a PURPLE COIN does not trigger the Free Spins Feature, then that PURPLE COIN may increase the ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL by 1 in the PURPLE HEN meter for the active bet option. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with RED COIN, then the number of free spins displayed in the RED HEN meter are awarded. If the Free Spins Feature was not triggered with RED COIN, then 7 free spins are awarded when Free Spins Feature is awarded. The RED HEN meter can display a maximum of 40 free spins. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with RED COIN, the RED HEN meter is reset to 9 free spins. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with YELLOW COIN, then each MYSTERY SYMBOL may reveal a jackpot symbol for the MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, or MINI Jackpots. The MEGA, GRAND, MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI have 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, and 2 spaces respectively. Jackpot symbols are stored in 1 of the spaces of the corresponding meter. If all spaces are filled for a meter, then the jackpot symbols for that meter are cleared, and the corresponding jackpot is awarded and then reset before any additional jackpots are awarded. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered with YELLOW COIN , the MAJOR, MAXI, MINOR, and MINI meters are reset. ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL appears only during Free Spins Feature triggered with PURPLE COIN. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with PURPLE COIN, then for each free spin a number of positions equal to the number displayed under PURPLE HEN will be covered by ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL. Position of ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL may change for every free spin. ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL will reveal WILD, CAPTAIN, PILOT, ATTENDANT, CASH, WHISKEY, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN. Symbols landing under ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL are not evaluated. For each free spin, both MYSTERY SYMBOL and ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL will reveal same symbol. The PURPLE HEN meter may display a maximum of 6 ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL. At the end of the Free Spins Feature that was triggered by PURPLE COIN, the PURPLE HEN meter is reset to 1 ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL. Mystery Stacked Reels™ During the Free Spins Feature, all MYSTERY SYMBOL will be replaced with WILD, CAPTAIN, PILOT, ATTENDANT, CASH, WHISKEY, ACE, KING, QUEEN, JACK, or TEN before pays are evaluated. During the Free Spins Feature, a WHITE COIN may appear on top of a symbol before pays are evaluated. WHITE COIN may award the Bonus Wheel and may award up to 7 additional free spins or may upgrade the Free Spins Feature based on the table below. The Free Spins Feature may be upgraded only once. During the Free Spins Feature, a maximum of 7 additional free spins may be awarded. If the Free Spins Feature was triggered with RED COINYELLOW COINPURPLE COIN, then the Free Spins Feature will not be upgraded. On any free spin, if the Free Spins Feature is upgraded, then the remaining free spins will be played as the Upgraded Free Spins Feature and respective meters will be reset at the end of the Free Spins Feature for the active bet option. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Feature. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except RED COIN, YELLOW COIN, and PURPLE COIN do not appear. The distribution of Free Spins Feature prizes changes with bet stake option. The size of the RED HEN, YELLOW HEN, PURPLE HEN, and WHITE HEN over time has no effect on the outcome. "Wheel Bonus" refers to the Wheel Bonus Feature. "Free Spins" refers to the Free Spins Feature being triggered with RED COIN. "Jackpot Free Spins" refers to the Free Spins Feature being triggered with YELLOW COIN. "Free Spins with ROAMING MYSTERY SYMBOL" refers to the Free Spins Feature being triggered with PURPLE COIN.
