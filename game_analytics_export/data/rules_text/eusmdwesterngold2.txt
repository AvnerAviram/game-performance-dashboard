Western Gold 2 About this Game Welcome to Western Gold 2. This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. This game has 40 lines. It features Free Spins, Super Free Spins, Wild symbols, Buy Free Spins, Buy Super Free Spins, random cash prizes, and Scatter pays. General Win on a line pays if the same symbol appears in succession on adjacent reels, from the leftmost position to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols, Wrangler symbols, Mustang symbols, and Scatters. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Wild symbols can create their own winning combination when landing in a payline combination. Free Spins and Scatter pays cannot trigger in the same spin. Super Free Spins and Scatter pays cannot trigger in the same spin. Wrangler symbols Wrangler symbols only appear on reels 1 and 5 in the base game. Landing 2 Wrangler symbols triggers the Cash Barrel and pays out a random cash prize. Wrangler symbols do not trigger the Cash Barrel on the spin that triggers Super Free Spins. Cash Barrel payouts are added to line wins. Scatters In the base game, landing at least 4 Scatters anywhere on the reels in one spin pays out. In the base game, Scatters pay when the spin is complete. Only the highest paying count of the Scatters pays out. The Scatter payout is awarded as a multiple of the total bet according to the PAYTABLE. Scatter payouts are added to line wins. Free Spins Free Spins symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game. 3 Free Spins symbols awards 3 Free Spins. Free Spins always starts with a Multiplier of x1. Free Spins has a Mustang symbol that exists only on reel 5. Landing a Mustang symbol triggers the Feature Barrel. In Free Spins, the Feature Barrel can award Cash Collect, Barrel Upgrade, Multiplier Boost, Reset Extra or Super Free Spins. In Free Spins, Scatters do not pay when they land, instead they are collected. Collected Scatters are tracked on the Cash Barrel. The Scatter pay is only awarded when the feature is complete and is based on the total number of Scatters collected. A maximum of 12 Scatters can be collected. When one or more Scatters are collected in a spin, and the total number of Scatters collected is less than 12, the Free Spins counter is reset at the end of that spin. When the maximum of 12 Scatters has been collected, any other Scatters remaining on the visible reel area will not be collected, and the Free Spins counter will not reset. The maximum cumulative total win in Free Spins is capped at 25000x bet. When the cap is reached the feature ends. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Super Free Spins Super Free Spins can be triggered from the base game or Free Spins. Landing 3 Free Spins and 2 Wrangler symbols in the base game awards 3 Super Free Spins with a starting Multiplier of x1. If Super Free Spins triggered from the Feature Barrel, all collected Scatters and all features unlocked in Free Spins will carry over to Super Free Spins. The current Scatter pay on the Cash Barrel pays out at the end of each spin. Collected Scatters are tracked on the Cash Barrel. A maximum of 12 Scatters can be collected. When one or more Scatters are collected in a spin, and the total number of Scatters collected is less than 12, the Free Spins counter is reset at the end of that spin. When the maximum of 12 Scatters has been collected, any other Scatters remaining on the visible reel area will not be collected, and the Free Spins counter will not reset. Super Free Spins has a Mustang symbol that exists only on reel 5. The maximum cumulative total win in Super Free Spins is capped at 25000x bet. When the cap is reached the feature ends. Landing a Mustang symbol triggers the Feature Barrel. In Super Free Spins the Feature Barrel can award Barrel Upgrade, Multiplier Boost or Reset Extra. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Super Free Spins bet amount. Mustang Symbol The Mustang symbol exists only on reel 5 in Free Spins and Super Free Spins. When the Mustang symbol lands it resets the Free Spins counter and triggers the Feature Barrel. When 12 Scatters are collected, the Mustang symbol does not reset the counter. Feature Barrel The Feature Barrel can award one of the following features: Cash Collect awards the cash prize of the Scatters currently collected on the Cash Barrel. Cash Collect feature is available after collecting 4 Scatters. Barrel Upgrade upgrades all the prizes on the Cash Barrel. Barrel Upgrade feature can be triggered up to 4 times. Multiplier Boost upgrades the Multiplier by x1. The Multiplier has a cap at x3. The Multiplier only applies to line wins. Reset Extra increases the Free Spins counter reset value by 1. The Free Spins counter has a cap of 6 spins. Super Free Spins upgrades the current Free Spins to Super Free Spins. Cash Collect and Super Free Spins are not available in the Feature Barrel in Super Free Spins. Buy Free Spins In the Buy Free Spins feature, guaranteed Free Spins can be purchased at a multiple of the current bet. 3 Free Spins can be purchased for 85x the current bet. When choosing the Buy Free Spins feature, 3 Free Spins are placed on reels 2, 3, and 4. There is a chance to trigger Super Free Spins on a regular Free Spins purchase. The Buy Free Spins feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Buy Free Spins feature can only be used at any time a bet change can take place. Buy Super Free Spins In the Buy Super Free Spins feature, guaranteed Super Free Spins can be purchased at a multiple of the current bet. 3 Super Free Spins can be purchased for 500x the current bet. When choosing the Buy Super Free Spins feature, 3 Free Spins symbols are placed on reels 2, 3, and 4 and 2 Wrangler symbols are placed on reel 1 and 5. The Buy Super Free Spins feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Buy Super Free Spins feature can only be used at any time a bet change can take place.
