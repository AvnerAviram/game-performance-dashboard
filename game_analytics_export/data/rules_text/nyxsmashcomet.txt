Smash Comet GAME RULES Prizes are displayed as the following amounts times the bet multiplier: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 10, 25 50, 75, 100, 250, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 2500 Prizes are randomly created and move through the play area from top to bottom. The Bet Radius,, may be moved by the player by pressing within the play area. With Auto Spin disabled: A bet will be placed when the player presses and releases within 0.5 seconds. Both the press and release need to take place in the game’s play area. With Auto Spin enabled: Bets will be placed while the player is pressing down in the play area. A bet will only be placed if there is at least one prize located within the Bet Radius. On each bet, one or more of the prize amounts included in the Bet Radius may randomly be awarded. Prizes that are partially within the Bet Radius will be included in the bet. The odds of winning each prize within the Bet Radius are adjusted dynamically depending upon the number and values of all prizes within the Bet Radius, in order to provide the same expected payback percentage regardless of which prizes are bet on. Player skill does not affect the expected payback percentage Before prize amounts are awarded, a random multiplier between 3x and 10x may be applied to all prizes included within the Bet Radius, increasing their value for the current bet. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Incomplete games will automatically complete after 21 days.
