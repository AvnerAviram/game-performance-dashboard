Mammoth Chase About the game Mammoth Chase is a 4x6 reel 4096 ways video slot featuring Free Spins and ProgressiveJackpots Theoretical payout is 96.94% The number of Ways Pays used in the game is fixed at 4096. Game Features WILD Symbols WILD Symbol is wild and substitutes for all other symbols except BONUS TRIGGERsymbol, 2X WILD, and 3X WILD. It counts as 1 matching symbol in pay combinations. 2X WILD and 3X WILD Symbols are wild and substitute for all other symbols exceptBONUS TRIGGER symbol and each other. 2X WILD counts as 2 matching symbols in asingle column and 3X WILD counts as 3 matching symbols in a single column in paycombinations. FREE SPINS 3 or more BONUS TRIGGER symbols in any position initiate the Free Spins. 6 BONUS TRIGGER symbols award 20 free spins. 5 BONUS TRIGGER symbols award 15 free spins. 4 BONUS TRIGGER symbols award 12 free spins. 3 BONUS TRIGGER symbols award 8 initial free spins. During free spins WILD symbols are replaced with 2X WILD and 3X WILD symbols. During Free Spins, additional free spins may be awarded according to the wincombinations above. Additionally, during Free Spins, 2 BONUS TRIGGER symbols in anyposition award 3 extra free spins. BONUS JACKPOTS The game has 3 bonus jackpot levels: Silver, Gold, and Platinum. When a player wins aBonus Jackpot he or she is awarded the value displayed on the interface for the level ofbonus jackpot won at the time that it is won. For every bet placed, each Bonus Jackpotincreases in proportion to the total bet placed. The value of each Bonus Jackpot is savedbetween different sessions for a single player and is saved distinctly for each player. If any combination of 14 or more MAMMOTH and WILD Symbols land on one spin playerwins the Platinum Level Bonus Jackpot; If any combination of 12 or more MAMMOTH and WILD Symbols land on one spin playerwins the Gold Level Bonus Jackpot; If any combination of 10 or more MAMMOTH and WILD Symbols land on one spin playerwins Silver Level Bonus Jackpot. 2X WILD symbols count as 2 symbols each, and 3X WILD symbols count as 3 symbolseach. The Bonus Jackpots are seeded and incremented as below: Platinum Bonus Jackpot Seed: 1000x bet; Player contribution: 1% of total bet Gold Bonus Jackpot Seed: 100x bet; Player contribution: 1.5% of total bet Silver Bonus Jackpot Seed: 20x bet; Player contribution: 2.25% of total bet. Only the highest winner is paid. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/8/2020
