Neptune's Fortune Megaways 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications: Type Slots Number of reels 6 Number of rows 3-6 Number of Ways Up to 46,656 Megaways Number of coins you can bet per line 20(cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Autospin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier Yes Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round Yes Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.03% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. The maximum win from a single game is £190,000 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. Megaways Megaways under license from Big Time Gaming.Each spin can have up to 46,656 ways to win. Wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbols, starting on reel 1, regardless of size. 3.2. Cascading Wins Symbols spin into position to form a number of Megaways. Any win in view will start a Cascade. Symbols in winning combinations will explode and new symbols will drop into position. Cascades will continue until no new wins are formed. 3.3. Wild The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Trident Scatter symbols, to form winning ways. Note : Wild symbols can only appear on reels 2 - 5. 3.4. Trident Scatter symbol Landing 4 or more Trident Scatter symbols will trigger Free Spins. 3.5. Treasure chests Landing a Wild symbol anywhere on the reels could trigger a Treasure chest. Treasure chests 1, 2, 4 or 5 can award multipliers up to x5. Treasure chest 3 can award the Neptune's Bonus. Note : If more than one multiplier has been awarded they would be additive. Any multipliers activated will be totalled and applied to the final win amount of the current win or cascade series. 3.6. Neptune's Bonus Trigger the middle Treasure Chest & it will award the Bonus Game. Pick 3 matching Pearls to win the Mini, Minor, Major or Grand Bonus prize! Possible prizes are: Mini, Minor, Major and Grand and will be payed according to the 3 matching symbols selected. Mini: 20x Bet Minor: 50x Bet Major: 200x Bet Grand: 1000x Bet 3.7. Free Spins Landing 4 or more Trident Scatter symbols will trigger Free Spins. During Free Spins, all low paying symbols are removed. Note : Any Treasure chest can be awarded during Free spins. Free Spins can be re-triggered by landing 4 or more Trident Scatter symbols. The player is awarded: 10 Free spins for 4 Trident Scatter symbols 12 Free spins for 5 Trident Scatter symbols 15 Free spins for 6 Trident Scatter symbols Notes : Bonus= Neptune's Bonus. Bonus_Won= Bonus Win. Value= Possible prize. Option= Pearls picked. MULTIPLIERS= Multipliers. 4. Other game buttons 4.1.
