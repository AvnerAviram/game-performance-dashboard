All Aboard Go West BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 50 credits. All wins are shown in currency except credit prizes. Values for MEGA BONUS, MAXI BONUS, MAJOR BONUS and MINI BONUS scale with the denomination and credits bet. The chance of winning GRAND JACKPOT will change with the credits bet per line.GRAND JACKPOT may be awarded randomly at the end of any bought game. Only Highest Winner Paid for each winning combination. Line pays occur on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel. All wins are added. Reset amount for GRAND JACKPOT scale with the denomination.Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3 [Scatter Symbol] trigger 5 free games. During the Free Game Feature, [Inner Symbol] appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only. At the end of the reel spin, all [Inner Symbol] reveal either [Wild Symbol] or [Bonus Symbol]. If two or more [Inner Symbol] appear, they will all reveal the same symbol. The All Aboard Feature can be triggered during the Free Game Feature. Additional free games can be won during the Free Game Feature. Different Reels are used during the Free Game Feature. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. ALL ABOARD FEATURE Any 6 or more [Bonus Symbol] trigger the ALL ABOARD FEATURE. All remaining positions become individual spinning reels. 3 bonus games are awarded. During the ALL ABOARD FEATURE, only [Bonus Symbol] and [Gold Coin Symbol] can appear on the reels. At the start of the ALL ABOARD FEATURE, all [Bonus Symbol] change to [Gold Coin Symbol] revealing either MEGA BONUS, MAXI BONUS, MAJOR BONUS, MINI BONUS or credit prizes. At the end of each bonus game, each [Bonus Symbol] changes into [Gold Coin Symbol] revealing either MEGA BONUS, MAXI BONUS, MAJOR BONUS, MINI BONUS or a credit prize unless all 15 positions are filled. Credit prizes can be 900, 450, 250, 150, 120, 100, 70 or 50 multiplied by the bet per line. The chance of winning MEGA BONUS, MAXI BONUS, MAJOR BONUS, MINI BONUS or a credit prize will change with the credits bet per line. During bonus games, the [Gold Coin Symbol] are held. If 1 or more [Bonus Symbol] appear during a bonus game, the total prizes on all [Gold Coin Symbol] are awarded, and the bonus games remaining is reset to 3. If no [Bonus Symbol] appear during the ALL ABOARD FEATURE, the initial prizes on GOLD COIN are awarded. The ALL ABOARD FEATURE ends when the number of bonus game remaining is 0 or when all 15 positions are either [Gold Coin Symbol] or [Bonus Symbol]. Different reels are used during the ALL ABOARD FEATURE for different wager amounts. The GRAND JACKPOT is awarded when all 15 positions are GOLD COIN or TRAIN at the end of a bonus game during the ALL ABOARD FEATURE. Credits bet are the same as the game that triggered the feature.
