Ten Ton Ways Odin's Gifts SCATTER During the base game, 4 or more Scatter symbols activate Free Games. Pays anywhere on the reels. Scatter wins are added to the payouts of any other winning combinations. WILD Appears on all reels during the base game. Substitutes for all symbols except Scatters. Appears only during Free Games. Acts as a sticky Wild and remains on the reels until it is part of a winning combination. Substitutes for all symbols except Scatters. Appears on the top reel during both the base game and Free Games. Randomly adds Wilds to the main reels, up to a maximum of 8. Substitutes for all symbols except Scatters. CASCADING WINS After every spin, if a winning ways combination occurs, a cascade sequence is triggered. The cascades repeat until there are no more winning combinations. During a cascade sequence: All symbols in a winning combination are paid and then disappear from the reels. All other symbols remain on the reels. The main reels cascade from top to bottom, while the top reel cascades from right to left. The activation of Free Games occurs after all cascades have been completed. During the base game, a global multiplier applies to both Ways wins and Scatter wins. The multiplier increases after every cascade and is reset to x1 at the start of each spin. The active multiplier is always visible on the screen and applies to all wins. Possible global multipliers are x1, x2, x3, x5, x7, x10, x12, x15, x17, x20, x22, x25, x30, x35, x40, x45, x50. FREE GAMES During the base game, 4 or more Scatter symbols trigger Free Games and award the following: 4 Scatter symbols = 7 Free Games + 2x total bet 5 Scatter symbols = 10 Free Games + 4x total bet 6 Scatter symbols = 15 Free Games + 8x total bet Free Games are played at the same bet value as the round that activated Free Games. Free Games start with an x1 multiplier, which increases after every cascade and does not reset between spins. Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols during Free Games awards 3 additional Free Games. After the Free Games end, the multiplier resets to x1, and the game returns to the base game round that triggered the Free Games. WAYS TO WIN Wins are paid when matching symbols appear on adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel and progressing from left to right. The number of ways is calculated by multiplying the number of symbols on each reel. Only the longest combination of matching symbols is paid. In total, the maximum number of possible ways to win is 10,000. 10,000 Ways to Win! Cascade Win! Malfunction voids all pays and plays For more information, see Game Rules.
