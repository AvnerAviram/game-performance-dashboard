Island Princess Game Features QUAD Shot Bonus Game Symbols The Major Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Game Symbols win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. The Minor Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Game Symbols win occurs with at least one substitute appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol Royals The Maxi Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Royals win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. The Mini Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Royals win occurs with at least one substitute appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. Awards 'Princess' substitutes for all symbols except scatters and 'QUAD Shot BONUS'. 'Princess' appears on reels 3, 4 and 5 during paid games only. ‘Coin’ is a scatter. Free Spins Any 3 or more scatters trigger 8 free games. 'Princess' appears on reel 2, 3 and 4 only. 'Coin' appears on reels 1 and 5 only. Any 2 scatters award 3 additional free games, and add more ‘Princess’ for all remaining free games. During the Feature, the corresponding Feature Paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered this Feature. Win Lines Scatters pay in any position. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. All wins on lit lines only except scatters and Bonuses. Highest win only on each line except scatters and Bonuses. All Wins are added. The game has a return to player of 94.33%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
