Rising Treasures Main Game Your aim is to land 3 or more matching symbols on adjacent reels, on one of the 20 winlines. All winning patterns start on the first reel on the left and run along the win lines to the right Expanding Wilds During both base and free games, 'Wild Temple' symbols appear on reels 2, 3 and 4 and expand to cover all positions on the reel. 'Wild Temple' substitutes for all symbols except scattered 'Statue'. Free Games Feature 12 Free Games are awarded. During Free Games, any 'Wild Temple' symbol appearing expands and upgrades picture symbols. Symbols remain upgraded during the Free Games Feature. Bet staked and lines played are the same as in the game that started the feature. Free Games can be re-triggered. Trigger: 3 or more scattered 'Statue' symbols trigger 12 Free Games. WILD Retrigger: 1, 2 or 3 scattered 'Wild Temple' symbols trigger an additional 1, 3 and 5 Free Games, respectively. Feature hit rate: 90-110 games. Top prize: 500 times credits bet. Rules Wild Temple substitutes for all symbols, except scatters. All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. RTP:95.02% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
