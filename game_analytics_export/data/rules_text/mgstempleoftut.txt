Temple of Tut Welcome to Temple of Tut, a Video Slot featuring Win Both Ways, Super Reels, and Free Spins with Super Reels. Game Rules Temple of Tut is a Video Slot with 5 reels, 3 rows, 10 lines and Win Both Ways. Win on a line pays if 3 or more of the same symbol appear in succession, from the leftmost position to the right or the rightmost to the left according to the PAYTABLE. A line win is calculated by multiplying the symbol win multiplier from the PAYTABLE by the total bet. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols. Wild symbols are only present on the Super Reels. RTP is 96.01% Super Reels The Super Reels randomly appear during the main game. 1-5 reels can turn into Super Reels. The Super Reels contain all symbols with the addition of Wild symbols. Free Spins 3 or more Free Spins symbols appearing anywhere on the reels in the main game activate Free Spins accordingly: 3 Free Spins symbols = 8 Free Spins 4 Free Spins symbols = 15 Free Spins 5 Free Spins symbols = 30 Free Spins The Super Reels will be active in every spin during Free Spins. 2 or more Free Spins symbols appearing anywhere on the reels in the Free Spins award accordingly: 2 Free Spins symbols = +2 Free Spins 3 Free Spins symbols = +8 Free Spins 4 Free Spins symbols = +15 Free Spins 5 Free Spins symbols = +30 Free Spins The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Winnings are automatically collected during this feature.
