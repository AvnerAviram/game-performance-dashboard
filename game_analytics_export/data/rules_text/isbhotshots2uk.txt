Hot Shots 2 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning way spread across the reels. Game specifications: Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 243 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 25 (cannot be changed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus Round No Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP Standard Play: 96% Ultra Bet Spin: 96.50% (91.5% + 5% Ultra Bet Stars) The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000 2. HOW TO PLAY Note : If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins - player input is required. On the info bar, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). To toggle between credit or your real currency, click in the BALANCE area (Available only for specific jurisdictions). 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: 1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Hot Shots symbols, to form winning combinations. Wild symbols can be found only on reels 3, 4 and 5. Every Wild symbol that lands into view expands vertically and horizontally. Wilds will expand one symbol vertically and horizontally. 3.2. Golden Wild The Golden Wild symbol is found in the Free Spins feature and replaces any other symbols, except the Hot Shots symbols, to form winning combinations. Every Golden Wild symbol that lands into view expands vertically and horizontally and increases the Win Multiplier by +1. Golden Wild symbols can be found only on reels 3, 4 and 5. Golden Wilds will expand one symbol vertically and horizontally and increase the multiplier by +1. If a Golden Wild lands in view it will apply new multiplier to that spin and any thereafter. 3.3. Hot Shot Symbol Landing 3 or more Hot Shots Symbols will trigger Free Spins. 3.5. Free Spins Landing 3 or more Hot Shots Symbols will trigger Free Spins: 20 Free Spins for 5 Hot Shots Symbols 15 Free Spins for 4 Hot Shots Symbols 10 Free Spins for 3 Hot Shots Symbols Every Golden Wild symbol that lands into view expands vertically and horizontally and increases the Win Multiplier by +1. Note :Golden Wild symbols can be found only on reels 3, 4 and 5. 4. AUTO SPIN FEATURE The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 4.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins: click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play. Loss limit: click + or - to select the value of the loss limit; Stop if a win reaches: click + or - to select the value of the maximum win; Stop if JACKPOT is won (only for games with Progressive Jackpots): to enable this setting, tick the checkbox; to disable it, remove the tick. Click the ACTIVATE button to close the AUTO SPIN settings window and to start the auto spin feature with the currently selected bet and auto spin setup. Click the CLOSE button to close the window without starting the auto spin feature. Click the STOP AUTO SPIN button to stop the auto spin feature at any time. 4.2.
