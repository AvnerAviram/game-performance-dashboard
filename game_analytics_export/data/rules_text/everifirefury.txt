Fire Fury GAME RULES All pays are shown in credits with bet multiplier applied. HOW TO PLAY Commit a wager for a chance to award prizes. All awarded prizes are displayed in a yellow border until the next wager is committed. On any winning play, 3 - 20 prizes may be awarded. Prizes range from 2 - 5,000 credits times bet multiplier. All prizes displayed have already been multiplied by bet multiplier. The total award for any win during normal play ranges from 12 - 7,602 credits times bet multiplier. The buffalo and flames are for entertainment purposes only and have no effect on the odds of winning. HIGH HEAT FEATURE The High Heat Feature may be activated on any winning play. During the High Heat feature, all awarded prizes are multiplied by a multiplier between 2-7. During the High Heat feature, 3 - 10 prizes may be awarded. All awarded prizes are displayed in a flaming border until the next wager is committed. All awarded prizes are reverted back to their original values upon initiation of the next wager. The total award for any win during the High Heat Feature ranges from 60 - 35,140 credits times bet multiplier. Game will automatically complete after 24-48 hours if left in a feature. Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. Lines pay in any direction on active payline. Payoffs on registered credits only. 2024 Everi Games Inc. All rights reserved.
