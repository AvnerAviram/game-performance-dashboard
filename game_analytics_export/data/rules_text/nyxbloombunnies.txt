Bloomin Bunnies Bloomin’ Bunnies Bloomin’ Bunnies is a Bet 20, 243 ways 3x5 Reel slot game with real persistence trigger, Free Games and Jackpots. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96.10% with any bet. Game Rules Rules Play bet 20 for 243 Ways. All wins shown at 1 credit bet. All wins multiplied by the bet multiplier. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels. Land Scatter Watering Can and pink bud to upgrade all pink buds to pink flowers. Land Scatter Watering Can and purple bud to upgrade all purple buds to purple flowers. Land Scatter Watering Can and yellow bud to upgrade all yellow buds to yellow flowers. Bets can be changed mid way through the 5 spins and spin counters and flower upgrades are saved for each bet option. Wild Collect replaces Wild Rabbit on Spin 5. During Spin 5, land Wild Collect and any flower to collect prizes or Jackpots. All upgraded flowers are removed and turned back to buds once spin 5 is complete. Spin counter resets back to Spin 1 after 5th spin or Free Games. Flower Prizes are available on the 5th spin of any bought game and Free games. Payouts are made according to the Paytable. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. 243 Ways For all symbols except scatters, winning combinations pay through any position on reels 1, 2, 3, 4 & 5 respectively. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Highest win paid per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. Free Games Upgrade pink, purple and yellow buds to flowers in a set of 5 spins to trigger 8 Free Games. Free Games unlocks after the 5th spin. Free Games triggered awards single spin bet back. Land Wild Collect and flower for prizes, Jackpots or 1, 2, 3 or 5 Free Games. Land Scatter Multiplying Watering Can together with any flower and Wild Collect to multiply all prizes and/or Free Games by 2x, 3x or 4x. All wins paying left to right including Scatter Multiplying Watering Can is multiplied by 2x, 3x or 4x. Landing a Wild Collect without a flower or Scatter Multiplying Watering Can awards 1 Free Game. Landing a Wild Collect and Scatter Multiplying Watering Can without a flower awards 2, 3 or 4 Free Games (based on the multiplier displayed on the Scatter Multiplying Watering Can). Free games are played at the triggering single spin bet. Free games cannot be retriggered. Jackpots Jackpot prizes are fixed, are not progressive, and are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Jackpot prizes are collected when a Minor or Major flower lands together with a Wild Collect. Minor and Major Jackpot Prizes may be won more than once. How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. The game displays all your details in the following display fields Balance: Displays your total balance Win: Displays the amount won in the spin Total Bet: Displays the total wager staked Total Win: Displays the accumulated wins during free games. AUTOPLAY View available AUTOPLAY options. AUTOPLAY allows automatic consecutive normal game plays. TOTAL BET View available bet options. TOTAL BET equals the Bet Multiplier x the coin value.
