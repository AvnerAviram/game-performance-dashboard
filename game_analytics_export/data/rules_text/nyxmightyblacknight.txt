Mighty Black Knight Game Introduction Mighty Black Knight is a 5 reel slot with each reel 10 symbols high. During Mighty Reels play over 100 lines and win Free Spins! 3 or more Scatter Symbols award Free Spins. Mighty Reels The game plays over the bottom half of the reel for up to 50 lines. When a Wild Knight symbol appears, it expands, raising the portcullis, exposing the top half of the reels and the game is played over the full height reel. The game then awards wins up to 100 lines. Free Spins The player can win Free Spins in every game. Any part of 3 or more Bonus symbols activate the Free Spins. If in Mighty Reels, Free Spins can be triggered from the top half of the reels. Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. In Big Bet spins, any part of the Wild Knight symbol in view will award Mighty Reels. Big Bet games may remove low value symbols which will give bigger wins. Big Bet games may hold Mighty Reels until the end of the game or a win is achieved, increasing the chance of wins over 100 lines. Additional information can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. How to Play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Paylines The game contains up to 100 win lines. The number of paylines in the game increases with stake up to 50 lines. The number of paylines in the game increases up to 100 lines when in Mighty Reels. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96%. Malfunction voids all pays and play. Last modification date: 12/29/2021
