Tiki Fortune Tiki Fortune is the second math model in the Ultimate Choice Jackpots game family, featuring a 3-level Jackpots. The game features short term persist WILD REEL in the base game where entire reel becomes WILD for a limited period. Jackpot Pick Bonus is the true pick bonus where player can win either Credit prize or one of Jackpots. GAME FEATURES Wild Reel Feature During Base game, collecting 3 COINs on a reel will activate WILD REEL for that reel. COINs may have multipliers of 2x or 3x. COINs appearing on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. WILD REEL turns all symbols into WILD for next 3 spins. (BN turns into BW) Multiplier COIN activates MULTIPLIER WILD REEL for that reel. MULTIPLIER WILD REEL turns all symbols into WILD MULTIPLIER for next 3 spins. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Free Spins Bonus 5 Free Spins are awarded by 3 Bonus or Bonus Wild symbols At the start of free games, 1x, 2x, 3x and/or 5x COINs are placed on top of Reels 2, 3 and 4. Each free games, COINs on Reels 2, 3 and/or 4 may activate WILD REEL or MULTIPLIER WILD REEL for that reel. Jackpot Pick Bonus During base game, any COIN on the screen may trigger Jackpot Pick Bonus randomly. During Jackpot Bonus, player may select 1 of 10 objects. Objects may reveal and award credit prizes or JACKPOT which reveal and awards one of jackpot prizes.
