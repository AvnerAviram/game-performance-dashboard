Goldwyns Fairies About Goldwyns Fairies Welcome to Goldwyn’s Fairies, a Video Slot featuring Wild substitution, Re-spin and Free Spins with RETURNING WILD feature. Game Rules Goldwyn’s Fairies is a Video Slot with 5 reels, 3 rows and 20 lines. Win on a line pays if 3 or more of the same symbol appear in succession, from the leftmost to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. A line win is calculated by multiplying the symbol win multiplier from the PAYTABLE by the total bet. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild Symbols There are 3 different Wild symbols in the game: Classic Wild symbol, Re-spin Wild symbol and Free Spin Wild symbol. All are regarded as Classic Wild symbols when calculating payout. Free Spin Wild symbol and Re-spin Wild symbol can appear on any reel during their respective game mode, except during Final Free Spin and Final Re-spin. Classic Wild symbol can appear on any reel during Regular game mode, Final Free Spin and Final Re-spin. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Re-spin with RETURNING WILD feature 1 Re-spin Wild symbol on reel 3 awards Re-spin mode with RETURNING WILD feature and a Final Re-spin. Any Classic Wild present on the reels when Re-spin mode is awarded turns into a RETURNING WILD. Re-spin mode continues until no more Re-spin Wild symbols appear. In Regular game mode, the Re-spin Wild symbol can only appear on reel 3. The Re-spin Wild symbol is the only wild symbol that appears during Respin mode except during the Final Re-spin. It is not possible to trigger the Re-spin and Free Spins mode in the same spin. It is not possible to trigger Free Spins within Re-spin mode. Free Spins with RETURNING WILD feature 3 Free Spins symbols appearing anywhere on reel 1, 3 and 5 award 10 free spins. 9 free spins with RETURNING WILD feature and 1 Final Free Spin. Free Spin Wild symbol is the only wild symbol that appears during Free Spins mode except during the Final Free Spin. It is not possible to trigger Re-spin within Free Spins mode. Free Spins cannot be retriggered. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Winnings are automatically collected during this feature. RETURNING WILD feature The position where the Re-spin Wild or Free Spin Wild appeared is marked with a RETURNING WILD until the Final Re-spin or Final Free Spin. During the Final Re-spin and Final Free Spin all positions marked with RETURNING WILD turn into Classic Wild Symbols. If a Re-spin or Free Spin Wild symbol lands on a RETURNING WILD, another RETURNING WILD will appear on a random position on the reels where no RETURNING WILD is present. During Re-spin and Free Spins mode RETURNING WILD can appear on maximum of 15 positions. Only Classic Wilds are present during the Final Re-spin and Final Free Spin.
