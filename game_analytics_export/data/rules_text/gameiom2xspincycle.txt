2X Spin Cycle 2X Spin Cycle combines classic slot symbols with exciting features in a 3x3, 5-line game. Multipliers boost wins or trigger the highest jackpot when three appear on a payline. Landing three Wheel Symbols activates a bonus wheel, awarding exciting free games or a jackpot prize. Retriggers during free games enhance the thrill, making each spin a high-stakes adventure. GAME FEATURES Multiplier Feature Multiplier Symbol appears on all reels and is wild for all symbols except Wheel Symbol 1 Multiplier Symbol in a non-jackpot winning combination will double the win. 2 Multiplier Symbol in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 4x the win. 3 Multiplier Symbols appearing on a payline awards the highest jackpot. Wheel Feature 3 Wheel Symbols in any position during base game awards 1 wheel spin. Wheel spin awards either free games or jackpot prize. During free games, the player can retrigger additional wheel spins. 2 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 1 additional wheel spin. 3 Wheel Symbols in any position awards 3 additional wheel spins. The odds of wheel bonus prizes are not equal in value.
