Epic Monopoly II Base Game WILD is wild for all symbols except BONUS. BONUS only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 of the MAIN REEL SET and reels 1, 3, and 5 of the COLOSSAL REEL SET. Only highest winner paid per winning combination on each reel set. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel on each reel set. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Line pays do not continue from one reel set to the next. Any four stacked WILD appearing on the MAIN REEL SET start a symbol transfer, turning the corresponding reel on the COLOSSAL REEL SET into WILD. All pays are evaluated after WILD transfers. Each reel set uses an alternate set of reels. Epic Wheel Bonus 3 or more scattered BONUS across both reel sets triggers the Epic Wheel Bonus. 6 scattered BONUS awards Epic Wheel Bonus + 100 x total bet 5 scattered BONUS awards Epic Wheel Bonus + 50 x total bet 4 scattered BONUS awards Epic Wheel Bonus + 10 x total bet 3 scattered BONUS awards Epic Wheel Bonus BONUS only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 of the MAIN REEL SET and reels 1, 3, and 5 of the COLOSSAL REEL SET. Click/Touch WHEEL to begin the Epic Wheel Bonus. The following prizes can be awarded in the Epic Wheel Bonus: Monetary award of 500x bet per 2 lines 7, 10, or 20 Free Spins 2, 5, or 10 dice rolls for the Around the Board Bonus Epic Chance Bonus 2x and Spin Again. Once this prize is awarded, it is replaced by a monetary award of 250x bet per 2 lines Epic Free Spin Bonus During the free spins, WILD is wild for all symbols except BONUS. BONUS only appears on reels 1, 3, and 5 of the MAIN REEL SET and reels 1, 3, and 5 of the COLOSSAL REEL SET. If one or more WILD from a stack appears on a reel of the MAIN REEL SET, the rest of the stack will expand, making all four reel positions on the reel WILD. The WILD will then transfer over to the COLOSSAL REEL SET, making the corresponding reel WILD. All pays are then evaluated. During free spins, an alternate set of reels is used for each reel set. The bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the Bonus. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. Epic Wheel Bonus cannot be triggered during the free spins. During free spins, 6, 5, 4 or 3 scattered BONUS across both reel sets adds additional free spins to the amount of free spins remaining equal to the amount of spins originally awarded, and award 100x, 50x, and 10x total bet respectively. Any multiplier awarded by the Epic Wheel Bonus or Epic Chance Bonus applies to all free spins awarded. All scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter pays are added to payline wins. Only highest winner paid per winning combination on each reel set. Epic Chance Bonus Pick from 5 CARD for one of the following prizes: Monetary prize of 200x-500x bet per 2 lines 20 Free Spins 10 dice rolls for the Around the Board Bonus 2x and Pick Again, or 3x and Pick Again, or 5x and Pick Again Around the Board Bonus Click/Touch ROLL to roll the dice. The player's token will advance the number of squares indicated by the dice roll outcome. When the player's token lands, a prize will be awarded: Property: Choose one of 5 property cards for one of the following prizes: Monetary award of 25x-300x bet per 2 lines; or 2x-5x and Pick Again Electric Company: Monetary award of 40x-1500x bet per 2 lines Income or Super Tax: Monetary award of 40x-200x bet per 2 lines Just Visiting: Monetary award of 40x-160x bet per 2 lines Free Parking: Monetary award of 80x-400x bet per 2 lines and an extra dice roll GO: Landing on or passing GO awards a Monetary award of 200x-800x bet per 2 lines Water Works: Choose one of 3 faucets for one of the following prizes: A monetary award of 25x-200x bet per 2 lines; or A monetary award of 25x-200x bet per 2 lines and an extra dice roll Station: Choose one of the 4 Stations for one of the following prizes: A monetary award of 40x-300x bet per 2 lines; 2x-5x and Pick Again; or A monetary award of 40x-200x bet per 2 lines plus an extra dice roll Multipliers apply to monetary award only Chance: Choose one of 3 Chance cards for one of the following prizes: A monetary award of 40x-80x bet per 2 lines; Advance to Mayfair; A monetary award of 25x bet per 2 lines plus an extra dice roll; Advance to nearest Station (Collect the GO Award if you pass GO!); or Advance to nearest Utility (Collect the GO Award if you pass GO!) Community Chest: Choose one of 3 Community Chest cards for one of the following prizes: A monetary award of 60x-100x bet per 2 lines; A monetary award of 20x-40x bet per 2 lines plus an extra dice roll; or A monetary award of 120x-200x bet per 2 lines plus the 2 unselected Community Chest card prizes; Go to Jail: You will be moved to Jail; you will not pass GO and you will not get the Just Visiting award Bonus play will continue until all dice rolls are exhausted. Any multiplier awarded by the triggering Epic Wheel Bonus or Epic Chance Bonus applies to all dice rolls awarded. Except for Free Parking, no extra dice rolls are awarded once the player has gone around the MONOPOLY board 3 times. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The Total Bet is the line bet amount added for every 2 lines. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96.01%.
