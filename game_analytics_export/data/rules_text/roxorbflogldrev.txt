Buffalo Gold Revolution GAME RULES All Pays XTRA REEL POWERTM cost is 75 coins. Bet is 75 coins x Coin Size. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Winning symbols can occur anywhere on all reels. Each symbol can be used once per winning combination. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. Each winning combination is made up of only one symbol from each reel. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. Scatter wins are added to XTRA REEL POWER wins. RTP: 95.05% The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 10/30/2024
