Blazin' Bank Run Blazin’ Bank Run is an exciting and new slots experience with various features that can expand your earnings. The Cash Vault Feature allows players to select between two vaults for a chance at triggering the Free Games Bonus or the Money Charge Bonus. The Free Games Bonus awards players up to 15 free games with up to 200 Wild Symbols added to the reels. The Money Charge Bonus is activated by landing 6 or more Money Ball symbols during the base game or by triggering the Cash Vault Feature. This Hold and Spin bonus locks in all Money Ball symbols that appear on the reels, and gives player chances of winning multiple Jackpots.
