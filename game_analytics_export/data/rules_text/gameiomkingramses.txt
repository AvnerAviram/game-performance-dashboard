King Ramses Game Features Game Symbols The Major Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Game Symbols win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'TRIPLE Shot BONUS' symbol. Royals The Maxi Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Royals win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'TRIPLE Shot BONUS' symbol. The Minor Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind win occurs with at least one substitute appearing in the winning combination and a 'TRIPLE Shot BONUS' symbol. Awards 'Pharaoh' substitutes for all symbols except scatters and ‘TRIPLE Shot BONUS’. 'King Ramses' is a scatter. Free Spins Any 3, 4 or 5 scatters trigger 10, 15 or 25 free games respectively. 'Pharaoh' appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Any 2 or more scatters will trigger an additional 5 free games. During this Feature, the corresponding Feature Paytable is applied. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered this Feature. Win Lines Scatters pay in any position. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line except scatters and bonuses. All wins are added. The game has a return to player of 94.31%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/5/2020
