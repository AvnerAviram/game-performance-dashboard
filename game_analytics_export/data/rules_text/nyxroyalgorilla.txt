COLLECT FEATURE When a WILD appears, all SCATTER FLOWER prizes are immediately awarded, and are not held for any HOLD & RESPIN feature triggered on that spin. Free Spins feature can also be won randomly on any paid bet when no HOLD & RESPIN feature is triggered and no Free Spin feature is won from SCATTER BONUS symbols. 10 Free Spins are awarded. WILD MULTI starts at 2. During Free Spins, whenever a WILD appears, the WILD MULTI is increased by 1 for the duration of the Free Spins. All SCATTER FLOWER symbols are held and other positions spun. Respins reset to 3 if another SCATTER FLOWER or WILD appears. SCATTER FLOWER prizes can be 0.5x, 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 10x, 20x, 25x, or 50x the total bet. All prizes are immediately awarded and not held if WILD appears during HOLD & RESPIN. All prizes that are collected with WILD will be multiplied by WILD MULTI. During HOLD & RESPIN, WILD can land on only one reel per spin. If all positions are filled with SCATTER FLOWER symbol, bonus of 250x total bet is awarded. Respins are completed if 0 respins remain or all reel positions are filled with SCATTER FLOWER symbols. GAME RULES Player selects a Total Bet size to play 178 Connecta Ways. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Coinciding wins are added. Highest win paid per winning combination. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels. Each symbol in the win can only be used once per winning combination. WILD appears on reels 2 and 4 during base game and Free Spins only. WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER BONUS and SCATTER FLOWER. Each WILD in a win multiplies the prize by the WILD MULTI. WILD MULTI is 2x during base game. WILD MULTI during the features is shown in the WILD MULTI panel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. CONNECTA WAYS All symbols are part of a CONNECTA WAYS win if they are on touching positions on adjacent left to right reels starting from reel 1. HOW TO PLAY Pick your bet amount and push spin button. Spin Button Places a bet then spins the reels. Bet Buttons Adjusts the BET staked. (+) button raises your BET, (-) button lowers your BET. Menu Button View the MENU, showing buttons (if available) such as BET, AUTOPLAY, SOUND, HOME, CASHOUT, HISTORY, and HELP. Not all buttons are available in all environments. Sound Buttons Turn sounds On or Off respectively. Info Button Display info pages. Autoplay Button (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) Opens Autoplay configuration window, where the player can select the desired number of autoplay and start autoplay. Bet Settings Button Opens Bet configuration window, where player can select the desired bet amount. Return to Game Button Go back to base game. Stop Autoplay Button (Optional, only applicable in some jurisdictions) Stops autoplay. Number of autoplays remaining will be shown over the button. Balance Meter Display the player's current balance. Total Bet Meter Display the total wager staked. Win Meter Display the current win amount. Fast Forward Button Fast Forward speeds up in-game animations. Fast Forward can be turned on or off at any time. Activating Fast Forward does not influence the result of spins. If supported in your jurisdiction, Fast Forward speeds up each spin.
