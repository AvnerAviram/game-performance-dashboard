Select Your Stake And Press Start Game To Begin. How To Play You Have 10 Spins To Match Numbers On The Reel With The Grid. Complete Slingos (Win Lines) To Trigger Bonus Games. Jokers Allow You To Mark Any Number In The Row Above. Super Jokers Allow You To Mark Any Number In The Grid. Devils Are Blocking Symbols. Bonus Spin Symbols Add An Extra Spin.
