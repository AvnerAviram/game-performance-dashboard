The Eternal Widow About this Game This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. This game has 30 lines. It features Wild Frenzy, Both Ways pay on Widow symbols, Free Spins with the Widow's Thirst, and Bloodlust features. General Win on a line pays if the same symbol appears in succession on adjacent reels, from the leftmost position to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Widow symbols in a winning combination on adjacent reels pays left to right, beginning with the leftmost reel and right to left, beginning with the rightmost reel. Only the highest win per line from both ways is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Wild symbols can appear anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols. Wild Frenzy Trigger Every time a Wild symbol lands there is a chance to trigger the Wild Frenzy feature. The Wild Frenzy feature cannot trigger on a spin that triggers the Free Spins feature. The Wild Frenzy feature cannot trigger during the Free Spins or the Wild Frenzy features. Wild Frenzy Wild Frenzy awards 1 extra spin. Wild symbols that are on the visible reel area when Wild Frenzy triggers become sticky for the duration of the Wild Frenzy. The Wild Frenzy feature randomly awards between 2 - 9 additional Wild symbols. The additional Wild symbols can only be placed on reels 2, 3 and 4. The additional Wild symbols cannot be placed on positions that are already occupied by another Wild symbol. The Free Spins feature cannot trigger during the Wild Frenzy feature. Free Spins If 3, 4, or 5 Free Spins symbols have landed anywhere on the reels, then 8, 12, or 16 Free Spins are awarded, respectively. The Free Spins includes the Widow's Thirst and Bloodlust feature. After each spin, all Widow and Wild symbols that landed will be collected. When 10 Widow symbols have been collected, the Widow's Thirst feature will be awarded. Any Widow symbols that were collected in addition to the 10 required will count towards the next Widow's Thirst feature. When 10 Wild symbols have been collected, the Bloodlust feature will be awarded. Any Wild symbols that were collected in addition to the 10 required will count towards the next Bloodlust feature. The Free Spins end when there are no more Free Spins left. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Bloodlust Any wins will be paid out before the Bloodlust feature is awarded. Wild symbols start with a multiplier of 1. The Bloodlust feature will increase the Wild symbol multiplier by 1. Each time the Bloodlust feature is triggered +1 Free Spins is awarded. Once the Wild symbol multiplier has reached 5, no more Wild symbols will be collected. Any line win including a Wild symbol will be multiplied by the current Wild symbol multiplier. Multiple Wild symbols on the same line win only apply the multiplier once. Widow's Thirst Any wins will be paid out before the Widow's Thirst feature is awarded. The Widow's Thirst feature will upgrade a symbol to the next more valuable symbol according to the PAYTABLE. Upgraded symbols will remain upgraded for the duration of Free Spins. Each time the Widow's Thirst feature is triggered +1 Free Spins is awarded. Once all upgradable symbols have been upgraded to Widow symbols, no more Widow symbols will be collected. Lobby button: Navigate back to the lobby. Free Games A Free Games offer is a number of free game spins awarded to a player. Each offer has a unique reference number. The bet configuration is set by the game managers and cannot be changed. Wins are added to the cash or bonus balance, depending on the offer. An offer can be played now, played later, or discarded. Select Play Now to begin playing a Free Games offer. Only one Free Game offer can be played at a time. Select Play Later to postpone the offer. A postponed offer will be available again when re-entering the game. Select the Bin icon to discard the Free Games. A discarded offer can only be re-opened by contacting the game managers and providing the reference number. When an offer is complete, discarded, or expired the next available offer is displayed. Each offer has an expiry date and time; if an offer is not played before this time, it will no longer be available. Select the Information icon to view bet information and the offer expiry date and time. Additional Information Some settings and features may not be available in this game. In the event of a disconnection, the final game state is displayed on return to the game. The balance is updated with the amount won or lost. The results of the last game played are displayed. If a request does not reach the server before disconnection, the results of the previous game played are displayed. Any features in progress that require interaction or selection can be continued. Return to Player The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by piding the total payouts by the total bets from a simulation of numerous game rounds. Values reflect the maximum expected return and do not change. RTP: 94.19%.
