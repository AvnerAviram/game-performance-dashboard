Free Games The appearance of 3, 4, or 5 initiates 7, 12, or 25 Free Games, respectively. The appearance of 3, 4, or 5 during Free Games retriggers 7, 12, or 25 Free Games, respectively. During Free Games, only will appear on the grid. During Free Games, each time at least one lands on the grid, the Progressive Pick Feature could be randomly triggered. Progressive Jackpot The Progressive Pick Feature must be accessed in order to win 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. The 4 Progressive Jackpots are: Mini, Minor, Major, and Mega. If 3 Minis are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Mini Progressive is awarded. If 3 Minors are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Minor Progressive is awarded. If 3 Majors are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Major Progressive is awarded. If 3 Megas are uncovered during the Progressive Pick Feature, the value of the Mega Progressive is awarded. It is not technically possible to have two simultaneous wins of the same Progressive Jackpot. There are separate Progressive Jackpots per bet multiplier. Contributions made to Progressive Jackpots at a certain bet multiplier are added to Progressive Jackpots that can only be achieved by making a bet at that same bet multiplier. The following contributions are made to Progressive Jackpots on each base game spin: .33% of the bet is contributed to the Mini Progressive Jackpot. .26% of the bet is contributed to the Minor Progressive Jackpot. .21% of the bet is contributed to the Major Progressive Jackpot. .15% of the bet is contributed to the Mega Progressive Jackpot. At a 1x bet multiplier, the start-up value for each Progressive Jackpot is: Mini: $2.50 Minor: $7.50 Major: $25.00 Mega: $150.00 The start-up values for the Progressive Jackpots at bet multipliers other than 1x are calculated by multiplying the bet multiplier by the start-up value at a 1x bet multiplier.
