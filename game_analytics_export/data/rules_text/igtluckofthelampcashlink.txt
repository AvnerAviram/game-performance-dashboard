Luck of the Lamp CashLink 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 20 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet Bet: 20 Side Bet: 30 Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols Yes Scatter symbols No Bonus round No Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP Normal Game: 94.00% Side bet: 94.04% Bonus buy: 94.25% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except the Gems , Collect & Lamp symbols, to form winning combinations. 3.2. Gems & Collect Cash Gems award Cash Values , as multiples of the bet, when landing with a Collect symbol. Cash Gems possible values are the following: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 25x, 30x. Jackpot Gems award MINI (50x), MAJOR (100x) or MEGA (250x) Jackpots when landing with a Collect symbol. Free Spins Gems award Free Spins when landing with a Collect symbol. The Collect symbol can land on any spin and collect all Cash Gems , Jackpot Gems and Free Spins Gems in view. Note : Collect can only land on Reel 5. 3.3. Lamp Modifiers If the Magic Lamp lands on Reel 5 it will trigger one of the following modifiers: Multiplier : The Genie will add a Win Multiplier of x2, x3, x5 or x10. Magic Wild : The Genie will drop 3-12 Wild symbols onto the reels. Wild Reels : The Genie will trigger between 1-5 Wild reels. 3.4. Free Spins Landing a Collect symbol with any Free Spins Gems will trigger Free Spins . The number of Free Spins awarded is the total of Free Spins Gem values in view. During Free Spins , Collect will collect any Cash Gems , Jackpot Gems or Free Spins Gems in view. Note : Gem symbols can be stacked during Free Spins . Note : Collect can only land on Reel 5. 3.5. Side Bet Activate the Side Bet for increased chance of Free Spins . The Side Bet will add 50% to the cost of each spin. Activating the Side Bet will not increase the paytable. 3.6. Bonus Buy Bonus Bu y costs 60 x Total bet. Bonus Buy is guaranteed to trigger Free Spins . Note : Bonus Buy awards 10-40 initial Free Spins . 4. Other game buttons 4.1. Auto spin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the auto spin feature automatically when the selected limit, plus your cumulative winnings, and minus your cumulative bets, is less than 0. 5.1. Desktop version Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN settings window where you can set up the following: Number of auto spins : click + or - to select the number of auto spins to play.
