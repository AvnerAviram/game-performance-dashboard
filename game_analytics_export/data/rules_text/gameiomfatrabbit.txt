Fat Rabbit A 96,45% RTP medium volatile slot, the base game has a 5 x 5 reel setup with 50 win lines. Fire up the tractor and get ready for some fun on the farm with Fat Rabbit. Plant those crunchy wild carrots for a rich harvest! Fat Rabbit offers players the chance to multiply their bet up to 6 405 times! Base Game Lookout for the Wild Rabbit or at least one Wild Carrot, if it lands anywhere on the reel it will start the Free Games Feature. Free games feature Feed the rabbit a carrot and watch him grow! In the Free Games Feature, a carrot meter will show on the screen the amount of Carrot Symbols needed to level up along with rabbit sizes for different levels. During the free spins, the Rabbit Symbol will eat any landing Carrot Symbols and add them to the carrot meter. Any collected additional Carrot Symbols will be carried to the next level. Expanding Wild Rabbit Keep the carrots coming to fatten him up into a bigger and juicier wild. The rabbit keeps on growing throughout the game. Snatch those carrots and chase that rabbit for hopping fat wins! The Rabbit Symbol will move up on the meter, grow and display the level up every time required Carrot Symbols are collected. The required Carrot Symbols will be refreshed at each level. The number of required carrots will progress while the Rabbit Symbol grows. When moving to the next level, 2 additional spins are awarded.
