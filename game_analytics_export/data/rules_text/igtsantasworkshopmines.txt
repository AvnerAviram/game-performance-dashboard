Santa's Workshop Mines 1. Overview The goal is to find Teddy Multipliers before uncovering a Jester Mine, which will end the game round. Game specifications : Type Mines Grid 5 x 5 grid (25 squares) Winning symbol Teddy Multiplier Losing symbol Jester Mine RTP 94.00% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. Malfunction void all pays and plays. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. On the info bar, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). To toggle between credit or your real currency, click in the BALANCE area (Available only for specific jurisdictions). 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Use the upper + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total BET is displayed in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). To toggle between credit or your real currency, click in the BALANCE area (Available only for specific jurisdictions). Use the lower + and - buttons to select the number of Jester Mines. Note: The more Jester Mines you choose, the faster your winnings can grow - but Teddy Multipliers are harder to find. Click PLAY! to start and choose a square to uncover. Note: Each Teddy Multiplier found will increase your prize, a Jester Mine will end the game round. Click CASH OUT to end the round and cash out. The total amount won will be shown in the WIN field from the info bar, in either credit or your real currency, depending on the current user's choice (Available only for specific jurisdictions). For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the paytable for that winning combination. Cash Out Shows the current value of your prize, which you can choose to collect ( Cash Out ). After a Teddy Multiplier is found, this value will increase and you can Cash Out after any successful pick. 3.2. Remaining Picks Shows the number of remaining picks you are allowed to make. Depending on the number of Jester Mines , you will have a set number of initial picks available. After each Teddy Multiplier is found, the number of remaining picks will count down by one. Note: If remaining picks run out, you will have reached the Maximum Win . 3.3. Maximum Win Shows the maximum possible win you can reach with the current grid. Maximum Win is dependent on the initial bet and the number of starting Jester Mines . Note: If Maximum Win is reached, no more picks are available and you must Cash Out . 3.4. Payouts The payout for the game is determined by a bet multiplier which increases after each successful pick. The multiplier is dependent on the number of winning picks found and the number of Jester Mines . 3.5. Next Win Shows the value of the next Teddy Multiplier if you choose to continue the round. 3.6. Found Shows how many Teddy Multipliers have been found from the total number on the grid. 4.
