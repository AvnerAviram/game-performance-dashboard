Lone Star Link: Eagle Wins Game Theme and Graphics Lone Star Link™ Eagle Wins is a 5 reel slot with 576 different ways to win and a jam-packed bonus feature. Main Game With Bonus Spins Feature, Eagle Wins Collect and Linked Jackpots, this slot is sure to keep you entertained. Your aim in Lone Star Link™ Eagle Wins™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 576 winning paths running from left to right. Bonus Spins Feature Look out for Star symbols, when 5 or more Star symbols appear anywhere on screen they trigger the Bonus Spins Feature. Any Star symbols are held and 3 bonus spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Star lands on the reels, the bonus spins counter is reset to 3. All prizes on Star symbols are paid at the conclusion of the bonus spins, or if the screen is completely filled with Stars. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on screen are opened and filled with Stars. Star symbol prizes can award, Major, Minor or Mini Jackpots or up to 40 x total bet. Getting a +1 Row Star will add another row to the Bonus Spins Feature. Landing a Multiplier Star will increase the multiplier on the left hand side of the reels, when the feature ends all Star Prizes will be multiplied by this multiplier (except for the Grand Jackpot and Major Jackpot). Eagle Wins Collect Any game with one or more Stars on screen, and not triggering the Bonus Spins Feature, may randomly trigger the Eagle Wins Collect. All Star prizes on screen will be collected and awarded immediately Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Star, Multiplier Star or Extra Row Star symbols. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 11/19/2024
