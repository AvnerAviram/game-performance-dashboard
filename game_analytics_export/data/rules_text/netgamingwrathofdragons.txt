Wrath of the Dragons Wrath of The Dragons Game Rules By playing our games, you agree that you do not find them offensive, unfair or indecent. How to Play Slots: Choose the amount you would like to bet with. Control the bet amount by clicking the arrows next to the Coin Value or choose Max Bet (Max Coin Value). Your winnings are automatically added to your account balance. You can see your Total Win displayed in the WIN section of the screen after each spin. Game Rules: The game is played with 10 fixed paylines and each game costs a minimum of 10 fixed coins. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Payline prizes are multiplied by coin value. All symbols pay from left to right in consecutive order, excluding the Bonus symbol, which pays in any direction. Highest win only paid per selected line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For this game, the long-term expected payback is 96.12%. Bonus Symbol: Bonus symbol wins are multiplied by the total cash bet. Landing 3 or more Bonus symbols starts a Free Game with 8, 10 or 12 Free Spins and coins. Free Spins can be re-triggered during the Free Game and will award the same amount of Free Spins. Wild Symbol: Wild symbols substitute for all symbols, excluding the Bonus symbol. Game Features: Wild Reels are generated in the main game and award a Free Game if there are less than 3 Bonus symbols on the reels. All winnings containing a Wild symbol are multiplied by the ID of the reel from which the Wild Reel is positioned. In case of more than one active Wild Reel the highest multiplier is used for all wins. Spawning Wild Reels trigger a series of Re-Spins. With each Re-Spin all Wild Reels move one position to the right. Re-Spins end when there are no more Wild Reels present. Different reel sets are used for Re-Spins. In case of multiple Wild Reels, the highest reel set is used. Bonus symbols or additional Wild Reels cannot appear during Re-Spins. Game Controls: This section lists the different buttons found in the game and describes their functions. Stop on single win limit: Based on the limit value selected, the Auto-bet function is stopped when your total win in a single game reaches the selected value. Stop on session loss limit: Based on the limit value selected, the Auto-bet function is stopped when total loss in the session reaches the selected value, which is derived by bet minus wins. If the game is interrupted due to connection loss during Free Spins or Bonus Rounds, the game will resume the remaining Free Spins or the pending Bonus Round upon re-opening. Auto Spin Game Rules: Auto Spin is similar to regular spins; you can choose Auto Spin for a selected number of spins. As soon as Auto Spin begins, the Auto Spin button is replaced by a counter displaying the remaining Auto-bet rounds. The Auto Spin button changes to the Stop Auto Spin icon. Auto Spin automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. Auto Spin can be stopped at any time by clicking the Auto Spin (Auto Stop) button. Auto Spin settings will return to default when you get any Feature Round (Free Spins/Bonus Round). Auto Spin will continue after the completion of Re-Spins. If you are disconnected while playing, all Auto-play settings will return to default when you reload the game. The in-game paytable shows symbol payouts as the multipliers on the total bet amount, which is calculated as 'total bet = (coin value * number of fixed coins)'. Hence the actual payouts will be coin value * paytable factor when bet lines are kept constant.
