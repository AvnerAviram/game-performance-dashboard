Hot Stepper MAIN GAME Any winning spin steps the reels to the right and adds a new bigger reel to the left. The new reel spins for free until no win is achieved for you invert the reels. Each new win is paid independently. BONUS ROUND 3 Or More [Bonus] Across any game triggers the bonus round. [Double Bonus] counts as two bonus symbols. Hitting [X] Ends the bonus round. JACKPOT ROUND Step up the tower to win one of the jackpots.
