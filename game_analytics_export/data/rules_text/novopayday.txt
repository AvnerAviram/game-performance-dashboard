Pay Day Game Theme and Graphics Leave your everyday life behind and play this colorful game. Try your chance and win the PAY DAY Prizes. Main Game Pay Day is played on 5 reels, each 3 rows in height, with 30 fixed win lines. Winning combinations run from the left to right across active win lines, except scatter symbols. The attraction of this game are The Pay Day prizes. When 3 or more Pay Day symbols appear scattered on the reels, the corresponding Pay Day prize is awarded. The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols, except Pay Day and Bonus symbols. 3 Bonus symbols scattered on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the Free Spins. Feature Trigger 3 Bonus symbols scattered on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the feature - which rewards 10, 15 or 20 Free Spins randomly. Free Spins The free spins bonus in Pay Day slots can be triggered if you collect three Bonus symbols anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4. Trigger the bonus and you will receive 10, 15 or 20 free spins randomly, plus a 3x multiplier, meaning every win you land during the bonus will be tripled in value. Retrigger the bonus feature for an additional 10, 15 or 20 free spins by collecting another three Bonus symbols. Rules All line wins are for combinations of kind. All line wins are for combinations left to right except scatters. All line wins start on the leftmost reel and are for combinations on adjacent reels. All wins are on selected lines except scatters. Highest win only paid per active line. Scatters pay in any position. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Bonus symbol and Pay Day. Line wins are multiplied by bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Gamble Feature Maximum gamble amount is 500x the total bet. For higher wins or during autoplay, gambling is not possible.
