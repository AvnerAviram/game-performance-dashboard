Honey Touch Game Theme and Graphics Try this honey touch adventure! Scratch the card, find a sweet win and multiply your win with great Multipliers. It is time to take a break and enjoy the sweet life in another scratch card game. Main Game Honey Touch is played on a 3x3 grid containing 9 cards. Each round starts with 9 opaque cards that are scratched one by one. Single Match, Double Match or Triple Match? How lucky are you? 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid create a winning match. The highest possible win is a Triple Match that can be multiplied by a 27x Multiplier. Up to 3 Multipliers can be awarded in one round. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Rules One winning combination consists of 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid. 3 ways to win: Single Match - 3 symbols of one kind Double Match - 3 symbols of one kind and 3 symbols of a second kind Triple Match - 3 symbols of one kind, 3 symbols of a second kind and 3 symbols of a third kind 3x Multiplier can appear on top of any revealed symbol. Up to 3 Multipliers can appear in one round. Last modification date: 4/16/2024
