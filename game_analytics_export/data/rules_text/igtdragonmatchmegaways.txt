Megaways Megaways under license from Big Time Gaming. Each spin can have up to 3,136 ways to win. Wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbols, starting on reel 1, regardless of size. 3.2. Cascading Wins Symbols spin into position to form a number of Megaways. Any win in view will start a Cascade. Symbols in winning combinations will explode and new symbols will drop into position. Cascades will continue until no new wins are formed. 3.3. Wild The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Dragon Match bonus symbols, to form winning ways. 3.4. Dragon Match bonus symbol 2 x Dragon Match bonus symbols on the top reel will trigger Free Spins. 3.5. Dragon Match Matching symbols on the top reel will turn Wild and also turn all instances of the same symbol on the two reels below into Wilds. 3.6. Free Spins Landing two Dragon Match bonus Symbols on the top reel will trigger Free Spins. At the starting of the Free Spins you get to choose between: 12 spins with x1 starting multiplier - 95.93% RTP 8 spins with x3 starting multiplier - 95.96% RTP 5 spins with x5 starting multiplier - 95.95% RTP a random choice, which can award anywhere from 5 to 12 spins with any multiplier from x1 to x5 - 95.99% RTP During Free Spins, 2 x Dragon Match bonus symbols on the top reel will award an additional 5 free spins. Note: To achieve maximum RTP (95.99%), the optimal strategy is to choose the Mystery Free Spins option. 3.7. Unlimited Multiplier An unlimited win multiplier, is active during Free Spins . After every cascading win during Free Spins, the multiplier will increase by x1. The new multiplier will apply to the next free spins win, whether it comes during the same series of cascades or on a later free spin. 4. Other game buttons 4.1.
