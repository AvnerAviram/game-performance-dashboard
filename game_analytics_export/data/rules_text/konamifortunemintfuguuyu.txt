Fortune Mint Fu Gui You Yu BASIC GAME RULES MINIMUM BET IS 68 CREDITS. THIS GAME SUPPORTS 243 WAYS. ALL PAYS ARE FROM THE LEFT MOST REEL TO THE RIGHT IN ANY POSITIONS ON ADJACENT REELS EXCEPT SCATTERS AND JACKPOT COINS. HIGHEST WIN ONLY FOR EACH COMBINATION. ONLY HIGHTEST SCATTER WIN PAYS. ONLY HIGHEST JACKPOT COIN WIN PAYS. ALL WINS EXCEPT SCATTERS AND JACKPOT COINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY THE BET LEVEL. SCATTER WINS ARE MULTIPLIED BY THE TOTAL CREDITS WAGERED. ALL WINS ARE ADDED. ALL WINS ARE SHOWN IN CURRENCY EXCEPT CREDIT PRIZES. TOTAL CREDITS WAGERED IS THE BET LEVEL MULTIPLIED BY 68 CREDITS. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. A PLAYER CANNOT INFLUENCE THE RESULT OF A GAME BY STOPPNG THE REEL SPINS. PLAYER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING CORRECT CREDITS ARE REGISTERED BEFORE PLAY. THE GAME DISPLAY DOES NOT INDICATE HOW CLOSE YOU WERE TO WINNING, AND CANNOT NECESSARLY BE USED TO DETERMINE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING OR LOSING IF YOU CONTINUTE TO PLAY.
