Arctic Empress Symbols pay from left to right and must be consecutive. All wins pay only on lines in play. Multiple bonuses can be won in one game. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are added. The paytable always shows the prizes for the currently selected bet and number of lines. Free spins can be won again during the free spins. Free spins are played at trigger bet and lines. Wild symbol may appear on all reels. Scatter symbol may only appear on reels 2, 3 and 4. All game outcomes are independent and randomly determined. The actual RTP is calculated as Total Win / Total Bet over a large number of games played and reflects the prizes awarded by a game as a percentage of all bets made. The expected RTP can vary widely in either direction for a small number of games due to statistical variance. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. The theoretical return to player of this game is 95.12%. Last modification date: 12/21/2020
