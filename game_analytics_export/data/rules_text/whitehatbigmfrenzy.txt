Big Money Frenzy INTRODUCTION Big Money awaits in this 10 win-line slot. look out for the Golden Dollar Symbol which can award Money Spins. Trigger Frenzy Spins and enter the vault for some Big Money action. Step 1: Getting Started Click the TOTAL BET button to increase or decrease the amount of your TOTAL BET. Select from the list of available stakes from the Total Bet options (depending on Operator configuration and currency equivalent). Click 'SPIN' to start your game. Step 2: How To Play Press 'SPIN' to start the game (depending on jurisdiction and Operator configuration, Spacebar may also be used to start a game). The reels will spin and eventually stop at random. If the combination of symbols appearing on the reels forms a line of 3, 4 or 5 matching symbols (starting from the left) on one of the win lines, then you win. Any winning lines you have will be highlighted and your winnings will be credited to your account balance. 3 or more MONEY Scatter Symbol in view awards Money Spins. Mystery Symbol The Mystery Symbol can appear in any position. All Mystery Symbols in view will reveal the same random symbol. MONEY Scatter MONEY Scatter Symbols can appear on any reel, in any position. 3 or more MONEY Scatter Symbol in view awards Money Spins. Available MONEY symbol outcomes during the base game are: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 10x, 14x, 16x, 18x, 20x, 1 Frenzy Spin, 2 Frenzy Spins and 3 Frenzy Spins. Money Spins At the start of Money Spins the triggering MONEY Scatter Symbols will be held in place. All other positions will spin inidually with only MONEY Scatter Symbols being active. The player has 3 spins to be awarded additional MONEY Scatter Symbols in view. Landing a MONEY Scatter Symbol in view will reset the Spins Remaining to 3. Landing in no MONEY Scatter Symbols 3 times in a row, or filling every position with MONEY Scatter Symbols will end Money Spins. During Money Spins if a Frenzy Spin symbol lands in view the player will be awarded 1 Frenzy Spin for each one in view. The Frenzy Spins are awarded at the end of Money Spins At the end of Money Spins, the player is awarded the value of the MONEY symbols displayed in view. Available MONEY symbol outcomes during Money Spins are: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 10x, 14x, 16x, 18x, 20x, 1 Frenzy Spin, 2 Frenzy Spins and 3 Frenzy Spins. Frenzy Spins The number of Frenzy Spins will be based on the amount that appeared in view during Money Spins. Landing MONEY Scatter Symbol in view will add the win to the Bonus Win panel. Every MONEY Scatter Symbol will add to the Bonus Win regardless of the number that land in view and do not hold over between spins. If a Frenzy Spin symbol lands in view an additional Frenzy Spin is awarded At the end of Frenzy Spins, the total winnings will be added to the player's Bonus Win. Available MONEY symbol outcomes during Frenzy Spins are: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 10x, 14x, 15x, 16x, 18x, 20x, 25x, 30x, 35x, 40x, 50x, 60x, 70x, 80x, 90x, 100x, 150x, 200x, 250x, 500x, 1 Frenzy Spin, 2 Frenzy Spins and 3 Frenzy Spins. Auto Play (subject to operator configuration and jurisdictional requirements) There is an Autoplay function in the game that will start each spin automatically. By selecting 'AUTOPLAY' you can choose to bet automatically, one after the other. Select your stake as normal then press the 'AUTOPLAY' button to select the number of 'Autoplay' bets you want to play. Auto Play will turn off if you get into any bonus round including Free Spins, if you reach the set Loss Limit or if a Progressive pot is awarded (where applicable).
