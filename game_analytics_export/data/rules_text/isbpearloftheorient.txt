Pearl of the Orient 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 10 (fixed) Number of coins you can bet 10 (fixed) Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes Bonus round No Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 95.97% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Wins on different selected lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each selected line is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Mobile version To play, follow these steps: Tap to open the settings screen. On the settings screen, tap then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features 3.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except Scatter symbol. The Wild appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4 and always expand to cover the whole reel as Stacked Wild. 3.2. Free Spins Three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels trigger 12 Free Spins and also pay according to the paytable. When the Free Spins start, the six high paying symbols are displayed above the reels, on a bar, in line, starting with the lowest in value on the left and continuing towards the highest in value on the right. During Free Spins, any Wild that lands on the reels expands and transforms the high paying symbol with the lowest value into the high paying symbol with the next higher value - that low value symbol will no longer appear on the reels during the current Free Spins as all its instances are now transformed. Note: During Free Spins, Wilds also award additional Free Spins as follows: 1 Wild - 1 extra Free Spin, 2 Wilds - 3 extra Free Spins, 3 Wilds - 5 extra Free Spins. If you get several Wilds on the reels, the transformations continue. Transformations no longer take place when only the high paying symbol with the highest value is displayed on the bar above the reels. Example: After you get the first Wild of a Free Spins round, the bar with high paying symbols will show, from left to right, two of the same high paying symbols with the second highest value (out of all six) plus the rest of the four high paying symbols with values higher than it. In addition, the high paying symbol with the lowest value (out of all six) will no longer appear on the reels during the current Free Spins as it has been transformed into the symbol with the very next higher value. Free Spins can be retriggered by three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels. Note: During Free Spins, the amount bet remains the same as the bet on the spin that has triggered the Free Spins and cannot be changed. 4.
