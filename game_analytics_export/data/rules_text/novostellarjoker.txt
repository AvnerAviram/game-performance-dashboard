Stellar Joker Game Theme and Graphics Stellar Joker features fruits and golden stars. In the super meter mode Stellar Joker is on fire. Watch for the expanding jokers! Main Game The game starts with the base game. The base game is played on 10 lines (fixed). Wins pay left to right and right to left. The super game is triggered when you win 4 x your bet or more. Super Game The default (super) bet is 4 x base bet. The bet is taken from the super meter. Max. 50 super spins can be played. Jokers expand and substitute for all symbols. Rules All prizes for combinations of a kind. Highest prize only per pay direction. 5 of a kind prizes paid once. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/8/2024
