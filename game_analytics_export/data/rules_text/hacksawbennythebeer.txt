FEATURES Stackways Land one of two types of Stackways symbols to cover that reel with identical symbols. The Normal Stackways symbol can stack up to 4 rows high, and with the Revealing type, it’s possible to fill the entire reel, 10 symbols in length, with matching symbols. The stacked symbol is chosen by the longest winning length of all wins present, and stacking only occurs if a win is possible after the stacking. BOOK OF STACKWAYS Kicking off with 10 Free Spins, the Book of Stackways bonus sees 2 pay symbols randomly selected to represent the Special symbols. Should either of these land across 3 or more reels, they will duly stack up to 10 rows high in classic Book fashion according to the respective numbers displayed under each reel. Should you manage a win with both Special symbols during the same spin, they will take it in turns to stack starting with the lesser value of the two. Re-triggers are possible; 2 FS symbols gets you an extra 2 spins and 3 grants 4 more. Catching 4 FS symbols at the same time upgrades the round to the SUPER BOOK OF STACKWAYS feature while awarding 4 additional spins on top of your remaining tally. SUPER BOOK OF STACKWAYS Triggered by 4 FS base game symbols landing at once, this round selects 4 pay symbols at random to be the Special symbols. As in the previous bonus feature, whenever any of these symbols drops together across at least 3 reels, stacking will be performed in accordance with the values displayed at the bottom of the reels. When more than one Special symbol is stacked, they will stack one by one from the lowest paying symbol onwards.
