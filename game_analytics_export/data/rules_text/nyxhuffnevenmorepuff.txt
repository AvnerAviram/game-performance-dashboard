BUZZ SAW FEATURE Before the free spins begin, each BUZZ SAW symbol will move to the right until off-screen, starting with the rightmost BUZZ SAW symbol. Each position that BUZZ SAW symbol touches while moving to the right, including its initial position, is evaluated as if HARD HAT symbol had landed there during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, one at a time. During the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE.
