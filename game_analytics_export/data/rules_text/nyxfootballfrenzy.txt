Football Frenzy The aim of the game is to try and get a football into one of cups at the bottom of the screen. Getting Started Once the game has loaded, you're shown a tutorial on how to play. You can control the tutorial with the left and right arrow buttons. Hit "Start" to move on, to setting your stake. Setting Your Stake You can set your stake with the left and right arrow buttons. The higher the stake. Hit "Play" once you're ready to play. Playing the Game You'll now see the bagatelle style play area presented as a football pitch. At the top of the screen you'll see a football rotating in a semi circle through 3 segments for direction. Once you're happy with the direction, follow the on-screen instructions and click the screen for kick off. This fires the ball onto the pitch area in the general direction chosen. How to win If the ball goes into one of the cups, the you will win any of the values below based upon the stake selected. Players on the field act as obstacles and randomly deflect the ball. The sides of the pitch keep the ball in play. If the ball lands in the "Golden Goal" cup, then you'll get to a different screen featuring a goalkeeper. The ball automatically fires into one of the areas of the net and a goal is scored. The value associated with that area of the net is then awarded to you. Penalty Bonus If the football falls into the "Penalty" hole, then you'll get to a different screen featuring a goalkeeper, for a "Penalty Bonus". Different areas of the net award you with different values. You're prompted to select an area to shoot. When the ball automatically fires and a goal is scored, you're then awarded the value according to the area of the net you selected. You don't win if the keeper saves the ball. Game Recovery If a real play game is interrupted (i.e. started but not completed due to connectivity issues or accidentally closing the browser etc), any winnings will be correctly credited to your account and will be exact according to the result, prior to the game being interrupted. If the game is started but "Kick Off" has been played, upon resumption you will be presented with the chosen stake in its default in play state. If you already released the ball, upon resumption you will be presented with the final outcome of the instant win with result displayed. The sequence and values of events leading to the display of the total award, may vary from the animation prior to game recovery. This will not affect the result of the game after the game has recovered. General Information This game has a theoretical Return to Player of 90.75%. The maximum payout in this game is £250,000.
