Straight Cash Straight Cash offers a unique slot experience with an innovative focus on concatenating number symbols. Each spin awards the total value of all fully visible number symbols on the reels, creating a dynamic and rewarding gameplay mechanic. The game provides three wager options that activate 1, 2, or 3 reels, with higher bets unlocking wheel bonuses and jackpots. The Respin Feature enhances the excitement by holding fully visible number symbols in place while the other reels respin, with the potential for additional respins if more tagged numbers appear. The Wheel Bonus Feature activates by landing 3 Wheel Bonus Symbols on all active reels, awarding a spin of the wheel for a chance to win exciting jackpot prizes. PAYS Each spin awards the total concatenated value of all number symbols landing on the reels. A number symbol counts towards this total only if it lands fully visible on an active reel.Only the highest left to right concatenated combination is paid. Reel 1 has Number Symbols 1, 2, 3, 5, and 10. Reel 2 has Number Symbols 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5. Reel 3 has Number Symbols 2, 5, 10, 20, 30, and 50. WAGERING OPTIONS There are 3 wager options available that will activate 1, 2 or 3 reels. A wager of 1 credit will activate reel 1 only. A wager of 3 credits will activate reels 1 & 2. A wager of 10 credits will activate all 3 reels. Wheel bonus and jackpot awards are available at 10 credits bet only. RESPIN FEATURE At least two or more reels needs to be active for the feature. If a number with spin tag lands fully visible on an active reel, the respin feature is awarded. Any reel(s) with fully visible number symbols are held, and all remaining non-number reel(s) will respin one time. If the respin hits an additional fully visible number with spin tag then an additional respin is awarded. The maximum number of respins in a single game is 2. When the respin feature is complete, the final concatenated prize of all numbers on the reels is awarded. No prize is awarded for numbers appearing on the reels before the respin feature is complete. WHEEL BONUS FEATURE 3 Wheel Bonus Symbols appearing fully visible on the reels with all 3 reels active will trigger the wheel bonus. one spin of the wheel is awarded and will award the grand jackpot, and major jackpot or a credit prize.
