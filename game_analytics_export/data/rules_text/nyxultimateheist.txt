Ultimate Heist RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: 14.5 GAME RULES This game is played at 243 ways. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wild symbols are wild and substitute for all symbols except the Bonus Symbols. Wild symbols substitutes for 3 Gypsy Symbols.] Wild symbols substitutes for 1 Violin symbol, Horse Carriage symbol, Low Pay symbols. Wild symbols appear on only reels 2, 3, and 4. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 or more Free Games Bonus Symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 10 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol, and only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Free Games Bonus symbol. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 3 Free Games High Pay 1 symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Free Games High Pay 2, Free Games High Pay 3, Free Games High Pay 4, Free Games Low Pay 1, Free Games Low Pay 2, Free Games Low Pay 3, or Free Games Low Pay 4 symbol. Free Games Wild symbol only appears on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically played on the same ways at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards 5 additional free games. Free Games Bonus symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain or when 250 free games have been.
