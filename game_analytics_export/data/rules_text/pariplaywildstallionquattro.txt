Wild Stallion Quattro GAME RULES A majestic symbol of the American west stampedes onto the reels in Wild Stallion Quattro. Tame the fery wild stallion and rein in the rewards. American gold waits to be claimed with Stacked Wild and Scatter Symbols. Win Free Spin scatter prizes or go all-in with the high stakes Quattro Mode. Grab your hat and pull up your boots – only a true cowboy can lasso the reels and strike it rich! Key Features Stacked Wild Symbols – Substitutes for all symbols except Scatter Symbols to increase your chances of winning! Stacked Scatter Symbols – Stack up Scatter Symbols and win Free Spins! Gamble – Double up with the Gamble feature and double (x2) or even quadruple (x4) your winnings! Quattro Mode – Select Quattro mode and play up to four (4) games simultaneously on a single screen for four times the thrill! Click/Tap the SPIN or AUTOPLAY buttons to start playing. Activate MAX BET to play with the maximum allowable TOTAL BET settings. Use the – and + BUTTONS to customize your bet settings. The theoretical minimum playback percentage (RTP) is 95.05 % Disclaimer: If an interrupted game is not fnished within 90 days, your game will be terminated. If your game gets terminated you will lose every active bet. Only the highest winning combination per active win line pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. How to Play Click the Spin or Autoplay buttons to start playing. Adjust your bet by choosing Max Bet to begin with the maximum allowable total bet. You can adjust your bet anytime by clicking the Total Bet button and using the – and + buttons. Select Game & Quattro Mode The Select Game option in the lower right corner allows you to choose between playing 1, 2, 3 or 4 games at once. Your current selection will be highlighted. Select Quattro to play 4 individual slot machines simultaneously on a single screen for four times the thrill! During the Free Spins Bonus Game, free spins may apply to multiple games at once. The number of Free Spins remaining per game is displayed at the top of each slot machine. Win Lines Each time you win, the winning line will be displayed. There are thirty (30) possible win line combinations that count as a Win, Big Win, or Mega Win. Win lines pay from lef to right only. Stacked Wild Symbols Wild Symbols substitute for all base game symbols – excluding Scatter Symbols. Stacked Wild Symbols are multiple Wild Symbols stacked on top of each other. The more Wild Symbols you land on the reels, the higher the prize value. Stacked Scatter Symbols Stacked Scatter Symbols are multiple Scatter Symbols stacked on top of each other. The more Stacked Scatter Symbols land anywhere on the reels during a single play, the higher their prize value. Once the Scatter Prize has been awarded, the Free Spins Bonus Game will begin. Free Spins awarded are played on a diferent set of reels, increasing your chances at better winning combinations. Stacked Scatter Symbols will not appear while playing with free spins. In Quattro Mode, the number of Free Spins remaining will be displayed at the top of each slot machine. Gamble – Double Up! Feeling lucky? Each time you win, you’ll be given the option to Gamble your winnings. The Gamble feature is represented by the playing cards button in the lower right corner of the screen. There are two types of bets to choose between: – Choose between Red or Black for a chance to double (x2) your winnings. Suit – Choose between Heart, Club, Diamond, or Spade suits for a chance to quadruple (x4) your winnings. Previous winning gamble history is shown at the bottom of the window. You may gamble up to 5 times or until the maximum bet coin prize has been won per active game. You may also choose to Collect your current winnings anytime. Click the Collect Button beside your current balance at the bottom of the screen to collect your winnings and exit the Gamble feature. BALANCE IN COINS The default setting for BALANCE IN COINS is OFF. When set to ON, the reels spin faster than normal spins. AUTO PLAY Tap the AUTO PLAY BUTTON to start playing. You can select the number of spins triggered in AUTO PLAY mode by changing the SPIN settings. The following advanced settings are also available: TOTAL BET TO GAMBLE: Stop AUTO PLAY if the active TOTAL BET exceeds the specifed TOTAL BET TO GAMBLE amount. LOSS LIMIT AMOUNT: Stop AUTO PLAY if the total amount of losses exceeds the specifed LOSS LIMIT amount. SINGLE WIN LIMIT AMOUNT: Stop AUTO PLAY if any win exceeds the specifed SINGLE WIN LIMIT amount. WIN LIMIT AMOUNT: Stop AUTO PLAY if the total winnings exceed the specifed WIN LIMIT amount. ANY WIN: Stop AUTO PLAY afer any win, regardless of the amount won. INCLUDE JACKPOT (IF APPLICABLE): Stop AUTO PLAY in case of a jackpot win (when applicable). Note: AUTO PLAY options can be diferent for some jurisdictions. Tap the AUTO PLAY button afer confguring these settings to begin playing using your new AUTO PLAY settings.
