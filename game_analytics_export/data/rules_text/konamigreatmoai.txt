Great Moai BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits, which includes 1 credit wagered per line (30 credits) plus additional features. All pays are on selected lines only from the left most reel to the right on adjacent reels except scatters. Highest win only on each lit line. Only highest scatter win pays. Wins on different lit lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All wins are shown in credits. Line wins are multiplied by credits wagered per line. Scatter wins are multiplied by the lines played times the bet per line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. ACTION STACKED SYMBOLSTM Each reel contains a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with one of the following symbols MAN, MASK, TURTLE, COCONUTS, FLOWERS, A, K, Q, J, 10, 9 before the reel spin is initiated. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. WILD FEATURE Randomly, extra 3X1 MOAI and 3X1 MOAI WITH 2X MULTIPLIER symbols appear on the reels during or after the reel spin. Pearl can appear on extra 3X1 MOAI and 3X1 MOAI WITH 2X MULTIPLIER. 3X1 MOAI WITH 2X MULTIPLIER substitutes for 3X1 MOAI, MAN, MASK, TURTLE, COCONUTS, FLOWERS, A, K, Q, J, 10, and 9. 3X1 MOAI WITH 2X MULTIPLIER will multiply all line pays 2X respectively. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3, 4 or 5 PEARL trigger 8, 12 or 20 free games respectively. Wild feature can frequently occur during the free games. Additional free games can be won during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 7/5/2022
