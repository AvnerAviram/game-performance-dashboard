5 Lion Festival BASIC GAME RULES Minimum bet is 45 credits. All wins are shown in currency. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. A player cannot influence the result of a game by stopping the reel spins. Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before play. The game display does not indicate how close you were to winning, and cannot necessarily be used to determine your chances of winning or losing if you continue to play. All pays shown are based on 45 credit bet. Only highest winner paid for each winning combination. Line pays occur on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel. All wins are added. 1st, 2nd, 4th and 5th reels contain a number of adjacent positions that are randomly replaced with symbols from the pay table except FIRECRACKER or GONG. All replacement positions are filled with the same symbol. Two symbols appear in each frame on reel 3. For each lit line passing through reel 3, the symbol in each frame are evaluated separately when part of a winning combination. FREE GAME FEATURE Any 3 or more GONG trigger the feature. Select one of the following free game features. 20 free games All FIRECRACKER change to FIRECRACKER2X, FIRECRACKER3X or FIRECRACKER5X when part of a winning combination. 10 free games All FIRECRACKER change to FIRECRACKER5X, FIRECRACKER8X or FIRECRACKER10X when part of a winning combination. Mystery choice Mystery number of free games with a mystery set of multipliers. 5 free games All FIRECRACKER change to FIRECRACKER10X, FIRECRACKER15X or FIRECRACKER30X when part of a winning combination. 3 free games All FIRECRACKER change to FIRECRACKER15X, FIRECRACKER30X or FIRECRACKER40X when part of a winning combination. FIRECRACKER2X, FIRECRACKER3X, FIRECRACKER5X, FIRECRACKER8X, FIRECRACKER10X, FIRECRACKER15X, FIRECRACKER30X and FIRECRACKER40X substitute for CHINESE LION, GOLD POT, DRUM, LANTERN, ENVELOPES, A, K, Q, J, 10 and 9. If either FIRECRACKER2X, FIRECRACKER3X, FIRECRACKER5X, FIRECRACKER8X, FIRECRACKER10X, FIRECRACKER15X, FIRECRACKER30X or FIRECRACKER40X substitutes in a win, they will multiply the pay 2X, 3X, 5X, 8X, 10X, 15X, 30X or 40X respectively. Different reels are used during the free games. Any 3 or more GONG re-trigger the selected free game feature during the free games. Credits bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the feature. Last modification date: 7/26/2024
