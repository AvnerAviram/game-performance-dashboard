12 Coins Grand Platinum Edition Santas Jackpots The game has 12 independent reels on a 3x4 grid. There are no regular symbols in the base game and prizes can only be won in the Bonus Game or in Cash Out feature. Drawing 4 Bonus symbols of any type on the middle row of the reels activates the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. The maximum win amount is 2500x bet. SPECIAL FEATURES CASH OUT During the base game, a Cash Out label can appear on a random reel and will stick to it for up to 15 spins. If a Bonus symbol of any type is drawn on that reel when the Cash Out countdown reaches 0, the symbol prize is paid. The Cash Out label is temporarily removed from the reels when changing bet levels and only appears on the bet level it was obtained on. CASH INFINITY™ SYMBOL Cash Infinity™ symbols may randomly appear in the base game and stick to the reels until the end of the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. All sticky Cash Infinity™ symbols are locked in place while other symbols are spun. When a Sticky symbol appears, the outcome of remaining non-sticky symbols is entirely random. Cash Infinity™ symbols award prizes ranging from 5x-15x player's bet. However, the prize will be paid at the end of the Bonus Game. If the Cash Infinity™ symbol is drawn on the middle row of the reels, it increases the chances of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Cash Infinity™ symbols are temporarily removed from the reels when changing bet levels and Volatility Level™ and only appear on the bet level and Volatility Level™ they were obtained on. HOLD THE JACKPOT™ BONUS GAME All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. 3 Respins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Respins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-10x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols may randomly appear during the Bonus Game and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash and Cash Infinity™ symbols and multiplies them randomly by 1-20x. Mystery symbol can transform into any other Bonus symbol, apart from Cash Infinity™ symbols. Jackpot Mystery symbol can only transform into MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols. Mystery and Jackpot Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Bonus symbol values, including MINI, MINOR, MAJOR Jackpots, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 12 Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 2500x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Bonus symbol values are not added to it. Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game is played at the same bet level and Volatility Level™ as the spin that activated the bonus. CHANCE LEVEL™ The game is played with the base bet set by default. However, the Chance Level™ feature can be enabled during the base game and you can select one of 3 levels: Chance Level™ x2 - the base bet is multiplied by 2, giving you at least a 2x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x5 - the base bet is multiplied by 5, giving you at least a 5x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x10 - the base bet is multiplied by 10, giving you at least a 11x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. The Chance Level™ feature does not affect the paytable, thus symbol values remain the same as for the base bet in both the base game and the Bonus Game. Sticky symbols are temporarily removed from the reels when changing Chance Level™ and only appear on the Chance Level™ they were obtained on. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game average return to player: 96.15% Player Return Percentage (% RTP – Percentage Return to Player) is the expected percentage of bets that a particular Game will return to the Player in the long run. It is equal to the total amount of contributions to the total returns. GAMBLE FEATURE The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. The Gamble feature can be activated only with the current win value during the ongoing game session. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the current win counter and returns to the main game. The Gamble feature can be played for a total of 7 rounds. The number of rounds in the Gamble feature depends on the max win available in the game. The win value from doubling up in the next round cannot be higher than: USD 250,000 Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
