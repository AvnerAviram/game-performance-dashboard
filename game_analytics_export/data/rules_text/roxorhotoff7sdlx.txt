Hit a win on any of the two main reel sets and simultaneously land a Feature symbol on the Bonus Reel to trigger the corresponding Feature. Super Respins You are awarded a Super Respin, with a chance to re-trigger the Feature again and again! The win Multiplier starts from x2 and grows by 1 after each Super Respin. Only the Super Respins and Blank symbols can land on the Bonus Reel during the Feature. Landing the Super Respins symbol awards you another Super Respin. Landing a Blank symbol ends the Feature. Each Super Respin guarantees a win! The Super Respins Feature uses the same bet as on the spin that triggered the Feature. Game Rules Total bet is 10 x Coin Size. 5 paylines are played in each individual reelset. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $95.00 of wins.
