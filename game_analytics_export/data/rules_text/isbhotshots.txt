Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay from left to right. Winnings on different ways are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. Note: The number of adjacent winning symbols (ways) is calculated by multiplying the number of winning symbols on each reel. For example, if there is 1 winning symbol on reel 1, 1 winning symbol on reel 2 and 2 winning symbols on reel 3, then the number of adjacent winning symbols is 1 x 1 x 2 = 2. In this case, the number of reels involved in the win is 3 – in the paytable, you can view the gain for 3 reels for the winning symbol. On the info bar(s) located under the main game screen, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbol except Scatter. Each Wild symbol on the screen will expand over the adjacent left and right symbols and over the adjacent top and bottom symbols; the symbols over which the Wild expands become wild. Note : If the Wild appears on the top row of the reels, it will expand only over the adjacent bottom symbol and over the adjacent left and right symbols. If the Wild appears on the bottom row of the reels, it will expand only over the adjacent top symbol and over the adjacent left and right symbols. A Wild symbol does not expand if, by expanding, it will cover a Scatter symbol – it will however expand over other adjacent symbols, except the Scatter symbol. Wild symbols appear only on reels 3, 4 and 5, during normal spins and during free spins. Note : A Wild symbol that appears on reel 3 can expand over symbols on reel 2 and therefore a Wild symbol may also appear on reel 2. 3.2. Scatter Three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels pay according to the paytable. All free spins awarded by Scatter symbols will pay the winnings at 3X. Scatter winnings during free spins are also multiplied by 3. During free spins, other free spins can be triggered; there is no limit to how many times free spins are re-triggered. The winnings from Scatter (4X, 6X, 10X the amount bet) are added to the total Free spins winnings. During free spins, the amount bet remains the same as the bet on the spin that has triggered the free spins and cannot be changed. 4.
