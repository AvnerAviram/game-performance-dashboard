La Voz BOTTOM GAME In this game the player decides whether to collect the prize by pressing the collect button or the play button to risk it. By taking a risk we can either get the top prize in the win plan or an equivalent bonus amount. Once the prize barrier corresponding to the three lemons figures is passed, the game becomes a seesaw game where the possible prizes are either up or down in the win plan. SUPERIOR GAME In order to access the top game you need to have sufficient bonus and credits when you win a prize, except for the blue 7, you can risk portions of this prize in the risk game which is accessed automatically. By risking a portion we can get double that portion or an equivalent value in bonuses. In the event that the player does not wish to risk that portion, he can collect it by pressing collect, and if he holds down, he can collect the rest of the prize without risking it. AVANCES Allows you to advance the figures on the reels to find a winning combination. The number of advances is drawn between 1 and 4. RETENCIONES Hold up to two reels in the same game to increase the chance of winning. MINI-GAMES ARE ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PRIZE LINES. MINIGAME CASTING 3 "VIP" figures in the lower game give access to the casting mini-game. Select a roller. MINIGAME AUDICIONES 3 "armchair" figures in the top game can give access to the auditions game. For a prize. MINIGAME ASALTOS 3 "buzzer" figures in the top game can give access to the game rounds. Get the best combination of prizes. MINIGAME PESCA 3 "the final" figures in the top game can give access to the final game. Press play to get prizes. ADDITIONAL FEATURES VERTICAL LINES Get micro figures and you can play 8 lines in top game. SIETE MIXTOS At high stakes get combinations of different figure sevens and get a random prize. ACCUMULATIVE PLAY Accumulate spotlights and you'll get an extra winning spin. EXTRA SPIN On a non-winning spin in the lower game, you can win a free bonus spin.
