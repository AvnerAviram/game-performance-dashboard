Spread Bet Roulette Spread-Bet Roulette is a fixed odds betting game, based on roulette. There are 37 numbered slots, from 0 to 36, on the outer wheel. Each of these can be bet on as in standard roulette. There is also a second inner wheel with matching numbered slots. In addition to, or instead of, regular roulette bets you can also place Spread-Bets on the number ranges 0-1, 2-11, 12-18, 19-33, 34-54, 55-67 and 68-72. Spread-Bet results are the sums of the inner wheel and the outer wheel. For example, if the ball lands on 10 on the outer wheel and 20 on the inner wheel then the Spread-Bet result is 30. THE ROULETTE WHEEL The outer wheel is spun in one direction, the inner wheel is spun in the opposite direction, and a ball is thrown in. Eventually the inner wheel will lock to the outer wheel and the ball will land in one of the numbered slots of the outer wheel. If you bet on the outer wheel numbered slot the ball has landed in, you will win a payout. You will also win a payout if you placed a Spread-Bet on the sum of the numbered pockets of the outer wheel and the inner wheel that the ball has landed in. The slot the ball landed in will be shown in the bottom left of the screen. After the ball has landed, the DOUBLE & SPIN and REBET & SPIN buttons will appear. Click DOUBLE & SPIN to double the previous bet(s) and start another game. Click REBET & SPIN to place the same bet(s) as in the previous game and start another game. Clicking the BACK button will return you to the Betting Table view. A marker will be placed on the winning number from the last spin. The REBET & SPIN and DOUBLE & SPIN buttons will also appear on the Betting Table. THE NEIGHBORS TABLE The Neighbors Table view can be accessed by clicking NEIGHBORS on the Betting Table view. The Neighbors Table allows you to make several types of bets unavailable on the Betting Table. There are 5 numbered NEIGHBORS buttons. Click a NEIGHBORS button then click on a number on the racecourse to bet on that number and the numbers neighboring it to the left and the right. For example, clicking the NEIGHBORS button labelled 1 then clicking on the number 2 on the racecourse will be on the number 2 and the numbers to the immediate right and left of 2 (21 to the right and 25 to the left). Clicking on the NEIGHBORS button labelled 4 then clicking on the number 2 on the racecourse will be on the number 2 and the 4 numbers to the immediate right and left of 2 (21, 4, 19 and 15 to the right, and 25, 17, 34 and 6 to the left.).
