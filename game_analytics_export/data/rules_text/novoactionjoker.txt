Action Joker Main Game Action Joker is played on 40 lines (fixed). Jokers substitute all symbols except stars (scatters). Jokers appear stacked on the reels. Feature 3, 4 or 5 scattered star symbols pay 10, 20 or 30 Free Games. During Free Games there are many more Jokers on the reels. Free Games can be retriggered. Pay Table Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. Highest win only paid on each line and per scatter combination. RTP:95.12% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
