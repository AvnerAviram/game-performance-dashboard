Gorilla Main Game In Gorilla™ you play on 5 reels and up to 50 win lines. Your goal is to get 5 matching symbols along a payline. In the regular game the win pattern starts at the left on the first reel, and continues along the win line to the right. In order to win, the symbols must be next to each other without any other symbols between them. The "Totem Mask" is the Wild and substitutes for all symbols except Scatters. 'Gorilla' symbols appear stacked. Rules Wild substitutes for all symbols, except Scatter. All pays are for wins of left to right except Scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding Scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Game Theme and Graphics In Gorilla the mighty silverback takes you to his jungle kingdom - he knows exactly where to hide lucrative profits between flowers and thickets. The continent of Africa offers rewarding Free Dpins, in which the rainforest, in the truest sense of the word, reveals its treasures to you, and with a little luck the mysterious totem mask will conjure up out of the beauty of the forest stunning winning combinations!
