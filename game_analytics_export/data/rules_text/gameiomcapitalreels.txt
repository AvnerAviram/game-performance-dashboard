WILD FEATURE The Wild Feature enhances every spin with its ability to substitute for key symbols and increase payout potential. The 2X symbol, when appearing in a non-jackpot winning combination, boosts wins by 2x with one symbol, and 4x with two symbols. When three 2X symbols land on a payline, players are rewarded with the game’s highest jackpot. Alternatively, three Wild symbols on a payline deliver the second-tier jackpot, and a combination of 2X and Wild symbols grants the third-tier prize. CASH ON REELS FEATURE Adding to the excitement, the Cash on Reels Feature introduces an extra layer of rewards. When a combination of Cash on Reel symbols, 2X symbols, or Wilds align on an active payline, players are paid the sum of the Cash on Reel values. This feature becomes even more rewarding when 2X symbols are involved, as 1 or 2 of these symbols will multiply the Cash on Reel payout by 2x or 4x. With possible Cash on Reel values ranging from 10 to 250, the potential for thrilling payouts is ever-present.
