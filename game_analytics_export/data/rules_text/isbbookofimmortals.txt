Book of Immortals 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Game specifications : Type Slots Number of reels 5 Number of rows 3 Number of lines 10 (cannot be changed) Number of coins you can bet 1 per line Progressive No Double feature No Auto spin feature Yes Wild symbols Yes Multiplier symbols No Scatter symbols Yes(Wild) Bonus round No Free s pins Yes Instant win No RTP 96.31% The game disconnects if you do not take any actions for a specific number of consecutive minutes (this number varies depending on jurisdiction); the session recovery feature will save the game progress so that the next time the game is loaded, you can continue where you left off. In the event of technical difficulties resulting in a dropped connection to the game server, the game you were playing is saved automatically and will load the next time you establish a connection to the game server. You will not be able to reconnect to the game server for the first 30 seconds after the disconnection. Note : In case of malfunction all pays and play are voided; any bet that was not yet accepted will be voided, any bet that was unsettled will be refunded. 2. How to Play Note: All winning lines pay from left to right. Winnings on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from Free Spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start Free Spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Mobile version To play, follow these steps: Tap to open the settings screen. On the settings screen, tap then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Tap to return to the main screen. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Tap to spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 3. Special Symbols and Features Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbol to form winning combinations and also acts as Scatter. Note: If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. Scatter Three or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels trigger 10 Free Spins and also pay a specific amount of coins, according to the pay table. Free Spins During Free Spins, a horizontal reel is displayed above the normal five reels. At the start of the Free Spins, the horizontal reel spins once to determine a special symbol - this will be the symbol that lands in the centre of the horizontal reel. When at least 2 (for high paying symbols) or at least 3 (for low paying symbols) instances of the special symbol land on the normal reels (not stacked - have to be on 2 or 3 or more reels) during Free Spins, those instances expand to cover the whole reel(s) where they landed and will pay even on non-adjacent reels. If any winnings are awarded as a result of the special symbol expanding, the horizontal reel will move one position to determine a new special symbol. If the special symbol is a +Free Spins symbol, then additional Free Spins are awarded and the horizontal reel moves one position to determine a new special symbol. If the special symbol is one of the high paying symbols (Gods symbols), that symbol is also highlighted on one of the obelisks displayed on each side of the reels and will expand whenever at least 2 instances of that symbol land on the reels (not stacked - have to be on 2 or 3 or more reels) until the current Free Spins end. Note : During Free Spins you cannot change the bet value. During Free Spins you cannot trigger other Free Spins. Scarab Swap Randomly, at the end of a normal paid spin, before any winnings are paid, a swarm of Scarabs can fly across the reels and attach themselves to random symbols transforming those symbols into the same symbol. Note : Any initial winning combinations are not paid; only winning combinations resulting from the symbol transformations are paid. 4.
