Maximum prize is 5500 x the bet. Higher amounts can be accumulated in the Free Spins. FREE SPINS Three or more Wild symbols give 10 Free Spins. These Free Spins do not consume balance, and all prizes obtained accumulate. You can get more Free Spins within Free Spins. EXPANDING SYMBOL A special EXPANDING SYMBOL is randomly chosen to help you win more on free spins. Watch it expand to cover the reel for more prizes! Symbols can appear anywhere along the active line to win! BONUS GAMES SELECT BONUS GAME Three or more ’Bonus’ symbols give access to the mini-game selection screen. Each entry in the mini-game selection screen will unlock access to Bonus games, one after another. BONUS BONES Selecting the first mini-game will start the Skeletons mini-game. The player must click on the characters until all have been selected or the Exit symbol appears. Each character can display a prize or the EXIT symbol. The final prize is the sum of the prizes accumulated. The maximum prize is 120 x the bet. BONUS CRYPT Selecting the second mini-game will start the Crypt mini-game. There are 3 hidden prizes. You have to choose one of them. The player chooses a character and takes the prize revealed. The maximum prize is 140 x the bet. BONUS DRACULA Selecting the third mini-game will start the Dracula mini-game. The game consists of spinning a reel to reveal the object used to attack Dracula. Each object has a prize. The game will stop when the object appears together with the EXIT symbol. The final prize is the sum of the prizes accumulated. The maximum prize is 150 x the bet.
