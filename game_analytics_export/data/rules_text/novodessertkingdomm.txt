Dessert Kingdom Dessert Kingdom is a tumbler game where wins are achieved when eight or more matching symbols appear anywhere on the reels. Winning symbols disappear, causing the remaining symbols to tumble down to fill the spaces they left. New symbols drop from above, filling the empty spaces. If new matches occur, the tumble process repeats until no new matches can be made. Bonus and multiplier symbols can appear on any spin or tumble. If you hunger for big prizes, now is the time to dine! Malfunction Voids all plays and pays Multiplier Feature Multiplier values can appear randomly during spins and tumbles in both the base game and the free spins feature. Available multipliers include: two times, three times, four times, five times, six times, eight times, ten times, twelve times, fifteen times, twenty times, twenty-five times, fifty times, one hundred times, two hundred fifty times, and five hundred times. At the end of each game round, the values of all multipliers on the screen are summed together and applied to the round’s total win. Free Spins Feature The free spins feature is triggered when four or more bonus symbols land on the reels during the base game, awarding fifteen free spins. This feature includes the same multiplier values as the base game. Retriggering Free Spins Landing three or more bonus symbols during a single spin or tumbling sequence grants five additional free spins. Persistent Multiplier Feature If a winning spin (tumbling sequence) includes multiplier values, their total will be added to the persistent multiplier meter at the end of the spin. This updated persistent multiplier value is then applied to that spin’s total win amount. Throughout the rest of the free spins feature, any winning spin (tumbling sequence) that includes a multiplier will add its value to the meter before applying it to the win amount. Ante Bet Feature Players can increase their chances of triggering the free spins feature by activating Ante Bet. This boosts the bet amount to one hundred twenty-five percent of its current value, doubling the likelihood of hitting the free spins feature. Game Rules Match eight or more symbols to win their values and trigger additional tumbles. Winning symbols disappear, making way for new symbols to tumble down. Multipliers appear randomly and increase the total win for that tumbling sequence. At the end of each spin (tumbling sequence), all multiplier values are summed and applied to the total win. The free spins feature is triggered by landing four or more bonus symbols during the base game, awarding fifteen free spins. Players can earn five additional free spins by landing three bonus symbols during a single spin or tumbling sequence. Multipliers remain persistent during free spins and are applied to each subsequent winning spin (tumbling sequence). The Ante Bet feature increases the odds of activating the free spins feature.
