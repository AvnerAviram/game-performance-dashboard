How to Bet Wheel of Fortune Triple Extreme Spin offers 720 ways to win. Bet Settings Menu Press the cog or TOTAL BET to open the Bet Settings menu. 720 Ways Displays the current number of ways being played. Betting is fixed at 720 ways. Coins Displays the current number of coins being played. Coin Value Displays the current coin value shown in currency. Change by selecting the desired total bet. On desktop devices, optionally press the minus button (–) to decrease the coin value or press the plus button (+) to increase the coin value. Press OK to confirm and close the Bet Settings menu. Spin Press the circular arrow to submit the bet and spin the reels. Auto Spin Feature Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of spins at 720 ways and the current coin value. For jurisdictions where Auto Spin is enabled: Select the number of auto spins and where available, the loss limit, and the (optional) single win limit. After selecting the desired bet configuration, press AUTO SPIN , and choose how many spins to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until STOP is pressed, a bonus is triggered, the balance is insufficient to spin, or any enabled win/loss limit settings are reached. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions. MultiWay Xtra Feature With the MultiWay Xtra feature, there are 720 ways to win on every spin. MultiWay Xtra wins pay for matching symbols in any position in adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column or the rightmost column. More than 1 matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild Symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Wheel of Fortune, Scatter, and Bonus Symbols. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra win for each symbol is paid per spin. Example of a MultiWay Xtra payout: The following diagram shows a sample reel outcome using the Orange Symbol. Because there is at least 1 Orange or Wild Symbol in each of the first 4 columns, the player has won the 4 Orange MultiWay Xtra win which pays 15 coins per way. Payout example: 1 x 2 x 3 x 1 Oranges = 6 winning ways: Column 1 Column 2 Column 3 Column 4 Column 5 Wild Orange Orange Wild Wild Wild Wild 1 Orange 2 Oranges 3 Oranges 1 Orange To determine the payout for this combination: Count the number of Orange and Wild Symbols in each column. Calculate the total number of winning ways (1 x 2 x 3 x 1 = 6). Multiply the number of ways by the 4 Orange Symbol win (6 x 15 = 90). The win is 90 coins. The actual amount won in currency is based on the coin value. Mini Wheel Bonus The Mini Wheel Bonus is triggered when 3 or more Scatter Symbols appear in any position on the columns. The symbols that triggered the bonus immediately turn into spinning wheels. The wheels stop in order from left to right. After the wheels stop, the values indicated are added together and then multiplied by the triggering coin value to determine the total bonus win. Each column has a different wheel associated with it; see the paytable for details. Mini Wheel Bonus wins are independent from MultiWay Xtra wins and are added to the total amount paid. Triple Extreme Spin Bonus The Triple Extreme Spin Bonus is triggered when 3 or more Bonus Scatter Symbols appear anywhere on the columns. 12 envelopes are displayed after entering the bonus. Select a number of envelopes equal to the number of triggering Bonus Symbols. For example, a Bonus triggered by 7 Bonus Symbols has 7 available picks. Each selected envelope awards a pointer for either the blue, red, or yellow wheel. Each wheel has 3 pointers. Any envelopes selected after the first 3 for the corresponding wheel pay a predetermined amount. See the paytable for details. If no pointers for the red or blue wheels are revealed, the centre pointer on the red wheel is awarded. After all picks are revealed, press the button to spin the wheels. The blue and red wheels contain win values, while the yellow wheel contains multiplier values. The bonus pays the sum of all values on slices aligned with active pointers on the blue and red wheels, plus any overflow values awarded during the pick bonus, multiplied by the sum of multiplier values on the yellow wheel aligned with active pointers, times the triggering coin value. Bonus Symbols appear on columns 2, 3, and 4 only. Options All Sound Toggle game sounds on or off. Music Toggle music on or off. Sound Effects Toggle sound effects on or off. Same symbol in same column multiplies win. MultiWay Xtra wins pay left to right or right to left. MultiWay Xtra winning combinations appearing on all 5 columns are only paid once. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra win for each symbol is paid per spin. Coinciding MultiWay Xtra wins from different symbols are added. MultiWay Xtra wins are multiplied by the coin value. Wild Symbol appears on columns 2, 3, and 4 in the base game only. Wild Symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Wheel of Fortune, Bonus, and Scatter Symbols. Bonus wins are independent from MultiWay Xtra wins and are added to the total amount paid. Wins are shown in currency. In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable. In some jurisdictions, regardless of bet size, there may be an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the bonus, plus the outcome which launched the bonus.
