Dolphin Treasure FREE GAMES FEATURE 15 FREE GAMES are won with 3, 4 or 5 Press the PLAY button to start the FREE GAMES FEATURE. The FREE GAMES will start automatically after 2 minutes. All wins during the FREE GAMES are TRIPLED. FREE GAMES FEATURE can be triggered again during the FREE GAMES FEATURE. TOTAL BET is the same as the game that triggered the FREE GAMES FEATURE. GAME RULES All wins shown in credits. 20 lines are played for line cost of 20 credits. Minimum bet 20 credits. Choose your TOTAL BET. TOTAL BET is the line cost multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. Select to choose your TOTAL BET. The PLAY button will immediately commence play. While the reels are spinning you may press the PLAY button to stop them. Touch CREDIT, TOTAL BET or WIN meter to show in currency or credits. All wins on played lines only except scatters. Wins on different lines are added. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Line wins multiplied by Bet Multiplier. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total credits bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Player is responsible to confirm credits registered before game start. AUTO PLAY (IF APPLICABLE) AUTO PLAY AUTOMATICALLY INITIATES A SPECIFIED NUMBER OF BOUGHT GAMES AT THE CURRENT TOTAL BET. CHOOSE YOUR TOTAL BET AND PRESS PRESS TO RETURN TO GAME WITHOUT INITIATING AUTO PLAY SPINS. SELECT A NUMBER OF AUTO PLAY SPINS. PRESS TO IMMEDIATELY COMMENCE PLAY. THE SELECTED NUMBER OF GAMES WILL BE PLAYED WHILE THERE IS SUFFICIENT BALANCE ON THE CREDIT METER OR UNTIL FREE GAMES ARE TRIGGERED. DURING AUTO PLAY, PRESS TO CANCEL REMAINING AUTO PLAY SPINS.
