Gold Inferno Gold Inferno Gold Inferno is a 3x3 game with 9 selectable lines, and jackpots that scale up as the bet per line increases. The game revolves around different tiers of WILD symbols that also act as multiplier symbols. Jackpots are awarded for landing three WILD symbols of different tiers on a played payline. GAME FEATURES Jackpots Feature Triggered by landing any 3 wilds on a played payline Three 3X wilds on an active payline award the highest tier jackpot. Three 2X wilds on an active payline award the second tier jackpot. Three 1X wilds on an active payline award the third tier jackpot. A combination of any three mixed wild symbols on an active payline awards the fourth tier jackpot. Jackpots scale up with the Bet per Line. Multiplier Wild Feature One 3X wild in a non-jackpot winning combination will triple the win, and two 3X wilds in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 9x the win. One 2X wild in a non-jackpot winning combination will triple the win, and two 2X wilds in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 4x the win. One 3X wild and one 2X wild in a non-jackpot winning combination will pay 6X the win.
