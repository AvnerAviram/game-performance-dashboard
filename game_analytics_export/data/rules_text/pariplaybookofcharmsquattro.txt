How to Play Click the Spin or Autoplay buttons to start playing. Adjust your bet by choosing Max Bet to begin with the maximum allowable total bet. You can adjust your bet anytime by clicking the Total Bet button and using the – and + buttons. Select Game The Select Game option in the lower right corner allows you to choose between playing 1, 2, 3 or 4 games at once. Your current selection will be highlighted. Select Quatro to play 4 slot machines simultaneously on a single screen for four times the thrill! Win Lines Each time you win, the winning line will be displayed. There are 10 possible win line combinations per machine that will count as a Win , Big Win , or Mega Win for a total of up to 40 possible win lines in Quatro mode . Win lines pay from lef to right only. Symbols & Features Wild Scater Symbols Scater Symbols are displayed as the Book of Charms and are Wild in this game. Scater Symbols will act as a substitute for all symbols except active Expanding Symbols . Land 3 or more Scater Symbols during a play to win a bonus Scater Prize and 10 Free Spins with an additional special Expanding Symbol ! Wild Scater Symbols substitute for all symbols except active Expanding Symbols during the Free Spins Bonus Game . Scater Symbols are Wild in this game and substitute for all symbols in the Base Game . Sticky Wild Scater Symbols are on reels one (1), two (2), three (3), four (4) and fve (5). Scater Symbols are also Sticky in this game, if a spin results in any Sticky Wild Scater Symbols in view then one (1) single free re-spin is awarded. All Sticky Wild Scater Symbols stick in place during the re-spin. Additional Sticky Wild Scater Symbols do not award further re-spins. Free Spins Bonus Game Land 3 or more Wild Scater Symbols – the Book of Charms – visible anywhere on the reels during a single play to trigger the Free Spins Bonus Game . The Free Spins Bonus Game automatically awards 10 free spins. During the Free Spins Bonus Game , one of the symbols on the reel will be randomly selected to become an Expanding Symbol . An additional 10 free spins will be awarded per every group of 3 or more Wild Scater Symbols landed during a single play of the Free Spins Bonus Game for a total of up to 40 free spins.
