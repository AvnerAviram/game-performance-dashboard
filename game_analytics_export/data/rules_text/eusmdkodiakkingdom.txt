Kodiak Kingdom About this Game Welcome to Kodiak Kingdom. This is a 6 reel 4 row video slot game. This game has 4096 Ways. It features Free Spins, Multiplier Wild, and Buy Free Spins. General Way pays if in succession on adjacent reels, from the leftmost position to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per Way is paid. l Simultaneous wins on different Ways are added. Wild symbols Wild and Multiplier Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols. Wild and Multiplier Wild substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a Way according to the PAYTABLE. The base game and Free Spins begin with a multiplier of X1. For every Multiplier Wild that lands on a spin, the multiplier increases by 1. Wilds and Multiplier Wilds appear on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 only. Every individual win in the base game is multiplied by the current multiplier. Free Spins In the base game, landing 3, 4, 5, or 6 Free Spins symbols anywhere on the reels award 10, 15, 20, and 30 Free Spins, respectively. Landing additional Free Spins symbols in the Free Spins feature award additional Free Spins. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 Free Spins symbols awards 1, 3, 10, 15, 20, and 30 additional Free Spins, respectively. For every Multiplier Wild that lands in Free Spins, the multiplier increases by 1. Every increase of the multiplier persists for the duration of Free Spins. Every individual win in Free Spins is multiplied by the current multiplier. Wins during Free Spins are added to the total win. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Buy Free Spins In the Buy Free Spins feature, guaranteed Free Spins can be purchased at a multiple of the current bet. At least 10 Free Spins can be purchased for 90X the current bet. When choosing the Buy Free Spins feature, 3 Free Spins symbols are placed on reels 1, 2, and 3. When choosing the Buy Free Spins feature, there is a chance to land more Free Spins symbols on reels 4, 5 and 6. The Buy Free Spins feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Buy Free Spins feature can only be used at any time a bet change can take place.
