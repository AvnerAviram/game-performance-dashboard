Scarab Kingdom About this Game l Welcome to Scarab Kingdom. l This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. l This game has 30 lines. l It features Pharaoh's Free Spins with progressive multiplier, Cleo’s Bonus, Wild Scrolls, and Scarab Scatters. General l Win on a line pays if in succession from the leftmost to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. l Only the highest win per line is paid. l Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. l Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols and Scarab Scatters symbols. l Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. l Wild Scrolls can trigger in the base game and in Pharaoh's Free Spins. l It is not possible to trigger Cleo’s Bonus within Pharaoh's Free Spins. Scarab Scatter Collection l In the base game, a Bonus Bar is located above the reel set. Each reel has a Scarab Holder located above the reel. l When a Scarab Scatter lands, it is added to the Scarab Holder and to the Bonus Bar. l 3 Scarab Scatters in a Scarab Holder trigger the Wild Scrolls feature on that reel. l Scarab Scatters do not appear on the reels on which Wild Scrolls is active. l Collecting 100 Scarab Scatters triggers the Cleo’s Bonus feature. l Scarab Holders, Wild Scrolls, Bonus Bar, and remaining reels persist for each bet level. l Scarab Scatter symbols appear in base game only. Wild Scrolls l The Wild Scrolls feature turns all positions on a reel into Wild symbols. l On every spin, Wild symbols on a Wild Scroll shift down one position on the reels. l Free Spins symbols are visible through the Wild Scrolls. l Free Spins can be won when Wild Scrolls is active. Cleo's Bonus l In Cleo’s Bonus, there are 4 types of bonus coins. l Each type has 3 coins. l In total, there are 12 bonus coins. l Each coin type corresponds to one cash prize: X15 bet, X40 bet, X200 bet, and X5000 bet. l Revealing 3 of the same coin type awards the corresponding cash prize according to the PAYTABLE. l When the feature ends, the Bonus Bar count is reduced by 100. Pharaoh's Free Spins l Free Spins symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game. l 3 Free Spins symbols awards an indefinite amount of Free Spins. l Free Spins begins with a multiplier of X1. l After every spin, the multiplier increases by 1. l Every individual win in Free Spins is multiplied by the current multiplier. l At the beginning of Free Spins, Wild Scrolls is automatically active on reel 3. l Landing a regular Wild on any reel activates Wild Scrolls. l Regular Wilds do not appear on the same reel where Wild Scrolls is active. l Wild Scrolls activate on the same spin it was triggered. l The feature ends when Wild Scrolls are no longer present on the visible reel area. l The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount.
