The Everlasting Scratchcard How to Play Reveal 3 matching symbols to win a guaranteed prize and trigger the game's regenerating mechanism. The regenerating mechanism works in the following manner: The 3 winning symbols are removed from the game grid. New symbols are automatically generated at random in the empty grid spaces. Any combination of three matching symbols pays a prize. These symbols are then removed from the game grid, leaving space into which the symbols above will drop - including symbols that are currently off screen. This is known as the regenerating mechanism. Should the regenerating mechanism cause 3 more matching symbols to be brought into view, either as completely new symbols on the grid or as a combination of existing and new symbols, this will then cause another win to be paid and the regenerating mechanism will re-trigger. All prizes are paid into cumulative bank, paid to the player at the end of the game. The game ends if the regenerating mechanism fails to bring any news combinations into view or the regenerating mechanism repeats six times then the game ends. At this point the player is paid out the sum total of wins in the cumulative bank.
