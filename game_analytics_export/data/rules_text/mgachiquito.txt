Chiquito MAIN GAME In this game, each prize could either be risked, by pressing the play button, or to be collected, by pressing the collect button. In case the prize is risked, it will either be given the amount of a one level higher prize or the equivalent amount of fistros. Once the line of the three lemons is reached, the game becomes an up down, where the possible prizes go up or down in the prize plan. UPPER GAME In order to play this game it will be required to have enough fistros and credits. Whenever a prize is obtained, unless it is a blue bars prize, the player will be offered to either risk a portion of the prize or to collect it, until there are no more portions left. Choosing to risk a portion may allow the player to either obtain the double of that portion or the equivalent value of that portion in fistros. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. RTP The Return to Player for this game is 90%. Last modification date: 7/22/2020
