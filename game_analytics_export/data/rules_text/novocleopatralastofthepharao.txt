Cleopatra Last of the Pharaohs Main Game Cleopatra LOTP is played on 5 reels and 10 win lines. Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along a win line. Winning combinations start on the first reel on the left and run along an active win line to the last reel on the right in the regular game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other without any other symbols between them. The Cleo (Wild) substitutes all other symbols and also triggers the Free Spins Bonus. Sand Storm Bonus The sand storm bonus is triggered randomly at the end of a losing game. The bonus will trigger through a tornado that sweeps across the top and bottom reel screen, when it sweeps over the reels, it can change between 1 and 5 reels to new reels with new symbols. Once the storm stops, they’ll be a winning combination in view. Pyramid Bonus You’ll start by selecting a square on the bottom row of the pyramid you’ll either reveal and arrow which will progress you up to the next row, or reveal a set of reels with stacked wild(s). The aim is to find an arrow on each row and progress up and reach the top of the pyramid where you’ll be awarded stacked wilds on all 5 reels. Free Spins All free spins will played out until the remaining bank reaches zero, but the free spins can be locked when the Ankh symbol lands in. This means the free spins remaining bank won’t count down anymore, The Black Cat symbol will unlock the free spins and the free spins remaining bank will start counting down, this process can trigger several times during a free spins game. RTP The Return to Player for this game is 95.17%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/18/2020
