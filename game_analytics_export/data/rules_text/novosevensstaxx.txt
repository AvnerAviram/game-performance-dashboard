Sevens Staxx Main Game With a large game screen of 5x4 symbols, Sevens Staxx gives you 1024 ways to win! Your aim is to land 5 identical symbols along one of these 1024 winning pathways, running from left to right across the screen. All symbols, except BONUS WHEEL, are stacked and can award you with huge full screen wins. Sevens Staxx Feature Land 4x Sevens on just one of the middle 3 reels and re-spin your way to huge Jackpot wins. Triggering a Sevens Staxx feature initially awards you with 3 re-spins on all unfilled positions on the triggering reel. Collecting just 5 additional Sevens symbols during respins will award you the respective Jackpot, as displayed above the reel, while being rewarded with additional prizes from the SEVENS along the way. Triggering the Sevens Staxx feature with WHITE SEVENS on reel 2 gives you a chance to win the Mini Jackpot, triggering the Sevens Staxx feature with BLUE SEVENS on reel 4 gives you a chance to win the Minor Jackpot, and triggering the Sevens Staxx feature with RED SEVENS on reel 3 gives you a chance to win the Grand Jackpot. Feature trigger : 4x WHITE SEVENS on reel 2 4x RED SEVENS on reel 3 4x or or BLUE SEVENS on reel 4. Feature hit rate: 70-100 games. Bonus Wheel Feature A spin of the Bonus Wheel can win up to 20x bet, or one of the three Sevens Staxx features, or even a Mini, Minor or Grand Jackpot. Feature trigger: 2x BONUS WHEEL symbols on reels 1 and 5. Feature hit rate: 100-140 games. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except SEVENS and BONUS. BONUS symbols appear on reels 1 and 5 only. WHITE SEVENS appear on reel 2 only. RED SEVENS appear on reel 3 only. BLUE SEVENS appear on reel 4 only. Highest win only paid per combination on screen. Free Spins are played at trigger bet.
