Rainbow Riches Fortune Favours How to Play Game Introduction This is a 5 reel game that features a Magic Fairy Bonus and a Big Bet that contains 4 reel sets. The game has 10 win lines in both the standard and Big Bet games. Magic Fairy Bonus The Magic Fairy Bonus is a mystery bonus. If a single Magic Fairy symbol appears on the reels, it will transform into another symbol with the potential to create a win. If multiple Magic Fairy symbols appear on the reels, they will transform into the same symbol with more winning potential. The Magic Fairy Bonus is active in both the standard game and the Big Bet game. Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 4 reel sets that spin consecutively. Additional benefits are available at the various stakes and include: Magic Fairy symbols in view transfer to subsequent reel sets. Wild symbol becomes active. Both Magic Fairy and Wild symbol transfer to subsequent reel sets. 10, J, Q symbols are eliminated from the reels for greater win potential. Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96%.
