Pirate Plunder Pirates Plunder GAME RULES Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play. Beginning with the left-most reel, pays left to right on consecutive reels. The Won field displays winnings in credits. All games are played with 15 paylines. Clicking SPIN or space bar (Flash only) repeats the last bet. In the flash version space bar can be used to spin. Select Multiplier Bet Level (1x to 20x). Only highest winner paid per line. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter. SCATTER wins (if available) are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter. WILD substitutes for all symbols except PIRATE SHIP, TREASURE MAP and SCATTER. Five natural WILD symbols on a payline at maximum bet level award the Jackpot. Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000. Mobile only Tapping SPIN repeats last bet Pirate Ship Bonus Feature 3, 4 or 5 adjacent PIRATE SHIP symbols starting from the leftmost reel on any payline will start a pick bonus. Select a Pirate Ship to reveal the prize. With 3 Ships, win 45x to 165x your bet per line. With 4 Ships, win 60x to 300x your bet per line. With 5 Ships, win 75x to 450x your bet per line. Treasure Island Bonus Feature The TREASURE MAP symbol is a scatter symbol. 3 TREASURE MAP symbols in any position on reels 2, 3 and 4 will start a pick bonus. Select the Treasure Chests until the Captain's Parrot is found. Win 5x to 100x your total credits bet. Scatter Pay 2, 3, 4 or 5 SCATTER symbols on any reel in any position awards a SCATTER pay. Only the highest winning SCATTER pay is awarded. Auto Spin: In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. In settings, choose Auto Spin "ON" and select the desired number of autospins. Select the STOP button to cancel Autospin. Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus.
