Fu Shen Zhu Fu 1 Introduction Fu Shen Zu Fu is a Chinese-American themed game with 50 base win lines and 5x4 base reels. The game features 3 pots that each award different variations of the Free Games bonus that can be awarded individually or all together. In addition to the standard base game, you also have the options to play Fortune Bet, to buy entry to the Free Games with Bonus Buy, and to gamble any winnings for either more cash or Free Games in the Dual Wheel Gamble. 2 How to Play Spin the reels by pressing the 'SPIN' button to try to achieve 2, 3, 4 or 5 of a kind reel wins. Select the 'CHANGE STAKE' button to increase or decrease the size of the bet. 2.1 Autoplay Press the 'AUTOPLAY' button to see the options. You must select the number of autoplay games, and your loss limit, before you have the option of starting the autoplay. 'Custom Loss Limit' refers to how much money you wish to lose before the auto play stops. For example, if you have 50 in your balance, and you choose a 20 loss limit, the auto play will stop as soon as your balance goes below 30, even if you have auto play games remaining. You can either choose to accept the suggested loss limit, which is your stake multiplied by the number of autoplay games you have selected, or manually type in a loss limit by pressing the 'Custom Loss Limit' button. Choose the 'More settings' option if you wish to stop the autoplay when you win over a certain amount. Select the 'AUTOPLAY' button during autoplay to stop them. 2.2 Turbo Mode Enabling 'Turbo Mode' increases the speed of a game by speeding up the reel spin animation. This does not impact the outcome of the game. 3 Game Rules All base games are played with 50 win lines. Free Games can be played with 50 or 88 win lines depending on the variation of Free Games that has been awarded. Wins can occur across any or all of the win lines. Only the highest award is paid per winning combination. All wins pay left to right on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel. Wins on different played lines are added. Your winnings will be credited to your account immediately. You will lose your bet amount if you lose the game. The wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbols and the Coins. The wild symbol appears on reels 2,3,4 and 5 in the Base Game and Free Games bonuses. During the base game modes, and some Free Games bonus variations, colour coded ingot symbols may land in view on the reels. Each colour coded ingot symbol is associated with the same coloured character above the reels, and can only land on specific reels; Green Ingot: Can only land on reel 1 and is associated with Fu Red Ingot: Can only land on reel 3 and is associated with Lu Purple Ingot: Can only land on reel 5 and is associated with Shou When 1 or 2 ingots land in view on the reels, you may be awarded a corresponding Free Games or Super Free Games Bonus. When all 3 ingots land in view on the reels, you will be awarded the Fortune Bonus Pick feature where you are guaranteed a Free Games bonus. 4 Fortune Bonus Pick At the start of the Fortune Bonus Pick feature, you will be presented with an array of 15 blank ingots. Behind each blank ingot is either a Fu, Lu, Shou or Wild bonus ingot. A Wild bonus ingot substitutes for all 3 bonus other bonus ingots and allows for multiple Free Games bonus variations to be awarded together. You must continue to pick blank ingots, revealing what is behind them, until one or more sets of 3 symbols are matched. Matching 3 Fu ingots awards the Fu Free games. Matching 3 Lu ingots awards the Lu Free games. Matching 3 Shou ingots awards the Shou Free games. Matching 3 Fu and 3 Lu ingots awards the Fu Lu super Free games. Matching 3 Fu and 3 Shou ingots awards the Fu Shou super Free games. Matching 3 Lu and 3 Shou ingots awards the Lu Shou super Free games. Matching 3 Fu, 3 Lu and 3 Shou ingots awards the Fu Lu Shou mega Free games. 5 Free Games Bonus Free Games variations may be awarded either individually (Free Games), as a pair (Super Free Games), or all at the same time (Mega Free Games) either via the pots activating in the base game or from being revealed in the Fortune Bonus Pick feature. The potential Free Games are: 5.1 Fu Bonus Free Games Fu Free Games is awarded when the green pot is activated, or 3 Fu ingots (or their substitute) are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 5 Free Games are awarded and the reels are increased to a height of 6 symbols, changing the game to 88 win lines. Whenever a wild symbol lands anywhere in view on the reels, the stack of wilds will ‘nudge’ up or down to fill that reel with wilds. Lines are awarded after any nudges. Ingot symbols may land on the reels and potentially award another Free Games bonus after the current one has concluded. 5.2 Lu Bonus Free Games Lu Free Games is awarded when the red pot is activated, or 3 Lu ingots (or their substitute) are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 10 Free Games are awarded and coin symbols are added to the reels. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards or Prize Pot letters. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Every time a new letter is revealed, it will add to the Prize Pot grid by the reels. The letter prizes are: [G] [R] [A] [N] and [D] for the grand Prize Pot [M] [A] [J] [O] and [R] for the major Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] [O] and [R] for the minor Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] and [I] for the mini Prize Pot If all the letters for a Prize Pot are collected, the corresponding Prize Pot is awarded. All Prize Pot grids will be reset after winning. Ingots will not be available on the reels, and additional Free Games bonuses cannot be awarded. 5.3 Shou Bonus Free Games Shou Free Games is awarded when the purple pot is activated, or 3 Shou ingots (or their substitute) are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 5 Free Games are awarded, coin symbols are added to the reels, and a x2 multiplier is added which is applied to all line wins. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards or extra free games. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Ingots may land on the reels and potentially award another Free Games bonus after the current one has concluded. 5.4 Fu Lu Bonus Super Free Games Fu Lu Super Free Games is awarded when the green and red pots are activated together, or 2 Fu, 2 Lu and a Wild ingot are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 10 Free Games are awarded, the reels are increased to a height of 6 symbols, changing the game to 88 win lines and coin symbols are added to the reels. Whenever a wild symbol lands anywhere in view on the reels, the stack of wilds will ‘nudge’ up or down to fill that reel with wilds. Lines are awarded after any nudges. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards or Prize Pot letters. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Every time a new letter is revealed, it will add to the Prize Pot grid by the reels. The letter prizes are: [G] [R] [A] [N] and [D] for the grand Prize Pot [M] [A] [J] [O] and [R] for the major Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] [O] and [R] for the minor Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] and [I] for the mini Prize Pot If all the letters for a Prize Pot are collected, the corresponding Prize Pot is awarded. All Prize Pot grids will be reset after winning. Ingot symbols will not be available on the reels, and additional Free Games bonuses cannot be awarded. 5.5 Fu Shou Bonus Super Free Games Fu Shou Super Free Games is awarded when the green and purple pots are activated together, or 2 Fu, 2 Shou and a Wild ingot are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 5 Free Games are awarded, the reels are increased to a height of 6 symbols, changing the game to 88 win lines, coin symbols are added to the reels, and a x2 multiplier is added which is applied to all line wins. Whenever a wild symbol lands anywhere in view on the reels, the stack of wilds will ‘nudge’ up or down to fill that reel with wilds. Lines are awarded after any nudges. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards or extra free games. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Ingot symbols may land on the reels and potentially award another Free Games bonus after the current one has concluded. 5.6 Lu Shou Bonus Super Free Games Lu Shou Super Free Games is awarded when the red and purple pots are activated together, or 2 Lu, 2 Shou ingots and a Wild ingot are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 10 Free Games are awarded, coin symbols are added to the reels, and a x2 multiplier is added which is applied to all line wins. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards, Prize Pot letters or extra free games. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Every time a new letter is revealed, it will add to the Prize Pot grid by the reels. The letter prizes are: [G] [R] [A] [N] and [D] for the grand Prize Pot [M] [A] [J] [O] and [R] for the major Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] [O] and [R] for the minor Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] and [I] for the mini Prize Pot If all the letters for a Prize Pot are collected, the corresponding Prize Pot is awarded. All Prize Pot grids will be reset after winning. Ingot symbols will not be available on the reels, and additional Free Games bonuses cannot be awarded. 5.7 Fu Lu Shou Bonus Mega Free Games Fu Lu Shou Mega Free Games is awarded when the green, red and purple pots are activated together, or 2 Fu, 2 Lu, 2 Shou ingots and a Wild ingot are revealed during the Fortune Bonus Pick. At the start of this bonus 10 Free Games are awarded, the reels are increased to a height of 6 symbols, changing the game to 88 win lines, coin symbols are added to the reels, and a x2 multiplier is added which is applied to all line wins. Coin symbols are available on all reels. Coins that land in view on the reels will randomly reveal either cash prize awards, Prize Pot letters or extra free games. Possible cash prize awards are 1x and 2x the current base bet. Every time a new letter is revealed, it will add to the Prize Pot grid by the reels. The letter prizes are: [G] [R] [A] [N] and [D] for the grand Prize Pot [M] [A] [J] [O] and [R] for the major Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] [O] and [R] for the minor Prize Pot [M] [I] [N] and [I] for the mini Prize Pot If all the letters for a Prize Pot are collected, the corresponding Prize Pot is awarded. All Prize Pot grids will be reset after winning. Ingot symbols will not be available on the reels, and additional Free Games bonuses cannot be awarded. 6 Fortune Bet Fortune Bet is an alternative to standard spins which can be activated by pressing on the Fortune Bet button. Activating the Fortune Bet allows you to select a new bet multiplier of 1.5x the current stake, in doing do, this increases the frequency of a Free Games bonus triggering. The awards, gamble entry and Big Win thresholds remain the same as the base stake. 7 Bonus Buy Bonus Buy allows you to purchase direct entry to a Free Games bonus and choose which level you purchase. When the Bonus Buy button is pressed, you are presented with 3 options to choose from: Free Games Bonus Super Free Games Bonus Mega Free Games Bonus The base stake can also be changed on the Bonus Buy modal. Changing this alters the cost of each option. All awards are determined by the current base stake. When a choice is confirmed for purchase, the game will spin in a random game that will award a Free Games bonus that attributes with the selection. Possible Free Games bonus games are Fu Free Games, Lu Free Games and Shou Free Games Possible Super Free Games bonus games are Fu Lu Super Free Games, Fu Shou Super Free Fames and Lu Shou Super Free Games. When choosing the Mega Free Games bonus the game will always spin in all 3 Free Games bonus ingots and award the Fu Lu Shou Mega Free Games bonus. 8 Gamble The gamble can be configured to be on, on if the win is greater than or equal to 5x stake, or off. If the gamble is on, any winnings awarded during the game will offer the gamble feature. If the gamble is on if the win is greater than or equal to 5x stake, any winnings greater than or equal to 5x stake awarded during the game will offer the gamble feature. The gamble entry trigger is based on the base stake before any stake multipliers are applied. When the gamble is entered, two wheels are presented. The left wheel offers the chance to increase the winnings by half, double the winnings, or triple the winnings. The win value can be adjusted by pressing the ‘-’ and ‘+’ arrows, this in turn will adjust the winning and losing segments of the wheel. The right wheel offers the chance to win the Free Games, Super Free Games, or Mega Free Games bonus. The level of the Free Games bonus awarded can be adjusted by pressing the ‘-’ and ‘+’ arrows, this in turn will adjust the winning and losing segments of the wheel. Press either ‘Gamble’ button during the feature to gamble your winnings and spin the pointer. If the pointer stops on the green segment, the cash value or Free Games bonus are awarded. If the pointer stops on the red segment, the player loses their winnings and exits to the base game. Press the ‘Collect’ button during the feature to collect your winnings. 8.1 Spin Chance Spin Chance is triggered automatically if the credit is reduced below the current price of play, yet is still greater than zero. Spin Chance offers the player an opportunity to wager their remaining credit in an attempt at winning an additional spin at the current bet. To play Spin Chance, select the ‘PLAY’ button to begin moving the pointer across the meter. Alternatively, select the ‘NO THANKS’ button to exit Spin Chance with the remaining credit. If the pointer stops on the green segment, a spin at the current bet is awarded and plays automatically. If the pointer stops on the red segment, the player loses any remaining credit and exits to the base game. The red and green segments are proportional to the amount of credit in relation to the current bet. The chance of winning a spin is determined by this ratio. 9 General Information 9.1 Game Recovery In the event of an unexpected interruption/termination of a real play game, the game will recover to the last known state. 9.2 Payback Information Your odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. This reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays, over an extended period of time. Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed 250,000.00. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every stake configuration. This game has been independently fairness tested. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
