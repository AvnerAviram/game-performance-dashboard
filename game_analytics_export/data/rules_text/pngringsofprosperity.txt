Rings of Prosperity Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules Rings of Prosperity is a 5-reel game with 243 payways. In the Base Game, when a BONUS SCATTER lands anywhere on the middle reel, the PROSPERITY WHEEL above the reels will stop and award one random prize. There are 6 possible prizes on this bonus wheel: 3 INSTANT PRIZE bonuses and 3 POT RE-SPINS features. The layout of the wheel does not reflect the actual chances of winning each prize. If an INSTANT PRIZE is won from the PROSPERITY WHEEL, it is paid immediately. A MINOR prize awards x30 of the total bet, a MAJOR prize awards x50 of the total bet, and a GRAND prize awards x100 of the total bet. If the PROSPERITY WHEEL stops on one of the three POTS: GREEN POT, RED POT, or PURPLE POT, a POT RE-SPIN bonus feature is triggered. Each feature awards 6 RE-SPINS, which cannot be re-triggered. The BONUS SCATTER that triggered the feature transforms into a DRAGON GOD SCATTER, and 2 additional DRAGON GOD SCATTERS are randomly placed on the reels at the start of the feature. Landing 3 or more DRAGON GOD SCATTERS anywhere on the reels during the Base Game triggers the FREE SPINS bonus feature. 6 FREE SPINS are awarded initially. During this feature, if a BONUS SCATTER lands anywhere on the middle reel, the bonus wheel above the reels stops to award additional FREE SPINS: +1, +3, or +5. The maximum number of FREE SPINS is capped at 70. The FREE SPINS feature cannot be triggered from the PROSPERITY WHEEL or during RE-SPINS. RINGS OF PROSPERITY appear during both the POT RE-SPINS and FREE SPINS features. Each time a DRAGON GOD SCATTER lands during these features, a RING either appears or upgrades at its position. There are 3 colors of RINGS: GREEN, RED, and PURPLE. At the end of the feature, each RING transforms into a bonus GOLD COIN that reveals an instant prize. A GREEN-GOLD COIN can reveal 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 4x, or 5x the total bet. A RED-GOLD COIN can award 6x, 7.5x, 8.75x, 10x, or 12.5x the total bet. PURPLE-GOLD COIN can reveal prizes of 15x, 17.5x, 20x, 22.5x, or 25x the total bet. Different RING mechanics apply during POT RE-SPINS and FREE SPINS. During POT RE-SPINS, any DRAGON GOD SCATTER that lands creates a RING on its reel position based on the color of the triggered POT from the PROSPERITY WHEEL: GREEN, RED, or PURPLE. A RING upgrade occurs only when the entire reel area is filled with RINGS of the same color: GREEN upgrades to RED, and RED upgrades to PURPLE. If all reel positions are already filled with PURPLE RINGS and another DRAGON GOD SCATTER lands, a bonus of x100 the total bet is awarded instantly. During FREE SPINS, the number and color of starting RINGS depend on the number of triggering DRAGON GOD SCATTERS: 3 SCATTERS = 3 GREEN RINGS, 4 SCATTERS = 4 RED RINGS, and 5 SCATTERS = 5 PURPLE RINGS. While in FREE SPINS, each DRAGON GOD SCATTER that lands creates a new RING or upgrades an existing one. If it lands on a reel position without a RING, a GREEN RING appears. If it lands on an existing GREEN RING, it upgrades to a RED RING. If it lands on a RED RING, it upgrades to a PURPLE RING. If it lands on a PURPLE RING, it moves to a new reel position and creates a GREEN RING. If no empty positions are available, the lowest-value RING on the reels is upgraded. If all reel positions are already filled with PURPLE RINGS and another DRAGON GOD SCATTER lands, a bonus of x100 the total bet is awarded instantly. This game also has a paying WILD symbol that can appear in any position on all 5 reels. It substitutes for all symbols except DRAGON GOD SCATTERS and BONUS SCATTERS to help form winning combinations. The average RTP is 94.20%. RTP is achieved through participation. Minimum Bet: 0.20 USD Maximum Bet: 100.00 USD In the menu, you can set your desired bet. There are 243 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, tap the spin button. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Actions Paytable - Open the menu and navigate to the Paytable page to review the features and payouts of the game. Auto Play - You can enable/disable the Auto Play feature in the menu. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Fast Play - Toggle on for a significantly faster gameplay. Hyper Spin - Pressing and holding down the SPIN button activates Hyper Spin mode after 1.5 seconds. The spins speed up immensely but slow down if a feature is activated. Spin / Start - Starts the game round with the currently selected bet. Stop - Stops the reels more quickly. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated.
