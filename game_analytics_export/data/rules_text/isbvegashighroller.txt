Vegas High Roller 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. Cash Chips and Scatter symbols can trigger wins anywhere on the reels. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Winnings on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from High Roller Free Spins, Cash Chip Spin, High Roller Bet game and Super Respins are added to the Cashier. The auto spin feature does not automatically start High Roller Free Spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Desktop version To play, follow these steps: Click and then use the + and - buttons to select a coin value. Note: The total bet, in your currency, is displayed above the currently selected coin value. Click to place the bet and spin the reels one time. The total amount won, in coins, according to the pay table, is shown in the Win popup. For each winning combination, the amount won is the amount shown in the pay table for that winning combination. The total amount won, in your currency, is shown on the info bar (WIN) and is the total amount you won in coins multiplied by the coin value. 2.2. Special Symbols and Features Note: The chip graphics are different during the three major features: High Roller Free Spins, Cash Chip Spin and High Roller Bet. 3.1. High Roller Free Spins Three or more Scatter symbols (Free Spins symbol) trigger High Roller Free spins, according to the pay table. During High Roller Free Spins, only chip symbols appear on the reels. No matter where a chip lands on the reels, you win the amount displayed on that chip, in coins. When multiple chips land on the reels, you win the amount that results from adding up the values on all the chips. Note : You cannot change the coin value during High Roller Free Spins. 3.2. Cash Chip Spin During normal spins, a Cash Chip Spin can be randomly triggered. During the Cash Chip Spin all chips that land in view will be cashed in with the Cashier. Note : During the Cash Chip Spin only chip symbols will be considered for any winnings. Once any chips have been collected they will show as blank symbols and have no value. 3.3. High Roller Bet To start the High Roller Bet game, click or tap the High Roller Bet button and then confirm your action. To end the High Roller Bet game at any time, click or tap Exit . Each High Roller spin will cost 200 x the currently selected coin value (20 x Total bet). During the High Roller Bet game, only chip symbols appear on the reels. No matter where a chip lands on the reels, you win the amount displayed on that chip, in coins. When multiple chips land on the reels, you win the amount that results from adding up the values on all the chips. During the High Roller bet game, whenever a chip lands in a position on the reels, that position is "marked" (turns red). When all positions are "marked", 3 Super Respins are triggered. When you exit the High Roller lounge, the positions that are already "marked" (red ) will be saved per individual coin value. 3.4. Super Respins (only during High Roller Bet) All chips that are on the reels on the spin that triggers the Super Respins remain in place until the respins end. Any new chip that lands on the reels during respins resets the respin counter (to 3 respins in total); in addition, it remains in place until the respins end. The respins end when either all the positions are filled or no respins remain. At the end of the respins, the amount collected from the respins is paid and then the High Roller Bet game resumes with all positions for the selected coin value as "unmarked". 4.
