Free Games are awarded on a separate screen with different reels Free Games are played at the same credits bet per spin as the triggering game Treasure Tier Bonus (pick Bonus) The number of tiers is equal to the number of picks awarded. Each tier contains four pick options. Pick to reveal either a credit amount or a multiplier amount. The bonus multiplier begins at 1 and is increased by the amount of each picked multiplier. If additional picks remain after an award is revealed, the other options are removed and a new set of 4 options is presented. At the end of the bonus, win the sum of all picked credits multiplied by the final pick bonus multiplier. Symbol Fusion Feature The game has 6 distinct play areas, each consisting of 4 symbol positions across 2 reels. When all 4 symbol positions in a given play area are filled with the same symbol, those 4 symbols fuse together to form a large version of that symbol.
