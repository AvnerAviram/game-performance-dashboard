Match More BetMGM Main Game Match More: Bet MGM is played on a 3x3 grid containing 9 cards. Each round starts with 9 face-down cards that are revealed one by one. Revealing 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid creates a winning match. Single Match, Double Match or Triple Match, how lucky are you? Up to 3 matches can be won in a single game! Additional stacking Multipliers can increase the value of your winnings by up to 27x! The highest possible win is a Triple Match that is multiplied by a 27x Multiplier. Rules One winning combination consists of 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid. 3 ways to win: Single Match - 3 symbols of one kind Double Match - 3 symbols of one kind and 3 symbols of a second kind Triple Match - 3 symbols of one kind, 3 symbols of a second kind and 3 symbols of a third kind 3x Multiplier can appear on top of any revealed symbol. Up to 3 Multipliers can appear in one round. When multiple Multipliers appear, their values are multiplied together and are then applied to the total match win for that game round.
