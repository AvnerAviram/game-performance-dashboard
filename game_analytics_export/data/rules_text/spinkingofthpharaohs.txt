King of the Pharaohs King of the Pharaohs HIGHLIGHTS King of the Pharaohs is a 50 line slot played on a 5x3 grid. Play Hold and Respin to win a jackpot! Stacked Wilds appear on reels 2,3,4, and 5! Win big with Wild Multipliers in Free Spins! Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels starting with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win paid on each payline. FREE SPINS 3,4, or 5 scattered Bonus symbols initiates the Free Spins and awards 2 times the total stake. Win a Scatter award and 8,12,0r 16 Free Spins for 3,4,or 5 scattered Bonus symbols respectively. During the Free Spins, 3,4, or 5 Bonus symbols award 8,12,or 16 additional Free Spins respectively. All wins using a Wild are multiplied by 2 for each Wild used. Free Spins are played with all 50 lines using the same total stake that triggered the Bonus. The Bonus ends when 0 Free Spins remain. Different reels are used during the Free Spins. JACKPOTS There are 4 jackpots available: Mini, Minor, Major, and Grand. HOLD AND RESPIN Major, Minor, Mini, or credits appear on each Hold and Respin symbol. Hold and Respin credit values are 250, 200, 150, 100, 50, 20, 15,10,5,4,3,2,1 multiplied by the total stake. 6 or more Hold and Respin symbols land, the spins remaining resets to 3. The Hold and Respin Bonus ends, when 0 spins remain or 15 Hold and Respin symbols have landed. Values on each Hold and Respin symbol are awarded at the end of the Hold and Respin feature.
