Jewels and Gems Game Theme and Graphics Everyone loves jewels and gems. Scratch the card and the gem treasures hidden on this card can be yours! Enjoy this wonderful game, the next game from the scratch card series. Main Game Jewels and Gems is played on a 3x3 grid containing 9 cards. Each round starts with 9 opaque cards that are scratched one by one. Single Match, Double Match or Triple Match? How lucky are you? 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid create a winning match. The highest possible win is a Triple Match, that can be multiplied by a 27x Multiplier. Up to 3 Multipliers can be awarded in one round Rules One winning combination consists of 3 symbols of a kind anywhere on the grid. 3 ways to win: Single Match - 3 symbols of one kind Double Match - 3 symbols of one kind and 3 symbols of a second kind Triple Match - 3 symbols of one kind, 3 symbols of a second kind and 3 symbols of a third kind 3x Multiplier can appear on top of any revealed symbol. Up to 3 Multipliers can appear in one round. Malfunction voids all pays and plays Last modification date: 3/13/2024
