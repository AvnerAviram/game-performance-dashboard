Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to line wins. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 Free Games Bonus Symbol in any position on reels 2, 3 and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 7, 9 or 11 free games randomly. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Different reels are used during the Free Games Bonus. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. SCATTER CAPTURE FEATURE (BASE AND BONUS) Two capture frames are located on the center position of the 1st and 5th reels. Only the Wild symbol and the 4 high pay symbols are eligible to activate the Scatter Capture feature. Two matching eligible symbols landing within the Capture Frames will activate the Scatter Capture Feature. During the Scatter Capture Feature, all symbols on the reels of the same type that activated the feature award a scatter pay.
