Cuzco Gold Inca leaders have hidden their most precious treasure in their main city, Cuzco. You discovered a chest of 35 gold coins. You have to pick them one by one or by selecting multiple coins but be very careful because there are 5 hidden bomb traps. You can cash out any time you prefer, going home with the coins you gathered. Game Rules Chance To Win The game has an average theoretical RTP of 95.5%, the actual value depends on player choice with perfect play giving a minimum RTP of 98.6%. Fairness The outcome of each coin chosen in this game is randomly determined in accordance with the advertised rules. The outcome of any one pick will not affect the outcome of any other picks. This game behaves fairly and has been independently tested. Maximum Liability Cuzco Gold has a maximum prize of 200x bet value for any given bet cycle. How to Win You can pick any of the gold coins from the 35 or you can swipe more than one. Any pick that you do (single or multiple coins) will increase your prize. You can cash out whenever you want. There are 5 hidden bombs under the gold coins. If you pick a gold coin that has a bomb, you will loose all your prize and the game will end. Guide to Betting Your balance and bet value is visible in the bottom center of the screen and also in the right menu. To change your bet value use the arrow buttons near the PLAY button from the right menu. This will alter the amount bet per game.
