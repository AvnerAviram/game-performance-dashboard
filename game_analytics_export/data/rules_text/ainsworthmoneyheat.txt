Money Heat Rules 'Dollar' substitutes for all symbols except scatters. 'Dollar' appears on reels 3, 4 and 5 only during paid games. 'Coin' is a scatter. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only on each line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. Wins on different lines are added. The game has a return to player of 94.34%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games Features 10 free games are triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Coin'. During the Free Games Feature, ‘Dollar’ appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. 'Dollar' substitutes for all symbols except scatters, 'Gold Dollar', 'x2', 'x3', and 'x5' 'Gold Dollar' appears on the reel 3 only and substitutes for all symbols except scatters, 'Dollar', 'x2', 'x3', and 'x5'. When 'Gold Dollar' substitutes in winning combination, the prize is randomly multiplied by x2, x3 or x5 as displayed. A bonus reel appears with 'Coin' and 3 new bonus symbols 'x2', 'x3', 'x5'. When ‘x2’, ‘x3’ or 'x5' appears anywhere on the bonus reel all wins for that game are multiplied by the bonus symbol multiplier, as displayed. Any 3 or more scattered 'Coin' will trigger an additional 5 free games. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games. Last modification date: 6/17/2020
