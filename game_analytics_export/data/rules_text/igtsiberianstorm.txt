Siberian Storm Enter the snow-capped forest of fortune in Siberian Storm , featuring MultiWay Xtra , with up to 720 ways to win on an eye-catching hexagonal 5-reel interface. The game pays in both directions - left-to-right and right-to-left - leading to even more ways to win! Scatter pays and stacks of wilds make this game a payout beast! How To Bet Siberian Storm offers 720 ways to win for only 50 coins. Bet Configuration Menu (mobile/tablet/mini games) Press the arrow to open the bet configuration menu. Press the check mark to confirm and close the bet configuration menu. 720 Ways Displays the current number of ways being played. Betting is fixed at 720 ways which cost 50 coins. Coin Value Displays the current coin value shown in currency. Press the minus button (-) to decrease the coin value. Press the plus button (+) to increase the coin value. Spin Press the circular arrow to submit the bet and spin the reels. Auto Spin Feature Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of spins at 720 ways and the current coin value. For UK jurisdictions where Auto Spin is enabled: Select the number of auto spins, the loss limit, and the (optional) single win limit. After selecting the desired bet configuration, press AUTO SPIN , and choose how many spins to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until STOP is pressed, the balance is insufficient to spin, or any enabled win/loss limit settings are reached. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions. Auto spin is not available in mini format. MultiWay Xtra Feature With the MultiWay Xtra feature, there are 720 ways to win on every spin. MultiWay Xtra wins pay for matching symbols in any position in adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column or the rightmost column. More than one matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Scatter and Bonus symbols. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra win for each symbol is paid per spin. Example of a MultiWay Xtra payout: The following diagram shows a sample reel outcome using the WhiteTiger symbol. Because there is at least one WhiteTiger or Wild symbol in each of the first four columns, the player has won the 4 WhiteTiger MultiWay Xtra win which pays 50 coins per way. Payout example: 1 x 2 x 3 x 1 WhiteTigers → 6 winning ways: Column 1 Column 2 Column 3 Column 4 Column 5 Wild WhiteTiger WhiteTiger Wild Wild Wild Wild WhiteTiger WhiteTiger WhiteTiger WhiteTiger WhiteTiger 1 WhiteTiger 2 WhiteTigers 3 WhiteTigers 1 WhiteTiger To determine the payout for this combination: Count the number of WhiteTiger and Wild symbols in each column. Calculate the total number of winning ways (1 x 2 x 3 x 1 = 6). Multiply the number of ways by the 4 WhiteTiger symbol win (6 x 50 = 300). The win is 300 coins. The actual amount won in currency is based on the coin value. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered when Bonus scatter symbols appear anywhere on 5 consecutive reels. Triggering the Free Spins Bonus awards 8 free spins. If more than one combination per spin awards free spins, the sum of the spins for all the winning combinations is awarded at once. Up to 96 spins is awarded initially. The Free Spins Bonus is retriggered when Bonus scatter symbols appear anywhere on 5 consecutive bonus reels, and additional 8 free spins is awarded. The bonus can be retriggered multiple times, up to a maximum of 240 free spins per bonus. Reels used in the Free Spins Bonus are different from the reels used in the base game. Free spins are played at same coin value as the triggering spin. Rules Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays. MultiWay Xtra wins pay for the same symbol in any position in adjacent columns. The same symbol in the same column multiplies the win. MultiWay Xtra wins pay left to right and right to left. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra win for each symbol is paid per spin. Coinciding MultiWay Xtra wins from different symbols are added. MultiWay Xtra wins are multiplied by the coin value. Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game and Free Spins Bonus . Only the highest scatter win is paid. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet. Scatter and bonus wins are independent from MultiWay Xtra wins and are added to the total amount paid. Wins are shown in currency. In some jurisdictions, regardless of bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Free Spins Bonus , plus the outcome which launched the bonus. If the award cap is reached in the bonus, the bonus ends immediately, even if there are remaining free spins. Additional Information Expected Payback In accordance with fair gaming practices required in most legal jurisdictions worldwide, each and every game outcome is completely independent except in cases where one spin unlocks a feature for a subsequent spin or series of spins, per the game rules. The odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. The odds do not vary based on prior outcomes, bet size, balance size, time of day, day of week, etc. For example, if the top award is won on a given play of the game, the chances of winning the top award on the next play are exactly the same. Likewise, prior losses do not change future odds. The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays by numerous players over an extended period of time. The results for any given player over a play session can vary widely from this long-term, expected average in either direction. The fewer the number of games played, the wider the normal variance of actual payback that may be experienced. Intellectual Property © 2017 IGT. All rights reserved.
