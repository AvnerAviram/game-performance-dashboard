Titans of the Sun - Theia About Titans of the Sun - Theia Titans of the Sun - Theia is a five reel, fifteen payline, and one hundred and fifty coin slot machine. Regular and special symbols Regular symbols Wild symbol Scatter symbol Bonus features and games Free Spins bonus game Mystery symbol feature Paytable Achievements feature Game play and advanced features Playing the game Free Games Regular symbols Regular symbols are all symbols on the reels that do not have a bonus feature but complete regular winning combinations on enabled paylines. Regular symbol payout rules: The Theia symbol creates a symbol stack on all reels. Matching symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. All payout tables display the number of coins or credits that are won for each combination. Regular payouts depend on your bet per payline. Only one winning combination is paid out per payline. If there is more than one possible winning combination on a payline, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size you select. Wild symbol The Titans of the Sun symbol is a wild symbol. This symbol: Substitutes for other symbols to complete wins. Creates its own winning combination when multiple symbols appear in a line on an enabled payline. Wild symbol substitution rules: Does not substitute for the scatter symbol. Does not substitute for the Mystery symbol. Only one winning combination is paid out per payline. If there is more than one possible winning combination on a payline, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. Scatter symbol The Lovers symbol is a scatter symbol. Scatter symbol rules: Two or more symbols must appear anywhere on the reels to create a win. This symbol cannot be substituted by the wild symbol. Three or more Lovers symbols scattered on the reels, activates the Free Spins bonus game. Scatter symbol payout rules: Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed, once the reels have come to a stop. All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. All payout tables display the number of coins or credits that are won for each combination. Scatter payouts depend on your total bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on consecutive reels to win. Free Spins bonus game Three or more Lovers symbols scattered on the reels, activates the Free Spins bonus game. In this game: You are awarded fifteen free spins. The Mystery symbol feature is available. The Mystery symbols can transform into wild symbols during the free spins. Free Spins bonus game rules: All wins are doubled during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. The bonus game can be re-activated during the free spins. So, if three or more Lovers symbols are scattered on the reels during the free spins, you are awarded an additional fifteen free spins. The extra free spins are added to the remaining number of free spins. Winnings are automatically collected during the bonus game. Scatter and bonus wins are added to regular wins. Mystery symbol feature One or more Mystery symbols displayed on the reels, activate the Mystery symbol feature. In this feature The Mystery symbols transform into other symbols. All the Mystery symbols transform into the same symbol and any winning combinations are resolved. The Mystery symbol creates a symbol stack. There is no payout for any Mystery symbol combination. The Mystery symbol does not transform into a scatter symbol. During the regular game the symbols do not transform into a wild symbol. During the Free Spins bonus game the symbols can transform into a wild symbol. Only one winning combination is paid out per payline. If there is more than one possible winning combination on a payline, you are paid out the value of the highest combination only. Paytable Achievements feature The Paytable Achievements feature enables you to track your winnings status by highlighting symbol combinations that you have won. Paytable Achievement rules: Once you have won all combinations for a particular symbol, you are informed of this achievement, and the symbol is highlighted on the payout table. Achievements are permanently highlighted on the payout table, even after you exit the game. Achievements are logged during the regular slot game. The following are not logged as achievements: A winning combination completed in the Free Spins bonus game. A winning combination completed by a wild symbol. Playing the game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on the payline. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. You can view your win history and casino balances in the game. Your wins are displayed in the Win box, and your current casino balance is displayed in the Balance box.
