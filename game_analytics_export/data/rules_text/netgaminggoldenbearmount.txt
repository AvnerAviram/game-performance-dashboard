Golden Bear Mountain Golden Bear Mountain Game Rules By playing our games, you agree that you do not find them offensive, unfair or indecent. How to Play Slots: Choose the amount you would like to bet with. Control the bet amount by clicking the arrows next to the Coin Value or choose Max Bet (Max Coin Value). Your winnings are automatically added to your account balance. You can see your Total Win displayed in the WIN section of the screen after each spin. Game Rules: The game is played with 10 fixed paylines and each game costs minimum of 10 fixed coins. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Payline prizes are multiplied by coin value. All symbols pay from left to right in consecutive order, excluding the Bonus symbol, which pays in any direction. Highest win only paid per selected line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. For this game, the long term expected payback is 94.41%. Bonus Symbol: Bonus symbol wins are multiplied by the total cash bet. Landing 3 or more Bonus symbols start a Free Game with 8, 10 or 12 Free Spins. Free Spins can be re-triggered during the Free Game, same number of Free Spins are awarded as in the main game. Wild Symbol: Wild symbols substitute for all symbols, excluding the Bonus symbol. Game Features: All winnings containing a Wild symbol are multiplied by the reel ID on which the Wild Reel is positioned. In case of more than one active Wild Reel the highest multiplier is used for all wins. Expanding Wild Reels trigger a series of Re-Spins. With each Re-Spin all Wild Reels move one position to the right. Re-Spins end when there are no more Wild Reels present. Bonus symbols or additional Wild Reels cannot appear during Re-Spins.
