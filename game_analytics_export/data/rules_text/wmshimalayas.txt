Himalayas How to Play Game Introduction This is a 100 win line game that features clumped symbols, wild symbols, expanding wilds, extended reel symbols, a Snow Slide Bonus and a Free Spins Bonus with the potential to re-trigger. The game has 100 win lines in both the standard and Big Bet games. Malfunction voids all pays and play Snow Slide Bonus The Snow Slide Bonus is a mystery bonus that may occur during any spin. Should you be fortunate enough to get caught up in one, the snow will sweep over the reels leaving in its wake, a scattering of Wild symbols. Free Spins Bonus The Free Spins Bonus is triggered when 3 or more bonus symbols are in view at the end of a spin. Free Spins have a chance of re-triggerring if a compass symbol lands in view on reel 5 during Free Spins. Free Spin reels contain double height Wild symbols that expand to fill the entire reel. Big Bet The Big Bet game adds the chance of triggering Free Spins with just 2 symbols and offers potentially more ways to win with increases in stake. Additional benefits are available at the various stakes and include: - 2 symbols trigger Free Spins Bonus. - Low paying symbols removed from reels. - During Free Spins, one of the reels will always be Wild. Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 100 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. Last modification date: 12/29/2021
