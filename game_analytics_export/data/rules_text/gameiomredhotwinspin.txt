Red Hot Win Spin Game Rules • RED HOT WIN SPIN is a 5-reel, 3-column, video slot with 243 Ways to Win featuring wild substitutes, respins and a free spins bonus game with stacked HOT symbols. • Players are paid a prize for any combination of 3 or more consecutive matching adjacent symbols starting in the leftmost reel. • Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE. • Multiple wins are possible on a single spin. • WILD symbol appears only on Reels 2 and 4. • Symbol wins award the coin values shown in the PAYTABLE based on a total bet of 20 coins per spin. • WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the WIN SPIN SCATTER and RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER symbols. • WIN SPIN SCATTER symbols appear on reels 1-4. • RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER only appears on reel 5. • Only the highest winning combination is awarded per symbol combination. • Simultaneous wins on a single spin are added together. Reels of Fire Respin Feature • 1 x WIN SPIN SCATTER and 1 x RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER appearing anywhere on the reels in the base game will trigger the REELS OF FIRE respin feature. • REELS OF FIRE respins are played with the modified versions of the base game’s reels. • WILD symbol appears only on Reels 2 and 4. • No Scatters are present on any of the game’s reels during the bonus. • Respin is retriggered if it lands any symbol win. • Respins can be retriggered until a no-win spin occurs. • Respins are played at the same bet level as the spin that triggered the feature from the base game. Win Spin Bonus Feature • Any combination of 3 x SCATTER symbols appearing anywhere in the base game triggers the feature, awarding the Player 4 LIVES. • Any combination of 4 x SCATTER symbols appearing anywhere in the base game triggers the feature, awarding the Player 5 LIVES. • Any combination of 5 x SCATTER symbols appearing anywhere in the base game triggers the feature, awarding the Player 6 LIVES. • WIN SPIN SCATTER and RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER symbols can be used in any combination to trigger the feature. • WIN SPIN BONUS is a round of free spins limited by LIVES awarded to the player. • A LIFE is lost if a free spin does not result in a win with the designated HOT symbol for that spin. • The feature ends when no LIVES remain. • The HOT symbol is selected from the game’s regular symbols, beginning with the lowest-ranking symbol (Cherry) at the start of the feature. • The HOT symbol is assigned to the next highest-ranking symbol after a win with the current HOT symbol. • The cycle loops back to the lowest-ranking symbol after a win with the highestranking symbol (Lucky 7). • Free spins in this feature are played with the modified versions of the base game’s reels. • Only the HOT symbol, WILD symbol and WIN SPIN SCATTER symbol are present on the reels. • WILD symbol appears only on Reels 2 and 4. • RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER only appears on Reel 5 if it was present in the spin that triggered the feature. • WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the WIN SPIN SCATTER and RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER symbols. • Designated HOT symbol can appear as a stacked symbol. • All wins are multiplied by the value shown in the MULTIPLIER meter to the right of the reels. • MULTIPLIER meter begins at x1. • MULTIPLIER meter increases by x1 after every complete cycle where each game symbol has been the HOT symbol once and only once. • 2, 3 or 4 x SCATTER symbols appearing anywhere in a free spin awards 1, 2 or 3 extra LIVES respectively. If this trigger event includes a RED HOT WIN SPIN SCATTER a GUARANTEED win is also awarded. • A GUARANTEED win forces the next free spin to land a HOT symbol win. • 5 x SCATTER symbols appearing anywhere in a free spin awards the WIN STREAK feature. • WIN STREAK feature awards 10 consecutive GUARANTEED wins with the HOT symbol through 10 levels. • All free spins are played at the same bet level as the spin that triggered the feature from the base game.
