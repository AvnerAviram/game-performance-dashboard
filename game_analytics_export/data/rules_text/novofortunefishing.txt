The symbols range from Icelandic Birds to Crabs, and to other game specific themed symbols and decorations. The main feature of the game are different fish symbols that may appear stacked in both the base- and feature game. During the base game, they pay mixed, whilst during the Free Games, each fish has its own value and pays like a scatter. A cash price is triggered every time the Fisher Girl (Wild) and Fish symbols appear on the same spin. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across active win lines in the normal game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on an active win line without any other symbols between them. The Fisher Girl (Wild) symbol substitutes all other symbols, except scatters and can therefore complete a winning combination. And what's more: land 3 or more Ship symbols anywhere on the reels to trigger Free Games with Fortune Fishing Bonus, with a retrigger when 2 or more Ship symbols appear during Free Games. Feature Trigger : 5 / 4 / 3 Ship symbols (Scatter) trigger 20 / 15 / 12 free games, respectively. Description : Free Games are played using the same bet and lines as the spin which triggered them. During the feature, different reel strips are used. The feature can be retriggered: 5 / 4 / 3 / 2 Ship symbols (Scatter) trigger 20 / 15 / 12 / 3 additional Free Games, respectively. Fortune Fishing Bonus : Credits are awarded for each visible pair of one Fisher Girl and at least one Fish symbol. The amount of credits awarded depends on the fish’s color. Rules All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins.
