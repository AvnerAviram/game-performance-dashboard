Camelot Pots GAME OBJECTIVES: CAMELOT POTS is a five-reel, ‘ALL WAYS SLOT’ game with 243 possible ways to win in the base game and FREE SPINS. Symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in consecutive combinations (WAYS). CAMELOT POTS contains the KINGS FORTUNE and CLASH OF KINGS feature games. When 3 or more ROUND TABLE symbols land anywhere in view the KINGS FORTUNE feature is triggered. FREE SPINS can be triggered during the feature. Any SHIELD symbol/(s) which land in view will reveal a cash value or a POT title. SHIELD symbols are then locked in place. The reels will RESPIN until no further SHIELD symbols land in view. When 6 or more SHIELD symbols land anywhere in view the CLASH OF KINGS feature is triggered. The POTS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and GRAND) are active during the CLASH OF KINGS feature. The WILD symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 in both the base game and FREE SPINS. The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the ROUND TABLE and SHIELD symbols. HOW TO PLAY: BET VALUE: When the player enters the game the default BET is set. The player can change the BET value by clicking the ‘ +’ or ‘ -‘ buttons either side of the BET icon on desktop and mobile. C licking the BET icon will display the BET SELECT window. The player can select their desired BET value between the MIN and MAX BET. REPEAT BET: The BET for the next game will be the same as the amount wagered on the previous game. VOLUME: Game music and sound effects are set ON as default. Clicking on the VOLUME button will toggle all game music and sound effects ON and OFF. In play-money mode, the AUTO PLAY option will be disabled. CREDIT / BALANCE: After placing the desired BET, the remaining game BALANCE is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Players current game BALANCE is the sum of the amounts displayed under the BALANCE meter and the BET meter. Game BALANCE is shown at the bottom of the game screen. AUTOPLAY: Select AUTOPLAY for hands-free play. To configure AUTOPLAY: Click the AUTOPLAY button then select the required number of plays. AUTO PLAY begins automatically. The remaining AUTOPLAY amount is displayed on the PLAY button. Click on the PLAY button to cancel AUTOPLAY at any time. MENU OPTIONS (MOBILE AND DESKTOP): Click the HAMBURGER icon to view game options and information. Click the ‘i’ icon to view game information and rules. Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game play . GAME RULES: CAMELOT POTS is a five-reel, ‘ALL WAYS SLOT’ game with 243 possible ways to win. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the currently selected or default BET amount. All payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE. The PAYTABLE will always display the win amounts for the currently selected BET. Coinciding wins on different ways are added to the balance. Win amounts are rounded down to the closest value. All symbols pay from LEFT to RIGHT in any position on adjacent reels starting from the leftmost reel, except for the ROUND TABLE and SHIELD symbols. Only the highest win on each possible win combination will be paid. All possible wins are added to the player’s BALANCE. ROUND TABLE and SHIELD symbols act as scatter symbols and do not have a direct payout. When 3 or more ROUND TABLE symbols land anywhere in view the KINGS FORTUNE feature will be triggered. Any SHIELD symbol(s) in view will be locked in position and RESPINS will be awarded. 6 or more SHIELD symbols landing anywhere in view will trigger the CLASH OF KINGS feature with the chance to win the POTS. When the KINGS FORTUNE and CLASH OF KINGS features are triggered during the same game, the CLASH OF KINGS feature will be completed first, followed by the KINGS FORTUNE feature. The WILD symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 in both the base game and FREE SPINS. The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the ROUND TABLE and SHIELD symbols. WILDS can form part of multiple wins. The SHIELD symbol is only available in the base game and the CLASH OF KINGS feature and can appear on any reel. The ROUND TABLE symbol is only available in the base game, RESPINS and FREE SPINS. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) excluding jackpot is 92.674%. The theoretical return to the player (RTP) including jackpot is 95.678%. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “HISTORY” inside any game will allow you to access your gameplay history. Win count-up animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (DESKTOP). MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. All pays are in USD. RESPINS: A RESPIN is awarded to the player when 1 or more SHIELD symbols land in view. RESPINS will retrigger if further SHIELD symbols land on subsequent spins. The SHIELD symbols lock and hold until no further SHIELD symbols land in view. If a minimum of 6 SHIELD symbols land during the RESPINS feature the CLASH OF KINGS feature will be triggered. The values displayed on the SHIELD symbols will carry forward to the CLASH OF KINGS feature. During the RESPINS feature the value displayed on the SHIELD symbols will not award any payout. During the RESPINS feature, the player has the chance to trigger both the KINGS FORTUNE and CLASH OF KINGS feature games. The CLASH OF KINGS feature takes precedence followed by the KINGS FORTUNE feature. If the KINGS FORTUNE and RESPINS features trigger in the same game, then the KINGS FORTUNE feature takes precedence followed by the RESPINS feature. KINGS FORTUNE: The KINGS FORTUNE feature is triggered when 3 or more ROUND TABLE symbols land anywhere in view. The player must click the PLAY button to spin the wheel. The ROUND TABLE spins randomly and awards 1 of 5 prizes: CLASH OF KINGS 2 FREE SPINS with 50 EXTRA WILDS and 2x MULTIPLIER 10 FREE SPINS with 50 EXTRA WILDS 10 FREE SPINS with 100 EXTRA WILDS 10 FREE SPINS with 150 EXTRA WILDS The CLASH OF KINGS feature can also be triggered from the KINGS FORTUNE but starts with zero SHIELD symbols seeded on the reels. When the CLASH OF KINGS feature is triggered from the KINGS FORTUNE feature, any SHIELD symbols displayed on the base game reels will not be carried into the CLASH OF KINGS feature. The feature continues until all spins are complete or all 15 SHIELD symbol positions have been filled. FREE SPINS: FREE SPINS commences with the awarded prize from the KINGS FORTUNE. FREE SPINS play automatically. During FREE SPINS, if 3 or more ROUND TABLE symbols land anywhere in view then the introductory award of FREE SPINS is retriggered. The additional FREE SPINS are added to the remaining FREE SPINS. Any introductory WILD and MULTIPLIERS are kept for the remaining FREE SPINS. FREE SPINS are played at the same BET as the triggering game. CLASH OF KINGS: The CLASH OF KINGS feature is triggered when 6 or more SHIELD symbols land anywhere in view during the base game. The CLASH OF KINGS feature starts with the same number of SHIELD symbols seeded in their landing reel positions. The CLASH OF KINGS feature is played with 15 independent reels spinning individually. The CLASH OF KINGS feature starts with 3 spins awarded to the player. Each additional SHIELD symbol(s) that land in view resets the spins counter to 3. Each SHIELD symbol that lands in view will have a random BET MULTIPLIER applied from the following range - 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x, 2.5x, 3x, 4x, 4.5x, 5x, 7.5x, 15x, 20x, 60x, 75x. The CLASH OF KINGS feature will continue until the player reaches zero spins or has filled all 15 SHIELD positions. Once the CLASH OF KINGS feature is completed all the SHIELD values are cumulated and awarded as winnings to the player. The POTS (MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and GRAND) can be won only during the CLASH OF KINGS feature: If a SHIELD symbol labeled with MINI lands anywhere in view, the MINI pot (10x) will be awarded to the player. If a SHIELD symbol labeled with MINOR lands anywhere in view, the MINOR pot (50x) will be awarded to the player. If a SHIELD symbol labeled with MAJOR lands anywhere in view, the MAJOR pot (500x) will be awarded to the player. If a SHIELD symbol labeled with GRAND lands anywhere in view, the GRAND pot (10000x) will be awarded to the player. More than 1 POT award can be won during the CLASH OF KINGS feature. The POT values are in ratio with the BET wagered. JACKPOT RULES: THE BIG ONE jackpot can be triggered either in the base game, during AUTOPLAY (if applicable) or after a FEATURE GAME. The main screen then changes to THE BIG ONE screen. All the Jackpots and their values are listed on the right-side area and a jackpot wheel is displayed on the left-side area. There are five different Jackpots, each of which has a unique color. All five Jackpots are shown on the wheel. By clicking on the SPIN button, the jackpot wheel spins and stops on a color and the respective Jackpot is awarded. The current jackpot values of all five Jackpots are displayed during the base game.
