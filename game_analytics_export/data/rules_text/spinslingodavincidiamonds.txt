Best Strategy The best strategy dictates that the selection for positions of Wilds and Super Wilds is always in the position that will move the player closest to completing a Slingo. Where multiple positions with the same criteria are available the preference is given to the positions that are included in the most Slingos (win lines) For example the central square would be given preference since it is included in a horizontal, vertical and two diagonal lines. Where multiple positions with the same criteria exist, again a random choice is made. Rules All symbols occur on all reels Free spins symbols award an additional spin Extra spins are available after each game. Based on best strategy that standard game has a theoretical RTP of 96.52% Based on best strategy each extra spin has a theoretical RTP of 96.28% Prices are rounded to the nearest whole value which may alter the RTP Malfunction voids pays and plays In extra spins some potential prizes may require Super Wilds.
