Hot Spot 777 Game Rules RESPIN A Double Jackpot Hot Spot symbol landing in the center position on reel 2 triggers the Respin. When the Respin triggers, reel 2 locks and the remaining reels respin. The Respin will continue to respin until a non-winning combination results. Reels are weighted differently in the Respin than in the base game. When betting lines 1-3, the maximum number of respins is 5. When betting lines 4 and 5, the maximum number of respins is 9. DOUBLE JACKPOT HOT SPOT The Double Jackpot Hot Spot symbol is wild and substitutes for all non-wild symbols to make winning combinations. One Double Jackpot Hot Spot symbol pays 2X the winning combination, except for jackpots. Two Double Jackpot Hot Spot symbols pay 4X the winning combination, except for jackpots.
