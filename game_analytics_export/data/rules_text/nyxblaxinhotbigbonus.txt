GOLIATH SYMBOL The GOLIATH SYMBOL cannot be a WILD or BONUS symbol. The GOLIATH SYMBOL can appear partially in view. GOLIATH SYMBOLS can appear on any reel. No more than 1 GOLIATH SYMBOL can appear at any one time. WILD SYMBOL The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS symbol. WILDS are only visible on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. FREE SPINS BONUS When 3 or more FREE SPIN symbols appear scattered in view the FREE SPINS Bonus is triggered. Triggering the Bonus with 3 BONUS symbols awards 8 FREE SPINS, triggering with 4 awards 10 FREE SPINS, and triggering with 5 awards 12 FREE SPINS. A GOLIATH SYMBOL will be visible on every FREE SPIN. It is not possible to retrigger the bonus. The FREE SPINS BONUS has a minimum win feature. MINIMUM WIN FEATURE When the FREE SPIN Bonus is triggered, the MINIMUM WIN FEATURE activates and becomes equal to your current stake. On each FREE SPIN, you are guaranteed to win a minimum of your bet value. If a reel win is obtained which exceeds the stake, the minimum win value will be set to the highest reel win value achieved so far. BUY PASS You can choose to play the FREE SPINS by pressing the BUY PASS™ button. This function may not be available due to the jurisdiction in which you are located. The cost of the BUY PASS™ feature is displayed on screen. Press CONFIRM to begin the game. The reels will spin to a position containing at least 3 BONUS symbols, and the FREE SPINS BONUS will begin.
