Shou Hu Shen Shou Hu Shen™ Shou Hu Shen is a Fu Lion themed game that features two guardian lions. Pays are from left to right and use the PowerXStream® evaluation method. Bonus trigger is a scattered trigger with a single trigger available on a single spin. The bonus symbols appear on all reels. This traditional vibrant game featuring a Fu symbol as the highest paying symbol, a fu lion as the second and third highest paying symbol, and a various themed objects as the remaining high and mid pays. Royals are used as the low pay symbols. GAME FEATURES Power XStream Evaluation- Maximizes number of ways to win by evaluating awards based on number of symbols in any position. Free Spins/Choose Your Own Volatility -Number of free spins awarded depends on the reel configuration player selects to play during bonus. 15 Free Spins – 3×5 reels 243 ways to win. 10 Free Spins – 4×5 reels 1024 ways to win. 5 Free Spins – 5×5 reels 3125 ways to win. Jackpot Bonus – This feature is randomly triggered any time a wild symbol lands on the reels during the base game. The pick bonus allows you to pick until you get three matching symbols. Pays are based on the three matching symbols you select. Malfunction voids all pays and play.
