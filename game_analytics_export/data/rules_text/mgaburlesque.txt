RF Burlesque MAIN GAME In this game, each prize could either be risked, by pressing the play button, or to be collected, by pressing the collect button. In case the prize is risked, it will either be given the amount of a one level higher prize or the equivalent amount of bonus. Once the line of the three bells is reached, the game becomes an up down, where the possible prizes go up or down in the prize plan." UPPER GAME In order to play this game it will be required to have enough bonus and credits. Whenever a prize is obtained, unless it is a gold ingot prize, the player will be offered to either risk a portion of the prize or to collect it, until there are no more portions left. Choosing to risk a portion may allow the player to either obtain the double of that portion or the equivalent value of that portion in bonus." NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. AUTO When nudges are activated, it allows you to rotate the reels automatically, always resulting in the best option for the player. If the button is permanently lit, this means that the auto option is activated. Otherwise, if the button is flashing, this indicates that the auto option is not activated. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. AUTO PLAY Activate the automatic game mode. The games will be launched automatically if the play button is kept pressed." AUTO RISK Activate the risk function in main and upper game. The maximum prize that can be obtained in a minigame is the maximum prize of the bet in which the game has been made. A MINI-GAMES ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES. BONUS GAME BOWLERS 3 window figures in the lower game give access to the 'bowlers' game. Choose a roller or a bowler to get prizes. UP YOUR AWARD Sometimes after a winning spin the player gets this opportunity. If this happens the player will have a new spin and will always get a higher prize. You can spin several times. SOMETIMES AFTER AN AWARD OF THE HEEL, CORSET OR LIP FIGURES, ACCESS TO A MINI-GAME OF 4 OPTIONS WILL BE GRANTED RANDOMLY. BONUS GAME DISCOVER THE TREASURE Select a reel to get your prize. BONUS GAME THREE OPTION Choose screens to discover prizes. Cash or play to find the best prize. BONUS GAME LIGHT BULBS Select bulbs until you get the word stop. After each level up the prizes plan will get greater. BONUS GAME CAROUSEL OF LUCK Get roses to accumulate prizes. The White rose will award one random prize.
