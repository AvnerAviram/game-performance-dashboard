Sushi Fruits Game Theme and Graphics Sushi Fruits™ is a 25 Win Line, 5 Reel slot with 3 different bonus features available. Main Game Between the Fullscreen Feature, Sushi Fruits™ Chef Feature and Free Games, this slot has alot to offer. Your aim in Sushi Fruits™ is to land 5 identical symbols on one of the 25 win lines that run from left to right across the screen. Sushi Fruits Chef Feature Keep an eye out for Chef Symbols. When a Chef symbol lands on Reel 3, the Chef Feature will be activated. The Chef will select one of four possible fruits and start slicing them with his blade to reveal prizes! Each time a fruit is sliced a new fruit takes its place, allowing a chance for more prizes to be won. Mini, Minor and Major prizes can also be won from the Fruit Prizes. Should the Chef manage to slice 12 total fruits, the Grand Prize will be awarded. The Feature will end when the Grand Prize is awarded or when the Chef runs out of fruit to slice. Free Games Feature If 3 or more scattered Star Symbols land on the reels at the same time, the Free Games Feature will trigger, awarding the player 10 Free Games. During the Free Games, the Chef Symbol has an increased chance of appearing, potentially allowing for multiple Chef Features during the Free Games. Free Games can also be re-triggered for additional Free Games. Fullscreen Feature If your lucky enough to land a Fullscreen of 15 Fruits (Plums, Oranges, Lemons or Cherrys), you will trigger the Fullscreen Feature. In addition to line wins, all the prize values on the winning fruits will be multiplied by 5 and then awarded to the player, resulting in massive wins. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except scatters. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 5/21/2025
