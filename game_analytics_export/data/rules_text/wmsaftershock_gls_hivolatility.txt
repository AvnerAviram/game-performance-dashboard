After Shock Base Game DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY appears on reels 1 and 3 only when Big Bet spins are not enabled. TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY appears on reel 2 only. DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on a payline pays 2x the winning combination. TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on a payline pays 3x the winning combination. Two DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on a payline pays 4x the winning combination. DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY and TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on a payline pays 6x the winning combination, except in pays already shown. DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY is wild for all symbols except WILD and TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY. TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY is wild for all symbols, except WILD and DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY. WILD is wild for all symbols, except DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY and TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Symbols in a winning combination on a payline can appear in any order, unless otherwise stated. AfterShock Frenzy Feature The AfterShock Frenzy Feature may be triggered during any wagered spin. During the AfterShock Frenzy Feature, every spin is guaranteed to be a winning combination with up to 7 spins awarded. Shocking Wilds During the AfterShock Frenzy Feature, if any DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY or TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY lands on or within one position of the center payline, the Shocking Wilds is triggered. The triggering DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY and/or TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY will remain and nudge to on or within one position of the center payline for the remainder of the AfterShock Frenzy Feature spins. An alternate set of outcomes are used during the AfterShock Frenzy Feature. Winning combinations are the same as base game. BIG BET Press BIG BET to begin to make a Big Bet wager. 5 Big Bet spins, played back-to-back, can be purchased for a selected amount. During Big Bet spins, DOUBLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on reels 1 and 3 are replaced with TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY. Two TRIPLE AFTERSHOCK FRENZY on a payline pays 9x the winning combination. The AfterShock Frenzy Feature can trigger during Big Bet spins. It is possible to trigger more than one AfterShock Frenzy Feature per set of Big Bet spins. An alternate set of outcomes are used during Big Bet spins. Winning combinations are the same as non-Big Bet spins. One complete game consists of 5 Big Bet spins. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 1/31/2017
