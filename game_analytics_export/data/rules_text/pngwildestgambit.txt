Wildest Gambit Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Payline wins occur on the number of selected paylines, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple paylines in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to payline wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet and same number of selected lines as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Symbol wins pay for any winning payline combination without any gaps. Only the highest win is paid per active win line. Scatter symbol wins (where applicable) pay for any winning combination, including any gaps. Game Rules Wildest Gambit is a 5-reel, 3-row game with 10 PAYLINES. The Player begins the game with access to three locations: Chile, Peru, and India, choosing locations in a non-linear fashion. Trigger FREE SPINS and charge the Phylactery SCATTER to unlock the mystical Cenotaph of Sekhmet in Egypt where incredible riches await. The final destination, Egypt, is unlocked by triggering FREE SPINS in each of the preceding locations, culminating in a grand finale where all character features combine to unlock immense treasures. Rich, Cat, and Gerard each have their unique character specific WILD FEATURE. WILDS appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 only! WILDS substitute for all SYMBOLS except SCATTER and have unique features with a chance to UPGRADE! MULTIPLIER WILD (Rich): Depending on the number of WILDS, may bring 2x, 4x, or 8x MULTIPLIERS when part of a win. EXPANDING WILD (Cat): When this WILD lands, it expands vertically to fill the entire reel, transforming all positions on that reel into WILDS. STICKY WILD (Gerard): When this WILD lands, it will become sticky and grant at least one RE-SPIN. Should additional STICKY WILDS land during a RE-SPIN, they too become sticky, and additional RE-SPIN awarded. Each location has a Lead WILD FEATURE with a chance to be UPGRADED by the Support WILD FEATURE. During FREE SPINS both Lead and Support WILD FEATURES are always active. Chile – The Chilean Adventure: In this location Rich leads with the MULTIPLIER WILD feature. Cat upgrades with a chance to expand these wilds. Depending on the number of wilds, may bring 2x, 4x, or 8x multipliers when part of a win. If Cat’s feature upgrade is activated, multipliers are counted after the expansion! Peru – The Peruvian Expedition: In this location Cat leads with the EXPANDING WILD feature. Gerard UPGRADES with a chance to make these WILDS STICKY with a RE-SPIN. When an EXPANDING WILD symbol lands on any of the reels, it activates the feature, causing the WILD to expand and cover an entire reel. If Gerard’s feature UPGRADE is activated, the expanded symbol becomes STICKY and grants at least one RE-SPIN. Should additional WILDS land during a RE-SPIN, they too expand and become STICKY with additional RE-SPINS awarded. India – The Indian Quest: In this location Gerard leads with the STICKY WILD feature. Rich upgrades with a chance to apply MULTIPLIER onto these. When a STICKY WILD symbol lands on any of the reels, it activates the feature, causing the WILD to become STICKY and trigger a RE-SPIN. If Rich’s feature upgrade is activated the MULTIPLIER feature is applied to the WILDS for the duration of the RE-SPIN. Depending on the number of WILDS, it may bring 2x, 4x, or 8x MULTIPLIERS when part of a win. Should additional WILDS land during a RE-SPIN, they too expand and become STICKY with additional RE-SPINS awarded. Egypt – The Egyptian Convergence: In this location Rich leads with the MULTIPLIER WILD feature. Cat UPGRADES with a chance to EXPAND these wilds. Gerard UPGRADES with a chance to make these WILDS STICKY with a RE-SPIN. Depending on the number of WILDS may bring 2x, 4x, or 8x MULTIPLIERS when part of a win. If Cat’s feature UPGRADE is activated, MULTIPLIERS are counted after the expansion! If Gerard’s feature UPGRADE is activated the expanded symbol becomes STICKY and grants at least one RE-SPIN. Should additional WILDS land during a RE-SPIN, they too expand and become STICKY with additional RE-SPINS awarded. The Phylactery is a SCATTER and can appear on any reel. Landing 3 or more SCATTER triggers FREE SPINS, offering 8 FREE SPINS with increasing SCATTER payouts. The SCATTER pays anywhere on the reels. SCATTER wins are multiplied by the total bet staked and are added to payline wins. It is possible to win more FREE SPINS by re-triggering the feature with the same 3 or more SCATTERS. Successfully triggering FREE SPINS in Chile, Peru, and India charges the SCATTER and unlocks the final destination: Egypt. Before FREE SPINS begins, a regular paying symbol (not including SCATTER) is randomly chosen to function as a special expanding symbol during FREE SPINS. After prizes for regular winning combinations have been awarded, the special symbol may expand to cover all three positions on its reel. The special symbol will expand only when additional prizes can be awarded along active PAYLINES for winning combinations. Expanded SYMBOLS can appear anywhere on the reels with gaps to create a winning combination. During FREE SPINS both Lead and Support WILD FEATURES are always active. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 20000 times total bet. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated. The bet may only be decreased and will not return to its original amount following any wins that would allow the initial bet size to be resumed. Last modification date: 4/29/2025
