Double Patriot Game Rules 2X WILD DOUBLE PATRIOT The Double Patriot symbol is wild and substitutes for Seven and Bar symbols that also land on the payline. One Double Patriot symbol in a winning combination pays 2x prize value. Two Double Patriot symbols in a winning combination pays 4x prize value. Three Double Patriot symbols on the payline does not multiply prize value. Only highest winner paid per line.
