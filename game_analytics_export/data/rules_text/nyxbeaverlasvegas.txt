Beaver Las Vegas BASE GAME RULES The game has 20 paylines which are active at all times. Wins must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far-left reel. The paytable reflects the current bet configuration. Only the highest winner paid per winning combination. Wins on different paylines are added. Only positions containing the winning symbol are used in determining the win for that symbol. WILDS The WILD Symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except Record Symbol and Bonus Symbol. BEAVER LAS VEGAS BONUS When 4 or more RECORD symbols appear anywhere on the reels the BEAVER LAS VEGAS bonus is triggered. Six spins are awarded. All RECORD symbols appear as gold level and persist for the duration of the bonus. Each RECORD symbol will display a corresponding prize value. Prizes range from 1x to 100x. Additional RECORD symbols appearing in the same position on the reel will double the value of the RECORD. At random, a BEAVER may appear over the reels and award between 1 and 15 extra spins. The bonus continues until there are no spins remaining, or all 15 reel positions contain a DIAMOND RECORD symbol. The values shown on each RECORD symbol will then be awarded. FREE SPINS BONUS The FREE SPINS bonus is triggered when 3 BONUS symbols appear in any position on reels 1, 3 and 5. Six FREE SPINS are awarded. If 1, 2 or 3 RECORD symbols appear in any position on the reels, the values shown on those symbols will be awarded. The BEAVER LAS VEGAS bonus can be triggered during FREE SPINS. It is not possible to retrigger the FREE SPINS bonus. It is not possible to trigger the FREE SPINS bonus at the same time. BONU$PLAY™ You can choose to play the BEAVER LAS VEGAS BONUS by pressing the BONU$PLAY™ button. This function may not be available due to the jurisdiction in which you are located. The cost of the BONU$PLAY™ feature is displayed on screen. Press CONFIRM to begin the game. The reels will spin to a position containing between 4 and 10 RECORD symbols, and the bonus will begin. The expected return for BONU$PLAY™ feature is 92.28%. This reflects the theoretical return across many plays. TURBO MODE The Turbo Mode button is displayed on the bottom right of the reels with an 'ON' and 'OFF' option. Turn ON Turbo Mode button to speed up each spin, it can be turned OFF at any point during the game. When Free Spins are awarded Turbo Mode will be unavailable. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins. Turbo Mode may not be available in your jurisdiction. LEGAL NOTICE In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent.
