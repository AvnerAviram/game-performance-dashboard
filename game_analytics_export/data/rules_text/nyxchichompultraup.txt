Chili Chomp Ultra Up Chili Chomp UltraUp is a Bet 40, 40 Lines 3x5 Reel slot game with Free Games and UltraUp Respins feature. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96.03% with any bet. Game Rules Wild Girl {WILD GIRL} substitutes for all symbols, except {SCATTER SKULL}, {SCATTER SKULL WILD} & {SCATTER BONUS}. Appears during Base Game and Free Games only. Scatter Skull 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12 or 13 {SCATTER SKULL} triggers 3 UltraUp Respins. Appears during Base Game. Appears during Free Games when player has opted for 6 Free Games with Scatter Skull. Scatter Skull Wild Random chance on any Base Game spin for all {SCATTER SKULL} to turn wild. Substitutes for all symbols except {SCATTER SKULL} and {SCATTER BONUS}. Does not act as Scatter when substituting for another symbol. 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14 or 15 {SCATTER SKULL WILD} triggers 3 UltraUp Respins. Appears during Base Game. Appears during Free Games when player has opted for 12 Free Games with Scatter Skull Wild. Scatter Bonus 3 {SCATTER BONUS} triggers Free Games. Appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Appears during base game only. Free Game 3 {SCATTER BONUS} triggers Free Games. At the start of Free Games players are given the choice of 6 Free Games with more Scatter Skull on the reels or 12 Free Games with all Scatter Skull as Scatter Skull Wild. At the conclusion of UltraUp Respins, the game returns to any Free Games remaining. Free Games cannot be retriggered. 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14 or 15 {SCATTER SKULL} triggers 3 UltraUp Respins during Free Games. 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13 or 14 {SCATTER SKULL WILD} triggers 3 UltraUp Respins during Free Games. UltraUp Respins Appears during UltraUp Respins only. At the start of UltraUp {SCATTER SKULL} & {SCATTER SKULL WILD} appear as {Level 1 CHILI} and are held. Additional {Level 1 CHILI} are held and resets Respins to 3 Land {UltraUp BORDER} on {Level 1 CHILI} or {Level 2 CHILI} to increase its level. Level 1 {CHILI} may contain: 20, 40, 80, 120 or 160 credit prizes. Level 2 {CHILI} may contain: 200, 240, 280, 320 credit prizes or Mini. Level 3 {CHILI} may contain: 800, 1200 credits prizes or Mini, Minor, Major or Grand. When no Respins remain, prizes are revealed and then awarded. Only one prize revealed per symbol held. 10 collected {Level 1 CHILI} unlocks 5 additional positions starting from the next Respin. 15 collected {Level 1 CHILI} unlocks 5 additional positions starting from the next Respin. Maximum of 25 positions. {UltraUp BORDER} and {Level 1 CHILI} cannot land on the same position and during the same Respin. {UltraUp BORDER} cannot land on a level 3 {CHILI}. Mini, Minor, Major or Grand prizes are fixed, are not progressive, and are not multiplied by the bet multiplier. During the reveal, Mini, Minor and Major Prizes may be won more than once. During the reveal, Grand Prize may be won only once. Up to 12 level 2 {CHILI} may be held at any one time. Up to 6 Level 3 {CHILI} may be held at any one time. Rules Play bet 40 for 40 lines. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER BONUS which pays any. All wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. All wins on selected paylines only except SCATTER BONUS. Wins on different paylines are added. Scatter wins are added to payline wins. Highest win only on each selected payline and SCATTER win. Payouts are made according to the Paytable. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. How to Play Step 1: Choose your BET Step 2: SPIN Paytable amounts are fixed and prizes are multiplied by the Bet Multiplier. The game displays all your details in the following display fields. Balance: Displays your total balance. Win: Displays the amount won in the spin. Total Bet: Displays the total wager staked. Total Win: Displays the accumulated wins during Free Games and/or Respins. Free Game: Displays the number of Free Games played together with the total number of Free Games awarded. Respins: Displays the number of respins left to play. Collected: Displays the total number of symbols held.
