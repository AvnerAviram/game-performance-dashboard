Power Potz UltraNudge Welcome to POWER POTZ, with ULTRANUDGE, the PRIZE CAROUSEL and LOTS O’ POTZ FREE SPINS! ULTRANUDGE™ Any win that includes the WILD will trigger the ULTRANUDGE™. The win will pay, and all symbols will nudge down by one position. The ULTRANUDGE win streak will continue until there are no more wins. During LOTS O' POTZ FREE SPINS any win will start ULTRANUDGE! POWER POTZ Above the reels is a Prize Carousel that will move one position right to left on every spin. The carousel can contain a FREE SPIN tile and CASH tiles with values of x2 to x1000 total bet. Landing 1 or more POWER POT on the reels will trigger the POWER POT Blast and win the prize above. POWER POT wins will pay on every spin or nudge on which the pot is still on screen. POWER POTZ can land on reels 1, 3 and 5 in the base game and on all reels in Free Spins LOTS O' POTZ FREE SPINS Triggering a POWER POT below a Prize Carousel item displaying FREE SPINS will award 10 free spins. Multiple POWER POT wins can trigger additional free spins either on the initial reel spin or during ULTRANUDGE streaks, during base game spins or nudges you will receive 10 free spins for every free spin tile hit by a POWER POT! During Free Spins: All wins start ULTRANUDGE, there are more POWER POTZ on the reels, EXTRA SPIN tiles are available that if hit will award +1 spins, and the special WIN ALL upgrade can be applied to new tiles. WIN ALL The WIN ALL tile is a special tile identified by a gold frame and arrows. It is an upgrade that can be applied to any tile due to appear in Free Spins. Hitting this with a POWER POT Blast will award all prizes on the carousel. BUY BONUS Players can pay x100 to buy into Free Spins with their choice of bet size. Players will be awarded 10 Free Spins. HOW TO CALCULATE PAYLINE WINS To calculate your total payline win, calculate all payline wins and add them together. To calculate a single payline win, count the number of identical symbols lined up in sequence on the line starting from the left most reel. If three or more identical symbols are lined up, find the win value in the dynamic Pay Table. There you can find the value for all symbols for 3 in a row, 4 in a row and 5 in a row. Only the highest winning combination per line is paid out. If the winning combination starts with three wilds, there can be many ways to interpret the win. In this scenario, only the highest win combination is paid out. The above doesn't include Free Spin symbols which do not need to be on a payline to award a prize. After the Free Spins round is completed, all wins are added to any win from the game that activated the Free Spins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RETURN TO PLAYER The RTP cannot be affected by player strategy. The overall theoretical RTP is 94.07%. The theoretical RTP with BUY BONUS on is 94.05%. MAX WIN Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed 250,000.00. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration.
