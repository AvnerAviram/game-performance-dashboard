3 Jockers+ Game Rules Win lines pay from left to right only. The game has 10 fixed pay lines. Payouts are made according to the paytable. Only the highest win per pay line is paid. "WILD IN THE BOX" At each Spin, the "Wild in the Box" Sign may appear. If this Sign appears, all Jokers on screen will be changed to Joker-Wilds. Each Joker-Wild expands completely to the left and substitutes all symbols except different coloured Joker-Wilds. MISCELLANEOUS: The theoretical minimum payback percentage (RTP) is 93.80%. The base game average RTP was determined by Monte Carlo simulation of 500 million regular bet spins by taking the total amount won and dividing it by the the total amount bet. The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of spins by numerous players over an extended period of time and cannot be affected by the player's strategy. The game maximum possible win amount is 1000x bet. Disclaimer: If an interrupted game is not finished within 90 days, your game will be terminated. If your game gets terminated you will lose every active bet. Only the highest winning combination per active win line pays. Malfunction of the virtual machine voids all plays and payment during the period of malfunction. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games are played at the bet and lines of game which triggered the game. Wins on different paylines are added. Payouts are multiplied by current active bet. Last modification date: 5/14/2025
