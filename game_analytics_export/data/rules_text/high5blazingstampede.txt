Blazing Stampede RTP: 94.0% VOLATILITIES: BASE GAME: 21.8 - EXTREME POWER BET 1: 19.8 – EXTREME POWER BET 2: 20.9 - EXTREME POWER BET COMBO: 23.8 - EXTREME GAME RULES This game is played at 1875 ways. All 6 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to ways wins. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. BASE GAME During the base game, 3 or more Bonus symbols appearing in any position on the reels awards bonus games and 2x the total bet. 3 bonus symbols award 7 bonus games. 4 bonus symbols award 10 bonus games. 5 bonus symbols award 15 bonus games. The Prize Path symbol can appear on reels 1-5 on non Prize Path award positions. At the beginning of every base spin, a random number of Prize Path symbols may be added to the non Prize Path positions on reels 1-5. The Wild symbol may only appear naturally on non Prize Path award positions on the initial spin on reels 2, 3 and 4. At the beginning of every base spin, a random number of Wild symbols may be added to the non Prize Path positions on reels 2-5. Starting from the leftmost reel, orthogonally connected Prize Path symbols to a Prize Path award position will trigger the award. Multiple Prize Path awards can be awarded. Respins – 5 repsins are awarded and all non Prize Path symbols on reels 1-5 respin. This continues until all respins are completed. Scatter – The amount displayed is awarded and can appear with the following values: 40, 60, 80, 120, 160, 200, 240, 320, 400, 600, 800, 1000, 1200, 1600, 2000, 2400, 3200, 4000, 6000, 8000 or 16000 in the base game. Add Wilds – A random number of Wilds are distributed on non Prize Path positions on reels 2-5. Bonus Games – Awards the number of Bonus Games displayed and awards 2x the total amount bet. Awards 7, 10, 15, 20 or 25 Bonus Games. Jackpots – Awards a Jackpot The Minor Jackpot awards 25x the total amount bet. The Major Jackpot awards 200x the total amount bet. The Grand Jackpot awards 4000x the total amount bet. Respins can only appear on reel 3 Prize Path award positions. The Major and Grand Jackpots can only appear on reel 6 Prize Path award positions. Grand Jackpots can only appear during the base game. At the end of every base game spin, the Grand Jackpot may be randomly awarded. The Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all non Prize Path award symbols except Bonus Games and Prize Path symbols. Add Wilds – A random number of Wilds are distributed on non Prize Path positions on reels 2-5. Bonus Games – Awards the number of Bonus Games displayed and awards 2x the total amount bet.
