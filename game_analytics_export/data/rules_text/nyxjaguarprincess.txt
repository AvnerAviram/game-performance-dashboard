Jaguar Princess RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: 6.0 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Choose the amount you want to bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter symbols award in any position on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol in the base game The reels contain stacks of mystery symbols that transform into one of the following symbols at the start of each spin: Wild, Bonus symbol in the base game, High Pay, Mid Pay or Low Pay symbol. All stacked mystery symbol positions on a reel transform into the same symbol. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 12 or more Free Games Bonus Symbol appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 starts the Free Games Bonus. 12 Bonus symbols awards 12 free games. 13 Bonus symbols awards 13 free games. 14 Bonus symbols awards 14 free games. 15 Bonus symbols awards 15 free games. 16 Bonus symbols awards 16 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet per line as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. Last modification date: 9/7/2022
