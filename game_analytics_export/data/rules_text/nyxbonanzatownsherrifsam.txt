Bonanza Town Sherrif Sam GAME DESCRIPTION “BONANZA TOWN SHERIFF SAM™, round up outlaws for a bounty of awards in a thrilling Wild West adventure. Three fun-filled features provide unique styles of gameplay. In the free game feature, one reel containing wild and credit prizes exclusively is used in each free game. In another free spin feature, multiple reels containing credit, wanted and bonanza prizes exclusively are lassoed with all captured prizes being won. In the wheel feature, a credit prize is replaced with wanted prize before each wheel spin. A wanted or bonanza prize is increased with each appearing wild. HOW TO PLAY Select your bet. GAMBLE FEATURE To enter the gamble game after a win, Press the GAMBLE button Select a Suit, Red/Black or Collect. Select RED/BLACK or a SUIT to gamble the total win. Win is quadrupled if suit selection is correct. Win is doubled if red/black selection is correct. Win may be gambled up to 5 times. Gamble will not be offered for main game wins part of feature trigger and feature wins. Gamble will not be offered during Autoplay. GAME RULES The game requires a minimum of 65 credits multiplied by bet per line to play. All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel except scatters. Wins on different lines are added. All wins multiplied by bet per line, except . Scatter wins are added to line wins. Only Highest win paid on each line. Wins on different lines are added. wins are multiplied by 30 and bet per line. Free Games and Bonus wins are added to line wins Malfunction voids all pays and plays. No skill is needed in any part of the game. LOBBY BAR: Home: - Player can return to lobby by selecting this button. Sound: - Choose to enable/disable the game sounds.
