Wins If the meter does not fill up completely during the game round, the next game round can be initiated before the meter has emptied. A winning bet occurs when the shred meter fills completely . 3 to 18 credit prizes are awarded on a winning bet and are displayed by the Yeti snowboarding down the hill collecting coins . The Yeti must hit and collect to win the associated prize. Any missed coins not hit by Yeti are not awarded. All prizes accumulate into the prize meter . The Multiplier Jump feature is triggered when the Yeti snowboards over the ramp . Any obstacle hit by the Yeti will end play, and the total win in the prize meter will be awarded. Crossing the finish line will also end play, and the total win in the prize meter will be awarded. Multiplier Jump Feature During the Multiplier Jump feature, the Yeti will fly in the air and can collect up to ten 2x multipliers, adding each one to the final multiplier.
