Betti The Yetti RTP: 95.0% Volatility: 12.3 – HIGH Game Rules Line wins must appear on played lines. Line win symbols must appear on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line. Line wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free GAMES Different reels are used during the Stepping Out Bonus. Free games are automatically played on the same lines at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Stepping Out Bonus. The Stepping Out Bonus features a bonus multiplier that awards 1 to 1000 times the total bet at the end of the bonus. Each Advance symbol appearing anywhere on the reels increases the bonus multiplier, up to 16 times. Additional free games cannot be awarded during the Stepping Out Bonus. The Stepping Out Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. Last modification date: 6/2/2022
