Hot Slot 777 Rubies The game has 5 reels, 3 rows and 20 fixed bet lines. Wins are awarded for drawing 2, 3, 4 or 5 symbols of a kind on an active bet line, from leftmost to right on consecutive reels. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Landing 2, 3, 4 or 5 Scatters awards MINI, MINOR, MAJOR, or GRAND Jackpot respectively. The maximum win amount is 500x bet. Only the highest win is paid for combinations with Scatter. Wins from different lines are added together. SPECIAL FEATURES MAGIC RESPIN When 1, 2, 3 or 4 Scatter symbols are drawn on the reels, you can bet on one of the remaining reels for a chance to draw one more Scatter symbol and win a Jackpot. When the MAGIC RESPIN feature is activated, all reels are locked except the selected one. Each Respin costs 10x, 25x, 50x or 150x your bet, depending on the number of Scatters you have previously drawn. You can use the feature multiple times. When using the MAGIC RESPIN feature, a Jackpot win is paid only if a new Scatter appears on the selected reel. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
