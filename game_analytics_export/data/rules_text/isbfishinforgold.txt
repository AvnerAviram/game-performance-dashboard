Wild The Wild symbol only appears during Free Spins and replaces any other symbols, except the Gone Fishin' symbol, to form winning combinations. Note: If no specific payouts for Wild are displayed in the pay table, a combination formed exclusively with Wild will pay just like the highest paying symbol, if that combination is the highest winning combination on a line. Wilds can have a win multiplier attached: 2x, 3x or 5x. The multipliers are multiplicative of each other. Note : If more than one multiplier Wild of the same kind appears on the same win line, they will not be multiplicative of each other (i.e x5 Wild and x5 Wild will still only multiply the win by x5). 3.2. Fishin' For Gold Bonus Landing 3 Golden Fish on Reels 2,3 and 4 will trigger the Fishin' For Gold Bonus. During the Fishin' For Gold Bonus round you have to choose your fisherman and start the hunt for gold. Catching Golden Fish symbols will award you Free Spins , and the other symbols caught in the Bonus Round will become Wilds during Free Spins . 3.3. Free Spins During Free Spins any symbols caught in the Bonus Round are Wild , and will appear as golden symbols. Wilds can have a win multiplier attached: 2x, 3x or 5x. The multipliers are multiplicative of each other. Note : If more than one multiplier Wild of the same kind appears on the same win line, they will not be multiplicative of each other (i.e x5 Wild and x5 Wild will still only multiply the win by x5). 3.4. Extra Spins For every 3 Gone Fishin' symbols that land during the Free Spins you will be awarded 2 extra Free Spins . 3.5. Bonus Chance Landing exactly 2 Bonus symbols will boost the Bonus Chance Meter . You can spin the wheel at any time for a chance to trigger the Bonus . The more Bonus symbols collected the greater the chance to trigger the Bonus . Note : Each spin of the wheel will cost 1x current bet amount. Wheel will reset to 0 after each spin. The Bonus Chance progress is saved per coin value. Note : Every time exactly 2 Scatters land, 1% of the Bonus Chance wheel will be filled. Spin the Bonus Chance wheel at any time for a chance to trigger the bonus. To completely fill the wheel, for a guaranteed Bonus trigger, you will require 100 collections. When the Bonus Chance wheel is full, the optimal strategy is to use it immediately. Scatters/Bonus symbols are no longer collected when the wheel is full and the game RTP will be lower. 4.
