Rolpng Reels™ Feature This feature can trigger in base game and Free Spins. This feature triggers after any paypne win. Winning symbols are removed from the reels and the symbols above move into the empty spaces. l This feature also triggers after landing a TNT Wild. When a TNT Wild or X-Wild lands, they are removed from the reel area. All symbols, except scatters and regular Wilds, adjacent to the TNT Wild or X-Wild are also removed. The feature ends when no more winning combinations nor TNT Wilds or X-Wilds are present on the reels. Scatter Pay In the base game, winning scatter combinations are awarded when 3, 4, or 5 Scatters appear anywhere on the reels. The scatter payout is awarded as a multiple of the total bet according to the PAYTABLE. Scatter payouts are added to pne wins. Big Boom Riches Free Spins In the base game, landing 3 or more scatter symbols awards 9 Free Spins. Scatters pay before Free Spins begins. In Free Spins, scatters do not pay when they land, they award extra Free Spins instead. One extra spin is awarded for every Free Spins scatter symbol that lands in Free Spins. Free Spins begin with a multipper of X1. l For every X-Wild that lands in Free Spins, the multipper increases by 1. Winnings are automatically collected during Free Spins.Every individual win in Free Spins is multipped by the current multipper. l The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Buy Free Spins In the Buy Free Spins feature, guaranteed Free Spins can be purchased at a multiple of the current bet. 9 Free Spins can be purchased for 50X the current bet. When choosing the Buy Free Spins feature, 3 scatter symbols are placed randomly on the reel area. Buy Free Spins feature can only be used at any time a bet change can take place.The Buy Free Spins feature may not be available in the game you are playing.
