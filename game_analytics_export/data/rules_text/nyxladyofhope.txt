Lady of Hope The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 94.9%. Game Rules Malfunction voids all pays and play This game is played at 243 ways and all 5 reels are always in play. Select Bet Multiplier Click the Spin button to spin the reels Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits stated Highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. All 5 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel, or ending with the rightmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Free Games Bonus symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbols are scatter symbols. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wilds symbols substitute for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol. Wild symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from below. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels. Free Games bonus 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards the Free Games Bonus with 10 free games. Free Games are played on a separate screen with different reels Free games are played at the same bet multiplier as the triggering game. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing anywhere on reels 2, 3, and 4 during the Free Games Bonus awards 5 additional free games. Free Games Bonus ends when 0 Free Games remain or when 60 Free Games have been played. Last modification date: 12/29/2021
