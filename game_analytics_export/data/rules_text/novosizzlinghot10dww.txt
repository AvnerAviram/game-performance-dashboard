Enjoy the Wild transfers and look out for the Scatter symbols! Main Game and Win Ways ™ Sizzling Hot™ deluxe 10: Win Ways™ is played on 10 reels across 2 reel sets. All symbols from winning combination are removed before new symbols fall into place. Any new winning combinations are paid. This continues until there are no more winning combinations in view. Wild Transfer Wilds can land in single, double, or triple stacked. Get any Wild symbols on reel set 1 to transfer over to the corresponding reel on reel set 2. Rules All prizes are for combinations left to right on adjacent symbols, except Scatters. Awards are multiplied by stake. Scatter symbols may only appear on reels 1, 3 and 5 of both reel sets. Wild symbols may appear on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 of both reel sets. Wild symbol substitutes all symbols except Scatter. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
