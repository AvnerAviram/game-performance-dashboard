Siren's Kingdom Scratch The return to player for this game is 95.00%. Click on the play button to start the game, clicking Play button again to reveal all remaining symbols. In addition a timer appears on the start button that will count down from 10 to 0 which will then automatically reveal all symbols. You win by matching three of the same symbols on active line. For information on prizes please view the paytable on the main game screen. Select up to 5 chances to win when you adjust your stake. On the main game screen you may see a selection of different icons: A 'MENU' icon which once opened shows you the in-game Help, Settings and Bet History pages. A 'HOME' icon which returns you to the home page. A 'SOUND' icon to mute and unmute in-game sound. A 'BET' icon which allows you to adjust your stake.
