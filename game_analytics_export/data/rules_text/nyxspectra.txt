Spectra Untitled Document Note: The Spectra video slot is referred to as Spectra, The Game or Game. Spectra is a video slot with 5 reels and 30 pay lines. The game features stacked and nudging wild symbols and respins. The theoretical return to player is 96.4%. A game round is started with the action button and plays the game with the selected bet level. No bets can be altered during a game round. Auto play plays the game automatically for the number of game rounds selected in the auto play page. The appearance of 1 or more new wild stacks on or directly adjacent to a pay line triggers a respin. During each respin the wild stacks nudge 1 symbol position vertically towards the center of the reels. All game payout and win combinations are paid out according to the pay table. Only the highest win per pay line is paid out, if in sequence from leftmost to right and in combinations according to the pay table. All pay line wins during a game round are added and presented at the end of the game round. All wins are presented and paid out in the selected currency. A wild symbol substitutes for any other symbol. In the event of game malfunction all affected bets and pays are rendered void. Last modification date: 3/28/2017
