Jaguar Princess Ways RTP: 96.0% VOLATILITY: MEDIUM – 13 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play Select Bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels This game is played at 243 ways. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Line wins are multiplied by the bet per line. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Free Games Bonus. 1 Wild symbol substitutes for 2 High Pay 1 symbols. 1 Wild substitutes for 1 High Pay 2, High Pay 3, High Pay 4, Low Pay 1, Low Pay 2, Low Pay 3, or Low Pay 4 symbol. Wild symbol appears only on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. FREE GAMES BONUS During the base game, 3 or more Free Games Bonus Symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 10 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol, and only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Wild symbol is wild and substitutes for all symbols except Free Games Bonus symbol. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 2 Free Games High Pay 1 symbols. 1 Free Games Wild symbol substitutes for 1 Free Games High Pay 2, Free Games High Pay 3, Free Games High Pay 4, Free Games Low Pay 1, Free Games Low Pay 2, Free Games Low Pay 3, or Free Games Low Pay 4 symbol. Free Games Wild symbol only appears on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. The Free Games Bonus uses different reels. Free games automatically played on the same ways at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. 3 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on reels 2, 3, and 4 awards 5 additional free games. Free Games Bonus symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain or when 200 free games have been played.
