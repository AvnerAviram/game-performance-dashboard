Progressive Jackpots The 5 Wheel Bonus must be accessed in order to have a chance of winning a Progressive Jackpot. The 5 Progressive Jackpots are: Green, Blue, Red, Purple, and Gold. If the wheel initiated by stops on the Progressive, the value of the Green Progressive Jackpot is won. If the wheel initiated by stops on the Progressive, the value of the Blue Progressive Jackpot is won. If the wheel initiated by stops on the Progressive, the value of the Red Progressive Jackpot is won. If the wheel initiated by stops on the Progressive, the value of the Purple Progressive Jackpot is won. If the wheel initiated by stops on the Progressive, the value of the Gold Progressive Jackpot is won. It is not technically possible to have two simultaneous wins of the same Progressive Jackpot. There are separate Progressive Jackpots per bet multiplier. Contributions made to Progressive Jackpots at a certain bet multiplier are added to Progressive Jackpots that can only be achieved by making a bet at that same bet multiplier. The following contributions are made to Progressive Jackpots on each base game spin: .28% of the bet is contributed to the Green Progressive Jackpot. .23% of the bet is contributed to the Blue Progressive Jackpot. .18% of the bet is contributed to the Red Progressive Jackpot. .16% of the bet is contributed to the Purple Progressive Jackpot. .13% of the bet is contributed to the Gold Progressive Jackpot. At a 1x bet multiplier, the start-up value for each Progressive Jackpot is: Green: $6.00 Blue: $12.00 Red: $30.00 Purple: $90.00 Gold: $300.00 The start-up values for the Progressive Jackpots at bet multipliers other than 1x are calculated by multiplying the bet multiplier by the start-up value at a 1x bet multiplier.
