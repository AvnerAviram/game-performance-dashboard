Bjorn the Frost Lord If a Bjorn Wild lands partially in view, it will move to be fully in view. Substitutes for all paying symbols Spreading Wilds When a Bjorn Wild lands on a winning line with of character symbols it will attempt to turn them into wilds. If the characters are successfully turned to wilds the infection will continue along that winline, when it fails all turned characters and Bjorn Wilds will stick in place while the reels respin. Any new characters that land on winning lines with Bjorn Wilds after the respin will also attempt to turn. Free Spins Get 3, 4 or 5 scatter symbols to trigger 10, 15 or 20 Free Spins. Every Bjorn Wild that lands and every character that turns will add 1 to the current character's meter. Fill a character's meter and they will turn wild for the duration of Free Spins and award +3 Free Spins. Paytable Win Lines Wins are achieved for any 3, 4 or 5 of a kind along a winline. This is a 20 win line game, paying from left to right only. Game Information The overall payout percentage of the game is 96.29%. This game is completely random. Malfunction voids all pays.
