Coin Fury Main Game The main game is played on 3 reels. The 5 win lines offer you 5 chances to win. If this is not enough, the Wild symbol will help to increase your chances and will substitute for almost any symbol to complete winning combinations! Game Features Hold & Spin Feature When each reel contains at least 1 Coin symbol the Hold & Spin Feature will be triggered, The values, or bonuses on the Coin symbols which will be spun in on the leftmost and the rightmost reels will be added to the value of every Coin symbol on the middle reel. The more coins on the middle reels the faster your winnings will increase. At the end of the feature you will receive all collected prizes and bonuses. Spotting stardust on the coins? This will give an extra boost to your winnings! Pile of Coins Feature Each coin landing on the reels can trigger the Pile of Coins Feature. The pile of coins shown on top of the reels will collapse and the Hold & Spin Feature will start.
