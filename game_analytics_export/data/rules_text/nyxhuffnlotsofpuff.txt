WHEEL FEATURE WHEEL symbol appears only on reels 2 and 4. WHEEL symbol landing in the Base Game for the active bet can do one of two things: - Upgrade a WHEEL wedge. - Trigger the WHEEL FEATURE. When WHEEL FEATURE is triggered, 1 spin of the wheel is awarded. The base wheel will have MANSIONS FEATURE, MEGA HAT FEATURE, SUPER JACKPOT, GRAND JACKPOT, MAJOR JACKPOT, MINOR JACKPOT, and MINI JACKPOT on the WHEEL wedges. The WHEEL wedges can upgrade as shown below: - MINI JACKPOT upgrades to MINOR JACKPOT. - MINOR JACKPOT upgrades to MAJOR JACKPOT. - MAJOR JACKPOT upgrades to GRAND JACKPOT. - GRAND JACKPOT upgrades to SUPER JACKPOT. - MEGA HAT FEATURE upgrades to SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE. - MANSIONS FEATURE upgrades to SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE. All upgraded wedges will remain on the WHEEL until the WHEEL FEATURE is triggered for an active bet. Once a WHEEL FEATURE is triggered, the WHEEL will reset to its base state. If the wheel lands on MANSIONS FEATURE, MEGA HAT FEATURE, SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE or SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE then that feature is triggered with 6 free spins. If the wheel lands on SUPER JACKPOT, GRAND JACKPOT, MAJOR JACKPOT, MINOR JACKPOT, and MINI JACKPOT then the value displayed in the corresponding meter is awarded. The segments on the wheel do not accurately reflect reality and do not have equal odds. BUZZ SAW FEATURE BUZZ SAW symbol with blue background lands only on reels 3 and 5. BUZZ SAW symbol landing in the Base Game for the active bet can do one of three things: - Do nothing. - Increment the number shown on the BUZZ SAW TOOLBOX by 1. - Trigger the BUZZ SAW FEATURE and award 6 free spins. If triggered, the number displayed on the TOOLBOX is the number of BUZZ SAWs with red background added to random positions on the reel array, after which the BUZZ SAW free spins are played. Once the BUZZ SAW FEATURE is triggered, the BUZZ SAW toolbox accrued amount will reset. The default count remains blank in the BUZZ SAW toolbox until the first increment, when 1 appears. The max accrual is 5. Before the free spins begin, each BUZZ SAW symbol will move to the right until off-screen, starting with the rightmost BUZZ SAW symbol. Each position that BUZZ SAW symbol touches while moving to the right, including its initial position, is evaluated as if HARD HAT symbol had landed there during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, one at a time. During the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the BUZZ SAW FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. MEGA HAT FEATURE Before the free spins begin, 1 Mega Hat spin is awarded and played. During the Mega Hat spin, all reels spin together and Mega Hat symbols MEGA HAT 2x2, MEGA HAT 3x3, and MEGA HAT 3x5 are the only active symbols. The Mega Hat spin cannot stop with Mega Hat symbols partially revealed. After the Mega Hat spin, STRAW FRAME is added to each position covered by Mega Hat symbols. During the MEGA HAT FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the MEGA HAT FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE During the SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE, the grid will expand to 6 rows high. Before the free spins begin, 1 Mega Hat spin is awarded and played. During the Mega Hat spin, all reels spin together and Mega Hat symbols MEGA HAT 2x2, MEGA HAT 3x3, and MEGA HAT 3x5 are the only active symbols. The Mega Hat spin cannot stop with Mega Hat symbols partially revealed. After the Mega Hat spin, STRAW FRAME is added to each position covered by Mega Hat symbols. During the SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the SUPER MEGA HAT FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. MANSIONS FEATURE When MANSIONS FEATURE is triggered, 3 HARD HAT symbols will be randomly placed on the grid and a BRICKFRAME is added to the position of each HARD HAT symbol. During the MANSIONS FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the MANSIONS FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE During the SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE, the grid will expand to 6 rows high. 3 HARD HAT symbols will be placed randomly on the bottom 3 rows and the same will be duplicated onto the top 3 rows. BRICKFRAME is added to the position of each HARD HAT symbol. During the SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the SUPER MANSIONS FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. FREE SPINS FEATURE A HARD HAT symbol landing in the Base Game may add to the HARD HAT TOOLBOX. 6 or more scattered HARD HAT symbols during the base game triggers the FREE SPINS FEATURE and award 6 free spins. If fewer than 6 HARD HAT symbols land, the HARD HAT TOOLBOX may randomly add between 1 and 6 HARD HAT symbols to the grid to trigger the FREE SPINS FEATURE. After the random trigger, the HARD HAT TOOLBOX resets to an empty TOOLBOX. STRAWFRAME is added to the position of each triggering HARD HAT symbol. During the FREE SPINS FEATURE, 3 or more scattered HARD HAT symbol award 1 additional free spin. An alternate set of reels is used during the FREE SPINS FEATURE. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game except BUZZ SAW symbol and WHEEL symbol do not appear. During the feature, prizes are awarded as shown. SIMULTANEOUS FEATURE TRIGGER If the WHEEL FEATURE, BUZZ SAW FEATURE, and FREE SPINS FEATURE are triggered on a single spin, the WHEEL FEATURE will play out first, followed by the BUZZ SAW FEATURE, and then the FREE SPINS FEATURE. TURBO MODE Turn ON Turbo Mode to speed up each spin. It can be turned off at any point during the game. When any Bonus is awarded Turbo Mode Button will automatically be turned OFF. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins. REEL WAYS™ PAYS Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. Pays on 243 ways.
