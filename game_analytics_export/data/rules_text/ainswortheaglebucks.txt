Eagle Bucks How to play The ‘TOTAL BET’ is the ‘BET’ multiplied by the number of ‘LINES’. GAME HISTORY: This can be viewed by clicking on the ‘clock’ button while in real play mode. RESULT: Any wins are indicated by the highlighted lines are symbols animating wherever applicable. Wins are show in the message box. The game rules are identical in both real play and demo play modes. The player is responsible for checking the correct bet has been registered before commencing play. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Game Rules ‘Eagle’ substitutes for all symbols except scatters. ‘Eagle’ is a scatter. All wins begin with left most reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters. Scatters pay in any position. All wins on lit lines only except scatters. Highest win only each line. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Wins on different lines are added. The game has return to player of 94.09%. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Free Games Feature 3 or more scattered ‘Shield’ randomly triggers either 10 free games or 10 free games with reel 5 held with ‘Eagle’. During 10 free games, ‘Eagle’ appears’ on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. During 10 free games with reel 5 held with ‘Eagle’, ‘Eagle’ appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only. If 10 free games with reel 5 held with ‘Eagle’ is triggered, reel 5 is held with ‘Eagle’. When ‘Eagle’ appears anywhere on reels 2, 3 or 4, all symbols on those reels are replaced by ‘Eagle’. 2 of a kind ‘Stone Arch’ and ‘Wolf’ do not pay during the Free Games Feature. During the Free Games Feature, any 3 or more scattered ‘Shield’ will trigger an additional 5 free games same as the initial triggered feature. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggers the Free Games Feature. Reel strips during the Free Games Feature are different to reel strips during paid games.
