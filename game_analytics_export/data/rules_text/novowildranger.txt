GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Saddle up for the journey as we trek through the western canyons in Wild Ranger™. This 5 reel slot with 20 lines, hosts some exciting Features. Main Game Your aim in Wild Ranger™ is to land 3 or more matching symbols on adjacent reels, on one of the 20 win lines. Wild Ranger has plenty of Features including Stacked Wilds, Free Games with GIANT symbols, the Wild Ranger Jackpots Feature and a 3 Level Jackpot, Free Games Feature The scatter BONUS symbol is your ride to the Free Games round. Land three scatter BONUS symbols and the Free Games Feature is triggered with 5 Free Games and GIANT 3x3 symbols. Free Games can be re-triggered. During Free Games: The GIANT BONUS symbol awards 3 additional Free Games. The GIANT STAR awards the Wild Ranger Jackpots Feature. Wild Ranger Jackpots Feature 6 or more Gold STAR symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Wild Ranger Jackpots Feature. The triggering Gold STAR symbols are held and 3 Bonus Spins are awarded for all other symbol positions. Each time a new Gold STAR lands on the reels, the Bonus Spins counter is reset to 3. The Grand Jackpot is awarded if all positions on the screen are filled with Gold STARS. Gold STAR prizes can award Minor and Mini Jackpots or up to 30x total bet Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Gold STARS and BONUS symbols. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per scatter combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
