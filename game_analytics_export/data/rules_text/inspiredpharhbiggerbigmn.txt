Pharaohs Bigger Big Money WIN LINES ALL GAMES ARE PLAYED WITH 10 WIN LINES. WINS CAN OCCUR ACROSS ANY OR ALL OF THE WIN LINES. ONLY THE HIGHEST AWARD IS PAID PER WINNING COMBINATION. WINS ON DIFFERENT PLAYED LINES ARE ADDED. ALL WINS PAY LEFT TO RIGHT ACROSS ADJACENT REELS, EXCEPT BONUS, STARTING FROM THE LEFTMOST REEL. WINS ON DIFFERENT WIN LINES ARE ACCUMULATED. BONUS SYMBOL THE BONUS SYMBOL IS AVAILABLE ON ALL REELS IN THE BASE GAME AND FORTUNE SPINS. 3 OR MORE BONUS SYMBOLS LANDING ANYWHERE IN VIEW ON THE REELS WILL TRIGGER THE BONUS. 3 IN ANY POSITION AWARDS BIG MONEY BONUS WITH A 1X STARTING MULTIPLIER. 4 IN ANY POSITION AWARDS SUPER BIG MONEY BONUS WITH A 2X STARTING MULTIPLIER. 5 IN ANY POSITION AWARDS MEGA BIG MONEY BONUS WITH A 3X STARTING MULTIPLIER. BONUS UPGRADES BEFORE ENTERING THE BONUS, THE PLAYER IS PRESENTED WITH A SCREEN DISPLAYING 8 OPTIONS THAT CAN BE SELECTED TO REVEAL UP TO 6 DIFFERENT BONUS UPGRADES. ONCE SELECTED AN OPTION WILL REVEAL ONE OF THE BONUS UPGRADES OR START THE BONUS. IF AN UPGRADE IS REVEALED THIS IS ADDED TO THE UPGRADE PANEL AND THE PLAYER CAN CONTINUE TO PICK UNREVEALED OPTIONS UNTIL ALL 6 UPGRADES HAVE BEEN REVEALED OR UNTIL START BONUS IS REVEALED. THE POSSIBLE BONUS UPGRADES ARE: CHASER FREEZE - THE CHASER WILL NOT MOVE ALONG THE TRAIL FOR THE FIRST 2 SPINS THAT WILD CHASER SYMBOLS LAND ON THE REELS GOLDEN WILD COLLECTOR - SPECIAL WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE REELS. AFTER LANDING, THESE SYMBOLS STEP DOWN ONE POSITION ON SUBSEQUENT SPINS UNTIL NO LONGER IN VIEW. EXTRA CASH SYMBOLS - MORE CASH SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE REELS. EXTRA WILD COLLECTORS - MORE WILD COLLECTORS SYMBOLS ARE ADDED TO THE REELS. INCREASED CASH SYMBOLS - THE CASH SYMBOLS VALUES INCREASED TO 50X, 100X AND 200X THE CURRENT BET. MULTIPLIERS UPGRADE - ALL MULTIPLIERS ALONG THE TRAIL ARE INCREASED, UP TO 20X CAN NOW BE ACHIEVED. BIG MONEY BONUS AT THE START OF THE BONUS, A PROGRESSIVE TRAIL WILL APPEAR ABOVE THE REELS. IF THE MULTIPLIERS UPGRADE HAS NOT BEEN AWARDED, THE AVAILABLE MULTIPLIERS THAT CAN BE OBTAINED VIA THE TRAIL ARE 2X, 3X, 4X, 6X, 8X AND 10X. IF THE MULTIPLIERS UPGRADE HAS BEEN AWARDED, THE AVAILABLE MULTIPLIERS THAT CAN BE OBTAINED VIA THE TRAIL ARE 3X, 4X, 6X, 8X, 10X AND 20X. THE FIRST MULTIPLIER IS LOCATED AT POSITION 6, WITH EACH ADDITIONAL MULTIPLIER INCREASING EVERY 3 POSITIONS. UPON REACHING A MULTIPLIER ON THE TRAIL, ALL SUBSEQUENT CASH SYMBOLS COLLECTED BY THE WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOL WILL BE MULTIPLIED BY THIS VALUE. WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOLS ADVANCE THE LEADER ON THE TRAIL AND ACT AS A CASH COLLECTOR. THE CASH SYMBOL VALUES WILL BE COLLECTED FOR EACH WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOL THAT LANDS ON THE REELS. WILD CHASER SYMBOLS PROGRESS THE CHASER ALONG THE TRAIL. IF MULTIPLE WILD CHASER OR WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOLS LAND IN VIEW, EACH WILL PROGRESS ONE POSITION ON THE TRAIL FOR EACH SYMBOL THAT LANDS ON THE REELS. WILD CHASER AND WILD COLLECTOR SYMBOLS ACT AS WILDS. THE FEATURE ENDS WHEN THE CHASER CATCHES THE LEADER AND THE 'BONUS OVER' RESULT COMES FROM THE PICK ME MINI GAME. GAMBLE THE GAMBLE CAN BE CONFIGURED TO BE ON, ON IF THE WIN IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 5X BET, OR OFF. IF THE GAMBLE IS ON, ANY WINNINGS AWARDED DURING THE GAME WILL OFFER THE GAMBLE FEATURE. IF THE GAMBLE IS ON AND IF THE WIN IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 5X BET, ANY WINNINGS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 5X BET AWARDED DURING THE GAME WILL OFFER THE GAMBLE FEATURE. THE GAMBLE THRESHOLD DOES NOT INCREASE WITH FORTUNE BET ENABLED. THE GAMBLE ENTRY TRIGGER IS BASED ON THE BASE BET BEFORE ANY BET MULTIPLIERS ARE APPLIED. WHEN THE GAMBLE IS ENTERED, TWO WHEELS ARE PRESENTED. THE LEFT WHEEL OFFERS THE CHANCE TO INCREASE THE WINNINGS BY ONE THIRD, DOUBLE THE WINNINGS, OR TRIPLE THE WINNINGS. THE RIGHT WHEEL OFFERS THE CHANCE TO GAMBLE FOR THE BIG MONEY BONUS, SUPER BIG MONEY BONUS OR MEGA BIG MONEY BONUS. THE WIN VALUE CAN BE ADJUSTED BY PRESSING THE '-' AND '+' ARROWS, THIS IN TURN WILL ADJUST THE WINNING AND LOSING SEGMENTS OF THE WHEEL. PRESS EITHER WHEEL DURING THE FEATURE TO GAMBLE YOUR WINNINGS AND SPIN THE POINTER. IF THE POINTER STOPS ON THE GREEN SEGMENT, THE CASH VALUE OR BONUS IS AWARDED. IF THE POINTER STOPS ON THE RED SEGMENT, THE PLAYER LOSES THEIR WINNINGS AND EXITS TO THE BASE GAME. WHEN THE POTENTIAL WINNING AREA OF THE GAMBLE WHEEL IS AT ITS MINIMUM SIZE THE CHANCE OF SUCCESS COULD BE SIGNIFICANTLY LESS THAN THE VISUAL REPRESENTATION SHOWN. IF THE RESULT OF A WINNING GAMBLE WOULD EXCEED THE WIN LIMIT OF 2500X BASE BET OR $250,000.00, WHICHEVER IS THE LOWER VALUE, WINNINGS ARE AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED, AND THE GAMBLE WILL BE EXITED. THE MAXIMUM THAT CAN BE ACHIEVED VIA THE FREE SPINS GAMBLE IS THE MEGA BIG MONEY BONUS. ONCE THIS IS REACHED, THE BIG MONEY BONUS WHEEL IS DISABLED, WHILE THE PLAYER IS STILL ABLE TO GAMBLE ON THE CASH WHEEL. PRESS THE 'COLLECT' BUTTON DURING THE FEATURE TO COLLECT YOUR WINNINGS. THE EXPECTED PAYBACK FOR THE GAMBLE GAME IS 100% GENERAL INFORMATION IN THE EVENT OF AN UNEXPECTED INTERRUPTION / TERMINATION OF A REAL PLAY GAME, THE GAME WILL RECOVER TO THE LAST KNOWN STATE.
