Joker Troupe spoils players with three breath-taking features, Blue Joker feature, Green Joker Wheel and Red Joker Freespins. This highly volatile game maximizes the chance of delivering the most insane action possible. Legendary joker games have truly faced their match with Joker Troupe! Base Game A 4 x 3 reel slot with 10 win lines Scatters Up to 3 Scatters can land on the reels at any time, bringing the possibility of big wins. Joker Troupe includes three different Scatters, Blue, Red and green Joker symbols. Three same Joker symbols in view will trigger a bonus feature. Blue Joker Feature Three Blue Jokers will award with Blue Joker Feature. By collecting blue hats, the player will build the stacks. Every three blue hat symbols will unlock a new row and increase the multiplier of further hat symbols up to 25x. The reel will expand up to 10 x 4 reel. Three Red Joker symbols will take the player straight to Red Joker Freespins. Green Joker Wheel This feature appears as a classical wheel at first, but it’s filled with coin wins along with two large Booster segments containing multipliers and Red Jokers. The wins will grow by hitting the multipliers in the wheel. Hitting the same Booster segment three times will end the wheel and award with Red Joker Freespins. Red Joker Freespins Three Red Joker symbols in view will award with 20 seconds of Red Joker Freespins. Any new Red Joker on the reel will increase the pace of spins and filling above the reels. When the meter is filled, the timer resets to 10 seconds and the meter starts over. During this, the frequency will increase dramatically. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
