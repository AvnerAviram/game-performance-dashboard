Aurora Beast Hunter About Aurora Beast Hunter Welcome to Aurora Beast Hunter, a video slot featuring Free Spins selection, Scatter Re-spin, Re-spin, Cash Prize, regular Wild, Stacked Wild, Sticky Wild Stack, Random Wild, and Expanding Wild. Game Rules Aurora Beast Hunter is a video slot with 5 reels and 40 lines. Win on a line pays if in succession from the leftmost to the rightmost position according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different lines are added. Free Spins, Scatter Re-spin, and random base game features cannot be triggered simultaneously. Wins during Re-spin and Scatter Re-spin are added to the total win. Wild symbols There are 4 types of Wild symbols: regular Wild, Stacked Wild, Expanding Wild, and Sticky Wild Stack. When a stack of 4 of the same symbols land (except Wild symbols), the stack will turn into a Stacked Wild. Stacked Wild appears only on reels 2 and 4. When an Expanding Wild symbol lands, it expands to cover the entire reel. Expanding Wild appears only on reels 1, 3, and 5. Sticky Wild Stack appears in The Sub Free Spins feature and as a random base game feature. Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Scatter symbols and other Wild symbols. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Expanding Wild and Stacked Wild replace Random Wild. Scatter symbols In the base game, Scatter symbols only appear on reels 1, 3, and 5. Landing 3 Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels award the Free Spins feature selection. - Confidential - Landing 2 Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels award one Scatter Re-spin. The reel without a Scatter symbol (reel 1, 3, or 5) will spin again. Expanding Wild symbols do not appear during the Scatter Re-spin. Free Spins There are 3 types of Free Spins features to choose: The Sub, Danger Zone, and Sandstorm. The last bet placed in the game is used as the Free Spins bet amount. Wins awarded during the feature are added to base game wins. The Sub Choosing The Sub awards 5 Free Spins. After each spin, one reel is randomly marked. If there is either an Expanding Wild or Stacked Wild on the marked reel, the reel will turn into a Sticky Wild Stack. Sticky Wild Stack sticks to its position for the remainder of The Sub Free Spins feature. Reels which already have a Sticky Wild Stack cannot be marked. When a Sticky Wild Stack appears, one additional Free Spins is awarded. Danger Zone Choosing Danger Zone awards 8 Free Spins. On every spin, Random Wild feature randomly awards anywhere between 1 - 5 regular Wild symbols. The Wild symbols can appear on any position. Danger Zone Free Spins cannot be retriggered. Sandstorm Choosing Sandstorm awards 12 Free Spins. In Sandstorm, Expanding Wild awards a Cash Prize of 1.5x the bet. In Sandstorm, Stacked Wild awards a Cash Prize of 2x the bet. Any regular Wild landing on the reel area will not award a Cash Prize. Any Cash Prize will be awarded irrespective of line wins. Cash Prize will be added to any line wins after each spin. Sandstorm Free Spins cannot be retriggered. In the base game during a spin, there’s a chance you’re awarded random features: Cash Prize, Random Wild, or Sticky Wild Stack. Only one random feature can trigger per spin. 1.0.0.1 Random Base Game Features - Cash Prize At least one Expanding Wild or Stacked Wild is guaranteed. Expanding Wild awards a Cash Prize of 1.5x the bet. Stacked Wild awards a Cash Prize of 2x the bet. Cash Prize will be added to any line wins after the spin. 1.0.0.2 Random Base Game Features - Random Wild Random Wild feature randomly awards anywhere between 1 - 5 regular Wild symbols. The Wild symbols can appear on any position on reel 1, 3, and 5. 1.0.0.3 Random Base Game Features - Sticky Wild Stack At least one Expanding Wild or Stacked Wild is guaranteed. Only one Expanding Wild or Stacked Wild will turn into a Sticky Wild Stack and a Respin is awarded. The reels without a Sticky Wild Stack will spin again. Return to Player (RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time. The RTP is fixed and does not change. This game has an RTP of 96.27%. Game Controls Game Keypad Item/Description Spin Button Start the game with the current bet size as displayed in the bet field.
