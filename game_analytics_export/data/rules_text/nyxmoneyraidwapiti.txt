BASE GAME In the base game, the GREEN ROCKET and PURPLE ROCKET symbols may only land on reel 1 and reel 6 respectively. The DOLLAR symbol can award x0.5 - x25 the total bet during the base game. DOLLAR symbols will be awarded only if the GREEN ROCKET, PURPLE ROCKET, or both rocket symbols land simultaneously. DOLLAR symbols pay for each rocket symbol that lands. GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER When the GREEN ROCKET lands on reel 1 in the base game for the active bet, one of the following actions will occur to the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER: - No Action. - Increase the spin count in the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER by 1. - Triggers the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER and awards MONEY RAID SPINS feature, with the accumulated spins on the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER. The default number of spins on the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER is 9 and it can increase up to 100. PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER When the PURPLE ROCKET lands on reel 6 in the base game for the active bet, one of the following actions will occur to the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER: - No Action. - The multiplier on the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER increases by 1. - Triggers the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER and awards BIG SPINS feature, with 7 free spins. The default multiplier value on the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER is x3. It can increase up to a maximum of x20. MONEY RAID SPINS When the MONEY RAID SPINS feature triggers, it awards 1x the total bet and the accumulated spins on the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER. During this feature, the GREEN ROCKET symbol expands and locks reel 1. During this feature, only DOLLAR symbols are evaluated. The feature gets over when all the spins are played out. An alternate set of reels is used during the MONEY RAID SPINS feature. After feature completion, the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER resets to 9. BIG SPINS When the BIG SPINS feature triggers, 1x the total bet and 7 FREE SPINS are awarded. During this feature, the PURPLE ROCKET symbol expands and locks reel 6. During this feature, only the DOLLAR symbol is evaluated. All DOLLAR symbols will have the same multiplier mentioned on the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER. The feature gets over when all the spins are played out. An alternate set of reels is used during the BIG SPINS feature. BIG MONEY RAID SPINS When both GREEN ROCKET and PURPLE ROCKET symbols land and trigger both CLUSTER simultaneously, the BIG MONEY RAID SPINS feature is awarded. When the BIG MONEY RAID SPINS feature triggers, it awards 2x the total bet and the accumulated spins on the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER. During this feature, the GREEN ROCKET and PURPLE ROCKET symbols expand and lock reels 1 and 6 respectively. During this feature, only the DOLLAR symbols are evaluated. All DOLLAR symbols will have the same multiplier mentioned on the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER. The feature gets over when all the spins are played out. An alternate set of reels is used during the BIG MONEY RAID SPINS. After feature completion, the GREEN ROCKET CLUSTER resets to 9, and the PURPLE ROCKET CLUSTER resets to x3. WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS 3 or more scattered BONUS symbols in the base game award x2, x5, x10 and x20 the total bet respectively and 10 Free Spins. BONUS pays are paid in addition to REEL WAYS™ PAYS. The WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS is exclusive and cannot be triggered with any other feature. WILD symbols may have x2, x3, x4, x5, or a GRAND, MAJOR or MINOR jackpot. If a WILD symbol with a multiplier of x2, x3, x4, or x5 is part of a winning combination, the REEL WAYS™ PAYS is multiplied. If multiple WILD symbols are part of a way win, their multipliers are added together and then applied to the REEL WAYS™ PAYS. The GRAND, MAJOR, or MINOR jackpot on the WILD symbol is awarded only when the WILD symbol is part of a winning combination. The GRAND, MAJOR, and MINOR jackpots are paid only once per spin, even if they appear in multiple winning combinations. GRAND, MAJOR and MINOR jackpots can only be won during the WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS. The GRAND and MAJOR jackpots can each be won only once during the WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS, with either one or both potentially being awarded simultaneously. WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS cannot be re-triggered, and the GREEN ROCKET, PURPLE ROCKET, and DOLLAR symbols do not appear during WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS. An alternate set of reels is used during the WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS. Winning combinations in this feature are identical to the base game. JACKPOTS MINOR Jackpot can be awarded in WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS multiple times. GRAND and MAJOR Jackpot can be awarded in WILD JACKPOT FREE SPINS only once per game. REEL WAYS™ PAYS Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations. Pays on 4096 ways.
