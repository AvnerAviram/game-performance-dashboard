Coins Wheel Feature The appearance of 3 scattered on the reels triggers 1 spin of the Coins Wheel. The Coins Wheel awards either Currency Wins or 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. Free Games Wheel Feature The appearance of 3 scattered on the reels triggers 1 spin of the Free Games Wheel. The Free Games Wheel awards either Free Games or 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. Free Games During Free Games, the player can trigger additional spins of the Coins Wheel or Free Games Wheel if 2 or more or 2 or more appear anywhere in view on the reels: 2 triggers 1 spin of the Coins Wheel. 3 triggers 3 spins of the Coins Wheel. 2 triggers 1 spins of the Free Games Wheel. 3 triggers 3 spins of the Free Games Wheel. Free Games end when 0 Free Games remain or when 1000 Free Games are played. Progressive Jackpots The Coins Wheel Feature or Free Games Wheel Feature must be accessed in order to have a chance of winning 1 of 4 Progressive Jackpots. The 4 Progressive Jackpots are: Mini, Minor, Major and Mega. If the Wheel stops on the Mini Progressive, the value of the Mini Progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Minor Progressive, the value of the Minor Progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Major Progressive, the value of the Major Progressive is won. If the Wheel stops on the Mega Progressive, the value of the Mega Progressive is won. It is not technically possible to have two simultaneous wins of the same Progressive Jackpot. There are separate Progressive Jackpots per bet multiplier. Contributions made to Progressive Jackpots at a certain bet multiplier are added to Progressive Jackpots that can only be achieved by making a bet at that same bet multiplier. The following contributions are made to Progressive Jackpots on each base game spin: 27% of the bet is contributed to the Mini Progressive Jackpot. 23% of the bet is contributed to the Minor Progressive Jackpot. 19% of the bet is contributed to the Major Progressive Jackpot. 16% of the bet is contributed to the Mega Progressive Jackpot. At a 1x bet multiplier, the start-up value for each Progressive Jackpot is: Mini: $9.00 Minor: $15.00 Major: $40.00 Mega: $100.00 The start-up values for the Progressive Jackpots at bet multipliers other than 1x are calculated by multiplying the bet multiplier by the start-up value at a 1x bet multiplier.
