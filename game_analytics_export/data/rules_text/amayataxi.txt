Taxi GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play All wins pay Left to Right All 25 lines always played Select Credits Per Line multiplier (1, 2, 3, 4, 5) x 25 lines Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current Credits Per Line selected Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits Per Line staked Only the highest winning combination is paid per line Payline wins are combined and added SCATTER wins are added to payline wins displayed in the WON meter Wild Substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER, BONUS The Won field displays winnings in Credits Spacebar can be used to spin Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000.
