Dragon Kingdom Characteristics and features All winning combinations pay from left to right only All wins are multiplied by bet per line Bets are made in coins per line To control bets, user is able to change the value of a coin in real currency and also increase the number of coins bet per line Balance, bets, wins are all displayed in both coins and real currency at all times There are 10 different symbols in the game. 4 high symbols represented by one female dragon warrior and 3 dragons. Always stacked during main game. 4 low symbols, represented by royals. Always stacked during main game. 1 Wild symbol. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Scatter. Always stacked during main game Scatter symbol – triggers Free Spins feature. Can hit on any reel Free Spins feature Free spins are awarded when 3 or more scatter symbols are hit anywhere on the reels User is awarded 5 free spins User is presented with 5 possible free spins option. Each option sees only one of the high symbols stacked or Wild stacked during the free spins round, with a different multiplier for the whole win during Free Spins, depending on the value of the symbol chosen After user has chosen option, special reels will be in play during the round, based on the option chosen Three or more Scatter symbols hit during the free spins round award 3 more free spins with the same settings as the currently selection option Paytable Artwork English
