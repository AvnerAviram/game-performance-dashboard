Heartburst Game Description Experience the rush of excitement in this sensational 100 line slot game where delicious, vibrant fruits of all descriptions spin alongside hearts that are bursting with fortunes just for you. When a Heart symbol substitutes in a winning combination you will get a 3x multiple on that win and you could be rewarded with up to 25 free spins when the Lucky 7 is triggered. What does your heart desire most of all? Plenty of juicy fun and a BIG win! How to Play Can you feel the excitement? Are you ready for a hearty win? Heartburst is exploding with winning possibilities so let’s learn how to use them to your advantage. Decide how many lines you want to play (you can play up to 100!) and the wager amount per line. Once you do that hit the “spin” button. If you want to speed up your heartbeat and make the gameplay faster try using the “autoplay” function. Once the action has started look out for the “Lucky 7s” symbol. If 3 or more appear on the reels you’ll win up to 25 FREE spins. But that’s not all! Whether you have won some FREE spins or not, each time the heart substitute appears in a winning combination you will TRIPLE your win!
