Draw the sword from the stone to receive treasures and powerful multipliers. Grab the hilt and start pulling for your chance to win big with Lucky Sword- Tapper! Main Game Lucky Sword - Tapper is a single click game with a Sword that is surrounded by 17 unique cash awards. Every click of the Spin button causes the Sword to shift with a chance to burst free, causing at least 3 prize values to be highlighted and won. Feeling lucky? If yes, then all 17 prize values can be yours! Multiplier Additionally, winning game rounds also have a chance to reveal a prize Multiplier. If a Multiplier appears after the Sword is pulled, then all winning prizes are multiplied by the displayed Multiplier value. The Multiplier can appear as 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x, or 10x. Rules The aim of this game is to pull the Sword from the Stone and win the displayed prize values. When the Sword is pulled the winning prizes are highlighted. The player can win anywhere from 3 to 17 unique prizes. Any win that results in less than 10 prizes has a chance to gain a multiplier of either 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x, or 10x.
