Bao Shi Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 7 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules BAO SHI is a 5-reel game with 1024 ways to win! The game includes two WILDS: DRUM and GOLD COIN, which substitute for all symbols except SCATTER. The DRUM only appears during the base game, while the GOLD COIN appears during LIORAS BLESSINGS and FREE SPINS features. SCATTER can appear on any reel, while WILDS can appear only on reels 2, 3, and 4. LIORAS BLESSINGS Feature: LIORAS above the reels collects a DRUM each time it lands. The LIORAS BLESSINGS feature can be randomly triggered during the base game, awarding 2 to 3 GOLD COIN symbols. GOLD COINS cannot overlap with SCATTER, DRUM, or GOLDEN LIORAS symbols. GOLD COINS may reveal an X2 or X3 MULTIPLIER. MULTIPLIERS are added together and applied to all wins. All LIORAS symbols on the reels are upgraded to GOLDEN LIORAS, resulting in increased payouts during this feature. The number of DRUM symbols collected does not affect the number of GOLD COINS or MULTIPLIERS awarded. FREE SPINS Feature: Landing 3 to 5 SCATTERS symbols anywhere on the reels, even if they are not consecutive, will trigger the feature. More SCATTERS award more initial FREE SPINS: • 5 SCATTERS award 15 FREE SPINS. • 4 SCATTERS award 12 FREE SPINS. • 3 SCATTERS award 8 FREE SPINS. During FREE SPINS, all LIORAS symbols on the reels are upgraded to GOLDEN LIORAS for the duration of the feature, resulting in increased payouts for this symbol. The DRUM symbol is not available during this feature. The LIORAS BLESSINGS feature can be randomly triggered during FREE SPINS, awarding 3 to 5 GOLD COINS. GOLD COINS cannot overlap with SCATTER, GOLD COIN, or GOLDEN LIORAS symbols. Each GOLD COIN symbol that appears during LIORAS BLESSINGS may reveal an X2, X3, X4, or X5 MULTIPLIER, which are added together and applied to each winning symbol in that spin. Each SCATTER that lands during FREE SPINS adds 1 FREE SPIN, up to a maximum of 100 FREE SPINS. Any SCATTER that lands after reaching the maximum FREE SPINS will not award additional spins. The average RTP is 94.20%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. There are 1024 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 15000 times total bet. Auto adjust bet - Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated.
