Wisps How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning ways pay depending on the current mode of the game. Winnings on different ways are all added. The winnings from Free Spins are added to the total balance. Only the highest winning combination on each way is paid. Note: The number of adjacent winning symbols (ways) is calculated by multiplying the number of winning symbols on each reel. For example, if there is 1 winning symbol on reel 1, 1 winning symbol on reel 2 and 2 winning symbols on reel 3, then the number of adjacent winning symbols is 1 x 1 x 2 = 2. In this case, the number of reels involved in the win is 3 – in the paytable, you can view the gain for 3 reels for the winning symbol. Note : On the info bar located under the main game buttons, you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WINNINGS, in your currency. To play, follow these steps: Use the coin value select button to choose a coin value. The coin value select button is located in the bottom-right side of the main game screen and shows the currently selected coin value. Click one of the BET buttons to place a bet and spin the reels one time or you can use the left and right arrows on your keyboard to select a BET and then click SPIN . To place the same bet again and spin the reels one more time, click SPIN . Note: BET 1 places a bet of 1x coins, BET 2 places a bet of 2x coins, BET 3 places a bet of 3x coins, BET 4 places a bet of 4x coins and BET 5 places a bet of 5x coins. Your bet will be: the coin bet (BET 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5) times 50 coins times coin value . The total bet, in your currency, is displayed on each of the BET buttons. The total amount won, in your currency, according to the paytable, is shown in the WINNINGS box. For each winning combination, the amount won is the number of adjacent winning symbols multiplied by the amount shown in the paytable for the number of reels involved in the win. Special Symbols and Features Game modes There are three game modes, only one of these being active at any one time. The switch between the three modes is done randomly (there is no succeeding order) and before the start of a spin, at random times. The switch cannot be done during free spins. The very first time you access this game, after the game loads, you start in mode 1. The next time you access the game, after the game loads, you start in the same mode you were in the last time before you closed the game. The three modes are: Mode 1: Winnings are paid from left to right Mode 2: Winnings are paid from left to right and right to left Mode 3: Winnings are paid from left to right The Transformer symbol can appear on the middle reel Wild The Wild symbol replaces any other symbols, except Transformer and Scatter. Scatter In all modes, Scatters pay according to the paytable. When you win free spins, at the beginning of the free spins, you can decide what type of free spins you will play: Bouncing Wilds free spins: 1 to 5 random symbols on the screen will become Wild during each free spin Spiral Wilds free spins: reels 2, 3, 4 or 5 can become Wild randomly during these free spins; up to three reels at one time can become Wild During free spins, other free spins can be triggered. The type of the re-triggered free spins cannot be changed; there is no limit to how many times free spins are re-triggered. The wins from Scatter (5X, 10X, 25X the amount bet) are added to the total Free spins win. During free spins, the amount bet remains the same as the bet on the spin that has triggered the free spins and cannot be changed. Transformer The Transformer symbol can appear only on the middle reel, during normal and free spins and only when the game is in mode 3 (Left to right Transformer mode). When the Transformer symbol appears, it will transform any low paying symbols (9 to A) on the screen and itself into one random normal symbol (not Wild or Scatter). Winnings from the transformation are added to the winnings prior to the transformation, if any. Autospin feature The loss limit setting allows stopping the autospin feature automatically when the selected limit plus your cumulative winnings minus your cumulative bets is less than 0. Note : The autospin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. Click the AUTO SPIN button to open the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window where you can set up the autospin feature. On the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window, you can set up the following: Loss limit - enter a number for the loss limit value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the loss limit value Stop if WIN reaches - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side and then enter a number for the WIN value or use the left and the right arrow buttons to select a number for the WIN value; click the button on its left side again to disable it Stop if JACKPOT is won - (only for games with Progressive Jackpots or with a specific Jackpot value displayed) - to enable this setting, click the button on its left side; click the button on its left side again to disable it Number of AUTO SPINS to play - use the left and the right arrow buttons to select the number of autospins Click START to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window and start the autospin feature with the currently selected bet and number of autospins or click CANCEL to close the AUTO SPIN SETTINGS window without starting the autospin feature. Click the STOP xx/yy button to stop the autospin feature at any time; xx represents the remaining number of autospins and yy represents the number of autospins to play that you selected on the AUTO SPINS SETTINGS window.
