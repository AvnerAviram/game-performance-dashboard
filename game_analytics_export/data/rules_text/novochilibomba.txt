INITIAL SPIN AFTER BONUS WAYS BONUS WAYS Stacks of CHILI symbols can land on all reels to award more ways to win. All games start with a 6 x 3 matrix and 729 ways to win. Each stack of CHILI symbols can grow the reel by up to 5 extra symbols and provide up to 262,144 ways to win. EXPLODING WINS Each win will EXPLODE, making way for new symbols to drop in which may in turn set off a chain of wins from a single spin. Combining BONUS WAYS and EXPLODING WINS will give players an experience they have never had before. FREE SPINS 3, 4, 5 or 6 scatters award 10, 15, 20 or 25 free spins respectively. Free spins have additional CHILI symbols which award up to 3 extra WILD symbols on reels 2, 3, 4 & 5 and 1 to 9 extra FREE SPINS on reels 1 & 6. RULES SUN (WILD) substitutes for all symbols except MARACAS and CHILI. SUN (WILD) appears on reels 2, 3 ,4 and 5 only. MARACAS pay in any position. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and and pay left to right on adjacent reels, excluding scatters.
