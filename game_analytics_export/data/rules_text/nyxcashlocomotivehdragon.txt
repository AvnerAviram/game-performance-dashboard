Cash Locomotive Happy Dragon CASH LOCOMOTIVE™ HAPPY DRAGON, steam ahead and collect prizes on the way to lucky town. All prizes, including jackpots and bonuses, displayed on special scatter symbols are won in the collect feature. When these special scatters fill the reels, a pick game is also triggered where additional prizes including jackpots and bonuses are won. Additionally, special credit prizes, bonuses and jackpots may be randomly won in bought games. Free games are played on special reels with extra wild. CASH LOCOMOTIVE™ HAPPY DRAGON, has a 3x5 matrix layout, with fixed 50 lines HOW TO PLAY Select your bet. Press the Spin button to start a game cycle METERS AND BUTTONS Bet – Shows the bet value of the current setting. Bet per line – Shows the current bet per line. Bet level – Displays the available bet options. Spin Button – Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Stop Button. Press the Stop Button to stop the reels immediately. Max – Displays the maximum bet. Autoplay (wherever applicable) – Player can set the game to spin without the need to press the spin button every time. You can choose the number of spins to play automatically by selecting through the various options available in the menu. The loss limit (wherever applicable) will avert you from losing above set limit during autoplay session. Autoplay can be manually stopped by pressing Stop Autoplay button. Win – Displays the win for the current or last win paid. Credits – Displays the credit amount of the player. All credit, bet and win meters would be shown in currency. Free Games 3 or more scattered 【FEATURE】 trigger 9 FREE GAMES. Different reels are used during the FREE GAMES. Reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 contain extra [WILD] during the FREE GAMES and any retriggered FREE GAMES. FREE GAMES can be triggered again during the FREE GAMES. If the COLLECT FEATURE and FREE GAMES are triggered in the same game, the FREE GAMES start after the COLLECT FEATURE or PRIZE PICK FEATURE ends. If COLLECT FEATURE is triggered during the FREE GAMES, remaining FREE GAMES resume after the COLLECT FEATURE or PRIZE PICK FEATURE ends. All bets and lines played during the FREE GAMES are the same as the game that triggered the FREE GAMES. COLLECT FEATURE 1 [GOLD] appearing in a bought game or during the FREE GAMES triggers the COLLECT FEATURE and is held for the duration of the COLLECT FEATURE. Any [BONUS] appearing in the same bought game with [GOLD] are also held for the duration of the COLLECT FEATURE. When the COLLECT FEATURE is triggered, 2 free spins are awarded. Each new 【GOLD】 that appears during the COLLECT FEATURE awards 2 additional free spins and is held in position until the COLLECT FEATURE ends. Any symbol stopping behind [GOLD] or [BONUS] is invalid. Different reels are used during the COLLECT FEATURE. COLLECT FEATURE ends after all awarded free spins have been played or the PRIZE PICK FEATURE is triggered. All bets during the COLLECT FEATURE are the same as the game that triggered the COLLECT FEATURE or the FREE GAMES. PRIZE PICK FEATURE During bought games or the COLLECT FEATURE, any combination of 15 [GOLD]/[BONUS] appearing on the reels trigger the PRIZE PICK FEATURE. In the PRIZE PICK FEATURE, pick 1 from among 10 [LOCOMOTIVE] displayed on the game screen and win each prize revealed. Each [LOCOMOTIVE] contains 1 prize. Available Prizes: Prize Amount: equal to the number of lines played multiplied by either 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 10 or 20 credits randomly as displayed on the game screen. MAJOR JACKPOT GRAND JACKPOT The availability of the GRAND JACKPOT prize is limited to 1 per PRIZE PICK FEATURE. The chances of revealing each prize are not equal. PRIZE PICK FEATURE ends when all available picks have been made.Jackpot This game has 2 levels of fixed jackpots and 2 Fixed Bonus prizes. Jackpot can be won during base game and free game. The maximum Jackpot win values are: Grand Jackpot $5,000.00 Major Jackpot $500.00 (Currency Unit varies according to jurisdictions) Jackpot and Bonus prize wins will be multiplied by bet per line. GAMBLE FEATURE To enter the gamble game after a win, Press the GAMBLE button. Select a Suit, Red/Black or Collect. Select RED/BLACK or a SUIT to gamble the total win. Win is quadrupled if suit selection is correct Win is doubled if red/black selection is correct. Gamble feature is not available for any feature win. Wins may be gambled up to 5 times. Gamble will not be offered for the wins awarded during the feature trigger. All wins cannot be gambled if the Jackpot is triggered. Gamble will not be offered during Autoplay. GAME RULES This game requires a minimum of 50 credits multiplied by bet per line to play. All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel except scatters. Each symbol appearing on a reel is counted once per winning combination. All wins on win lines only except scatters. Line wins are multiplied by bet per line. Wins on different lines are added. Scatter wins are added to line wins. [Feature] wins are multiplied by the total bet. All wins are multiplied by bet per line,except scatter winsFree Games wins are added to line wins. Only highest win is paid on each line. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. All open game rounds (pending state) will be automatically settled after 30 days. AUTOPLAY SETTINGS Number of spins: Select number of Auto spins as per available choice. Autoplay Advanced Settings (wherever applicable): Loss Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Win Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached.Single win limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Stop on any win: Stops on any wins. NET POSITION Net position (wherever applicable): The Net position will display customer’s net position, in the currency of their account or product (e.g., pounds sterling, dollar, Euro) since the session started. Net position is defined as the total of all winnings minus the sum of all losses since the start of the session. REALITY CHECK Reality check (wherever applicable): The Reality check feature may display as a periodic in-game notifications for the real money players. Game gets suspended whenever the check appears during game play and only resumes once the player has acknowledged the check and has taken displayed actions.“Back to game”:- Notifying player to resume the game “To Lobby”:- Quits the current game (after a final prompt) and back to the Operators Main Game Menu Screen. MALFUNCTION In case of any malfunction of hardware or software during the game play, all current game bets and payouts are considered void and all bets affected are refunded.
