Giant Gems Giant Gems is a 5 reel slot game with Fortune Spins to boost your winnings. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols on any of the 5 reels. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The Theoretical Return to Player is: 93.75%-96.13% Game Rules All line pay symbols must appear on an active payline and on adjacent reels only the highest win is paid on each active payline Select a side bet amount to activate Giant Gems symbols. This amount is added to your line bet and the total is your bet. As your side bet increases, more Giant Gems symbols are activated, and lower paying symbols are removed from the reels. Giant Gems wins are paid when one Giant Gem symbol is fully in view on reels 2, 3 and 4. Refer to the Giant Gems paytable for Giant Gem prize amounts. Malfunction voids all pays and plays How to Play The bet that you want to play can be adjusted through the (+) and (-) buttons alongside the respective options. Spin: Spins the reels. Fortune Spins: A side bet amount to activate Giant Gems and remove lower paying symbols. Cancel Fortune Spins: Cancel Fortune Spins and return to non side bet wagering. Autoplay: Allows automatic consecutive normal game plays for a certain amount of spins. Options for spins range from 10 spins to 50 spins. Paytable: Access pay awards for winning symbol combinations. Also contains the rules for the game. The game displays all your details in the following display fields: Balance: Displays your total balance Win: Displays the amount won in the spin Total Bet: The total wager staked.
