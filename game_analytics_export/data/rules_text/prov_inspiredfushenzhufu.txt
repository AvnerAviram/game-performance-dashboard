Based on the popular North American (Illinois) retail version of the game, it is a descendant of Gold Mine Fortune™. This fun slot features a unique and thrilling “three bags” mechanic that sets the stage for powerful bonus combinations and golden winning opportunities. At the heart of the game, golden ingots landing on reels 1, 3, and 5 trigger one of three exciting Free Games bonuses, each linked to a prosperity pot. The “Fu” (green) pot delivers expanding reels with 88 win-lines and nudging Wilds for thrilling visual impact. The “Lu” (red) pot activates prize posts and drops valuable cash coins. The “Shou” (purple) pot boosts line wins with 2x multipliers and adds both cash and retrigger coins to the reels. When two bonuses combine players unlock the Super Free Games, while the Mega Free Games is unlocked with three bonus combinations, where every feature is active at once for maximum reward potential. The “three bags” feature creates seven possible bonus combinations, offering everything from reel expansions and Wilds to jackpots and retriggering coins. Players can watch as ingots drop into their matching pots. When all three ingots land together, a Match-3 Pick-Me game determines whether the player unleashes a regular, Super, or Mega bonus, each packed with escalating excitement and win potential. For those who want to accelerate their path to prosperity, Fu Shen Zhu Fu includes a suite of powerful play options. The Bonus Buy feature offers instant access to the game’s rich bonus rounds, while Fortune Bet increases the odds of triggering a feature during standard spins. Meanwhile, the Gamble feature invites players to risk their winnings for a shot at even greater prizes or direct entry into a bonus round. Visually stunning and filled with fortune, Fu Shen Zhu Fu combines a rich cultural theme with an innovative mechanic to create an unforgettable slot experience, and the chance of winning magical riches with every spin.
