Note Catcher GAME OBJECTIVE: ­­ NOTE CATCHER is a 5x3 reel slot game with 10 fixed paylines and played with imitation BANK NOTES. Notes pay from left to right and right to left, when 3 or more matching Notes land on adjacent reels on a payline either from the left most reel or from right most reel, except for the GOLD SAFE and SILVER SAFE symbols which are SCATTER symbols. Anytime a GOLD SAFE or SILVER SAFE symbol lands anywhere in view it will add to the corresponding SAFE above the reels. The dial on the SAFE symbol will spin to partially or fully open the SAFE symbol to indicate the progression towards triggering the feature. Opening the GOLD SAFE above the reels will trigger the NOTE GRABBER feature. Opening the SILVER SAFE above the reels will trigger the NOTE PICKER feature. Partially opening a SAFE above the reels is purely cosmetic and has no relationship with the chances of opening the safe completely when the next SAFE symbol lands in view on the reels. Click the ‘PAYTABLE’ icon to view the game awards. Click the ‘X’ icon to close the menu and return to the game play . GAME RULES: NOTE CATCHER is a 5x3 Reel, 10 fixed Paylines slot game. The BET value can be changed by using the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons. Click on the PLAY button to begin the game with the currently selected or default BET amount. All payouts are made according to the Paytable. The Paytable will always show the win amounts for the currently selected BET. Line Pays are from both left to right and right to left except for 5 of a kind win, which is paid only once. Notes pay from left to right and right to left, when 3 or more matching Notes land on adjacent reels on a payline either from the left most reel or from right most reel, except for the GOLD SAFE and SILVER SAFE symbols which are SCATTER symbols. Only the highest win amount on each payline is paid . All wins are added to the players BALANCE. The SILVER SAFE and GOLD SAFE above the reels represents the NOTE GRABBER (GOLD SAFE) and the NOTE PICKER (SILVER SAFE) features. Each SILVER or GOLD SAFE symbol which lands anywhere in view is added to the corresponding colored SAFE above the reels and spins the dial to partially or fully open the SAFE. When GOLD SAFE and SILVER SAFE symbols lands on view along with payline win, GOLD SAFE and SILVER SAFE symbols were added to the corresponding colored SAFE first, followed by normal payline wins. Partially opening a SAFE above the reels is purely cosmetic and has no relationship to the chances of opening the SAFE completely when the next SAFE symbol lands in view on the reels. The NOTE GRABBER feature is awarded when the GOLD SAFE symbol above the reels is fully opened. The NOTE PICKER feature is awarded when the SILVER SAFE symbol above the reels is fully opened. If the NOTE GRABBER and the NOTE PICKER features are triggered in the same game, then the NOTE GRABBER feature will take precedence, followed by the NOTE PICKER feature. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “History” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. Logging into your account on the desktop website and selecting the option “Game Logs” inside any game will allow you to access your game play history. Coinciding wins on different paylines are paid and added to players BALANCE. The theoretical return to player of this game excluding JACKPOT is 91.12%. The theoretical return to player of this game including JACKPOT is 94.13%. MALFUNCTION VOIDS ALL PAYS AND PLAYS. Win animations can be skipped by clicking anywhere or by pressing the spacebar (desktop). All pays are shown in USD currency. NOTE GRABBER: The NOTE GRABBER feature is awarded when the GOLD SAFE above the reels is fully opened in the base game. Imitation BANK NOTES fall from top to bottom on the screen. The player must grab the notes by clicking them as they fall. Clicking on a BANK NOTE will reveal either a CASH VALUE or a GOLD NOTE. If a CASH VALUE is revealed, the amount shown is will be added to the TOTAL WIN display box. If a GOLD NOTE is revealed, that revealed note is added to GOLD NOTE display box. Only one GOLD NOTE can be collected during the feature round. At the end of the feature, the GOLD NOTE will reveal a MYSTERY CASH PRIZE which will be added to the TOTAL WIN. The NOTE GRABBER feature is played at the same BET as the triggering game. The CASH VALUE amounts revealed are dependent on the BET amount. The NOTE GRABBER feature will end at a random point. At the end of the NOTE GRABBER feature, the MYSTERY CASH PRIZE collected from GOLD NOTE will be revealed as the GOLD NOTE BONUS. The game's outcome isn't influenced by player 's skill or dexterity, as falling note values are predetermined. NOTE PICKER: The NOTE PICKER feature is awarded when the SILVER SAFE above the reels is fully opened in the base game. The player is shown a grid of 24 imitation BANK NOTES on screen. The player continues selecting BANK NOTES until 3 matching BANK NOTE VALUES are revealed. Once 3 matching BANK NOTES have been revealed, the player is awarded with the amount shown on the matching BANK NOTES. For example if player selects 3 matching BANK NOTES of 100 as their value, then player will be awarded with 100 win amount. The NOTE PICKER feature is played at the same BET as the triggering game. A GOLD NOTE can also be revealed during the NOTE PICKER feature and will award a MYSTERY CASH PRIZE at the end of the feature, in addition to the winning value of the 3 matching BANK NOTES. MYSTERY CASH PRIZE will have a random multiplier range from : 5X, 6X, 7X, 8X, 9X, 10X, 12X, 14X, 16X, 18X, 20X, 25X, 30X, 40X, 50X, 60X, 70X, 80X which will be multiplied by BET amount and will be awarded to the player. GOLD NOTE GRAB: During the base game, there is a possibility that the player will be awarded with GOLD NOTE GRAB feature. Player will be awarded with GOLD NOTE GRAB feature only if there is no GOLD SAFE or SILVER SAFE symbol in the view. If player grabs the GOLD NOTE, player will be awarded with one of the following features NOTE GRABBER NOTE PICKER GOLD NOTE BONUS The multipliers available for GOLD NOTE BONUS in base game are 1X, 2X, 3X, 4X, 5X, 6X, 7X, 8X, 10X, 20X which will be multiplied by BET amount and will be awarded to the player. If the player fails to grab the GOLD NOTE, the note will persist into the next game with no line wins, or safe symbols in view. The persisted GOLD Note will be auto revealed . GOLD NOTE held in GOLD NOTE GRAB feature persist by BET placed and are platform dependent. If NOTE GRABBER or NOTE PICKER FEATURE triggers from GOLD NOTE, the multipliers available for GOLD NOTE in those features will range from 5X, 6X, 7X, 8X, 9X, 10X, 12X, 14X, 16X, 18X, 20X, 25X, 30X, 40X, 50X, 60X, 70X, 80X .
