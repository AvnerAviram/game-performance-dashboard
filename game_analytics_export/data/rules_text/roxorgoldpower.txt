Gold Power RAINING REELS A cascading feature where you can win multiple times on every wager. Winning symbols are removed and replaced with new symbols; leading to exciting chain reactions and more potential wins! In Gold Power, each cascade powers the Power Meter (see below). With each cascade you climb towards higher win multipliers until you reach the top. ALL PAYS Starting with the left most re el, combinations of the same symbol (or wilds) on consecutive reels create a payout according to the paytable above POWER METER Each cascade powers the Power Meter a little more - gradually unlocking larger cascade win multipliers until it reaches the top. Each win will have the active multiplier applied to it before it is added to your total and the meter charges again. Meter Upgrades Each new wager has the potential to power up the Power Meter. When this happens, the multiplier values on the Power Meter are upgraded. Once upgraded, these values stay throughout the cascades. All meters start at x1. The top multiplier on the most powerful meter is x25. Return to Player RTP: 95.00%. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wageri game. Total bet is 25 x coin size. RULES All wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only the highest paying combination for each symbol is paid per spin. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
