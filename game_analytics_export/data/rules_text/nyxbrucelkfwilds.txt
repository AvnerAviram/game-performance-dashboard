Bruce Lee Kung Fu Wilds Welcome to BRUCE LEE KUNG FU WILDS featuring DRAGONS MYSTERY, WILD NUDGES, KUNG-FU WILDS and ONE INCH PUNCH JACKPOT. DRAGON MYSTERY On any spin, DRAGON MYSTERY symbols can land to reveal all of the same symbol - including wilds! WILD NUDGE If a wild lands on reel 1 or 6, the wild will nudge to fill the entire reel. These wilds are given a multiplier equal to the number of wilds originally landed. For example if 2 wilds land they will nudge to fill the reel and become worth x2 on qualifying wins. KUNG-FU WILDS Landing 6 or more Wilds triggers the KUNG-FU WILDS feature. All wilds and multipliers become locked and 3 respins are awarded. During respins, any wilds landed will be held, and respins will be reset to 3. Any wilds can land with a multiplier between 1 and 3. Respins will end when no respins are remaining or the grid is filled. HOLLYWOOD WILD SPIN When respins end, the HOLLYWOOD WILD SPIN will be awarded. All KUNG-FU WILDS will remain locked. All unlocked positions will spin and regular symbols will land. After the spin, all wins will be paid and the round is over. Wins will be paid using the base bet. If all positions are filled with wilds during KUNG-FU WILDS respins then no HOLLYWOOD WILD SPIN is awarded and the player wins on a full grid of wilds! ONE INCH PUNCH JACKPOT Jackpot symbols can appear on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5. Landing any jackpot symbols fills the WARRIORS GOLD POT and can trigger the jackpot feature. Once the jackpot has been triggered, 12 pots will be presented. Picking the pots reveals a GRAND, MAJOR, MIDI or MINI jackpot. Collecting 3 of the same jackpot awards the jackpot and ends the round. MINI Jackpot will award x10 base stake MIDI Jackpot will award x50 base stake MAJOR Jackpot will award x100 base stake GRAND Jackpot will award x1000 base stake SUPER BET Activate SUPER BET to increase the chances of landing more wilds. SUPER BET costs 50% extra per spin. BUY PASS You can choose your desired bet and buy a spin where 6 or more wilds will land and trigger KUNG-FU WILDS. BUY PASS costs 70x your stake. SUPER BUY PASS You can choose your desired bet and buy a spin that guarantees KUNG-FU WILDS with x3 wilds on reel 1 and 6. SUPER BUY PASS costs 200x your stake. HOW TO CALCULATE PAYLINE WINS To calculate your total payline win, calculate all payline wins and add them together. To calculate a single payline win, count the number of identical symbols lined up in sequence on the line starting from the left most reel. If three or more identical symbols are lined up, find the win value in the dynamic Pay Table. There you can find the value for all symbols for 3 in a row, 4 in a row and 5 in a row. Only the highest winning combination per line is paid out. All symbol payouts are calculated using the base bet. If the winning combination starts with three wilds, there can be many ways to interpret the win. In this scenario, only the highest win combination is paid out. MAX WIN Any combination of wins in a single game is limited and will not exceed $250,000.00. It may not be possible to reach this limit in a single game from every bet configuration.
