El Tesoro de la Jungla MAIN GAME In this game, each prize could either be risked, by pressing the play button, or to be collected, by pressing the collect button. In case the prize is risked, it will either be given the amount of a one level higher prize or the equivalent amount of bonus. Once the line of the three bells is reached, the game becomes an up down, where the possible prizes go up or down in the prize plan. UPPER GAME In order to play this game it will be required to have enough bonus and credits. Whenever a prize is obtained, unless it is a blue 7 prize, the player will be offered to either risk a portion of the prize or to collect it, until there are no more portions left. Choosing to risk a portion may allow the player to either obtain the double of that portion or the equivalent value of that portion in bonus. NUDGES The advance function allows the player to move reels forward one place seeking a winning combination. The number of advances can be between 1 and 4. HOLDS Allows up to two reels to be held up during a game to increase the probability of getting prize. AUTO When nudges are activated, it allows you to rotate the reels automatically, always resulting in the best option for the player. If the button is permanently lit, this means that the auto option is activated. Otherwise, if the button is flashing, this indicates that the auto option is not activated. AUTO PLAY Activate the risk function in main and upper game. The maximum prize that can be obtained in a minigame is the maximum prize of the bet in which the game has been made. A MINI-GAMES ACCESSED RANDOMLY WHEN YOU GET CERTAIN PAYLINES AND YOU HAVE THE REQUIRED GEMS UNLOCKED. JUNGLE BONUS GAME 3 hats in main game give Access to the jungle. Draw a prize GEMS COUNTER Indicate the gems achieved. Find the gems in the mini game to get access to the next mini game. PLATFORM BONUS GAME Stop the platform for move along. Each platform have a prize. Maximum prize that can be achieved is the maximum prize of the current bet. BRIDGE BONUS GAME Must have 1 gem unlocked. Find the correct way. Select a bridge and win a prize. TREASURES BONUS GAME Must have 2 gems unlocked. Take the treasures from the place, press 'play' to sum up the best prize. SECRET ROOM BONUS GAME Must have 3 gems unlocked. Take the gem for discover the treasure. Choose one to win a prize. ADDITIONAL FEATURES CUMULATIVE GAME Accumulate fires and you will have an extra winner. SEVEN MIXED In high bets you can get combinations of different 7's and get a random prize. VERTICAL LINES Get the totem figures and you can play 8 lines in top game. Malfunctions will void all prizes.
