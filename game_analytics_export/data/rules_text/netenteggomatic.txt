EggOMatic™ Game Rules EggOMatic™ is a 5-reel, 3-row, 20-line video slot featuring Wild substitutions, Free Spins and 4 different feature eggs – a Spreading Wild Egg, Free Spins Egg, Coin Win Egg and a Surprise Egg. The game is played with 20 bet lines (fixed), 1-10 bet levels and different coin values. The bet level is set using the LEVEL selector. The coin value is set using the COIN VALUE selector. COINS displays the number of coins the player has available to wager. MAX BET plays the game at 20 bet lines, highest bet level, and the current coin value. AUTOPLAY automatically plays the game for the selected number of rounds. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the PAYTABLE . Alternatively, click any symbol on the reels during the main game to view a mini paytable for that symbol. Bet level is the number of coins bet per bet line. A bet line win in coins is equal to the value shown on the PAYTABLE multiplied by the bet level. A bet line win in currency is equal to the win in coins multiplied by the coin value. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Bet line wins pay if in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added. All coin wins apply to bet line wins and Coin Win Eggs only. Simultaneous wins on more than one feature egg are added together. The EggOMatic™ and Conveyor Belt The game features the EggOMatic™ egg dispenser loaded with different feature eggs; a Spreading Wild Egg, Free Spins Egg, a Coin Win Egg and a Surprise Egg. The feature contained in the egg on the conveyor belt above the reels is activated when a Wild symbol appears randomly on the reel below. Spreading Wild Eggs turn all adjacent and diagonal symbols into additional Wild symbols. The additional Wild symbols from the Spreading Wild do not activate any feature eggs on the conveyor belt above them. Free Spins Eggs activate the number of Free Spins displayed on the egg. Coin Win Eggs add coin wins to the total bet line win. Wins with Coin Win Eggs are equal to the value displayed on the egg. The Surprise Egg contains one of the feature eggs. The EggOMatic™ egg dispenser is located to the right of the reels and the conveyor belt is located directly above the reels. The conveyor belt is empty at the start of the game. Each spin can deliver a random feature egg from the EggOMatic™ onto the conveyor belt above the rightmost reel. Subsequent spins at the same bet level and coin value, move the egg along the conveyor belt one reel to the left per spin. If the current bet is raised and you click spin, all eggs on the conveyor belt will be removed. A maximum of 5 eggs can appear on the conveyor belt at the same time. Wild and Spreading Wild Wild symbols can appear on any reel and substitute for all symbols in the main game and in Free Spins. Wild symbol and Spreading Wild symbols substitute for the highest possible winning combination on a bet line according to the PAYTABLE . In the main game and in Free Spins, Spreading Wilds are activated when the Spreading Wild Egg drops onto a regular Wild symbol appearing on the reel and substitute for all symbols except for regular Wild symbols. The Spreading Wild symbol turns all adjacent and diagonal symbols into additional Wild symbols. Free Spins Free Spin eggs on the conveyor belt directly above a Wild symbol activate Free Spins. The number of Free Spins won is a random amount and is displayed on the egg according to the PAYTABLE . At the start of Free Spins, the EggOMatic™ will deliver 4 eggs onto the conveyor belt. A fifth egg is added while the reels are spinning. The next spin and each subsequent spin places a new egg on the conveyor belt, so eggs are always moving along the conveyor belt. Free Spins are played at the same bet level and coin value as the round that activated Free Spins. Free Spin wins are added to any bet line wins. Additional Free Spins are activated if there is at least one Free Spins Egg on the reels in the Free Spin. Additional Free Spins won are automatically added to the current Free Spins. The TOTAL WIN field includes any wins from the main game added to any wins from Free Spins. At the end of Free Spins, the game returns to the round that activated Free Spins. The conveyor belt reverts back to the state it had when Free Spins was activated. Game Functions The table below lists the different buttons found in the game and describes their functions.
