Spartan Fire Spartan Fire is a Bet 40, 40 line 4x5 Reel slot game with Spartan Spin, Picking Screen and Free Game bonus. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols by matching symbols on at least reels 1 and 2. Payouts depend on the number of coins played and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 95.85% with any bet. Game Rules: Free Games Rules Free Games are played with any ‘new’ options chosen in the Shield Pick Screen. If both {WI} and {LD} are selected in the Shield Picking Screen, {WILD MASK}, {WILD WOMAN}, {WILD MAN} substitute for all except {SHIELD}. If only one or none of {WI} and {LD} are selected in the Shield Picking Screen, {WILD MASK} substitutes for all except {SHIELD}. 3, 4 or 5 {SHIELD} retriggers and accumulates Spartan Fire Bonus. Shield Pick Rules A pick screen is displayed with 10 shields that may reveal: WI, LD, +1 Free Game, +2 Free Games, 2x, 3x, 5x or Crossed Swords. 6 Free Games are awarded with applicable Multipliers, Wilds and Free games. If both {WI} and {LD} are picked, 2 extra wilds are activated during Free Games. Any Multiplier picked multiplies all wins during Free Games with a chance to win up to 30x. Picking two {Crossed Swords} end the picking stage and Free Games begin. Spartan Spin {WOMAN} and {MAN} become {WILD WOMAN} and {WILD MAN} respectively for 1 spin. {WILD MASK}, {WILD WOMAN}, {WILD MAN} substitute for all symbols except {SHIELD}. Spartan Spin is played randomly and may appear during Base Game and/or Free Games. For Free Games, it only may appear when only one or none of the {WI} and/or {LD} options are chosen from Shield Pick screen. RULES Play bet 40 for 40 lines. WILD MASK is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER. All wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER which pays any. All wins multiplied by bet multiplier, except SCATTER which are multiplied by the total bet. All wins on selected paylines only. Wins on different paylines are added. Highest win only on each selected payline. Payouts are made according to the Paytable. 3, 4 or 5 SCATTER triggers Spartan Fire Bonus. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
