Seven 7's SEVEN 7's™ is a single reel 1 line slot machine including: Regular Symbols 2X Wild Multiplier Symbol 'BAR' Symbol CONTINUWIN™ - Added Reels Feature Game Rules General A single payline is permanently active. Your bet multiplier is equal to the number of credits played divided by 0.20. For example, 0.40 credits bet per spin = 0.40 / 0.20 = a bet multiplier of 2. All payout tables display the number of credits that are won for each combination for the current bet multiplier. All payout table win amounts in PlayCheck are displayed in coins. 2 or more matching symbols on the payline represent a winning combination. Only the highest regular winning combination is paid per line. Regular Symbols Matching symbols must be on an active payline. Payouts depend on your bet multiplier per spin. 2X Wild Multiplier Symbol Is a wild multiplier and substitutes for all paying symbols. Each wild multiplier symbol included in a payline win consisting of 6 symbols or less multiplies the pay for that win by 2. The wild multiplier appears on reels 2, 3, 5 and 6 only. 'BAR' Symbol This symbol is a ‘BAR’ symbol and does not pay. CONTINUWIN™ Added Reels Feature The base game includes a single payline and a gameboard consisting of 1 reel, with 1 symbol row. Landing a ‘7’ on the payline adds a new spinning reel. Additional spinning reels that land a ‘7’ or wild multiplier on the payline continue adding spinning reels for up to 7 reels total. The gameboard resets to 1 reel at the beginning of each game. Winning combinations are highlighted on the reels. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you may play again. Paytable Achievements Feature This feature may not be available in the game you are playing. Tracks your win status by highlighting symbol combinations you have won. Achievements are highlighted on the payout table with a check mark. Return to Player (RTP) Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of total money bet by players in a specific game, that is paid out as winnings over time.
