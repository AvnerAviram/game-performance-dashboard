Wolf 500g WOLF 500G GAME DESCRIPTION Wolf 500G is themed on the wolf and moon. WOLF 500G offers numbers drawing feature called the "Wolf Feature", once the bonus is triggered giving player a chance where each bonus symbol will award 3, 6, 9, 12, 15 or 30 free games. Player could win up to 500 free games. The game is a five-reel, 30 Win lines video slot. During the normal game, if one or more SCATTER symbols stop on the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd reels, respectively, the Wolf feature will be triggered. If Wolf Feature is triggered with 15 bonus symbols, 50 extra free games are awarded as the "Full Moon Special". HOW TO PLAY Select your bet. Press the Spin button to start a game cycle. UI: - BUTTONS Title Icon Action Spin Initiates a reel spin. Spin Stop Stops the reel spin immediately (wherever applicable). Autoplay Displays the Autoplay settings screen to show all available options to initiate multiple spins simultaneously. Autoplay Stop Press to stop Autoplay session manually. Title Icon Action Bet Displays the bet options for the player to select the level of their Bet. Menu Displays a settings screen with the following options to choose: Bet, Info and Help. Audio Choose to enable/disable the sound. Turbo Spin Turn on/off to change the speed of the Spin to a faster/normal spin. SETTING BUTTONS Title Icon Behavior Paytable & Help Choose to view the Paytable and feature descriptions for the game. Bet Settings Displays the Bet per line Options available for the player to select and change the level of their bet. Setting Close Close the window. Title Icon Behavior Max Display the maximum bet. [Important]: Autoplay and turbo may not be available in the game you are playing. METERS AND BUTTONS Bet – Shows the value of bet of the current setting. Bet per line – Shows the current bet per line. Spin Button – Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Stop Button. Press the Stop Button to stop the reels immediately. Max:-Display the maximum bet. Autoplay – Player can set the game to spin without the need to press the spin button every time. You can choose the number of spins to play automatically by selecting through the various options available in the menu. The loss limit (wherever applicable) may avert you from losing above set limit during autoplay session. Autoplay can be manually stopped by pressing Stop Autoplay button. Win – Displays the win for the current or last win paid. All wins are shown in currency. Credits – Displays the balance amount of the player. WOLF FEATURE [SCATTER] appearing on 3 or more consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel trigger WOLF FEATURE. When WOLF FEATURE is triggered, a number appears on each [SCATTER] that is involved in triggering the FEATURE. The sum of the numbers that appeared is awarded as free games in WOLF FREE GAMES. 3, 6, 9, 12, 15 or 30 free games are awarded for each [SCATTER]. If WOLF FEATURE is triggered with 15 [SCATTER], 50 extra free games are awarded as FULL MOON SPECIAL. The number of free games awarded for 1 set of WOLF FREE GAMES ranges from 9 to 500 games. After the number of free games is decided, the player may select either WOLF FREE GAMES or WOLF SPIN BONUS in which a randomly selected credit award is won. WOLF FEATURE cannot be triggered again during WOLF FREE GAMES and WOLF SPIN BONUS. WOLF FREE GAMES WOLF FREE GAMES consists of the number of free games decided at WOLF FEATURE. Different reels are used during WOLF FREE GAMES. All bets during WOLF FREE GAMES are same as the game that triggered the WOLF FEATURE. During WOLF FREE GAMES, all wins are multiplied by either 2, 3, 4, or 5. WOLF FREE GAMES can be re-triggered during WOLF FREE GAMES when [SCATTER] appears on 3 or more consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel. When WOLF FREE GAMES is retriggered, a number appears on each [SCATTER] that is involved in re-triggering the FREE GAMES. The sum of the numbers that appeared is awarded as additional free games. 3, 6, 9, 12, 15 or 30 additional free games are awarded for each [SCATTER]. If WOLF FREE GAMES is retriggered with 15 [SCATTER], 50 extra free games are awarded as FULL MOON SPECIAL. WOLF SPIN BONUS 1 randomly selected credit award is won from the 10 credit awards on the screen. After the selected credit award is won, WOLF SPIN BONUS ends. WOLF SPIN BONUS credit award: The number of awarded WOLF FREE GAMES multiplied by either 10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90 or 100 and multiplied by the bet per line. GAME RULES All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel. All wins are multiplied by bet per line except scatter. Only highest scatter winning combination is paid. All wins are awarded on win lines except [SCATTER]. Only Highest win paid on each line. Wins on different lines are added. Free Games and Bonus wins are added to line wins. Each reel contains consecutive symbols that randomly change into any one symbol at the start of reel spin. All consecutive symbols on the reels change into the same symbol. LOBBY BAR: Home: - Player can return to lobby by selecting this button. Sound: - Choose to enable/disable the game sounds. REALITY CHECK Reality check (wherever applicable): The Reality check feature may displays as a periodic in-game notifications for the real money players. Game gets suspended whenever the check appears during game play and only resumes once the player has acknowledged the check and has taken displayed actions. “Back to game”:- Notifying player to resume the game “To Lobby”:- Quits the current game (after a final prompt) and back to the Operators Main Game Menu Screen.
