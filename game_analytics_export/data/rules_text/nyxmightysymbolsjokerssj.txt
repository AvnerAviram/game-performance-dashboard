Mighty Symbols: Jokers Santa's Jackpots The game has 15 independent reels on a 3x5 grid. There are no regular symbols in the base game and prizes can only be won in the Bonus Game. All Bonus symbols pay only in the Bonus Game. Drawing at least 6 Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus symbols activates the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus symbols include Cash, Cash Infinity™, MINI, MINOR, and MAJOR Jackpot symbols, Collector, Mystery and Jackpot Mystery symbols. Drawing at least 6 Jokers Rampage™ Bonus symbols activates the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. Jokers Rampage™ Bonus symbols include Joker, Mystery Joker, Mystery Jackpot Joker and Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbols. SPECIAL FEATURES CASH INFINITY™ Cash Infinity™ symbols may randomly appear in the base game and stick to the reels until the end of the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. All sticky Cash Infinity™ symbols are locked in place while other symbols are spun. If the Cash Infinity™ symbol is drawn on the reels, it increases the chances of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. Cash Infinity™ symbols award prizes ranging from 5x-10x player's bet. However, the prize will be paid at the end of the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game. STICKY TO INFINITY™ Two types of Mystery Jackpot Joker and Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbols may randomly appear in the base game: symbols that stick to the reels for 5-9 spins, symbols with the Sticky to Infinity™ feature that stick to the reels until the end of the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. All Mystery Jackpot Joker and Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbols are locked in place while other symbols are spun. If Mystery Jackpot Joker and Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbols are drawn on the reels, they increase the chances of activating the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. HOLD THE JACKPOT™ BONUS GAME The game is played on 15 reels. All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. During Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game only Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus symbols can be drawn on the reels. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-5x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols may randomly appear during the Bonus Game and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash and Cash Infinity™ symbols and multiplies them randomly by 1-10x. Mystery symbol can transform into any other Hold The Jackpot™ Bonus symbol, apart from Cash Infinity™ symbols. Jackpot Mystery symbol can only transform into MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols. Mystery and Jackpot Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Bonus symbol values, including MINI, MINOR, MAJOR Jackpots, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 15 Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 750x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Bonus symbol values are not added to it. JOKERS RAMPAGE™ BONUS GAME Drawing at least 6 Jokers Rampage™ Bonus symbols activates the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. During Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game only Jokers Rampage™ Bonus symbols can be drawn on the reels. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Mystery Joker symbol can transform into Joker, MINI, MINOR or MAJOR Joker Jackpot symbols. Mystery Jackpot Joker symbol can only transform into MINI, MINOR or MAJOR Joker Jackpot symbols. Mystery Joker and Mystery Jackpot Joker symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. At the end of the Bonus Game, a multiplier ranging from 2-5x is randomly drawn for the Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbol. If the Joker Mystery Multiplier™ symbol is a part of a winning combination, it multiplies the value of that bet line win. MINI, MINOR, and MAJOR Joker Jackpot symbols can only be drawn using Mystery Joker or Mystery Jackpot Joker symbols and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. When the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Joker symbols bet line wins, including Joker Mystery Multiplier™ if applicable, and Joker Jackpot wins, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Only the highest win per active bet line is paid. Joker and Joker Jackpot symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 15 Jokers Rampage™ Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 750x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Joker symbol bet line wins and Joker Jackpot wins are not added to it. Symbols pay from leftmost to right on consecutive reels. CHANCE LEVEL™ The game is played with the base bet set by default. However, the Chance Level™ feature can be enabled during the base game and you can select one of 3 levels: Chance Level™ x2 – the base bet is multiplied by 2, giving you a 2x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game and the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x5 - the base bet is multiplied by 5, giving you a 5x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game and the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. Chance Level™ x10 - the base bet is multiplied by 10, giving you a 10x higher chance of activating the Hold the Jackpot™ Bonus Game and the Jokers Rampage™ Bonus Game. The Chance Level™ feature does not affect the paytable, thus symbol values remain the same as for the base bet in both the base game and the Bonus Game. Sticky symbols are temporarily removed from the reels when changing Chance Level™ and only appear on the Chance Level™ they were obtained on. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Volatility Levels™ Low volatility: The payouts you receive in this mode are more frequent but in most cases smaller. This level is perfect for players who prefer shorter game sessions. Standard volatility: This mode is a mixture between Low and High and is dedicated to players who are looking to feel the thrill of big wins in a fair frequency. High volatility: In this mode you can hit BIG WINs but they are not as frequent as winning in Low mode. High is an excellent mode for players who are not afraid of taking a risk to win big. GAMBLE FEATURE The Gamble feature gives a chance to double each win. The Gamble feature can be activated only with the current win value during the ongoing game session. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. Each draw is independent of the previous one and has the same probability of winning. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the current win counter and returns to the main game. The Gamble feature can be played for a total of 7 rounds. The number of rounds in the Gamble feature depends on the max win available in the game. The win value from doubling up in the next round cannot be higher than: USD 75,000 Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
