Fire In The Hole 2 Game Rules Fire In The Hole 2 is a 6-reel video slot featuring up to 6 rows and a minimum of 64 ways to win. The game includes multiple mechanics such as Collapsing Mine, xBomb Wild Multiplier, xSplit, Buried Features, Wild Mining, xBet, and Lucky Wagon Spins. Winning combinations are formed from left to right on adjacent reels. A Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Bonus symbols and helps create the highest possible winning combinations. The theoretical return to player (RTP) is approximately 94.08%, with a maximum simulated payout of 65,000 times the base bet. Payouts The payout is based on the number of matching symbols appearing on adjacent reels. Additional rows unlock with each collapse up to a maximum of 6 rows. Collapses occur after wins, xBomb explosions, or Wild Mining. Winning symbols are removed and replaced by new symbols dropping from above. Lucky Wagon Spins Triggered by 3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols. The feature starts with 3 spins and resets when coins land. Coins have values based on the bet, and enhancers appear on the top row such as Coin Values, Multipliers, Dynamite, Beer, and Dwarf. Upgrade Feature Coins landing on upgrade positions remain active for additional spins. Certain enhancers become persistent until the feature ends. Enhancers Coin Values set coin amounts. Multipliers increase coin values. Dynamite removes dirt, doubles coins, or activates chests. Beer reactivates old coins. Dwarf collects all values in a column. Collect Chests Activated by Dynamite, these collect values from their column for every spin. xBomb Wild Multiplier Wild symbols explode and remove adjacent symbols while increasing a global multiplier by +1 per explosion. The multiplier has no upper limit. Wild Mining Triggered when matching symbols align without forming a win. These symbols transform into Wilds depending on their count. Buried Features Dirt symbols may contain Wilds, xSplit, or Bonus symbols, which activate once the dirt is removed. xSplit Splits symbols across a row, doubling their count. Cannot affect Bonus or xSplit symbols. xBet Costs 2.5× the base bet and guarantees at least one Bonus symbol while removing all dirt from the reels. Bonus Buy – Guaranteed Dwarf Costs 3600× the base bet and starts the bonus with a guaranteed persistent Dwarf feature. General Rules Wins are calculated by multiplying the symbol value with the number of winning ways. Only the highest win per way is paid. Coinciding wins across multiple combinations are paid. Different reel configurations may apply depending on the game mode. The game round ends if the total win reaches 65,000× the bet. Malfunctions void all wins and gameplay. Unfinished rounds are automatically closed after a set period, and winnings are paid out. Gameplay Information Autoplay allows automatic spins with customizable stop conditions such as balance limits or win thresholds. Game history allows players to review past rounds, including total bet, total win, and replay options. Settings allow customization of sound, gameplay speed, and interface preferences. The balance indicator displays available funds, and bets must be within the allowed limits. All payouts are displayed in the same currency as the placed bet. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
