Megaways Megaways under license from Big Time Gaming. Each spin can have up to 117,649 ways to win per reel. Wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbols, starting on reel 1, regardless of size. 3.2. Diamond symbol Landing 3 Diamond symbols anywhere on the reels will trigger 15 Free Spins. 5 more Free Spins will be added for each additional Diamond symbol, up to a maximum of 30 Free Spins . Any Diamond after the 3rd Diamond symbol will be shown as +5. 3.3. Wild Wild substitutes for all symbols, except Diamond , Mystery or Bonus symbols. Wild symbols can only be found on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 in the top tracker. 3.4. Mystery symbol Mystery symbols transform into any symbol except Wild , Diamond or Bonus . Mystery symbols transform into the same symbol. Note: Mystery symbols are only available in Mega Mystery reels. 3.5. Mega Max symbol Only available in Free Spins top tracker in main game reels! Collect 3 yellow Star Bonus symbols to unlock Mega Max Megaways reels. 3.6. Mega Mystery symbol Only available in Free Spins top tracker in main game reels! Collect 3 red Star Bonus symbols to unlock Mega Mystery reels. 3.7. Top Tracker The horizontal tracker on top of reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 will add a symbol to each reel it is above. 3.8. Cascading Wins Symbols spin into position to form a number of Megaways . Any win in view will start a Cascade. Symbols in winning combinations will explode and new symbols will drop into position. Cascades will continue until no new wins are formed. Winning symbols on the horizontal top tracker will be replaced by symbols shifting from right to left. Note: During cascading wins, the number of ways is fixed at the same amount as on the initial winning spin. Diamond symbols cannot be broken and will remain on screen until no more wins are formed. 3.9. Free Spins Landing 3 Diamond symbols anywhere in view will trigger 15 Free Spins . Each additional Diamond symbol will add +5 Free Spins , up to a maximum of 30 spins. During Free Spins there will be 3 reel sets, Main reels, which will always be in play and two locked reels, Mega Mystery & Mega Max Megaways . Collect 3 Red Star Bonus symbols to unlock Mega Mystery Symbols reels for one spin with the modifier active. Collect 3 Yellow Star Bonus symbols to unlock Mega Max Megaways reels for one spin with the modifier active. Both reels can be triggered together in which case Mega Mystery plays out first, then Mega Max Megaways . Upon finishing either reels, they will lock and the player will need to collect 3 more Bonus symbols to activate them again! Free Spins can be retriggered with 3 Diamond symbols, only found in the top tracker on Mega Mystery and Mega Max Megaways reels! 3.9.1. Mega Max Megaways Mega Max Megaways can be triggered on any spin. This will force the reels into a 117,649 way outcome. The multiplier starts at x1 and increases by one after every cascading win. Winnings are multiplied by the multiplier active at the time of the win. The multiplier ends when no more wins are made. Mega Max Megaways uses Mega Max Megaways reels . The multiplier is only active for the current paid spin, and will NOT be carried over to Mega Max Megaways or Mega Mystery Reels in Free Spins (where a new unlimited multiplier is active). 3.9.2. Mega Mystery Symbols Mega Mystery Symbols can be triggered on any spin. This will land Mystery symbols which will transform into the same paid symbol. The multiplier starts at x1 and increases by one after every cascading win. Winnings are multiplied by the multiplier active at the time of the win. The multiplier ends when no more wins are made. Mega Mystery Symbols uses Mega Mystery Symbols reels . The multiplier is only active for the current paid spin, and will NOT be carried over to Mega Max Megaways or Mega Mystery Reels in Free Spins (where a new unlimited multiplier is active). 3.9.3. Unlimited Multiplier An Unlimited win multiplier, is active during Free Spins . After every cascading win during Free Spins , the multiplier will increase by x1. The new multiplier will apply to the next free spins win, whether it comes during the same series of cascades or on a later free spin. 3.10. Win cap The maximum total win in a single game is capped at 1,000,000 coins. Any wins shown and played in the background after the win cap is applied on a single game are not awarded. Wins after the win cap will be shown as 0 in the Game History. If the win cap is reached during Free Spins feature, the feature will end regardless of spins remaining.
