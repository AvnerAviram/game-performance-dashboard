Temple of Ausar Game Description Let us take you to a mystical world, a world of powerful gods and pharaohs. Ausar, god of the afterlife is the main character in the long awaited sequel to “Temple of Isis”, our first and one of our most successful games. With amazing graphics, exciting gameplay and plenty of chances to win big this timeless game will win over your customers every time. How to Play Ready to go on an adventure? Simply choose your initial wager by clicking on the “chip stack” icon, then choose how many pay lines you want to play (up to 25) and how much you want to stake per line. Once you’re ready to go hit SPIN to get those reels rolling. Temple of Ausar also offers an Auto Play feature, just choose how many games you’d like to play in a row (between 10-100). Once you start playing be sure to look out for the Isis scatter as well as the Ausar substitute, which can help complete winning combinations! And remember, Ausar can replace any symbol on the reels, except for the scatter. Bonus Feature FREE Games To trigger your FREE Games Bonus you’ll have to spin 3 or more Isis Scatters anywhere on the reels. All of your wins will be TRIPLED and in addition, you can reactivate this bonus up to 15 times! RTP The Return to Player for this game is 95.4%.
