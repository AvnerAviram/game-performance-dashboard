Thundering Buffalo RTP: 94.9% VOLATILITY: 10.6 GAME RULES Malfunction voids all pays and play This game is played at 720 ways and all 5 reels are always in play. Select Bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels Payouts are made according to the Paytable amounts for the Credits stated Highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. All 5 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel, or ending with the rightmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to ways wins. Wilds symbols substitute for all symbols except the Free Games Bonus symbol and Scatter symbol. Wild symbols only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. FREE GAMES BONUS 5 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on 5 consecutive reels awards the Free Games Bonus Awards 8 free games. Free Games are awarded on a separate screen with different reels Free games are played at the same bet multiplier as the triggering game. 5 Free Games Bonus symbols appearing in any position on 5 consecutive reels awards 8 additional free games per winning combination. Free Games Bonus ends when 0 Free Games remain or when 240 Free Games have been played. Last modification date: 7/19/2022
