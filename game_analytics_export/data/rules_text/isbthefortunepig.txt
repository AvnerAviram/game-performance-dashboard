The Fortune Pig 1. Overview The goal is to obtain a winning combination on a winning line spread across the reels. How to Play Note: If you do not have enough money to play a game, the bet can be set to the minimum selectable amount through a dialog window informing about this. All winning lines pay from left to right. Winnings on different lines are all added. Only the highest winning combination on each line is paid. Winnings from free spins are added to the total win. The auto spin feature does not automatically start free spins – player input is required. On the info bar(s), you can view the current BET, your BALANCE and your current WIN, in your currency, and your current CREDIT, in coins. 2.1. Wild The Wild symbol is stacked and you can get a Wild in one, two or three positions on the same reel. The Wild symbol can replace any other symbols, except Scatters and Money Bag, to form winning combinations. 3.2. Fortune Free Spins During normal spins, three Scatter symbols anywhere on reels 1, 3 and 5 trigger 8 Fortune Free Spins. During Fortune Free Spins, at the start of each spin, between 2 and 5 reels are selected, randomly, to spin in sync (same symbols on each reel). Note : Fortune Free Spins cannot be re-triggered. Winnings from Fortune Free Spins are added to the total win. During Fortune Free Spins you cannot change the bet. 3.3. Cash Respins Five or more Money Bag symbols anywhere on the reels trigger 3 Cash Respins - these are played on a special set of reels where only Money Bag and Fortune Pigs symbols appear. The re-spins start with the initial Money Bag symbols in place. During Cash Respins, when either a Money Bag symbol or Fortune Pig symbol that collects Money Bags, lands on the reels, the number of re-spins is reset to 3. All Money Bag symbols that land during re-spins remain in place until all re-spins end. In addition, during re-spins, if the Fortune Pig symbol lands on the reels, the values of all Money Bag symbols that are currently present on the reels are accumulated on the position of the Fortune Pig and the re-spins are reset to 3. The re-spins end when no additional Money Bag symbols appear on the special set of reels or when all the positions on the special set of reels are filled. The sum of the values from each position on the special set of reels is added to your balance. Note : The values displayed on Money Bag symbols are in coins. Cash Respins cannot be triggered during Fortune Free Spins. During Cash Respins you cannot change the bet. 4.
