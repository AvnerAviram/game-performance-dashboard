Bubbly Wild Game Features Bubble Line Symbols appearing in a bubble are placed on the Bubble Line. The Bubble Line pays like a normal payline but is multiplied by the Bubble Line Multiplier. The Bubble Line Multiplier is equal to the number of lines played, except when 20 Lines are played the multiplier is increased to x22. Bubble Popper Bonus Pop a bubble to win a prize. All wins are multiplied by the coin size bet. Return To Player (RTP) RTP: 96.02% This RTP is based on playing 20 lines. The RTP is the amount we pay out to players relative to the amount of wagering on the game. For example, if we take $100 of wagers we will, on average, pay out $96.02 of wins. Rules Total bet is the coin size multiplied by the number of lines played. Payline wins pay left to right on consecutive reels from the leftmost reel. Only highest win paid on any active payline. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 3/20/2024
