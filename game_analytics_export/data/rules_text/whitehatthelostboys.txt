The Lost Boys Step 1: Getting Started Click the TOTAL BET button to increase or decrease the amount of your TOTAL BET. Select from the list of available stakes from the Total Bet options (depending on Operator configuration and currency equivalent). Click 'SPIN' to start your game. Step 2: How To Play Press 'SPIN' to start the game (depending on jurisdiction and Operator configuration, Spacebar may also be used to start a game). The reels will spin and eventually stop at random. If the combination of symbols appearing on consecutive reels forms a line of 3, 4 or 5 matching symbols (starting from the leftmost reel) on one of the win lines, then you win. Any winning lines you have will be highlighted and your winnings will be credited to your account balance. Wild Symbol The Wild Symbol can be used to help form wins by substituting for other symbols excluding scatter symbols. Wild symbols can only appear on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 Only the highest win is paid per winning combination. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Scatter and MONEY symbols. Modifiers Modifiers can be triggered randomly during any spin, awarding one of the following modifiers: High Stakes - Randomly adds Cash symbols to the Reels. Bonus Boost - Additional Bonus symbols can be added to the reels. Blood Lust - Wild symbols are randomly added to the reels. One Of Us - Symbols are removed from the reels and new symbols drop in to award a win . Cash Collect During any spin where a Cash symbol lands in view on reels 1-4, if a COLLECT Scatter symbol lands in view on reel 5 then the Cash Collect feature is triggered. The COLLECT symbol will expand to cover the 5th reel and collect the cash values shown on the Cash symbols. Landing any cashpot money symbol (MINI: 10xbet; MINOR: 25xbet; MAJOR: 100xbet; MEGA: 1000xbet) in view will award that pot value. Available Coin cash symbol outcomes: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 7x, 8x, 9x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x, MINI, MINOR, MAJOR and MEGA (Multiplied by Total Bet). RESPIN Collect will collect any cash symbols in view before holding the RESPIN Collect in place and respinning the first 4 reels. Any new Cash Symbols that land in view are also collected by the RESPIN Collect. BOOST Collect will boost any cash symbols in view by up to 5x bet and then proceed to collect any cash symbols. SUPER COLLECT will collect any cash symbols in view up to 5 times. Free Spins 3 Bonus Scatter symbols in view on reels 2, 3 and 4 will trigger the Free Spins Bonus. 3 Bonus symbols in view awards 10 Free Spins. On any spin where a collect symbol lands in view, the prize is awarded for each Coin Cash symbol that also lands in view. Collecting 3 Collect symbols will award 5 additional spins along with a x2 multiplier for any Cash Coin symbols collected. Collecting 6 Collect symbols will award 5 additional spins along with a x3 multiplier for any Cash Coin symbols collected. Collecting 9 Collect symbols will award 5 additional spins along with a x5 multiplier for any Cash Coin symbols collected. Collecting 12 Collect symbols will award 5 additional spins along with a x10 multiplier for any Cash Coin symbols collected. Free Spins continue until all Free Spins are used, where the player is awarded the amount of the bonus winnings accumulated. Free Spins are played automatically at the same number of lines and bet per line as the initiating game. Auto Play (subject to operator configuration and jurisdictional requirements) There is an Autoplay function in the game that will start each spin automatically. By selecting 'AUTOPLAY' you can choose to bet automatically, one after the other. Select your stake as normal then press the 'AUTOPLAY' button to select the number of 'Autoplay' bets you want to play. Select your Loss Limit (maximum amount willing to lose for the autoplay session) and then press PLAY to begin your autoplays (other options are available). Auto Play will turn off if you get into any bonus round including Free Spins, if you reach the set Loss Limit or if a Progressive pot is awarded (where applicable). You can stop Auto Play from continuing onto the next spin by clicking the 'STOP' button. Payout Information and Game Rules Volatility may vary depending on number of games played. Line wins are multiplied by the value bet on the winning line. Wins on different paylines are added. All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels beginning with the left most reel (10 lines always active). Only the highest line win is paid on each line. Multiple scatter features can be won in a single game. Scatter wins are paid in addition to reel wins.
