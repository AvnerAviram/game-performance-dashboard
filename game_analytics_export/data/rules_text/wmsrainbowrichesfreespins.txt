Rainbow Riches Free Spins Game Introduction Rainbow Riches Free spins is a 5 reel game with 3 symbols in view for each reel. How to play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Win lines The default win line selection is 10 win lines. The win lines are not adjustable in this game. Start the game Press SPIN to initiate the game. Help All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. Free Spin Bonus BONUS symbol on reels 1, 3 and 5 initiates the Free Spin Bonus. BONUS symbols are stacked on the first reel and can award 10, 15 or 20 free spins. This also applies to the free spins bonus game play as well, multiple extra spins can be awarded for a retrigger depending on how many symbols are in view on the first reel. During Free Spins Bonus, an alternate set of reels is used. The bet per line remain the same as the spin that triggered the Free Spins Bonus. All Free spin games use the same awards as the base game. The Bonus ends when 0 free spins remain or after 999 free spins have been played. Last modification date: 3/1/2017
