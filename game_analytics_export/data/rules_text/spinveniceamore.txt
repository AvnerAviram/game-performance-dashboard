Venice Amore Basic Game Rules Jackpots The grande, medio and piccolo jackpots are won with any wager by landing on the associated wheel segments. The grande jackpot awards the displayed value plus 100x wager. Jackpots may be won in either the base game or Venice nights free spins bonus. The probability of winning the grande jackpot is proportional to wager. Each wager consists of a bet multiplier upon all 30 lines plus an additional bet equal to 20 x bet multiplier. Only highest win paid per line. All line pays are left to right and adjacent, starting with the leftmost reel, and multiplied by bet multiplier. Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Money Wheel Bonus Within the base game, 3,4 or 5 scattered wheel triggers the piccolo, medio or grande money wheel, respectively. The possible piccolo money wheel awards include 250x 750x bet multiplier, piccolo jackpot and the medio money wheel. The possible medio money wheel awards include 1,000x-2000x bet multiplier, medio jackpot and the grande money wheel. The possible grande money wheel awards include 2,500x 10,000x bet multiplier and the grande jackpot. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Only highest win paid per line. Wins on all lines are added. Payoffs on registered credits only. All pays in currency. Line wins mush occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the left most reel. Last modification date: 8/18/2020
