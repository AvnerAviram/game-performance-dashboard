Burning Stars 3 BURNING STARS 3™ The game has 9 independent reels on a 3x3 grid. Wins are paid for at least 4 identical symbols on any of the reels. Only the highest win is paid. Wild is a paying symbol and substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbol. SPECIAL FEATURES Hold the Jackpot Bonus A draw of at least 3 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Bonus symbols that activated the Hold the Jackpot Bonus and those drawn during the bonus stay on the reels. 3 respins are granted at the beginning of the bonus round. Every time a Bonus symbol is drawn, it resets the number of respins to 3. The bonus ends when all respins are finished or the all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. When Hold the Jackpot Bonus ends, the prize is paid for the number of collected Bonus symbols, according to the paytable. Hold the Jackpot Bonus is played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated the bonus. SPECIAL SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOL Wild is a paying symbol and substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbol. BONUS SYMBOL A draw of at least 3 Bonus symbols during the base game activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
