Casino Island Base Game FEATURE Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. HOW TO PLAY To play with the maximum bet, click the “Max Bet” button. Then the highest bet is chosen and all the lines are selected and the game starts automatically. To choose the number of lines you wish to play, click the minus or plus button below the “Lines” text. Select the bet by clicking the minus or plus button below the “Bet per line” text. Click the "Play" button to start the game. If any of the symbol combinations on the played lines correspond to a combination in the paytable, you win the corresponding amount. When you win, the lines you’ve won on are highlighted as well as the amount that it pays in the paytable. Please note that all symbol combinations are counted from the left to the right. Feature Game The feature game starts when at least two FEATURE symbols are shown on one or more played lines. Select the destination of your choice by clicking on the island you wish to sail to. In the game there are three underwater rocks. If you sail aground your feature win will be lower. However, you always win in the feature game! A maximum of one feature game can be gained per each game. The FEATURE symbol can be found anywhere on a played line. Rock Award: 1 - 3 times the bet per line. Island Award: 5 - 40 times the bet per line. Rock and Island Awards are also multiplied by the number of played lines that trigger the feature. Paylines Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line. Only active paylines can register wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Last modification date: 3/27/2017
