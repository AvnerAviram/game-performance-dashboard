Royal Elephant FESTIVAL MULTIPLIERS Festival Multipliers may occur on any random base game spin that does not trigger the Free Spin Bonus. On a spin with Festival Multipliers, a random multiplier of 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 50x, or 100x is revealed before the reels stop. The multiplier applies to all payline combinations for that single reel stop. FREE SPIN BONUS Three Bonus symbols landing scattered on reels 1, 3, and 5 trigger the Free Spin Bonus. The bonus awards8 free spins and a trigger pay of 2 times the total bet in credits. Festival Multipliers occur on every frees spin and apply a random multiplier of 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 7x, 10x, 15x, 25x, 50x, or 100x to each spin. During the free spins an alternate set of reels is used. MULTIPLY WINS BY BET MULTIPLIER Elephant symbol is 2-high stacks and is present on all 5 reels. Wild symbols appear in stacks on all 5 reels. One or more Wild symbols can appear on the reels to complete a winning symbol combination.
