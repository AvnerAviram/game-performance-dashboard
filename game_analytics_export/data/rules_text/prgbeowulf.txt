Beowulf Characteristics and features All winning combinations, except for combinations with the Beowulf symbol pay from left to right only. Beowulf symbol pays left-to-right and right-to-left All wins are multiplied by bet per line Bets are made in coins per line To control bets, user is able to change the value of a coin in real currency and also increase the number of coins bet per line Balance, bets, wins are all displayed in both coins and real currency at all times There are 12 different symbols in the game. All symbols except for Bonus and Scatter are stacked in groups of the same symbol. 3 high symbols, represented by portrait of characters 3 medium symbols, represented by Viking objects 3 low symbols, represented by suits 1 Wild symbol. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Scatter. Wild does not pay by itself. Scatter symbol – triggers Free Spins feature and pays by itself 3 Scatter - 1x total bet 4 Scatter – 5x total bet 5 Scatter – 25x total bet Free Spins feature Free spins are awarded when three or more scatter symbols are hit anywhere on the reels. Depending on the number of scatter symbols hit, more or less free spins are awarded, as follows: 3 scatters – 10 FS 4 scatters – 15 fs 5 scatters – 20 fs During the free spins round, if more scatter symbols are hit, more free spins are awarded, on the same scheme as in the normal game Super Respin Feature When 4 stacked Beowulf symbols hit on the first reel, they trigger the feature During the feature, regular reels fade away and are replaced by special reels containing only empty spaces and Beowulf symbols User starts with 3 respins. All Beowulf symbols that hit remain on the screen until the end of the round Additional respins are added every time at least one Beowulf symbol hits during a respin When all respins are complete and no more Bewoulf symbols are added to the reels anymore, win is calculated for all combinations, left to right and right to left Paytable Artwork English
