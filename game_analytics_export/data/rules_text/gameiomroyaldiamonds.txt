Royal Diamonds Game Features Free Games Feature Free Games Feature is triggered by any 3 or more scattered 'Diamond'. The feature options are During the Free Games Feature, 'Crest' appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only. Any 3 or more scattered 'Diamond' will trigger the same number of free games and multiplier as the selected feature option. Bet and lines played are the same as the game that triggered this Feature. QUAD Shot Bonus Game Symbols The Major Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Game Symbols win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. The Minor Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Game Symbols win occurs with at least one substitute appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. Royals The Maxi Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Royals win occurs with no substitutes appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. The Mini Bonus is awarded when a 5 of a kind Royals win occurs with at least one substitute appearing in the winning combination and a 'QUAD Shot BONUS' symbol. Win Lines 'Crest' substitutes for all symbols except scatters and 'QUAD Shot BONUS'. 'Diamond' is a scatter. 'Ruby' appears during the Free Games Feature only. Scatters pay in any position. All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels, except scatters and Bonuses. All wins on lit lines only except scatters and Bonuses. Highest win only on each line except scatters and Bonuses. All Wins are added. The game has a return to player of 96.63%.
