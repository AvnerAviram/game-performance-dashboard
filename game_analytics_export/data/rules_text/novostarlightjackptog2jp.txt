Mini Jackpot symbol, Minor Jackpot symbol, Major Jackpot symbol, Grand Jackpot symbol. The Free Games Wheel is a presentation of a predetermined outcome. Starlight Jackpots™ Starlight Jackpots™ can only be won in Free Games. Collect 3 stars per pot to win a jackpot. Grand Jackpot value is 1000x base game Total Bet, and 0.125% of every stake goes directly into the Grand Jackpot. Major Jackpot value is 200x base game Total Bet, and 0.375% of every stake goes directly into the Major Jackpot. Minor Jackpot value is 25x base game Total Bet. Mini Jackpot value is 10x base game Total Bet. Grand and Major jackpots can only be won once per bonus. Mini and Minor jackpots can be won more than once per bonus. Rules Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the paytable. Symbol payout values shown in the paytable are dynamic and change in accordance with the selected bet value. All prizes are for combinations left to right on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel. The leftmost reel is reel 1, the rightmost reel is reel 5. All prizes are for combinations of a kind. Wins are only paid for combinations on selected ways, unless stated otherwise. Only the highest win per way and per scatter combination is paid. Scatter symbols in any position trigger the feature game. Scatter wins and way are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. ALL WAYS PAYS There are 243 ways to win.
