Bobby 7s GAME RULES Play 1 to 25 lines Payouts are made according to the Paytable Payline wins are multiplied by the amount staked per payline Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount staked Scatter wins are added to payline wins Only the highest win on a pay line pays Coinciding wins on different paylines are added All wins occur on selected lines except THIEF, POLICE 7, JUDGE 7, and CROOK 7 All symbols pay Pays left to right on consecutive reels except THIEF, POLICE 7, JUDGE 7, and CROOK 7 BOBBY is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except THIEF, POLICE 7, JUDGE 7, and CROOK 7 THIEF appears on Reels 1, 3 and 5 only POLICE 7 appears on Reels 1, 2 and 3 only JUDGE 7 appears on Reels 2, 3 and 4 only CROOK 7 appears on Reels 3, 4 and 5 only POLICE 7 HI/LO FEATURE The Police 7 Hi/Lo Feature is triggered when POLICE 7 appears anywhere on Reels 1, 2 and 3 at the same time Play the Hi/Lo game and work your way up the Cash Ladder Select 'HI' or 'LO' if you think the next number will be Higher or Lower Correct selections move you up the Cash Ladder An incorrect selection concludes the feature and you are awarded the prize on the Cash Ladder JUDGE 7 MULTIPLIER FEATURE The Judge 7 Feature is triggered when JUDGE 7 appears anywhere on Reels 2, 3 and 4 at the same time A Gavel will appear and will Slam down up to 5 times The number of times the Gavel slams down is the multiplier of your bet CROOK 7 LOOT FEATURE The Crook 7 Feature is triggered when CROOK 7 appears anywhere on Reels 3, 4 and 5 at the same time Select one of the CROOKs by selecting one of the triggering CROOK 7 symbols Selecting a CROOK awards a prize depending on how much Loot is in his bag FREE SPINS FEATURE RULES A Free Spin Feature is awarded when THIEF appears on Reels 1, 3 and 5 at the same time You are immediately awarded a prize of 2X your bet Select one of the THIEF symbols and the number of Free Spins are revealed All Free Spins prizes are DOUBLED The Free Spins Feature can be re-triggered Free Spins are played at the lines and bet of the triggering spin Any of the 7'S FEATURES can be won during the Free Spins Feature Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play The theoretical return to player of this game is 95.00%.
