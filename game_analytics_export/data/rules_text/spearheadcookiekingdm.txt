Cookie Kingdom Cookie Kingdom GAME RULES Cookie Kingdom is a 6x5 layout that “pays anywhere” videoslot featuring cascading reels, Free Spins, Golden Bet option to increase the chance of having Free Spins and Buy Bonus option. Landing total number of matching symbols anywhere on the reels for each spin creates a win according to the paytable for each symbol. Tumble Winnings are active during the game to have the chance of more combos and more payouts. During the base game, landing 4 or more Scatter symbols triggers 10 Free Spins. Cookie Jar and the Cookie Wrap symbols are multiplier symbols that may land anywhere on the reels during free spins. The global multiplier is active during free spins. During free spins, once a win occurs with multiplier symbol(s), the total win multiplied by the total global multiplier at the end of the cascade. In a winning cascade, Cookie Jar symbols increase the total win by adding their multipliers to the global multiplier only for that cascade. Cookie Jar multipliers have no impact on the global multiplier. In a winning cascade, Cookie Wrap symbols increase the total win by adding their multipliers to the global multiplier. Cookie Wrap multipliers increase the global multiplier by adding their multipliers in a winning cascade. Multipliers of Cookie Jar, Cookie Wrap symbols and the Global Multiplier can have an impact on the total win only in a winning round with multiplier symbols. Each of Cookie Jar and Cookie Wrap symbols multipliers can have at most 100x in one spin. Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols during Free Spins awards additional 5 Free Spins.
