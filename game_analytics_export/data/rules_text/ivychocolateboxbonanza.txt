Chocolate Box Bonanza Game Objective: Chocolate Box Bonanza is a six-reel reactions game with cascading wins and symbols pay anywhere on the reel view . Free spins feature is triggers when 4 or more Scatter symbols land on the reel. During free spins 3 or more Scatter symbols appearing anywhere on the reel view retriggers 5 more free spins. How to play: Setting 'BETS': When you enter the game, a default bet is set. You can change the bet value by clicking the or > buttons either side of the BET meter. MAX BET: Click on ‘MAX BET’ to select the maximum possible bet. If your balance is not sufficient, this option will be disabled. Paytable: Paytable can be viewed by clicking on the ‘ i ’ button located in a panel beside the bet panel. You can go back to the game by clicking on the X mark on the Paytable screen. Spin: SPIN button in the lower middle starts the game. Volume: Volume icon will play the game music. Click on the Volume icon to mute the volume. CREDITS: Your remaining game balance after you have placed the desired bet is shown in ‘BALANCE’ meter i.e., the current game balance minus the total bet placed. Repeat bet/change bet: The default bet for the next spin will be the same as the amount you wagered on the previous spin. Just click on 'SPIN' to place the same bet again. To change your bet, increase or decrease the Bet shown on the game screen. Credits: After you have placed the desired bet, your remaining game balance is shown under ‘BALANCE’. Your current game balance is the sum of the amounts displayed under the ‘BALANCE’ meter and the 'BET' meter. Your game balance is shown at the bottom of the game screen. Game Settings: To make use of Game Settings, click on the ‘settings button’ at the bottom of the game screen in between Paytable and Sound icons. Volume: Players can set the volume of the sound effects and ambience by sliding using the volume button. Sound effects: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game win animation sounds. Ambience sound: Toggle between On / Off to enable or disable the game background sound. Space bar to spin: By checking this option, the space bar can be used to trigger a spin. Game Rules: Chocolate Box Bonanza is a six-reel reactions game with cascading wins and symbols pay anywhere on the reel. When a player enters the game, a default BET is set. SPIN begins the game with currently selected bet. Bet amount can be changed by using ‘ ’ buttons. Payouts are paid according to the paytable. Win amounts are rounded off to the closest value. All payouts in this game are multiplied by BET amount. If 8 or more symbols appearing anywhere in reel view, payouts will be paid according to paytable. All possible wins are added to the total win. Only the highest win amount is paid on each win formation. 4 or more Scatter symbols appearing anywhere in the reel view award the Free Spins Feature and scatter payout will be paid according to the paytable. The maximum win in this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency. Further Free spins or cascading wins will be forfeited if the highest win is reached. The min bet is 0.20 CAD and max bet is 20.00 CAD. The theoretical return to the player is 93.26%. Game history can be accessed by logging into player account on the desktop website and choosing menu Game logs inside game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Cascading Wins: Any symbol which forms a win will explode after the prize has been awarded. New symbols are “Cascaded” into the gaps left by the previous winning symbols to produce new possibilities for extra wins. The main reel symbols are dropped from above. Cascading feature will stop automatically if there are no new winning combinations to form a win or a max amount of 250,000 EUR is reached or its equivalent amount in your currency. All wins accumulated during these cascading wins are credited to player after the cascade feature has been completed. Free Spins Feature: The Free Spins Feature will be triggered when 4 or more Scatter symbols appear anywhere in the reel view in the main game. Player will enter the Free Spins Feature round once all the cascading wins are completed in main game. Player will be awarded with 10 free spins. All cascading wins in a single free spin will be added up to TUMBLE WIN meter. During the Free Spins Feature, there is a chance of getting a Nutty Chocolate symbol with some random multiplier value (2x,3x,4x,5x,7x,10x,12x,15x,20x,25x,30x,40x,50x,75x,100x) and it remains on the screen until the end of cascading wins. Nutty Chocolate symbol does not appear in the main game and appears only during freespins and awards a random multiplier value. Win amount of current free spin is multiplied by this random multiplier value. At the end of cascading win, the total Nutty Chocolate multiplier symbols are added up and multiplied by the total win amount from the single free spin. If 2 or more Nutty Chocolate symbols appear on same free spin, then Total win amount will be sum of all random multiplier values on nutty chocolate symbols multiplied by cascaded win amounts of same free spin. During free spins if 3 or more Scatter symbols appear anywhere in the reel views retriggers 5 more free spins.
