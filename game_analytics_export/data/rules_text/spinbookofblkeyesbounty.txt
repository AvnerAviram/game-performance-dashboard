Book of Blackeye's Bounty Game Rules Wins on multiple lines are added together. All line wins pay from left to right on adjacent reels, starting with the leftmost reel. Only highest line win is paid on each active pay line. Only highest scatter win paid per play. Scatter wins are paid in addition to line wins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. This game has a win limit of 8000x your bet. BASIC RULES The base game is played on a 5x3 grid, with 10 lines. Cursed Reels in base game randomly selects a symbol to expand. Side Bet increases scatter probability for an increased cost & faster access to Free Spins. Buy Bonus for direct access to Free Spins. In Free Spins reels can expand up to a 5x7 grid, with 50 lines. Wild symbol Wilds substitutes for all symbols except Scatters CURSED REELS On any random game round, a normal symbol is selected for the cursed reel feature. The selected symbol expands, and pays as a scatter. This feature is a guaranteed win spin. FREE SPINS Landing 3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols activates free spins. After triggering the Free Spins, players can select a symbol to expand across the respective reels they land on. The symbols will only expand if a win can be created. Expanded symbols require the same minimum number of symbols for a win as per normal rules, except that they no longer need to be adjacent - this is a scatter pay. If a win can be created by expanding symbols, normal wins are paid out first, then the selected symbol expands and the newly created wins are paid out. Players can increase the reel area and paylines by collecting book Scatter Symbols. Once each threshold has been achieved by the collection of book Scatters, the reels will expand and additional free spins are awarded before the next spin. The maximum number of free spins which can be collected is 18. The collector counter resets after each threshold is filled. EXPANDED SYMBOL SELECTION OVERVIEW Very high volatility - very high wins but less frequent. Very high volatility - very high wins but less frequent. High volatility - high wins but less frequent. Medium volatility - medium wins but more frequent. GAMBLE Players may gamble for a larger Free Spins start value, unless they win the maximum of 12 Free Spins. When winning Free Spins, Select to Start or Gamble. Gamble may result in all free spins being lost. When losing, the player is returned to the main game. When proceeding to Gamble free spins, the space occupied by the current, previous and/or lower free spin value converts to a losing area. BUY BONUS (WHERE AVAILABLE) The Free Spins Feature can be instantly triggered from the base game, by buying it for 60x current total bet. Book Scatter wins are not awarded when buying Free Spins. When buying the Free Spins feature, the same Free Spins rules apply. The Side Bet cost is excluded from the Buy Bonus cost and bet. SIDE BET (WHERE AVAILABLE) For an additional fee of 30% of the player's current bet, the probability of getting into Free Spins is increased by the same percentage. When using the Side Bet feature to gain early access to Free Spins, the base bet value is used for win calculations - exclusive of the additional 30% fee.
