Fu Lai Cai Lai Stack Wild Luxury GAME DESCRIPTION FU LAI CAI LAI STACK WILD LUXURY, experience an enriching variety of gameplay and surprising riches. Increasing the bet level and credit per play improve several elements of gameplay; such as adding multiplying wild to an increasing number of reels, allowing access to increased jackpot levels. This game has 5 reels, 3 rows and 243 ways to win (EVERY WAY). HOW TO PLAY Select your bet. Press the Spin button to start a game cycle. AUTOPLAY AND TURBO MAY NOT BE AVAILABLE IN THE GAME YOU ARE PLAYING. METERS AND BUTTONS Bet: Displays the current bet. Bet is determined by multiplication of the current coin value with current minimum bet. Coin Value: The coin value determines your bet on the spin. Spin Button: Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Stop Button. Press the Stop Button to stop the reels immediately. Max: Displays the maximum bet. Autoplay (wherever applicable) – Player can set the game to spin without the need to press the spin button every time. You can choose the number of spins to play automatically by selecting through the various options available in the menu. The loss limit (wherever applicable) will avert you from losing above set limit during autoplay session. Autoplay can be manually stopped by pressing Stop Autoplay button. Win: Displays the win for the current or last win paid. All wins are shown in currency. Credits: Displays the credit amount of the player. WILD Substitutes for all symbols except scatters. Appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 according to the bet level played. Does not appear on reel 1. According to the bet level played, [COIN] becomes [GOLD WILD] on the reels as shown below. FREE GAMES FEATURE 3 or more [Scatter] appearing on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel trigger the FREE GAMES FEATURE. 8 free games will be awarded when FREE GAME FEATURE is triggered. Different reels are used during the FREE GAMES. During the FREE GAMES, all symbols on 1 or more reels (reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 according to bet level played) may randomly become [GOLD WILD]. FREE GAMES FEATURE cannot be triggered again during the FREE GAMES FEATURE. If the FREE GAMES FEATURE is triggered in the same game as the JACKPOT FEATURE, the FREE GAMES FEATURE starts after the JACKPOT FEATURE ends. If the JACKPOT FEATURE is triggered during the free games, any remaining free games resume after the JACKPOT FEATURE ends. All bets and ways played during the FREE GAMES FEATURE are the same as the game that triggered the FREE GAMES FEATURE JACKPOT FEATURE The JACKPOT FEATURE can be triggered when playing bet level 2, 3, 4 or 5 only. There is a chance the JACKPOT FEATURE may be triggered when [GOLD WILD] appears. There are 4 JACKPOT levels. [GRAND], [MAJOR], [MINOR] AND [MINI] In the JACKPOT FEATURE, select from 12 ingots until 3 matching symbols corresponding to a JACKPOT level are revealed. When 3 matching JACKPOT level symbols have been revealed, the corresponding JACKPOT level is won and the JACKPOT FEATURE ends According to the bet level played, up to 4 JACKPOT levels may be available GAMBLE FEATURE To enter the gamble game after a win, press the GAMBLE button. Select a Suit, Red/Black or Collect. Select RED/BLACK or a SUIT to gamble the total win. Win is quadrupled if suit selection is correct Win is doubled if red/black selection is correct. Gamble feature is not available for any feature win. Win may be gambled up to 5 times. Gamble will not be offered for the wins awarded during the feature trigger. All wins cannot be gambled if the Jackpot is triggered. Gamble will not be offered during Autoplay. GAME RULES This game requires a minimum of 11 CREDITS to play. Select bet level. All wins on consecutive reels beginning with the leftmost reel. Each symbol appearing on a reel is counted once per winning combination. Coinciding wins are added. [EVERYWAY] wins multiplied by the coin value. [Scatter] wins are added to [EVERY WAY] wins. All wins are multiplied by coin value. Scatter wins are multiplied by total bet. Free Games wins are added to ways wins. Highest win paid only. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. All open game rounds (pending state) will be automatically settled after 30 days. AUTOPLAY SETTINGS Number of spins: Select number of Auto spins as per available choice. Autoplay Advanced Settings (wherever applicable). Loss Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Win Limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Single win limit: Stops autoplay spins if selected limit reached. Stop on any win: Stops on any wins. NET POSITION Net position (wherever applicable): The Net position will display customer’s net position, in the currency of their account or product (e.g., pounds sterling, dollar, Euro) since the session started. Net position is defined as the total of all winnings minus the sum of all losses since the start of the session. REALITY CHECK Reality check (wherever applicable): The Reality check feature may displays as a periodic in-game notifications for the real money players. Game gets suspended whenever the check appears during game play and only resumes once the player has acknowledged the check and has taken displayed actions. “Back to game”:Notifying player to resume the game “To Lobby”:Quits the current game (after a final prompt) and back to the Operators Main Game Menu Screen. MALFUNCTION In case of any malfunction of hardware or software during the game play, all current game bets and payouts are considered void and all bets affected are refunded.
