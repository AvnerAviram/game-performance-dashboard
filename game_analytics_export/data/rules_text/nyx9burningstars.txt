9 Burning Stars 9 Burning Stars The game has 9 independent reels on a 3x3 grid. A win is awarded for drawing at least 5 symbols of a kind anywhere on the reels. Multiplier symbol multiplies the total win by 2x, 3x, 5x, 7x or 10x. The maximum win amount is 1500x bet. Wins for Bonus symbols are paid out only during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game. SPECIAL FEATURES BONUS MATRIX When a Cash symbol appears on a reel, it is saved with its value on an empty corresponding field in the Bonus Matrix. When the Bonus Matrix is full, the Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game is activated. All Cash symbol values saved on the Bonus Matrix are accumulated by a Matrix Collector symbol which is added to the central reel during the Bonus Game. HOLD THE JACKPOT BONUS GAME All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-10x, 12x and 15x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols may randomly appear during the Bonus Game and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash symbols and multiplies them randomly by 1-9x. Mystery symbol can transform into any other Bonus symbol, apart from Matrix Collector symbol. Jackpot Mystery symbol can only transform into MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols. Mystery and Jackpot Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Bonus symbol values, including MINI, MINOR, MAJOR Jackpots, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 9 Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 1500x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Bonus symbol values are not added to it. Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game is played at the same bet level and Volatility Level™ as the spin that activated the bonus. Extreme Volatility Extreme Volatility Using the Buy Feature, Hold the Jackpot Bonus can be activated on Extreme Volatility level, characterized by even bigger wins than other Volatility Levels™.
