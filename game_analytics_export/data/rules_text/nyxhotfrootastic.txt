Hot Frootastic GAME RULES All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels, beginning with the far left reel. Only the highest win is paid on each line played. All line wins are multiplied by the stake per line Wins that occur on all selected lines are added to the total win. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent. The chances of getting a particular outcome are always the same at the start of every game.
