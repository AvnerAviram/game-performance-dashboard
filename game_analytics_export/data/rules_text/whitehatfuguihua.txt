Fu Gui Hua Fu Gui Hua™ Step 1: Getting Started Press the TOTAL BET button to increase or decrease the amount of your TOTAL BET. Select from the list of available stakes from the Total Bet options (depending on Operator configuration and currency equivalent). Press 'SPIN' to start the game. Step 2: How To Play Press 'SPIN' to start the game (depending on jurisdiction and Operator configuration, Spacebar may also be used to start a game). The reels will spin and eventually stop at random. If the combination of symbols appearing on consecutive reels forms a line of 3, 4 or 5 matching symbols starting from the leftmost reel on one of the win lines, then you win. Any winning lines you have will be highlighted and your winnings will be credited to your account balance. 2 Bonus Scatter symbols in view on reels 1 and 3 with a special bonus symbol on reel 5 awards one of three Free Spins Features. 6 or more MONEY Scatter Symbols in view awards Thunder Spins. Wild Symbol The Wild Symbol can be used to help form wins by substituting for other symbols excluding Scatter symbols and MONEY Scatter Symbols. Only the highest win is paid per winning combination. Wild substitutes for all symbols except Scatter and MONEY Scatter Symbols. Bonus Scatter The BONUS Scatter Symbol can only appear on reels 1, 3 and 5. BONUS Scatter wins are awarded in addition to reel wins. 2 Bonus Scatter symbols in view on reels 1 and 3 with a special bonus symbol on reel 5 awards one of three Free Spins Features. Noble Princess Free Spins The player is awarded 10 Free Spins. During Free Spins the lowest paying symbols Jack-Ace are removed from the reels. 3 bonus symbols in view awards 5 extra Free Spins. The Bonus Scatter Symbol can only appear on reels 1, 3 and 5. 6 or more MONEY Scatter symbols in view activates Thunder Spins. The Total Bet that was used to trigger the Free Spins will be used for the duration of Free Spins. At the end of Free Spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited to the player's account and regular play then resumes. Grand Admiral Free Spins The player is awarded 10 Free Spins. During Free Spins 3 or more adjacent reels will be highlighted and award the same symbol outcomes on each reel. 3 or more bonus symbols in view awards 5 extra Free Spins. 6 or more MONEY Scatter symbols in view activates Thunder Spins. The Total Bet that was used to trigger the Free Spins will be used for the duration of Free Spins. At the end of Free Spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited to the player's account and regular play then resumes. Immortal Empress Free Spins The player is awarded 10 Free Spins. During Free Spins, any 3 adjacent reels will change to form a 3x3 colossal reel each spin. A colossal bonus symbol in view awards 5 extra Free Spins. 6 or more MONEY Scatter symbols in view activates Thunder Spins. The Total Bet that was used to trigger the Free Spins will be used for the duration of Free Spins. At the end of Free Spins, the total winnings for the spins will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited to the player's account and regular play then resumes. Money Scatter MONEY Scatter Symbols can appear on any reel, in any position. MONEY Scatter wins are awarded in addition to reel wins. 6 or more MONEY Scatter Symbols in view awards Thunder Spins. Thunder Spins At the start of Thunder Spins the triggering MONEY Scatter Symbols will be held in place. All other positions will spin using special Thunder Spins reels which only contain MONEY Scatter Symbols or blank symbols. The player has 3 spins to be awarded additional MONEY Scatter Symbols in view. Landing a MONEY Scatter Symbol in view will reset the Spins Remaining to 3. Blank Symbols have no value. Landing only blank symbols 3 times in a row will end Thunder Spins. At the end of Thunder Spins, the total winnings will be displayed. Any winnings will be credited to the player's account and regular play then resumes. Landing any CASHPOT MONEY SYMBOL (MINI, MAJOR or MEGA) in view will award that pot value. Fill every window position during Thunder Spins to be awarded the GRAND pot. Autoplay(SUBJECT TO OPERATOR CONFIGURATION AND JURISDICTIONAL REQUIREMENTS) There is an Autoplay function in the game that will start each spin automatically. By selecting 'AUTOPLAY' you can choose to bet automatically, one after the other. Select your stake as normal then press the 'AUTOPLAY' button to select the number of 'Autoplay' bets you want to play. You can stop Auto play from continuing onto the next spin by clicking the 'STOP' button. (The following additional functions are also applicable depending on jurisdiction) Auto Play will turn off if you get into any bonus round including Free Spins, if you reach the set Loss Limit or if a Progressive pot is awarded. Select your Loss Limit (maximum amount willing to lose for the autoplay session) and then press PLAY to begin your autoplays (other options are available). Payout Information and Game Rules Line wins are multiplied by the value bet on the winning line. 20 win lines are active in the main game and in Free Spins. Wins on different pay-lines are added. All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels beginning with the far left reel. Only the highest line win is paid on each line. Multiple scatter features can be won in a single game. Scatter wins are paid in addition to reel wins. Free Spins are played on the same number of lines and bet per line/Total Bet as the triggering game.
