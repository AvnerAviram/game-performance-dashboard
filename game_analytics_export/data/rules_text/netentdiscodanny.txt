Disco Danny Disco Danny™ is a 3-reel, 3-row video slot that features the Golden Multiplier feature and Free Spins. The game is played with 5 bet lines (fixed) and different bet values, coin values, and bet levels (only when playing with coins). The game can be played with cash or coins. To switch between cash and coins, go to the Game Settings. Note: Not all betting modes are available in all Casinos. BET is the amount of coins or cash to bet. When playing with coins, the bet value is set by clicking the Bet Settings button and selecting the coin value and the bet level. When playing with cash, the bet value is set by clicking the Bet Settings button and selecting the bet. BALANCE displays the amount of cash or coins available to bet. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the Paytable. Symbol payout values shown in the Paytable are dynamic and change in accordance with the selected bet value. A bet line win is equal to the value shown in the Paytable. Bet line wins pay if in succession from the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. Only the highest win per bet line is paid. Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added. The game features a combination win mechanics, where any combination of the following symbols awards a win. All coin/cash wins, except for Disco King wins, Golden Multiplier feature wins and Free Spins wins, pay on bet lines only. 3 Disco King symbols appearing anywhere on the reels in the main game award a win according to the Paytable. Scatter Symbols There are two types of Scatter symbols in the game: Silver Scatter symbols and Golden Scatter symbols. The Silver Scatter symbol can appear anywhere on the reels in the main game. The Golden Scatter symbol can appear anywhere on the reels in the main game and Free Spins. Golden Multiplier feature A Golden Scatter symbol landing anywhere on the reels in the main game awards a random win equal to 10 or 20 times the bet. In the main game, only one Golden Scatter symbol can appear on the reels at any time. Free Spins 3 Silver Scatter symbols or 1 Golden Scatter and 2 Silver Scatter symbols landing anywhere on the reels in the main game activate Free Spins. In Free Spins, there are 5 different levels with an increasing Multiplier. Free Spins start with level 1 and a x1 Multiplier. When you advance to a new level, the Multiplier increases according to the following table: Level Level Multiplier 1 x1 2 x2 3 x3 4 x5 5 x10 During Free Spins, only Golden Scatter symbols can appear on the reels. The 3 Scatter symbols that activated Free Spins turn into Golden Scatter symbols and stick to the reels at the beginning of Free Spins. All Golden Scatter symbols that land on the reels in Free Spins remain on the reels until the entire play area is filled with Golden Scatter symbols. When the entire play area is filled with Golden Scatter symbols, you advance to the next level. Each Golden Scatter symbol landing on the reels in Free Spins is assigned a random Multiplier between x1 and x50. The Multiplier assigned to each Golden Scatter symbol remains unchanged until you advance to the next level. Each time you advance to the next level or at the end of Free Spins, you are awarded with a win equal to the sum of the Multipliers assigned to all Golden Scatter symbols on the reels multiplied by the Multiplier of the current level and multiplied by the bet. The sum of the Multipliers assigned to all Golden Scatter symbols on the reels is displayed in the Level Win field. The starting number of Free Spins is 3. One or more Golden Scatter symbol landing on the reels in Free Spins resets the number of Free Spins to 3. Each time you advance to the next level, all Golden Scatter symbols disappear from the reels. Free Spins end either when there are no Free Spins left or when the entire play area is filled with Golden Scatter symbols on level 5. At the end of Free Spins, the game returns to the round that activated Free Spins. At the end of Free Spins, the total win from Free Spins is added to any wins from the round that activated Free Spins.
