Gangster Paradise GAME DESIGN Game Theme and Graphics Rise through the ranks and become a kingpin in Gangster Paradise™. In this 5 reel 25 line slot you must make your way through the windy city, outsmart then cops and lure the beautiful ladies. Land symbol stacks in full view during the Free Games and they will become stacks of wilds, land this and then you will have the power and the money.Main Game To become a true wise guy you have to land 5 matching symbols on one of the 25 win lines, which cannot be interrupted by other symbols. All winning patterns start on the first reel on the left and run along the win lines to the right. The revolver replaces all symbols except the Bonus symbol (Badge). Feature 3 or more Bonus symbols (Badge) trigger 7, 10 or 15 Free Games. During the Free Games when any stack of symbols appear and covers a full reel, that full reel becomes WILD. During Free Games, 2 or more Bonus (Badge) symbols that appear on the reels will award an additional 5 Free Games. Feature trigger: 3, 4 or 5 Bonus symbols Feature hit rate: 140-170 games. Feature top prize: 12500 x bet per line, playing 25 lines. Rules WILD substitutes for all symbols except Scatter. All pays are for wins of left to right. All prizes are on selected lines, excluding Scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by lines played times bet per line. Scatter wins are added to line wins. All pays are for wins of left to right, except Scatters, which pay any. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. RTP-95.26 The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
