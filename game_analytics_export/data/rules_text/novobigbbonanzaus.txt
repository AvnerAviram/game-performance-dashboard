Big Bankin Bonanza Game Theme and Graphics Walk in to the Piggy Bank for Grand rewards in Big Bankin Bonanza™. This 5 reel 10 line slot, is host to some exciting Features. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across the win lines in the base game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on a win line without any other symbols between them. Land 3 or more Card (Scatter) symbols anywhere on the reels to trigger 10 or more Free Games. Free Games Feature 3, 4 or 5 Card (Scatter) symbols appearing anywhere on screen triggers the Free Games Feature and awards 10, 15 or 20 Free Games respectively. During the Free Games Feature every 4th Wild symbol collected retriggers the Feature and awards 7 more Free Games. When a Banker symbol lands on screen all values on Pig symbols will be collected and paid out. Free Games begin with Pig symbols as MONEY symbols. Collecting 4 Banker (Wild) symbols will upgrade all Money Bag symbols on the reels into MONEY symbols and awards an additional 7 Free Games. Collecting a further 4 Banker (Wild) symbols will upgrade all Hat symbols on the reels into MONEY symbols and awards an additional 7 Free Games. Collecting a further 4 Banker (Wild) symbols will upgrade all Vault symbols on the reels into MONEY symbols and awards an additional 7 Free Games. The collection of Banker (Wild) symbols is displayed on the top of the Free Games Feature screen. Once a total of 12 Banker (Wild) Symbols have been collected, no additional Free Games will be awarded. Rules All prizes are for combinations of a kind. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Wild substitutes for all symbols. Scatter wins line wins are added. Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
