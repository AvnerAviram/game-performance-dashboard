Quarterback Fields of Glory BASE GAME RULES Only the highest winner paid per winning combination. The base game contains up to 243 ways to win. Wins must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far-left reel. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. All wins on multiple ways are added together. WILDS The WILD symbol is WILD and substitutes for all symbols except Foam Finger Symbol, Free Spins Symbol, Quarterback Symbol, Reciever Symbol. The WILD symbol is available during both the FREE SPINS and BASE GAME. WILD ONLY wins will pay out for 5 of a kind wins. For any wins which contain 3 or 4 of a kind WILDS, all way combinations which ONLY contain WILDS are ignored. FOAM FINGER BONUS When 3 or more Foam Finger symbols appear scattered in view the FOAM FINGER BONUS is triggered. Select a symbol to reveal a prize. Prizes are a multiple of the total bet. Prizes range from 4x to 1000x. FREE SPINS BONUS The Free Spins bonus is triggered when 3 Free Spins symbols appear in any position on reels 1, 3 and 5. 10 Free Spins are awarded. It is not possible to trigger other bonus games when this bonus is active. Free Spins are played with the same total bet, awards and winning combinations as the base game which triggered the bonus. Free Spins can retrigger. Free Spins will end when there are no Free Spins remaining. FIELDS OF GLORY BONUS When a Quarterback symbol appears on reel 1, and a Receiver appears on reel 5, the FIELDS OF GLORY bonus is triggered. Pick from one of three bonuses: RUN THE YARDS, FIELD GOAL or PICK A BALL. RUN THE YARDS will award a win of 1x total bet for each yard the player runs on the field, which is displayed on screen. FIELD GOAL will award a prize for kicking the ball between the goalposts. Three kicks are awarded. There is no skill element to this game. Wins are a multiple of the total bet. PICK A BALL will award a prize when one football is picked. Wins are a multiple of the total bet. Any of the three bonuses may award a MINI, MINOR, MAJOR or GRAND jackpot in place of a regular prize. TURBO MODE The Turbo Mode button is displayed on the bottom right of the reels with an 'ON' and 'OFF' option. Turn ON Turbo Mode button to speed up each spin, it can be turned OFF at any point during the game. When Free Spins are awarded Turbo Mode will be unavailable. Activating Turbo Mode does not influence the results of the spins. Turbo Mode may not be available in your jurisdiction. LEGAL NOTICE In accordance with fair gaming practices, the outcome of each and every game is completely independent.
