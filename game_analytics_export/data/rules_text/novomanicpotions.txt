Main Game Manic Potions™ features a respin bonus that contians modifiers, and a Lock & Spin bonus that contains the new Wincreasing mechanic. For every potion bottle win, a respin and modifier is triggered at no extra cost. Any new wins will be paid out, and if the same or new potion bottle win occurs, an additional respin will be awarded. This process can repeat until no more potion bottle wins occur. When this happens, the potion bottles will reset. Lock & Spin - Wincreasing™ x4 or more Scatter symbols triggers the Lock & Spin Wincreasing™ bonus. Modifiers A mystery multiplier is awarded to any wins after a repin. After a respin, any Wilds that land in view will expand into a stack. An additional row of symbols is available during a respin. An additional reel is available and wins also pay right to left. Rules All prizes are for combinations left to right on adjacent symbols, starting with the leftmost reel. Awards are multiplied by stake. Scatter wins and Line wins are added. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per scatter combination. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Wild symbols may only appear on reels 2, 3, 4 or 5.
