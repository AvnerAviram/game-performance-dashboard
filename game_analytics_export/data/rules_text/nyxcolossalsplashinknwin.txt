Colossal Splash Ink and Win Payouts Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the far-left reel. Wins that occur on all played lines are added to the total win. Only the highest win is paid on each win line played. The paytable reflects the current bet configuration. All awards shown are per winning line. WILD The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except the BONUS and FISH symbols. The WILD symbol can appear on any reel in the base game and COLOSSAL FREE SPINS. BONUS The BONUS symbol can appear on any reel. The BONUS symbol only appears in the base game. The BONUS symbol is a scatter symbol. COLOSSAL FREE SPINS cannot be retriggered. COLOSSAL FREE SPINS trigger as per table below: BONUS TRIGGER 3: 8 Free Spins 4: 10 Free Spins 5: 12 Free Spins COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL At the start of any spin, the COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL can be added to the reels on reels 2, 3, and 4. These will appear as a 1x3, 2x3, or 3x3 symbol. When all reels come to a stop, the COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL will turn into the same symbols, or a combination of any VALUE FISH symbols during COLOSSAL FREE SPINS. The COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL cannot reveal the WILD or BONUS symbols, and cannot appear during the INK & WIN feature. The COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL cannot appear as a 1x3 symbol across the middle row. COLOSSAL FREE SPINS The VALUE FISH can appear on any reel during COLOSSAL FREE SPINS and the INK & WIN feature. In COLOSSAL FREE SPINS, the COLOSSAL MYSTERY SYMBOL will appear more frequently than in the base game. It can also reveal any combination of VALUE FISH symbols. These VALUE FISH act as scatter symbols. Land 3 or more of these symbols on 3 different reels to trigger the INK & WIN feature. INK & WIN Each INK & WIN feature will start with 3 LIVES, shown as shells. The reels in INK & WIN will spin, and only contain FISH symbols and empty positions. Any FISH symbol that lands on the reels will be locked in place for the following spins, and its value will be added to the feature winnings meter. This will also reset the LIVES counter back to 3. The INK & WIN feature will end when three spins in a row do not land a FISH symbol on the reels, and the LIVES counter is empty. At the end of the feature, the value awarded will be equal to the sum of all the values displayed on the reels at that point. Once the feature completes, you will return back to COLOSSAL FREE SPINS if you still have remaining Free Spins to play. The SMALL FISH, MEDIUM FISH and LARGE FISH symbols are referred to as VALUE FISH and can appear during COLOSSAL FREE SPINS and INK & WIN. The VALUE FISH will only have a value applied to them during the INK & WIN feature. The ADD FISH & COLLECT FISH symbols are exclusive to the INK & WIN feature, and are referred to as MODIFIER FISH. If more than one MODIFIER FISH lands simultaneously, the ADD FISH will be applied first, reel by reel from top to bottom, followed by the COLLECT FISH with the same order. Whenever a MODIFIER FISH causes an update to any values, the win meter will also be updated accordingly. The MODIFIER FISH and VALUE FISH are collectively referred to as FISH symbols. The COLLECT FISH will add up all of the current values on the INK & WIN reels, and display the total as its value. The ADD FISH will display a random value: 0.50, 1.00, 2.00, 3.00, 5.00, 10.00. It will then add the current bet to all other values. SMALL FISH 0.50, 1.00, 2.00, 3.00, 5.00, 10.00 MEDIUM FISH 12.00, 15.00, 20.00, 25.00 LARGE FISH 30.00, 35.00, 40.00, 50.00 The INK & WIN feature begins by transferring the VALUE FISH symbols from the triggering spin in COLOSSAL FREE SPINS to the INK & WIN reel array. A random value will then be applied to each of the VALUE FISH, the possible values can be found in the tables above. After the values are applied, the OCTOPUS will mark 5 sections on the reel array with Ink, these are INKED HOT ZONES. The central reel position will always be an INKED HOT ZONE. Values in INK & WIN are displayed as cash values. Once all of the INKED HOT ZONES are filled with symbols, the OCTOPUS will land on the middle position and remove all INKED FISH symbols from the reels. The OCTOPUS will collect the values of the removed symbols and adds the sum to the win amount and its own value. After this, it will multiply its own value by 2, and keeps this as its value until the next collection. 4 new INKED HOT ZONES will then be randomly applied to any positions on the reels. The OCTOPUS will remain in the middle INKED HOT ZONE for the rest of the feature. The OCTOPUS collection can only happen after the first INK & WIN spin. Win Lines All games are played with 25 win lines. Wins can occur across any of the win lines.
