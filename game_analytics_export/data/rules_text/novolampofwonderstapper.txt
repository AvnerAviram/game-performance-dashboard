Lamp of Wonders – Tapper Game Theme and Graphics Welcome to the Lamp of Wonders! Each rub of the lamp has a chance to summon a magical Genie who will reward you with opulent prizes. Uncover the valuable multipliers for even greater riches! Get your wishes ready for Lamp of Wonder - Tapper! Main Game Lamp of Wonders - Tapper is a single click game with a Lamp that is surrounded by 17 prize symbols, each with a unique value. Every click of the Spin button causes the Lamp to be touched with a chance to summon the Genie, causing at least 3 unique prize values to be highlighted and won. With a powerful enough wish all 17 prize values can be yours! Multiplier Additionally, winning game rounds also have a chance to reveal a prize Multiplier. If a Multiplier appears after the Genie appears, then all winning prizes are multiplied by the displayed Multiplier value. The Multiplier can appear as 2x, 3x or 5x. Rules The aim of this game is to summon the Genie from the lamp and win the prize values displayed on the symbols. When the Genie appears, the winning prizes are highlighted. The player can win anywhere from 3 to 17 unique prizes. Any win that results in less than 10 prizes has a chance to gain a Multiplier of either 2x, 3x or 5x.
