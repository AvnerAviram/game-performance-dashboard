Gem Machine Game Introduction Gem Machine™ is a 5x3 slot game which features free spins and instant cash awards! Choose to play with between 1 and 5 reels! Select the number of reels to play with by clicking the buttons above the reels. Win cash awards by landing GEM symbols on the reels! The cash prize on GEM symbols will be instantly awarded. Bets Selecting your bet: To adjust the BET, use the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons to increase or decrease the Total Bet. Press the SPIN button to initiate the game. Gems Awards on GEM symbols will already be multiplied by the total bet per reel. GEM symbols appear on all reels. Free Spins Win Free Spins by landing FREE SPINS symbols. Between 1 and 5 Free Spins can be awarded per symbol. Landing a FREE SPINS symbol during a free spin will award further free spins; these will be added to the number of free spins remaining. FREE SPINS symbols appear on all reels. Jackpots The jackpots available in this game are MINI, MINOR, MAJOR, MEGA, and GRAND. The jackpot values are displayed above the reel set. The value of each jackpot is dependent on the initial bet. Disclaimer Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Only symbols that land on selected reels will be awarded. This includes awards from all symbol types - GEM, FREE SPINS and JACKPOT symbols. RTP is 97% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
