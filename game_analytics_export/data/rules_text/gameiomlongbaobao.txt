Long Bao Bao Long Bao Bao Try your luck with Long Bao Bao, a thrilling slot packed with Ingot Prizes, Wild Coins, and an array of exciting bonus features! Trigger Pot Bonuses for exciting special rounds, rack up Extra Spins for even more chances to win, and watch your payouts soar with the Symbol Upgrade Feature. The Grow Reels Feature expands the grid with multipliers, and when you stack these features together, you’re in for an unforgettable, reward-filled adventure! INGOT PRIZES FEATURE The Ingot Prizes feature offers opportunities to win big with every spin! Each Ingot Symbol is packed with a randomly selected credit prize, giving you the chance to score massive rewards. Prizes on reels 1 and 2 range from 20 to 80, while reels 3 and 4 hold prizes from 100 to 250! Lastly, reel 5 contains prizes that can range from 200 to an amazing 1500! WILDS AND POT BONUS FEATURES The classic wilds mechanic takes on a new form using Blue, Red, or Green Coin Symbols. These coins substitute for all symbols to create bigger winning combinations. Coins also have the chance to trigger the Pot Bonus Feature, where players activate the Extra Spin, Symbol Upgrade, or Grow Reels features—sometimes even all three at once, ramping up the excitement and keeping the fun going! Features triggered during the bonus stay active throughout your extra spins, and any additional spins earned will keep the momentum going. EXTRA SPIN FEATURE The Extra Spin feature is one of the thrilling highlights Long Bao Bao has to offer! When activated, +1 and +2 Extra Spin Symbols are added to reel 1. The excitement builds with every spin, unlocking even more chances to win and keep the luck flowing. SYMBOL UPGRADE FEATURE Get ready for the Symbol Upgrade feature, one of the game’s most electrifying highlights! Once activated, a Level 1 collection meter appears on-screen, and four Jackpot symbols are added to reel 5. During extra spins, every wild Blue, Red, Green, or Gold Coin that lands is collected into the meter. With every level, a specific symbol, such as the Drum or Frog Symbols, will be upgraded to a Ingot Symbol for the remaining spins, inching you closer to the Ingot Prize! GROW REELS FEATURE Prepare for the ultimate thrill with the Grow Reels feature! When activated, all reels expand to a towering five symbol positions high, unlocking 50 lines for even more ways to win. Multipliers are added to reel 5 to further bump your chances of landing big rewards! GAME INFO Malfunction void all pays and plays.
