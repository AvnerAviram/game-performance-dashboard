Boulder Bucks Game Introduction Boulder Buck$ is a 5 reel game with 3 symbols in view for each reel. There are three Free Spins bonus features and the player chooses which to play. How to play Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake per line. Win lines The default win line selection is 10 win lines. The win lines are not adjustable in this game. Start the game Press SPIN to initiate the game. Help All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table as multiplier values. These can be viewed by pressing the "i" button. UPick Free Spins Bonus Scattered feature symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 trigger the Free Spins Bonus The Player can be awarded 1, 2 or 3 selections The player can choose 1 of 3 Free Spins Games Mammoth Spins - 20 Free Spins with Expanding Wilds Pterodactyl Spins - 10 Free Spins with Side-Expanding Wilds Sabre-Toothed Tiger Spins - 6 Free Spins with Persistent Expanding Wilds Egg Pick Me Bonus Scattered feature symbols on reels 2, 3 and 4 trigger the Egg Pick Me Bonus The Player can be awarded a multiplier of stake win or entry to Upick Free Spins Bonus Big Bet A Big Bet game consists of 5 linked spins of the reels This contains the Boulder Smash Bonus or Mega Boulder Smash Bonus These bonuses give increased chance of entry to a UPick Free Spins Bonus The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 98%.
