Boogie Monsters About Boogie Monsters Boogie Monsters is a five reel, forty payline, and four hundred coin slot machine, with: A scatter symbol. A Fat Bat bonus game. A Free Spins bonus game. Game Payouts Winnings paid out on the slot machine are dependent on the symbols displayed on the payline, once the reels have come to a stop. Regular Payouts All payout tables display the number of coins that are won for each combination. The total number of coins paid out depends on the number of coins you play per payline. Regular wins are calculated by taking the number of coins won, multiplied by the number of coins played per payline. The number of credits won is dependent on the total number of coins won multiplied by the coin size you select. For a combination of regular symbols to produce a winning regular combination, the following criteria must be met: The symbols must be next to each other on an enabled payline. Regular winning combinations pay out from left-to-right, so one symbol must be displayed on the first slot reel. A symbol combination starting from the second, third, fourth or fifth slot reel does not pay out. Scatter Symbol Payouts Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the scatter symbol combination payout by the total number of regular coins bet. Scatter wins are added to regular wins. If you have a scatter win and a regular win, you are paid out for both wins, as the scatter symbol does not need to appear on an enabled payline to win. For a combination of scatter symbols to produce a winning scatter combination, the following criteria must be met: Completes a winning scatter combination when three or more appear on the reels. Must appear on consecutive reels and the first symbol must be displayed on the first reel. Free Spins Bonus Game Payouts You are awarded thirteen free spins. All regular wins are multiplied by 5 during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. The Fat Bat bonus game cannot be activated during the free spins. If three or more Fat Bat symbols are scattered from left-to-right on consecutive reels during the free spins, they complete a winning scatter symbol combination. Fat Bat Bonus Game Payouts You can win up to 24 000 coins in the bonus game. The win amounts displayed in the bonus game are already multiplied by your total bet amount. Only the bonus win amount is displayed. You win the total of all the bonus win amounts revealed. Game Rules Click the links for more information about the game rules. Activates the Fat Bat bonus game when three or more Fat Bat symbols appear on consecutive reels. Activates the Free Spins bonus game when three or more Lamp symbols appear on consecutive reels. Scatter wins are calculated by multiplying the scatter symbol combination payout by the total number of regular coins bet. Fat Bat Bonus Game Three or more Fat Bat symbols scattered from left-to-right on consecutive reels activate the Fat Bat bonus game. In this game: You can win up to 24 000 coins in the bonus game. You choose objects to reveal random bonus win amounts. Twelve hiding places are displayed. Behind each hiding place is a random bonus win amount. You can choose three hiding places. The win amounts displayed in the bonus game are already multiplied by your total bet amount. Only the bonus win amount is displayed. You win the total of all the bonus win amounts revealed. To play the bonus game: Click three objects. Random bonus amounts are revealed. A confirmation screen is displayed with your total bonus win amount. You are automatically returned to the regular slot game. Free Spins Bonus Game Three or more Lamp symbols scattered from left-to-right on consecutive reels activate the Free Spins bonus game. In this game: You are awarded thirteen free spins. All regular wins are multiplied by 5 during the free spins. All the bets and paylines played in the free spins are the same as the spin that activated the bonus game. Free spins can be re-activated during the bonus game. So, if three or more Lamp symbols are scattered on the reels during the free spins, you are awarded additional free spins. The extra free spins are added to the remaining number of free spins. The Fat Bat bonus game cannot be activated during the free spins. If three or more Fat Bat symbols are scattered from left-to-right on consecutive reels during the free spins, they complete a winning scatter symbol combination. To play the bonus game: The reels automatically start spinning the free spins. The free spins are complete. Your total bonus win amount is counted in the bonus Win box. You are automatically returned to the regular slot game. Playing the game The objective of the game is to spin the reels so the symbols form a winning combination on the payline. The payout is dependent on the winning combination formed. To play: Click Lines to select the number of paylines you want to play. Place a bet. How? Click Increase (+) or Decrease (-) to select the coin size you want to bet. To increase the number of coins you want to bet, click Coins . To bet the maximum number of coins permitted, click Bet Max . The number of coins bet increases to the maximum and the game automatically starts playing. 3. Click Spin . The reels start spinning. If you have a winning combination lined up on the payline, the win is highlighted on the reels. You are paid out the win amount as displayed in the Win box. If you do not have a winning combination, the game is over and you can play again. In other games this button enables you to choose the number of spins you want to play and your stop options. Once you have configured these options, click Start to begin your AutoPlay session.
