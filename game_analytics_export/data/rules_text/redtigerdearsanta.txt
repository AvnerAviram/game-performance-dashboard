Winners LIVE Feature buy toggle Game Features Snow White Wild Snow White Wild substitutes for all paying symbols and lands on the 1st reel during the normal game and Free Spins. It comes with a random win multiplier of 2x, 3x, 5x, or 10x, which applies to all line wins that include the wild. Additionally, it counts as the symbol forming the longest winning combination on a single win line. Santa Wild Santa Wild substitutes for all paying symbols and lands on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the normal game. It expands to cover the entire reel if it contributes to a winning combination. During Free Spins, 1 Santa Wild expands on the middle reel for the entire bonus round, with no additional Santa Wilds landing. It also counts as the symbol forming the longest winning combination on a single win line. Christmas Letters Collect Christmas Letters that land only on the last reel to transform into Christmas Present Wilds during Free Spins. Each letter is stored in the mailbox above the last reel. Christmas Present Wilds Christmas Present Wilds substitute for all paying symbols and appear only during Free Spins. They count as the symbol that forms the longest winning combination on a single win line. Free Spins Trigger 10 Free Spins by landing 3 scatters on reels 2, 3, and 4, or by collecting 60 or more Christmas Letters in the mailbox. Free Spins scatters cannot appear during the bonus round, and no retriggers are possible. At the start, a Santa Wild is placed on the middle reel and stays there for the duration. Santa will throw Christmas Present Wilds on reels 2 and/or 4 for each Christmas Letter collected, with up to 6 Present Wilds appearing per spin. Any remaining Christmas Letters in the mailbox after Free Spins carry over into the normal game, contributing to future bonus rounds. Bonus Buy - Free Spins with 10 Letters: Guarantees 3 Free Spins scatters and starts the bonus round with 10 Christmas Letters collected for 40x the stake. - Free Spins with 25 Letters: Guarantees 3 Free Spins scatters and starts the bonus round with 25 Letters collected for 100x the stake. - Free Spins with 60 Letters: Guarantees 3 Free Spins scatters and starts the bonus round with 60 Letters collected for 300x the stake.
