Rabbit Fields About this Game This is a 5 reel 4 row video slot game. This game has 30 lines. It features Free Spins and Super Free Spins with progressive Multipliers, Bonus Game, Carrot Wilds and Bonus Tokens. General Line wins begin with the leftmost reel and pay left to right only on adjacent reels according to the PAYTABLE. Only the highest win per line is paid. Multiple wins can be awarded per spin. It is not possible to trigger Bonus Game within Free Spins and Super Free Spins. Bonus Game and Free Spins of any type cannot trigger simultaneously. The Paytable displays the payout for each symbol combination based on the current bet. Wild Symbols Wild symbols substitute for all symbols except Free Spins symbols, Super Free Spins symbols and Carrot Sign symbols. Wild symbols substitution pays the highest possible winning combination on a line according to the PAYTABLE. Wild symbols can create their own winning combination when landing in a payline combination. Regular Wilds do not appear in Free Spins. Carrot Sign Symbols Collection Carrot Sign symbols only appear in the base game. Each reel has a Carrot Sign counter located above it. Each Carrot Sign symbol that lands is added to its respective Carrot Sign counter. Collect 3 Carrot Sign symbols to activate the Carrot Wilds feature on that reel Carrot Sign symbols do not appear on reels with an active Carrot Wilds feature. Carrot Sign counter, Carrot Wilds feature and remaining reels persist for each bet level. Carrot Wilds Feature Activate the Carrot Wilds in the base game, Free Spins and Super Free Spins. Carrot Wilds feature adds expanding sticky Carrot Wild symbols to the reel while active. When the Carrot Wilds feature is activated in the base game, on the next spin a Carrot Wild symbol is placed on the top position of the triggering reel. On every subsequent spin, the Carrot Wild symbol expands down 1 position on the reel. Once the Carrot Wild symbol has expanded to the bottom position, the feature on that reel ends and the Carrot Wild symbol is removed on the following spin. Free Spins symbols are visible through the Carrot Wild symbols. Trigger Free Spins, Super Free Spins and Bonus Game during Carrot Wilds feature. Bonus Game Trigger Collecting 1 or more Bonus Tokens scattered on screen gives a random chance at triggering the Bonus Game feature. The Bonus Tokens can only appear on mid symbols on all reels in the base game. Only the Bonus Tokens collected on the current spin contribute to the chance of triggering Bonus Game. The graphical collection pot in the game is purely for visual entertainment purposes and as such does not influence awarding the Bonus Game. If Free Spins or Super Free Spins triggers and Bonus Tokens are collected simultaneously, only the Free Spins or Super Free Spins feature plays out. Bonus Game There are 4 types of symbols: Mini, Minor, Major and Mega. Pressing a selectable item will reveal a symbol. Revealing 3 of the same symbol awards the corresponding cash prize according to the PAYTABLE. The Bonus Game feature ends when a prize has been awarded. Free Spins Free Spins symbols appear anywhere on reels 2, 3 and 4 only in the base game. Land 3 Free Spins symbols to trigger an indefinite number of Free Spins. Free Spins begins with a multiplier of x1. After every spin, the multiplier increases by 1. Every individual win in Free Spins is multiplied by the current multiplier. At the start of Free Spins, Carrot Wilds feature is active on reel 3, starting from the top reel position. Land a Carrot Wild symbol on any reel to trigger Carrot Wilds feature. Carrot Wilds feature starts from the same reel position as the triggering Carrot Wild symbol. Carrot Wild symbols do not appear on active Carrot Wilds feature reels. The feature ends when Carrot Wild symbols are no longer present on the visible reel area. All bets and lines played are the same as the spin that triggered the feature. Super Free Spins Collection Land 2 Free Spins symbols in the base game to add 1 point to the Super Free Spins upgrade meter. Landing more than 2 Free Spins symbols will not contribute any points to the Super Free Spins upgrade meter. Collect 20 points to upgrade Free Spins to Super Free Spins. The next time 3 Free Spins symbols land, Super Free Spins will be triggered instead of Free Spins. The Super Free Spins upgrade meter cannot collect more than 20 points. The Super Free Spins upgrade meter persists for each bet level. Super Free Spins Super Free Spins begins with a multiplier of x5. After every spin, the multiplier increases by 1. At the start of Super Free Spins, Carrot Wilds feature is active on reels 2 and 4, starting from the top reel position. When the feature ends, the Super Free Spins upgrade meter is reset to 0. Some settings and features may not be available in this game. Free Games A Free Games offer is a number of free game spins awarded to a player. Each offer has a unique reference number. The bet configuration is set by the game managers and cannot be changed. Wins are added to the cash or bonus balance, depending on the offer. An offer can be played now, played later, or discarded. Select Play Now to begin playing a Free Games offer. Only one Free Game offer can be played at a time. Select Play Later to postpone the offer. A postponed offer will be available again when re-entering the game. Select the Bin icon to discard the Free Games. A discarded offer can only be re-opened by contacting the game managers and providing the reference number. When an offer is complete, discarded, or expired the next available offer is displayed. Each offer has an expiry date and time; if an offer is not played before this time, it will no longer be available. Select the Information icon to view bet information and the offer expiry date and time. Additional Information Some settings and features may not be available in this game. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Any changes to game rules will be conducted in accordance with regulatory requirements. In the event of a disconnection, the final game state is displayed on return to the game. The balance is updated with the amount won or lost. The results of the last game played are displayed. If a request does not reach the server before disconnection, the results of the previous game played are displayed. Any features in progress that require interaction or selection can be continued. Return to Player The Return to Player (RTP) is the theoretical statistical percentage of the total money bet in a game that is paid out as winnings over time. Values are calculated initially by dividing the total payouts by the total bets from a simulation of numerous game rounds. Values reflect the maximum expected return and do not change. RTP: 94.15%.
