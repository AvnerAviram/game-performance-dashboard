777 Burn Em Up How to Play Game Introduction Burn ’em Up is a 10 win line game that features a ‘Free Spins Bonus’ game featuring upgrading 7’s symbols. In the base game any mixed 7’s symbols will pay on a winning line. Free Spins Bonus 3 x Burn ’em Up symbols in view at the end of a spin will award 12 Free Spins. 4 x Burn ’em Up symbols in view at the end of a spin will award 18 Free Spins. 5 x Burn ’em Up symbols in view at the end of a spin will award 24 Free Spins. During Free Spins, 7’s symbols that are part of a win are upgraded to match the highest 7’s symbol on a winning line. Free Spins use the same award card as the base game spin that triggered the feature . Select your stake To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button.
