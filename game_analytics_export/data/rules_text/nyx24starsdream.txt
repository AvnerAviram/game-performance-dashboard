To click/tap on this button does not influence the results of the spin. ABOUT THE GAME 24 Stars Dream is a 4-tall, 6-reel ways game featuring the Star Level Meter, Big Spin, and Star Ladder Jackpot. Matching symbols in any position on three or more adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel constitutes a pay way win. Multiple matching symbols on a reel contribute to the winnings multiplicatively. Only the longest matching combination for each symbol is paid. Simultaneous wins on are added. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the Paytable. The Paytable reflects the current bet configuration and changes in accordance with the total bet. All bets and payouts are in currency. Payouts are based on the total bet. SPECIAL SYMBOLS The Bonus symbol can land on reels 2,3,4 and 5 in the Base Game. The Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. The Coin symbol can land on reels 2,3,4 and 5 in the Base Game. The Coin symbol is a scatter symbol. The Big Coin symbol has a size of 4x4 and can land in the Big Spin. The Big Coin symbol works as 16 individual Coin symbols. The JACKPOT symbol has a size of 4x4 and can land in the Big Spin. The GRAND symbol has a size of 4x4 and can land in the Big Spin. The Blank symbol is a blocker. It does not take part in win evaluation. The Star symbol can land in the Star Ladder Jackpot. The Star symbol is a scatter symbol. STAR LEVEL METER Every time the Coin symbol lands, 1 point is added to the meter. At 100 points, the meter is full and the Star Ladder Jackpot Level is increased by 1. After each upgrade of the Star Ladder Jackpot Level, the meter is reset to 0. If the number of points exceeds 100, the Star Ladder Jackpot Level is increased, and the difference is added to the meterafter it is reset to 0. If the Star Ladder Jackpot Level reaches the maximum of 12, no points are added to the meter until the Star Ladder Jackpotis activated.
