General Information Table Game Type Video Slot Number of Ways 4608 ways Number of Reels and Rows 5x4/6/8 Return to Player 93.17% Feature Spin™ RTP 10 free spins 93.13% Feature Spin™ RTP 12 free spins 93.16% Feature Spin™ RTP 15 free spins 93.19% Buy Feature RTP 10 free spins 93.15% Buy Feature RTP 12 free spins 93.14% Buy Feature RTP 15 free spins 93.16% Ying Yang mode RTP 93.15% Game Features RED ENVELOPE LINK FEATURE Each Red Envelope symbol displays a stake multiplier or pot award. Possible stake multipliers on Red Envelope symbols:x.5 - x100. Possible pot awards on Red Envelope symbols: MINI (x150), MINOR (x200), MAJOR (x250) and GRAND (x500). If Red Envelope symbol lands on a reel, then all Red Envelope symbols on that reel are held for the next 3 paid spins. Every time a new Red Envelope symbol lands on that reel, the number of spins resets back to 3. The number of spins where Red Envelope symbols will remain on the reels is shown at the bottom of that reel. Red Envelope Link feature is triggered when every position on a reel is filled with Red Envelope symbols. Red Envelope Link feature awards all the winnings from the Red Envelope symbols. When winnings are collected, the feature ends and Red Envelope symbols are no longer held on that reel. Symbols that land behind Red Envelope symbols are not evaluated. The state of Red Envelope symbols is saved for each stake and is not carried on over other stakes. FREE SPINS FEATURE When 3, 4 or 5 Free Spins symbols land on the reels, it awards 10, 12 or 15 Free Spins respectively. Only Red Envelope symbols with multipliers are active during Free Spins feature. Possible stake multipliers on Red Envelope symbols:x.5 - x100. Red Envelope symbols with pot awards are not active during Free Spins. Red Envelope symbols are sticky on the reels positions during the feature and are held for the remainder of Free Spins. If full reel is filled with Red Envelope symbols, then reel multiplier is applied to all Red Envelope symbols on that reel. The range of reel multipliers: Reel 1 - x2-x10; Reel 2 - x2-x10; Reel 3 - x2-x10; Reel 4 - x2-x8; Reel 5 - x2-x6; Free Spins feature ends when no Free Spins remain. At the end of Free Spins, winnings from each Red Envelope symbol are collected and awarded. Free Spins symbols are not available during the Free Spins feature. All symbols besides Red Envelope symbols are grayed out and not evaluated during Free Spins feature. Free Spins feature starts after clicking the "Start" button on the Free Spins feature pop-up or after time out. The Free Spins bar fills each time a Free Spins symbol lands on the reels. However, Free Spins are only triggered when at least 3 Free Spins symbols appear in a single spin. Landing one or two Free Spins symbols will not trigger the feature via the progress bar. YING YANG MODE Ying Yang mode doubles the stake and Collection symbol becomes active on the base game reels. The paytable stays the same. Collection symbol is a Ying Yang symbol. Сollection symbol collects the multipliers and pots from all Red Envelope symbols on the reels. Multipliers are summed up, multiply the current stake and the winnings are awarded. Collected Red Envelope symbols are not held on the reels for Red Envelope Link feature. Collection symbol is not active during Free Spins. FEATURE RESPIN™ Feature Respin™ is available after Free Spins which are triggered from Base game/Feature Spin™/Buy Feature. It gives a choice to take the winnings from Free Spins or to replay the feature. In case of choosing replay, winnings from Free Spins are burned. Feature Respin™ is available after Free Spins and can be played only once. The best strategy is to replay Free Spins when the win amount in Free Spins is less or equal than: -For base game: x32.00 stake when offered 10 Free Spins x36.00 stake when offered 12 Free Spins x41.00 stake when offered 15 Free Spins -For Feature Spin™: x16.00 stake when offered 10 Free Spins x18.00 stake when offered 12 Free Spins x20.50 stake when offered 15 Free Spins -For Buy feature: x31.00 stake when offered 10 Free Spins x47.00 stake when offered 12 Free Spins x64.00 stake when offered 15 Free Spins Otherwise the best strategy is to take win. Choosing the Best Strategy does not guarantee that it will award a higher return. If no selection is made, then the best strategy decision will be made after timeout. Buy Feature Buy Feature is an additional way to trigger Free Spins feature instantly. Select “Buy Feature” button and configure Free Spins number and the amount that need to be paid to immediately initiate the feature. Feature Spin™ Additional way to trigger Free Spins feature. During the Feature Spin™ pre-game there is only one mode of play. Each game uses only active and inactive Free Spins symbols on the reels. Select the number of Free Spins and match 3 Free Spins symbols on the reels to win the Free Spins feature. Gamble Enter Gamble feature via “Gamble” button after any winnings awarded during the game. Choose a colour or suit to play the Gamble feature. A card is dealt. Each card is drawn from a new 52 card deck. The gamble amount is doubled if the correct colour is chosen or quadrupled if the correct suit is chosen. If incorrect, the gamble is lost. Press “Collect” button to exit the gamble feature. You can gamble up to 5 times or until the take win amount is greater than $250,000.00. No more than $250,000.00 can be won from the gamble feature. The gamble feature is not available when the game winnings are greater than $125,000.00. The gamble feature will return an average payout of 100%. Please note that this does not mean that every play or series of plays will return this percentage. The outcome of each gamble is random and is unaffected by previous results. The Gamble enables/disables in the settings by the Gamble toggle. If no selection is made, the Gamble will time out and collect any winnings. Bet Chance Once a player’s credit becomes less than the last spin stake amount the bet chance is activated. The dial becomes active. The odds of a successful bet chance are represented by the green segment. Landing on the green is a successful bet chance, red is a losing bet chance. On pressing SPIN the pointer will spin. If landing on the green, a spin of the reels is awarded at the same stake level that was used prior to bet chance activation. If red is landed, then the remaining balance is taken. When pressing cancel button the screen changes from the bet chance to the game, without any deduction from the remaining balance. The Bet chance disables/enables in the settings by the Bet chance toggle. Playing the Game Stake To play a round, configure your bet using the + / - buttons. Spin Options Click the Spin button or press the Spacebar to spin. The Spacebar disables/enables in the settings by the Spacebar to spin toggle. To play rounds faster, the player can use the Turbo option. Turbo – the reels spin and stop faster with no extra animation. Turbo can be activated by holding the Spacebar or turning it on from the Burger Menu. Payouts Rules Winning combinations are paid beginning with the leftmost reel to the rightmost reel. Winning combinations and payouts are calculated according to the game's “Paytable”, which can be accessed through the “Info” button. All awards (except Red Envelope symbols, Free Spins symbols and Collection symbols) pay on active winning way only. The highest win only paid per active winning way. Winnings on different ways are added up. Maximum win is limited to $250,000.00. Malfunction voids all pays. If a disconnection from the game or a malfunction of the game occurs and you restart the game, your balance is reset to the amount before the interruption occurred. All open sessions, which are not concluded by the player, plus any future transactions for those sessions, will be terminated after 90 days maximum and a minimum of 7 days depending on the residence of the player. Please be aware that each account allows only a single game to be played at any one time. Therefore, a game should not be played on more than one device, or multiple games on the same device simultaneously. Doing so may lead to various errors. For the best gaming experience, use of the latest version of software is recommended. Free Spins triggered from the Feature Spin™ are played at the base game stake corresponding to Feature Spin™ stake. To see the corresponding base game stake open Paytable in Feature Spin™ mode.
