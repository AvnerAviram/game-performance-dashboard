Acca Roulette Objective: The objective of the game isto accurately guess the outcome of the roulette spin (whether the right number or the right group) based on the questions answered and the player will win if the outcome of the roulette wheel matches with the answered question. The Game: Acca Roulette is played using a wheel with 37 numbered slots (1-36, plus a 0), the player must predict all the 6 questions in the BET SLIP and click on ‘PLACE BET & SPIN’ after choosing the stake amount to be wagered. The ball is spun around the wheel and will come to rest in one of the numbered slots, indicating the winning number/ group. How To play The game is a prediction-based game where player must choose either YES or NO to the questions displayed in the BET SLIP to start the game. The player will be asked 6 questions, the player must answer YES or NO to each question. The associated odds are added to the “BET SLIP”. This is repeated until all the questions in the BET SLIP are answered. After answering all the questions, player need to select the bet amount to be wagered. Once player has finished placing bets, Click on PLACE BET & SPIN button to start the game. When the ball comes to rest, the winning number is highlighted on the wheel. Game will automatically continue if the previous spin is win, if not new game will be initiated. PLACE BET button will be enabled and available after choosing YES or NO options from 2 nd question. Win amount for the spin depends on the option player selected for that question and will be shown to the player on game screen after Win icon. Player has an option to place bet from second question by skipping to answer for further questions. First Question YES/NO odds are multiplied with the further selected question odds for calculating the payouts. For example, if player selects all YES for the questions displayed, then the Total will be multiplication of all YES odds for the questions. Player has an option to ‘Cashout/Half Cashout’ the winnings only after third spin win. This “Cashout/Half Cashout” options are available for 4 th and 5 th spins as well. The Cashout amount will be 90% of the win amount*stake selected by the player. Half Cashout is half of the Cashout amount will be awarded to the player. Half Cashout amounts will be added to the player balance once all the spins for the questions are completed. Player can click on ‘Spin’ button to continue with further questions without Cashing out the winnings. All pays are rounded towards most accurate accumulated return to the player. All rounding in this roulette uses the "Half Round Up" method. This means cents go up if the value has decimal point greater than or equal to 5 and stay with same value if the decimal point is less than 5. Round off examples 1.7650 becomes 1.77 1.7760 becomes 1.78 1.7640 becomes 1.76 All payouts (Cashout, Half Cashout, total win amounts) will be rounded according to this 'Half Round Up' method. STAKE: The total bet amount is shown in the STAKE meter at the bottom of BET SLIP. TOTAL: Total amount is the multiplication of win amounts for all 6 questions based on player choice. Maximum win from this game is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency. Minimum and maximum bet for a bet slip will be displayed on the game UI after answering Question 2. Any bets below or above the minimum or maximum bets will be rejected. Maximum bet is set to player based on Maximum win which is capped to 250,000 EUR or its equivalent amount in your currency. POTENTIAL RETURNS: Potential Returns in the game are calculated as selected STAKE * TOTAL. Differences in potential return values will be corrected after placing a bet. Game balance is displayed at the bottom of the game screen. Tap on the Help button from the game menu on the top right of the game screen to view the game rules. To switch on or off sound in the game, tap on Sound button that is located at the right top corner of the screen in the game menu. Player can disable sounds option in the settings panel as well. Turbo-mode: Wheel spin animation will speed up on selecting turbo mode in the settings panel. Tele Type: Keeping this check box checked will stop the tele type animation coming for the questions. Uncheck this box to continue tele type animation in the game. LUCKY DIP: Player can click this button option which selects the lowest odds between YES/NO for all the further questions. Lucky dip will randomly pick the questions from the set. Lucky Dip option will disappear when player choose YES/NO for 5 th question.
