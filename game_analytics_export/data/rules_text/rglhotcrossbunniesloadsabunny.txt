Hot Cross Bunnies - LoadsABunny Game overview Hot Cross Bunnies - Loadsabunny is a five reel, 20 win line slot game featuring a Bunny Money Free Spins Bonus Round. If 3 or 4 Bunny Scatter symbols appear anywhere on the reels, the Bunny Money Free Spins Bonus Round is awarded. If a further 3 or 4 Scatter symbols appear consecutively during the feature, a Retrigger of either 10 or 12 more Free Spins will be awarded. 5 Bunny scatter award the Jackpot. Hot Cross Bunnies - Loadsabunny features a maximum prize of 5000 x the Stake per Line value. Formats This document provides information on Hot Cross Bunnies - Loadsabunny for the following game formats: Flash HTML5 Tablet HTML5 Phone. Playing the game Bet value Select the total bet value. Start button Flash game: Press the start button to spin the reels. Tablet and Phone games: Press the start button to spin the reels. The tablet game has the additional option to use thumb control start buttons – these buttons can be turned on / off by pressing the tool button and selecting the on / off toggle button. Auto button Flash game: Pressing the auto button will provide various auto play options. Once the desired options have been selected, press the begin button to start auto play. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Tablet game: Pressing the auto button will provide the option to select 10, 25 or 50 auto spins. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Phone game: Pressing the auto button will start 10 auto spins. Pressing the stop button at any time cancels auto play. Displays Various displays are used to show important information to the player during the game. On phone, some of these displays appear as pop-ups, only when needed. Information display At different points during game play, this will show information about the game. Bet display This displays the total bet value charged per game. Win display At different points during game play this will display: Total win line payouts. Paid display This displays the total paid for a complete bet. Game views The game can be played in different views, as follows: Flash: Full cabinet view. Close-up view of the reels only. Press the Real 2 Reel button to toggle between the views. Tablet and Phone: Both Tablet and Phone games can be played in landscape and portrait view. Payouts Win line payouts When a winning line is obtained: The winning symbols will be highlighted. The win value will be shown. The total amount won will be shown. Payout values Hot Cross Bunnies - Loadsabunny has a maximum payout of 5000 x Stake per Line value. Bunny Money Free Spins Feature The Free Spins feature is awarded when 3 or 4 Scatter symbols appear in any position on the reels. During the Free Spins Feature, each symbol that lands on the win line will light the corresponding symbol on the award table. Light 3, 4, or 5 consecutive symbols to win the displayed prize. After an award is paid, the winning symbols are un-lit, ready to be used again. Light 3, 4, or 5 consecutive Bunnies to win more Free Spins, or the Jackpot! All prizes displayed are multiplied by the Total Bet Value. The maximum payout in any one game is 250 x the Total Bet value. The feature will end when either the maximum payout is reached or no more free spins remain. The RTP is expressed as the percentage returned to players in winnings of the total amount staked, e.g. if £1,000,000 was staked and £960,000 was returned, the RTP of the game for that period of play would be 96%. The expected RTP for this game is no less than 95.47%. The actual RTP during a playing session can vary widely, in either direction, from the theoretical return.
