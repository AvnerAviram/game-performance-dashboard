Winners LIVE Feature buy toggle Game Features Lucky Leprechaun A Lucky Leprechaun may appear unexpectedly, puffing on his pipe, triggering a feature at random: Random Tile: Releases single tiles of paying symbols or Wilds, generating 3 or more pairs of these symbols on the first reels, left to right. Mega Random Tile: Ejects super paying symbols or Super Wilds, creating 3 or more of these symbols on the first reels, holding multipliers between x2 and x7, and counting for multiple symbols in win ways. Free Spins: Unveils 3 or 4 Free Spin symbols, placed on central reels, triggering Free Spins or Mega Free Spins. Beer Bonus: Launches 3 or 4 Beer Bonus symbols onto central reels, triggering the Beer Bonus or Mega Beer Bonus. Fairy Bonus: Unleashes 3 or 4 Fairy Bonus symbols on central reels, initiating Fairy Bonus level 1 or level 2. Beer Bonus 3 or 4 Beer Bonus symbols on reels 2 - 5 trigger the Beer Bonus, with 4 symbols granting the Mega Beer Bonus. 4 taps appear atop the middle 4 reels at the Beer Bonus start. In the regular Beer Bonus, pick 1 tap for an instant cash prize. In the Mega Beer Bonus, all 4 taps are awarded without picking. The taps pour beer into glasses over the reels, each granting a stake multiplier cash prize. Fairy Bonus 3 or more Fairy Bonus symbols activate the Fairy Bonus, advancing to level 2, 3, or 4 with 4, 5, or 6 scatters, respectively. The bonus holds 6 levels, each with 6 crystals to select. Picking crystals unveils instant cash prizes, with some crystals on higher levels offering level-up arrows, granting an extra pick on the next bonus level. Free Spins Free spins activate with 3 or 4 symbols landing on reels 2-5, granting 10 free spins or Mega Free Spins if 4 symbols trigger it. During free spins, every win elevates a global win multiplier, starting at x1 for Free Spins and x5 for Mega Free Spins. The Leprechaun may still appear, triggering only Random Tiles or Mega Random Tiles. Beer Bonus or Fairy Bonus symbols won’t appear in free spins, and each additional free spin symbol in the bonus round rewards 1 extra spin. Feature Buy 1. Lucky Leprechaun for 20x the stake. 2. Beer Bonus or Mega Beer Bonus for 45x the stake. 3. Fairy Bonus or Mega Fairy Bonus for 35x the stake. 4. Free Spins or Mega Free Spins for 80x the stake.
