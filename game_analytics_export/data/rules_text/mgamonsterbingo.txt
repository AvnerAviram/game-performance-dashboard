Monsters Bingo STANDARD GAME RULES Bingo-based draw in which 30 balls are drawn from a drum containing 60 balls. The player has up to four cards with 15 numbers on each one. If a win figure is matched on a card, the prize associated will be awarded from the paytable. EXTRA BALL RULES Sometimes the player is offered the option of buying extra balls at additional cost. JACKPOT The top payout will be awarded if the card is complete within the first 30 balls drawn. PRIZE TABLE Prizes specified in credits. When bonus labelled figure in prizeplan is matched, player will access in a bonus game. BONUS GAME 1 Select a cell in the active row where a prize, an end bonus or a wildcard are hidden. The final prize is the accumulated one. BONUS GAME 2 In each row there are prizes and arrows. If the arrow is chosen you access the next row with better prizes. The final prize is the one obtained. BONUS GAME 3 Consists of a race between 4 characters of different colors. In each round a character chosen randomly moves a random number of positions. The resulting award is the value contained in the display reached. BONUS GAME 4 The game is about pressing "PLAY" when you want to stop the selection. You will get the highlighted prize.
