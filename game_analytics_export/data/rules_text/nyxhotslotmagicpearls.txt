Hot Slot Magic Pearls The game has 25 independent reels on a 5x5 grid. A win is awarded for drawing at least 10 symbols of a kind anywhere on the reels. Only the highest win is paid. The maximum win amount is 2500x bet. When a Sticky symbol appears, the outcome of remaining non-sticky symbols is entirely random. Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Bonus symbols. Drawing at least 6 Bonus symbols of any type activates the Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game. If 3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols are drawn, they stick to the reels for one spin, increasing the chances of activating the Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game. All Sticky symbols are locked in place while other symbols are spun. SPECIAL FEATURES HOLD THE JACKPOT BONUS GAME The game is played on 25 reels. All Bonus symbols stick to the reels during the Bonus Game. 3 Re-Spins are granted at the beginning of the Bonus Game. Each new Bonus symbol resets the number of Re-Spins to 3. The Bonus Game continues until Re-Spins are finished, or all reels are filled with Bonus symbols. Cash symbols award prizes ranging from 1x-10x player's bet. MINI, MINOR and MAJOR Jackpot symbols may randomly appear during the Bonus Game and award corresponding Jackpots. Multiple Jackpots of the same type may be won during the Bonus Game. Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash symbols. Double Collector symbol accumulates all values from Cash, Collector and Double Collector symbols and and doubles their value. Mystery symbol can transform into any other Bonus symbol. Mystery symbols open one by one at the end of the Bonus Game. When the Bonus Game ends, the prize is paid for the sum of Bonus symbol values, including MINI, MINOR, MAJOR Jackpots, unless the GRAND Jackpot is won. Bonus symbols pay only at the end of the Bonus Game. Collecting all 25 Bonus symbols of any type awards the GRAND Jackpot of 2500x player's bet. The GRAND Jackpot is the maximum prize in the game and Bonus symbol values are not added to it. Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game is played at the same bet level and Volatility Level™ as the spin that activated the bonus. Ultra and Extreme Volatility Using the Buy Feature, Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game can be activated on Ultra Volatility and Extreme Volatility levels, characterized by even bigger wins than other Volatility Levels™.
