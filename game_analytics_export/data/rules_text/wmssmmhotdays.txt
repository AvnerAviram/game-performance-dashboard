Super Monopoly Money Base Game MM WILD is wild for all symbols except FREE PARKING. WILD is wild for all symbols except BONUS, MM BONUS, MM WILD, and FREE PARKING. Only highest winner paid per winning combination. Paytable reflects current bet configuration. Line pays must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. FREE PARKING appears on reels 2-5 only. MM WILD appears on reel 5 only. MM BONUS only appears in Free Parking Feature. 5 JACKPOT symbols pay is different than 5 of kind JACKPOT symbol with WILD combination. FREE PARKING FEATURE The Free Parking Feature may be triggered when 1 or more FREE PARKING appears on the reels and can either create a win, trigger the Free Spin Bonus, or possibly increase existing wins. Pick 1 of 5 FREE PARKING to reveal a symbol. All FREE PARKING will be replaced with the selected symbol, before the evaluation. The Free Parking pick will always contain a WILD and MM BONUS, plus the 3 symbols that have landed on reel 1. If JACKPOT lands on reel 1, the pick will be replaced with a WILD. In the Free Spin Bonus the FREE PARKING pick items will always contain a WILD and BONUS, plus the 3 symbols that have landed on reel 1. If Auto Play is enabled when the Free Parking Feature is triggered, a pick is made at random automatically. FREE PARKING appears on reels 2-5 only. MM BONUS only appears in Free Parking Feature. FREE SPIN BONUS 3 or more BONUS and/or MM BONUS appearing left to right on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel on any active paylines trigger the Free Spin Bonus. 15, 10, or 8 free spins are awarded for 5, 4, or 3 BONUS and/or MM BONUS, respectively, per triggering line combination. If the Free Spin Bonus is triggered using a MM BONUS or the MM WILD, then the value won in the Free Spins is awarded in MONOPOLY MONEY, in addition to a monetary award. During Free Spin Bonus, WILD is wild for all symbols except BONUS and FREE PARKING. In the Free Spin Bonus, the FREE PARKING pick items will always contain a WILD and BONUS, plus the 3 symbols that have landed on reel 1. FREE PARKING appears on reels 2-5 only. During free spins, an alternate set of reels is used. During Free Spin Bonus, 3 or more BONUS appearing left to right on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel on any active paylines award additional Free Spins to the amount of Free Spins remaining. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game. During Free Spins, the bet per line and the active paylines remain the same as the spin that triggered the feature. MM WILD, MM BONUS, and JACKPOT do not appear during the Free Spin Bonus. Wheel Bonus cannot be triggered during the Free Spin Bonus. MONOPOLY MONEY When MM WILD appears anywhere on reel 5, it will expand to fill the reel and is wild for all symbols except FREE PARKING. If the MM WILD appears on reel 5, any winning combinations awarded for that spin are awarded in MONOPOLY MONEY in addition to a monetary award. If the MM WILD appears on reel 5 on a spin resulting in no winning combinations, a random amount of 10 to 100 times the bet per line may be awarded in MONOPOLY MONEY. MONOPOLY MONEY can be accumulated. A maximum amount of MONOPOLY MONEY, displayed in the in-game help pages, can be spun on the wheel at one time. If more than the maximum amount of MONOPOLY MONEY is accumulated, then the remainder of the value after the spin remains in MONOPOLY MONEY. WHEEL BONUS Click WHEEL BONUS to start the Wheel Bonus. Spin the wheel by clicking on MR. MONOPOLY. The multiplier or Feature the on-screen arrow points to after the wheel stops is awarded. If the wheel stops on a multiplier value, the value is awarded and the bonus ends. Multiplier values range from 1x to 100x. If the wheel stops on the Go! Re-spin, then an additional spin of the wheel is awarded. Multiplier values on the Go! Re-spin wheel range from 10x to 200x. If the wheel stops on Chance, then Chance Feature is triggered. Choose 1 of 3 Chance Cards for a multiplier value (5x or 10x) or Go! Re-spin. If the wheel stops on Chance during a Go! Re-spin, the multiplier values range from 10x to 200x and the Go! Re-spin is no longer available. If the wheel stops on Community Chest, then Community Chest Feature is triggered. Choose 1 of 12 Community Chest Cards for a multiplier value (1x to 25x) or Award All. Selecting the Award All card awards the sum of all Community Chest Cards. If the wheel stops on Community Chest during a Go! Re-spin, the multiplier values range from 10x to 50x. Awarded multiplier values are applied to the MONOPOLY MONEY used for that wheel bonus and the total amount is awarded as a monetary prize to the player. Click the CANCEL button to return to the base game and not spin the wheel. Click the CASH OUT button to open the Cash Out menu. Within the Cash Out menu, click the Take Win button to accept 5x the accumulated MONOPOLY MONEY as a monetary prize, click the Spin button to exit the Cash Out menu and start the wheel spin, or click the Return To Game button to exit the Cash Out menu and Wheel Bonus with MONOPOLY MONEY intact. MONOPOLY MONEY Hotel/House Awards A House or Hotel prize may be awarded after any wagered spin where MONOPOLY MONEY is not awarded. MONOPOLY MONEY Houses and Hotels are awarded in MONOPOLY MONEY only. The chance of winning a MONOPOLY MONEY House or Hotel increases with the amount wagered per spin. MONOPOLY MONEY House and Hotel prizes are awarded randomly from individual ranges, both displayed within the in-game help pages. QUIKSET PANEL Clicking on the Total Bet meter opens and closes the Quikset panel. All values in the Quikset panel are equated with maximum paylines enabled. Click a value to immediately change the Total Bet value to that amount. The number of active paylines and the bet per line will be updated to reflect the Total Bet amount selected in the Quikset panel. Clicking on a value in the Quikset panel will not set the reels into motion. PAYLINES Wins on multiple paylines are added together. Total line bet must be divided equally between each active payline; therefore, all total bet amounts are not available. The total bet is the number of lines multiplied by the cash bet on each line plus the feature bet. The feature bet is forced wager of 10x the bet per line. Only active paylines can register wins.
