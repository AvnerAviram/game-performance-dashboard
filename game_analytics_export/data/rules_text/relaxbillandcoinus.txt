Bill and Coin Game Rules Bill & Coin is a 6 reel and 4 row slot machine with 4096 ways to win. Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the paytable. Only the longest matching combination for each symbol is paid. Multiple matching symbols on a reel contribute to the winnings multiplicatively. The wild symbol substitutes for all symbols for normal wins. The game also has cascading mechanics, meaning that winning symbols get destroyed and new ones fall in, for new chances to win. Once a spin has been initiated, the bet size for that round is locked and cannot be changed. The theoretical return to player for this game is 94.00%. Winnings are presented in the users currency. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Incomplete games will be automatically resolved after 2 days. Any winnings resulting from automatic resolve shall be paid to the players account. If your balance decreases so it becomes lower than the selected bet size, the bet size will automatically be lowered to the highest applicable bet size. (Applicable based on market) The English version of the game rules prevails. Buy Bonus Rules The Buy Bonus awards a spin that guarantees 3 Bonus symbols. The theoretical return to player for the Buy Bonus is 94.40%. The Buy Bonus spins is played at 46x your regular bet. Buy Super Bonus Rules The Buy Super Bonus can only be activated in the base game. The Buy Super Bonus awards a spin that guarantees 2 Bonus symbols and 1 Super Bonus symbol. The theoretical return to player for the Buy Super Bonus is 94.61%. The Buy Super Bonus is played at 102x your regular bet.
