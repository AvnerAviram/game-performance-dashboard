Magic Merlin Megaways Intro By the light of Merlin’s Sorcery, players can experience Megaways™ like never before! Surrounded by bubbling cauldrons and bewitching animations, this spellbinding take on a familiar mechanic will enchant and mesmerize players. How to Play Pressing the PLAY button will cause the reels to spin. Players can choose between stakes by pressing the + and – buttons. The current stake is displayed in between these buttons. The top row adds extra symbols above reels 2, 3, 4 and 5. Each spin can have up to 117649 Megaways™ to win. Every winning symbol forms part of a reaction and is replaced by symbols coming from above on the reels and from the right of the top row. Symbols (excluding Freespins, Freespins Retrigger and Wild) appearing in the top row can randomly be replaced with a Wild symbol. Wild symbols substitute for all other symbols apart from freespins and freespins retriggers. Mystery cards can randomly appear after a spin to reveal winning symbols. Get four or more scatters to win freespins. Any extra scatters award extra freespins. Features will cancel any autoplays. Paytable The Paytable can be found in the information screens by pressing the button labelled ‘i’. Adjusting the stake will dynamically alter the winning values shown in the Paytable. Megaways Each spin can have up to 117649 Megaways™ to win. Wins are paid left to right on adjacent symbols regardless of symbol size. Feature Get four scatters to win 10 freespins. Every extra scatter adds an extra 5 freespins. The unlimited win multiplier begins at one and increases after every win reaction. The freespins retrigger scatter symbol appears only in the top row. If 3 freespins retrigger scatters appear in the top row, the player is awarded 5 extra freespins. If 4 freespins retrigger scatters appear, an extra 10 freespins are awarded. Once the number of freespins remaining reaches 0, the feature will come to an end.
