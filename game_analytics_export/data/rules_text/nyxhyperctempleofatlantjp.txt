HYPERCHARGED HOLD AND SPIN FEATURE During the base game, each PYRAMID displays MAJOR, MINOR, MINI, or a bet stake multiplier. During the Free Spins Bonus, each PYRAMID displays MINOR, MINI, or a bet stake multiplier. When the feature is triggered from the Free Spins Bonus, the GRAND Jackpot is not available. During the base game and Free Spins Bonus, 6 or more scattered PYRAMID trigger the Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature and 6 spins are awarded. All triggering PYRAMID turn into COIN and remain held in place for the remainder of the feature unless they are collected by CHEST , PYRAMID , or SUPER PYRAMID. If a triggering PYRAMID lands on the center position, it is turned into a COIN , shifted to another position, and held for the remainder of the feature unless collected by CHEST , PYRAMID , or SUPER PYRAMID . When the feature is triggered from the base game, any COIN which lands during the feature displays a MAJOR, MINOR, MINI, or a bet stake multiplier. When the feature is triggered from the Free Spins Bonus, any COIN which lands during the feature displays a MINOR, MINI, or a bet stake multiplier. Prizes on PYRAMID triggering the feature are shown as COIN in the feature and their values are retained. All COIN values in the feature range from 0.5x - 40x Total Bet Stake or the Mini, Minor or Major Jackpot. If CHEST lands on screen during the feature, 2 additional spins are awarded and all COIN displayed on screen are collected. The prizes displayed on these COIN are summed and displayed on the collecting CHEST , which is shifted to another position and held until the end of the feature, unless collected by a PYRAMID or SUPER PYRAMID . If PYRAMID lands on screen during the feature, 4 additional spins are awarded and all COIN and CHEST displayed on screen are collected. The prizes displayed on these COIN and CHEST are summed and displayed on the collecting PYRAMID , which is shifted to another position and held until the end of the feature, unless collected by a SUPER PYRAMID . If PYRAMID with GRAND lands on screen during the feature, the GRAND JACKPOT meter is awarded, then the symbol acts the same as a regular PYRAMID . If any SUPER PYRAMID lands on screen during the feature, 6 additional spins are awarded, and all symbols displayed on screen are collected. The prizes displayed on these symbols are summed and displayed on the collecting SUPER PYRAMID , which is shifted to another position and held until the end of the feature, unless collected by another SUPER PYRAMID . SUPER PYRAMID will only land on the center position if at least one PYRAMID or SUPER PYRAMID is on the grid. If there are no symbols for CHEST or PYRAMID to collect, then the additional spins are awarded but CHEST or PYRAMID are not held. At the end of the Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature, all prizes displayed on COIN , CHEST , PYRAMID , and/or SUPER PYRAMID are awarded. An alternate set of reels is used during the Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature. Each bet stake uses a different set of reels, unless below the Progressive active bet range. Only COIN , CHEST , PYRAMID , and SUPER PYRAMID appear during the feature. CHEST , PYRAMID , and SUPER PYRAMID will only land on the center position. COIN will not land on the center position. PYRAMID with GRAND is available only when the feature is triggered from the base game and at the active bet amount. During the Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature, the distribution of prizes changes with bet stake. SUPER HYPERCHARGED HOLD AND SPIN FEATURE When 1 to 5 PYRAMID land during a base game spin, they are collected to randomly award the Super Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature. The Super Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature increases the amount of CHEST, PYRAMID, and SUPER PYRAMID that may land on the center position. When the feature is awarded, the player receives 6 spins with 1 to 5 COIN randomly appearing in the feature and multiplier prizes ranging from 0.5x - 40x Total Bet Stake or the Mini, Minor or Major Jackpots. At the end of the Super Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature, all prizes displayed on COIN , CHEST , PYRAMID, and/or SUPER PYRAMID are awarded. An alternate set of reels is used during the Super Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature. Each bet stake uses a different set of reels, unless below the Progressive active bet range. Only COIN , CHEST , PYRAMID , and SUPER PYRAMID appear during the feature. CHEST , PYRAMID , and SUPER PYRAMID will only land on the center position. COIN will not land on the center position. During the Super Hypercharged Hold and Spin Feature, the distribution of prizes changes with bet stake. FREE SPINS BONUS When only 1 or 2 scattered BONUS land during a base game spin, 8 free spins may be randomly awarded. However, if 3, 4, or 5 scattered BONUS land during a base game spin, 8, 10, or 15 free spins are awarded, respectively. 4 or 5 scattered BONUS also award 15x or 100x total bet stake, respectively. During the Free Spins, 2, 3, 4, or 5 scattered BONUS that land awards 3, 5, 8, or 10 free spins, respectively. 4 or 5 scattered BONUS also award 15x or 100x total bet stake in the free spins, respectively. All line pays awarded during the Free Spins Bonus are multiplied by 2x. An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Spins Bonus. The size and state of the Perceived Persistence Statue in the base game has no effect on triggering the Free Spins Bonus. Winning combinations for these reels are identical to the base game.
