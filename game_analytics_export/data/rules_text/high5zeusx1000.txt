Zeus X 1000 RTP: 94.0% VOLATILITIES: BASE GAME: 26.1 - EXTREME POWER BET 1: 30.2 - EXTREME GAME RULES All 6 reels are always in play. All symbols are scatter symbols Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Only the highest win is paid for each scatter symbol combination. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total amount bet. Scatter wins are awarded in addition to line wins. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. All symbols except multipliers and Bonus symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above on reels 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. BONUS GAMES The Bonus Games feature is initiated by landing 4 Bonus symbols during a base game spin and awards 15 Bonus Games. 3 or more Bonus symbols appearing in any position on the reels after all tumbling is complete awards 5 additional bonus games. A bonus multiplier accumulates over the course of the Bonus. Each time a multiplier appears during a winning spin, it adds its value to the bonus multiplier. Multipliers can appear with the following values: 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 8x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 250x, 500x, 1000x. After all tumbling is complete, the bonus multiplier is applied to the total win accumulated during the spin. The Bonus Games ends when 0 bonus games remain. BASE GAME MULTIPLIER FEATURE After all tumbling is complete, the sum of all multipliers is applied to the total win accumulated during the spin. Multipliers can appear with the following values: 2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x, 8x, 10x, 12x, 15x, 20x, 25x, 50x, 100x, 250x, 500x, 1000x.
