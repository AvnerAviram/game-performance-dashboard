Fire Star About • Fire Star is played over 3 reels. • All awards pay left to right only. • All awards pay for consecutive symbols on active win lines only. Repeat Wins • After a winning spin and on a chance basis the win may be repeated. • The win is awarded again. On a chance basis, further repeats may be awarded. • The bonus ends when no further repeats are given. • The repeat win may only be awarded if the total potential win is less than or equal to the max win. Wild Reels • On a chance basis during any spin, reel 2, or reels 2 and 3 can turn wild. • Any wins are then awarded. • Wilds are removed in the next spin unless the same reels are also turned wild on the next spin. Gamble • Select red, black or a suit to gamble or take win. • If the players selection matches the hidden card, the player wins. Wilds • Wild substitutes for all symbols. Win Lines • The base game has 5 independent win lines. • £20 and £30 high roller has 9 independent win lines. • £50 high roller has 15 independent win lines. Turbo • Activating the turbo will speed up the reel spin. • Turbo cannot be used in high roller. Additional Info Expected payback is at least 95%. In accordance with fair gaming practices required in most legal jurisdictions worldwide, each and every game outcome is completely independent, the odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. The odds do not vary based on prior ourcomes, bet size, time of day, day of week, etc. The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays by numerous players over an extended period of time.
