Star Gods You can change your game play and sound settings. Click Settings Choose the required settings Close the window to return to game RTP Is 95.975%. Available settings: Audio Volume Adjust the volume of in-game audio or turn it off completely. Sound Effects Adjust the volume of in-game sound effects or turn them off completely. Music Adjust the volume of in-game music or turn it off completely. Turbo Adjust the speed of the reels. This does not change the result of the spins. AutoPlay enables you to automatically play a set number of consecutive games without interacting with the game for up to 100 spins. AutoPlay plays with the last bet value you selected, so you must select a bet amount before starting AutoPlay. The number of spins remaining in your AutoPlay session is displayed in the game. During autoplay, you can click on the 2 triangles on the Spin button to skip any animations on that spin. Autoplay stops automatically if any feature is triggered, selected number of spins is played out or the balance is less than the total bet per spin. Please see breakdown of autoplay options below: AutoPlay Options Number of spins Set the number of spins you want to play, up to 100 spins. Loss Limit Set a limit on the maximum amount to lose to stop AutoPlay. Single win limit Set the limit on the maximum amount you want to win in a single spin to stop AutoPlay. Click on the Custom next to each Autoplay value to enter it manually. Start Autoplay Select the bet amount you wish to play and click on Autoplay button. Select your Autoplay options – number of spins, loss limit and single win limit. Click Spin to start Autoplay Click Stop to end Autoplay.
