Huolong Valley Huolong Valley is a 5 reel slot game with a BONUS FEATURE to boost your winnings. During regular play, the goal is to achieve a winning combination of symbols on any of the 20 lines. Payouts depend on the selected bet value and the winning combination of symbols achieved. The theoretical average return to player (RTP) is 96.988%. BONUS FEATURE Free Games are awarded when 3 or more Bonus symbols appear on the reels. Players will pick 1 of 6 lucky lanterns to determine the number of Free Games awarded. The lantern will award 4 to 7 Free Games. Players will also pick 1 of 6 coins that are dropped from the select lantern to award a multiplier for the Free Games. The coin will award a multiplier from 3x to 6x. One or more WILD substituting on a winning line pays 2X the win in Free Games in addition to Free Games multiplier. Free Games are played at the paylines and bet of the trigger game. Free Games feature can be awarded during Free Games feature awarding the same number of Games and Multiplier as the current Free Games feature. GAME RULES Play 20 lines for 20 coins Payouts are made according to the Paytable Payline wins are multiplied by the bet Highest win only on each selected payline Wins on different paylines are added All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on consecutive reels, except SCATTER and BONUS which pays any WILD substitutes for all symbols except SCATTER and BONUS One or more WILD substituting on a winning line pays 2X the win Malfunction voids all pays and plays Scatter wins are multiplied by the Total Bet Scatter wins are added to payline wins All wins multiplied by the bet except BONUS and Scatter How to Play Choose your BET SPIN MAIN GAME SCREEN BUTTONS: SPIN: Spins the reels AUTOPLAY: When more than zero AUTOPLAYs is selected the SPIN button will display the number of AUTOPLAYs that will be played. Stop: Stop the reels STOP AUTOPLAY: Once auto play begins the SPIN button turns into a STOP AUTOPLAY button and the number of AUTOPLAYs remaining will be displayed in the STOP button. Continue: During free games, this button when appears allow players to skip to the next free game. HOME: Accesses casino menu SETTINGS: Accesses the SETTING page for HOME, INFO, PLAY, SOUND Option, BET and AUTOPLAY options BET: Adjusts the BET. The UP/DOWN buttons adjust the BET up or down SETTINGS PAGE BUTTONS: LINES: Lines cannot be adjusted. This game is fixed for 20 lines.
