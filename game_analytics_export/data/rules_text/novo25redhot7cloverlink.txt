It is a 25 lines, 5 reels / 3 rows game. Don't miss out on your chance to hit 2 jackpots. In the Clover Link Edition you can enter the CLOVER FEATURE by hitting 5 or more Clovers. Collect as many Clovers as you can while in the CLOVER FEATURE and get a great credits reward. Main Game The aim of 25 Red Hot 7 Clover Link™ is to line up five identical symbols next to each other from left to right across 25 fixed win lines. A minimum combination of three matching symbols starting from the leftmost reel pays out. The Wild symbol can appear on every reel to substitute all symbols except the Scatter to form winning combinations. There is a Scatter symbol in this game, which can appear on every reel. Most amazing is the chance to enter the CLOVER FEATURE, where you have 3 spins to collect as many Clovers as possible. With every new Clover, while in the CLOVER FEATURE, your respin count will be reset to 3. At the end of the CLOVER FEATURE all amounts on visibl e Clover symbols will be added together and paid out. If there is a full screen of Clover symbols the prize will be doubled, except a jackpot. There are 2 possible jackpots to win while in the main game and in the CLOVER FEATURE. Rules All prizes are for combinations of adjacent symbols of a kind on selected lines, except Scatters. All prizes are for combinations left to right, except Scatters. Highest win only paid on each selected line and per Scatter combination. Scatter symbols pay at any position on screen. Scatter wins and line wins are added. Scatter symbols can appear on all reels. Wild symbols appear on all reels and substitute for all symbols on the same reel, except Scatters. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. 5 or more scattered Clovers of any color trigger the CLOVER FEATURE with 3 re- spins. The Clovers that triggered the CLOVER FEATURE are held and all other positions are re-spun. Appearing new Clovers during the CLOVER FEATURE are also held and the number of remaining re-spins is reset to 3. The CLOVER FEATURE ends when either no re-spins are remaining or when the screen is fully filled with Clovers. All visible prizes on Clovers are awarded at the end of the CLOVER FEATURE. All CLOVER FEATURE games are played with triggering bet and lines. A Fullscreen of 15 Clovers during the CLOVER FEATURE doubles all visible prizes, including the MINI and MINOR bonus, but excluding the GRAND and MAJOR JACKPOT. Golden Clover symbols can appear multiple times during the base game and CLOVER FEATURE. Pays only during the CLOVER FEATURE. Available prize multipliers are up to 100 times of total bet. In case of a jackpot win up to 400 times of total bet. Grand Jackpot Clover appears on reels 1, 3 and 5 during the base game and CLOVER FEATURE, with 1 being the leftmost, and 5 being the rightmost reel. Pays immediately when it appears on all three reels. If only one or two GRAND symbols appear during the CLOVER FEATURE they will transform into Clovers with prize multipliers. Major Jackpot Clover appears only once per base game or CLOVER FEATURE. Pays immediately upon appearing. Mini or Minor Clover can appear multiple times during the base game and CLOVER FEATURE. Pays only during the CLOVER FEATURE. Pays each time it appears on screen during the CLOVER FEATURE.
