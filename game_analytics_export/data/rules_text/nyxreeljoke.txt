Reel Joke The game has 6 reels, 4 rows and 20 paylines. All wins are paid for combinations of 3, 4, 5 or 6 symbols on an active bet line, from left to right. Only the highest win per active bet line is paid. Wild is a paying symbol and substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and special symbols. Scatter pays at any position on the reels, only the number of Scatter symbols counts. SPECIAL FEATURES Variable Infinite Multiplier The game features 4 variable multiplier symbols. Drawing one of the multipliers during the base game sets the initial value of Infinite Multiplier active during Free Spins. Following multiplier values are available: x3, x5, x7, x9. Multipliers have no effect in the base game, only in Free Spins. FREE SPINS WITH MOVING REELS AND INFINITE MULTIPLIER WITH INITIAL VALUE A draw of at least 3 Scatters triggers 10 Free Spins. The winnings from Free Spins accumulate until the end of the feature. When a winning combination is drawn, the reels move one position to the right and new symbols are drawn on the first reel. The mechanism is repeated as long as new winning combinations appear. During Free Spins, the Infinite Multiplier is active. The initial value of Infinite Multiplier depends on which multiplier was active when Free Spins were triggered. The value of Infinite Multiplier increases by 1 after every win. All winnings are multiplied by the current multiplier value. Special symbols can increase the number of remaining Free Spins by 3, 4 or 5. Free Spins are played at the same bet level and Volatility as the spin that activated the bonus. SPECIAL SYMBOLS WILD SYMBOL Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for Scatters and other special symbols. SCATTER SYMBOL Drawing at least 3 Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels pays the win and activates Free Spins. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. After activating the gamble feature, a player can: take the win and end the gamble feature, continue playing the gamble feature by selecting one of the two symbols. After selecting a symbol, a draw is made to determine the winning symbol. The player can end the gamble feature after each round. In the event of a loss, the player loses the win value specified in the Current win counter and returns to the main game. The gamble feature has seven rounds. Current win – amount of win that can be taken. Double to – win amount after a correct guess.
