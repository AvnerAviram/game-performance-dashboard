Lucky Lassie RTP: 96.5% Volatility: 6.7 Game Rules Malfunction voids all pays and play Choose the amount you want to bet Click the Spin button to spin the reels This game is played at 243 ways. All 5 reels are always in play. Ways wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. The same symbol, or its substitute, in a different position on the same reel pays that ways win again. Only the highest win is paid for each ways symbol combination. Ways wins are multiplied by the bet multiplier. Scatter symbols award in any position on the reels. Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency. Free Games bonus During the base game, 3 Free Games Bonus Symbol in any position on reels 2, 3 and 4 starts the Free Games Bonus. Awards 7, 8 or 9 free games. Free Games Bonus symbol is a scatter symbol. Different reels are used during the Free Games Bonus. Free games automatically play on the same lines at the same bet multiplier as the game that initiated the Free Games Bonus. The Free Games Bonus does not award additional free games. The Free Games Bonus ends when 0 free games remain. TUMBLING REELS All symbols in winning combinations disappear. The empty symbol positions are filled as symbols tumble from above. Afterwards, symbols are re-evaluated for new winning combinations. Reels continue to tumble until no winning symbol combinations are created as a result of the tumbling reels. UPGRADING SPLIT SYMBOLS The upgrade symbol appearing in any position on reel 5 will upgrade itself and all top high pay symbols on the reels to an upgraded high pay symbol. Any high pay symbols or upgrade symbols that tumble in for the duration of the spin after upgrading will tumble in as an upgraded high pay symbol. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
