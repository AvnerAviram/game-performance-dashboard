Bruno Bingo Rules On any card, only the highest value win is paid when a pattern is overlapped with exception of the bonus feature winnings. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Jackpot The jackpot is won if bingo is achieved with a maximum of 30 balls. As soon as the jackpot is won the round will end. Joker Ball On buying an extra ball Bruno might appear. Now the player can select his own best number. Extra Balls When the player has a total possible win value of 40 times bet, he will be able to purchase extra balls. The cost for each extra ball is clearly displayed and the player can decide to buy the extra ball or to collect his winnings. Every now and then extra balls will be offered for free! There is a total of 11 extra balls that can be bought. Game In the base game 30 out of 60 balls will be drawn. Winning patterns will be paid and any “1-to-go” will be clearly marked yellow. After the base game the player might be offered to buy extra balls. RTP The Return to Player for this game is 97.48%. The maximum win from a single game is £250,000. Last modification date: 6/29/2020
