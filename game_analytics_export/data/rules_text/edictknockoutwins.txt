Knockout Wins Return to Player (RTP) The expected return to player (RTP) is 96.05%. This RTP represents the long term theoretical game payout. Rules KNOCKOUT WINS is a 5-reel 3-row game with 243 fixed win ways (SUPERWAYS). Stakes range from €0.10 to €20.00 (or currency equivalent). Malfunction voids all plays and pays. Wins Each win line is calculated after the reels have come to a stop. All wins are paid in accordance with the pay table. All pays are for wins on lines played on consecutive reels read left to right. The highest win will be paid for each win line. Coinciding wins on different win lines are added together to give the final result. All wins and winning combinations are shown in the pay table. WILDCARD Symbol symbol substitutes for all symbols except the scatter symbol . appears only on reels 2, 3 and 4. Scatter Symbol is a scatter symbol. Scatter wins are paid in addition to other winning lines. Free Games 3 or more award 15 free games. During the free games a multiplier of X2, X4 or X8 is awarded for 1, 2 or 3 3 on screen respectively. Stakes do not have to be paid for the free games. Your effective bet for these free games is your actual bet when you won the free games. The resulting winnings will be shown in the pay table. Last modification date: 12/6/2017
