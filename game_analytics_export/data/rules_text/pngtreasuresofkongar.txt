Treasures of Kongar Treasures of Kongar Unfinished Games If your game round is disrupted, all game information and placed bets are stored until you reopen the game. You may continue your unfinished round from the point of interruption. Unresolved bets placed but remaining undecided in incomplete games will become void after 90 days. Malfunction voids all pays and plays. Information Way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations, according to the information in the paytable and game rules. When winning on multiple ways in a single game round, all winnings are added together. All winnings from Features (such as Free Spins), Bonus Games and/or Scatters (if applicable) are also added to way wins. All winning combinations are paid out at the end of a game round. Free Spin features are played with the same bet as the game round that triggered the feature – unless otherwise stated. The bet cannot be changed during a currently running game round. Please refer to the game rules for more information. All wins pay left to right, beginning with the left-most reel. Only the highest win is paid per winning symbol combination. Game Rules TREASURES OF KONGAR is a 5-reel video slot game with 1024 ways to win! The game includes two WILDS: GEMSTONE and KONGAR COIN, which substitute for all symbols except SCATTER. The GEMSTONE only appears during the base game, while the KONGAR COIN appears during KONGAR COINS and KONGAR’S RICHES features. SCATTER can appear on any reel, while WILDS can appear only on reels 2, 3, and 4. KONGAR COINS Feature: KONGAR above the reels collects a GEMSTONE each time it lands. KONGAR COINS feature can be randomly triggered during the base game, awarding 2 to 3 KONGAR COIN symbols. KONGAR COINS cannot overlap with SCATTER, GEMSTONE, or KONGAR symbols. KONGAR COINS may reveal an X2 or X3 MULTIPLIER. MULTIPLIERS are added together and applied to all wins. All KONGAR symbols on the reels are upgraded, increasing their payout during this feature. The number of GEMSTONE symbols collected does not affect the number of KONGAR COINS or MULTIPLIERS awarded. KONGAR’S RICHES Feature: Landing 3 to 5 SCATTER symbols anywhere on the reels, even if they are not adjacent, will trigger the feature and award FREE SPINS. The more SCATTERS you land, the more initial FREE SPINS are awarded: • 5 SCATTERS award 15 FREE SPINS. • 4 SCATTERS award 12 FREE SPINS. • 3 SCATTERS award 8 FREE SPINS. During KONGAR'S RICHES, all KONGAR symbols are upgraded, increasing their payout throughout the feature. GEMSTONE symbols do not appear on the reels during FREE SPINS. Each KONGAR COIN symbol that appears during KONGAR’S RICHES may reveal an X2, X3, X4, or X5 MULTIPLIER, which are added together and applied to all wins. KONGAR COINS feature can be randomly triggered during KONGAR’S RICHES, awarding 3 to 5 KONGAR COINS. KONGAR COINS cannot overlap with SCATTER, KONGAR COIN, or KONGAR symbols. Each SCATTER that lands during KONGAR'S RICHES adds 1 FREE SPIN, up to a maximum of 100 FREE SPINS. Any SCATTER that lands after reaching the maximum FREE SPINS will not add additional spins. The average RTP is 94.20%. RTP is achieved through participation. Bets are selected using the bet buttons. Click the plus and minus buttons to change the bet one step at a time. There are 1024 fixed pay ways. Way wins are awarded for left to right adjacent symbol combinations. To start the round, click SPIN. When the reels stop, the symbols displayed determine your prize according to the paytable. Minimum Bet: 0.10 USD Maximum Bet: 20.00 USD Any combination of wins resulting from a single game cycle, including the results of any possible triggered bonuses, is capped at a maximum of 15000 times total bet. Actions Paytable- Toggles the display of the paytable. Auto Play- Click the AUTO PLAY button to enable/disable the Auto Play feature. In Auto Play mode, a number of consecutive game rounds are initiated automatically using your current bet settings. The Auto Play mode is automatically disabled depending on your settings or if your balance becomes too low. Hyper Spin- Pressing and holding down the SPACE button activates Hyper Spin mode after 1.5 seconds. The spins speed up immensely but slow down if a feature is activated. Spin / Start / Spacebar- Starts the game round with the currently selected bet. Press Spin to begin. Stop- Stops the reels more quickly. Fast Play- Toggle on for a significantly faster gameplay. Auto adjust bet- Automatically reduces the total bet when starting a new spin, if the balance is too low to initiate a spin at the current bet level. The reduced total bet selected will be the highest possible bet but will still be lower than the selected bet size when autospins were initiated.
