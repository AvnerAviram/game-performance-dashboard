Mad Hit Mr Coin Mad Hit Mr Coin is a 3-reel, 3-row video slot featuring the Mad Hit Instant Win, Mad Hit Collect and Win, and Mad Hit Collection features. Wins are awarded only through feature activation. All reels are in play at all times. The Total Bet is set using the bet selector. Autoplay automatically plays the game for the desired number of rounds. Increasing and decreasing your Total Bet will result in the Prize value adjusting by the same proportion. All win values shown are in the currency wagered. In the base game, only one feature can be activated at a time. MAD HIT INSTANT WIN The Mad Hit Instant Win feature is activated during the base game when at least one Mad Hit symbol lands on reel 2 together with one or more Prize symbols on reel 1 or 3. This feature cannot be activated in the same game round as Mad Hit Collect and Win or Mad Hit Collection. When activated, each Prize symbol on the reels awards its displayed value. The Mad Hit symbol appears only on reel 2. The Prize symbol appears only on reels 1 and 3. Prize value ranges from 0.5x to 100x the total bet and includes jackpots: Mini, Minor, Major, and Grand. MAD HIT COLLECT AND WIN The Mad Hit Collect and Win feature is activated when at least one Mad Hit symbol lands on reel 2 together with Prize symbols on both reels 1 and 3. The feature starts with the same reel set of symbols that was visible when it was activated. The feature begins with 3 respins. During the feature, only Prize symbols and Mad Hit symbols can land on the reels. The Mad Hit symbol appears only on reel 2 and remains on the reel throughout the feature. Any new Mad Hit symbol that lands, remains on the reel during the feature. The Prize symbol appears only on reels 1 and 3. Every time a Mad Hit or Prize symbol lands on the reels, the number of respins resets to 3. Each Prize symbol awards its displayed value once for every Mad Hit symbol on the reels. The Mad Hit symbols accumulate the displayed values of all Prize symbols. The feature ends when no more spins remain. When the feature ends, the total value accumulated by all Mad Hit symbols is awarded as the total win. MAD HIT COLLECTION During the base game, any high-tier Prize symbol on the reels may randomly activate the Mad Hit Collection feature. This feature cannot be activated during the same round as Mad Hit Collect and Win. Upon activation, a random number of medium- and high-tier Prize symbols and Mad Hit symbols are added to the reels. The feature then transitions directly into the Mad Hit Collect and Win feature, starting with the symbols from the triggering spin. BUY FEATURE Tapping Buy Feature initiates the Buy Feature Dialog which allows the player to activate MAD HIT COLLECT AND WIN at any time. Buy Feature (MAD HIT COLLECT AND WIN) can be triggered from the base game only by buying it for 50x the current total bet. The Buy Feature confirm button initiates the feature triggering spin at the selected bet. The feature triggering spin will award all coinciding wins. After the feature is completed, the bet amount will return to the original setting before the Buy Feature was purchased. The RTP of the game when using Buy Feature is 94.33%. Return to Player The theoretical return to player for this game is 94.36%. AUTOPLAY Enables playing the game automatically for a predetermined number of rounds at the current Total Bet. How to use the autoplay feature Open autoplay popup. Set the autoplay number of rounds by choosing it from the list. To activate a limit, click on the relevant textbox and enter a numeric value. To exit the autoplay screen without starting the autoplay rounds, click the 'X' button. Click the 'Start' button. Once clicked, the autoplay popup will close and spin button will change to a stop button which will allow you to stop the autoplay whenever you choose. Note. If you are disconnected while playing, all Autoplay settings will return to default when you reload the game. Autoplay Limits Single Win - Will stop the autoplay function and a popup message will be displayed when a single win payout is >= the 'Single Win' value entered. Win Limit - Allows you to control your combined wins by entering a numeric value that will stop the autoplay function and trigger a popup message when the win limit is reached. Calculated as follows: (Sum of wins) - (Sum of bets) >= (Win Limit amount). Loss Limit - Allows you to control your combined losses by entering a numeric value that will stop the autoplay function and trigger a popup message when the loss limit is reached. Calculated as follows: (Sum of bets) - (Sum of wins) >= (Loss Limit amount). When the limit's conditions are met, the autoplay will stop and the relevant popup message will appear in the middle of the screen. The messages for the limits will appear as follows: Win Limit - 'Win limit reached' Single Win - 'Single win reached' Loss limit - 'Loss limit reached' Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
