Book of Ra Temple of Gold Game Theme and Graphics The ultimate adventurer’s dream! Three ancient editions of the Book of Ra™ reveal the secret key to El Dorado’s legendary Temple of Gold™. Main Game Your aim is to land 5 matching symbols along a win line. Winning combinations run from left to right across active win lines in the normal game. To win, the symbols must lie next to each other on an active win line without any other symbols coming between them. The "Book of Ra" symbol substitutes all other symbols and can therefore complete a winning combination. And what's more: land 3 or more "Book of Ra" symbols anywhere on the reels to trigger 10 free spins. Free Games BOOK OF RA substitutes for all symbols except the scattered SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOL(S) of the feature. 3 or more BOOK OF RA symbols trigger 10 free games. Features triggered with 3, 4 or 5 BOOKS respectively allocate 1, 2 or 3 SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOLS that expand to cover all three positions on the respective reels and pay on any position on selected lines. Free games can be won again during the feature and are played at current bet and lines. Trigger: Any 3, 4 or 5 scattered BOOK symbols trigger 10 Free Games. Top prize: 5000 * credits bet per line Approx.: 170-210 games Rules SCATTER (Book) substitutes for all symbols except scattered SPECIAL EXPANDING SYMBOLS. All pays are for wins of left to right except scatters, which pay any. All prizes are on selected lines excluding scatters. Highest win only paid per selected line. Line wins are multiplied by credits bet per line. Scatter prizes are multiplied by total bet. Scatter wins are added to line wins. RTP:95.28% The maximum win from a single game is £250,000.
