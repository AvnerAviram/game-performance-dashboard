Monopoly On The Money How to Play Game Introduction This is a 5 reel game where the player can win Free Spins with cash multipliers. The reel has 10 win lines. 3 or more Scatter Symbols awards Free Spins. Free Spins When Free Spins are awarded the Cash multipliers become active. If a Mr Monopoly Symbol appears in the same spin as a Multiplier Symbol the the cash is added to the win. If there is more than one Mr Monopoly Symbol, each will award all values independently. Additional information can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. Big Bet A Big Bet game initially consists of 5 linked spins of the reels. Additional benefits awarded include: - Scatter Symbols are Wild and Persistent to the end of the Big Bet spins. - Red Hot Respin Bonus If the player has two Scatter Symbols on the reels at the end of the Big Bet spins, this bonus may award an extra spin of the reels with just Scatter Symbols present. If any extra Scatter Symbols are presented then the player is awarded Free Spins. - Chance Card Bonus At the end of Free Spins the Chance Card Bonus will award one of three options: A cash prize. Extra Free Spins. Game over. - Reel Extensions A House Extension extends the reels by one row. A Hotel Extension extend the reels by two rows. Additional information can be viewed by pressing the "?" button. Select your stake - To adjust the STAKE, use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase or decrease the size of your stake. Press the SPIN Button to initiate the game. Win lines The game contains 10 win lines. All line win amounts are shown in the games pay table. These can be viewed by pressing the "?" button.
