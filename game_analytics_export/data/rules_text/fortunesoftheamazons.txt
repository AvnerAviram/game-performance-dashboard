Fortunes of The Amazons GAME RULES Malfunctions void all pays and play. All 50 lines always played. All credits staked are multiplied by the credit value. All pays left to right on consecutive reels starting with the leftmost reel. All symbols pay once in any position on the reel, except substituted wins which may pay multiple times. Select credits per spin (50, 100, 150, 200 or 250). Paytable amounts initially displayed are based on current credits selected. The Won field displays winnings in credits. Only the highest win on a pay line pays. Wins on different paylines are added together. WILD substitutes for all symbols, except FREE SPINS and BONUS WHEEL. Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON field. Features are played at the same credits per spin as the triggering spin. Game Features/Bonus Refer to Paytable for more information Amazons’ Fire Feature Free Spins Feature Luna Bonus Wheel Spin In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. Flash only Clicking SPIN or space bar repeats the last bet Mobile only Tapping SPIN repeats last bet. Auto Spin In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for 5, 10, 20, 50 or 100 spins. Auto Spin mode is cancelled and must be activated after a Big Win occurs. Flash only Click the AUTO SPIN button and select the number from the pop-up menu. Click on STOP to cancel Auto Spin. Mobile only Press and hold the Spin button to activate the Auto Spin function or access Auto Spin from the Game Settings menu.
